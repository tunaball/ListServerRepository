--- Christopher DeRose <CDerose at Merion.com> wrote:
> Hey folks, newbie here w/ a quick question.  
> 
> My duo 230 hasn't been booted up for a while and
> works fine in the dock but won't start up by itself.

Try a Power Manager reset. With the battery OUT and
the
lid closed, hold the rear power button for about 30
seconds. Now boot up with an AC adaptor or in a Dock
_then_ install the battery.

Leave it sit and both the PRAM and main batteries
should recharge.
