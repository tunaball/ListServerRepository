You will want to go here:
<http://www.quovadimus.net/serialbridge.html>
LW_Serial_Bridge will bring your 310 back to life. It costs $20, but 
it's
worth the cost to keep the printer going. I used it for a while with my
310 and now it's been passed on to my parents who have been using
it for a couple years. It's a tiny bit slower to print than with OS 7.x,
but a lot cheaper than buying a new laser printer.

-wayne
