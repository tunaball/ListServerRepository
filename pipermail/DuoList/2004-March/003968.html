On Mar 24, 2004, at 7:02 PM, Tom and Lisa P wrote:

> I am trying to connect an external CD ROM to my 2400.  I have it 
> terminated and hooked up correctly (as far as I can tell), but I cant 
> get the 2400 to even see the hardware.  Is there some sort of 
> extension required on the 2400 for SCSI use ?

Unless it's an Apple CD-ROM, you need to use something other than the 
Apple CD/DVD extension. CD-ROM Toolkit™, CD-Sunrise, CLD Access, or D2 
CD are a few more common ones. It really depends on the drive. I find 
CD-Sunrise works with almost everything.

Also, if you're using a dual-purpose HDI30 SCSI adapter, make sure the 
switch is in the SCSI position, not the Dock position.
