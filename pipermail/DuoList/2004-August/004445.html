Justin Mayrand has published " There are 3 iterations
of the DuoDock:
DuoDock (with 512k VRAM, FPU and HDD optional),
DuoDock II which gave the 68030 powered Duos a 32K L2
cache (with 512k VRAM, FPU and HDD optional, though
usually included) and the 
DuoDock Plus which further added an AAUI port for
onboard Ethernet (with 512k VRAM, FPU and HDD
optional, though usually included)."
Apple has published "Upgrade the Duo Dock
to a Duo Dock II by adding a
new logic board and cover.
These parts are available
individually, not as a kit." 
"The Duo Dock Plus has the same features as the Duo
Dock II,
but without the FPU and cache."

Don Pruitt
