You might be looking for a 2.5" to 3.5" SCSI adapter.  I found them for $15
on eBay about 2 years ago using "2.5 inch to 3.5 inch SCSI" or "2.5 inch
SCSI"  for search terms.

You might also want to check out the 500 MB and 1 GB RAM (yes, RAM) SCSI
notebook drives with a Google search on the net.

The notebook SCSI standard (40 pin) was something that Apple and IBM both
used.  Don't know about others.  All SCSI PowerBooks seem to have used them.
