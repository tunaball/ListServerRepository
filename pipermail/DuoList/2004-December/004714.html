>It sounds like you haven't tried to get rid of that death light yet,
>so you might not have any major problem.  First thing I would try is
>to disconnect the a/c adapter and battery, and open up that little
>port protector door on the back of the laptop.  Then, there's a tiny
>button you can press above the floppy drive port and the graphic
>denotes that with a power symbol (circle with an I in it).  Push the
>button and hold it for a few seconds (some say up to 40 seconds).
>Then, plug in your battery and a/c adapter and try turning the machine
>on.  If it's nothing major, you should be all set after doing that!
