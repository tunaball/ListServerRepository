My theory is that they are 2 different circuits charged in parallel. 
Furthermore, I am guessing that the recharging of the cells stops 
when either of the cells reaches a certain limit.  If you examine 
used PRAM cells, its seems like one is drained, and the other isnt 
much drained.  I rigged up a little do-dad that allows the duo to 
charge only one of the cells (most depleted) to get old PRAMS back up 
to snuff.
