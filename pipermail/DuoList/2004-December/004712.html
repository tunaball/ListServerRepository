Ahh But Morris faster is not always better.  4200 or 5400 rpm is slower 
but creates less heat than say a 7200 rpm drive, the faster it goes the 
more heat is created.   Older Books (and some newerbooks also) don't 
deal with heat all that well. Stick a 7200 rpm HD and a Processor 
upgrade (G3 etc..) and your looking at GLOD or worse... toasting 
something else. As for size 20, 30, 40 will work.  IBM Travelstar are 
pretty good!
