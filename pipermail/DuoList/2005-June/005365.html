Yup...there are three Apple Duo batteries.   The type I is really only 
useable in the 210/230 due to limited capacity....it uses AA cells inside and is 
easily rebuildable.  The Type II and Type III use the 4/5th's "A" cells and are a 
bit tougher to rebuild.  All will work in any Duo unit, at least thru my 
collection (230s, 270, 280 & 2300s).
