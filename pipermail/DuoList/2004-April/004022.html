You probably blew the SCSI term power fuse swapping out SCSI adapters 
while powered up (a big no-no). It's a flea-sized SMD 2 amp fuse on the 
back side in the center of the mobo. If you're not handy soldering and 
taking apart PowerBooks, time to send it out for repair... or sell it 
for parts.
