Hello All:

I am new to this list.  I found some posts via google researching the 2400c.  Someone wanted to know how easy it is to put a CF Card with an addonics adapter into a 2400c... well, I have never done that, but I just recently put the CF card/addonics adapter into a PowerBook 5300ce.  That was harder than the 1400c transplant, which LowEndMac covered, and which I also did.

I wrote an article about it on my blog, This Old Mac.  Check it out and use it and LowEndMac as a guide.  Should you crack open the 2400c and try this, having some of the supplies I used for the 5300ce would be a good idea.  I am almost 100% sure that it will work without a problem in the 2400c, where you may or may not need to seat the addonics adapter with sticky tape.
