I've performed the addonics reader upgrade in two of my laptops:  a  
1400 and a Duo 270c.  They work great, but the upgrade takes a little  
getting used to because of the absence of noise, especially during  
boot.  Anecdotally, the batteries seem to last longer with the CF  
drives as well.
