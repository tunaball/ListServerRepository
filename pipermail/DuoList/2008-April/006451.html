Is this just for your home?   If that is the case something like the
Logitech Play Link might work, it would be 11Mbps/second (802.11b
speeds) but on a 2300 that shouldn't be a huge deal anyway.  I have
one that I use to get internet to my Xbox, one box at your router, the
other at your Docking station and you're good to go.  It also uses RF
signals to avoid interference.  You can find them pretty cheap these
days:
