Hello,

Based on the previous list discussions, there is no need to enable 
the card bus of PB2400c to use the PCMCIA card for wireless ethernet 
and modem,though some cards will require a dongle to connect an 
ethernet/modem card to a cell phone. But for some other card like 
USB, firewire,storage, etc, they will work only on "card bus enabled" 
PB2400.
