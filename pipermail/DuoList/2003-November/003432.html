You might try a regular DB-25 cable, like one used for serial ports on 
PC's. I had the same problem with a Iomega cable, it is in the landfill 
now, and use a regular cable. I use a regular cable on my Perfofma 6400 
for my Iomega zip drive model number Z100S. It works with all my Mac's 
from this one to my Classic and Performa 200 and Duo 280c and Powerbook 
160. The Iomega cable would not work correctly with my PII 300 BSD 
machine with a Adaptec 1540 SCSI card.
