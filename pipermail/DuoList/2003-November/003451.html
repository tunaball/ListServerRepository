I use CatFinder, which I like a lot.  It has a very nice finder-like 
interface.  I keep Catfinder catalogs of all my HDs and removeable disks on all my 
computers, so I know about what files I have even if I am on the road.

Findit (part of IomegaWare, at least earlier versions) works nicely, too.  I 
believe the automatic cataloging upon shutdown or disk eject will not work 
under OS9, but one can just catalog manually.
