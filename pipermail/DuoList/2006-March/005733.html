I have done the yellow wire cardbus mod an about 4 2400's.

Be very sure you are cutting the wires on top of the Motherboard next  
to the ram slot.

just snip them you do not need to cut them away from the board.

There are yellow wires on the bottom of the motherboard also.

DO NOT CUT THOSE.
