All true, and all good software suggestions. But I want to mention that 
no application will extract audio from a Sony PRD-650, because the 
hardware of that drive (which is a portable) doesn't support digital 
audio extraction. These days all CD-ROM drives support DAE it but that 
was not necessarily the case several years ago. It can't be done. Believe 
me. I have one. With another drive, iTunes would probably work fine.
