At 12:52 PM -0500 6/25/2003, Adrian Cussins wrote:
>.... anybody know of an adaptor or cable that would allow me to 
>connect USB devices to the ADB port on the back of my Microdock (for 
>my Duo 2300c).


No and you have two chances of finding such a thing, fat and slim. 
ADB has a data rate of 1,200 BITs/s.  USB is 12,000,000 or 10,000 
times faster.  ADB would likely be swamped by the housekeeping 
traffic for USB with no bandwidth left for data.  That is if someone 
were to try.

The other thing to consider with something like this is there is so 
little market for it that even if it were technically possible (yes) 
or practical (no) there just wouldn't be enough interest in it to 
bother.  Add to that the fact that a number of the drivers needed to 
work with USB probably wouldn't work either.
