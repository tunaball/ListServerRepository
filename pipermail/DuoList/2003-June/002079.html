OK, I think I've been on this list for almost 5 years (IIRC I 
subscribed about a month after it started), but I just realized this 
morning I haven't actually *read* it since early March. I haven't 
used my 2400c's in over a year now since I moved to my Ti. The 
infamous green 2400c is dismantled in my closet... awaiting some time 
from me to resuscitate from it's last demise, the spare part machine 
is in my desk drawer at work. I guess I'm not really a 2400 user 
anymore. I have too much mail to read as it is...

So I guess it is time to say goodbye.


I will say this list has always lived up to the tagline "The 
friendliest place on the Net!"... I was here when that quote was 
first uttered:

At 21:25 -0800 3/16/99, Timothy Sales wrote:
>Hey everybody,
>
>I just want to say that this is the friendliest place on the Net.

...and in so many ways it is still true.
