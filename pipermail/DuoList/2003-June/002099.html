Steven,

There are 3 ways,

1. Drag card icon from desktop to the trash, should
eject the card. ( you may get a message that says the
card is in use, you then have to turn it off. this
varies with card type.)

2. When the system is shut down press the card eject
button. these are 2 small circular buttons on the top
left next to the hinge, 1 button for each slot.

3. Manual eject using a bent paper clip in the small
hole next to the card slot.


Hope this help.
