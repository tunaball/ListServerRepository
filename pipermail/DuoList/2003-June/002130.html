> The card is a 'Linksys EtherFast 10/100 PC Card',
> Model No. PCMPC100.

There is no easy answer for this one. Linksys changed
chipset in 2000 or 2001 from PNIC 82c169 to ADMtek
AN985, the trick is to know which version card you
have, there maybe a verion number on the card itself
or a mfg date code.

Once you figure that out you will need to find a mfg
using the same chipset with a mac driver for their
card(I have not done enough research on this yet) and
try their driver.

A lot of work, you may just want to buy a different
card. You can probally get one for $20 or $30.
