I have successfully installed 9.2.2 on a twentieth anniversary 
macintosh that is tricked out with a G3 processor, ethernet, firewire 
and USB upgrades by using the info at 
http://www.OS9forever.com/os92x.html . Believe I accomplished this 
last year. Resulting system is stable. I'd poke around this website 
to locate any updates related to this sequence of hacks, as the 
author mentioned that they were in the process of streamlining the 
procedure at the time. Did it without partitioning, but I did do a 
complete backup, as well as backups of the intermediate system 
folders that were generated by the process.
