>While I like it, I have no intention of switching from my 280c (and I have

2300c's 48mb RAM will work in DUO 280c. But the maximum RAM your 280c 
can accept is 40mb so you have 8mb more from your 2300c. The maximum 
RAM for 2300c is 56mb, btw, do you have 2300c logic board you might 
want to sell?
