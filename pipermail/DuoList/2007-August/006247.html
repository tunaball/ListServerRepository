This sort of problem _could_ arise from the power supply acting up or  
the power from the wall falling too low or high.  Check the voltage  
at the plug for about 3 minutes to see the variation.  About 5 or 6  
PM is a good time to check,  when everyone is getting home and  
turning all their toys on.
