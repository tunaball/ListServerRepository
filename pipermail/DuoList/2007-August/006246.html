The PRAM battery in my 2400 has been mostly dead for some time now.   
The thing that I find I have to do is zap the PRAM with the "4- 
fingered salute" pretty much whenever the machine completely loses  
power (e.g. main battery goes completely flat and I forget to plug it  
in immediately.)  As long as you maintain some main power (battery or  
AC adapter), it doesn't really care about the PRAM battery's state.
