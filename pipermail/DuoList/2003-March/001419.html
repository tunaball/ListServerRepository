Currently there are two for sale on the Mac2400 site:

http://webobjects.uwaterloo.ca/mac2400/

Both sellers are asking $300, and these are the Newer G3-240mhz w/512
backside cache.  It's actually somewhat rare these days to find the CARD
ALONE.  As I've heard it said on the list here before, it's often an easier
feat to find an already-upgraded unit than to find an upgrade card alone.

If you're eager to do it, I'd recommend jumping on one of those two cards
that are listed on the 2400 site.  While they've been listed there for a few
months, in my experience stuff there can sell very fast.  I have personally
missed out on some stuff, and I sold a G3 card last summer via the site...
it went in 5 days.

If you have the patience, you can try to find it on eBay.  Someone on the
list got an Interware 400 for something ridiculously low like $140 a while
back.
