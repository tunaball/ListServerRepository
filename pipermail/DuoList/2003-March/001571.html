Steve wrote:

> Please let me know about the Mac users list. I designed a Mac
> frontend for L/N in 1991.

To subscribe to the mailing list, you can point your browser to:

<http://mailman.nipltd.com/mailman/listinfo/notesmac>

or drop a line to:

<mailto:notesmac-request at lyris.nipltd.com?subject=subscribe>

to see the archive, point your browser to:

<http://mailman.nipltd.com/pipermail/notesmac>


It's a relatively low traffic mailing list, so don't be surprised if 
it's
pretty quiet. When there's a new version or someone's having
problems the traffic picks up. There hasn't been much lately
but I'm pretty sure that will change with the switch to daylight
savings time because the Mac client doesn't handle the change
well - everyone's meetings will be off by an hour. I've already
created duplicate meetings for myself so that I'll know what
time they're really supposed to happen.
