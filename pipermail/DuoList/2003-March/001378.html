There was a floppy on ebay just the other day (any search for 2400c 
will turn one up). Unless you specifically need to load from a floppy 
getting a SCSI cable and CD-Rom will do you just fine. That's my 
situation and I haven't needed the floppy yet. You can still find 
batteries for the 2400, but you might consider a higher capacity 
external battery; if you have one it will work with other Powerbooks as 
well.
