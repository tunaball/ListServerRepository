	The point of the colour lid is that the colour Duos are thicker,
and need more room which that lid affords.  If you have a door on the
front, and a smooth top, it is a colour lid.  There is absolutely NO reason
for any functional dock not to grab any duo which reaches it.  Take the lid
off and use it to get a better look at what's wrong.
