I have several....all networked.  An old Apple LWIIg, a newer HP 4MV at work, 
and the newest, an HP 4050N.  Gave up on inkjets as the speed just ain't 
there.  With 32 to 64 megs of RAM in each laser, I can dump large volumes of 
files to the printers at a time.  I'm spoiled!!  Ethernet and lasers are the 
way to go unless you need color.  As for consumables, I'm replacing the cart. 
in the work unit about once a year with heavy use.  The home unit (HP 4050N) 
is just now needing a cart....1.5 years since install.  I print alot, and the 
inkjet mess/cost was getting to me.  Heck, the ole LWIIg is still great, just 
a tad slow and lower rez (300 dpi vs 600 dpi).  The Macs are easy to 
connect...the PCs more of a pain, but they now happily share printers.
