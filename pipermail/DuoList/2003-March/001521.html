Nah.  We just like taking our units apart and playing with them!  Other than 
a failed logic, a bad memory card and the occasional bad keyboard, my Duos 
have been excellent units.  Please understand that I've built/rebuilt/mod'ed 
around 25 units so far, most purchased secondhand.  Look around for a 
DOS/WinDoze unit that is still as popular as a Duo....you won't find one.  My 
Duo has been to 15+ countries, still runs great.  Batteries are an easy 
rebuild, drives are cheap and I've never seen the screen problems some speak 
about.  Get one....you may have to tinker with it, but it will not be a PITA.
