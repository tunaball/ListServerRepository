Dave,

	Depending on the size of the hard drive in your 2300, why don't you 
partition it with the 8.1 on one partition and 8.6 on another.  My 2300 
is set up this way so that I can use some software that is 8.6 
specific.  I usually boot into 8.1 and when it's needed I use the 8.6 
partition, I also have a partition just for another 8.1 and Machten. 
And another for data and a copy of norton utilities.  This also gives 
you a "recovery" disk or two right on board (unless the entire hard 
drive dies). BTW, all my partitions are just on a 10Ggig drive.

	 Another fun benefit of all these partitions is that you get to name 
the partitions and give them custom icons.  And then you get explain to 
window using friends why "Reginald" and his top hat and monocle are on 
your desktop with "Celeste" and "Helga" ;-).  Here's some good 
information on it:

http://sciencequest.org/support/computers/mac/topics/partitioning.html
