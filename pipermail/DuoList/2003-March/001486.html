Only machines starting around the iMac have the serial number in 
software (and not always then it seems).  I suspect it has to do with 
flashable firmware.  You wouldn't put the serial number in ROM, it 
would require custom ROMs for every machine.  But flash lets you 
write it custom for each machine.  It's possible there is something 
in ROM that prevents burning that part of flash to avoid overwriting 
the serial number, just a guess.  In all my poking and proding inside 
machines I've never encountered a duplicate copy of the serial 
number.  And it would be something I'd notice.
