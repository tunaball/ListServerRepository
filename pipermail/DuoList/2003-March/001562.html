The PB 2400 supports several resolutions through the
video out port. It is the built-in screen of the
powerbook that only supports 800x600. There are some
resolutions that allow video mirroring (very handy
sometimes), but the LCD must support the resolution(s)
that the 2400 requires for mirroring.

I am sure that you could get any external LCD screen
and have it be supported by the 2400.
