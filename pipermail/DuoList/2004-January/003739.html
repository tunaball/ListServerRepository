Justin Young wrote:

>If I get my 2400 (OS 8.1) up and running wirelessly, will I be able to
>print to the networked HP4050?
>
>-Network is a simple peer-to-peer network with Win2000 boxes
>-Network has a LinkSys 802.11g router
>-There is an OS 8 driver for the HP4050
>
>TIA!
>
Generally speaking, the wireless connections are just like wired ones, 
just minus the wiring. As long as the 2400 and the printer have IPs in 
the same range (I think Linksys boxes use the 192 set, so your printer 
might be 192.168.1.2 and your 2400 192.168.1.3) then it should work 
flawlessly.
