Wow, I learned something new: USB booting is indeed possible. I just had 
no idea.

However, this Apple knowledge base article seems to suggest that Yikes 
(PCI) machines can't do it; the feature was introduced with slot-loading 
iMacs and Sawtooth (AGP) G4's. (not that I'm trying to spoil the party, 
but I thought you would want to know before you bang your head against 
any walls).

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=58430
