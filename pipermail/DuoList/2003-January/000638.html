I have a feeling that the inverter board may have gone.  That provides the
necessary 'juice' to the backlight, and is more likely to fail than a
backlight itself (and much cheaper to replace!).  It does mean, on the 2400,
that the screen has to be pulled apart to replace the board. :-(
