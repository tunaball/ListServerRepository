i tried headphone to microphone many times with both home stereo to 
powerbook and powerbook to powerbook, if you go rca to 3.5mm you will 
need a stereo preamp

Julian wrote:
> A headphone jack is not line level. It is much higher and the impedance is
> very low (8 ohms). Since the input on the 2400c is a mic jack, it is high
> sensitivity and will likely be overloaded with an headphone output. Use the
> L/R RCA pin outs on your home stereo, if it has them. You will then need a
> RCA pin x 2 to 3.5mm adaptor cable.
