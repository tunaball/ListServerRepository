I have gone straight RCA line-level output from a stereo to the line 
level on the 2400 without a stereo preamp -- but if levels do seem low 
(which they are sometimes, depending on the source), then I guess a 
preamp could be helpful, or you could try the headphone output of course. 
I have occasionally gone from a headphone output and gotten good results.

>Julian wrote:
>> A headphone jack is not line level. It is much higher and the impedance is
>> very low (8 ohms). Since the input on the 2400c is a mic jack, it is high
>> sensitivity and will likely be overloaded with an headphone output. Use the
>> L/R RCA pin outs on your home stereo, if it has them. You will then need a
>> RCA pin x 2 to 3.5mm adaptor cable.
