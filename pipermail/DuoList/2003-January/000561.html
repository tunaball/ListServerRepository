> At start up is
> there any way for me to force it back to booting from my  8.1 partition? How
> about forcing it to start from the external HD?

Either hold down 'c' to boot from the CD,
or hold down (i'm serious here): Command-Option-Shift-Delete
That will force it to boot from an 'external device' and IIRC
it will keep trying until it boots successfully, rather than
probe once and boot from the default.
