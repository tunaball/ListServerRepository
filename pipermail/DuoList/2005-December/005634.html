I took the approach of getting rid of the resistors on the mainboard  
at locations c375 & c377 to enable cardbus for my 2400c. I used the  
"snip" method. Interestingly as I began to use a pair of guitar fret  
wire cutters the resistor being made of ceramic gave way before the  
solder. This wasn't a problem as it left a very clean gap where the  
resistor used to be - I did the same with the second resistor and  
then carefully cleaned the area of any residue - Once the new RAM  
arrives I'll put the patient back together and see what I've got or  
what I've trashed. I took the risk as I have a second "backup"  
mainboard if I inadvertently toasted this one.
