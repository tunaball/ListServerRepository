Actually, if you go to the hardware store and buy these little rubber 
“footie” things that you stick on the bottom of furniture to keep it 
from marring the floor. Get the thickest, biggest ones you can find and 
apply one to each bottom back corner or the PB. It you are looking for 
at least a 1/2 inch gap between the bottom of the PB and the surface it 
is resting on, to allow air to circulate under the PB. That has worked 
for me.
