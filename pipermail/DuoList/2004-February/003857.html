Paul, One of my 2400s won't run on a battery either. Sidney Ho 
suggested it was probably just the contacts inside the case. I haven't 
opened it up, but it may be worth checking out, since both batteries 
exhibit the same condition.

That said, I'd try a known good battery just to see what happens and/or 
try the batteries on another 2400.

There is a slight possibility that if he was using both batteries 
regularly, recharging them each night, etc., that they both ended their 
charging cycle life at the same time.
