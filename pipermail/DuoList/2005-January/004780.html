There are "Force-Quit" and "Force-Restart" key combinations if I 
remember correctly.
They are Cmd-Opt-Escape and Cmd-Ctrl-Power, respectively. After 
force-quit, you should restart your machine. Force-quit is technically 
less severe than force-restart and should always be tried first.
