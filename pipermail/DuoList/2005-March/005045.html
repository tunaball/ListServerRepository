Cody,

Strata VideoShop (http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macos/4286) and BTV (http://www.btv.org.uk/) are the only video editing programs I've tried on classic macs (Performa 6320/120, PowerMac 7300/180 & 8600/300) although not on a 2400c.  They seem very similiar, and BTV may be just a reincarnation of the VideoShop program. BTV web page has a trial download. Video editing in general seems to require a lot of time and tweaking, and my results were mixed.
