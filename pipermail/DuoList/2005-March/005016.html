For the video side you may be able to use a Apple DB15->VGA
adaptor, and then a conventional PC KVM switch.  If you're
using an inexpensive mechanical switch, it may even be
possible to make some ADB adaptor cables, although ADB is
not supposed to be hot-plug (which might be an issue).  Come
to think of it, I haven't got the video out port on my Newer
Technologies UltraDock 16sce working yet, even with a
genuine Apple monitor.  It's no great loss though, the built
-in active-matrix colour display is nice.
