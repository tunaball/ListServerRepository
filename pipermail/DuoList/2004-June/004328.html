This is a standard pin config, so any drive cable you find will work.

Pin 1 = 12  / yellow wire
pin 2 = gr / Black wire
pin 3 = gr / Black wire
pin 4 = 5 / Red wire

Also most drives have the pins numbered also, so you can double check.
