The GLOD condition is the result of a faulty design in the circuitry over
sensitive to power supply fluctuations (my assumption). 
It can be repaired by replacing 2 very tiny transistors  
which can be purchased at an electronics store for pennies.

The transistors sit on a board. 

The board is accessible just below the upper case. 

A very fine soldering iron and soldering dexterity needed.  

About an hour for the round trip in and out of the computer. 

The details of above have been written up many times and can be searched on
2400 Web sites.
