You can overclock the bus speed on the 2300 by changing the 33 MHz 
oscillator to 36 or higher.  I have done this a couple of time. 
Changing to 36 MHz in turn increases the CPU speed to 108 MHz.  NNot 
sure if you can notice this, but it is an increase.
