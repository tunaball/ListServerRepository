>Where do I go for technical info.  I want to remove the hard drive from a
>Duo Dock?
>-

	Take off lid.  Get a skinny long straight blaided screwdriver, and
unscrew the screws through the two holes under where the duo sits.  Once
they are full;y unscrewed, the top of the dock will slide forward about a
1/4" and then will come off.  Turn it over and there it is.
