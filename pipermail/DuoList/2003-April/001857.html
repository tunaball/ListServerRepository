Have a look at  http://docs.info.apple.com/article2.html?artnum=16168
for a list of all the adapters for powerbooks of all ages.
The M3037 is billed as just for 5300 and 190 series machines; the Duos use a
range of adapters:
  M7783 for 210, 230, 250, 270c and 280 (25 Watt)
  M2693 for the 280c (36 Watt)
  M4174 (36 Watt) for the Duo 2300c and all Duo computers.
