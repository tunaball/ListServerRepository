Both my jaz drives (1GB and 2GB) worked with both my Duos (230 and 2300).
Overall speed of the book will slow down a bit and you will not get the full
performance from the jaz. To reformat jaz disks you need the iomegaware
utility (available from their website). I see no objections to using jaz (or
- even better - syquests) with a duo.
Just my 2p
