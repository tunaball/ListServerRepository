The 2300c has 8MB on board.  By adding 48 MB, you reach the
maximum of 56MB.  

48 MB Duo memory modules are relatively rare.  Sometimes
you can find smaller modules on eBay, but I do not recall
seeing a 48 MB module recently.

They are no long in production, so there is no source for
new modules.  And most of those already in circulation are
installed.  So they become available only with a Duo or
in the case of someone parting out a 2300c.

In most cases, because they are in short supply, the
price tends to be relatively high.  Smaller modules
like 20 MB are much less expensive.

I hope this gives you a clearer picture.
