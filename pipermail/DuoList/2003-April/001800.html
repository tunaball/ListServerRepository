i would imagine that the processes involves attatching your 2400 to another
working computer with a SCSI cable. however, you will need the HDI-30 (or
perhaps HDI-29) SCSI adaptor that will tell your 2400 to go into SCSI disk
mode.... the adaptor that i have has a little switch on it to toggle between
regular (HDI-30 i think) mode and SCSI disk mode (HDI-29 i think)..
