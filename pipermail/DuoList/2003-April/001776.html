Given the problem I had with OS 9.x and my 96MB module, I'd go back 
to baselines.

extensions off...using either module.  Then swap modules, then turn 
on extensions, etc.  Work your way up from the bottom...tedious, but 
complete.

Wish you had the original 64MB to try this with.  A bad slot is 
certainly a possibility, but I don't remember it occurring in the 
history of the DuoList.  You could be the first. ;-)
