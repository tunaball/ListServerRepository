like the Mac II, IIci and IIsi.  They can drive 640x480 and some 
variants but not 800x600 or 832x624.   They are also Sync-On-Green 
which is usually the gotcha for using VGA output (unless the 
particular monitor can take Sync-On-Green which a few can.
