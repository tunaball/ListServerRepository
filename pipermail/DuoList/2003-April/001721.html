Yup, I realized that after I opened them both up.  It still worked out fine, 
though.  The RAM card in the 280 was a 36mb and the one in the 2300 was a 
14mb, so I just swapped them, and both seem to be working just fine, and now 
we have enough built-in memory on the 2300 to run Ferazel's Wand and a bunch 
of emulated Gameboy games (among other things) on our long drive to Florida 
tomorrow!  

Plus, I was finally able to get our digital camera to connect to it through 
the serial port, so now I can easily off-load the images to the 500mb 
partition I set up on the HD, eliminating the necessity for me to go buy a 
bigger CompactFlash card.  

I love these little Macs -- too bad I can't find any decent batteries for it 
without spending a fortune!  (I hollowed one of them out to use as a 
light-weight "filler" for the battery compartment, since all of the dead ones 
I have cause it to shut down when inserted.)

Thanks!
