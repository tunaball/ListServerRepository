>I have several batteries I am trying to recondition with a 
>Supercharger2.  With some of the batteries, if I push the recondition 
>button, it lights up for only a second. 
>Does anyone know if something bad will happen if . . .
LOL, there's only a few listees who include such phrases in their 
questions as this . . . MD being one them of course!

>I use something to keep the recondition button held down ?
Why bother? If there is no charge to 'burn off', there's no point in 
holding the button down. Would it hurt anything if you did hold the 
button down anyway? I don't know, but you try it and let us know how it 
works out.  :-)

Are you sure the battery is actually connecting to the charger's 
contacts? I've had several batts that weren't, but a bit of judicious 
trimming of the plastic around the batteries' contacts did the trick.

If charging the battery in the SC2 for days and days doesn't get it to 
the point of being able to 'recondition', I'm afraid what you have there 
is a case for cell replacement.

BTW, with what type battery(s) are you currently struggling? FYI, with 
_apparently-dead_ batteries, I've had no luck SC2-reviving anything 
except T3s . . .
