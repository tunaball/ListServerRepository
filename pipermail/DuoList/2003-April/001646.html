At 1:41 PM -0500 4/6/2003, Bill Planey wrote:
>If I have a stock (non-cardbus enabled) 2400c, can I use any of the GV
>modem/ethernet cards I may come across without a problem?  Do these cards
>only work in the 32-bit context?


They are almost certainly 16 bit.  All of the CardBus cards I've seen 
had functions which needed the speed, USB, FireWire, SCSI, 10/100Mbps 
Ethernet.  Modems have much lower throughput so have no need to be 
CardBus.
