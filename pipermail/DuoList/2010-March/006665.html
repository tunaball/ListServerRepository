Does the original hard drive still work? It's possible that the  
problem is not with the drives, but with the ATA circuitry on the  
motherboard. If that's the case, you'll pretty much need to get a new  
motherboard, or only use the Duo with an external drive or with a Duo  
Dock that has a hard drive.
