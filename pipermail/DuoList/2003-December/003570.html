My track record so far:

1) Dissemble to upgrade hard drive, after assembly, blow fuse, creating
GLOD.
2) Disemble again to make sure all connections are good, in process,
incorrectly cardbus mod the board.
3) Repair blown fuse and partially repair cardbus mod attempt.  It boots,
but no bottom PCMCIA, and acts funny with 8.5 and up.
