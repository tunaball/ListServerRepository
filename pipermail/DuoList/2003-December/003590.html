lot easier than having to dissect it and paint it. Which leads to question #2...

2.) Has anyone tried to use Krylon Fusion paint on their 2400c? This is a new type of paint,
designed specifically for all types of plastics. To view the colors, hit this link:
<http://www.ne-design.net/rain-barrel-paint.html>

involved in getting the paint to stay on. This seems to have solved it.

result be?
