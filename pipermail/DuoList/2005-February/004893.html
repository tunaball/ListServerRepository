Samual,

The 280 is usually a preHy cool running little Powerbook. Find where the heat is coming from: the area to tne right of tne trackpad (or trackball:-) is the harddrive.  

To really check things out you'll have to take things apart.  Just a little to the left of the center of the keyboard is the mighty 68LC40 processor.  Check that the processor is seated well against the heat sink--there should be some special grease on the sink.  The harddrive should have some copper colored heat shielding around it.  

good luck ,
