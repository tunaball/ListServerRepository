When I rebuild Duos, I like to take off the apple supplied gap pad 
(corrugated) and replace it with a full gap pad I get from 
digikey.com (various thicknesses are available).  I tested the 
operating temps and found the new gap pad seems to decrease the CPU 
temp a few degrees, although I wont claim my methodology would stand 
up to a peer review process. : )

If I was real gung ho I would find a thin but rigid copper plate to 
sandwich in between the frame and CPU, making sure to insulate and 
spots that might come in contact with the motherboard.  THe 2300 has 
a thin "bat-wing" plate, but it is very thin.
