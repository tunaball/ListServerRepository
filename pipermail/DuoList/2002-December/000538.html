The 64 may work but the 128 likely will not. There is a limitation 
built into the 2400c's MB. AdTec developed the special custom 96 MB 
chip that hacked around the limitation. They could not do a 128 or they 
would have made a bigger fortune in the 2400c's heyday. Others have 
reported also trying a similar hack for a 128 without success.

But if the 128s work, I'll buy several for my 2400c's and eat an 
additional one. :-)
