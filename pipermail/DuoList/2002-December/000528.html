You can search ebay for 'completed items' and you'll likely turn up a 
few. I saw one that sold for $223.00. My guess if it's a stock 180mhz 
without any accessories or pumped up ram would be around 200 to 300 
bucks. Based on a good screen, no cracks in the case, ac adapter and 
one battery. It would likely part out for that much. In many 
instances, it may part out for more as so many folks are just trying 
to hang on to them and need the parts.
