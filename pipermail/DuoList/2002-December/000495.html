Hi,

I had the same problem using  Drive Setup 1.7.3 to initialize a duo 230.

The problem went away when i used Apple's HD SC setup 7.3.5 to
initialize.

According to the drive setup patch guide located at
http://www.macfaq.de/macfaqdaten/minifaqs/dspg.html

"There is a problem with Drive Setup 1.6.2 and up when formatting/
updating hard disks on 68030 Macs (specifically: IIci, Duo230); after a
restart the drive does not mount. In all cases the drive could be
reinitilized with Drive Setup 1.6.1 or earlier w/o that problem.
Localization does not seem to matter."
