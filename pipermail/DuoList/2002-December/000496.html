Several Questions/Comments 2400c/400/80Meg/40GB/Cardbus

1. Via this forum I picked up a "IBM Travelstar 8E" external HD PC card
unit. I was able to transfer small files to it but large files locked up
the unit. I bought a NEO Jukebox 20GB MP3 player over a year ago. The
power plug broke because I think of bad design. So I had over 12GB of
MP3's on it that I had no back up for. I didn't have my 40GB HD 2400 at
the time to back up too. So I then took the 20GB HD out of the NEO
Jukebox and put it in the IBM Travelstar 8E unit and was then able to
back up all my MP3's and transfer large files with no problem. So the
IBM Travelstar 8E is a good unit to use as a external HD pc card unit.
Maybe reformatting the 8GB unit would have made that HD usuable I don't
know. I just saw them on eBay for around $60.
