You should replace the PRAM battery.

At any rate, unplug the ac power adapter and take out the battery.  Then, hold down the reset button for 40 seconds and then let go.

Put the battery back in and then plug in the ac adapter and then try and start it up.

After trying this, consult my specs page for further help (may be some info on there for you)

http://thisoldmac.ca/specs/PB2400C.html
