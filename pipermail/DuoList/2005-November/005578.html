Copying a system folder from the active startup drive can cause  
problems.

To ensure a trouble free drag and drop copy you should boot from a
startup partition (or CD, external drive etc)   other than the one
you want to use for your drag and drop copy.

I ran my Lombard from a 256MB CF card for some time without trouble
and with a nice boost in speed.

Now with the drop in price of the larger high speed CF cards I think
this is a great solution for increased performance with a 2400!
