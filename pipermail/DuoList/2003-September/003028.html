In a message dated 9/24/03 8:17:01 AM, rich at daniellefineart.com writes:

<< There is nothing on the screen.  It is bright but nothing shows up.  
Also I tried resetting the PRAM, but I never got a second start up 
sound.  I also tried starting from the Disk tools disk, but got nothing 
there either.  The floppy makes noises like it is trying to read from 
it, but nothing comes on the screen.>>

Hmmm...had one that did the same thing...video cable had started to slide out 
of the socket.  Reseated the vid cable (pull keyboard, upper center of the 
logic is the vid cable) and it worked again.  While you're in there, do check to 
see if the RAM card is seated.  Right hand side center is the RAM card.  
Press fit connector.  I use a piece of foam between the case and the card to make 
sure it doesn't come loose.

<<Where is the RAM card and how to I reseat it? >>

See above.  Hope this helps!
