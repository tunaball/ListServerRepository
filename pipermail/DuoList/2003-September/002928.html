In the System Folder, click on System the hit
Command I on the keyboard and see what it says.
Do the same on Finder. The ones on the hard drive
should be 7.5.5. If you're booted off a Norton
CD, it's likely to be Mac OS 7.6.1 on it for the
later 68k compatable versions.

Speaking of Norton Utilities, a local thrift shop has
a box, manual and two full floppy sets of version 2.0
which is both System 6 and 7 compatable.
