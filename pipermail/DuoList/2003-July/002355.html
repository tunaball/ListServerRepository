--- Jeffrey Freeman <freejdf at aol.com> wrote:
> Just replaced PRAM battery in 280C. Attemped to Zal
> Pram and now the Duo 
>   goes black and the Green Light on lid blinks..
> What can I do to fix???

The PRAM batteries in these are rechargeable.

Thing to do if the PRAM battery isn't charging is to
reset the Power Manager.

1. Remove the battery and disconnect the AC adaptor.

2. Hold the power button on the back for 30 seconds.

3. Connect AC adaptor and boot up.

4. With the Duo running, insert the battery.

5. Shut down and leave connected to the AC adaptor
long enough for the battery to fully charge.

The PRAM battery should charge up too.
