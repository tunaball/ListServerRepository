--- orbital <orbital at able.es> wrote:
> Duo 210:
> 
> I have bought a Powerbook Duo 210 with the DuoDock
> but the battery it´s died
> and the only way it works it's inside the dock and
> atached to my Performa so
> I have no idea if the display works.

With the Duo out of the Dock and the battery out,
hold the power button on the back edge for 30
seconds. Insert the Duo into the Dock and boot it up.
Then insert the battery and leave it for a few hours.
Shut down, remove the Duo from the Dock and see if it
will power up with the battery.

If that does the trick and the Duo works fine, then
it's time to buy an AC adaptor and a monitor, keyboard
and mouse to use with the dock. :)
