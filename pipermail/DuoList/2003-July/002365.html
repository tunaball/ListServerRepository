	GET AN AC ADAPTER!  There is absolutely NO reason to have ANY
powerbook without one.  The Duo will work with one from any Duo, the 2400,
and the G3's.  They are all on the same concentric pin within a cylinder
format, and the Duo will only use the power from it that it needs (even
though the later ones will put out more power).
