> Do the docks have the expanded VRAM? How about an
> FPU?
> You don't need the FPU in the dock with the 2300C.

> If the Dock has the expanded VRAM (the SIMM near the
> back) remove it and see what happens. Removing the
> FPU is trickier. Ideally you should use a PLCC chip

> it

> flatblade
> screwdrivers to remove it. Just don't let them gouge
> across the board when the chip pops out.
