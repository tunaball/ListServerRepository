It is PS/2.   That is the symbol used on PS/2 for the mouse port.

connects a USB / PS/2 mouse to a PS/2 port.  The mouse can talk to 
either USB or PS/2 and this adapter is included to make the 
electrical connection to a PS/2 port.  I have one of the adapters 
laying around somewhere.
