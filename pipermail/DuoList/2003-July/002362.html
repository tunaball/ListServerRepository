Rich...that cable is easily torn and, as the Duo has been out of production 
for awhile, not easy to find.  The "simple" mod is to get a dead 2XX Duo, gut 
it and use the top/bottom plastics and the trackball to replace the dead 
trackpad.  I've got a 2300 at home with the trackball...works fine, as the 2300 
logic board was designed as an upgrade to the 2XX series Duo, as well as a 
complete unit.  It will function with all displays and input devices (ie trackball & 
trackpad).  I used to have some Duo bits and pieces floating around...if I can 
find a trackball and upper housing (yup, have to get a 2XX series upper as 
the 2300 upper is made for trackpad only) I'll email you.

The good part is that lots of folks like trackballs better than trackpads.  
Heh..at least it is not that accursed "eraser" pointer foisted on PeeCee users!!
