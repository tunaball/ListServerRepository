On Thursday, Feb 13, 2003, at 06:18 US/Central, Bill Fox wrote:

> Sorry, I don't know about IrDA but to work on the disk there are 
> several options: you can connect the two by ethernet, put the 2400c 
> into SCSI disk mode and connect it to a Mac with a SCSI port, find an 
> external SCSI CD drive that works or take the 6 GB drive out and put 
> it in your Pismo. I do not recommend the latter since working inside 
> the 2400c is very difficult for the uninitiated.
>


	With a 6 gig hard drive you won't be able to use SCSI disk mode.  The 
2400 will corrupt data with any hard drive over 4 Gigs when used in 
SCSI disk mode.  Some have reported that it won't if the partitions are 
all under 4 Gigs but I think it's if the hard drive itself is over 4 
Gigs.
