Scott, 

from what I've read and heard (including from a very capable Apple repair
lady here in Fort Lauderdale) the PRAM battery can be a GLOD-contributor or
even catalyst, so replacing it may not be a bad plan, especially when it's
dead (as yours seems). Have never opened my 2400, so I can't say anything
about the degree of difficulty. Good luck!

-tobias.
