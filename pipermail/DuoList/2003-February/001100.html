It sounds like you are set up for two screens, with the duo screen as 
the "second" screen.

One option would be to plug a screen into the video out on the 
Ultradock. You should see the main screen. You can then call up the 
monitors control panel, and drag the menu bar to your LCD screen to 
make it the startup.

If that is not an option, it may be possible to recover by starting up 
with extensions off (shift during startup) and then opening up the 
monitors control panel.

Hope that this helps.

DTL
