Helen:

If you have a Batteries Plus outlet nearby, you may want to inquire about
the Duo batteries they offer in their catalog.

The catalog I have lists a 12-volt, 1100mAh battery (part number
H120AA-10PBD for Duos 210, 230 and 250)) and a 12-volt, 1600mAh
battery(part number H160AE-10PBD for Duos 270, 280 and 2300). No prices are
listed.

You can find the nearest store by calling 1-800-677-7278 (1-800-MR START).
Web site is www.batteriesplus.com

I imagine it would be prudent to ask about the batteries' date of
manufacture. If it's been sitting around for a while, it may have lost some
of its juice.

Batteries Plus also offers battery rebuilding. You might want to inquire
about rebuilding your current battery if it's economical to do so. A
rebuilt battery may make it possible to increase the cell capacities above
the 1100mAh and 1600mAh offered by the new battery offerings.

My local outlet has rebuilt two batteries for my Nikon digital camera with
excellent results.

Good luck.

Bob Donaldson
