>From what I understand, most of the benefit in 9.2.x is either for recent 
hardware or Classic, so I don't think there's much point. I can't think 
of any software offhand that requires it versus 9.1. There are no UI 
benefits at all, as far as I know. If you want the very latest of certain 
drivers (e.g. FireWire), you can extract the extensions with TomeViewer 
from the 9.2.1 or 9.2.2 update, and add them to 9.1 (or even 8.6 in some 
cases) as desired.
