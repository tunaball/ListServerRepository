Terry,
This from the manual, page 16:

TROUBLE ??
• The dock doesn't eject the PowerBook.
   The dock may be locked. Unlock it by turning the key to the vertical 
(unlocked) position, and                   press the eject button again.
There may be no power to the dock. To eject the PowerBook manually, 
insert the dock's key, a small screwdriver, or a similar object into 
the square hole on the side of the dock and press firmly.The dock is 
released and you can gently pull the PowerBook from the dock.


WARNING: Manually ejecting the PowerBook when it is running can damage 
the system. Check that your PowerBook is turned off before manually 
ejecting it.
