Click the Hardware link on the left, and the Disassembly link on the 
upgrade page. You can link to the guide I wrote directly at 
http://webobjects.uwaterloo.ca/mac2400/piecemeal.html, and there are 
plenty of other good resources (videos, pictures, etc) on the Disassembly 
page.

If you choose to use the guide I wrote, follow the instructions through 
step 33. Then, on the underside of the motherboard, you'll be able to 
plainly see the PRAM battery, and if you follow its wire you can see 
where it is plugged into on the top side of the motherboard.

Good luck,
Ivan.
