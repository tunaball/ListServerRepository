FWIW, I'd only spend the time and money on an Interware/Vimage G3.  
They're not only faster than the Newer, but they run cooler.  I was  
wondering what the G3/400 was going to go for.  It was around $160  
with just a few minutes left.  I actually thought it would hit $400.

I was still getting at least 90 minutes on a new battery with mine.  
It's probably more of an issue of what apps and energy management  
settings you are running.
