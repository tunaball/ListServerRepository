Holding the power button with the battery out and the AC (well,  
technically it's DC...) disconnected will "hard-reset" the PMU (power  
management unit).  Hold it in for at least a minute, then plug in the  
AC (leave the battery out.)  Hit the KEYBOARD power button and see  
what happens.
