Most sellers with Yahoo Japan don't ship internationally. But Rinka is a
third party. I've used them for a number of things that are more difficult
to get here. They charge a small fee to bid and win the items for you and
then they ship them to you once the seller has sent the item to their
offices in Japan.

Works great. I've purchased several hundreds of dollars worth of hard to get
or just too expensive things from Yahoo Japan that way.

Www.rinkya.com for more details.

P.S. - I have no affiliation with them. I just spend money that way. :-)
