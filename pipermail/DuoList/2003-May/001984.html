Congrats on the new Duo! A very cool little machine with a great color
screen.

Hopefully someone else can point you toward batteries (there's always
eBay...), but I wouldn't automatically assume that the use of external
modem means the internal modem is dead. It's probably more likely that
the previous owner just wanted a faster connection than the internal
modem. It's not fast, but probably OK for emergencies or checking email.

It's been a while since I surfed the web with a duo's internal modem,
but it's S-L-O-W when compared to today's technology...

I'd keep trying to troubleshoot the internal modem, if for no other
reason than a backup.

Enjoy your 280c!
