My 280Cs (on which I based my original answer) offer 640*480 and 640*400.
Several Apple portables also offer choices. But the 2400 Service Manual
only lists 800*600.

I think by the time of the 2400, buyers just wanted the biggest/most
colours.
<http://everymac.com/systems/apple/powerbook/stats/mac_powerbook2400c_180.html>
just says '10.4" Color active-matrix (16-bit, thousands of colors) SVGA
LCD'.

You can sometimes get a list of 'other' resolutions by holding down the
'Option' key when selecting from the control panel. ymmv.
