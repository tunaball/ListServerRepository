There are SCSI Compact Flash readers, but you'd need a SCSI dock for
your Duo.  Kodak had some readers on clearance a year or two ago- they
might be hard to find now.  I wish I had bought more than one.  
Another possibility-  media storage on compact flash is (usually?) in
a standard format- an older camera with a serial port and 68k software
*might* be able to read the cards and transfer the photos.
