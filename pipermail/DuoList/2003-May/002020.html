There are a lot of ways to go here; don't worry. There are many sources, 
including eBay, for old installer CD's, but you may be able to accomplish 
your goal for free.

Apple still offers OS 7.5.3 for free download 
<http://www.info.apple.com/support/oldersoftwarelist.html>. It's an 
unwieldy 19-part download but it can be done. IMO 7.5.3 is an improvement 
over 7.5.1 for every machine I've encountered so far. The OS 7.5.5 
updater is free too.

Important: How much installed (physical) RAM is in the 280? The OS 
versions get bigger as time goes by, so this is good to know, along with 
the capacity of the internal HD. Click once and Get Info on the HD icon 
to find out the latter.
