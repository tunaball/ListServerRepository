I had to 'roll my own'
the 'pc' side is a male 9pin 'D' connector
(not a PC itself) this cable is 'straight through'
it basically changes the 8pin minidin on a mac to the
same 9pin port you would see on a PC... so you can
connect the cable that came with the serial device to
it.. (no matter what it is.. 
modem/pda/digicam/nullmodemcable/whatever)
the numbers with dashes (like 4-8) are pins that are
connected together on the same side...

PC       MAC
2        5
3        3
5        4-8
7        1
8        2
6-4


but if you have a USB port and dont feel like
'rolling your own' you might consider one of
those usb to RS232 adapters...
