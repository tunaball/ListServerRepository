On Nov 8, 2004, at 4:56 PM, Morris Rieger wrote:

> I was interested in using an 802.11b wireless network at my
> university, but it states you need VPN software to access it.  Does
> MacOS 8.6+, or the software that comes with any Wireless card (i.e.
> Orinoco silver/gold, or any others) include VPN software?  If not, is
> it something I can find freely and easily?  Thanks!
>

NO. The VPN client depends entirely upon the VPN server and/or software 
used. The university should be able to supply you with client software, 
or tell you which VPN client is compatible. Mac VPN clients are few and 
far between, however. I use Netgear hardware VPN here and no client 
software is required. I also use a Farallon Skyline card in my 
PowerBook 2400C with great results.
