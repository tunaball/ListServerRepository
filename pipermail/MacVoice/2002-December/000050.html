Apple has released an update to Jaguar that addresses the USB audio 
stutter problem.

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=107263

 From the above page:

* Addresses an issue in which audio recorded from a USB device with a 
sample rate of 48k or 96k may include distortion.

* Improves results when recording from a USB microphone with a sample 
rate of 11.025k or 22.050k.
