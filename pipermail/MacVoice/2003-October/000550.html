> I know that the official release of Panther is still a couple of weeks away,
> but I wonder if anyone has had the opportunity to be an early bird and try
> either ViaVoice or iListen with OS 10.3 and can tell us whether we will
> (inadvertently) give up our speech recognition capacity if we upgrade the

We (MacSpeech, Inc.) cannot say whether or not iListen is compatible with
Panther until we test it with a shipping release. There is always a
possibility that Apple will have made changes between the developer releases
and the shipping release.

Should an update be necessary for Panther the update will be made freely
available to registered owners of 1.6.2

Note: current owners of versions 1.5 through 1.6.1 can upgrade to 1.6.2 for
free. Owners of 1.2.1 and earlier can upgrade to 1.6.2 for $39.95
