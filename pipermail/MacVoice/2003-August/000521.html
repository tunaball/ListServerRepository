On 8/25/03 4:26 PM, "Camner" <camner at iname.com> wrote:

> What is the difference in what happens when you press both "create phonetic"
> and "set phonetic" vs. just "set phonetic"?  It appears that you are
> suggesting do both when one is NOT changing the phonetic description and
> only "set phonetic" when you ARE changing the phonetic.
> 
> Correct?

Correct.

Pressing "create phonetic" creates a phonetic based on what you typed in the
"what you say" box. This is fine for *most* cases. However, when manually
editing a phonetic you want that box ignored--so you should not press that
button.

Pressing "set phonetic" tells iListen to use the manually or automatically
created phonetic and is always necessary.
