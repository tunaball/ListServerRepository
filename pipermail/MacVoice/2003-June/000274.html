David,

Based on the feedback I have received over the years from several 
people who use UK English when speaking but have attempted to use 
U.S. English versions of ViaVoice the recognition accuracy as much 
greater for someone who uses UK English when using the UK English 
version of ViaVoice.

In a couple of cases I have personally witnessed the difference in 
recognition accuracy is absolutely astonishing.

Beyond recognition accuracy and spelling there are other differences 
as well such as the software correctly handling the phrase "full 
stop", and other phrases if the UK English version is used. This 
makes a difference in the overall usability of the software.

I have always recommended that anyone who uses UK English use the UK 
English version of what ever speech recognition software they choose. 
(For some speech recognition software there is not a separate UK 
English version, but the user can select a UK English language model.)
