Hi All:

We have recently received a couple of reports of apostrophes being replaced
by colons in iListen 1.6.

The problem would be manifested with contractions such as "can't" being
output as "can:t".

It seems as though we were outputting text a little fast for Mac OS X in
some circumstances - that was overrunning something it should not have been.

We have posted an updater that will address this problem. This updater
should be applied to the US version of iListen 1.6 only.

-   iListen 1.6 to iListen 1.6.1 Updater

<ftp://macsp_int:In15rH@ftp3.macspeech.com/iListen1.6_1.6.1.Updater.bin>
size:   5.1MB


Regards,

Donald
