The Telex M560 is a directional desktop microphone. It has mediocre accuracy
and tends to easily pickup any background noise as a word or words.

If you are disabled, you are better off with a high quality handheld
microphone that is mounted in a stand or on our Balanced Microphone Arm. You
should be facing the microphone and fairly close to it (5 or 6" maximum) but
it is easy to have someone adjust the arm so it is near you. I recommend you
use our Foam Windscreen to mitigate stray noise pickup. See the excellent
Sennheiser e835s and the arm at:
