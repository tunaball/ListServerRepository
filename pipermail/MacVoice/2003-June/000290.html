I thought this would be worth sharing to the group.  I have been using
ViaVoice for about a year to dictate my office notes on patients.
Recently I was amazed to find an significant improvement in accuracy
when I placed the mike about 2 cm above my lips rather that in line with
them as demonstrated in the set up instructions.
