powered.  So I bought a powered USB hub and connected my microphone and 
sound pod directly to the port on the G4.  I had thought a USB hub was 
like an Ethernet hub, silly me.  I have noticed better accuracy but I 
don't know whether that is due to my getting better at talking to a 
speech recognition program or to bypassing the USB hub and connecting 
directly to the G4.  My other peripherals now work however.  Thanks for 
all the help.
