Hello,

I have a Powerbook 400Mhz G3. I have iListen 1.5.2 installed.

I can talk to my powerbook and use iListen.

I have been using an Andreas NC-71 microphone with a standard audio 
jack plugged into the input jack with the circle and the 2 triangles 
as an icon on the back of the powerbook.

I would however, like to shift my research and development to my dual 
G4. However, I have not been able to get any real sound input from an 
external microphone into my G4 cube or my Dual G4 quicksilver desktop.

Some more information about my powerbook:

On my Sound Preferences window in the "Input" tab I have the 
following choices for the "Choose a Device for Sound Input" function
	External microphone / Line In
	Sound Input
	Zoomed Video
	Internal microphone

First Question: What is Zoomed Video?

Now, iListen seems to work and I have read 2 training stories. The 
microphone level seems to go well into the green. Microphone level on 
the "Input" tab is about 75% towards Max.

Some more information about my G4:

Dual G4 1.42 GHz Quicksilver, 1.5 GB RAM.

iListen 1.5.2 installed.

On my Sound Preferences window in the "Input" tab I have the 
following choices for the "Choose a Device for Sound Input" function
	Line In

Now, Macspeech says that they do not support microphones from Labtec, 
Platronics and Andreas. Although I can get an Andreas mic to work on
