It really is pretty simple math: at $79 there would be no way we could  
afford to also include a headset. Also, since the cross grade ONLY

not to have a second microphone.

To be honest, we really never thought anyone would think twice about  
it since, if they already had iListen, they already had a mic.

But I have forwarded your message to the person in charge of our web
