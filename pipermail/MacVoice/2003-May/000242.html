It really depends on the hub itself. Some USB devices work better with a
powered hub.

We prefer standard microphones connected to an Andrea USB sound pod. This
gives you the ability to change microphones at will. You will do better with
an Andrea ANC microphone (rather than an NC-7100) as they are active noise
canceling. We also find that the VXI TalkPro works very well with an Andrea
USB sound pod.
