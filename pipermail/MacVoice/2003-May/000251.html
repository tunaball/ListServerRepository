You're welcome. Keep in mind that a) the update must be installed 
when ViaVoice is NOT running and b) the update does not change the 
version number you see in the splash screen.

One other thing *if* you have used ViaVoice extensively under Jaguar 
without the update (from what you say you have not done this but 
there may be others on the list who have) and still have accuracy 
problems a day or so after installing the update then its worth 
creating a new voice profile.  What has happened in this case is the 
audio problems have corrupted the profile.
