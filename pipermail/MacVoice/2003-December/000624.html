 I guess it is time for me step in.  I do not know enough about the
software, other than as an intermittent user.  For me, it is doing
everything just fine.

 I have been using ViaVoice off and on for two years on this same machine.
I have not noticed any change in ability through the various OS X upgrades
including today's 10.3.1.  It works consistently and if I'm reading text, it
provides a very accurate input.  It is only when I am thinking and dictating
at the same time that many errors end up in the text input.

I am able to dictate directly into other applications, but my preference is
to use SpeakPad.  Because it allows me to make corrections and have more
control over what I¹m doing.  However, the accuracy when dictating into
applications appears to be about the same and highly accurate.  I make very
few corrections.  Certainly it is much faster than when I am typing.

There is no delay (about 1 second) from the time I finish speaking until the
text appears on the screen.  Even when I am reading quite fast.
