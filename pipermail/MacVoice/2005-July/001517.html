I gave up on Via-Voice after the same problems in later versions of 
Panther.  Wasted a lot of time trying to make it work, scansoft help 
service said they could not advise any date for upgrade to work with 
later 10.3 (they seemed to have given-up on it).  Despair turned to 
relief on discovering iListen.  It does what it says on the box and 
support is good and on-going (relief turning to joy).  My via-voice 
supplied mic worked with iListen and I haven't looked back (except to 
trash Via-Voice). I note Via-Voice worked with Tiger, but I won't be 
bothering with it again.  Shame on Scansoft, I used to use Naturally 
Speaking on a PC for years and my confidence in them has now been 
destroyed.
