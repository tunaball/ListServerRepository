Kai-Man (and everyone else):

We have tested enough headsets to tell you that the TalkPro and  
TalkPro Express from VXi will still give you the very best accuracy,  
especially in noisy environments. But if you would like a stereo  
headset, the Plantronics Audio .85 will give you satisfactory  
performance. The difference in accuracy is very small in quiet  
environments, although you may notice it more in noisy environments.
