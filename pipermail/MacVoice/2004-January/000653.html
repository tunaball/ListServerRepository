The purpose of the 1.6.4 release was to further extend the ability of
iListen to control applications and the operating system under Panther. With
this release you can now write AppleScripts that access hierarchical menus.
We have also added several new global commands.

ViaVoice is a dead product. In April of 2003 IBM entered an agreement with
ScanSoft (the makers of NaturallySpeaking) whereby ScanSoft became
responsible for all sales, support, and distribution of the ViaVoice product
line. While IBM did retain development rights, given the fact that ScanSoft
manufactures a competing Windows product it is highly unlikely that IBM will
ever do further development on ViaVoice for either the Macintosh or for
Windows.
