Loren,

You may, or may not get everything working but I must tell you and every one
else on the list the following:

MacSpeech, Inc. *does not* support the use of third-party processor cards or
third-party USB cards with iListen. There are too many variables and
possible incompatibilities for us to support the use of such third-party
cards with iListen.
