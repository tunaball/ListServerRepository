Henry,

No MacSpeech Dictate won't run on a PowerBook. This isn't because  
MacSpeech doesn't want to support the PowerPC, it's because the  
Dragon Engine is specific to the x86 processor. So it's Apple's shift  
to Intel that's making it possible for us to get the Dragon engine on  
a Mac.

Since all the Founders have iListen, we qualify to get it at the  
upgrade price anyway. My guess is that it's worth waiting for the  
founders offer than cross-grading now.
