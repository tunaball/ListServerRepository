It depends on how much you value your time, Chuck. Installation of a  
predefined vocabulary (even if you only use 10%), probably takes 15  
minutes, tops. Adding those 2000 words via Learn My Style will take  
around 130 hours once you add it all up between importing your files  
[assuming you have plenty of valid ones] and then making sure iListen  
recognises them accurately (which is by no means the case when you're  
dealing with those long medical terms!).

There is a market here for very specialised vocabularies and I have  
never understood your reluctance to produce them, as you do with  
ScriptPaks.
