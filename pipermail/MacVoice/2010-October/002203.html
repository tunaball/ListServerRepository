Great tip!

As an FYI, APE does some nasty things that are VERY discouraged by Apple in terms of how it deals with calls meant for the OS. If it is working well for you, that's great. But if you are having any problems on your system at all, this would be the first thing I would recommend removing. (And, in case you didn't get the hint: I would really recommend removing it entirely to prevent future problems.)

On Oct 2, 2010, at 6:58 AM, Brian A. Weeks wrote:

> Listmates:
> 	Unsanity's "Application Enhancer" v.2.6.1 crashes, and crashes hard, Dragon Dictate 2.0 (9361). Frequent and random hangs, and it won't quit at all unless you force quit.
