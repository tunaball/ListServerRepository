One thing I've found that helps make correction easier in iListen is  
to extend the correction command set to include commands to move or  
extend the selection by more than one word at a time. Now when I'm  
correcting by voice, instead of having to say "Press Extend Left"  
three times I can just say "Extend Left Three".
