The short answer is certainly, I dunno. F8 has been a lifesaver for 
me, and I got it from some kindly soul on this list.

However, here are a couple of thoughts:

1. What OS X are you using. I switched bback from 10.4.8 to 10.4.6, 
mainly because of other problems involving Classic apps. But I seem 
to remember more problems with ViaVoice with each OS upgrade. I'm 
guessing that ViaVoice will finally die with OS X 10.5.

2. In at least one situation, it seems to help to take a little bit 
more time than would otherwise be necessary. That's when I do Manage 
My Vocabulary, training a new word that I've just added. When I push 
the radio button at the right, to record a new pronunciation, it 
helps if I wait 1/4 - 1/2 sec before I dictate the pronunciation. If 
I don't, I'm much more likely to get the "This Microphone Is Been 
Used Exclusively By Another Application" message.
