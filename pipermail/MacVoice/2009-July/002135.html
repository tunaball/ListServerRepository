  My impression is that the biggest hurdle is wether the USB hub is
self-powered or not.  even then some devices still want to be plugged into
the computer.  a powered hub would let you offload things like your
keyboard, mouse, printer and scanner.  Some even advertise that they give
the same speed to all of the ports.
