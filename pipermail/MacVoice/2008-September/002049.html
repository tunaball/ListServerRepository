Hey guys,

Squabbling and posturing does nothing to move things forward in  
regards to speech recognition on the Mac.

Arnie - it seems that there are a lot of people who are getting  
tremendous benefit from MacSpeech Dictate. There have also been a few  
who find the features it currently lacks too inhibiting and have  
decided to stick with Dragon NaturallySpeaking on the PC. Everyone is  
entitled to their opinions and preferences. If there are some specific  
questions you have, I would be happy to answer them to the best of my  
ability, as long as you understand I am not a spokesman for MacSpeech  
or this list - just someone who wants to help. But complaining about  
MacSpeech and its software does nothing to help all the other  
subscribers to this list who are interested in moving forward with  
speech recognition on the Mac.

If you do have questions or issues you think I might be able to help  
with, please feel free to email me off list and I will do my best to  
assist.

Chuck Rogers
