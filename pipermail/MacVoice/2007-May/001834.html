Gavin (and everyone else):

I would if I could - all I can tell you is that I contacted some  
people who use DNS and that's what they told me. They weren't  
specific about the actual commands. What I was told was that Dragon,  
like iListen, has a "Command Only" mode, and they also have a "trick"  
for forcing the program to do dictation when it incorrectly here's a  
command (although they didn't tell me what it was). So obviously, it  
must be a big enough issue for the company to provide a solution for it.

This is one of those things where them being in business more years  
has allowed them to provide more bells and whistles. And, of course,  
the fact they haven't had to rewrite much of their code for Windows  
compatible hardware or software in the past 14 years has given them a  
lot more time than we have had to make improvements in the core  
functionality of their program.
