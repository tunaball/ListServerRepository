MacSpeech Announces iListen Road Show

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Salem, NH, July 1, 2004 - Macintosh Speech Recognition authority 
MacSpeech will be demonstrating its popular iListen program in Apple's 
flagship retail stores during a July road show that will take it to New 
York, San Francisco, Los Angeles, and Chicago.

Demonstrations are scheduled for July 6th at 6:30 PM in the New York 
Apple Store in SOHO, 6PM July 19th in the San Francisco store, 2PM July 
21st in the Los Angeles store at The Grove, and 6PM July 26th at the 
Chicago store on Michigan Avenue. Anyone interested in speech 
recognition on the Macintosh is invited to attend. MacSpeech will be 
demonstrating its exclusive TalkAnywhere technology with programs such 
as FileMaker Pro, Mariner Write, Microsoft Word, The Print Shop, and 
many others. A schedule with links to pages that have directions to the 
Apple stores where the demonstrations are being held is available on 
the MacSpeech web site (www.macspeech.com).

MacSpeech is a Mac-only company dedicated to producing the finest 
Speech products for the Macintosh since 1998. More information about 
MacSpeech can be found at www.macspeech.com.
