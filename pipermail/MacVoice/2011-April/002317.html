On Apr 20, 2011, at 11:48 PM, Fabian Fang wrote:

> I do not know about "most folks" and can only write about my own
> experience.

Exactly.

However, the topic of OmniPage Pro for the Macintosh has come up on  
several Mac discussion lists that I am on.  Let me assure you that  
folks have had a horrible time getting OmniPage Pro installed on  
recent versions of Mac OS X.  Unless you know of something that has  
changed quite recently, that problem continues to this day.

The only folks that I know who have been able to get OmniPage Pro to  
run on recent versions of Mac OS X are those who installed it using  
much earlier versions of OS X and who still had it installed after  
upgrading to more recent versions of OS X.  (I'm one of those users.)

I haven't heard of any users who have been able to get OmniPage Pro  
to work under OS X 10.6, and Nuance even admits in its tech notes  
that the product doesn't work under OS X 10.6, but that isn't  
mentioned on their sales page.
