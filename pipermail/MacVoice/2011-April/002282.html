All:

As an FYI, anyone who is having trouble with Dragon Dictate (it definitely does not "suck"), can get training from The Emergent Institute. In the interest of full disclosure, I run the training program for them and we have excellent instructors. http://www.theemergentinstitute.org.

Dragon Dictate uses the exact same speech engine as Dragon NaturallySpeaking. Other than that, it was developed 100% my Mac engineers for the Mac platform. But there is only so much you can do in terms of user interface for a program whose main function is to stay out of your way and translate what it thinks you said to text on the screen.
