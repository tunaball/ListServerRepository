MacSpeech Announces iLife Voice Solution

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Salem, NH, April 23th, 2003 - MacSpeech, Inc., the Speech Recognition
authority for the Mac, today announced the release of it's iLife Voice
Solution.

The iLife Voice Solution allows iListen users to use their voice to control
common functions and Menus within the four iLife applications: iTunes,
iPhoto, iDVD and iMovie.

³We continuously look for ways to enhance the iListen experience,² said
MacSpeech¹s Donald MacCormick. ³Adding Digital Voice Recognition and Voice
Command & Control to iLife, adds a new and helpful mechanism to the Digital
Hub. With almost 200 new voice commands in MacSpeech¹s iLife Voice Solution
gives you that helpful third hand.²

The iLife ScriptPak bundle is available from MacSpeech for $25.00. Each
ScriptPak for the iLife applications can be purchased separately for $10.00
each. 

MacSpeech is a Mac-only company dedicated to producing the finest Speech
products for the Macintosh since 1998. More information about MacSpeech can
be found at the company's web site at http://www.macspeech.com.
