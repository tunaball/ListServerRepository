I suppose that one *could* say that it "works", depending on one's 
definition of the word. On my MDD 1.25 GHz G4 running 10.2.3 with 1.5 
GB of RAM, there is a noticeable increase in speed. (I've never 
bothered to listen for the glitches in the recorded sound.) My 
principal gripe, namely that in both 10.2.2 and 10.2.3, the "Add word 
or phrase..." in the connection window is still non-functional, has in 
no way been addressed. Without this feature, ViaVoice is in my opinion 
almost completely useless--but when it *did* work, ViaVoice would have 
been on my list of most useful applications. As it is now, it isn't 
even worth the trouble of putting on my headset. And unless iListen 
improves a hell of a lot, it is basically of no use also.
