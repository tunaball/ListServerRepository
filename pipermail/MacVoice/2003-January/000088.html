John,

I did not quite understand why having this one particular feature not 
work puts ViaVoice in the category of being almost useless.  There 
are other ways new words can be added to the vocabulary.  They can be 
added at the time documents are analyzed, added with the document is 
analyzed on save, or added using the vocabulary manager.  If I need 
to add a new word or phrase on the spot I use the vocabulary manager 
(easily accessed by clicking on the middle button in the second row 
buttons that appears when you open the Voice Center drawer).  Yes, 
being able to do this directly from the correction window would be 
more convenient; but the lack of this capability hardly places the 
software in the category of being unusable in my opinion.
