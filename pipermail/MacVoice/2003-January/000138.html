>is an 800MHz LCD imac fast enough for OSX and viavoice?  any 
>complaints/issues, etc.?

If you need to have several programs open besides ViaVoice you'll 
want something faster.  Having one or two programs besides ViaVoice 
running does not cause problems.  Both OS X and ViaVoice are memory 
hungry so be sure to get 768 MB to 1 GB of memory.

I do not have any complaints.  For *me* I would consider an 800 MHz 
G4 the minimum processor.
