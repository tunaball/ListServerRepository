The new version of iListen has several minor improvements. For me, the 
biggest improvement it is additional control of spacing and 
capitalization. But then, I'm using iListen to dictate program code. 
:-)

Still, I think most people will find that saying "Caps Next" to 
capitalize the next word spoken will be easier saying "Capitalize That" 
to capitalize the last word spoken. Also, there are many other minor 
improvements, including fewer crashes.
