MACSPEECH ANNOUNCES NEW OCTOBER ROAD SHOW DATES

  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

  Salem, NH, 2004-10-15:  Macintosh speech recognition authority 
MacSpeech announced today it will be doing presentations on speech 
recognition featuring iListen at two Minneapolis area Apple stores the 
week of October 18th.  MacSpeech will be presenting at the Mall of 
America Apple store in Bloomington, MN at 7:00PM on Thursday, October 
21st, and again at the Southdale Center Apple store at 2:00 PM on 
Saturday, October 23rd. Anyone interested in using Speech Recognition 
on their Mac is invited to attend.  "We are thrilled to be presenting 
iListen in the Apple stores across the country," said MacSpeech Chief 
Evangelist Chuck Rogers. "The response has been overwhelmingly 
positive, and we are excited to see how much interest there is in 
speech recognition at these stores."
