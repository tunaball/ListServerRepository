You will not go wrong using a VXI TalkPro and an Andrea USB sound pod. You are
correct in that Andrea ANC microphones do not work well because the nature of
the dual active noise cancellation microphone elements makes for a lower output
gain then iListen requires. A TalkMic may work but is not as noise canceling as
a VXI TalkPro.
