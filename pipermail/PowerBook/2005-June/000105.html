I got my pb about a month or 2 ago, it's 15" 1.67Ghz, 1GB RAM,
128MB/dual-head video, etc.

I did calibrate my battery (found that  in the manual while looking
for the answer to my 'how did I manage to get my keyboard to light up
like that?' question haha) and I turn off my airport and bluetooth
unless I need them, which isn't so often. I get about 3.5 hours of
battery life if I just listen to bassdrive.com [boh! ;)] and surf the
net, use neo office, etc.

The battery life is shortened by about an hour if I decide to mess
around with blender, or when I tell fink to try compiling some linux
app that I miss.

I was really glad when I realized that I could turn my airport and
bluetooth off, because 2 hours of battery just didn't seem right at
all...  I don't foresee myself going somewhere that I couldn't charge
my battery any time soon, so I haven't considered getting an extra
battery.
