I was finally able to switch my TiBook 500 over to G speed wireless using an
AT&T PC Card (PCMCIA) and the Orangeware driver. This was the third card
tried with a D-Link and Linksys not working. Interesting that the AT&T card
was purchased at the local Shopko. Thanks to all that responded to my
earlier post indicating that this was possible. And for those that wonder
the purpose of the G card is for computer to computer transfers.
