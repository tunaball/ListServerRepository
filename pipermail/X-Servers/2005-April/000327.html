Thus spake Bob Bergey <bob at bergey.net>, circa 3/17/2005 11:57 AM:
> Also -- is there a way to move old messages from either email
> or a Filemaker database into the new Mailman archives?

There is a built-in tool that converts MBOX files into Mailman archives, but
there is no GUI. CD to /usr/share/mailman/bin and type

./arch

For the instructions.
