Ah,  I was calling them XServe and XRaid

It seems that Apple calls them 'XServe' and 'XServe Raid'

These are two different boxes.

The XServe takes max of 4 disks, raid 0,1 or unraided. and is a server.

The XServe Raid, is a raid array - 'no server'
hold up to 14 disks, does raid 0,1,5,10,50.

Optimally, you would connect to two together.
