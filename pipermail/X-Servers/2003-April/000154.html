Hello guys,

For software, I recommend using Zterm for Mac OS X:

http://homepage.mac.com/dalverson/zterm/

This works flawlessly with the Keyspan USB to 9 Pin Serial Adapter here:

http://www.keyspan.com/products/usb/pdaadapter/

Get the latest drivers here:

http://www.keyspan.com/support/macosx/usa/



It's simple.  You just hook up the adapter, run Zterm and select the Adapter
from the list and your set.  I use it all the time to talk to Cisco, HP,
3Com, Nortel and other stuff.  Zterm works just like HyperTerm does in
Winders.  You setup your Terminal type (9600-8-1-N) and your set to go.  It
also works for dialing into stuff too.  I can't say enough good stuff about
Zterm.
