Bummer.  Well, you could always build postfix and use that. :)  The nullclient
config for that is dead simple as well:
 
  config_directory = /etc/postfix
  daemon_directory = /usr/lib/postfix
  myorigin = $myhostname
  mydestination =
  relayhost = [mail.pointinspace.com]
 
:-)  You might not even need the top two lines (and they'd probably be
different for your installation anyway).
