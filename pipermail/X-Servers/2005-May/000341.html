1GB RAM
80GB Hard drive (x2)
Combo drive
dual gigabit ethernet
no video card
Tiger Server (unlimited client)

PowerMac G5 (dual 2.0GHz)
1.5GB RAM
250GB Hard drive (x2)
16X SuperDrive
dual gigabit ethernet (Sonnett PCI card already on campus)
Radeon 9600 (128MB) video card
Tiger Server (unlimited client)
