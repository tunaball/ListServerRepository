Note:

>Currently my Mounts CN contains the following:
> 
>1> cn: Faculty:/Users; 

This means the share will be mounted at /Network/Servers/Faculty/Users/


> 1> userSharedFolderOther: /Network/Servers/faculty/Users/fbeck; 

...But this says the user's path will be below

/Network/Servers/faculty

...Note the capitalization.

FWIW I'm not a fan of poluting enterprise directory with mounts classes. It's easiest to keep them in a peer Open Directory (this way you can use wgm to manage mac specific data, if that's your thing).
