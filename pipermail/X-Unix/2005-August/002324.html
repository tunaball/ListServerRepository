Richard,

Most programs out there available with source come with that sequence  
for installation.  It appears that your version of gcc (the GNU C  
Compiler) is not configured to output executables.  Try going to  
gnu.org and getting a current copy of GCC and try the installation  
again.

Also, posting the output of config.log would help tremendously.
