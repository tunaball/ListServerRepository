There is certainly no way of doing this with a Unix filesystem. As 
inodes are an add-on to HFS+ it's highly unlikely Apple has added a 
reverse lookup.

'find' is the way to go. Recursive 'ls' would be a much more complex, 
much slower way of doing it, with no advantage.
