A quick refresher (having just reminded myself):

spamc (written in C) is the client for spamd (written in perl) a daemonized 
version of spamassassin (written in perl).

Calling /usr/bin/spamassassin for every mail you process can certainly bring 
servers to their knees, calling spamc/spamd is the lower overhead option.
