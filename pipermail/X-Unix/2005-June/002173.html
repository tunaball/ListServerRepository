1) Insert a clause in the license that prohibits you from running OS  
X on non-Apple hardware
2) Refuse to support OS X on non-Apple hardware

will prevent almost all serious buyers from considering the clone  
option. Corporate IT will not want to run afoul of licensing issues,  
and most professional users insist on support.
