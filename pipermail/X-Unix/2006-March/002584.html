To manually launch the scripts called by your launchd .plist, you should
simply invoke it manually, ie:

$ sudo /usr/local/bin/<yourscriptnamehere.sh>

To unload a launchd job, use:

$ sudo launchctl unload -w /Library/LaunchDaemons/yourlaunchditem.plist

 read further in: man launchctl
