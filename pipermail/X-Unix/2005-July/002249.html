Well, then EVERY installation or 'upgrade' you do, will be treated  
like a new fresh, clean install. Read this:
http://developer.apple.com/documentation/DeveloperTools/Conceptual/ 
SoftwareDistribution/Concepts/sd_install_upgrade.html

That means downloading iTunes 4.9 and installing it over 4.8 could  
WIPE your library, as the installer might clean-install iTunes.

This is Dangerous - alltough it's your risk...
