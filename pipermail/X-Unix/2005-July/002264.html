The problem is "solved"....

Short version is this: I had tried tweaking things on my Powerbook  
and Dell laptops at home, and the FreeBSD machine that I dial into  
(both Mac and PC laptops were dialing into the same machine).

Failing everything else, I tried to think about solving the problem  
from a different direction.

Before I left the office one day, I unplugged the 2nd Linksys router  
and plugged the FreeBSD machine directly into the LAN.

I have not had any issues since.  Dialup succeeds on the first  
attempt and I am always connected.

Now I have to figure out how to make this work so that I don't have  
to manually unplug things when I leave the office.

I have a Linksys switch (not router) that I think I will try next  
week and see if that works.


So remember, simplify as much as possible when looking for possible  
causes.
