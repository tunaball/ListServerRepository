> periodic.conf
> set to mail the results to 'charles'.
>
> I can handle everything else, but what pop3 servers are available

http://blog.kung-foo.tv/archives/000654.php is an article on setting  
up qpopper on 10.3 - free, but unsupported from Qualcomm.

A much better choice would be Postfix Enabler, $10, from Cutedge -  
http://www.cutedgesystems.com/software/PostfixEnabler/, which  
explicitly supports 10.4.x and has detailed setup documentation.
