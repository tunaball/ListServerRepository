Peter Schumacher wrote on Tuesday, August 3, 2004:
>  how can check who's logged into an Airport (standard, not extreme)?

No one is ever "logged in" to an Airport.  It's just a router.

You can, however, find out what IP nodes have been using your Airport and their MAC addresses.  Go to <http://www.apple.com/support/airport/index.html> and download the Airport Management Tools (right hand side under "Resources").  Then fire up Airport Client Monitor.
