Thanks for the help/suggestions.

I installed gimp-print and ESP Ghostscript 7.05 - things appear to be 
working OK - I can now print postscript (a2ps and enscript output) to 
my deskjet - there had been no problem printing from various apps like 
word, text-edit, etc.  Apparently gimp-print and cups conspired to put 
the correct print-filter in place - before this "it" didn't recognize 
the postscript files as printable - finally read the cups log 
(/var/log/cups/error_log).  Gimp-print was probably not necessary - 
other drivers may have worked just as well.  What was required was 
something more systematic than my blindly hacking about  :-)
