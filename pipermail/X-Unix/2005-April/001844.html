On Apr 21, 2005, at 1:32 PM, Russell McGaha wrote:


> I've been charged with setting up a small [10-15 user] e-mail  
> server at work; could anyone make any suggestions, or provide any  
> links they think would be good reading, for such an undertaking?
>

Panther, Postfix, and Postfix Enabler (http://www.cutedgesystems.com/ 
weblog/Tutorials/PostfixEnabler.html). It includes IMAP* and POP.

If you want an inexpensive and easy to use Linux solution to throw on  
an old PC take a look at ClarkConnect (http://www.clarkconnect.com).  
All of the administration is done via a web interface.


Phil

*There are IMAPs I like better than UW's, but it's adequate for most  
uses.
