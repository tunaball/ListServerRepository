I've never seen the point of these environments on Unix.

My vote has gone for a long time, and still goes, to fvwm2 as window 
manager. It's fast and still very configurable. Between that for 
windows management and my own shell scripting skills I have no need for 
further functionality.

And fink works pretty good on average. I had a conflict between some 
packages a while ago, but the guys on the mailing lists are very 
helpful in overcoming that sort of thing.
