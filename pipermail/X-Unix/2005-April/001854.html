>Try this:
>
>In OS X, just open your Terminal and type:
>
>   sudo rm
>
>  and then drag and drop the items (open trash, drag all the items) 
>to anywhere in the terminal window and hit <return>. It'll ask for 
>your password, type it in and hit <return> again.
>
>At first it will seem the items are still in Trash, but ckick once 
>in Trash and they're actually gone. (It takes an 'action' of any 
>kind before the Finder 'catches up' with the reality of the folder's 
>situation).
>
>If you ever do the OS 9 'trick', again, don't try deleting .trash, 
>just dump the Trash contents onto the desktop and, from OS 9 select 
>the items, drag to your Trash and delete.
