If you have nmap installed you can scan her IP for open ports to  
satisfy yourself that it is or isn't listening.

     nmap -p 22 <ip number>

nmap can be downloaded at     http://www.insecure.org/nmap/ 
nmap_download.html

You can also use Xnmap, a GUI mac application of the nmap command  
which can be found at http://homepage.mac.com/natritmeyer/
