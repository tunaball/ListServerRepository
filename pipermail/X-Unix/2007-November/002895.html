> From what I understand, netinfo has been replaced with Directory

You can also access some of it (short username, login shell, home  
folder) by going into System Prefs > Users. Unlock for access, then  
right-click on any user's name to pop up and "Advanced..." menu. It'll  
bring up a panel where you can make the changes.
