On Mon, Jan 05, 2004 at 10:26:35AM +0100, Kuestner, Bjoern wrote:

> And while we're at it ... although I found lots of documentation for getting
> Postfix to run:
> 
> Does anybody know how to implement a mail-processing script?
> 
> Mail comes in --> processing magic -->  Processed mail goes out.

What do you want to happen w/ the magic?  Do you want to sort the
messages into folders or do you want to scan for viruses and spam and
bounce messages that have either?  If you want to actually bounce the
message you can use a postfix content filter (which is described in
FILTER_README in the postfix distribution).
