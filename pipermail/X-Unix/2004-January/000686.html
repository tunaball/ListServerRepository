	Hi. You can always crawl back to the www.opendarwin.org site and get 
help there, there might be a few mailing lists you can join, apart from

irc.freenode.net, most of the opendarwin developers hang around there 
most of the day (one of them is discussing on development of the

issues you have, since *they* produce the iso images. On pros and cons 
about the OpenDarwin system, I cannot help you...
