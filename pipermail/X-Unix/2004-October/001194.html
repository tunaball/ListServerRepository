In a more specific manner, to get something that looks like the default 
red-hat prompt,

PS1="[\u@\h \W]\\$

or to get the prompt I personally love and use (full paths):

PS1='\[\33[1;37\][\!]\[\33[1;32\]\@\:\[\33[1;34\]\ $ \[\33[\]'
