On 06 Nov, 2004, at 23:35, Richard wrote:

> Is there a way, perhaps to, remove .ds_store globally ?
> and have a cron setup for every 3 hours, or 4 hours..?

find . -name \.DS_Store

will list them...

find . -name \.DS_Store -exec rm {} \;

will remove them

Put that in a cron script.

However, if you have any significant number of files on the system you 
will cause a much greater impact scanning the system every few hours 
than the files will cause.

What do you think you are accomplishing by erasing them every couple of 
hours?
