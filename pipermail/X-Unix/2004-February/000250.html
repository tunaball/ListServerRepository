> A) What does the line indicate?
> 
> B) Why is the DMG there? -- after reboot, i.e. when the DMG is NOT
> mounted.
> 
> C) Why don't all the other DMG files I have on various drives
> (including on that same drive) show up in Disk Utility?
> 
> D) Why doesn't it go away?

Think of the left side of the DU window as a type of sidebar, like in the
finder. When you create a DMG, it gets added to that sidebar, _in case_ you
want to do something more with it. Only those you create from DU show up
there, as well as any you manipulate directly with DU.

To remove it, just drag it off - poof!
