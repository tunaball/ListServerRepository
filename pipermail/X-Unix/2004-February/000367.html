> My AppleFileServer service crashes occasionally (on an eMac Jag system

> restarted without rebooting the eMac.
>

i haver a machine that keeps turning it's AppleFileServer off...

in the terminal;

sudo AppleFileServer


that's it...  it will ask you for your password.

or you can just go into SystemPreferences and just turn file sharing 
back on...
