You don't need sudo, but you do need the active interface.
I happen to use airport, and "en1" not "en0" is the active interface.
(type "ifconfig" to see what's there.)

It does "fail silently," and $? will yield a "1" ... the program DID 
complete successfully, it just had nothing to say about the interface.
