Following up again,  because I see I missed a chance to shill for one 
of my favorite projects, Fink,

<http://fink.sourceforge.net>

They have ported a lot of software from the *nix/open source community 
to run on MacOSX.  Precompiled binaries can be installed with Debian's 
apt-get and the fink command can be used to automate compilations of 
packages *and all dependencies*. 'Tis a most  cool effort.

Other programs compile fine out of the box on Mac OS X. (exuberant 
ctags was as simple as ./configure; make; make install).
