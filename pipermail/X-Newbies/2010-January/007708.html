The people at OpenDNS gave me this explanation which I feel is the  
correct one:

"Comcasts DHCP switch/router in your neighborhood/area had cached  
information that said all listened-traffic from a specific IP was to  
be directed to OpenDNS DNS servers unless specified by the user. This  
could happen because a user who had your IP before you had setup  
OpenDNS, and, when you inherited the IP, inherited the settings."

So long as my lock is set on the TCP/IP panel, it should not happen  
again.
