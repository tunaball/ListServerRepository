On Sunday, May 4, 2003, at 15:15 US/Central, David Crandon wrote:
> I seem to remember hearing that Mail has difficulty handling large
> folders of email. Is this in terms of the number of messages, or the
> folders size? My biggest folder is about 3500 messages/70 megabytes.

I have both large size (170MB) and large number (5000+) and don't have 
problems with either.  The one with 5000+ takes closer to a full second 
than other mailboxes with fewer mails; however, it's never long enough 
that I feel like I'm waiting.

> Is is possible to pickup email from different email accounts (from the
> same ISP) without quitting and restarting Mail for each one? I have to
> do this with Netscape Communicator.

I don't see why not.  You would just have to set up accounts for each 
in Mail > Preferences > Accounts.  Under the Advanced menu for each, 
there are options to "enable this account" and "include when 
automatically checking for mail" that you may or may not want enabled.
