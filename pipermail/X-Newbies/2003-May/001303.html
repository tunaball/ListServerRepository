My hot tip of the day . . .

In the Finder, CMD-arrow up brings you to your home directory.

Maybe everyone else already knew this. I didn't, and it's a heck of a 
lot easier to remember than CMD-SHFT-H or whatever.

This could go some way toward resolving the conceptualizing 
difficulties some folks are having with the multi-user environment.
