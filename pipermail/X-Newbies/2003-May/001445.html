> i am having a problem with mail
> i can't delete and address from my address book

To delete an address from the Address Book program, you must open it 
up. You can't do it from Mail.

> or get that address out of a distribution list

Mail automatically keeps track of the last few hundred names you've 
sent mail to, even if they are not in your Address Book file. I don't 
know of any way to customise or purge that behaviour, which is 
"borrowed" from Outlook/Entourage behaviour.

Check with MacOSXHints.com for possible workarounds/cures.

> and then searching mail help
>
You're looking in the wrong place. You want Address Book help, not Mail 
help.
