Jaguar likes lots of RAM, 320 MB is probably sufficient unless you tend to
open a lot of apps at the same time. That said; you can add a lot more to
your laptop if it's a relatively new model. I have two 512 MB sticks in my
Pismo. It ran Jaguar great as a G3 then I did the Bluechip 500 MHZ G4
upgrade and it is just about as zippy as my 867 MHZ single G4 Desktop
machine.
