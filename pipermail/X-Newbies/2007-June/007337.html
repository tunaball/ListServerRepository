Making that apply to all finder windows would, I think, involve  
opening every folder on your drive (at least those you might actually  
open in the future). I think the only hope is some sort of 'defaults  
write...' command that might convince the Finder to permanently hide  
the sidebar. That, of course, would depend on Apple's programmers  
having left in a hook to make that possible.

As for the macosxhints script... which script, exactly, are you  
referring to? I didn't see anything on htat page regarding finder  
windows.There's a script to open many windows in Terminal, but that  
doesn't help much I think.
