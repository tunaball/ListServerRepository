You have one printer, or you're looking for one printer?

I had to source an OS9 USB printer last year for a client. We ended  
up going with an Epson R200. It has drivers for OS 9 and OS X. It's  
no longer available in stores, so I ended up finding a new in box  
unit on eBay for $110 including shipping. This was back in September.

You may find it listed on various online stores. You might want to  
call before ordering, as at least some of them are not well  
maintained. Don't bother ordering from Legend Micro, btw. I went down  
a two-day dead-end ordering from them (112 inc. shipping). The site  
said they had it in stock, but two days later I received email  
stating it was 'backordered'.

Despite following the instructions in the email, replying to cancel  
the order, I am still to this day receiving "your order is back- 
ordered" emails from them.
