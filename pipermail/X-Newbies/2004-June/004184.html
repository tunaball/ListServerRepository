If you delete all your accounts in Mail, it will look at .Mac 
preferences and pull in your .Mac account. If it's another account, you 
can either remove the account info in the Internet prefs pane (in 
Jaguar) or move aside the com.apple.internetconfig.plist from your 
Preferences folder (in Panther).
