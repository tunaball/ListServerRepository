Believe or not, it's a feature, not a bug... well, actually it's a 
turkey (design flaw). The order isn't random, but it's not anything 
useful to you. There's a partial work-around: switch to Column view 
before copying. The pasted list will be sorted by item name. It's 
partial, because this way you don't get a recursive listing.

> So it's not useful at all for me, sadly.

If you need this, use another solution -- such as ls.
