On Monday, September 22, 2003, at 11:12 AM, Paul Korntheuer wrote:

> Is there a spot for reviews on cable modems? Comcast will charge 3 
> bucks a month to "lease" one, but I figured I'd just fork out the 
> money and buy one myself.

Probably not cost effective. You don't know when they're going to 
upgrade their equipment - the Motorola modem I had for a couple of 
years just quit about 6 months ago when my cable company upgraded some 
of their hub infrastructure. They swapped it - no cost to me.

If you own the modem, when there's a problem and you call in for 
service, you're only going to hear "the problem lies with your modem".

With my cable company, the cost of the modem is bundled - you pay for 
it regardless. If I had the option, I still wouldn't buy my own.
