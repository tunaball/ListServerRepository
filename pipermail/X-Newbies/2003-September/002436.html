Once you have shared a file on your PC you should, from the finder
menu on the OS X machine, select go and then select connect to server.
or you can press <Command>+K

in the address bar at the bottom of the window that opens type in
smb://yourcomputernameorIPaddress/sharename then hit enter.  you will
be prompted for a username and password and workgroup name.  unless
you changed the default from workgroup you can just enter workgroup
along with a username and password combo that exists on the windows
computer.  if you entered everything correctly the share will mount on
your desktop and you can use it as though it was on your local
machine.
