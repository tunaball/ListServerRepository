I am using  an Asante FR1004 , 4 port Router to Network 2 Power Macs ((

a cable modem into the internet with Comcast. net as my ISP using a 
single account #.

Mac. They have other more powerful models that maybe more Mac-PC 
printer oriented

Also, go to Finder Help and type in Networking. This will give you 
links on how to set it up

Good luck
