After having no end of problems with an ATTBI RCA supplied modem that 
had to be reset about once a week, I decided to also get the Toshiba 
modem after reading a number of reviews. Since I got it about a year 
ago, I've had to perform only a few resets, and most of those weren't 
necessary since the problem was Comcast's frequent downtime for its DNS 
server. Rather than resetting as a first step (a somewhat knee-jerk 
reaction), I first check my router for a valid assigned address and do 
some other trouble-shooting. I've been very satisfied with the Toshiba.
