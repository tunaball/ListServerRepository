This mailing list is for Mac OS X help.  So it's not really the right
place to ask your question about Mac OS 8.x upgrades.

Having said this, you should be able to install Mac OS 8.5 on top of
your current Mac OS 8.1 without erasing your hard drive.  Does the
installer force you to erase your hard drive?

Of course, before any OS upgrade, you should back up your hard drive.
And if your Power Mac 6500 has any room, you can actually buy and
install an IDE hard drive.

Also, it might be worth the trip to visit your nearest Apple Store.

	http://www.apple.com/retail/

Or at least see if there is a Mac user group in your city or state.

	http://www.apple.com/usergroups/
