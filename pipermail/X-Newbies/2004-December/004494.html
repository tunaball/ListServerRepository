> I called my local Mac dealer to ask them how to add the AT commands to

I'm not sure what your problem is, but just some advice as to how to 
approach this kind of problem in general: If you talk to party A and 
they give you some advice which you're not sure about, when you go to 
party B for further advice, tell them the *whole* story. Newbies 
frequently get caught up in whirlwind of advice by taking each 
recommendation to a new expert.

In this case, unless you have an exceptional ISP, I would completely 
disregard any Mac advice coming from them.
