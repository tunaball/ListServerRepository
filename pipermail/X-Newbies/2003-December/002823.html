> How do I get this? It's  a .tiff embedded not as attachment and I 
> can't copy it for anything............

You might want to use the help system. Search for "picture".

"If someone using a .Mac mail account sets up his or her account to 
share pictures, when you view a message from that person in Mail you'll 
see the shared picture. The picture is not sent in the message itself; 
instead, when you view the message, Mail looks for the picture on the 
.Mac server. You can only view shared pictures from a .Mac account 
using Mail and webmail.mac.com."
