Well, not much really -- Mail's junk filter in particular seems lots 
better to me (particularly when supplemented with a few rules). The 
main difference between the two is that Entourage has "iCal-like" 
functions built into the program, and it's own address book which are

difference. I'd say you should just import your mail over from OE into 
Mail and work with it.
