>I would like to know how to import/export Eudora's address book from
>my account to my wife's account.  She uses Eudora and I would like to

>OS 10.3.1

That's actually pretty easy. Find your "Eudora Nicknames" (use find; 
it can be in various places depending on how you installed) and copy 
it someplace your wife can see (option drag it); the bottom level of 
your hard drive should be fine; then your wife drags it to replace 
her copy.
