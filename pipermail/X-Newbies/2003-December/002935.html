Charles,

Geneva is a proportional font. Monaco and Courier are two monospaced 
fonts that go back to the very earliest days of the Mac. Monaco was the 
bit-mapped font seen on the original Mac 128K machine and Courier was 
the Postscript font first seen on the Mac when the original Laserwriter 
was released, if I remember correctly.
