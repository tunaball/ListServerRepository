>Good evening,
>	Was hoping the airport express was going to be released in time for my
>trip, but I guess its not to be.  I am going to bring an old base station
>(80211.b) and plug it into the high speed internet that comes in the hotel
>room.  Was wondering the best way to set it up; will be staying with a
>friend and we both have powerbooks.  Does one powerbook need to be wired to
>the base station to register and then once its setup both are good to go or
>is there something that needs to be done first?


As long as your base station is set up for dhcp you should have no 
problems. it would be just simple as hooking up the ethernet cable 
and then plugging in your ABS. and 30-40 seconds after that you both 
should be surfing the web.


>  Is it even possible to use
>an airport base station in a hotel room where you need to pay for the
>internet by launching a web browser?

If the connection is ethernet it is on and connected (so you will be 
charged) the second the ABS is on.
