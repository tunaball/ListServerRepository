Here's a list of supported video cards.

Quartz Extreme Requirements
Quartz Extreme functionality is supported by the following video GPUs:
NVIDIA GeForce2 MX, GeForce3, GeForce4 MX, or GeForce4 Ti or
  any AGP-based ATI RADEON GPU. A minimum of 16MB VRAM is required.
