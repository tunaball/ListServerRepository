> CUPS, as the name says, is a printing system. To print, you need a 
> compatible printer driver. IIRC, the PLW 300 is a serial-only 
> QuickDraw printer. (Being shared on AT suggests you have ColorShare 
> installed on a Mac running 0S 9 or earlier.) Which really means that, 
> CUPS or no CUPS, you're SOL. Don't take my word for it (I haven't 
> researched this in depth), but I don't think you'll find a driver for 
> this printer anywhere. If you insists on using it (after all it's, 
> what, almost 10 years old?), I'd say the best bet is to do so from OS 
> 9. Save your OS X output as PDF and write an AppleScript to print the 
> PDF automatically from Acrobat on the OS 9 machine.
