On 12/3/02 9:27 PM, "Anne Keller-Smith" <earthpigz at earthlink.net> wrote:

> Oh, and my present G4 is always asking me to update software, but
> I don't know if I want to and actually the last time I tried, stuff didn't

Anne, that's your Software Update Preferences. You can fix that by opening
your System Preferences, going to Software Update and turning off
"Automatically Check for Updates".

Hope that helps.
