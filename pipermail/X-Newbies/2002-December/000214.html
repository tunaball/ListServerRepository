Actually, most USB mice are "plug and play" at least for their basic
functions, you usually only need the special drivers for their extra
features. If you have a USB port and a mouse, just try it. If you have no
USB port, you could try getting an ADB to USB adaptor and connect your USB
mouse that way. (This would require a driver for the adaptor.)
