Dear fellow list members,

Many of us complain about the massive numbers of emails we receive -- we
cannot possibly read them all and work also.  Just a reminder and
suggestion:

     Please try to make the Subject line suggest exactly what your message
is about.  This will draw the attention of those you want to reach and allow
people without interest or expertise in your question (comment) to skip on
to the next.

Thanks for helping make this list really useful for us all.
