> Here's my question... The ability to run classic applications under OS

> When I try to run them under X the classic environment begins starting 
> up
> and then I get error messages.  I have to boot into OS 9 to run them.

Either hang on to your old Mac until you're completely  tired of the 
games, or hope Apple keeps improving Classic. Even though it seems like 
every new version of OS X Classic handles more old apps, there's a 
limit to what Apple can do considering some of the "tricks" software 
developers use to eek out a little more performance. The sad part it 
those tricks they used to get acceptable performance on a pre-PPC or 
G1/2 Mac are probably unnecessary for a G3/4 system.
