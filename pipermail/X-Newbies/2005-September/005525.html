Dennis:

- Make sure you have the latest version of iDVD
- Restart your computer before burning the disk
- Use 2x media if you can find it
- Make sure you have at least 8GB of disk left
- You need to have at least 640MB of RAM for iDVD to burn properly.

These are all from experience, your mileage may vary... but I hope  
they help.
