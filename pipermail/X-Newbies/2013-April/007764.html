> System Preferences next to: When you insert a video DVD, it says - Ignore.

I don't know why your settings in System Preferences wouldn't  be sticking.  You may have a corrupted preferences file.

You can try trashing the file:
com.apple.systempreferences.plist
 
located at:
 
~/Library/Preferences
(Option-click the Go menu in the Finder.  Your ~/Library folder will appear in the menu.)

You may ultimately have to reinstall a fresh copy of OS X to fix this.
