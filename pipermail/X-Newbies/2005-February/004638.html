To which on 05/02/2005, at 7:01 AM, Vincent Cayenne replied:

> I've seen the Brother HL-2700CN recommended. I have no direct 
> experience with it though I do use a Brother HL-4000CN. For that 
> model, I had to manually install the PPDs in OS X but it's worked

I've installed an HL-2700CN at the office several months ago and am 
close to installing another one. I agree, it's a great printer, rock 
solid, fast performer too. Plays nicely with anything from Win95 
upwards (including Terminal Server sessions) and of course Mac OS X. In 
fact we have three Brothers of different models (HL-1440's being the 
other two) and I've always been pleasantly surprised at how good both 
the hardware and software is.

I'd also second Randy's caveat about it not doing photorealistic output 
as well as an inkjet, but for those 99% of prints that don't require 
such high accuracy it's just right. I'd also add that Brother warn that 
you cannot use photo (shiny) paper in it. Unless anyone has 
laser-friendly alternatives they can recommend?

HTH, and cheers,

Paul.
