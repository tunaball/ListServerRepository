Thanks for this info, it's very helpful. BTW, the term "Mac" is short  
for "Macintosh." It's not an acronym, thus it should not be  
capitalised. There's another computer term that uses MAC (all caps) so  
to avoid confusion, don't capitalise when you are talking about a Mac  
(of any kind) meaning the computer product from Apple.

Ditto for iPod. :)

I think Carole has hit the nail on the head, and another keyboard  
layout has been accidentally invoked. If you don't see a flag of some  
kind in your menubar, just go to "International" in your System  
Preferences, click on "Input Menu" and make sure that ONLY the  
language you want to use is checked and no other.
