On Thu, 6 Jan 2005 08:39:00 -0600, Sue Rodriguez <Sue.Rodriguez at usm.edu> wrote:

> I have been following this discussion and I'm convinced that one should
> "repair permissions" before installing, but I have no idea how to do it.
> Where can I get information on how to do it?  Is it a major undertaking?  If
> so, I might just stick where I am at.

Go to Applications -> Utilities -> Disk Utility, select a disk from
the left pane, and click "Verify Disk Permissions," then "Repair disk
Permissions" from the right pane.

It is qjick and easy, not a major undertaking.
