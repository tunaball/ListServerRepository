Some trainable/learning mail clients will junk additional mail when a 
pivotal or threshold mail item is designated as junk by the user. So 
I will sometimes select one piece of mail and (in Eudora) junk it and 
then see a few other similar items also go to my Junk folder. But 
it's been accurate in its "assessment" so it has been quite a 
positive feature for me. I've not had mass or random deletions ever 
occur though.
