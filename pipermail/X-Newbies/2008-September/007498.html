Hello Brian,
	Im sure you have probably tried this but if not my suggestion would  
be to do a restart and zap the p-ram. As soon as your machine  
restarts, hold down the option key and the command key and the "P" and  
"R" keys. Wait for the chime to get louder the second time  and let  
the keys go after the third chime! This usually clears most problems.  
Good Luck!
