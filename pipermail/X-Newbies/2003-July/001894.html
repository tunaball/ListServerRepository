On 7/23/03 11:12, "Morland, Chris" <cmorland at cra.lacity.org> wrote:

> 
> when i drag the new fonts to the system library's font folder a message says
> that the "font folder" cannot be modified. will someone help me out with this?
> 

You can put them in the ~/Library/Fonts folder but not in the
~/System/Library folder. Fonts in the ~/Library/Fonts folder are available
to all users. 
You can also put them in ~/Users/yourfolder/Library/Fonts, but those fonts
won't be available to other accounts on your machine.
