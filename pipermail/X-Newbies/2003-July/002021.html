Man pages=system manual pages.  You probably get missing man page 
errors during the whatis database rebuild.  It's not a problem.

Man pages are the "helpful" little manuals that are supposed to explain 
how to use various system commands, tools, etc.

Their _real_ purpose, though, is to keep *nix geeks from feeling too 
superior and confident in their abilities.  So, whenever you're feeling 
way too powerful (ala BOFH) and overly confident in your 133+ h4x0r 
5ki11z, start reading man pages.  I guarantee that you'll find at least 
one to humble you.

For more info on man pages, in Terminal, type:

man man

It'll be as clear as mud! ;)
