Are you asking for software to allow you to backup to removable media?

Actually, though there is a ton of backup software available for the Mac, 
not much of it will allow you to backup to removable media easily and 
reliably.

These will:

Intego's Personal Back-Up $70
http://www.intego.com/personalbackup/

Impression $99 
http://babelcompany.com/impression/

Either would be a good choice, but I recommend Personal Back-Up over 
Impression.
