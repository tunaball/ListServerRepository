I launched it, played the song (after hitting "Hijack") - that put a 
copy on my desktop (which I could have set to iTunes) - when it was done 
playing, I hit "Done", double clicked on the desktop file, it went into 
iTunes, and it played.
And it put a copy into iTunes, which I can nw listen to when ever I want.

That's it!
