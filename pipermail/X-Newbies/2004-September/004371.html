> clamXav
>
> clamXav is a free virus checker for Mac OS X. It uses a slightly 
> modified
> version of the tried, tested and very popular clamav open source
> antivirus engine as a back end, mated to a new graphic user interface.
>
> This program does not do automatic or background scanning. Some folks
> see this as a positive feature, because it means that your anti-virus
> program is not intrusive and it won't slow the performance of your
> Macintosh.
>
> Drawbacks:  clamXav cannot repair infected files, it can only identify
> them. (You must trash them manually.) It does not look for Classic
> viruses.
>
> On the plus side, the program will look for Windows viruses.
