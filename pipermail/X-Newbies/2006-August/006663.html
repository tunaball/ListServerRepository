 Hi Keith, 
 
 Before you do the complicated stuff, do the simple ones:
 Do what Aranzazu is suggesting.
 
 Here is how to do it:
 Before launching Appleworks
 - Go to your "Library" folder, 
 - then to the "Preferences" folder, 
 - find the Appleworks prefs which should be called something like "com.appleworks.plist"
 - trash them
 
 You have 2 "Libray" folders, one on your hardrive, the other one under your user name (little house icon), find in which folder the prefs are. Do a search for "Appleworks", to make sure you delete the right one.
 
 Good luck and let us know.
