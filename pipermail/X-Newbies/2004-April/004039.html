If this is truly in "Computer", then he renamed "Macintosh HD" to his 
name. If it is in Macintosh HD:Users, he renamed his home folder. Since 
the system believes his home folder should really be called 
'his-shortname', it promptly recreated a folder called 'his-shortname'. 
This would also explain the iTunes music folder 'disappearing'.
Your user will likely continue to be confiused about where his files 
are (home (shortname), or not-really-home (fullname). You really need 
to do one of two things: Either change the user's shortname following 
the kbase-article-from-hell, or educate him - he is NEVER to rename 
that folder. Perhaps a finder-label of "red" may help?

Better to get him straightened out now, rather than April 14th 2005.

outside the US).
