Two possibilities:
1. Change your screen resolution. See if the window is moveable. Change 
back to original resolution when done.

2. Select the window by clicking on it - it should now be 'active'. 
Since you still won't be able to reach the title bar or buttons, close 
it by using 'apple-w' on the keyboard (hold down the apple key, and 
press the letter w).
Now open a new browser window (File -->New Window from the menu bar, or 
'apple-n' on the keyboard).  Quit Explorer, then relaunch it. 
Hopefully,any new windows will be in a more normal position.
