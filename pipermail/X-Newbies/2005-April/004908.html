> The printer my wife uses with her dual iBook has developed problems and
> I wondered if there was an easy way to let her print via the printer
> hooked into my G5.

Yes, there's (literally) nothing to it.

1. Open Sharing system preference.
2. Turn on "Printer Sharing."
3. Download and install a printer driver for your printer on her 
machine.
4. Use Printer Center on her machine to add the printer to her active 
list.
5. She can print.
