- open the Little Snitch preference pane
- "click the lock to make changes"
- click on the gear tab (next to the "All Rules" tab) and select "Preferences..."
- check ON the "Confirm the Little Snitch alert automatically" checkbox
- click ON the Automatically "Deny" connections radio button
- click on "Save"

Finally, delete any rulesets for the "Word" application.  Now run Word
again and let Little Snitch catch it trying to reach the alien mothership.
