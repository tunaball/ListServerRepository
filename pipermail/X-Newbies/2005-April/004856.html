Mail and Safari. I use both Mail and Safari for e-mail and often write

Russian, etc. I have installed CocoAspell
http://cocoaspell.leuski.net/ which works well, but is rather clunky

http://www.eg.bucknell.edu/~excalibr/excalibur.html , but I believe
one has to go through a similar process. Isn't there a way to more
easily/elegantly switch between the dictionaries in various languages
