You're looking for the Griffin iMic

<http://www.griffintechnology.com/products/imic/index.php>

which is at least twice as much. And, btw, connecting it to the 
turntable's headphone jack is a recipe for poor sound; you should hook 
it to the preamp's output -- or the record player's line output, if the 
preamp is built-in.
