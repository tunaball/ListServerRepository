This has nothing to do with your other problem.

Your friend is right, setting up OpenFirmware password disables several 
trip keys, including "C" for booting from the CD. See

<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106482>

for details.

That is, in fact, the point of OF password -- to make it a little more 
difficult to gain access to the data.
