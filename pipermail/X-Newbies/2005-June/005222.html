I got the download information today, not tomorrow. I downloaded and  
intstalled Deep Freeze and it went without a hitch, following the  
instructions in the .pdf manual. It involves activating root, but is  
not complex and instructions are detailed and complete. A short trial  
with a 'test' user seemed to work as advertised. It freezes only  
regular user accounts and does not effect administrator accounts. If  
you are an system admin. with several regular users or are very  
careful about security and normally function as a regular user when  
you don't need admin. powers, this is for you.

potential individual users.) Multiple licenses are available in lots  
of 10 and up.
