> make use of OS 9 space [...]
> OS 10.3.8 to OS 9.2 partition

Don't let yourself be confused by terminology. You don't have an "OS 
9.2 partition" or "OS 9 space". You have a plain partition which 
happens to contain a Mac OS 9 System Folder. As far as Mac OS X is 
concerned, it's a partition just like any other (except the boot 
partition), and it can be used and accessed just like any other 
partition.
