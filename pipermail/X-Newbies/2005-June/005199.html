Perhaps. Whatever I'm getting, the point is simple. Mac users living 
outside privileged areas may find it neither "VERY easy" nor "dirt 
cheap" to purchase the wherewithal for regular backups. Therefore, 
before busting the chops of a Machead who posts a plea for help with a 
crashed disk, take a moment to consider where he's posting from. Before 
telling him that

>> There is NO EXCUSE for people who, after 20+ years of
>> being warned to make backups, fail to have a backup

ask yourself whether he might not have a reasonable excuse. He may know 
as well as you do -- who knows, perhaps even better than you do -- how 
important a backup is. But, in his neck of the woods, just owning a Mac 
and being on-line may be more than most of his compatriots could 
afford.
