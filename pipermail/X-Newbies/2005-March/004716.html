Not for OS X.  Plus Optimizer came with older versions of Disk Warrior, 
but Plus Optimizer runs in OS 9 only and is no longer included with Disk 
Warrior.

"defragmenting" or "optimizing" your Directory.  That isn't the same 
thing as defragmenting your hard drive.
