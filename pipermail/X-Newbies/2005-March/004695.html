> Go to the Network Preference Pane. Click on your active network
> location (probably Automatic, as this is the default location). Click
> the Configure... button at the bottom left. You will see a popup menu
> labeled "Show". Make sure "Internal Modem" is selected. Then click on
> the PPP tab. You can enter your primary and secondary phone numbers in
> the dialog box. Click on the Apply Now Button to make this selection
> active.
>
