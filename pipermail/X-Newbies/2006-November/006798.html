I also recommend MacPorts, but want to make clear that it's not just  
"another package system like Fink". Fink installs a GNU/SYSV like UNIX  
environment that interposes itself over OS X's UNIX environment (I,  
myself, would say gets in the way of.), whereas MacPorts directly  
leverages OS X's UNIX environment.
