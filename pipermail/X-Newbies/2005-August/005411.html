You can easily clean the heads of Epson Color Stylus 740i with  
Control Panel buttons on the printer, without going through the Mac  
OS.  The procedure is detailed on Page 127-129 of the User Manual.   
If you do not have the User Manual, it can be readily downloaded from  
the Epson website:
