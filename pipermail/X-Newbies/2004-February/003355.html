Alex I do not agree that Photoshop Elements 2 is "super" lite.  It is 
missing some applications for those who are actual graphic artists. I 
was not fortunate to have it bundled for cheap, but got some really 
light applications that I could not use.  I have used both Graphic 
Converter which came bundled with my G5 and Photoshop Elements to edit 
digital photos and much prefer the latter.  When you look at the price 
of Adobe Photoshop at $649.00, paying $99 for a lighter version that is 
very adequate for most people does not look like a bad deal to me.  
Just my 2¢.
