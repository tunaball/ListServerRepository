If you look in the Safari Help menu and search on "save image" you'd 
find the following:

Saving webpages and webpage content

You may be able to save webpages and the content they contain, such as 
text, images, and other items.

  You can save a webpage or a frame as a file. You can copy text, 
images, and other items in webpage then paste it in another documents. 
You can also drag selected text, images, and other items from the 
webpage to the desktop or a document.
	▪ 	 To save a page, choose Save As from the File menu. You can also 
hold down the Control key and click in the page, then choose Save Page 
As from the menu.
	▪ 	 To save a frame of a webpage, hold down the Control key and click 
the frame, then choose Save Frame As from the menu.
	▪ 	 To copy text, select it and choose Copy from the Edit menu. You 
can then paste the text in another application. You can also drag the 
text from the webpage to the desktop or a document in another 
application.
	▪ 	 To save an image, drag it from the webpage to the desktop or a 
document in another application. You can also hold down the Control key 
and click the image, then choose Save Image As to save it as a file or 
Copy Image to Clipboard to paste it in another document.
	▪ 	 To save other types of content in a webpage, try dragging the item 
from the webpage to the desktop or holding down the Control key and 
clicking the item.

  Some webpages may prevent you from saving items on the page.



Hope this helps.
