Bob Baron said:

>Does Panther do nightly/weekly routine maintenance while the 'puter 
>is in Sleep Mode? Thanks.


No.  The computer must be awake for the cron jobs to run.

Have a look at MacJanitor:
http://personalpages.tds.net/~brian_hill/macjanitor.html

or

OnyX
http://www.titanium.free.fr/us/onyx/

Both of which are free utilities that can be used to run the cron jobs at 
any time.

For more information, and other similar utilties, see:

http://www.macattorney.com/tutorial.html
