If you look carefully :

> <http://www.fdic.gov@211.191.224.108:3180/index.htm>

See the @211.191.224.108? The "@"  means the numerical IP address is
the one being used, and anything in front of that is simply data being
sent along for the ride. That's how some sites determine who "refers"
the browser to them (mostly used for ads). In this case though, its
simply being used to obfuscate the actual address.
