Too bad. It's unfortunate when the client wants a repair/tune up done, 
but then won't let you open the hood and make it happen,  In this case, 
you back up the Users etc (OK, back up the entire HD, confirm the back 
up, run from the back up) THEN, Archive and Install or even wipe and 
install, then bring all the User 'stuff' back over. Keep the backup 
around for a while.  Otherwise, you are going to spend forever trying 
to fix problems without knowing the cause etc, as in the 'missing' Disk 
Utility.
