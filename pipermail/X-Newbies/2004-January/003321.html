>I'm about to get a PowerBook with the AirPort card in it.  I already
>have a 17" iMac with the AirPort card in it too.  I use Safari for
>the browser and Eudora for email.  Has anyone gone through growing
>pains for syncing two together so I could update "telecom" files from
>one to the other without just using my USB JumpDrive.
>
>Many Thanks,
>              Bob

Use Firewire target mode. Reboot the new machine holding down the T 
key, and it becomes a giant firewire hard drive. Cable it to the 
firewire port of the old mac and it will mount on the desktop. Use 
Carbon Copy cloner to copy your user folder over. You may also want 
to move parts of the library. You can also copy over some 
applications, or clone the apps folder. You may have to reinstall 
several of them.
