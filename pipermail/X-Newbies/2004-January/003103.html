On Sunday, Jan 18, 2004, at 17:13 Canada/Eastern, John Wilson wrote:

> Does anyone know if there's a way to tell the maximum recording speed 
> of a blank, unmarked CD-R disk?

Not really. The issue is actually more complex than it appears. (For 
more details, have a look at 
<http://www.osta.org/technology/cdqa.htm>.) Your best bet is probably 
to use a tool such as Roxio Toast, which estimates the speed based on 
the calibration controls provided by the manufacturer in the pregroove; 
it's usually (not always) right.
