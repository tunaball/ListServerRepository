I have seven Epson printers, two HPs, one Apple (an apparently 
indestructible LaserWriter 4/600). My Epson Stylus Photo 875DC, Epson 
Stylus Photo 785EPX, Epson Stylus Color 800N and the LaserWriter just 
keep working. I print [from a WallStreet running Jaguar over a 
wireless connection, two Lombards running Jaguar (one wired, one 
wireless), a TiBook (AirPort) running Panther, an iBook (AirPort) 
with Panther, and a G4 400 PowerMac (wired) running Jaguar] to any of 
these, and from a couple of Windows 2000 servers to the Epson Stylus 
Color 800N. I can print from my machines to my Epson Stylus Color 740 
which I keep as the only one with parallel, serial and USB connection 
capability. I print from another G4 400 PowerMac running OS 9 to the 
LW 4/600.

I have used the Epson Printer Utility with both Jaguar and Panther.

I also have a Stylus Color 600 and a pair of 777s mothballed that I 
simply have had no reason to try to use in many months.

I have two Epson scanners - a Perfection 1650 and a Perfection 1670 
Photo. They are both fully usable from both Jaguar and Panther, front 
panel buttons and all.
