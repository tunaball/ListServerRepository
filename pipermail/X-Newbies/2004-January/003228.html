You will have to learn "new" ways to cut&paste and a 3-button mouse 
helps (I use a 2-button mouse with a roller that doubles as the middle 
button--It's an X-Window thing). In addition, long passages are not 
easily copied because the X-Window clipboard is much smaller than the 
Aqua one that we are used to. It's something that may not bother you or 
may drive you crazy. It may also get better in time. I hope so.
