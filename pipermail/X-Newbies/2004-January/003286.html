There is a not-very-busy Word for Mac list. From the list subscription info:

---------------
   WORD-MAC is a moderated list devoted to discussion of Microsoft Word
   on the Macintosh platform...

Content
=======
   Posts to WORD-MAC should concern, not surprisingly, Microsoft Word
   for the Macintosh OS.  Not DOS.  Not Windows.  Messages that stray too
   far from this topic (as judged by me, the moderator), will not be
   posted and will likely be returned to the poster, or possibly just
   deleted, depending on how busy I am.

Subscriptions
=============
   To subscribe to WORD-MAC send mail to:

      listproc at scu.edu.au

   with the following message in the body of the message:

      subscribe word-mac YourRealFirstName YourRealLastName

   Do not use quotes, brackets or anything else in the message.
