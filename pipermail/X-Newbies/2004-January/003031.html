Chris Morland said:

>i upgraded to panther last week and it quit installing somewhere on the 
>second disk: the message said: "stopped due to an unexpected error. my 
>G4 seems to be running fine; what would advise? what's on the second 
>and third disk? thanks in advance.


I've now heard from at least a half dozen folks who have had the exact 
same thing happen.

Apparently the installation stops after doing a complete install of your 
OS software, but some or all of your extras, such as Safari and other 
programs, may not get installed.  The way to deal with it at the point 
you are at is to do a Custom install of all of the extras in the Panther 
installer.  They should install and work without a problem.
