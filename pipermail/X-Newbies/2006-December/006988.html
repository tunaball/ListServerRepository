OS X "Panther" (10.3) & "Tiger" (10.4) both offer
basic built-in backup solutions. Apple calls there
solution "Backup". And it doesn't cost you a dime! For
that matter, I would say that for most users "Backup"
will meet our run-of-the-mill needs.

I run a business using it and it works great.
