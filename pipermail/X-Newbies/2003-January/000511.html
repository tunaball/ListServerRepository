> Hello folks,
> Strange question...does anyone know of a way to make the finder open
> directly to the favorites folder?  Obviously, Apple offers the choice of
> Computer or Home, but i'd like to make it open to the favorites folder
> automatically.  I know I can just keep it in the toolbar or hit
> command>shift>F, but for the students at the school I work at, i'd like them
> to go straight to the favorites folder.  Unfortunately, the "Simple Finder"
> is just a little too simple and doesn't offer much elbow room to work with.
> Thanks for any suggestions.
> -Brian
> 

Brian,

Open, System Preferences > Login Items > Add

Navigate to your Favorites folder (Users > "name" > Library > Favorites).

Click "Add". Your Favorites folder will launch when you log in/restart.
