I used Norton SystemWorks 2, unfortunately I installed it on my new OS 
10.3.  It did not work.  Uninstalling was so fun.  It had grown like a 
cancer or virus into almost every little corner.  I got rid of it, 
downloaded a free Virex from my .mac membership and placed an order for 
Norton Utilites 8.  I am NOT going to install NU because you almost 
always have to boot from the CD to make repairs anyway.  Tech Tool is 
popular, but they are very slow in their updates.
