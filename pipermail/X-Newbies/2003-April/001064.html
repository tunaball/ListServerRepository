Don't know about modifying the windows, but for text editing, try 
Hydra, available at VersionTracker. It will even allow collaborative 
editing using Rendezvous on your local area network, and has syntax 
highlighting for a number of programming languages such as HTML, 
PHP,Perl, XML, etc. It needs a bit of work yet for line numbering and 
find and replace capabilities, but is better than TextEdit for what you 
want to do...and it's freeware.
