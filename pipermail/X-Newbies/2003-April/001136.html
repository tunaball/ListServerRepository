> How do I determine *which* firewall is running? Would there be any 
> tell-tale
> settings or files that I could look for?

The first thing I would check is for OSX's built-in firewall.  It is 
located in Apple Menu > System Preferences > Sharing > Firewall tab.  
If it says it is on, click Stop to turn it off.

If it is another program that you are running, what I would do is open 
Process Viewer, as Diego suggested.  On the menu that says Show: I'd 
first set it to User Processes, as this will narrow the number of items 
displayed.
