A couple of things come to mind:

1. Do you have two accounts configured to point to the same mail server 
and same user name
2. Do you have any rules that forward, redirect, or reply to messages 
that send a message to your email address?
3. Are the messages that are duplicated going to a mailing list? If you 
are on a list and someone sends a message to you, you will get two 
copies, one directly to you and one coming from the list itself
4. You could have a messed up table of contents for one of your 
particular mailboxes. Try doing a Rebuild Mailbox from the Mailbox menu 
with the suspect mailbox selected
