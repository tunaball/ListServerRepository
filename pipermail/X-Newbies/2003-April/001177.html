I have OSX 10.2.5 on two of the original iBooks.  I recently upgraded RAM to
the new 512 chips available for the early iBooks (avail at OWC, as well as
other online stores).  Everything works wonderfully.  Of course 300MB is now
a tad slow, but not slow enough to worry about, and I definitely recommend
the RAM upgrade to go with it.  If you have not already installed a larger
hard drive than the original 3GB or 6GB, now is the time to do so.
