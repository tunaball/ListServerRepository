When Disk Warrior fixed the directory, it removes the files and places 
them into memory. If something goes wrong and the computer freezes, 
crashes or otherwise loses power, you can lose the whole shebang, drivers 
included and end up with your drive disappearing. That has happened to me 
only once, and I had the person's files backed up so it was just a 
question of reinstalling her.
It won't happen again, I can assure you.
