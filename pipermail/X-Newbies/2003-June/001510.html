Macro viruses are severely limited under X.

> If you have outlook, it can probably script the sending of mail too.

No. Microsloth Mac e-mail apps (Outlook, Entourage) are not affected by 
macro viruses. Moreover, the General>Macro virus protection preference, 
which should have been on by default in Word X, warns about documents 
which might contain macros and gives the option of opening them without 
the macros. It's a good idea to leave this preference on and not allow 
macros to run.
