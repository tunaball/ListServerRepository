>  And then put an alias of your iTunes (drop the ‘alias’ suffix on the 
> file name) folder back into the Music folder in your home directory. 
> iTunes will then automatically pick up your music library via the

You can also just move the iTunes Music folder and simply tell iTunes 
to look in the new location for the music folder.  Go to
iTunes > Preferences > Advanced and click the <Change...> button and 
choose the new location in the dialogue window that pops up.
