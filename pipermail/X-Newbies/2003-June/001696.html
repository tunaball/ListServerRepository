Follow the instructions found at 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=93195>

These directions are for moving your music library to a location where 
others using the same computer can also access your music, but it works 
for what you want as well. Just insert the location of your choice for 
"a publicly accessible location."
