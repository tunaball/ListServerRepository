On Wednesday, Jun 11, 2003, at 19:31 US/Central, David Boag wrote:

>  The other problem though is that at startup, there is literally a 75 
> second pause at network initialization. Anyone have any thoughts on 
> why?

Is this on boot?  If so, go into Applications > Utilities > Directory 
Access and disable any of the services that you do not use like NIS, 
LDAPv2 or v3, NetInfo, etc.  That may help speed the network 
initialization process up, since the computer won't be trying to find a 
server/service that doesn't exist.

Here's a link to a related Apple kbase about this:
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=107279>
