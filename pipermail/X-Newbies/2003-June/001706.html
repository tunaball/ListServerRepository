The national Do Not Call List went effective yesterday.   
www.donotcall.gov
After you register there is a confirming e-mail.   I never received 
mine.  This AM's New York Times mentioned that some had not received 
the confirming e-mail perhaps because the e-mails were not making it 
through junk mail filters.  Lo and behold, they were correct, Mail 
treats the confirming e-mails as junk.  Check your junk mailbox.  You 
need to respond to the e-mail to confirm the listing on the national 
list.
