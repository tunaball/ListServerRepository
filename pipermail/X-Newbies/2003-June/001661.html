INCORRECT!

Disc Burner doesn't support it, but Disc Copy does. In the Burn Disc
dialog, click the blue disclosure button at the top right to reveal Burn
Options. Under "After Burning" there is a checkbox for "Allow additional
burns" -- that's multisession.
