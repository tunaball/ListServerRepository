Mark:

Lucky for you, I have had that EXACT same problem, and figured out how 
to solve it.

The problem is caused by your having turned off a font the Terminal 
application REQUIRES. You may have done this in Font Book or some other 
font manager, but Terminal will be inoperative until you restore it. I 
don't know which one it is, but you'll turn back on the "old" system 
fonts you turned off, you'll probably get Terminal working again.

But in the meantime, I'll save you some time: you don't have the Opener 
malware. Nobody on this list does. It's never been reported "in the 
wild."
