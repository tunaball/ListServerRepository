On Nov 11, 2004, at 09:31, John Park wrote:

> Has anybody used airtunes?

Yes!

> And if so what are the pros and cons of it?

Pros:	-easy to use; just turn on the preference in iTunes
		-you don't actually have to join the airport express network to use 
airTunes
		-the sound is as good as your stereo setup and SO much better than 
the internal speakers
		-you can control the volume with your stereo remote from across the 
room/house/apartment
		-the software is FREE!  :)

Cons: 	hmmmm... I can't think of any, really.

It's just super cool, really.  It _should_ be that easy and simple, and 
Apple has made it so.  It makes me feel (slightly) sorry for all those 
Windows users who will never get to experience this neatness.
