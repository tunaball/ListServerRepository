Are you a recent switcher from Windows?

You are unlikely to "mess up your [Mac] laptop" by installing Canon  
drivers for your all-in-one.

The worst drivers for a printer or scanner that I know of were  
drivers for HP printers.
See:
How To Deal With Common OS X 10.2 Jaguar Problems
http://www.macattorney.com/tutorial.html

http://support.apple.com/kb/TA25467?viewlocale=en_US

These HP drivers caused slowdowns and spinning beachballs.  But even  
then you could easily fix the problem by simply uninstalling the HP  
drivers.

I haven't heard of any cases where users have installed Canon drivers  
and have experienced any problems at all.
