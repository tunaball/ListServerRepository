> Are there any easy to use programs where I can convert a text based

I don't know if this is what you want but I downloaded a program  
called 'Mailto Guard' it is a flash program, you put in the email you  
want, then press convert, it then converts it a code which you copy  
and paste into your page in place of your email, when you click on  
the link it opens your mail program with your mail address in a new  
mail.
It doesn't display your email address on your site, it has been a  
while since I used it but I think you link it to your 'Contact me'  
button.
