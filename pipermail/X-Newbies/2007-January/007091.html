On Jan 29, 2007, at 1:51 , Brian Durant wrote:

> Anyone know how to modify the time display to a 24 hour clock for apps
> like iCal? OS X is quite confusing on this issue in my personal
> opinion. I have changed the display to a 24 hour clock in the Date and
> Time (Clock) preferences, but in international I have the region
> format set to Denmark (English) and "Times" has AM and PM. I know I
> need to click the "customize" tab and delete "PM", but that still
> doesn't give me a 24 hour clock in apps like iCal. What else do I need
> to do?

When you click the customize button, you should then see an example  
format, with each element in a blue oval. Hover the mouse over the  
"7" oval (hours), then click the triangle that appears. In the popup  
list, select "00-23". Then place the cursor after the "PM" oval and  
delete it. Close the window, relaunch iCal.

That is one hell of a non-intuitive interface.
