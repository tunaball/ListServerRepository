Apple gives you iLife with new computers:  iPhoto; iMovie; iDVD plus  
Garageband which would not exactly apply to art.  When I import new  
photos from my camera, they go into iPhoto.  Double click on what I  
want to manipulate goes into Adobe Photoshop.  These programs alone  
cannot be matched by Windows.  iMovie and iDVD are awesome free  
applications.  I use them and PC people are in awe.  I know that PC's  
also have these programs but they are not as good or intuitive. Of  
course there are many other art applications  Also, students could  
use the internet without fear of virus's or worms infecting the  
computers.  For website design, instead of awful FrontPage, students  
can use Dreamweaver for Mac or even the free Apache program and  
TextEdit that comes with the software.

I have just started with iWork and love it.  It could apply to art  
for brochures and flyers.  Just some of my thoughts.  Good luck
