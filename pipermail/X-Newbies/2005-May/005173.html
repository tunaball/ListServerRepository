> Is there a way to click on and open a numbered item on the "Today's
> Topics" list at the beginning of a mailing list? It's awkward to try
> remembering the numbers of topics I'm interested in reading.
> I'm on Mail and OS10.3.9.
> Sorry if this is a dumb question!

Not a dumb question at all.

You're apparently set for "digest" messages, which means you get a  
day's worth of email in one loooong message roughly once a day.  
There's no way I know of to "jump" to just the stuff you're  
interested in -- that's not really the way mailing lists work.

You might want to switch to the more conventional "individual emails"  
route, but set up a "rule" or "filter" in your email program that all  
mail from this list go into a special folder. Then you can look into  
the folder and only "open" those subjects you're interested in. Mail  
(Apple's default mail program) will even thread them for you if you  
like!
