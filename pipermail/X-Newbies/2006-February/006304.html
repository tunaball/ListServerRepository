New Worm Aims at Macs
By REUTERS

SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 16 (Reuters) ? A malicious computer worm has been 
found that singles out Apple Computer's Mac OS X operating system, 
believed to be the first such virus aimed specifically at the Mac 
platform.

The worm is called OSX/Leap-A, according to a posting on the Web site 
of the antivirus software company Sophos, which said the worm was 
spread via Apple's iChat instant messaging program.
