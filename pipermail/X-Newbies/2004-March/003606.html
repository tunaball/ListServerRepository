> Would you please explain how you get your photo embedded in your
> outgoing mail? Is it possible to do this for some mailboxes (say
> personal mail) and not others? It's seems like one of those cool Apple
> ideas that I haven't seen Pogue explain.


It's a feature of .Mac, which is oddly activated by going to the 
mac.com webmail site.

You can pick any picture in your "pictures" folder of your idisk and 
that will be the photo people see in the email.

I am told that there is a way for non-.Mac subscribers to do something 
similar using a third-party program, but I don't know anything about 
that. A search for "picture in mail" at Versiontracker would probably 
turn up something.
