> Greeetings folks...

> up, but  old "account" seems to still be in there somewhere as well. 
> When on occasion my .mac will not send outgoing mail it asks if I want 
> to revert to my old outgoing account...
> Is there anyway I can delete that old account? It does not show up in 
> accounts in preferences...

My unfortunate experience with this same situation was that deleting 
the old account deleted the mailbox and all traces of any mail that was 
in it. You can make the account inactive and preserve the mailbox or 
you can backup the mailbox to another drive and then delete the 
account. In mail preferences>accounts> highlight the account you want 
inactive and go to >advanced and uncheck "enable this account". Backing 
up the messages I am not sure how to do but I am sure someone else 
does.
