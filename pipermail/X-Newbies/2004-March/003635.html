The background patterns are stored in ppat resources in the Appearance 
control panel. Probably the easiest way is to use ResEdit to open ppat 
ID 20018, copy it, and paste in Photoshop. (You can find ResEdit in 
various places on the 'net. It runs in Classic, but it's free.) The 
actual values are, in QuickDraw hex, 0000,451E,4A3D; that is, 0,69,74 
(but I wouldn't swear to my conversion).
