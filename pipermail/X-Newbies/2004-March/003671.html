More often than not, that behavior is consistent with a corrupted 
preference file. I've had problems with applications unexpectedly 
quitting (crashing) on occasion in every OS. Follow these steps:

Quit BBEdit Lite
Move the BBedit Lite preference out of your user Library Preferences 
folder ( located at '/Users/nacohen/Library/Preferences/BBEdit Lite 
Preferences/BBEdit Lite Prefs Data' on my system)
Restart BBEdit Lite and try to recreate the problem.
If it continues, try removing the entire BBEdit Lite Preferences folder 
with BBEdit not running and then restart BBEdit Lite.
If that doesn't work, remove BBEdit Lite and reinstall.

Most of the time, one of these steps will get your program working 
again.
