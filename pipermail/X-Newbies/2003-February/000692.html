>  . . . After I restart I can get my mail, but it downloads the same 
> messages I have already received and deleted.   Any thoughts on what I 
> can do?

Make sure you have "Remove copy from server after retrieving message" 
checked and the time option set to something short, like one day or 
less. If your mailbox on the server has a month or more of messages in 
it, that can make checking mail really slow.
