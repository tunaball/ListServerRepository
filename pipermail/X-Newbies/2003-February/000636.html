Yes, but then I have to open it to awaken the PB. However, was just 
given another tip: Press the Power button, which brings up the "Are 
you sure you want to Shut Down?" dialog, then, instead of using the 
trackpad/cursor to click "sleep," just press the S key. So it can be 
done in two steps from the keyboard alone.
