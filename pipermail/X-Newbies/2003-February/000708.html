Don't want to give out incorrect information (someone correct me if I am
wrong), but if you can not leave your machine on overnight (I can't) - go to
<http://www.versiontracker.com> and grab an amazing little utility called
MacJanitor from the MacOSX section.

It will run these clean up/maintenance routines for you in about 2 minutes
(and you don't have to leave the machine on for the OS to handle it
naturally at 12:01am or whenever it is).
