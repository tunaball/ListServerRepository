Find a pdf on your machine, click on it once and enter CMD-I on the
keyboard.

When the info panel pops up, select the section that says "Open with". Click
on the drop down tab and select your version of Acrobat - then select the
"Change All" button below that. If Acrobat doesn't appear in that menu yu
have to 'navigate' to it and select it.

Should do the trick.
