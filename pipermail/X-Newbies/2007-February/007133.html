I've really never had that much luck with Canon personally.

The key points here are that the printer does:
1. Network - ethernet port
2. Scan
3. Copy
4. Color
5. Fax (optional)

I really don't do that much color printing, but I need it sometimes  
for printing up photos or the kids doing school reports.
