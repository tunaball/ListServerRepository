BTW, try the following also, as everyone has Safari:

Go to http://news.google.com/ and do a search like "bird flu",
"Turkey", "europe", etc. then press the "Atom" link on the left side
of the Google page. Bookmark the resulting page. Your bookmark should
show a number next to it when news items get updated. Go to the
bookmarked page and you have an updated news search without any
effort.
