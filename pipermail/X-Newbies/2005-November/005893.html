Unless we know what your critical software is, we can't be of much help.

We're well past the stage where Apple puts things on software update  
that universally hose everyone - and if 10.4.3 did that, you'd have  
heard about it by now.

So you need to ask something specific, and probably on a forum  
dedicated to your important software that can't be interrupted. If  
you really want to be sure, you need a test box with a comparable  
configuration. If the update hoses anything, it will most likely be  
something obscure that is associated with your specific  
configuration, not general users at large.

I've done every update immediately since I started with OS X back at  
10.2.something without ever having any problems - but my 4 Macs are  
very generic.
