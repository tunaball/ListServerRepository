> OK , I'm stumped. How do you delete an album cover from iTunes, once

1. Select a song in iTunes that has the album cover you want to remove.
2. Get info on the song (command-i).
3. Select "Artwork." Press "Delete."

This is in iTunes Help, btw, though not as explicitly as I've stated  
it here.
