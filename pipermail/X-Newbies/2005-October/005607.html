I agree that Shiira is quite impressive. The only real problem I have 
with it is that hitting the tab key takes you to the URL window, not to 
the next data entry field. That drives me insane and keeps me from 
turning it into my main browser!

Though it starts slow, Omni remains my default browser because it 
handles almost everything with few complaints; the pop-up page-specific 
settings are handy; support is great; its built-in RSS ability is an 
added plus and saves me from needing yet another application (I'm still 
on 10.3.9). I also keep updating iCab for its own unique charms, 
including superb filtering, direct developer feedback, and general 
eccentricity.
