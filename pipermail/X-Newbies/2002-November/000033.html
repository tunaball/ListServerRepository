You can start-up from the installer disk; however, you do not have a 
full user environment. When starting from the install disk, you can run 
the Disk Utility program to check, repair or erase your hard drive, you 
can reset a password in most configurations, and you can install or 
reinstall your OS X system. What you cannot do includes connecting to 
the internet, checking e-mail, running iPhoto, and just about all other 
activities.

So, in the scenario you described, you can start up from the installer 
disk and make the repairs and reinstall the operating system.
