I am able to make aliases of these icons...

1. In Finder Preferences check all of the 'Show these items'

2. On the desktop create a new folder. Call it Drives, or anything  
you want.

3. Do a CMD-L on each of the drives that you want to include.

4. Drag each of these aliases into your new  'Drives'  folder.

5. Then uncheck the items in Finder Preferences. That leaves your  
'Drives' folder.

6. Find a good looking icon to copy over the generic 'Drives' folder  
icon.
