Tic tic is more like tisk tisk. Remember that sound your mom made when you
did something wrong that went against you like losing a dollar because you
did not put it in the bank? Tisk-tisk, shame shame. You make an :-o with
your mouth and pull back with your tongue off the top of your mouth two
times. No more no less. I think that is tic-tic or tisk-tisk. When I write
tisk-tisk, Entourage does not recognize the spelling but it does recognize
tic-tic. Perhaps tisk-tisk is supposed to be spelled tic-tic.
Now to be clear, I am not supporting or defending either position, just
trying to clarify the phrase tic-tic.
