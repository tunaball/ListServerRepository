Does it freeze because it is trying to open? If so, you can prevent 
Mail from reading it by dragging the gray bar (the one that separates 
the list of messages from the text of the selected message) all the way 
to the bottom of the window. Then, when you click on the message, it 
won't be opened, and you can delete it without seeing the dreaded 
spinning beach ball.

Alternatively, if the message above or below the offending message is 
destined for the trash, you can click-drag across both (or multiple) 
messages. They won't be opened, but they can be trashed.

Hope that helps.
