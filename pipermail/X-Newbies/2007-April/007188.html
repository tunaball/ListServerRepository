On possible complication is that OS 10.4 usually comes on DVD.  Some 
iBook G3s do not support DVDs.  Also depending on the iBook G3 model, 
you may need to use a third party utility to assist in the 
installation.  That would be XpostFacto.  Check it out at:
http://eshop.macsales.com/OSXCenter/XPostFacto/
If you pass these hoops, of the several upgrade paths on the installer 
disk, it is best to use "Archive and Install."
