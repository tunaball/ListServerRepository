Do you have a second mac/pc close by that has a monitor attached?
If so, you can buy a cheap monitor switch box and a couple of extra monitor
cables and attach the monitor to the switch box and share the monitor
between the other mac and the mac mini by flipping the switch. Works very
well! 
just remember to wake up the mac mini if it has gone into a monitor sleep
mode by pressing any key or shaking the mouse. Or you can also set the
monitor to never sleep in the system pref pane.
