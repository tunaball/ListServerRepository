On Jan 19, 2006, at 1:33 PM, Brian Durant wrote:

> How do I find out what version of firmware I am using? I am using OS X
> Tiger (10.4.4), but I need to confirm the firmware version.

I believe that is what is listed in the System Profiler as boot rom  
version. In my G5 tower it's listed as 5.2.4f1.

To find it either search for "System Profiler" or go under the Apple  
in the menubar, click on "About this Mac", and then "More info".
