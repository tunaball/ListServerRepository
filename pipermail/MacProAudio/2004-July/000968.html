audio... pretty much like you described, you can assign whole sequences to
MIDI notes...

wanted you could use your various song sections as samples and trigger them
when you wanted; I know a couple guys who used to record this way,
triggering song sections with samplers and using an ADAT as a master, before
they got ProTools...
