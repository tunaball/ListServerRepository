  Second question first, yes Reason will slave to Live via ReWire.
Live is kind of difficult to explain, But think of it as a grid with 
Channels that can contain loops, Midi parts  or samples /audio and 
effects and the other axis of the grid. is song sections like verse, 
chorus or any snapshot across your channels called scenes. Both 
channels and scenes can be triggered by MIDI notes.
It won't do exactly what you described. (drag select and cut across 
channels and then paste as a Midi controlled part) but its darn close. 
and it's great for live stuff if you have your ducks in a row, so to 
speak.
What I don't understand if you are recording/sequencing in Cubase why 
you can't just cut and paste to achieve your goal? I can understand if 
you are performing live. But otherwise you can drag select whatever in 
Cubase and paste it as a new arrangement or into new channels or insert 
into the existing channels . I do it all the time.
I agree though it could be useful to have multiple clipboard files that 
could be assigned to keys or MIDI notes.
