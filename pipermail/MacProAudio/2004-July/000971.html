I get your idea, I thought of something similar a few years ago.

It is possible to implement in Ableton Live, fussy to setup but will 
work.

As you can assign any audio clip on any track to midi note triggers you 
could
just assign the elements of the verse for example to a group of midi 
notes and then
using a midi controller group the note on commands for these notes 
together.
Then when you hit the group key your verse will play and when in record 
mode
will come into the arrangement at the point you play the note. And so 
on for chorus
etc.

All the effects you are using will still be there on the respective 
clips audio tracks.
