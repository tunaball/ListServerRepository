I just got a CD of drum loops from Spectrasonics called "Liquid 
Grooves". Audio drum loops in aiff file format. You can cut and loop 
them to fit your creative mood. Very good selection of drum loops from 
53 bpm to 117 bpm. Spectrasonics has other CD's of different drums and 
a wide range of sounds. You can hear mp3 samples at either the 
Spectrasonics website or the Ilio website. Apparently Ilio sells the 
Spectrasonic line via the internet.
