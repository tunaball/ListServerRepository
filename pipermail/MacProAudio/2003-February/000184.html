I started digitizing my record collection about a year ago. And we ARE 
talking some obscure stuff that will never be released on CD.

Toast for the Mac comes with an app just for this purpose called "CD 
Spindoctor." It will get the track names off the CDDB (internet 
database of recordings). It IS time consuming! So far, in over a year, 
I've done about 5 lp's. Hahahahaha! 495 to go! I hope I live a long 
time!
