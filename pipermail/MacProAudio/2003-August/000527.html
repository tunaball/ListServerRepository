> If you'd like to see the lowdown, go to  http://www.vintagesynth.com/, 
>  then click on "artists" at the upper left, then click on "BT" among 
> the artist on the page (the others there are very interesting too.)
>
> BT is pretty much credited with inventing Trance while he was living 
> in Europe in the mid-90's.  He is also the person who single-handedly 
> popularized the use of stutter-edits (ie *Nsync's hit "Dirty Pop"), 
> granular synthesis, and Kyma (a $$$ computer-based dsp/ synth system).
>
> He has remixed everyone, including *Nsync (Dirty Pop"), Tori Amos 
> ("Blue Skies"), Sarah McLachlen ("I Love You"), Peter Gabriel, Seal, 
> Madonna and pretty much anyone else who is making cool music right > now.
>
> The reason Apple chose him to do the G5 demo is because he is one of 
> the top users of Logic in the world and he loves Macs.  And because 
> they new he would have that G5 running for it's life in very short 
> order.
>
