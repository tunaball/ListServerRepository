Definitely get rid of that RCA cable.
A good article on ground loops is available at: 
http://www.epanorama.net/documents/groundloop/index.html
Also UPSs can be problematic. (Big transformers, sine wave clipping and 
floating grounds.) maybe look at a power conditioner instead.
