You'll need a way to get the tapes' audio into your G4 iBook.
A cheap method is Griffin iMic
http://www.griffintechnology.com/products/imic
Tape deck to iMic (USB) to G4 iBook.
There are plenty of applications to edit the audio before burning it to 
a CD.
Many are free.  Amadeus, Audacity are popular.
