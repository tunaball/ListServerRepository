I often record live performances and town hall meetings with an older 
Mac powerbook, using the audio line in port, direct to a line out on 
the PA board, and ProTools. My daughter's iBook has no line-in, so I 
use an iMic from Griffin allowing the line out form the PA board to 
USB on the iMac. No problem either way. Of course, there are only two 
tracks, but I've never needed more on a live performance.
