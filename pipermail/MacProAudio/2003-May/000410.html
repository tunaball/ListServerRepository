Yes and yes. 
I have and use the VXPocket (2-channel) in my Titanium 800. I use it with Spectra-Foo. Very nice card. Though the dongle is a bit clumsy (four XLRs and two RCA jacks) and its connector is fragile on the card end.

I also have the Audiomedia III, and it is working fine with Protools OSX 6.01 in my G4 DP 800.
