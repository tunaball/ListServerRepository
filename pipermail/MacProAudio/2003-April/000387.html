(indistinguishable from original by audiophiles).  96K AAC is supposedly

still don't actually get to use it except on my iBook.  I use a RioVolt

AAC->MP3, then I can't use the music.  Bummer.  I suppose someone could
write an AAC->MP3 hacker, but then maybe you're back to being illegal,
which defeats the purpose somewhat (except that the artists and all of
the middlemen will have gotten their cut).  I wonder if anyone is still
updating the firmware for RioVolts?
