You can use iTunes 4 to convert the AAC files to MP3 files. I am doing 
it right now. You simply set the preferences to the MP3 encoding scheme 
of your choice and then select the file(s) you want to convert and pull 
down menu Advanced—>Convert to MP3 and let the process roll. No problem.
