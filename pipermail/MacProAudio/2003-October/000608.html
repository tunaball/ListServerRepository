I don't know if this is a quirk of my system (I think everything else 
is working fine) or the fact that I have a slow powerbook (550 
TiBook), but in the newer versions of the program, sometimes when I 
go to insert a marker, my hand accidentally drags the cursor a tiny 
bit, and I create a region instead. What then happens, is when I hit 
the space bar to play (to hear if my marker is placed correctly), I
