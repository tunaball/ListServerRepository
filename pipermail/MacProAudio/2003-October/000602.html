	Speaking of the G5, I just bought a MOTU 2408 mk3 (core 
system) and 24 i/o (expander) only to find out that the enclosed 
PCI-424 card doesn't fit in the G5's new PCI-X slots.  MOTU has an 
upgrade path... if you've got an 'older' PCI-324 card, you can 
upgrade it to the PCI-424 card for $300.  You can then upgrade the 
424 card to a PCI-424 G5 card for about $70 more, and if you bought 
the 424 card after July 1st, the upgrade to the PCI-424 G5 card is 
free.  You just have to fax MOTU a copy of your dated sales receipt 
and your registration card, and they give you an invoice number to 
write on the outside of the package when you return the PCI-424 card 
to them.  They should've received my 424 card yesterday... here's 
hoping the G5 card arrives this month!
