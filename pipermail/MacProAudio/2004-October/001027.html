> Coral Cache is run by the University of New York and it will cache 
> files
> for you in the case of your server getting slashdotted. Check it out 
> here:
>
>     http://www.scs.cs.nyu.edu/coral/
>
> Basically, say you have a file called 
> http://www.myserver.org/big_fat_file
>
> and your server is getting slashdotted, change your link to
> http://www.myserver.org.nyud.net:8090/big_fat_file
>
> and Coral Cache will do the rest.
