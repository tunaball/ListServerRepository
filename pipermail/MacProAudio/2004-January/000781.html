To go both ways, there's the M-Audio Co2 (~$65):
http://www.soundprofessionals.com/cgi-bin/gold/category.cgi?category=580&item=MM-CO2&type=store

http://www.ramelectronics.net/html/audio_toslink_adapters.html#pof-820

http://www.ramelectronics.net/html/audio_toslink_adapters.html#pof-830
