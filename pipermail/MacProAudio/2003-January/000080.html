Hello

The Beta 1 version of the plug in is now available on www.geethree.com :-)

This driver allow to use most of the Serial midi interfaces with MAC OS
X 10.2.3 and the Stealth port

So far it has been reported working with :

          - 1 Emagic Unitor 8
          - 2 Motu MIDI Time Piece II AV
          - 1 MAC Midiman
          - 1 Motu MIDI Time Piece AV
          - 1 J L Cooper synclink serial
          - 1 Motu MidiExpress+ MT4
          - 2 Motu MIDI Time Piece II
          - 1 Opcode Studio 4
          - 1 Opcode Studio 64 + Emagic Unitor 8 MarkII (USB)
          - 1 Motu MTP A/V and 1 Motu MTP II
