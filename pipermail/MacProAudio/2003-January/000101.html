Sound Designer was one of the first Digital recording software programs 
for Mac.    It was put out by Digidesign.  It was Pro Tools little 
brother.  Although Digidesign dropped support of it years ago, the file 
format became a standard and remains today.

The first Midi sequencer to incorporate audio was Vision.  Sigh.... I 
really miss Studio Vision.
