Well, I may modify what I said earlier. If you are using one of those
inexpensive allinone stereo's, I don't know what those jacks are really for...

If those are the only jacks, I'd bet that they are INPUT jacks for
hooking up another source. Most inexpensive allinone stereos don't have
any output jacks, and usually no input jacks either.

I think you'll have to buy/borrow a cassette deck to do what you want.
