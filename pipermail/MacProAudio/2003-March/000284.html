> 	I've had great luck with Yamaha RH-5M headphones.  They're not quite

> string players.  They've got plenty of output; far more than the 
> 'older' AKG and Sennheisers.  I've also seen ads for the 'new' AKG

> cord (YAY!).  Granted, they're around $95, but way worth it for

Anybody ever tried the AudioTechnica ATH-M40fs?

http://www.audiotechnica.com/guide/other/headset/ATHM40fs.html

I have been using them for a few years and I really like the flat 
response; they don't have that exagerated boom that muds up the mix but 
rather feature a very "polite" bass, that only shows what's really 
there.
Oh, output is no problem for them, and they also feature replaceable 
parts. Maybe a bit on the expensive side, too, but as Scott said, way 
worth it. :-)
