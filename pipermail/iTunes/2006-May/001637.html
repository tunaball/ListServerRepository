No, none of them would.

Do you have a lot of smart playlists? They will slow down launch  
time, but not much else. I've never heard of any real slowdowns with  
truly big libraries, but I've got an article coming up in the new  
Macworld about managing music in large libraries (using external  
disks for part of the music, for example...).
