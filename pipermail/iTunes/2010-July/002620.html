On Jul 12, 2010, at 8:54 AM, Michael Prete wrote:

> Using iTunes v9.2, I notice that although I continually check the box on the Summary tab for my iPhone next to "Manually manage music and videos", the next time I open the app, the box is unchecked.
> 
> First, I would appreciate a clear explanation of the purpose and result of checking that option. Why would one select this option?What changes when this is selected?

iTunes will not copy music or videos (or audiobooks or podcasts) automatically. To copy stuff to the device, drag items - songs, albums, artist names, etc. To remove them, you need to click the triangle next to the device icon, then select, say, Music and choose the items you want to delete, then press the Delete key.
> 
> Second, what might cause this option to deselect itself with no input from me?
