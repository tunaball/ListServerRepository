Hmm. Although I don't recognise the power supply part, this sounds a 
lot like a problem I was having with iTunes until recently: whenever I 
did anything (like firing up Limewire, or plugging a USB device or 
making a cup of coffee), iTunes would stop playing, in fact the whole 
USB audio thing would stop so my machine would no longer produce a 
peep. It was strange and annoying and, although I found a couple of 
other affected users, I couldn't find it in the Apple KBase or 
elsewhere. I have no idea what fixed it, except maybe just upgrading 
all affected apps and the OS. I am now on the absolutely current 
version of OS X and I haven't seen the problem for months.
