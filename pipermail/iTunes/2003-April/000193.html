Yup,
Getting higher fidelity than what you started with is sort of like 
water flowing uphill. You can never get improved fidelity than what you 
start out with. In the case of mp3 to AAC, you begin with the fidelity 
of the mp3 (which is less than the original CD); hence, the resultant 
AAC can never be as good as the mp3 (Signals & Systems EE410 and with a 
little entropy thrown in for good measure).
