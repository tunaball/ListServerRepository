OK, look it's very simple to rip CDs again and keep the tags: if all the tags are the same when you rip the CD into AAC, it will give you the option to "replace" the old MP3s. If you choose replace, you will NOT lose your tags; iTunes will transfer the tags to the new files for you. :)
