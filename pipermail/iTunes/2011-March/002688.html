Hi Becca:

Make a playlist for the podcasts you want to listen to.  Then add podcasts to that playlist as they update.  When you start to listen go to that playlist and start, it will play the podcasts one after the other allowing you to move from Science Friday to Quirks and Quarks when SciFri is over without interrupting your driving.

In regards to Science Friday, it seems that they are now sending just two segments each week, Hour 1 and Hour 2.
