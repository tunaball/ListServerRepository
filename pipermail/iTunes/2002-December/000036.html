iTunes would be even more useful if Smart Playlists could use both 
AND and OR logical operators in the same playlist specification.

ALL does only AND; ANY does only OR.

So if you want a playlist that selects your three favorite singers 
and focuses on their recent hits, you'd want to select
	(SingerA OR SingerB OR SingerC) AND (Year is greater than 1996)

I don't know of any way to mix AND and OR in iTunes. Seems like there 
oughht to be.
