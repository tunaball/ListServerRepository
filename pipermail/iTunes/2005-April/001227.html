On Apr 17, 2005, at 9:06 PM, tom dalbec wrote:

> Is there a special way to back up to DVD's???  Once there, are they  
> playable
> on a DVD player?

You can back up to DVDs the same as to CDs. To make a DVD that you  
can play on a DVD player (if it's MP3 compatible) go to the iTunes  
Burning preferences and check MP3 CD. Try one to see if it works with  
your DVD player; you may not be able to get a DVD-RW to work, though.
