Apple only allows 3 computers to be authorized to play the music, so 
you may need to have them deauthorize all your computers and 
reauthorize the working ones. Reformatting the hard drive causes iTunes 
to think you have a new computer. Now you have the previously formatted 
disk authorized, plus your currently formatted disk. You only have one 
more left. Always remember to deauthorize your computer before you sell 
it or reformat the disk (if possible). You have to call Apple to get 
them to deauthorize machines you do not have access to, and they will 
only deauthorize all computers at once. I had to do this procedure 
once.
