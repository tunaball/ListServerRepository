It sounds like the disc wasn't closed. Was it burned w/ a "Drag to Disc" or
"DirectCD" utility? Those typically allow you to copy files directly on the
the drive w/ the blank disc by dragging them over or cutting and pasting,
and don't require you to go through the cd burning utility interface. If
this is the case and the disc isn't closed, only that computer will be able
to read it. You'll need to reference that utility's documentation to see how
to close the disc. Its typically a menu option...
