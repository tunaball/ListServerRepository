On May 24, 2004, at 7:11 PM, mk111 wrote:

> I have itunes at work computer and at home. How can I take the songs 
> downloaded at work and put them on the home computer?
>

Yes,
You will have to authorize the computer at home with your ITMS account 
name and password.  I believe with the introduction of itunes 4.5 you 
can have up to 5 computers authorized to play your tunes.  Friendly 
reminder, before you sell, trade-in, or give away an old computer do 
not forget to de-authorize that computer from your account.
