I'm somewhere in between as to the size of my library, but my "wish 
list" would include the ability to address more than one library. 
Having classical music and doowop in the same library is ludicrous. I 
have semi-solved the problem by devoting two networked beige G3 
computers to the separate genres. With the beauty of 10.2.6 and iTunes 
4's ability to share, I have essentially what I want. Plus I put one of 
them in each room with their sound outputs driving speakers, then 
control them from my iBook with Timbuktu. I also use NetTunes to 
remotely control one of them. It really is a terrific system and I 
highly recommend it. A $75 computer with a $50 40gig drive is the best 
kept secret in music!

But wouldn't it be nice if all that was built in to iTunes 5? I have 
submitted it to the feedback link, by the way, so who knows???
