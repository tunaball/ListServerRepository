I'm with you, Travis.  For me, it's spoken word - I have a couple 
hundred CDs of spoken word (radio shows, comedy, ZBS media dramas, 
audiobooks, etc) and over a thousand CDs of music (with several added 
every week).  I like to random play the music across the entire music 
library, never the spoken word.
Further, I prefer to keep those libraries on difference drives because 
of disk space considerations, so I want to be able easy change which 
library I'm currently ripping to.

What I currently do is have a symbolic link named "iTunes" in my music 
folder, and I change it to point to iTunes.music or iTunes.spoken, as 
appropriate.  But I'd much prefer having something that could switch 
the current library from within iTunes or better, would stream from 
both libraries simultaneously to other Macs in the house.
