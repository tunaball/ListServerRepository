I only know one way to do what you want.  Set up a new playlist on  
your iPod/iPhone.  Drag the podcasts you
want to that playlist.  In the View menu select "View Options" and  
check "Release Date".  You can now sort the
podcasts according to the date they were recorded.  The iPod/iPhone  
will play them in that order.
