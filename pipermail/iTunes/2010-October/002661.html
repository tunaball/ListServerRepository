I thought of another trick I use when making compilation albums. In some cases I may have only one or just a few tracks from an album and want to combine these with other tracks from other albums to make my own compilation album. In these cases you must use a single album name for your compilation which would replace the original album name. In this case I put the original album name in the comments section, like this;

From: Worlds Away by Strange Advance

That way if I ever want to search for an album, I can still find it.
