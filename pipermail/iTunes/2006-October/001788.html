One of the things I'm really missing from my pre-iTunes days is a "queue" that
isn't dependant on what's displayed on the screen.

For example, if I start playing Album X, and move to Album Y it won't advance to
the next song in Album X OR more commonly... I start a Podcast and go into my
Music library (for example, to find missing artwork) and it doesn't advance to
the next episode unless I switch back to the podcast section before the end of
the episode (in which case it DOES continue, so I know it's dependant on what's
displayed)

Am I doing something wrong? I'm miffed as it is that I can't just add random
