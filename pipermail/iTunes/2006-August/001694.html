> I have been looking up all the info on all my 1000 songs to fill in  
> all the tags but I don't know what grouping is or part of a  
> compilation & BPM. I know that is beats per minute but where do I  
> find that info..
>
> Is someone knows what these are & where to find the info please let  
> me know.

Grouping is used mainly for classical music; the name of a work,  
where individual tracks are, say, movements.

Compilation is an album with different artists, in most cases - think  
Last Waltz, some movie soundtracks, or The Best Hits of the 80s.

BPM is beats per minute; useful for DJs.
