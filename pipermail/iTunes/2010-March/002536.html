> Hi,
> I’ve been adding music to iTunes (9.0.3) on my unibody Macbook  
> (10.6.2) and what happens when the CD is inserted is different than  
> is use to be.  The CD spins and iTunes checks for track names on the  
> internet.  The track names then briefly flash on the screen and then  
> disappear.  The CD is never shown in the Sidebar and it doesn’t  
> appear on the desktop.  If I eject the disc and reinsert it  
> everything works normally except iTunes already knows the track names.

Perhaps the preference files (.plist) are corrupted? You could A)  
search for and delete the preference file for iTunes, B) download and  
use Preferential Treatment to locate and isolate any corrupted  
preference files, or C) [dare I say it?] run AppleJack. I suppose  
there is really option D)  put up with it. I'd leave that option for  
last, though
