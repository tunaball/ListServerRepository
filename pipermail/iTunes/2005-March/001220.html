Just in case anyone is considering buying Moby:Hotel from the iTunes 
Music store.  I just got an email from Apple promoting it.

Apple lists it as having an "exclusive Bonus Track".  It is "by Album 
only", implying you must buy the whole album from Apple in order to get 
the 15th track.

I bought the CD at Best Buy and not only did it include the "Bonus 
Track" (unlisted) but there is a second CD "Hotel Ambient" which is not 
included in the iTunes Music store purchase.

Buyer beware!
