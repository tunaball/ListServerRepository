I was told by a friend on one of my book lists about a new feature.  
When I load an audiobook from CDs the discs always goes into the Music  
folder. When I download a book from Audible.com it goes in Audiobooks  
folder. Now it's easy to just select all the tracks in Music and  
Option I to bring up that window, then in Options select the Media  
Type. Bingo everything selected will move to the place you choose. Oh,  
and while you're there remember to check that you want it to remember  
the position.
Now all of my books can be where they should be. Very tidy and when  
you have as many books as I have this makes it easier to find the one  
I'm looking for since I only have to search one folder.
