On 9/18/04 3:19 AM, "Greg McNeil" <gmcneil99 at mchsi.com> wrote:

> Hi,
> How in the world does the iPod sort songs within the different
> playlists?
> 
> The songs at the top of the playlist are by the artist who's name comes
> first in the alphabet, but the song I just added recently are at the
> bottom of the list even though that is not where they should be if the
> playlist is sorted alphabetically
> 
>   I would like for the songs in my playlists to appear in alphabetical
> order by song.
> 
> Is there any way to make the playlists sort the way I want?

The iPod depends entirely on iTunes. Its sort order is that in the playlist
in iTunes. If you want to change the sort order, click one of the column
headers to sort in that order (title, artist, etc.) If you want to drag the
songs in your own order, click the left-most column header - the short one
with no label - then drag the songs in the order you want.

As I say (repeatedly) in my forthcoming iPod book, the key to understanding
the iPod is understanding iTunes.
