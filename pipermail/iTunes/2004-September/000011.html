Kirk,
Sorting from within iTunes didn't solve my problem, but here is what 
did. I wanted the songs in my Playlists to be listed alphabetically by 
song title rather than by artist. I found the info in another iPod 
discussion forum.

Select the playlist you want to sort then select which category you 
want to sort (song name, time, artist etc) the songs then are sorted. 
To get them to be sorted on the iPod control click (right click on a 
PC)  the playlist name and go to "Copy to Play Order"

The songs in the Playlist then appear in the order you selected.
