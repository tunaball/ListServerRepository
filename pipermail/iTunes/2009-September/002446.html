On Sep 28, 2009, at 4:48 PM, list boy wrote:

> Wasn't it possible back in 7 to set different import settings for  
> mono/stereo?
>
> I have an app (StarPlayr) that records satellite radio as an AIFF;  
> then iTunes encodes as mp3.
>
> My problem is I want  to record in the morning (Howard Stern) at 24  
> kbps, then later on record music (at 128 kbps).
>
> I seem to remember older versions being able to accommodate this.  
> Now it seems like one size fits all.

I think what you're looking for is still available. Look in iTunes- 
Preferences-General. Click on 'Import Settings'. Beside 'Setting:'  
click on 'Custom...'

You can change the Bit Rate, change between Stereo and Mono, and  
change the Sample Rate.

Is that what you were looking for?
