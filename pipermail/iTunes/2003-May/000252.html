On 5/2/03 11:17 AM Thubten Kunga wrote:

> TIP: You can find Cover Art for almost any album at Amazon.com.
> Moreover the art is in 20k JPEG files adding almost nothing to each of
> your songs' file sizes.
> 
> Just find the album, click on the image or see larger text below it to
> transfer to the image file page, control click choose "Download Image
> To Disk" from the pop-up menu and you have your cover art in a flash.
> Isolate your album tracks in an iTunes 4 window, command-A to select
> all, command-i to get an album tag dialog box, check the selector box
> next to the image BOX, drag the 20k image from Amazon to that BOX,
> press OK and the album cover art will be added to each track's tag info.
