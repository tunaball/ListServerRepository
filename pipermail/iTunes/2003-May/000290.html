To delete a graphic from a song file, do a "Get Info" (Command-I) on 
the song while it's selected in iTunes. Click on the "Artwork" tab, 
select the picture you want to remove, and hit Delete.

You can add multiple images (front cover, back cover, etc) to a song 
file and use the arrows to look through them while the song is playing 
(or selected as per your setting). You can also re-order the images in 
the "Artwork" tab window. The first image is what shows first in the 
main image window, second shows second, and so on.
