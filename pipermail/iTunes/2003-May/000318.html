Henri (drev at bellsouth.net) on Thursday, 8 May 2003 wrote:

>Is there a way to get the size of each 
>playlist from iTunes?

When you select a Playlist or Smart Playlist look to the bottom of the
iTunes window on the "brushed metal" edge. This tells you number of songs
in Playlist, amount of time the playlist will take to play and the size
of the playlist. If you click on this it gets a bit more detailed or vice
versa depending on which is showing when you do your click.
