Had them for over 2 years and thought I loved the sound. I heard the 
Klipsch Promedia 2.1 system, purchased them and *gave* the Soundsticks 
away!! The low end is *leaps and bounds* above the Soundsticks. USB 
audio is more headache than pleasure. Sound sometimes drops out under 
heavy processor load and it slows things down a tiny bit as USB uses 
the processor. The Klipsch system has its own volume adjustment, 
separate sub control, headphone output and aux input all on your 
desktop within your reach. To boot, they are cheaper than the 
Soundsticks. Go to your local Comp USA and take a listen. I'm sure you 
will change your mind.

Chris
