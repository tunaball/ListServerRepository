I was more interested in doing a quick column sort to zero in on 
certain criteria such as just instrumental tracks. I find I mostly use 
iTunes with a large shuffled selection, just the genre generally, but 
would like a handy method to narrow that down. I've used the comment 
field to first list the 'label/album code', for sorting by that, then 
list the various players & instrument for searches; this is also the 
info from the cd insert I want if/when I trade-in the original cd. Use 
the BPM field for key codes and you can do a mass tagging of many 
albums or artists at once without messing with comments that are unique 
for every track.

field as XML; the same or a different app could search this 
'comment-XML' and translate results to html for display in your web 
browser.
