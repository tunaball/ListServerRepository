I second the endorsement of the Monsoons.

The make for really great near-field listening.  And they look cool too.

I just did a little audio upgrade to my MDD G4 system as i was getting 
frustrated with my USB setup.  Found the M-Audio 24/96 PCI card ( 
http://www.m-audio.net/products/m-audio/audiophile.php ) and since it 
has SPDIF in & out (as well as standard analog line in/out), I 
connected it to an AMC DAC8 digital-to-analog converter (sorry, no URL) 
then out to the Monsoons.

Amazing sound.  Simply amazing.
