Yes, when the song finishes on your iPod, it will update the "Last Played"
tag on that song and the play count.

When you sync to your computer, it will update the iTunes library. Then the
smart playlist will kick in and it will remove songs from the playlist and
update it with a fresh batch of songs. So if you played 17 songs, it will
remove those 17 songs and add 17 new songs.

You can also use your iPod to change the ratings of any of your songs. When
you sync back to iTunes, it will update the ratings as well.....

Gotta love those smart playlists.  :-D
