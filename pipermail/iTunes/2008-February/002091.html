 From what I can see you should be able to charge from either the USB  
port
on your PC or from the wall charger.  All iPods with a dock connector  
(the
connector on the bottom of your Nano) can use the wall charger.  You  
should
see a battery with the lightening icon while the battery is charging.   
A battery
with the plug icon indicates the battery is charged.
