Do you have any scripts running that are forcing iTunes to reopen? I have
a script that puts what I am listening to on iTunes at the bottom of my
email set as a Cron event.

There was a time a while ago when I shut down my computer and before it
completed the task iTunes re-launched. I was stumped for days. Then I
remembered that I had set the script to check itunes every 45 sec. It was
checking iTunes before the computer shut down and so had to relaunch the
program.

Now I know that is going on and I disable the Cron event before I shut
down the computer.
