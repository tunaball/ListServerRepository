> The iTunes folder is found in your Music folder which is in your user
> folder. What has happened is that the path info to your iTunes music 
> folder
> somehow got fudged.
>
> What you can do is get iTunes to do a "Consolidate" Music command 
> (under the
> Advanced Menu). It then goes through your entire hard drive and 
> transfers
> all your music anywhere it can find it to iTunes. Or you could do an 
> Import
> from the File menu and pick the iTunes folder through the Open/Save 
> dialog.
>
> Or, finally you could just navigate with the Finder to your iTunes 
> folder
> and do a drag and drop of the folder on the iTunes application.
>
> After doing this, it would be wise to have iTunes eliminate duplicates.
