On 12/12/04 3:41 AM, "ML B" <audiomix55 at hotmail.com> wrote:

> I'm trying to figure out how to have an extra  external hard drive with
> music on it connected to my iTunes playlists.  Since my music folder is on
> my internal HD it wants to copy it.  Is there any other way?

Uncheck the setting in the iTunes preferences > Advanced: Copy files to
iTunes Music folder when adding to library. Your music will stay on the
external hard disk and not get copied.
