>> When you get info on a song it has fields for artist & Album artist.
>> What is the difference?

Designating an album as a compilation will result in 'Various  
Artists' displaying in Cover Flow view, whereas there may be cases  
where most tracks on an album are by a particular artists but some  
tracks involve the participation of someone else also. You can  
specify the difference on a per-track basis ('with Such-and-such'',  
for instance) but avoid Cover Flow displaying two instances of the  
album cover by filling in the principal artist's name as Album Artist.

<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=304389> goes in to  
further detail.
