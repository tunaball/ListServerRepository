Not to beat a dead horse, but how big is your iTunes library. If you  
have iTunes automatically load the Shuffle then it might be loading  
other songs instead of the podcast. If this is the case, you need to  
manually load your Shuffle by either moving the podcast into a  
smaller playlist and sync'ing that with the Shuffle or choosing the  
podcast with the check boxes and loading only the checked songs.
