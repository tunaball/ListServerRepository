--- benaccetto at mac.com wrote:

> Click on My Computer.
> Open the Shared Documents folder, you can either use the
> Shared Music  
> folder or make a new folder.
> Open each individual users' folder (ie. Becca's Documents).
> Open the My Music folder, then iTunes, then iTunes Music. Drag
> the  
> files in there to the Shared Music folder.
> For each user, open iTunes. Go to Edit -> Preferences.
> Click on the Advanced Tab.
> Click the Change button under the iTunes Music folder
> location.  
> Navigate to the My Computers -> Shared Documents -> Shared
> Music (or  
> what you named your music folder). Click on OK.
> Say Yes to the "Keep iTunes Music folder organized" dialog.
> 
> All of your users will now point to the same library and any
> future  
> music will be added to the same library. You will probably
> have to  
> make individual playlists for each of the users and then sync
> any  
> iPods to each individual playlist.
> 
> Hope this helps.
>
