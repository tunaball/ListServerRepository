In the old days (the OS 7 thu 9 days that is) if an application was 
acting up,
all you had to do was delete the applications preferences file, and 
reconfigure the applications preferences manually.

Under OSX there's a file called "com.apple.iTunes.plist" that, when 
deleted will, accomplish the same task.
