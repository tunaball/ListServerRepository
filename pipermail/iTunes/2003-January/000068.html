Welcome to the list. Not sure why the tunes were not in order when you
draged the cd to the playlist. At any rate, to organize a playlist, click
on the playlist icon/name in the left hand column of the itunes window.
Then you will see all items in the playlist.

Then, click the leftmost column in the song list. If you have Song Name,
Time, Artist, Album, etc. selected, it will sort by that field and you
cannor arrange the songs. When you click the left column (no column title,
just numbers in the data fields), you can then click & drag the songs in
whatever order you want.

When you burn after that, it will be in the proper order.
