http://www.icommune.net/

Streams your iTunes music to other macs, it's an iTunes plug-in. Integrates
beautifully with the iTunes interface - it appears where iPods and playlists
show up. Neat.

Looks promising, we have three macs and three lots of music in my flat, and
if I could listen to music on someone elses mac but out of my headphones, it
would be very cool.
