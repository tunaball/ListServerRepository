> I would suggest three things:
>
> First, unplug and disconnect the cable modem from the router.  Leave

> internally.  Then reconnect it and try again.
>
> Second, try connecting the cable modem directly to your computer 
> without using the router.  This will ascertain whether or not the 
> router may be causing the problem.
>
> Finally, you may have to try 'uninstalling' and then doing a clean 
> install of iTunes.
>
> Good Luck!
