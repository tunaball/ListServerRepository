It is a good idea to edit all your track tags BEFORE you rip because 
your system will always remember those tags every time you place the CD 
in your computer in future. If you edit the MP3 files after ripping, 
your system will have no memory of those tracks next time you insert 
the CD and you would have to start editing all over again. Edit all 
your tags as completely and accurately as you want them to be before 
you rip the album.
