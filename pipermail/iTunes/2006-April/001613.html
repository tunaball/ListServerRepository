James Hubbell wrote:
> Don't know what happened but some of my songs have dropped the Artist 
> name when I open iTunes.  If I play the song or go into Get Info, the 
> Artist repopulates.  Anyone have an idea how to get a mass change for 
> the Artists who are missing without a major redo?  I have some 14,000 
> songs in iTunes and this just occurred.  Thanks.
> 

With iTunes open, select Library, choose "Select All" from Edit menu, 
then select "Get Info" from File menu. If it worked for individual 
songs, then it should work for All songs, although it may take quite a 
few minutes for a 14,000 song library. Then I would download and run 
Preferential Treatment (find it on Versiontracker.com) to check for 
corrupt preferences.
