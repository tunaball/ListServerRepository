On Jan 7, 2011, at 8:31 PM, Ray wrote:

> What would be the easiest way to move an existing playlist and the associated songs/files to the new library?

I move my iTunes library to an external hard drive to make more room on my internal drive. I just copied the library to the new drive, configured iTunes to go to the new location, then deleted the old library.

You change the library location in Preferences/Advanced.
