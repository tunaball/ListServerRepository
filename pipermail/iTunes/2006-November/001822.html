On Nov 13, 2006, at 12:55 AM, Mark Swanson wrote:

> I am using 10.4.8 and iTunes 7.0.1	on my Quicksilver G4.  I create  
> a playlist and burn it to audio CD with text enabled.  When I put  
> the CD in my iBook G4 (same system and iTunes) the CD shows up  
> displaying only track  numbers.  When I put it back in the  
> quicksilver, it show all information that was listed in the  
> playlist.  The mp3s are from my CDs I ripped into iTunes.  Why  
> doesn't the playlist info (title, artist, album) show up on my iBook?

Because that info isn't written to the CD. However, it is stored in a  
database file on the computer that burned the CD, which is why you  
see it there.
