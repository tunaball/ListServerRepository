Brian,

One thought I had would be to open a playlist and select all the songs 
in that playlist and drag n drop them from iTunes into a folder.  This 
will copy all the files into that folder.  You can do that for each 
playlist you want on the CD and then burn them.  Once you have burned 
them, you can delete the folders and copied files.
