Pod 2 Pod (1.0)
A tool that will help make an exact clone of an iPod to another 
iPod--great for those with 2 iPods. P2P will also allow you to backup 
your single (or both) iPods to your hard drive, in case of theft or 
loss. Restore the backup to any iPod!
