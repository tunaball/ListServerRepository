On May 19, 2005, at 10:27 PM, Terry Pogue wrote:

> That fixed it Kirk. thanks! Now how do I prevent that from  
> happening in the future? I listen to audiobooks so I have several  
> cds in a book. Is there a way to fix all of them at once in stead  
> of needed to "get info" on each individual disc?

Select a bunch of tracks, then File > Get Info. You'll see the Volume  
Adjustment at the bottom. Set it in the center, if it's not, then  
check the box next to it; that will change all the selected tracks to  
"normal" volume.
