>Go into iTunes preferences, and see if Sound Check is enabled in the  
>Audio tab in the preferences. Sound Check tries to make the volume  
>equal on all songs, so you don't have to change the volume for each  
>song; it lowers the volume on certain songs, and raises it on others.  
>Turn if off if you want your tracks to have the normal volume they  
>were recorded at.
