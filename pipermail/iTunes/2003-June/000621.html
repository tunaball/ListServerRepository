buy.com has nice ones.

I usually copy them to the clipboard, scale them to 300x300  with 
Graphic Converter, save them as "web ready" .jpg files at reasonable 
quality, then drag them into iTunes.  That seams to keep the size small 
(20K or so).

Never drag straight from a WWW browser or it will put uncompressed 
image data in there seriously increasing the size of your music files.
