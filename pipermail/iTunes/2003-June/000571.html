AFAIK, the only real issue is whether the hub is powered or not. A USB 
port only puts out limited power. Some USB peripherals require more 
power than the USB port can supply (or in the case of a hub, multiple 
peripherals may need more than it can supply.)

If you have power hungry peripherals, look for a self-powered Hub.
