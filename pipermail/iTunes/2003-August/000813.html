On Thursday, August 14, 2003, at 10:55  AM, Jim Wickman wrote:

> How do I remove a song/songs from a Smart Playlist?  I know I've done 
> it before, but I've forgotten the magic ritual.

In any playlist, hold down the Option key and hit delete to move files 
located in the Music folder to the trash. If the files aren't located 
in the Music folder, hit Cmd + R to locate them in the Finder.
