The easiest (and most limited) way is to have the video/artwork viewer
open in the bottom left of iTunes' window and just drag and drop an
image into there to add artwork for the currently playing track.

Better to do a get info on the songs in question and add the artwork
there. Just click inside the artwork frame and paste the image in
(after having copied it from an Amazon webpage, for example), or drag
and drop it in.
