The settings allow you to customize your podcast downloads.  "Download  
the most recent" does just
that.  "Download all" is for podcasts that may issue multiple podcasts  
between downloads.  "Do nothing"
is the setting I use for podcasts that issue multiple podcasts a day  
where I want to manually select
podcasts from the list.  I download 20 podcasts a day, the default  
setting allows me to set most on
"Download most recent" and then go and individually change those that  
fit the other two types.

You can delete archive podcasts that you aren't interested in by  
select them and then hit delete while
holding down the option button.
