If the click is a couple milli-seconds of track 2 (for example) at the end
of track one, just select the song, press command-I and click the options
tab. shave off a bit of time in the "stop time" field.

Another thing to try is to play with the crossfade settings, in
preferences, under effects.

Hope this helps,
