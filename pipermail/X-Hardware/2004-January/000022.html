I have done this on a powerbook and it works i am not sure it will 
work for you in an iBook but it wouldn't hurt to try

shutdown your powerbook. wait 10 seconds then hold down the three 
corner buttons. that should be function, control and shift and the 
power button at the same time. you hold it down for 3-5 seconds. you 
wait 5 seconds and then hit the power button. the screen will take 
longer than usual to turn. just remember to plug in your powerbook 
before doing this.

this also works if a powerbook wont start up.

I hope it helps
