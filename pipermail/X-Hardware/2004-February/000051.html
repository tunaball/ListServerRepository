Hi,
I have that modem and it has caused huge problems on my AGP Graphics G4 
running OSX, but being a starving artist I've kept it and worked around 
the problems. I always make sure to unplug the USB cable before shutting 
down the computer, and I always start up at least one application before 
I plug the cable in after starting the computer.  Doing these things and 
repairing permissions with Disk Utility at least once every couple of 
days seems to take care of the problems.  I also occasionally boot up on 
a second OSX partition and run Disk Warrior.
I don't think that Diamond even exists any longer, but if anyone has 
newer drivers I'd be happy to give them a try.
