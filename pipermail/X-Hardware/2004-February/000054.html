You must distinguish between the capabilities of the platform (hardware 
+ OS) and the functions of the application. In this instance, it's not 
a matter of whether the Mac can do the same thing as XP (it can), but 
of whether or not you can find a Mac application which does the same 
thing as Visionary. I don't know if there is any, but you might get 
more informed replies if you posted your request elsewhere. You could 
also try <http://www.versiontracker.com/>, <http://www.macupdate.com/>, 
as well as search the 'net for groups of Mac users in the legal 
profession.
