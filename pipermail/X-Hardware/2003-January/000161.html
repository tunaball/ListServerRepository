> Has anybody any idea, what I could do against it? Without spending 
> again so much money?

You mentioned that you have headphones, but have you thought about 
purchasing noise canceling ones?  I have not heard the wind tunnel G4s 
in action, yet, but I have quite a bit of ambient noise in my office.  
My office doubles as the network server closet, so with about a dozen 
1U rack-mount linux servers (various chipsets, single and dual), plus a 
free-standing in-room AC unit (for when they turn the building AC off 
at 10pm), plus a couple of network switches, a DLT tape drive, my DP 1G 
G4 (non-wind-tunnel), and whatever other PC or Mac I may have on to 
work on, it gets a bit loud.  I purchased a pair of Panasonic noise 
canceling headphones for about US$40 and they _really_ help cut down on 
the noise, even if you're not listening to music.  You can spend as 
much as you want on noise-canceling head/ear phones; Sony makes a nice 
in-ear set for about US$300, but my pair work just fine and are 
comfortable for me to wear for 8+ hours a day/5 days a week.  They 
won't make everything silent, by any means, but they _really_ help.  
Just a thought.
