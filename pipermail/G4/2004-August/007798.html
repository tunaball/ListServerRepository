The CD is NOT a SCSI drive. Try the CD drive by it's self first. After 
you get to booting on the CD to work, go for the HD. You will need to 
Zap the PRAM before trying to boot to the CD or the HD. ZAP= 
Opt+Cammand+P+R.......hold until you hear chim at least four times. Then 
hold the C key for a CD boot.

Test the PRAM battery before anything else. It is about the age that the 
PRAM battery can go bad.
