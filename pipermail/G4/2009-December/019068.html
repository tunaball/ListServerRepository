Amazing or not but it's just standard.
The Yikes, Sawtooth and GE have 4 RAM slots and can take 2 GB.
The Cube, DA and QuickSilvers have 3 RAM slots and can take 1.5 GB.
Therefore the DA and QS have 4 PCI slots, the first 3 have 3 PCI slots.
