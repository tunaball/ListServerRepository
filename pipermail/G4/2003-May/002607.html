Jim, I had a similar problem with a 120 Gig Western Digital HD in an 
ADS Firewire/USB2 enclosure. Messing around in OS 10.2.6 trying to 
partition the drive, I managed to "lose" all but 32 Gigs of disk 
space. I went through all the shenanigans below, but finally pulled 
the drive out of the enclosure and connected it up inside my G4 
Sawtooth as a slave drive and reformatted it with Hard Disk Tool Kit 
3.5 in OS 9.2.1. Magically the drive popped back up to the 117 odd 
Gigs that it originally had. I then put everything back the way it 
was and backed up all my drives (two SCSI partitions and one IDE) to 
the Firewire external using Retrospect Express 5.
Can you boot your G4 933 in OS 9. If so, the above route is the way to go.
