I have a G3 equipped 9500 running Jaguar. Its possible on the 8500 but 2
things must be done.
1. buy and install a G3 or G4 processor upgrade.
2. install XPostFacto on your machine (freeware).

Also you'll need lots of ram, 512 or better for optimum performance. And a
hard drive of 4 or more gigs for a roomy install.

Subscribe to the PCI powermac list for further details and help.
