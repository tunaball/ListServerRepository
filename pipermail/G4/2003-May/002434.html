i got close to 2x the performance when i moved my 120 GB WD SE (8mb 
cache) drive to an ata133 card (acard) i now have a pair of them on the 
card, and use them for DV. i believe they can be booted off of even on 
the card....

in short, yes you will see increased performance booting off of the WD 
drive on an ata 133 card.

sandor
