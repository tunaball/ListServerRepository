Really? This Apple document
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106712> (which, IMHO,
assumes Apple's standard setup for their machines; i.e., a single startup
volume) implies that repairing permissions from the startup
volume/drive/partition is the preferred method:

"For Mac OS X 10.2 or later, open Disk Utility (/Applications/Utilities/).
Select your Mac OS X startup volume in the left-hand column of the Disk
Utility window, then click the First Aid tab. Click the Repair Disk
Permissions button. "
