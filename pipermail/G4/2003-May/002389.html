I have not tried it myself but, many people with ata 66 IDE controllers have used hard drives larger than 120GB.  Because the ATA 66 controller can not address more than 128GB, the drive will only appear to be 128GB.  The extra storage (128GB+ to 180GB) would be unused. 

If you then added a ATA 100 or 120 controller card, or upgraded to a computer with ATA 100 or 120 controller the entire 180GB would be available to use.
