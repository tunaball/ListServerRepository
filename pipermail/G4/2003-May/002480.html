IIRC, Macworld magazine had an article on the 133 cards last year and the
reviewer said that, in effect, the cards were more or less the same: i.e.,
either the same card (Acard, I believe, rebadged under the other companies'
names) or the same performance. The only differences being, IIRC, price and
warranty: most offered 1 or 2 year warranties, while SIIG (probably an
Acard) offered 5. 

The SIIG is also the cheapest. OWC has it for $75 with the 5 year warranty.
