During the Apple G3 PowerBook power adaptor exchange program, I exchanged the 
part by re-using the shipping envelope that Apple mailed the adaptor in to me.

The shipping label was a two-part label with the RMA information and address 
under the label with my address.

I would feel that an "exchange" program was just that, a program to swap a 
defective part for a replacement part, and not a free part give-away. But, I 
haven't participated in this exchange program, so I have no positive evidence 
either way and would only go with my sense of obligation to return the part I 
received an exchange for.

It would be nice to see the actual mailing labels and shipping instructions 
that come with the exchange parts.

Just my $.02 worth.
