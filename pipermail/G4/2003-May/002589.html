> BootCD.dmg
>
> Then make sure your application is registered first. then run BootCD  
> and it will make a boot cd and ask for what programs you want to put  
> into it and places them into doc in image. Then burn the image in disc  
> image when done. Log into Emergency boot cd with root/bootcd.
