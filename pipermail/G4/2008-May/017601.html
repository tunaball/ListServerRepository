Hello all,

as many of you would know, the MDD G4 Power Macs had a reputation for 
being rather noisy - so much so that Apple initiated (after pressure 
from many disgruntled users) a program to replace the power supply and 
CPU fan in offending machines.

I have a G4 MDD 1.25 GHz DP FW400, and I tried to use it for a few days 
- I work as a sole trader in a fairly quiet room in the house - I found 
the noise to be so intolerable that I went back to my G4 933 MHz, which 
is what I’m using now. My MDD has the original PS and fan.

I read on the Internet that most people reported the replacement fan 
and PS made a real difference in noise levels. So, if anyone out there 
has one of these revised power supplies or both fan and power supply, 
please let me know. I am keen to pick one up. I believe the replacement 
PS was marked “REV B”, as opposed to the “REV A” of the original.

Thanks
Paul
