Fabio:

All  you need to get if you do not already have it is an Airport Card  
for the Quicksilver.  I hope someone else can tell you exactly which   
card you should look for in the used market.  I do not see one at the  
Apple Store on-line.  You do not need a base station or Airport  
Express or Extreme unless you also decide to go wireless to the  
internet.

On your MacBook Pro, in Finder Help, type "Airport."  Then you will  
see the details you need to know, plus more.  You want to create a  
"computer-to-computer network."  It may be best to do this part on the  
new machine.  I recommend you choose the "128-bit WEP" for security.   
On the Quicksilver, you want "Joining an AirPort network."
