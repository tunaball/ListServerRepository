I have some (developing) interest in ProTools.

I have an Audiomedia3 pci soundcard.

Digidesign are VERY specific about what
hardware/software is compatible.

Somehow I don't think they have tested your old
hardware with a CPU upgrade!

If you look on their website you will find lots of
info about what s/w, os, hardware combinations are
approved.

I am using a G4 dual 450MHz tower, with ATTO UL3S and
SCSI drives, for this reason. I'm running 10.3.9 but
had to experiment with versions of quicktime and
iTunes to make it still work with the Audiomedia 3 and
ProTools LE 6.2.3.

I cannot now upgrade anything without breaking my
working combination! A good example of LEM, cos it's
still a very usable tool, which would cost a lot to
replace!
