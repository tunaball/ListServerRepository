A power conditioner is different from a UPS system. There is no  
battery back up at all.

It is still a benefit to run since it will stop noise, surges, power  
spikes, over voltages, and most under voltage conditions. It will also  
help protect from most lightning strikes. Sola makes some of the best  
power conditioners. As long as you have it, I would say use it.
