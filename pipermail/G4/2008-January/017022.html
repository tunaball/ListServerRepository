I don't know about that particular product, but my computers seem
(completely un-scientific; just my impressions) to be more stable and
reliable since I started using UPSes, which include power
conditioning.  I started with an APC Back-UPS XS 1500 and have since
picked up a couple APC Smart-UPS 1500s.  They are all still in service
and have all been completely trouble-free.  I liked them enough that I
picked up smaller units for my mom and sister, too.
