SATA drives also use much smaller connectors and skinnier cables, which
makes them nicer to deal with in cramped computer cases.

You can buy an SATA card for your G4 MDD that will allow you to use SATA
drives, but you won't be able to use those drives if you don't buy an SATA
card.
