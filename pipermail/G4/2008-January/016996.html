On our 2002 QS dual 1gig we have 5 hard drives - 2 ATA, 3 SCSI, two 
digital monitors - a17" apple studio & a 19" viewsonic, and we sleep it 
using the Apple monitor any time we leave it for more than the 15 
minutes in which it will sleep automatically.  That's the setup the man 
who bought the computer new used and he said he had no problems with 
it.  He sold it to us because he was upgrading to an intel mac.  Just 
my 2 cents for what it's worth.
