I too have a QS2002/800. Most probably when you try to the install  
you will get a message that says that your computer is unsuitable for  
this install of tiger (or words to that effect).

You might want to try to get around this by installing the OS onto an  
external drive (using the MBP) and then installing the external drive  
into the QS.

Will it work? Perhaps, it depends on just how specific the installs  
really are. Almost certainly your QS2002 has no need for trackpad  
software...

I have install discs for a G4 ibook and I will  experiment with this  
idea once I get a spare HD to play with.
