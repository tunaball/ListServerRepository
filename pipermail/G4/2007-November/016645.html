Jeff,
	You only need the keyboard attached to the MDD while actually 
booting, so you could disconnect your keyboard from the G5, attach it 
to the MDD, boot the MDD into Firewire Target, then disconnect the 
keyboard from the MDD and reattach to the G5. Since you don't have 
"video out" from the MDD, you will not be able to tell by seeing the 
Firewire symbol on your MDD attached monitor when the process has 
occurred; however, it has been my experience that the process occurs 
fairly quickly, so if you boot the MDD and keep the "T" button 
depressed for a minute, that should be more than enough time for the 
process to occur (providing it does at all).
