 From what I have found out your Mirror Drive Door G4 only supports 
ATA100 which means the maximum amount of disk drive it will support 
without special software is 128 Gb.

If your platform supported 180 Gb drives prior to installation of your 
new drive, then you should have the software somewhere on one of the 
old drives that allows drive larger than 128 Gb.

I don't remember the name of the application, however perhaps someone 
else will know. Once you find this application and put it back into 
your startup path then you should be able to see the full drive.
