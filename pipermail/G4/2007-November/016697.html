Going from a G4 to G5 Gets you more than a faster CPU.  You get a 
faster Frontside bus, ability to upgrade to more RAM, the odds that 
it will Run OS X.6.

So if you can afford it go for the G5.  I would recommend staying 
away for any G5 that is water cooled as they are showing problems.
