Open the outer cover and use a large sized paper clip to put in the tiny 
hole on the CD drive. Push in with the paper clip to get the CD drawer 
to open. You have to bend the paper clip to a straight shape for it to work.

The large CDs are too cheap to use anything else; 15 to 20cents each.
