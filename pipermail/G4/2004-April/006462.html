You should be able to copy your music over and authorize your machine 
to play anything you have bought from the iTMS. Anything you have 
ripped will not have a problem, as it has no Digital Restrictions 
Management embedded in it. Make sure when you burn the disk that you do 
it as an ISO 9660 disk, since Windows will not read Mac format disks. 
Mac Help says that Disk Burner creates hybrid disks that Windows can 
read, but I don't know if iTunes does. Toast will work also.
