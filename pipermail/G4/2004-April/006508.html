My two cents, for what they're worth.  I would suggest going for a 
machine with a faster processor.  When doing video, the greatest 
amount of time is rendering.  The faster the processor, the quicker 
the end result.  Also, as was suggested by others, you will need a 
DVD burner, lots of RAM, and a large fast hard drive, 7200 RPM 
minimum, at least 120 GB.  And if you're using Final Cut Pro or 
Express or Adobe Premier, a second monitor is recommended.

If you have space constraints, I'd look at a new G4 eMac with a large 
HD and DVD burner.  Then you get a brand new machine.

Good luck with your project.
