Adaptec 2906 SCSI Card works fine all the way to OSX 10.3 with the new 
driver at Adaptec.com  .  I used it in my G4 400Mhz Sawtooth (OS9) and 
now in my G4 dual 1.25 Ghz (OSX 10.2 and 10.3).  I was pleasantly 
surprised, to say the least.

They are not expensive now, even brand new.  But I am sure you could 
get one on ebay used.
