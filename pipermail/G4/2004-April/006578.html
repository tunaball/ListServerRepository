And you could download a program called Pacifist from 
versiontracker.com and run it to adjust your pre-binding.  Pre-bindings 
are like a short cut for the system to launch programs more 
efficiently, but run your Disk Utility first, from your install CD and 
repair your disk untill it reports no errors and then repair permisions 
and then run Pacifist for the pre-binding.
