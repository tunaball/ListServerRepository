MicroMat's TechTool Pro v4 will do the job for you. Cost: around $100.

If you have a large drive, be prepared to wait for a couple of hours or 
more for the defragmentation to be completed.

I run the defrag portion of TechTool when I find more than 15% 
fragmentation on my drives. It does help to run programs a bit faster.
