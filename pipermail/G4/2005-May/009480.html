I just bought a Belkin wireless keyboard and optical mouse combo 
(particulars below). Basically it seems to be pretty well designed, 
However, there is an issue which this list has suggested isn't all that 
uncommon with third-party peripherals: the sleep function no longer 
works properly.

Now when it's time for the machine to nod off, rather than blinking 
amber, the light stays green. The monitor goes into standby mode. 
Pressing keys, even the one called 'Wake Up' has no effect. Naturally, 
pressing the green button turns off the computer. I know I can disable 
sleep and do everything manually, but I was hoping someone might know a 
work-around.

BTW, Belkin doesn't support Mac very well in this instance. The 
accompanying CD is almost exclusively for Windows users; we Mac people 
have to download a driver from their website. There is NO Read-Me 
document with it. The local (Australian) tech support team don't know 
from Macs and seemed surprised to learn this unit was being marketed to 
us. Two e-mails to Belkin's US tech address and one to Sales have gone 
unanswered.

I'd appreciate any suggestions.
