The best card you can get at retail for a G4 is the Radeon 9800 Pro  
128 MB. Anything newer/faster works only in a G5. If you don't want  
to spend $250 or so, OWC has the Radeon 9600 from the G5 that has  
been modified to work in the G4 for about $120. I have one on order  
myself. These are also the only two retail cards for a G4 that  
support Core Video.
