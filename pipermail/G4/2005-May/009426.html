On Apr 30, 2005, at 23:01, Mel Krewall wrote:

> All your Address Book info is in ~/Library/Application 
> Support/AddressBook where ~ is your home folder.

Right. In addition, here's how you can find out this type of 
information:

- Launch Activity Monitor
- Launch Address Book
- Switch back to Activity Monitor immediately and locate Address Book 
in the Processes list, then select it
- Double-click on it, or click the Inspect button
- Click the Open Files tab
- Examine the open files list; the files are given with their full paths
