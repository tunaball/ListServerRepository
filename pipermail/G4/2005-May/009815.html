This issue is a pain in the ass on the G3; as many things are. You  
need a VGA adapter that has dip switches. Many of these have markings  
to tell you how to set the switches for certain resolutions and for  
Multi-Res for certain monitor sizes. Here is the crunch: The G3 is  
different than other Macs in regard to the jumpers. Monitor companies  
like Viewsonic can tell you what to use for the G3 but companies like  
Compaq would not know the right settings. I would go to the Google  
Mac and search for G3 VGA settings. Finding out who really made the  
monitor would help. If all this fails you will have to try setting  
until one works.

Also, A smart monitor person can probably give an answer if the have  
the pin-out of the mac and the monitor; I am not the smart guy.
