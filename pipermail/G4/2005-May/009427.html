Sorry, for the confusion I have an Addresses folder in Library and I

File-Find in the Finder. A search for "address" would have helped you 
find what your were looking for. Keep in mind that we are using a 
Macintosh and that most files have a reasonable humane filename. So, 
searching in most cases will help you find what you are looking for.
