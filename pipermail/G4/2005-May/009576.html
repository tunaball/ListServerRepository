On 14 May, 2005, at 12:48, Mark Laurint wrote:

I have a G4-400 graphite running 10.2.8. There are 4 international 
preference buttons in my system preference panel. They seem to be 
multiplying! Does anyone know how to get rid of them?

It could be possible that you have erroneously created copies of that 
preference panel. Do a "Get Info" on each to see if it is a copy by 
looking at the creation date.

Save the chronologically oldest version and dump the rest and do a 
restart. They should not come back. If they do, you have done something 
else that needs attention to.
