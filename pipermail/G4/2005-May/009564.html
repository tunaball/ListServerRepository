Richard Dion --

On my PowerBook G4, 10.2.8, System Preferences ==> Energy Saver ==>
(tab) Options there is the available checkbox:

    Other Options
        --
       |  | Restart automatically after a power failure
        --

See if that is checked and uncheck it.  Then dismiss the System 
Preferences/Energy Saver window (red dismiss lozenge in upper left
corner) and try an ordinary "Shut Down..." from the Apple window..

Clearly, non-server-intended Mac's, such as my PowerBook also seem
to have that option.
