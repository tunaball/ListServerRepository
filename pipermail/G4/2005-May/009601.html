On 17 May, 2005, at 15:32, Derek Gross wrote:

Also, concerning the disk burning, what does everyone elses computer 
say because mine says "not supported" however, I always burn through 
toast.  My drive is a pioneer cd/dvd rw and it works great. Does anyone 
know what this means?

It means that your particular burner is not supported for burning from 
the OS in iTunes or iDVD. You have to use a third-party program like 
Toast to burn with that burner. If you had an Apple burner with the 
Apple ROM installed on the circuit board, it would be "supported" by 
your OS.
