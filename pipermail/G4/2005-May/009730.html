Also check the pins on the monitor cable to be sure there are none 
broken.  I had a similar situation happen to me and, upon close 
examination, discovered that one of the pins had broken off and was 
still lodged in the PC's VGA port.  So every time I moved the monitor 
back to the PC, it worked fine; try it on the Mac... no go.  It was 
very confusing until I found that broken pin!
