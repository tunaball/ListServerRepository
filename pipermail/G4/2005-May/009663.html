I suggest you find a store that carries Epson printers. They make 3 printers 
(I believe they are RX200, 300 & 600) that print directly on the CD/DVD as 
well as the usual paper stuff. It has a CD holder that inserts into the printer 
and moves the CD under the printheads. I have a RX200 ($99.00 when I bought 
mine)  and it works in OS9. I haven't tried OSX yet. I think it's much better 
than having the disc get stuck in a slot load player. I tried putting a label on 
CD's, but found out they don't play for beans in CD players that use 
cartridges.
