Have you updated Adobe Acrobat Reader recently? The latest version 
seems to open pdf's in a browser window (in my case Safari) instead of 
downloading them and then opening them in the stand alone program.

You can force the download by control-clicking the file icon, which 
brings up a window where one of the options is "download linked file"
