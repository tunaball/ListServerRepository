at a piece of software called patchburn. I used it on my G4 with my
Lite-On DVD burner. Before patchburn I couldn't burn from iTunes, I
had to burn from Toast after I ran patchburn I was able to burn from
insided iTunes. I haven't tried iDVD yet though. YMMV
