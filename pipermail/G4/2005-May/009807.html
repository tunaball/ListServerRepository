Apple has not made a CRT in couple years. A Mitsubishi Diamond Pro or  
a Pro model Viewsonic would be a top notch CRT. Sony Pro line is good  
but too over priced and not quite as good as those mentioned. I have  
all the monitors I have mentioned and prefer the Pro version  
Viewsonics. These monitors have much better warranties than Apple.  
The Studio Display and the LaCie blue series are Mitsubishi Diamond  
Pro CRTs.

You can get these great CRTs dirt cheap right now. Mitsubishi has  
stopped making CRTs and others will follow. Mitsubishi Diamond Pros  
are still available right now.
