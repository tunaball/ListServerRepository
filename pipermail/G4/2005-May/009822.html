The reason that the DVR-109's are being discounted is to clear the 
inventory for the new DVR-110's.  The 110's will also be dual layer 
(DL) capable, but will do DL -R as well as DL +R.  According to what 
I read, the 109's will only do DL +R.  With single layer, the -R 
technology appears to be  compatible with more players than the +R 
technology so it will be worth it to me to wait around for the 110s 
to emerge on the marketplace in June or July. I can easily wait a few 
more months after that for the 110s to come down in price a bit, 
because I already have a DVR-105 in my Quicksilver as well as a 
DVR-109 in an external enclosure.  Once I get the 110, I will swap 
out the 105.
