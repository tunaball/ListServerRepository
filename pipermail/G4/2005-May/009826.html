There are a number of things that can cause the sleep problem, PCI  
cards, USB acessorie, video cards, processor upgrades,etc.

You can go through them one at time removing and testing sleep  
function or just set the power preferences to never sleep the system.


That is what I did, display sleeps disks spin down but system never  
sleeps
