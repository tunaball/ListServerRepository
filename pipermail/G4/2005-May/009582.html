On May 15, 2005, at 11:42, Marvin Wilson wrote:

> I installed the OSX Tiger software on my G4 Dual processor.[...]
> When it restarted there were errors all over the place.
>
> Here are the errors on start up:
>
> Panic (cpu 0 caller 0x002DE020): unable to find driver for this 
> platform:
> “PowerMac3, 3”.

It says it can't find the correct drivers for a DP G4. It may be 
because your disk had a problem prior to installation. You should 
always check the disk prior to installing, unless you intend to 
reformat the disk first -- any directory problem is going to be made 
worse during the installation.

Alternatively, it may mean that you installed, rather than the public 
release of Tiger, an early beta version which lacked support for your 
specific machine.
