I had a lot of problems. First there was some previous file that  
Tiger didn't like. It was Quick something. Maybe someone can remember  
the name. Tiger was very slow until I removed those files.

Next, I was getting constant CUPSD errors. I had to reinstall the  
print drivers.

Also there was a memory leak with Safari and Widgets. With 1.5 Gig of  
memory installed, I would be down to 100 Meg free and everything was  
slowing down. Safari would use 300 meg and Widgets were at 120 Meg.  
The Tiger update of 10.4.1 seemed to stop the Safari leak but not the  
Widgets.


I recommend running Permissions Fix right after upgrading. Then, use  
Console and check your console.log and system.log for errors. Use  
Activity Monitor (in Utilities Folder) to check free RAM.

Try going to http://www.apple.com/support/tiger/ for answers.
