I always leave my MDD asleep.  That way I can wake it from my iBook and print to
the MDD attached printer or I can get to the files I need.

I use the open source program WOL (Wake On Lan) to wake it up.

Every so often it would crash on wake up.  So I bought a UPS.

I *NEVER* turn my iBook off.  I always use sleep mode.  I routinely go more than
a month between boots.
