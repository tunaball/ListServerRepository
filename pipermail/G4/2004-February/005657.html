I use Retrospect and have for years. I back up all my Macs (and two 
PCs, forgive me) with it. I usually do the following

a) I backup the important folders in my individual Macs into large 
files on the central backup computer (with lots of hard drives) -- 
backing up the Documents, Desktop, Library folders and so on of the 
users I need the files for. I use the normal scheduled backup feature

b) just for extra safety, I use the duplicate feature to duplicate my 
entire home folder to other computers, since hard drives are so big 
these days there's always an iMac or eMac or G4 with extra room on 
the hardd rive
