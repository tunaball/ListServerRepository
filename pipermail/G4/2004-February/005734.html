There are no special problems with SCSI in either Jaguar or Panther. 
The issue is that, at the consumer level, there is no advantage 
whatever in using SCSI and quite a few disadvantages (more difficult to 
configure, more troublesome external cables). In your instance, first 
you must make sure that your card supports LVD devices -- you might 
damage either the devices or the card or both if it doesn't.
