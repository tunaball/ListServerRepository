While I can't say for sure regarding your SCSI card; I would add a word of
warning about how you mount it. I made the mistake of mounting my old SCSI
drive from my old machine into my new Quicksilver G4 a couple of years ago.
The mount for the original ATA that came with it seemed ideal for mounting
the old SCSI above it, thereby saving me the trouble of moving the old drive
mount. Unfortunately these new high speed ATA drives generate quite a bit of
heat and I ended up frying my old drive.
So a word of warning; mount your SCSI drive seperately from your ATA drives.
Strangely enough, mounting 2 ATAs one above the other doesn't seem to cause
problems. I replaced my old SCSI with another ATA and both have been working
well for over 2 years.
