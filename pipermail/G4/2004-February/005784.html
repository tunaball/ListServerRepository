I'm surprised nobody has mentioned USB VideoBus by Belkin.  It provides 
acceptable quality in both video and audio and has both RCA jacks and 
S-Video input.   It comes with its own software CD and is simple to 
use--just plug it in.  The window is drag to resize and you can use the 
Strata VideoShop software on the CD to record A/V.

Sure there are higher end products out there.  How many bucks do you want to 
spend?  On my budget I opt for something that works well and has a lower 
price.  This one qualifies.
