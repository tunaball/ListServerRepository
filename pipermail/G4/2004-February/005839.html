I like these:  
http://www.buffalotech.com/wireless/products/airstation/WBRG54.html
or
http://www.envynews.com/index.php?ID=445&page=1

The reason being I can print wireless to the Apple Laserwriter 16/600  
and the Xante we have on the wired network. I had a hard time with the  
Linksys even with assigning a static IP to both printers, finally gave  
up and traded/purchased the Airstation for a PC friend and we've both  
been happy ever since. Had another Mac client we setup with the SMC and  
he also had an older 16/600 that his 4 wireless stations are able to  
print to.....If you don't have the need to print to a wired network  
printer then I guess it really doesn't matter because I've found they  
pretty much all work.
