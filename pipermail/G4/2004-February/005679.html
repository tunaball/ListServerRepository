As the last writer (at bottom) suggests - It is BAD to mix PC100 and PC 
133 RAM together.  It is supposed to work but it does screw up 
sometimes.

  And from personal experience, I had more weird corruptions of my OS 
when mixed but as soon as I put all one (PC100 in tower and PC133 in 
cube) or the other in the computer things ran faster and less problems. 
The tower is OS9.2 and Cube is OSX 10.26
