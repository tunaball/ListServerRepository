Renita,

Why don't you just let the PC record the TV shows that you want to 
watch, and then watch them on your mac? Just make sure that video that 
the Pc records in is capable of being played on your macs using 
quicktime. So it depends on what you want to do, if you don't need to 
watch live tv on your mac then use the pc to record the shows you want 
to watch. If you need to watch Live TV on your mac's screen then go 
with one of the tuners that are connected to the Mac. I would recommend 
something from elgato simply because their products are specifically 
designed for recording TV. The formac stuff seems to be made to do 
more, Video, audio input, etc. So it depends on what you want to do.
