Hello,

You need to get the grey colored disks that shipped with your MDD  
from the factory. Retail editions of OS 9 will not work, I found out  
the hard way.

I understand that you can order these MDD specific disks from Apple,  
but I don't know what the price is.

The proper MDD specific grey colored disks will put OS 10.3 and 9.2  
on one drive, then you can update up to 10.4.11.

You cannot install from black colored or white colored disks and run  
both OS 10 and OS 9. I found out the hard way.

Hope this helps,

Scot "Bruce" Nakamura
