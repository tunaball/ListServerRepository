If the new G4's video output was set to a resolution or frequency that the Samsung would not support, this would result in the behavior that you've seen. Click on the Apple symbol/System Preferences/Displays, and try "Detect Displays". Or, try reducing the Refresh Rate to 60 Hz; that should work with about any display. Let us know if this fixes it.
