Howard, if lightening got into your G4 and all that you lost was your 
eNet port then consider yourself very lucky. The ethernet interface is 
controlled by one chip and unless you have the special tools that are 
needed to replace the chip without doing other damage to the 
mother-board you will need to send your G4 into a repair shop, Apple 
would be best even though they will charge the most.

Your best bet, if you have an open slot is to purchase an ethernet PCI 
card.

You should also find out why the telephone company and/or your cable 
company hasn't provided you with lightening protection. Lightening 
protection is provide ether by a gas tube that shorts when the voltage 
rises above the ionization point or by a carbon block which acts the 
same way. The gas tube reacts faster and carbon can develop a short 
path which shortens there life.
