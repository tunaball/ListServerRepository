I, just this morning did a speed test on my ComCast Broadband 
connection to the internet and the results were 6.8 Mbits therefore 
wanting or needing anything faster than the speed of a 802.11b wireless 
interface is a moot point.

802.11g would only be needed if I were communicating with other 
computers that are on my LAN, which there are none.

So you really need to be sure that you need the faster speed before 
committing to the extra cost.
