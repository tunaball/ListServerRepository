Although they go bad some times, the cause can be internal. Before  
doing anything else check with a voltmeter if the charger is sending  
the appropriate voltage. (If you can't do that, Im sure you can have  
it done in a Radio Shack store).

The voltage its posted in the back of the charger and it can vary a  
few volts with no problem, if its too low, then you have found the  
cause cheaply.
