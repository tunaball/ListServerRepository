I agree but rather than complain to Apple, I gave up on Mail early on 
and switched to Gyazmail which is much more satisfactory for my 
purposes.  Gyazmail allows for subject "exists" or "doesn't exist" in 
the rules which should solve this problem.

However, in my case it isn't necessary as any mail from someone not in 
my address book goes into the spam folder and I have a keyboard 
shortcut to delete messages in a single step after I take a quick look 
to make sure that there is nothing I want to keep.  I let Earthlink 
filter out about 75% of my spam so that I never see it and after a year 
or so I don't know of any missed emails.

Of course I recognize that my system won't work for everyone, such as 
those that receive many messages from new correspondents but I have 
used it for years with no problems.
