I'm not familiar with OS 9 helper. However, since you can't boot into to
those other volumes, but can boot into OS 9 from some volume, then boot
into it and drag & drop the System Folder from the bootable OS 9 volume
onto those other ones that don't boot now, open Startup Disk, select one of
them, and restart. That should fix your problem.
