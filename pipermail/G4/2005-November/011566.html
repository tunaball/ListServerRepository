On Nov 12, 2005, at 10:48 AM, Bob Schmidt wrote:

> This is probably a stupid question, but here goes. I have a G4 AGP  
> Graphics
> running 10.4.3 with 512mb of ram. I was looking in the system  
> profiler and
> noticed that this computer has pc100 sdram. Can I take pc100 sdram  
> from a pc
> and use it in this mac?

Yes, it most likely will work given that it's decent quality memory,  
but since you already have 512 MB you probably won't notice any  
difference. It has been shown repeatedly by various magazines and web  
sites that 512 MB is the knee of the performance curve for current  
OS's and applications. On the other hand if it's just going to waste  
in a PC you no longer use it can't hurt.
