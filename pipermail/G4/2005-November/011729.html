> 1. Will Disk First Aid work on later systems or should one upgrade this

Your Disk First Aid, original with your QuickSilver, sounds like the OS 
9 version.  In OS X, First Aid is part of Disk Utility.  Open Disk 
Utility and find the First Aid tab or button, depending on how Jaguar 
does it.

> 2. Should I upgrade the Diskwarrior to version 3.1 or 3.3 or should I
> buy a new version altogether? The new version would seem to be bootable
> and that would be nice.

If "bootable" is the difference, for the update price, I would go for 
it.  You would end up with v3.0.3.  I do not see info on a 3.1 or 3.3.

> 3. Any opinions on which utility/utilities/combination are better to
> have on board? (Norton, Techtool Pro, etc.)

Avoid Norton like the plague, actually there is no suitable version for 
OS X.  After updating DiskWarrior, it is adviseable to have TechTool 
Pro as well because it does many different things that DW does not.
