> Here is what you have to do to get it,  Go to "System Preferences",  
> click on "International" icon, click  on the tab labeled "Input  
> Menu". Then click the "Show input menu in menu bar". Then make sure  
> that the Keyboard layout window is scrolled to the top. Then check  
> the checkbox next to "Keyboard Viewer".  Then go to the menu bar  
> and under your keyboard flag you can then get to what we use to  
> call "Key Caps" now labeled "Show Keyboard Viewer".  I myself saved  
> the old Key Caps Application from the finder
