Second question first. Your drive door opening on startup is usually 
indicative of a USB peripheral making an incorrect call. Easiest test 
is to disconnect all USB peripherals but mouse and keyboard, then try 
and boot. If that works, open the DVD drive, insert your system DVD 
and reboot holding down the option key. You should get screen that 
has buttons for each bootable system disk. Cursur over and click on 
the system DVD and the right arrow. This procedure does not change 
the startup disk.

Make sure you fix your permissions with Apple Disk Utility before and 
after installing software.
