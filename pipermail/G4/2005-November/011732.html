Start by looking for an install log that tells you what was installed  
and where.
Look for an obvious Earthlink folder and see what it contains.  
Trashingg that folder ought to be a good start.
Also search for .plist files (preferences) for the Earthlink  
application. Trash it/them and see if that takes care of your problem.
Finally, delete the Earthlink items you trashed after the results you  
seek have been achieved with no negative behavior encountered.
