Davis,

Yes go with the deals from  newegg.com and get the Pioneer DVR DVD  
burner. I bought the Pioneer DVR -109  for my computer and used the  
drive fine with patchburn installed under mac os 10.3, but now in  
10.4 the drive is completely supported and I no longer needed  
parchburn. Note that  apple uses the DVR-109 and other pioneer drives  
in there computers, so they are most definitely compatible with  
macs.  In your situation you would just have to install patchburn to  
get it to burn, which was a painless process.

http://www.patchburn.de/
