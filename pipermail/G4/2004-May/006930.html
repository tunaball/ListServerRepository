John,

To have itunes start up when you boot or login to your mac , you can 
put itunes on the start-up items list.

here is how..

Go to system preferences, then Accounts, then click on your user name, 
and finally click on the start-up items tab. This is where you can add 
itunes to the list of things to automatically start.....
