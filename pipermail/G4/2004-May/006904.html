Steve,

I believe the limit is 120gb with the apple controller. Installing a 
second controller can remove that limit. You can also have a larger 
drive if you use firewire.  I installed a 160gb drive internally as a 
second drive and all I could see was 120gb. I installed the same drive 
in a firewire case and could see all 160gig.
