First, is this about storing CDs, or CD-R/RWs? The issue is not  
academic. I assume it's CD-Rs.

ISO is preparing a standard on the subject, which will replace ANSI  
IT9-25, but I don't think it's ready yet. IMHO, it doesn't matter much  
in the short run. In the long run, it may, but the life span of a CD-R  
is limited (less than 5 yrs), and I'd worry about dye degradation  
before altered disc geometry. However, have a look at

<http://www.kodak.com/global/en/service/publications/tib4300.jhtml>
<http://www.itl.nist.gov/div895/carefordisc/ 
CDandDVDCareandHandlingGuide.pdf>
