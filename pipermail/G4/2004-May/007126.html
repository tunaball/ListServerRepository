Good places to check for info on Macs, past and present, are  
<http://www.apple-history.com> and <http://www.theapplemuseum.com>. You  
can search them with Google, using the "site" statement.

For instance, to find what Apple History has on the Pismo, you'd search  
for

pismo site:www.apple-history.com

which will find

<http://www.apple-history.com/frames/ 
body.php?page=gallery&model=pg3sfirewire>

If you want a searchable database on your desktop, then check out  
<http://www.mactracker.ca/>.
