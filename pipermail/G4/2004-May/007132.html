Occasionally (about every two months) I copy my entire drive to a larger 
external drive, allowing it to overwrite everything.  I don't use that drive 
for anything else.  That keeps everything updated and current.  Anything you 
feel you absolutely must keep in its original form you can just put on a CD. 
  Also I have found it to be very important to know what you can do without 
so you can dump it.  I hope I'm not being too simplistic.

I don't know whether anyone has suggested this particular website, but it is 
full of info of all kinds that you can download.  http://www.everymac.com/
