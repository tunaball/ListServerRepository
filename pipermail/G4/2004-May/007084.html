Out of curiosity, I tried that workaround right before. Much to my 
surprise, it's working!

Conditions:
- The USB-Printer has to be connected to the machine running OS X.

- A driver for a LaserPrinter (I took "LaserWriter 8") must be enabled 
on the machine running Mac OS 9 in order to let Desktop Printer Utility 
do its job.

Result: A DesktopPrinter-symbol looking like a LaserWriter, but named 
"Deskjet 930C" and working without limitations.


Anyway, this solution is only close to what I, personally, wanted to 
find. I'd like to leave the printer at the OS 9-machine, sharing it 
with the one running OS X. May be, somebody has a solution for this 
scenario?
