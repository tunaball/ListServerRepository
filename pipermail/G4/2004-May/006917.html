Thanks to those who offered advice.  I finally figured
out the problem.  My PC notebook does have a firewire
port but it was not meant for use except for attaching
to a video camera--it has 4 pin not 6 pin.  To get a
fully working firewire port I must add a firewire card
that allows a 6 pin connection.  This costs about $40
apparently.  So, watch out for those 4 pin versions on
PCs.
