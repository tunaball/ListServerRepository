The 500mHz dual USB model had a 66mHz bus. The 600mHz-900mHz model G3 
dual USB iBooks had 100mHz bus. The iBook G4s have 133mHz bus.

I went from a 500mHz Pismo to a 600mHz iBook and found them to be about 
the same. I now have a 900mHz iBook G3 and it's MUCH faster than the 
Pismo was.

The benefit of the Pismo is that it'll hold 1GB of RAM and it's 
versatile and easy to work on (swap hard drive, use 2 batteries, etc.). 
The downside is that you're stuck with a Rage128 8MB video card. The 
last generation of iBook G3s had 32MB Radeon 7500 chips.

Just my $0.02.

-Hal
