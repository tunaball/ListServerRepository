You can't use the Airport Extreme card in your computer but yes you can use
the original Airport card with the AE base station with no problem. There is
however a overall slowdown to your wireless network when an 802.11B signal
is being used on a "G" network. Another option that I have read about is the
Linksys cards are plug and play and available at places like Amazon for like
$60.
