Partitioning is a personal choice. Some swear that it is mandatory 
but others don't. I look at it this way: if separate partitions are 
better than 1, then that is the way Apple would set up the computers 
from the factory. Basically it is more of a convenience thing. My old 
Bondi iMac is partitioned due to the 8 GB limit; my G4 iMac is not 
partitioned.
