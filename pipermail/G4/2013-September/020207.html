First of all, there is no need to post three almost identical messages.

Apple released five different G4 17" PowerBook models:
<http://www.everymac.com/systems/apple/powerbook_g4/index-powerbook-g4.html>
All five models can only support Mac OS up to Leopard 10.5.8.

For the current OS running on your PowerBook, from the Apple icon at the
upper left corner of the top menu bar, click "About This Mac".
