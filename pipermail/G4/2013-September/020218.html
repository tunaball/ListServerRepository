>  I have a MDD 1.25 ghz, FW400. What is the largest size drive this 
>machine will
>  support, for backup purposes?

All onboard IDE interfaces will recognise devices up to 500 GB 
capacity (which is as high as they made IDE drives anyway). There are 
four 3.5" bays, two at the rear which sit next to the CPU, to at the 
lower front.

If you want serious storage for your room-heater, look at getting an 
SiiG, ACARD or Sonnet SATA controller card for one of the PCI slots. 
You can then put up to 2 TB in each bay, and they're all bootable, 
too.
