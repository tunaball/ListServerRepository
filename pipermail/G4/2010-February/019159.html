Hi Rich

I don't think there is any charge for the album cover artwork.  However, 
you do have to be registered on Apple's system and if memory serves this 
means giving them your credit/debit card details.  I didn't want to do 
this at the time so I have never bothered with the artwork. 

A few months ago Mac Format magazine said you could get free iTunes 
artwork from www.widget-foundry.com.  I looked in to this one too but 
something in the licence agreement or the sign-up process put me off 
actually using it!

I hate reading through pages of terms and conditions, but I think that 
it is wise.  I use my old Mac as first choice although my PC is faster.  
Windows Media Player finds and downloads artwork in most cases, and it 
didn't require me to sign-up to anything.  I wish iTunes worked like this.

Hope this is helpful

Alan
