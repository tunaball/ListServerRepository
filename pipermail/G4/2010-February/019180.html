>
> Try the Open Firmware resets.  These, for some reason, correct lots of
> strange problems.  It's where I always start.  Simple and often works.
>
> On startup, after the chime, hold down these 4 keys:  CMD (apple), Option,
> letter O and letter F.
>
> When told to release the keys to continue, type the following three lines.
>  NOTE:  the dash is necessary.  It should say OK after each return.
>
> reset-nvram (return )
>
> set-defaults (return)
>
> reset-all (return)
>
> The machine should then reboot and startup normally.  Good Luck!
