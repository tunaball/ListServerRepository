Greetings Bob,

It sounds like you have a problem with the AGP Video card.

You might want to try shutting down the platform, removing the AGP  
card and then reseating it into the slot. If the card is covered with  
fuzz and cobwebs you should (using compressed caned air) blow out the  
crud, then reseat the card. Perhaps while you have the card out of the  
slot use the canned air to clean the AGP slot of anything that might  
have fallen in.

If that doesn't fix your problem then AGP cards can be had on lemswap

http://lowendmac.com/lists/swap.html

Just post a "Wanted" or perhaps look for one on eBay.

Cheers
