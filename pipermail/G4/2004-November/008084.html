On 11/04/04, Gary Willard <gwillard at mac.com> wrote:
>
> Maybe this is too simple of a question, but I've burned an iMovie from
> iDVD. Since it takes HOURS to burn the DVD, what's the best way to make
> another copy? Use Disk Copy? Create a disk image? Copy the whole DVD to
> my hard drive and burn via the Finder?

Pop in the movie DVD and, using Disk Utiltiy, create a new image onto a
local hard drive. Eject the movie DVD, pop a blank DVD into your burner, go
back to Disk Utility, click on burn, select the .dmg, the blank DVD, and
burn it.
