Hi Kevin. If the drive doesn't spin down by itself (which you may be 
able to cause to happen by checking "put hard disks to sleep when 
possible" in the Enery Saver preference pane under OS X), just drag it 
to the trash to unmount it which will cause it to unmount and sleep. To 
remount it use the Disk Utility app. John
