On 26 Oct, 2004, at 8:11, deepthought wrote:

I was wondering whether anyone has had any problems after installing
their Encore ST/G4 accelerator.  I've had it for about 1 1/2 to 2 years
and it's been working fine until this week.  It seems my G4 AGP is
getting unstable to the point where it will freeze after about 5-10
minutes.

One question to ask is whether you have installed some program lately 
that may be affecting the performance of your system. I suggest that 
you review your latest installations for compatibility and system 
requirements.

Another question is whether you have performed the normal maintenance 
processes during the time that you have had the G4 accelerator 
installed and running. Have you used a program like Onyx or Cocktail to 
keep your system maintenances up to date?

You should try both of these remedies before doing anything more 
extensive in your investigation of the slow-down you are experiencing. 
You can also use Disk Utilities in the Mac OS as an alternative to a 
third party program to repair permissions or examine your drive for 
possible problems.
