Rich, there is a software (freeware actually) program called "Wire Tap" 
that will record any sound your Mac makes - then turn it into an audio 
file that's importable into iTunes, QuickTime, etc...

It's at "http://www.ambrosiasw.com/utilities/freebies/"

It's awesome software!

Good luck!!!
