Use the custom install option on the ATI Universal ROM Updater.  Select 
the 126 version of ROM for the 8500 then continue...it will flash the 
card back to the 126 version ROM.  I too had problems with the newer 
ROMs and have since backed up to the original with no problems.  
However, I have also noticed that the new 10.3.6 drivers seem to be a 
bit slow on my 8500 G4 and even on an iMac G5.
