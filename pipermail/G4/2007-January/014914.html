System Profiler is not going to make the printer work. It will just  
show if it is recognized on the USB.
Instead go into System Preferences  ( Lightswitch icon in dock) click  
on the printer prefs,  and work through the printer panels to add  
your printer to the printer list, choose it as the active printer and  
set it up for pages.
