Paste the following TCP/IP address into your Browser address pane and 
you should be taken to your router.

http://192.168.2.1/

Well at least that is the address of my "Belkin" router, and it is the 
address that a lot of manufactures assigned to their SOHO routers.

Once you are there you will be able to locate the reset buttons that 
are associated with your router. If the address doesn't work then 
you'll need to locate the documentation that came with your router or 
contact the manufacture for the information. Some manufactures' s have 
place the information on-line and you can down-load it from their Tech. 
Support Help page.
