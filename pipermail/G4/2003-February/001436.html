i have a b&w g3 400 that i installed the powerligix g4zif 550 in. it has
made a HUGE difference in the OS and overall speed. 
i also threw in an ati 7000, once again, BIG difference.. primarily on os X.

the reason i went with the powerlogix versus the sonnetwas the fact that you
don't need an enabler for osX, to make the processor function.  in os 9 you
do for both. sadly, the 550 isn't made anymore. you MIGHT look at the g3/800
upgrade, with full speed backside cache, as a bigger boost, if you don't use
a lot of altivec applications.
