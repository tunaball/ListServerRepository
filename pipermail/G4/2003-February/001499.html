Apple offers Power Mac Power Supply Exchange Program
by Jim Dalrymple, jdalrymple at maccentral.com
February 21, 2003 1:30 pm ET 

Apple on Friday posted details of a new Power Mac G4 Power Supply Exchange
Program that will take effect tomorrow, Saturday, February 21, 2003 and end
on June 30, 2003.. 

The exchange program allows owners of a Power Mac G4 (mirrored door) to
exchange their original power supplies and system fans for new, quieter
versions, reducing the amount of noise generated by the computer. 
The Power Supply Exchange Program kit is available at no charge, with a
shipping and handling fee of US$19.95. The kit includes a power supply (360
W); system fan; allen key (2.5 mm); installation instructions and a prepaid
return airbill and instructions for returning the original components to
Apple. 

More information on the program is available from Apple's Web site.
