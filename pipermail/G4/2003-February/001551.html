Hi all:

I just went through a similar fire drill with my TiPBG4-800 using 
10.2.4, my Canon EluraMC2 with MMC cards, and a Dazzle 4-in-1 PC Card 
adapter.

My PB couldn't read photos from the MMC card that had been formatted in 
the camera.  I had to reformat the MMC card FAT16 in my Windows 2000 
notebook, then validate that the card could be read in my PB and in the 
camera.  Then I took photos and brought them into iPhoto with no 
further problems.  Same process worked for an SD card.
