I concur. Suggest you download the Oct 02 ATI Update and install it, 
then power down completely (ie disconnect from the wall), remove the 
old card. press the reset (CUDA) button. install the new card, 
connect back up and restart. Personally, I did not have a problem 
installing my ATI 8500, but have had problems installing processor 
upgrades on older Macs.

Good luck. The 8500 is a great card.
