Here is how you add a picture.

Go to http://www.mac.com and log into the page and then into the mail 
section. Once you are logged into the mail web-mail page click on 
"Preferences" in the toolbar. Then on the the preferences under the 
section "Composing" you will see a place to add a picture. Thats all 
there is to it.
