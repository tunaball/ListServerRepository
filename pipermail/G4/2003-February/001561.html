I moved at the end of January and when I disassembled my computer for 
the move,
I put the keyboard upside down on the top rack of our dishwasher. Then 
ran it without
soap. Placed it in front of a box heater for a few hours, and packed it. 
It was several
days till I hooked it all back up. No more sticky keys or filthy looks. 
Works great.
Just my 2¢ worth.
Scott
