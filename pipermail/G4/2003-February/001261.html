Hi Tony  Are they the Apple Pro Speakers with the unique little Apple 
Mini Jack on the end if the cable?

If so there is a conversion cable which turns it into a 3.5 mm stereo 
cable which might make it a little longer.

I think that extending it your self may be a more suitable option so 
you can customise the length to suit your desired need.  Just cut the 
cable at the point before it splits into two, and add some more wire, 
solder the joints for longevity, and maybe use shrink wrap to insulate 
the joints from short ciricuiting.  I would use those Auto crimp little 
tubes for Auto Electrics instead of Shrink Wrap and you could make 
quite a nice long sort of extension.

I just have my MDD 1 foot to the right of my right leg and the existing 
cable is more than long enough on the Apple Pro (haha) Speakers.  I 
find they do a nice job for simple monitoring purposes.  I wouldn't 
expect they can handle to much heavy stuff though.
