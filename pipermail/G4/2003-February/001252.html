Hey,

What you should be on the lookout for is damage to the system files or 
other directory damage. If you find Disk First Aid (CMD-F to find 
files) try running a check on his boot volume. Damage to the directory 
structure has always caused massive slowdown. But I don't see what can 
really be causing that much of a slowdown in MacOS9 unless there is 
serious damage to the operating system. Hopefully Disk First Aid can 
repair any damage but you might want to consider moving your friend to 
MacOSX.
