Hi!

I've had good experience with the D-Link DSB-H4 USB hub. You can get them at Staples for like $20 or $30.

However, the absolute *best* USB hub I've ever used are the ones that come built-in to the 22" LaCie Blue monitors in my Art department. They support dynamic USB/Video switching right out of the box so you can have a single monitor and USB keyboard/mouse/anything else and switch back and forth between 2 computers just by pressing a button on the monitor. A *very* nice way of doing KVM, if rather pricey.
