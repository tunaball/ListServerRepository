Do you have a domain name server listing in your TCP/IP Control panel 
for your connection? That sounds like what the problem is. If, when you 
are connected, you put http://17.254.3.183 in your browser, and it goes 
to apple.com. Then that is probably your problem. If that doesn't work, 
then you might need to put in a router address in addition to the DNS 
listing.

BTW, I ran slackware when I was an ISP, and didn't have any trouble 
supporting multiple OS's from Mac, to Windows, to Linux. As long as you 
know where the control panel is, they can help walk you through the 
setup.
