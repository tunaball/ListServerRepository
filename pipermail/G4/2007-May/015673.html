If you're speaking of the one from Keyspan, then yes, it should work  
just fine.  The only Apple models it won't work on are the X-Serve  
and the G5's, and any Powermac older than the 4400 (inclusive)  
(http://www.keyspan.com/products/sxpro/homepage.2.reqs.spml).

And all of the PCI slots in the MDD are 64-bit capable (http:// 
support.apple.com/specs/powermac/ 
Power_Mac_G4_Mirrored_Drive_Doors.html).
