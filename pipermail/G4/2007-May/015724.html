On 29 May, 2007, at 8:44 PM, Bruce Ryan Nakamura wrote:

> What is the best way for me to connect my HP Laserjet 4M to my G4  
> mirrored drive door Powermac?

Use a Cat 5 cable that will reach from one to the other. Cat 5 cable  
is also known as Ethernet cable.

The selection of that printer as your default printer will depend on  
which OS you are using and whether or not you have installed drivers  
for the HP printer.
