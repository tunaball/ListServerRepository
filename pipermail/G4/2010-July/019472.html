Hold down the control key and press the disc eject key and a dialogue box with the choices of 'restart', 'sleep', 'cancel' and 'shutdown' will pop up, whether you have a monitor attached or not. If you press the first letter of each choice, it will activate that action; i.e., pressing the 'r' key will restart the machine, except for 'shutdown' which requires pressing the 'return' key, because the 's' key alone will put the machine to sleep. For your situation, after pressing 'r' will restart the computer. G'luck.

> Some of the G4s have two small buttons on the front panel below the power button. The one that protrudes slightly and has a triangle symbol on it is the "Restart" button. Push it once to make the tower restart.
> 
> Some G4s only have that extra button.
> 
> If there is a second button with a strange symbol, that is the "Controller" button and should only be used during certain procedures, not for a restart.
