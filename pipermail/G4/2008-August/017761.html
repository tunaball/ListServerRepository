Robert,

There is still a lot of life in your machine. I use a slightly earlier
model (400mhz) and it still serves me well.

Bruce's suggestions are very good. Check out the One World Computing
site. They offer a lot of information, but you have to look around the
site to find it.

It wouldn't cost too much to make it reasonably fast to do video
editing. Ultimately it would be cheaper than dropping the bucks to buy
a Mac Pro tower. Examine the site of the video software that you want
to use and then examine what you have now. The cheapest upgrade would
be getting more RAM. Max your machine out. That should be roughly
2gigs of RAM if memory serves me. I believe that your machine is
limited to that. But I might be wrong about that.

So go to http://www.xlr8yourmac.com/

This site also offers a wealth of information on various upgrades.
