	You are correct; the MDD requires the original Airport card 
(and it will just be 802.11b). I have found it better to purchase the 
new "n" version of Airport Express and use the MDD's Ethernet port to 
connect to the Airport Express. Depending on whether there are other 
a/b wireless receivers on your network, you can then run at "n" speed.
