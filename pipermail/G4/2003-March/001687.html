Another way to go, allbeit much more expensive, is something like the 
Digi001 from Digidesign.  Great i/o box w/ 8 audio ins, 8 audio outs, 2 mic 
ins and 2 MIDI ins and outs.  Sounds like overkill for what you want to do, 
but if you are looking to the future with an eye on hard disk recording and 
editing, it can't be beat as it comes with the greatest program ever, 
Protools.  (sorry, just a little biased)  I bought it for about $800 a 
couple years ago...probably too much, but just figured I'd share my 
experience to give you a wider range of options:  www.protools.com
