Hey,

I'm assuming these are ATA as two 80 GB SCSI drives would cost a 
fortune. I don't see how heat would cause that kind of problem. Of 
course it could be contributing. I think your problem could be:

1)You have a damaged OSX partition and the heat from the case could be 
causing the drives to error out.

2)One of the drives could be bad and since they're RAID'ed it could be 
bringing down the other drive with it even though the boot partition 
isn't RAID'ed.

3)There is an Internet Explorer bug that causes damaged Systems under 
Jag. But this is only on the old version they have since fixed it. 
Check to make sure Internet Explorer is up to date.

4) Reset the CUDA switch it's a little black button somewhere on the 
mother board.
