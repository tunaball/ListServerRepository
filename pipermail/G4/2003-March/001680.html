One option that I  haven't seen mentioned yet is the one that I use: 
the MOTU-828 Firewire attached audio unit. I use this with my G4/733 
and with my iBook. However, this is somewhat expensive commercial level 
equipment.

I'm considering getting an iMic for my iBook to use with my little Sony 
stereo microphone (presumably that will work). Sometimes I want to 
record live stereo into the laptop but don't want to deal with the 828 
setup, ie. the power cords, condenser mics, mic cables, mic stands.
