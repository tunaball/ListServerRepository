many of the "pc" brands, such as the cd stomper something or other i 
got for $8 bucks at compusa, include templates for word, appleworks, 
photoshop, etc. so with them there really is no software involved in 
making the cd labels, you simply design from the template and print. in 
fact, if you would want to be really stingy, you could simply buy the 
neato or whatever refill packs of cd labels, and create your own 
template in whatever software you wanted to. (i just did that today at 
work for some 35mm slide labels i have for a drug study my office is 
involved in)
