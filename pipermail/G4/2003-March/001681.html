> One option that I  haven't seen mentioned yet is the one that I use:

I bought a Onkyo USB audio processor that has line level controls, mike and
line in, as well as digital out and a 3-port USB hub, and a nice collection
of software including Peak LE. It cost around $40-45 at Fry's and has
performed very well for my G4 MDD.
