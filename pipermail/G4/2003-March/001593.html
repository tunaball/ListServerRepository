The IDE bus in the 533-G4 is your limiting factor. When you install a drive
larger than 137GB, you will only see 128GB. What you need to do is install
an ATA100 or ATA133 PCI card. This card had two additional IDE busses that
will accept up to four IDE devices.

Here's is one explanation you can read:
http://www.mrbarrett.com/mt/archives/2002_06_11.html
