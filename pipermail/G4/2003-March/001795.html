Yes, I've been considering the same thing.

The refurbished drive at Overstock is the older DRU-500A not the latest
DRU-500AX.  Flashing the firmware should give the 500A functional
equivalency to the 500AX.

Also, he may need to get Toast in order to burn the DVD's since Apple's Disc
burning software may not recognize the add-on drive (OSX 10.2.4 might have
added partial support, but I'm not positive).  I prefer Toast anyway.
