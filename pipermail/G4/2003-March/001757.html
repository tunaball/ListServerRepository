as i normally do, i would recommend the epson 820. its a $99 printer 
that has great quality, 6 color, decent speed, and a good price. fyi, 
it is the same print head as the epson 1280, only in a 8 1/2 x 11 print 
size. at work i have used an hp 5550 and a 990cse... both are horrid 
for photos and are way over priced compared to the 820. the 825 and the 
960? epsons are the same print heads as the 820, only they have the 
capability to print directly from a cf card, something i never need.

just to give you an idea of the 820/1280 quality, i graduated from one 
of the top photography schools in the US, and myself as well as many of 
my friends printed our portfolios on these printers. what did the 
commercial photographers say when they saw our portfolios during job 
interviews??  "wow, you said you printed all of these images yourself? 
i didnt know anyone spent that much time in the color darkrooms anymore"

suffice it to say, the quality was superb.
