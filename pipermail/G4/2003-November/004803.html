On 11/22/03, Jerry Bunn <jbunn at woh.rr.com> wrote:
>
> Since I've installed Panther on my dual 1 Ghz G4 I cannot use Stuffit
> Expander.  Everytime I try to use it, it says "Sorry, Expander requires
> Stuffit Engine 8.0.0 or later.  Please reinstall from the original
> installer."
> I have reinstalled from the Panther disc and that hasn't helped.
> I don't have any trouble with this on my 17" iMac 1.25 Ghz running
> Panther.
> Any ideas?

Sounds like you've installed StuffitExpander 8+, but still have the engine
for 7+. The engine is located in /Library/CFMSupport. Check there. If
StuffItEngineShell.cfm isn't ver 8 or 8.0.1, delete it, and reinstall
StuffitExpander 8.0 and update it to 8.01.
