Those screen messages are Kernel Panics. More than likely, you have
out-of-spec RAM. Strongly suggest running the Apple Hardware Test program
that came with your machine. If everything checks out OK, then disconnect
any peripheral equipment and try installing again. Here's a $5 downloadable
e-book that should help, even though it's directed at upgrading to Panther,
it's equally applicable to Jaguar installations: "Take Control of Upgrading
to Panther" available at <http://www.tidbits.com/takecontrol/>.
