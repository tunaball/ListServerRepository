On Nov 28, 2003, at 6:26 AM, Mel Krewall wrote:

> I am about to get a new cell phone and will be syncing my phone with 
> my work address book and calendar. I have tried iSync in its early 
> versions, but now I will be giving it another chance since it has been 
> revised. Does anyone know how to delete/archive the existing iCal 
> calendars and Address Book entries so that I can start fresh with 
> syncing the phone to them? I browsed around the Library but was not 
> successful. Anyone know how to do this?
> Thanks,
> Mel

If you are using Panther, just Make a backup of the Address Book 
database, the latest version will ask you where to store the backup. 
This is much safer than just copying the database around by hand.

iCal lets you do the same thing with its Export Calendar option.
