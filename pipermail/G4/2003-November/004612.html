My Sawtooth did work again after I fiddled around a little. Appears 
Firewalk X doesn't quite work right with Panther. Everything else works 
great though. Shouldn't cause a problem for you. As for whether to 
upgrade or clean install, I would recommend an upgrade, but back up 
first. When you select the destination volume in the installer click 
the options button and select "Archive and Install". That will archive 
all the 10.1.5 stuff to another folder on your hard drive. Just to be 
safe, I'd back up though.
