Mike Bechtold wrote:
> Rob, it depends on the video card; there are both AGP
> and PCI versions available. The AGP versions are faster;
> mine is a flashed Nvidia GeForce 6200 AGP that I bought 
> from an eBay vendor for $85, and which works fine. 
> Yes, you remove the old video card, and replace it with
> the new one. Check to make sure that the card you choose
> will support your display's interface, VGA, DVI, HDMI,
> or S-Video. Also, be sure that it will support dual 
> monitors if you want that capability, and that with 
> the correct connectors. You can usually adapt between
> VGA and DVI, but HDMI is more difficult.
> - Michael B. in Cincinnati
