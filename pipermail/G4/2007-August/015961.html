Just keep in mind the drive limits on these machines if you shop for  
a new drive. My G4 Digital Audio can only see 100gb, no matter how  
big the drive is.

As for 40 not being big enough, it really depends on what you want to  
do.  I have 80 gig.  30gb is music, 20gig is pics and videos, I have  
12 gig free.   So Tiger and all my apps only take up 20gig.
