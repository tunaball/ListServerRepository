You don't need Airport. What you will need is a bigger HD. 40 is Way  
too small. Get at least an 80.
Tiger is on all of my machines at work form a 533 G-4 to an MacPro  
Intel Tower. Also up your ram if you can but I think 1.5 gigs is all  
you can put in a QS.
