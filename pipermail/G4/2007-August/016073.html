Hi Rob,

What do you mean by "freeze"? Does the entire system lock up on you,  
requiring a restart, or does the mouse pointer just stutter on the  
screen at odd times?

If it's the latter, I had this same issue and the fix was as simple  
as changing the surface I was using my mouse on. The optical mice can  
be very finicky--I had a black mouse pad with a logo on it, and my  
pointer hesitated on the screen whenever it went over the logo.   
Another mouse works fine on the same pad. Testing another mouse/pad  
is a quick way to check.
