Rob,
Very thorough identifying your devices, but one question I have is, did 
you recently add/install any new devices or software? Even if you 
haven't, try a process of elimination by disconnecting USB hubs, the 
mouse (replace with Apple's mouse if you still have it), and other 
peripherals until it's just the CPU and the monitor. If none of the 
disconnections do not resolve the problem, then it's time to go through 
the software apps, especially utilities and/or firmware (drivers in PC 
lingo). Another question is when was the last time you updated anything 
on your machine? Another utility that may help is Applejack, which I 
think is freeware. Google it and you should get to its web site.

I wish I had an answer for you. Someone else on this list may have 
something.

Rob
