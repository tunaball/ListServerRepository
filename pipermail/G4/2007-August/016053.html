> In particular I'm wondering if I can use a 16:10 LCD
> panel with resolution: 1440 x 900?

>
> just give 1280 x 1024 as the max resolution but I know

I haven't tried it, but it should work.  The video card must have
enough memory to support a given resolution.  If your card can do 1600
x 1200 then it has plenty of memory for 1440 x 900.  With that out of
the way, the video drivers have to support the 16:10 modes.  If you're
running a recent version of Tiger then I assume the drivers can handle
16:10 (or 16:9).

I'd give it a shot.  Just check the return policy before you buy a new
monitor, just in case.
