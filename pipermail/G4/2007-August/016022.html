I would leave it for dead and just add a USB 2.0 PCI card. The USB  
ports on the DA are USB1, so the transfer rates are ungodly slow. I  
have a 1.2 Ghz DA as well, and I have both USB 2.0 and FW 800 pci  
cards that work great.
