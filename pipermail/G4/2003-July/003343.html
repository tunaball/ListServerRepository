I've been using the Logitech Elite Duo (which is the "Elite Black" 
keyboard you saw paired with a wireless optical mouse) for several 
months, they're great. The "OS X Only" isn't entirely true either, as 
the keyboard works just fine under OS 9 with this exception: the 
command and option keys are transposed. The Logitech Control Center is 
an OSX application with no OS9 equivalent, so the extra keys and 
programmability aren't available under 9, but the keyboard is otherwise 
functional.
