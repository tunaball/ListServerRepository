I love the MacAlly iKey USB keyboard. It feels a lot better to me than 
the Apple brand keyboards.

Has a power switch on it that can be used to bring up the RESTART  
SLEEP  CANCEL  SHUT DOWN buttons that the Apple keyboards no longer 
have. F12 and Option F12 work as optical ejection keys. Only $29 I 
think. Been using mine since mid 1999 - four years with no problems.
