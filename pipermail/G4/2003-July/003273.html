Remember, if you are adding a second card it will need to be a 
(generally) more expensive, elusive PCI card.  But if you are replacing 
your AGP card you can get a new card or a better used card with the 
support you need. Maybe a chance to get Quartz Extreme working for you 
(32 meg or more).

The PCI cards that have 32 meg or more are very sought after by people 
with pre G4 machines -competition can be steep.
