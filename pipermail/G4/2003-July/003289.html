I would look at the Sony MD recorders. They run around $200-300. Mine 
worked for me at guitar camp. Each MD disk digitally records 70 minutes 
of music. They can be attached to your G4 through line input, and you 
can upload to your computer using programs like SoundStudio.

Have a good workshop
