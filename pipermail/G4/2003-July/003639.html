You would see a HUGE improvement in game playing by going to the  8500.

The whole point to have a video accelerator card is so that the system 
can offload
the video processing to a dedicated graphics CPU. The speed of the G4 
will effect
the graphics speed to a certain degree, but the video card  plays a 
much greater role
in determining frame rates and such.

I don't know if the Geforce4 titanium is compatible with 2X AGP
