The computer comes with one DVI to VGA adapter. If you want to use two 
VGA monitors, you'll also need an ADC to VGA adapter, one of which is 
Dr. Bott's ADC VGA Extractor. Do a Google to find the best price. I got 
one for $27 a few months ago. It works great, no software or anything 
else. Not even a reboot. Just plug it in and go. John
