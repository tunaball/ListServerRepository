I did a quick look at the OWC site. I don't see any indication that any 
of their cases could take a SCSI and then is adapted to Firewire. Come

incorporate SCSI HDs—they've all been, in my somewhat limited

may be a way to incorporate SCSI in a Firewire chain (maybe an adapter 
card??).

good luck with them in all correspondences.
