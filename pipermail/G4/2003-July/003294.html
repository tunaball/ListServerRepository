Hi David,

You can use the diff command with two directory names (folders) as your 
arguments, as in:

diff Widgets OlderWidgets

You'll get a listing of files that are only in one directory or the 
other, and of files that are in both. It will even search recursively 
if you ask. More options available under man diff.


I would imagine you could pipe the output to a file, sorting it as you 
go...
