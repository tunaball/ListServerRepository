No problem, glad everything is working better for you. I would suggest 
running the Permissions
verify/repair and an fsck -y to double check all is really well. Small 
problems can amplify quickly,
best to nip them in the bud. I always run at least the fsck -y after 
any type of crash. Those usually
come after downloading Freeware apps. or system hacks. Best to create a 
second user account
just for test purposes. In fact it is a good idea to create a second 
Admin account after installing a
new version of OS X. If you have wonkiness, just log in as your second 
admin to see if you still have
problems. I always keep a second account just for emergencies.
