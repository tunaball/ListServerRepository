Thank you all for your comments and suggestions.

I ordered a replacement power supply this afternoon.  $249 delivered. 
Used.  90-day warranty.

Reasoning:  A new machine will not support OSX 3.9 which is 
mission-critical as I do not want to replace or upgrade Adobe 
Creative Suite 2.  And, I love the machine.  It has been wonderful, 
and very, very reliable.  It's a dual boot FW 400 model, packed with 
RAM with a Vortex fan on the CPU.

I took a chance on a used power supply; probably not the brightest 
thing to do, but <hardcoremac> tests them to make sure they are 
strong.  He said he's never had one come back during warranty.  The 
alternative was spending an additional $100 for a refurbished unit, 
with a 90-day warranty.  "Ya pays yer money, and ya takes yer 
chances."

Thanks again for your perspectives.  Members of this list have been 
very helpful over the years!  Interesting to read too.  I'm no expert 
or technician, just a mac user whose first machine was an SE.  :-)

Cheers!
