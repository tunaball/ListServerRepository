I put the drives in enclosures from Other World Computing and
connected them using the MoBo FW800 connection and a three (external)
port FW800 PCI card. OWC offers external cases for both IDE and SATA
drives. The external cases use power supplies that plug into AC power
independently of the computer or monitor.
