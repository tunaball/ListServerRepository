On Thu, 4 Mar 2010 05:21:14 +0100, stefan moller wrote:
> what is required to connect two screen monitors to my old quicksilver ? 

You need either a dual pci graphics card, or two single cards. Just plug both monitors in...no software needed. Go into your Monitor setup in the sys prefs to make any changes, if needed. The two monitors will work together as one double-wide monitor. That's all thee is to it.
