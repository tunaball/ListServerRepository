That's correct, no internal HDs. If you run either an eSATA or FW PCI
card (OWC has both) you just connect to the card. I bought twp of OWCs
Elite AL enclosures and put the drives from my Dual 1.42 into them.
These were IDE drives but they offer the same box for SATAs. I flashed
the enclosures to "see" the drives individually and they mount on my
MacBook or either Mini in the house just fine. I also have a 750 SATA
in Newer Technology's Mini-Stack III enclosure. This matches the Mini
form factor and is actually a great hub vbecause it has FW 400 & 800,
USB and (IIRC) eSATA ports. However they are more expensive than the
straight ahead external enclouses.
