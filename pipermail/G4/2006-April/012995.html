There is definately no firmware-upgrade for Yikes!. A far as I recall, 
using PC133-memory in this G4 is problematic. Furthermore, I think the 
memory-chips should be placed on both sides of the module. If they are 
all on the same side, your Mac may recognize a maximum of 128 MB only.
