On Apr 5, 2006, at 4:04 PM, Christian Loor wrote:

> Is it wise or unwise to install TIGER on a G4 CUBE?
> The folks at my local Apple Store (NY Soho) claim that
> it will make my machine more sluggish. Is this true?
> What are everyone's recommendations?

In general each release has gotten faster (sometimes significantly so  
for specific features), though you're unlikely to notice the  
difference during day to day use. Here's a site that specifically  
tracks releases of OS X on Cube performance:

http://www.macsonly.com/arch00505.html#21

I was able to find several articles related to this question with a  
simple search for "tiger vs panther".

I run 10.4.x server on a G4 at 400 AGP for my home network. Make sure  
you have at least 512 MBs of memory and you'll be happy.
