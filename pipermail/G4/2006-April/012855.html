> *

According to a document at the Apple site (http://docs.info.apple.com/ 
article.html?artnum=107372), they should mix well:

"When you mix 802.11b (AirPort) and 802.11g (AirPort Extreme) clients  
on an AirPort Extreme network, each type of client receives an  
appropriate data throughput rate. The 802.11g clients continue to  
receive data at a higher rate than 802.11b clients."
