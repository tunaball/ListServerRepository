Not sure how you arrived at that conclusion.  When I went through the  
proper search by selecting this model from the various menus, it  
states very clearly:

We recommend a minimum memory total of at least 512MB. And since your  
Apple Power Mac G4 (Dual 867MHz DDR) supports up to 2048MB of memory,  
you may want to install even more, especially if you work with  
several programs at a time or use memory-intensive software, such as  
image-editing software.
(Keep in mind that sometimes BIOS updates and other hardware  
configurations can alter memory recommendations.)

What you may have found is that the machine will support the 1 Gb RAM  
sticks, but only 512 Mb will be recognized as often happens when  
using unsupported RAM.
