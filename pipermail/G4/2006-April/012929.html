The trick with the "No Brand" is quite simple. You just have to find out
which "brand" or "no-brand" the profis in your area are using. Let's give a
clear example:
One of my best friends is a prof. video-editor. ASA the job has finished he
will burn to CD or DVD. The disks he has to use to do this job must be of a
quality thats allows his principals to produce factory-pressed CD/DVD's.
No coasters allowed!!! Well these CD/DVD's I'm using too. In Amsterdam we
have 3 stores selling these prof. "no-brands". Asking the shop-owners for
real brand-names will produce a big smile and the question "Any complains
concerning our disks??". I must say: "No!!".
