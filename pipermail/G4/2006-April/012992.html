> 3. Is it true that I can only boot off a apple branded rom?

>     I've got a 24x CD in the G4, and wish to upgrade to a DVD.
>     Do I HAVE to get an apple one or will any generic brand do?
>    AND still let me use a boot disk in it?

(The following applies to DVD writers. Just about any DVD-ROM should  
do anything you want.) It depends. You can do anything except use  
Apple's DVD apps. There were some hacks around this, but Apple  
suppressed them. Each release of OS X has gotten better at supporting  
non-Apple drives and there is PatchBurn for most of those that  
aren't. One option is to buy a pioneer DVD. If installed internally a  
Pioneer will usually be seen as an Apple Superdrive.
