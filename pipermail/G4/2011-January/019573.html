Go to this link

http://www.everymac.com/

Select "Power Mac G4" and drop down to the MDD section.  Select your  
model of the MDD.  Near the top of the Specs page, select "Ports".   
The 867 DP model has NO microphone inputs but DOES have a "Sound In"  
port.

Hope this helps
