Your message is way off-topic for the G4 List, as the PowerBook 3400c
is nowhere near a G4 Mac.

In any case, the PowerBook 3400c Service Manual from Apple states:

Power

Note:  You will hear only the click of the power-on button when you
attempt to start up a computer that lacks sufficient power to start.

Computer won’t power up:

1 If sleep LED is continually on, backup battery power has been
interrupted. Restart computer by holding down reset actuator 10-20
seconds. If computer doesn’t restart, repeat 3–4 times.
2 Try known-good power adapter.
3 Try known-good, charged battery.
4 Connect power adapter and restart computer in 3–4 minutes.
5 Replace power supply board.
6 Replace logic board.

For further discussion you may wish to post to the LEM PowerBooks List:
<http://www.lowendmac.com/lists/powerbooks.shtml>, where many members
own the 3400c and similar PowerBooks.
