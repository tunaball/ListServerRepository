On 2011/01/16 17:00, alistair mackinnon so eloquently wrote:
> have a question...
> whats the best usb 2.0 card?

The BELKIN Hi-speed USB 2.0 5-Port PCI Card F5U220 is a good card. These 
days all USB 2 PCI cards are OHCI compliant so compatibility is not an 
issue. Just stay away from cards marked 'Full Speed' or 'Supports USB 2 
Devices' like the Macally UH-241, they are actually USB 1 speed.

Full Speed = USB 1
Hi-Speed = USB 2
Super Speed = USB 3 (not yet supported by Apple)
