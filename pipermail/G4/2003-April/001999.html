On 4/2/03, Michael Oldfield <amobel at reach.net> wrote:
>
> New Drive as the primary:  I was certainly in "panic mode" and Mel's
> info helped. However, and it's a BIG however, I can't seem to be able
> to copy over my OSX folder to the new drive. My goal is to have the
> new 7200 maxtor 40GB as my main drive. I've split it into 3
> partitions. I would then like to use the original 5400 20GB (2
> partitions currently with 10.2.4 on one and 9.2.2 on the other) as my
> back-up.

> So far every time I try to copy the original OSX partition something
> goes amiss. Even used that great little app Carbon Copy Cloner but
> ran into problems with the Library folder. I must be doing something
> dumb! Never had this problem with OS9! Any suggestions?

Make sure you're using the latest version of CCC (2.1.6b)
<http://www.bombich.com/software/ccc.html>. You didn't have that problem
with OS 9 because you had few if any invisible or hidden files and folders,
and, because OS 9 doesn't have a permissions security protocol. That's why
you need to use something like CCC to ensure that all the required hidden
and invisible items are copied over and to ensure that permissions are
retained. Strongly suggest you review the article at
<http://www.bombich.com/mactips/image.html> and try CCC again.

Cheers
