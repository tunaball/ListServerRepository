If you are interested in RAM open the little memory triangle in System 
Profiler and it will show all your ram chips and their capacity.
Or just do "about this mac".
If interested in Disc space you can use the devices panel in System 
profiler and open up the parts about your drives or  OR simply open the 
root drive window and select the menu option: View/Show status bar. 
(Easy)
That's it. These are the only two things on your mac that are measured 
in gigabytes .
