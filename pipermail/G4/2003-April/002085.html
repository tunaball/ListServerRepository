Hi Barry,

Iused to have the same problem priodically with 9.04 & Appleworks in
particular, 
though it accreted over months and not a single sesssion.

I resolved it by using Norton to defrag and optimize my drives.  Having
larger blocks 
and more highly adjacent areas of available memory made a huge difference. 
I 
highly recommend backing up anything you don't want to lose prior to the
cleaning, 
as well as trashing everything you won't need later.  Doing a full tilt
virus scan before 
backing up would also be a good precaution.

HTH,
