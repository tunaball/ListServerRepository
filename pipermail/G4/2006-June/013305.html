On Jun 12, 2006, at 3:57 PM, Keon Alderman wrote:

> Hello folks, I have a G4 and I would like to change the processor  
> out for a faster one. Is there a site I can visit that has  
> reasonable prices. I would also like to know is there any way to  
> convert a G4 that isn't a digital audio version into one.
> Is it an issue with my sound card or something else.

First of all you should use the New button and not the Reply button  
to create a new message. That way you don't pollute an existing  
thread with an unrelated question and you can supply an appropriate  
subject line.

As to your question, http://www.macsales.com/, a.k.a. Other World  
Computing, has a good selection of processor upgrades and reasonable  
prices.

Digital audio is easily added via USB devices. The one I use is  
digital out only (from Turtle Beach), but I know that there are other  
such devices depending on your needs. OWC probably has information on  
this type of device. Also a little web searching is bound to turn up  
lots of information.
