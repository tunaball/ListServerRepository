Can a weak power supply in my Powermac G4 MDD cause kernal panics when 
loading software from a genuine Apple Install/Restore DVD?

partition.  However, when I tried to install Panther onto the second 
partition, the installation kept crashing and I kept getting a "You

warmed up and began to get weak.
