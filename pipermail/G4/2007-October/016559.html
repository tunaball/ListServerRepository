Get a FWHD. If your machine isn't an ICBM (Intel-chip-based Mac), then your
backup/clone isn't bootable and, IMHO, a nonbootable backup/clone is relatively
useless. FWIW, FW 400 is at least 30% faster than USB 2 and well worth the
additional expense.
