I just installed a Radeon 9800 in my 1.25GHz MDD.   Before you get one 
(they're available at OWC and AMD/ATI after a long dry spell), be aware that it does 
not really work with OS 9.   AMD/ATI states that the card requires OS X.   
While the primary graphics functions seem to work in Classic, there is no video 
acceleration, so clips are jerky.   There's a report on the web that you can 
boot into OS 9 with a 9800, but it always hangs on me.   For the few legacy 
applications that I need, the 9800's performance in Classic is acceptable.   It 
works great in OS X.
