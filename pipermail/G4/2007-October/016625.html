Someone was wondering if Leopard would work with aftermarket CPUs,
like those from PowerLogix.  Here's the answer from an OWC
(http://www.macsales.com/) email:

"Note - Leopard requires that you have a G4/867MHz Processor or faster
in your Mac. So what about processor upgraded machines? You are GOLDEN
(at least so tested with our lines) :). Any PowerMac G4 model upgraded
with an OWC, PowerLogix, or NewerTech model upgrade G4/900MHz or
Faster meets the processor requirement for Leopard. Dropped a
NewerTech G4/2.0GHz into your Sawtooth 350MHz - the cat will be very
happy and your investment is safe.

Also requires - DVD Drive, Built in FireWire (All G4s & later have
this), and 512MB min of memory."
