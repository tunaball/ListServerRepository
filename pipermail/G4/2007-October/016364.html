Have you verified that this IS true? The additional single drive mounting
sleds are not always apparent to the novice user since they look just like
the inside bottom of the chassis they are mounted on. Also, one type of sled

That gives you at least one additional drive mounting location if this type
of sled in inside the chassis.

just have to be willing to pay their price. Or, you can do a search for the
sleds on eBay or Craig's List.
