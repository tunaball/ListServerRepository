No, not if you burn them as normal audio files to play
on an ordinary cd player. If you burn them as mp3's
then all the info is stored. Some cd players can then
play the mp3's directly, which means you get more
songs per cd.

Go to iTunes preferences > Advanced > Burning, and
select MP3 CD instead of Audio CD (which is the
default).
