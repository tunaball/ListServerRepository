The Quicksilver (M8667LL/A - 2002), at least, does NOT support Cable 
select and must be configured as Master and Slave (following the drive 
manufacturer's instructions).

see the following link:  
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=50018

Scroll down to the Quicksilver section (about the middle of the page).  
The manual I'm referring to is the English version in the Quicksilver 
section.
After you download the manual, see page 73.
