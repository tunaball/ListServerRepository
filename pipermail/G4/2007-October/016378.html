On Oct 6, 2007, at 3:51 PM, Earle Jones wrote:

> *
> Hi!
>
> One step better than a PRAM reset is this:
>
> Hold down "Command-Option OF" when starting up.  This puts the Mac 
> into "open firmware" mode.
>
> type:  reset-nvram (CR)   [nvram = non-volatile RAM}
            set-defaults
>           reset-all       (CR)
>
> (CR) = carriage return
>
> You may have to repeat this twice.
>
> This should turn on the startup chime and perhaps fix some other 
> glitches.
>
>
> NOTE:  Command-Option PR resets PRAM
>              Command-Option OF resets NVRAM
>              Command-Option S opens in "single-user" mode
>
> Good luck!
>
