Try Skype for the Mac, it's FREE and does almost everything that you 
get from Vonage.

http://www.skype.com/download/skype/macosx/
Give it a try, as calls that are PC to PC or Mac to PC any where in the

through the end of the year and then you will only have to pay pennies 
a minute, without a monthly charge.

While you are at the Sky URL you can also check out the cost per minute 
to standard telephones anywhere in the world.
