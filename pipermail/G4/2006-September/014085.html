Look under preference...

Or if your simple trying enlarge the screen view find
the fourth (4th icon from the left [just below the
red/yellow/green radio button] and your see a small
"A" followed by a large "A". Push the larger "A" and
your get a zone effect. Push the smaller "A" and the
view will get smaller.
