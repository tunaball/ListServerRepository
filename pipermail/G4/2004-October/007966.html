I've run a 60GB 7200rpm Hitachi/IBM Travelstar in both a 1gHz TiBook 
and a 12" AlBook (1.33gHz). In both cases, it worked great. I'm using 
the AlBook right now in fact.

In both cases, I didn't see a noticeable drop in battery life, but I 
did notice a visible increase in overall system performance. Everything 
just seemed "snappier".

As usual, your mileage may vary...
