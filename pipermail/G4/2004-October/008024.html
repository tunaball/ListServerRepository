>It sounds as though you have "archive via rename" turned on for your stuffit installation. Assuming you have stuffit deluxe v.8 or better installed on your system (I don't recall if this worked for v.7 or not), open system preferences and select the preference pane called "Stuffit AVR." Turn off the option to archive via rename and you should no longer have the issues you describe.
