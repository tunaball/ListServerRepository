On Oct 5, 2004, at 8:02 PM, rich northouse wrote:

I would also like to install a third hard drive. I have purchased a 
Sonnet Tempo ATA 133 PCI card to support this third drive -- in 
particular because it is a  200 gig hard drive. Can someone tell me 
where I can mount this 3rd drive?


According to MacTracker, your machine is capable of using up to four 
separate 3.5" devices internally. The main drive is usually mounted in 
the right-most position on the chassis when looking at it with the rear 
of the case on your right.

Do you have a two-drive mounting bracket under your main drive? If you 
do, all you need is a short three-connector plug IDE ribbon to use with 
the drives mounted one atop the other. Be sure to identify them as 
"Master" and "Slave" to prevent identification problems.

If you only have the single drive mounting brackets, you can mount the 
second drive right next to the original drive, but you will need a 
longer IDE ribbon to connect them.
