On Oct 12, 2004, at 9:32 AM, Melissa Crowe wrote:

Hello All,
I have installed Toast 6 on my Dual 1 Gig G4 & promptly lost my serial 
number. Now I am unable to use the Panther cd-burning software because 
I am prompted to "Toast It" instead. Anyone know how to fix that?
Thanks,
Melissa


I've used both programs and like Toast better. If you want to get it 
back and running again, simply send an email to Roxio explaining your 
loss and they should give you another registration code if you did 
register it in the first place. If you didn't register Toast, simply 
toss it in the trash, delete it, and then use System Preferences to 
reset your CD/DVD preference to ask you what to do when you insert a 
blank disc or whatever other choice you would like.

HTH,
