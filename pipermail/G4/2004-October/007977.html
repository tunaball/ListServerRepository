If the monitor is analog (e.g., VGA), you can buy a manual switch for 
about $15 or less, and a complete KVM switch (Keyboard, VGA, Mouse) 
to switch all three at once for well under $100--these have gotten 
really cheap in the past few years, but I have no idea how cheap, as 
I have not bought one lately.  However, if you are talking digital 
monitor then the solution begins to become much more expensive.  I 
use a Gefen ex-tend-it "ADC" switcher (ADC for my Mac and DVI for my 
PC as well as the USB devices attached to them), but it cost around 
$400!
