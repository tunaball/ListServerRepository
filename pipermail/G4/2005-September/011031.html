DL it. It's at <http://media4.big-boys.com/content/hpphoto.wmv>. Paste in
the URL, hold down the OPT key, and hit the Enter key->DLs to your DL
folder. Plays without interruption on WMP 9.

File a bug and let Apple's engineering folks sort it out. Send bug reports
and enhancement requests to Apple via its Bug Reporter system. Go to
<https://connect.apple.com/MemberSite> and join the Apple Developer
Connection (ADC)-it's free and available for all Mac users and gets you a
look at some development software. Then, go to
<https://bugreport.apple.com/>, click on the Login to the Apple BugReporter
link (using your ADC username and password), and file your bug
report/enhancement request. The nice thing with this procedure is that you
get a response and a follow-up number; thus, starting a dialog with
engineering. If your item isn't fixed with a new update, you can fire a
follow-up on your original, via e-mail.
