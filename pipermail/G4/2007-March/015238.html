Just got it fixed!!!!

I ran all of the updates.  The problem was in my Time Zone panel within the
Date & Time System Preference. For some reason it was showing my location
with CST.  I clicked on it and it changed to DST and everything started
working.  

If your time is hosed, double-check the Time Zone panel for DST
