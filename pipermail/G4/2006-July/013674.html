An old buddy gave me a useful clue: minimize the number of objects on 
your desktop. Seems that keeping track and re-drawing the items on 
the desktop requires a lot of computation cycles.

I had loads of items, so I put them in a folder on the desktop that I 
called "DesktopStuff."  Sped things up a lot.
