DON'T DO IT!  Back when I got my first NEW Mac, a Quicksilver 867, I  
put one of the mini CD's in it and tried to play it.  I even bought  
one of those adapter rings to fit on it.  Result:  The CD jammed in  
the drive and the adapter broke into pieces.  Thank God that I had  
just purchased the Mac only a month earlier.
I brought it back to the Apple Store and they had it back for me  
within 2 days with a new DVD drive.  Seems that the CD had broken  
quite a few of the internals, not to mention all of the broken pieces  
of the adapter.
After that, I swore I would never attempt that again.
