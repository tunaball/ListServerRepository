> Can IMS Twin Turbo 128 PCI video card be used in a G4

They stopped working in 10.3. (I just tested a couple to be sure that  
they hadn't started working again in 10.4.)

You should be able to find a Rage 128 for next to nothing with a  
little Web/Ebay searching. Other options are the ATI Radeon 7000 or  
9200 - making sure you're getting the PCI Mac version.
