I can't recall the model number.  I had been using it for more than five years.  Just a wired keyboard, but good feel in the keys, and, yes F12 does eject the CD/DVD discs.  Oh, I just looked in the closet at the MacAlly box.  It said "iKey" USB for mac.  But, I used it as well as a Logitech mouse (wired) with an electronic KVM switch (Geffen--expensive, ~$400) to run both a PC and a G4 Quicksilver from the same Apple 23" Cinema Display (ADC connector), keyboard and Mouse. 

I have spent the better part of the last week or so, slowly removing the maze of wiring that fed all that, including the peripherals for each computer (external CD/DVD drives, HD's, DYMO LabelWriter, etc.) and re-wiring neatly for my new Intel 20" iMac in which I run Windows XP  as a virtual macAnd, I do not miss either all the internal fan noise from the G4 and the PC, nor all the heat that they threw off into my home office during the summer.  Big task coming up will be boxing all those spare peripherals and finding a place to store them all.
