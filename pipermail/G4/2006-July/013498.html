> You don't say whether you have a PRO model or not, or whether  
> you're connecting via analog (VGA) or digital (DVI). (If the card  
> has DVI then you have a PRO model.) From what I've been able to  
> find on the net either model card should be able to drive this  
> monitor at it's native resolution via the VGA connector. However,  
> older cards like these lack the digital bandwidth needed to support  
> the native resolution via the DVI (digital) connector. (You're  
> likely limited to 1280x1024.)
>
> You'll need to upgrade your card if you want to use a digital  
> connection. Check with a knowledgeable source like http:// 
> www.smalldog.com or perhaps OWC (http://www.macsales.com).
