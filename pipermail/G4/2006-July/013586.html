I like the Kensington Keyboard-in-a-box.  i think I got mine for $15  
a year ago.  It's got the power key on it, but no volume up and down  
buttons nor an eject button like the standard apple keyboard, but  
it's no biggie to me.   Also, the cable going from the keyboard to  
the USB jack is so much longer that I don't need an extension USB  
cable like I did with the Apple keyboard.  The Warranty is awesome  
too.  I got a screw caught in mine while building a model, and i took  
the keyboard apart.  I couldn't get the spacebar to work right so I  
called hoping to get a schematic or replacement springs.  But they  
said it was still under warranty in spite of me disassembling it and  
they sent one out at no charge, even free shipping!
