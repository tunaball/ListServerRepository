On my G5, I run Office 2004 and of course Word.  I also have Pages  
which is part of iWork and Mellel.  On this MacBook, Office runs like  
a cow in mud so I looked for Universals.  To my surprise Mellel and  
Pages are both Universal and I use either one or the other on it.   
Actually Mellel has more options than Pages although Pages has some  
features that can't be overlooked either.
So, to make a long email short, Pages and Mellel are both less than  
$100 and work very well.  Too bad you don't subscribe to MacAddict as  
they reviewed 12 alternatives to Word and rated them all and included  
pricing information.
