A2 is necessary for making reasonable size prints of art work. I wouldn't
call that massive, kind of normal in fact. An Epson Pro 4000 costs $1999AUD
and a 4800 costs $2849AUD. Either way more than I can afford so I don't make
prints for sale. I would never insult my artwork by producing anything for
public consumption on the 1200. The lightfastness is too poor in all
circumstances. I do use it however as a creative tool as I work out images.
(sort of like a sketch)

It is a dream to be able to actually make an art print. But not until I get
a good enough printer, and that may never happen.
