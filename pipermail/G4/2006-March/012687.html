Mark,

Check inside the case case, but i don't think you can easily add two  
5.25" drives to your Quicksilver G4. You would have to make some  
modifications to the drive carrier etc, to do that.  In that case you  
would be better off getting an external firewire 5.25" drive  
enclosure.  The only g4's to support two 5.25" drives with out  
modification are the G4 Mirrored Drive Door ( a.k.a. MDD) or later  
computers if I am not mistaken.
