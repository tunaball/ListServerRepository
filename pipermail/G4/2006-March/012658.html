Pioneer DVR-111D DVD+/-R/RW Writer

The Pioneer 111D is the latest in the popular line of Pioneer DVD  
writers for home users and professional duplicators, and is the  
successor to the popular Pioneer 110D
There are two main differences between the Pioneer 111D and its  
former version the Pioneer 110D.

- The chassis has been redesigned to reduce noise and vibration from  
the drive.
- The Pioneer 111D is capable of 5X reading with DVD-RAM, whereas the  
Pioneer 110D would only read DVD-RAM at 2X speed.
