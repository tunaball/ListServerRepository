Tony,
You don't have to actually re-type the sig every time you want to add it to
a mailbox's available sigs. Once it's created in the "All Signatures" list,
click on the identifying name you gave it in the middle pane of the window
and drag it onto the name of the mailbox you want to have that sig and it
will copy itself to that mailbox's sig list.
Hope that helps save you some time.
