You can just save your user identity on the external.

Harddrive / Users / YourIdentity

Saving your identity will save all your data (of that user) and it can  
be transfered to your fresh install.

After you do your fresh install make sure you repair permissions  and  
repair them every couple months.  This will head off many small  
problems that occur over time.  There is also a daily/weekly/monthly  
house-clean your computer will do if you leave it on and awake over  
night.  You can force it to do the housekeeping in terminal.
