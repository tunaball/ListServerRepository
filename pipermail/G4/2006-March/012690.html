Aaron:

Do yourself a favour and do not mix your RAM.  READ your manual or 
check for it at Apple and get the RAM that is recommended by Apple as 
the correct module.  IMO The DDR will not function in that computer and 
could actually break it.  Each of these RAM chips you named has 
different bus speeds and different latency and are vastly different 
from one another.

It's your computer and your money so what you do and how you spend your 
dough is up to you.
