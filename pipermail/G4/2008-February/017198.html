Hello, Bill...
I have a wonderful old UMAX Powerlook II with transparency adaptor and it
does a great job hooked up to my G3 266 beige tower. I also have a SCSI to
Firewire adaptor for it and was planning on hooking the scanner up to my MDD
or DA G4. What stopped me from doing it was the OS. The scanner software
only goes as high as OS9. A scanner this old does not have support for any
version of OSX. I might be able to make it work using classic on the G4, but
as long as my old G3 holds up I see no real benefit to changing. I also have
a Nikon LS-2000 dedicated film scanner hooked up to this G3 and I can still
download software for OS9 from Nikon and UMAX for both of these scanners. I
move the scans to my G4s with a Zip and work on them in CS2 or CS3
Photoshop. Works just fine.
Kathi
