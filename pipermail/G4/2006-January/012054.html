> Anyone else starting to notice the change in FireWire drive

> I'm starting to add external FireWire drives that are dual FW 400/800
> with 6 pin (400) and 9 pin (800) connectors. The old FW400 drives had
> two 6-pin connections for easy daisy-chaining. The new dual speed
> drives have two 9-pin (800) and one 6-pin which doesn't allow for
> connecting two FW400 drives.

> Do 6-pin/9-pin converters exist? if so do they work?

I do not know about converters, but 6 to 9 pin cables exist in various 
lengths.  Check them out at Apple's web site, made by Belkin, and 
various catalogues and maybe Office Depot, Staples, Best Buy.

I cannot vouch for functionality, but I assume these cables are for 
what you want to do.  You can get more info in Apple's support pages on 
queries for 9-pin etc, and Firewire 800.
