Try deleting this file in your home folder:
"/Users/yourname/Library/Preferences/com.apple.systempreferences.plist"

Then reboot and check your Display Preferences again.

I use a NVIDIA GeForce2 MX with TwinView (64MB) on my Quicksilver and  
have no problem using two displays. I'm not sure if the NVIDIA  
GeForce2 MX (32MB) will do the same, but I believe it should. Check  
your System Profiler for exact description of your card.

HTH,

Rick
