Hello Jerry

Open the terminal And type 'sudo nvram boot-args=romndrv=1' then hit  
enter.
It will then ask you for your password.
After that reboot and your voodoo5 will come to life.

If your start your machine with another system X, and then go back it  
will have defaulted to a black n white 640 x 480 and you will have to  
type it back in again, I have the line of text in an .rtf file, to  
copy and then paste into terminal (I am too lazy to learn it).
