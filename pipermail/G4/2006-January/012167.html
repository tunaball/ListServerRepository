I've upgraded my computer G4 350mhz up to 1 GHz from Sonnet (brand  
name @ newegg.com) for $200. I purchased my online and been fine ever  
since.  I've upgraded several others and I'm getting good results. I  
normally purchase items from either newegg, amazon, or owc. In your  
situation, the prices would range from $200 to $600, depending on  
what you want.
