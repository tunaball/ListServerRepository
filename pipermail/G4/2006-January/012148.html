On 16 January, 2006, at 10:38:39, TmB wrote:

> Also is a hundred bucks for 120 gig internal HD a good deal?
>

A: No, you should be able to find replacement hard drives for less  
than $.60/Gb. As long as it is an IDE or EIDE drive and Ultra ATA 66  
compatible, you should be able to format it to work with your G4.

> do i have an alternative to getting a airport card to add this old  
> baby to my wireless network?

A: Yes, you can use an internal PCI wireless card, just be sure to  
check that it is compatible with Macintosh computers. Check the  
manufacturers Belkin, LinkSys, and D-Link for card compatibility.
