When the MDD is totally dead, it usually means that the power supply  
has died. My MDD suffered the same fate as yours with no light on the  
power button. I had good luck with a rebuilt power supply from Ebay  
seller baltimora2000, which brought my MDD back to life.  
baltimora2000 also bought my dead power supply to fix and resell.

Hope this helps,
