Correction: You can drag an icon from the dock anywhere on the screen
(not just the trash) and it'll go up in smoke. The original application
(or folder/file, as the case may be) will still be on the hard drive.

> drag it back to the dock later if you want. That way
> you can keep your dock uncluttered, and have all your
> most used apps to hand.
