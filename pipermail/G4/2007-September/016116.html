Just got the new Apple wired keyboard.  I have it hooked to my  
PowerMac G4/533 running OSX 10.4 and all the keys seem to work as  
they should.  I am very happy with the feel and have not had any  
trouble typing.  It is so thin it feels like the keys are on the  
table itself.  I had the original plastic keyboard made before the  
Eject key was added and really like having volume, eject and forward- 
delete finally.  I was kind of afraid it would not give me all the  
bells and whistles when hooked to my Elder Mac but it does, so I  
thought I would share in case others were concerned.

The box states you need OSX 10.4.10 or later, and I did need to  
download drivers to get it to work.  I am guessing the drivers won't  
work on older OSes.
