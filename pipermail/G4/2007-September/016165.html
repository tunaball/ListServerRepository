Cheaper: nope.
Easier: Roxio Toast 6 or later will automate the process completely.  
Go to Copy. Insert your disk. Click the copy button. Tell Toast to do  
10. Click Go. When it spits out your original disk, put the first  
blank DVD in. Repeat for the rest of the copies.
