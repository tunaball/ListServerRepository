On 09/14/07,  "russells at strodtbeck.org" <russells at strodtbeck.org> wrote:
>
> When I open Safari Browser in OS 10.3.9 the Key Chain dialog box comes up
>asking for a password. How does one keep this from coming up with
> out the need to enter a password.

Safari->Preferences->Autofill->check the bottom two items for your Homepage and
delete that entry. Restart Safari. Alternatively, change the Homepage to
something that doesn't require a password.
