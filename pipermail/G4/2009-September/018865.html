Hi Stefan,

nice ode to G4! I fully subscribe to what you wrote.

I run two G4s "Digital Audio" with CPU upgrades. One with a Sonnet 1.4 
GHz CPU on 10.4.11 for using Final Cut, the other with a 800MHz MP 
(dual) from Quicksilver on 10.3.9 for using Protools.

Most important is to upgrade the RAM to it's maximum (1,5G in Digital 
Audio). And then maybe you could put an SATA card and speed up the disk 
access. Maybe you find a CPU upgrade on Ebay.
