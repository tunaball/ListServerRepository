Ken
Since you feel comfortable installing a new hard drive ,you'll have no problem with this
That G4 Tower face plate comes off easily.
Small flat screwdriver upper left side , near the CD/DVD drive area. Pry out gently
Do the same below in the area of the Zip.
The right side tabs are a bit longer but can be gently disengaged to reveal
VOILA , the emergency eject hole.
That should work & try reloading the disk with the face plate off
( in case it gets stuck again )
If the problem is the drive ( as I suspect it is ) you can even get
an external to work with the tower
BTW if you want to replace the drive with an internal let me know
