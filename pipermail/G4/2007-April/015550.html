Nope, all of the Airport base stations are backward compatible,  
meaning that even the newest base stations support computers  
connecting with the original Airport cards. They're just limited to  
the speed of the card (802.11b runs at 11mb/s, rather than the  
theoretical 54 for 802.11g or even faster for 802.11n). You should  
just have to reset the network password on your eMac to the one on  
your new networks. Just make sure you're using the same security  
encryption on both sides.

Hope this helps.
