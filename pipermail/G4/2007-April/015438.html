I would advise you to visit www.otherworldcomputing.com and see all  
the upgrades available for your particular model.
Personally I would think about a 1gz or better cpu, at least another  
512 of mem, and a slightly used display card ( Nvidia  mx4 or better?)
That could put you in the ballpark and come in about $300 if you  
choose correctly.
I have had good luck with their stuff. (mostly) At the very least you  
can find out the exact parts and shop somewhere else (ebay).
