> If you look in the following folder, you'll find a menu extra that  
> you can add to the finder bar, in the clock area:
>
> <hard drive> /System/Library/CoreServices/Menu Extras
>
> Drag the item named "Eject.menu" to the finder bar, next to where  
> you see the clock and volume icons and such.  This will give you a  
> drop-down menu that you can use to open your drive tray.
