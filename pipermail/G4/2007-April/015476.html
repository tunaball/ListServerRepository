Tom,

I'm late to this thread, but . .

I'd consider disconnecting the hard drive's power connector and  
restarting with the CD in the drive. Do a PRAM reset on the first  
boot attempt, and then see if the system will boot. If successful, go  
into System Preferences and set the CD as your selected boot drive.  
Then shut down, reconnect the HD power, hold the C key and pray.

More extreme measures would include moving the drive to another  
system (internally or via firewire case) for formatting. Give the  
above method a try first, and good luck.

Rick
