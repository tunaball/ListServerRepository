As reported by Other World Computing:

MacWarehouse was just sold to CDW on Monday followed by MicroWarehouse
(parent of MacWarehouse) then filing bankruptcy. Suppliers have taken a hit off this one as while CDW paid 22 Million to attain all the 'good assets' of MacWarehouse leaving their debts behind. Customer's of the cataloger appear for most part unaffected, but if you've got an open order or warranty issue with them from prior to the CDW sale, I would recommend following up on it.
