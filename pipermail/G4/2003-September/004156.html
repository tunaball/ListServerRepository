If you have another drive on that Mac; boot to it and look at "devices" 
in the "Apple System Profiler" to see if the drive shows up. If it does 
you want to boot to OS9 on the working drive. Use a utility like La 
Cie's "Silverlining Lite" (works best for this) to find and mount the 
drive. Run Disk warrior and Norton.

You may also try the drive on something other than the Otto.

If you don't have Silverlining Lite contact me direct.
