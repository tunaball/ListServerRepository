OWC memory is very unstable. I have purchased it many times because of 
the price and 75% of the time it's off spec and causes problems with 
MacOSX. Mac OS9 has less of a standard for memory but Mac OSX doesn't 
take off spec RAM very well at all. If money isn't that big of an 
issue, try RamJet (http://www.ramjet.com/). Never had a single problem 
with their memory, full lifetime warranty. I believe you get the same 
with OWC but it's a hassle sending memory sticks back and forth to find 
a good one.
