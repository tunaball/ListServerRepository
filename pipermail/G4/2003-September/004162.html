If anyone is familiar with the very popular title for Xbox, Halo is due 
out for Mac soon. The PC version has gone Gold Master and is due out 
next week. But the Mac version is still in Alpha. MacSoft, who is 
publishing the game, hired Westlake Interactive to do the MacOSX port. 
Rough system requirements according to the latest Faq is 800-900Mhz 
processor, at least 32MB of VRAM and 256MB of RAM free. Just thought 
I'd let everyone know...this means a lot to Mac gamers...all 8 of them!
