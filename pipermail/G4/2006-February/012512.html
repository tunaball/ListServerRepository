Jerry,
I had a similar problem a while back.  After trying another power supply and 
logic board to no avail I changed keyboards.  I swapped the pro keyboard for 
one from an older model like an AGP or PCI model, the kind with a power 
buttom on it.  It fired up just fine, the only thing was that the power 
button on the case was not lit.  Checked the cables but it still stayed 
dark.  I found a power button on ebay for around $25 and every thing has 
been fine since.  Good luck.
