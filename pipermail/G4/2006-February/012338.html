Hi Marcos,

In OSX, select  'System Preferences' icon from the dock;

select 'International' (UN flag) from the Personal row;

in the Language tab, scroll down and select 'Portugues' from the lower part
of the list;

select the Formats tab check the 'Show All Regions' box - Brazil is a
subset of Portugal,  and change 'Dates', 'Time' & 
'Numbers' as needed;

select Input Menu tab, scroll down 1/3 and click on the box next to the
Brazilian flag;

click on the red button at the upper left of the window to quit the
'International' and save your changes.



Hope this helps,

Jon
