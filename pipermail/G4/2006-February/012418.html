First make sure the airport card is turned off.

Than go to system preferences and under the ethernet setting and  
click on renew DHCP lease.

After that:

When you Bring up ethernet in system preferences is there an IP  
address if so what is it? (linksys routers use 192.168.1.xxx)

What does the Network status say when you bring up network under  
system preferences?
