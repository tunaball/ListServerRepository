I did a side by side test with Kodak Pro ISO 160 color neg on a studio 
product shot.  Made 20" c-prints at same magnification, same 
sharpening, and there was no comparison.  The SLR/n image had no 
visible grain and had a marked increase in apparent sharpness.  It 
wasn't even close.
