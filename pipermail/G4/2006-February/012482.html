	iTunes does give several options for importing songs under 
its Preferences-Advanced-Importing section, one of which is AIFF 
Encoder. If that doesn't solve your problem, then I would suggest 
Amazing Slow Downer from www.ronimusic.com. Among it other attributes 
is the ability to change format between AIFF, mp3, Quicktime, and 
AAC/M4A. It would also give you the ability to change the speed and 
pitch of your sounds, should you so desire.
