DVD-ROM drives shipped stock in G4 AGPs (Sawtooth). My Sawtooth had  
one which I replaced with a Pioneer DVR-109.
But the DVD-ROM drive, as I remember, could read DVDs, just not write  
them.

Not being able to watch movies on DVD may be a function of the app,  
ie, DVD Player not working with the drive or system. Does the DVD  
mount on the desktop? Is the DVD disk clear of smudges and fingerprints?

I once had a problem with the DVD-ROM not reading CDs/DVDs well. I  
bought a ~$10 laser lens cleaning disk at CompUSA and that helped  
restore good function to the DVD-ROM drive.

HTH.
