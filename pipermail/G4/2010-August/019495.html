Hi John

I can answer just part of your message.  As the external USB drive has 
its own power it should work when plugged in to your new keyboard.  
However, although the keyboard has a built in USB 2 hub you will not get 
USB 2 performance unless the keyboard is connected to a USB 2 port on 
your computer.  I am not familiar with all the G4 specifications but I 
think  it's true to say that you are unlikely to have a USB 2 port 
unless a PCI card adding this functionality has been added since its 
manufacture.

Sorry I can't answer your questions about startup drives.
