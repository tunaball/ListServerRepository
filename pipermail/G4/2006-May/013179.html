In my humble opinion, the best NewsReader by far for Macintosh users

Northwestern University and for the last n years has been maintained  
and upgraded by Simon Fraser:

His website is   http://www.smfr.org/

His email address is   mailto:smfr_mtnw at smfr.org

I suggest you download this (free) and give it a try.  It has been my  
Number One newsreader for the past 8 or 10 years.
