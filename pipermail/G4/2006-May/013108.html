These facts are the critical issue here. Steve's machine just may be  
one of those that CANNOT boot to an OS-9 CD unless it is a certain  
version of OS-9.

I have repaired many G3 iMacs (tray and slot loaders alike) as part  
of my volunteer work with the school district and found that the  
later slot loader machines want at least OS-9.1 or .2 to boot from.  
Otherwise, they refuse to boot at all and keep giving the flashing  
question mark icon.
