I agree with Bill. I recently installed, for the first time ever, an 
internal DVD-RAM LG GSA-H10A in my G4/400/AGP, it took a few minutes, 
and it works wonderfully. It's a multi format driver and the cost is 
only a third of an external driver, in my case  the equivalent to just 
over 50 US dollars (i live in Sweden). I can also vouch for Patchburn, 
it immediately recognized this driver, so now it works with Finder, 
itunes, and Toast.

Read the intructions how to install from a pdf-file (choose Digital 
Audio), available to download from:

http://www.apple.com/support/manuals/powermac/

Cheers!
