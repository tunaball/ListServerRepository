Never mind - read all of the comments and it appears that resizeVolume 
will only work on Intel Macs. I'm sure what you want to do is possible. 
A tool that may help you would be Carbon Copy Cloner. It will allow you 
to copy an OS X volume to another drive AND make it bootable. Not sure 
how well it handles bootable OS 9 partitions, though.
