Steve - I might be able to help a little with your advice query.
Replacing the CD drive with the Pioneer 111D is about as easy as it 
gets. I am nervous when entering the guts of my Quicksilver, and I did 
the same replacement in about 15 minutes, including time to calm the 
butterflies in my gut. Try the OWC website for very detailed ( with 
step-by-step ) photos  of the install procedure.
I also looked at the sweet multiport, but was informed by the dealer - 
who is a top flight Mac mecahnic that "

it uses the a jumper from your built
in firewire and usb.  You end up with more ports total though but are
relagated to the built in speeds."

I can't speak to your USB 2.0/FW 800 question as it relates to OS9.
Hope that helps,
