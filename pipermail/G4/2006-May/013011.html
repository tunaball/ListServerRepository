>What kind and size of HD would you recommend for a G4 AGP 450? How big would be too big?
>Also, where have you found is the best place to get one. Thanks to all.

You'll find that the built-in ATA controller is limited by hardware to about 128 GB.

There are PCI options that allow more and also allow for SCSI. The second disk is easy using existing wiring provided by Apple. The third takes more work.
