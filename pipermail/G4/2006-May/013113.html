An anonymous army captain once said: if the terrain and the map don't 
match, trust the terrain. Here is my proposal: Run Xbench with and 
without the erroneous sticks, and compare if the memory performance 
differs in any way. According to Mactracker, the sticks must comply with 
the following: PC133 3.3 V, unbuffered, 8-byte, non-parity 168-pin 
SDRAM. I have also read somewhere that they cannot have ECC (Error 
Correction Code). I hope some of this was helpful./Cheers Lars
