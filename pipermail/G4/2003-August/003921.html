On 8/20/03 5:51 PM, "Shannon Nugent" <shannon at nuge.com> wrote:

> Is there a way I can organize the names and phone numbers of businesses
> that I have sent my resumes to?

Most of the contacts databases automate much of this for you. For example,
if you send your resume by email, Entourage for OS X keeps track of when you
sent it, and you can add all kinds of stuff to the address book entry that's
created automatically just by sending the email. You can put all such
contacts in a "job hunting" category so that later on you can search your
activity by date or whatever other razor you wish. If you want to store the
ads you're responding to, scan them and put the scanned images into one of
the fields in the contacts database.
