Ryan,
The fastest PCI video card that will run on a Mac is the ATI Radeon Mac 
Edition. It is actually faster than the Radeon 7000 that came after it. 
Unless you can run across some old stock somewhere, you would probably 
have to find it on eBay. I have one in my Beige G3 and used the Quartz 
Extreme hack to enable QE on the machine. It is a dramatic improvement 
over the stock video and provided acceptable 3D performance within the 
bounds of the Beige architecture. You would likely see better 
performance in your Sawtooth than I have.
HTH,
Mel
