I have used many (in the hundreds) IBM drives in different Power Macs and 
have never found one that "would only work with a G3 or G4 Mac". As long as they  
showed up in the Drive Setup window and let me format them, they would work 
in any Mac that I was working on at the time, from the 6400 up to the 9600 
models and G3 desktop and towers. It didn't matter whether the drive was an IDE or 
SCSI drive as long as I was using a newer version of Drive Setup such as the 
version that comes with OS 8.5 or 8.6
