Go to versiontracker.com and look for Safari Enhancer.  This wee 
program will require the BSD subsystem (of OSX) be installed to 
function (located on your OSX install disc).  It will turn on the Debug 
Menu of Safari and a few other things.  This menu has a choice to 
import favourites (IE) into your bookmarks list. And the Enhancer will 
allow you to import any html page of bookmarks as well.  AWESOME useful 
program.

jj

PS and its free.
