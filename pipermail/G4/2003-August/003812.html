If you can be PATIENT and carefully study Sunday Paper ad inserts, 
there are really good deals after rebates from OfficeMax, Circuit City, 
CompUSA and Best Buy. I was able to "buy" three 256 MB - 2100 DDR 
Kingston RAM modules for my dual 867 MDD for FREE one per month May, 
June and July. Then, with a friend's help as a second rebate recipient, 
I was able to buy TWO 512 MB - 2700 DDR Kingston RAM modules for $92 
including tax after rebates (for both, 2100 and 1.6 GHz G5  compatible) 
at Circuit City in July. So that was about $100 total for 1.75 GB of 
RAM.
