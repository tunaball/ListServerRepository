Two notes - the Front Side Bus on the new G5's is 1/2 the processor 
speed.  The lowest-end G5 has a 800MHz FSB.

Also, in naval warship design, it's a rule of thumb to limit the 
amount of "new" technology to only about 10% of the overall design. 
Why?  Because new technologies are that - new, not thoroughly tested, 
and more prone to breakage.  Reliability of warships is paramount to 
their survival in a battle.

The new G5's have a lot that's new in them.  I wouldn't trust any 
mission-critical work to them until they've had some shake-down time.
