On Friday, August 22, 2003, at 07:16  AM, Dale Hoffman wrote:

> How can I force named items to drop to the bottom of an alphabetical
> window listing in OSX?
>

The short list of characters I've found that'll alphabetically force to 
bottom:
      π (option p)
      ∏ (shift option p)
      ∑ (option w)
      ∆ (option j)
      Ω (option z)
      µ (option m)
