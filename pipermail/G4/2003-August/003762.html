PCI video cards aren't too good with Jaguar, so I'd try a different
solution. The Radeon 9000 you have in the Sawtooth is WASTED because the
Sawtooth can't drive an ADC monitor, so I'd suggest selling it and getting a
Radeon 8500, which does offer dual monitor support (DVI and VGA). Or else
try trading it straight up for an 8500, maybe there's somebody with an 8500
who wants to use an ADC display.
