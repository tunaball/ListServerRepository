Peter,
           Many times with Digidesign-as applications are added to the G4
that might not nessessarily have anything to do directly with Pro Tools, the
"Preferences" get a little tweeked!. Trash the router prefs' and Digi prefs'
and restart. If the prefs don't reload at start-up, then replace them from
your applications install disks.........My G4 goes through this every so
often, and I use Pro Tools also.
