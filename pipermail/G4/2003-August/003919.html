Take a look at Sticky Brain http://www.chronusnet.com.  It is a flat 
file with nearly instant lookup on anything in the file.  But, you 
can create different data sheets with different categories.  One nice 
thing about it is that you can just paste any text into it without 
having to fill it in field-by-field.  It is shareware for about US$40.
