it all depends what you are going to use the second monitor for. i use 
dual monitors, one on a GeForce3, one on a voodoo3 every day in jaguar, 
and have absolutely no problems with it.  a cheap pci card (like the 
voodoo) is perfect if you dont need 3D acceleration. now if you were 
going to go out and get a mac edition radeon for $120, then i would 
recommend the 8500 dual head or something along those lines because 
price for performance, the mac edition is the worst card out there.
