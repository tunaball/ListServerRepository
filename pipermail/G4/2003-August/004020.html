On 8/27/03, Kurt Appling <wappling at tampabay.rr.com> wrote:
>
> hi all, my presant safari is corruted ,and shuts off and quits
> constantly,I wish to know how to save the bookmarks seperately then
> erase and reinstall safari and then my old bookmarks,is there a simple
> way to do this ?  thankyou in advance

They're stored in the ~/Library/Safari folder-that's your users folder ~/.
Make a copy, trash that folder, the safari.plist, and Safari. Reinstall,
launch Safari, quit Safari, replace the new bookmarks file with the copy
and you're good to go.
