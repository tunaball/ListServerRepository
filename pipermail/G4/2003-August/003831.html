Upgrading the video card for your Mac may not do much for your Virtual
PC applications. I remember reading that Virtual PC does not support
video cards for running PC games, I do not think that Windows
applications can use the video cards feature. If this is why you are
upgrading the video card, you might want to poke around the VirtualPC
support site and see if there is an answer before you shell out the
bucks.
