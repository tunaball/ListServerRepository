On 3 August, 2006, at 3:48:50, Chris wrote:

> My first question is "What have I got?" I mean to say "what is my  
> machine referred to as?"

I suggest that you do a google search for "Mactracker", download it,   
and compare all the information that this free program will make  
available to you.

The Mactracker database has all the specification information for  
each Mac made and you can even determine what upgrades can be done to  
the model you now own. The database is regularly updated so you will  
be getting the latest information when you download it.

I use Mactracker constantly in my volunteer work  because it is much  
easier to refer to it than to try to memorize all the specs for  
dozens of Macs.
