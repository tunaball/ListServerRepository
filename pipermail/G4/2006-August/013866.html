> I ran disk repair from another startup disk (fixed a lot of stuff)

Lots of freezes can certainly lead to lots of disk problems due to  
unclosed files and unflushed buffers and caches.

Freeze as in locks up (mouse and/or keyboard stop responding) or as  
in a translucent grey screen indicating a kernel crash? In either  
case this should happen rarely, something is definitely wrong with  
your computer. It's time to break out your hardware test CD. Also you  
may want to look into this: http://www.memtestosx.org/
