On 3 August, 2006, at 11:48:32, Ron Michaels wrote:

> The woofer is between my two 19-inch NEC monitors. Suddenly my  
> monitors have "screwed up" color.

Your posting gives the reason for your problem. The speakers have  
stronger magnets in them than you had before, the stronger magnets  
have more effect on the travel of the electron beams to the monitor  
screen, your image color goes all WONKY when that happens.

Solution:    Move the speakers away from the monitor until the  
wonkiness stops, then don't move them any closer.
