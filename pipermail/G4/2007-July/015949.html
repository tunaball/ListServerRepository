http://listserver.themacintoshguy.com/mailman/listinfo/g4

or from digest mode, change your password, etc.), visit your
subscription page at:

http://listserver.themacintoshguy.com/mailman/options/g4/kiwi88usa%40yahoo.com

G4-request at listserver.themacintoshguy.com

quotes), and you will get back a message with instructions.

the password, itself) or to unsubscribe. It is:

will email your current password to you.
