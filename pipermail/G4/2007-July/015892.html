I was responding to Roxylee that a Rage 128 card is not an ATA  
controller. Perhaps confusion arised because of its brand being ATI?

And to answer question 2, Roxylee is talking about using a very large  
hard drive in that Mac, and it isn't supported at its full capacity  
with the built-in controller. Ergo, a third-party controller card  
would be an excellent solution at this point.

3, I don't know which video card was removed and which was installed.  
I'd simply encourage Roxylee to use the newest available card, since  
there is no logical reason not to. The better the video card, the  
more capable the system is of various video-related tasks; most  
especially, of course, 3D.
