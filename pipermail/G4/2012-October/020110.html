I run 10.5 on an MDD 867 Dual with no problems. You'll want to have as much
RAM as you can fit in the PowerMac, mine is maxed out at 2GB and runs
great. One thing to note is you will need a retail disk of 10.5 to install,
not a disk that came with a different Mac as those are machine specific.
