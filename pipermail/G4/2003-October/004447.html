Good advice from Sandor.... follow it.

FYI, CCC got hung up one time on my machine.  I rebooted from the backup
drive and ran the Disk Utility to repair the primary drive. Rebooted from
the Primary Drive and then used Disk Utility to Erase (then repair) the
Backup Drive.  Ran CCC and it worked perfect.

I make it a point to always Erase and Repair the backup drive before running
CCC.  Seems to work faster  too.
