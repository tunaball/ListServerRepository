     Christine Cano wrote:

>Gimp-print does not work with all hp printers.
>It does not work with mine which is an hp2100m.

>the non-Gimp-supported printers.

     The list of supported printers is at:

	http://gimp-print.sourceforge.net/p_Supported_Printers.php3 .

At the bottom of the list is a list of other, PostScript printers 
(including the 2100) that are not officially supported, but have been 
reported to work, at least "incompletely, or the may not actually 
work at all" at PostScript level 2 (but not yet 3) via their native 
(in this case, pcl-6) drivers. I was wondering if you'd given it a 
try. The 2100m is nominally a 2100 with PostScript support added; it 
should still support PCL.
