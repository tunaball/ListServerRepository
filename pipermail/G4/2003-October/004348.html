If I remember correctly it must be slave or master but not Cable 
Select.  It would only runn at 66 mhz if you attatch it to the same ATA 
bus as your DVD/CD drive.  As a second HD on the same bus as your 
current HD (slave or master) it will communicate at full speed (ie 100 
or 167 mhz).
