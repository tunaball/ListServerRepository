Hi

There used to be a sleep (low power mode) problem with G4s not waking  
from sleep if they had cards in the PCI slots, have you got a card in  
one of these slots, I had a problem with my G4 and eventually got so  
fed up restarting I stopped it going to sleep, I don't know if it  
still a problem as I haven't tested it for years now.
If this is the problem you can go to your profiler and it will say  
something like 'low power mode disabled'.
