received a total of 60,000 Power PC 970 chips from Apple.

Further revs of this chip, the site said, include 2GHz parts which are
likely to be around in mid-June.
And the same site said that the PPC 970 will fly at 2.3GHz and 2.5GHz by the
end of the year.
