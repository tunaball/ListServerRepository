Sonnett.
Unfortunately, at the same time I upgraded the system from OS 9.0.4 to
9.2.2. Perhaps out of fear of more upgrades, my computer no longer goes
to sleep in the regular manner. If I put the machine to sleep, it drops
off immediately; but, when I try to awaken it, it crashes. (If I set the

over corner. Also, the CD burner does not work. When I attempt to start
from the CD's of DiskWarrior and TechTool Pro, the computer freezes.

thought about a clean install, but all of the OS upgrades were
downloaded. My son has OS 9.2.2 on a CD that came with his laptop. Could
I use that? Or could I somehow load the system that is on the desktop
onto a zip disk? And, if so, How?
Other than those problems everything seems to be working well. Maybe it
is just G5 envy.
