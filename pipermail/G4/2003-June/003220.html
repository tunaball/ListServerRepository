If you go to the archive for this list:
http://lists.themacintoshguy.com/Lists/
and search for keyboard cleaning, there are some hints and links. I can't
remember if I got this tip from there: in the case of a nasty spill into the
keyboard, it is a-ok to pour distilled water (after disconnecting OF
COURSE), into the keyboard and let drip out of corner, and then allow to dry
completely.
