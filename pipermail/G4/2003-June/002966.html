Oh yeah, one other thing I should mention about USB adapters and 
sound - DO NOT get one of these things, any brand, if you intend to 
use it when recording multi-track audio, even for home use. The 
latency caused by re-routing the audio signal through the USB is 
horrific, and will be made even worse if you try it through a hub.
