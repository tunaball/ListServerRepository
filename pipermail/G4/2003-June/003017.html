Surfing apples pages in their forums and found out that there is an 
invisible file that deals with deep sleep in os x.

The post said to delete the "com.apple.Powermanagement.xml " file in 
this directory path "Macintosh HD/hidden/var/db/SystemConfiguration"

Use TinkerTool 2.32 <http://www.bresink.de/osx/TinkerTool2.html> to 
turn on your invisible files and you will find the culprit in no time 
at all.
