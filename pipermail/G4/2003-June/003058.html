I suggest that you get the largest monitor that you can 1) reasonably fit on 
your desktop and 2) your wallet will let you buy.

I tried the multiple monitor setup, but found that smaller screens did not 
give my tired, old eyes the size image that they need in order to see the 
details. Multiple monitors means more space taken up on the table/desktop than a 
single large monitor.
