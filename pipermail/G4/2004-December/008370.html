I figured out what was happening.  I installed Panther on another HD 
when I was trying to solve a video card issue.  When I tried to back up 
my home directory to that HD, I selected "overwrite" for the existing 
files.  Apparently, versa-portrait90R.pct is part of the system folder. 
  So, it would not let me overwrite the home directory that was already 
on that HD.  So, I just renamed the old directory, and then was able to 
copy the new one to that drive.  Thanks for the info.
