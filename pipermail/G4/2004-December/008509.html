Quite a while ago, I replaced my CDR with a Pioneer DVR-105, but the 
latest model out is a DVR-108.  You can buy one for less than US$100. 
Look at dealmac.com for the latest pricing, or go to newegg.com or 
macsales.com.

Replacing the drive is no big deal.  Just keep track of what you are 
undoing, and then substitute the new drive and re-do what you undid 
in the reverse order.   The only problem that I had was getting the 
front plate to line up with the door, so that took a bit of jiggling 
around.  Maybe added 5-10 minutes to the process.
