I upgraded my Dual 450 Mystic with 1.2GHz Sonnet and was a little 
disappointed with the gain in performance. I ended up going to a dual 
1.2GHz GigaDesigns. Both these companies are very professional and have 
good backup.

Different manufactures have different system requirements so read the 
specs carefully, you may need to upgrade your firmware and some AGPs 
don't support Duals.

I looked for the CPU that just plugs in and goes. If you can stretch 
your budget, go with a dual.
