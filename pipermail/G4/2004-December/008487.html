I have the Miglia TV/DVR tuner card running on Mac mirrored-door 
1.25mhz and couldn't be happier. I use it for some minor home/consumer 
type production, nothing fancy, but mostly just to watch TV on my 
second monitor while I work. The quality is very good for the money. 
You can click and record whatever you're watching on TV. Also has a 
timer, I think. I paid about $105 when OWC ran a sale. I also make use 
of the FM tuner and it sounds great through my external speakers. Had 
to deal with support once and they responded quickly and answered my 
questions. I recommend it highly. They update the software regularly 
and several people have made plug-ins for various uses.
