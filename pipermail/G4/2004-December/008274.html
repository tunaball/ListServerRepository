>Does anybody know a sure fire way to tell if a Radeon 8500 is a Mac
>edition or a flashed PC?

Only from the barcode label affixed to the back of the card.

>ATI said they can't identify the card  without the P/N 102 number?

The barcode label has this serial number, as well as the model name: 
"Radeon8500Mac64M"

If someone has removed the label, there's no way to tell, since the 
exact same hardware was used for PCs too.  The only difference was in 
the firmware, flashed for PC or Mac.
