Eric:

Use Target Mode to move your data and settings.

Your G4 HD would probably boot the G5 but it may be lacking a few 
refinements that would be installed on the G5, but not on a G4.  Apple 
OS Installs change according to the computer being installed upon.

Your new G5 will have a system Install CD with it - that will be the 
'best' system for your G5 until it is updated.
