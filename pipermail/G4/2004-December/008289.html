I just installed a wireless system on my G4 using the Motorola Card and 
works fine. I have a Linksys WGT54G wireless router. My neighbor a 1/2 
mile  away bought a 15 db antenna and he can log onto my network and 
internet connection. The router was using its stock antenna and he was 
getting a 14% signal level. I also installed a Linksys PCI card in a PC 
that I have to use to run a couple programs and I use it solve other 
people's PC problems. My Mac just keeps charging alon. Also both Mac & 
PC talk with each other and share files. It has been a learning 
experience for me
