As long as it is a newer version of Unstuffit (I think they introduced 
SITX on 7.x), but a quick peek at their web site would tell for sure.  
The way to know is if the SITX file doesn't have a custom icon, then 
chances are you don't have the right version on your machine - when you 
upgrade the custom icon for SITX files will appear (as opposed to the 
generic document form).
