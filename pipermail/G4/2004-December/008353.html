If you are burning a full DVD, that's 4.4GB with only 3.2GB free on the 
HD. That could mean significant fragmentation exists in the source. You 
could check this with TTP4, and could also defragment the HD (including 
the source data for the burn).
I'd try the Dragon Burn demo to see what result you get. In my 
experience, Toast 5.2 was much more prone to buffer underrun than 6.0.7 
is.
