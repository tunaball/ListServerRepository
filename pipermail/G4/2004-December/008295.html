I have a Sonnet Aria Extreme and a Linksys WRT54G router. The Aria 
Extreme works fine *except* that you must manually reconnect to the 
network each time you start the G4. It's only two clicks, but it's still 
annoying. Speeds using WPA encryption were extremely disappointing (2.5 
mbps, ugh), so I had to switch to WEP. I'm not sure whether to blame the 
Aria, the Linksys or my PC for the slow speeds.
