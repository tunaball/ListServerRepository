DA has a few advantages over Sawtooth:
DA has a 133mHz system bus, the Sawtooth has a 100mHz
DA has 1 more PCI slot (4 vs. 3)
DA's AGP slot supports ADC, Sawtooth does not supply power to the AGP  
slot to run an ADC display.

I think that about covers it.
