Hello Mark,

To find out all the details of your Mac, do following:
1) Look for the Serial Number (and NOT Model No or...!). You can find that
in ASP under Hardware and (mostly) also on a sticker with bar-codes
somewhere (mostly on the back) on your Mac. Mark you! The Serial Number has
always 11 characters!! Write it down.
2) Now goto <http://www.chipmunk.nl/klantenservice/applemodel.html> and
enter your Serial Number.
OK, it's a Dutch site, but don't be afraid it is in English as well.
