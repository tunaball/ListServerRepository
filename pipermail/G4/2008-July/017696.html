My experiences with some of the ATTO cards showed me that the majority  
of the cards that had Ultra-Wide
 SCSI ports for the external

installed card with a standard SCSI 25-pin connection, I could boot to  
the external drives.

kinds of ATTO cards and I cannot remember their part numbers. Sorry  
that I cannot give more information than what I have.
