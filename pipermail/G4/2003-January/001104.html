There are some USB modems, but no PCI modems that I know of for Macs. 
Not a very big market, since most come built-in. The Zoom USB modem 
seems to be one of a very few that are current for the Mac. Best Data 
used to sell one, but I don't know if they still do. You could perhaps 
add a serial port PCI card to your Mac if you want to go that 
direction.  Good luck.
