> Does a hub also function as a firewall? I'm using an Assante
> FriendlyNET hub on a small home network because the AT&T signal was
> so poor so much of the time that my connection was slower than
> dial-up with a router.

A hub is NOT a firewall, at least in most cases a hub only distributes
ethernet signals around a LAN. A router can be addressed and controlled to
set up what ports will be open and closed and if you want to have a DMZ and
other things. Assante also makes a FriendlyNET router in addition to the
hub. I have both.
