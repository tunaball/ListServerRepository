He may be able to use the software "drivers" from Epson to directly 
scan, but more likely the drivers from Epson will enable other graphics 
programs to access the scanner. What model of scanner is this [sorry I 
got in on this late]? If he does use Photoshop as long as the Epson 
driver supports TWAIN he will be fine, if he doesn't use Photoshop he 
could use Graphic Converter [shareware], or perhaps, depending on the 
model of scanner he could just use Image Capture and import through it. 
I would also see what info you can get on iPhoto 2.0 because if I 
recall it will support scanning.
