Hi Diane,

My husband did a little research into this issue for our old G4 and he 
was told that if you replace all the chips with faster ones, the 
machine will run better.  But if one or more slower chips are mixed in 
with the faster ones, the processor will adjust to the speed of the 
slower chip.

His suggestion is to go for the faster chip if they aren't that much 
more.

Hope this helps,
