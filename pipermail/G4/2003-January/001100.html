In a message dated 1/30/03 7:24:21 PM, iwanabee at attbi.com writes:

<< They are used for maybe three periods per day. They get recharged in 
between classes. Is there any reason why during the later part of the 
day some of the iBooks lose the internet connection? >>

Several thoughts come to mind:

1. Are the books put to sleep while they are being recharged?
2. Why aren't they plugged into the charger/converter all day long?
3. Are they completely shut down at any time during the day?
4. What is the 1/2 life time period of the TCP/IP address given by your 
server?

Any of the above listed conditions could cause a loss of connection. There 
are some other reasons for losing connection, but not that I think would 
exist in a classroom setting (except for student mis-handling of equipment).
