>> 	1.  I have an Epson Stylus Color 800 printer, and it is not seen by

>
> Look for Gimp-print on versiontracker.com. It is an open source driver 
> package that includes nearly all Epson printers. You will also need to 
> get a USB->parallel adapter, since OS X doesn't support serial 
> printing. I got an Inland adapter for $20 at Microcenter. Belkin's is 
> supposed to work too. Together, I have perfect support for my old 
> Epson 600.
>
>> 	2.  The new OS doesn't see my SCSI hard drive as mountable.
>
> Brand and model? OS X is very picky about SCSI. You may need to reset 
> some jumpers, double check termination, etc.
