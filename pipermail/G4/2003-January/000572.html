I am using a Handspring Visor Edge and a Sony Clie' at work with 
Outlook on Win 2000 and at home on my Mac with Palm Desktop. Handspring 
and Sony (I don't know about Palm) include third party utilities to 
sync Outlook on Windows and they have both worked fine for me. I have 
never used Entourage so I'm afraid I can't help you on that one, but 
the Sony syncs fine to Palm Desktop in OS X even though Sony says they 
don't support Mac OS.
I would give my highest recommendation to the Sony organizers. It's 
funny that Sony has the best take on Palm's OS, but I've played around 
with just about all of them and Sony has the nicest screens and great 
value-added features.
