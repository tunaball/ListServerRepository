5200's!? What OS are you using on them? I still have one in the 
basement, it serves as a phone/fax machine.
Apple states "You cannot use earlier versions of the administration 
application to manage Network Assistant clients." So if 4.0 is on the 
eMac you can't use 3.5.2 to manage it. NWA 4.0 should work fine on the 
iMacs. I don't know if it will work on the 5200's. You can find more 
info at http://www.apple.com/networking/ana

That's a mini-VGA port on the eMac, the adaptor is shown here 
http://store.apple.com/1-800-MY-APPLE/WebObjects/AppleStore?productLearnMore=M8639G/A
You can buy it from your Apple dealer.
