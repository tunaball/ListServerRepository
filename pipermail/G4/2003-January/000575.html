I have a G4 Dual 450 and replaced the AGP Rage pro128 by an  AGP ATI Radeon
Mac edition (32MB) ,(the first that came out, just bought it second hand)
And I also bought the PCI  Radeon 7000 , also ATI and 32 MB for a second
display, 
Everything works fine, the QuartzExtremeCheck-1.1.app
(http://www2.entropy.ch/download/QuartzExtremeCheck-1.1.app.sit)
 told me the AGP display had Quartz extreme, the PCI not,
I enabled it with PCI Extreme!
(http://pages.cthome.net/zacks/pciextreme.sit)
And both run now Quartz extreme everything is OK my sleep is OK
I alsoupdated to the latest ATI driver: radeon-0210
(http://pdownload.mii.instacontent.net/ati/drivers/RADEON-0210.hqx)

Hope this can help somebody?
