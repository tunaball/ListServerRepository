After waiting for someone to respond I stumbled across a Momo at my
local Circuit City so I had to try it out.  Bottom line, it works.

As for the wheel itself, it feels kind of odd, like there's some sort
of gear in there so it's not smooth like a real wheel.  I mean, you
can steer smoothly, but the rubbing feel you get (even when not plugged
in) is strange.  I'd prefer not to have the extra grinding going on.
