Thanks for the pointer. To save others the trouble of searching, 
here's the simple solution, thanks to Milton van der Veen:

" You can just export the bookmarks from Chimera to a bookmarks file 
and then drag that file directly into Safari's Bookmark window [click 
the button at the right of the toolbar] and voila you're in business. 
This works even if you've already imported IE's bookmarks."
