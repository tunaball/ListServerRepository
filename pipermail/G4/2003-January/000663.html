"Does anyone use inDesign? I'm looking for some 
sort of
guide that outlines Quark 4.11 key codes vs. inDesign
key codes. I'm hoping to make the leap to inDesign,
but my speed and skill lies with Quark. I'm also
hesitant to change inDesign's settings to match Quark,
so I thought a guide might prove useful.

Thank you so much!!

Tara"

Indesign will mimic all the key commands for 
Quark. There is a choice in the prefs to set key 
commands as Adobe does or as Quark does. This 
feature makes the migration very easy. And 
Indesign 2 works in OS X -not Quark.

I used Pagemaker before so Adobe keys are what I 
got used to. For you, after the first project, the 
key commands will flow smoother. The menus will 
give you most of your Key shortcuts when you do it 
the slow way. Not a big curve, but still, climbing 
required.
