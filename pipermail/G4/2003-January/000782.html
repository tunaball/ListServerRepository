Also listed on that Apple www page is "Synchronize!" which I use for my 
HD-to-HD backups, if that is what you are looking for when you say 
"mirroring" (ie. not RAID-0 implemented in software, although that 
might be available too somewhere).

I have used Synchronize! under OS9 and I now run it under OSX using the 
"X Plus" version of the package. The reason I bought Synchronize! was 
because of good user reviews. It has worked well for me with zero 
problems.
