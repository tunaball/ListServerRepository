I bought a used DP 800 Quicksilver with a superdrive
for 1700.00.  I had a a 733mhz Audio Digital(the older
model) before that and I notice a big difference.

Great machine for OS X.  The machine has a twinview
graphic card for a VGA and a TFT monitor that can be
used at the same time for dual monitor set-up. Great!!

The DVD burner is great and the advantage is that the
apple burner works perfect with the IApps.  The
disadvantage is that I have had success burning apple
DVD blanks only.  More expensive.

The only disadvantage I have found is that the
Harddrive likes to sleep alot and can be quite slow to
wake up.

Hope this helps
