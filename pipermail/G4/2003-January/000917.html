It sounds as though you corrupted your system software when the 
re-installation attempt went hay-wire.

Instead of trying to perform a system software installation right away, you 
need to use the drive set-up function of the utilities folder to reformat 
your hard drive first.

After doing the reformatting, you should be able to "see" your hard drive and 
designate it as the destination of a system software installation. Then, it's 
just a matter of making the right choices during the installation process.

Good luck.
