Pioneer Releases v1.57 Firmware For DVR-109 and DVR-A09
10:43 AM CST
Posted By: Ian
Pioneer Japan has released yet another set of firmware updates for  
their second generation 16x DVD±RW drives, the DVR-109 and DVR-A09.  
Here is the change log for firmware v1.57:
DVD-R/+R DL Writability has improved
  DVD-R/+R Writability has improved
  Support New +R 8x Media
  (8x writing:UmeDisc/Wealth Fair)

DVR-109 v1.57 - Download

DVR-A09 v1.57 - Download
  Pioneer has also updated their list of supported media to reflect the  
changes in the new 1.57 firmware. If you have any comments or  
questions, feel free to leave them in our forum.
