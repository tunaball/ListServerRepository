I have Macs with Creatures, Apple Pro w/ iSub, Creative, Monsoon 2000  
& 500s and RSL (Roger Sound Lab). Creatures and RSL are extremely  
good under $100 speakers. They beat most speakers at two and three  
times the price. Both systems create a slightly different output that  
would require a subjective choice. The RSL speakers are only $69 and  
have a real 30 day money back offer. The RSL is well made but pretty  
ugly. The Creatures are $20 more and cool looking. I have ended up  
using the RSL spearers the most. The RSL and Creatures speakers have  
good subwoofers and get pretty loud in a 15x24 room.

I have read several reviews of both systems and reviewers find them  
to be the very best of class. I like both systems as much as $200 and  
$300 systems I have.
