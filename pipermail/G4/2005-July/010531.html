I'll just insert a couple of comments here.
I have found that when you have more than two USB devices, it's a good 
idea if you use a powered USB hub.
You don't mention whether your hub is merely a multiple port feed from 
an existing USB port, or it's powered with it's own AC transformer.
I solved a number of mysterious USB problems by going to a powered hub.
