Anne,

Don't read the retrospect Manual, You really don't have too. Look at 
the programs Tabs... Immediate is  Now then under that there is a list 
of buttons Backup, Restore, etc and each of those buttons  has a good 
description of what it does right next to it.. You should never read 
that book from cover to cover, it is a waste of your time. You should 
only look at it if you get stuck on something..

So, Anne to answer your question Click on "Automate" then "Scripts" and 
then "New"...  Set up your monthly complete back up, Chose what to back 
and were to, etc... And once that is done then make another new script 
and this time only select you "working files" assuming your home 
directory or Documents folder instead of your Hard Drive (boot disk).

If you still are confused I would suggest reading some of  the Chapter 
on "Automated Operations" (Chapter 5 in my Book). You don't need to 
read it all just the stuff you want to do probably the first ten pages 
. You should probably stop when you see "Scripted Archive".
