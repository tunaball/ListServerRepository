Hi Anne. Does the drive have an on/off switch? If so, unmount the drive 
after you're finished backing up by dragging the drive icon to the 
trash (which changes to the unmount icon as soon as you grab the drive 
icon), then turn off the drive via the on/off switch. If you unplug the 
drive while it's mounted you could possibly corrupt its data and should 
get an error message. John
