Yes, but we need to know what computer he has. Is it an old Sawtooth G4 
400? Or a Yikes, or something else?  This will determine what video 
card will fit and function and/or if ADC is supported.  The Sawtooth 
will not support ADC without an adapter because there is no USB power 
on the AGP slot.  The adapter uses a USB port to supply the power 
needed.
