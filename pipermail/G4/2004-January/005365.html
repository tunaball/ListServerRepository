My wife has had hers for 8 months, since they first came out. Very 
small, very tightly constructed, and well made. The heat is the only 
downside -- it gets hot, especially in hotter weather (it's nice 
right now, in the winter in Japan), especially on its left side. The 
heat doesn't cause problems, and there's either no fan noise or it's 
so quiet that I've never noticed it coming on. It's well designed and 
I like it a lot -- I'd consider getting one if they ever make a 
breakthrough on the heat. I use a 17 inch since it's also a joy to 
use and doesn't get hot at all.
