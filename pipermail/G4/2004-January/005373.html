Recommendation:

A software review by Walter S. Mossberg (Wall Street Journal) reprinted 
in Tucson- AZ Daily Star

<http://www.dailystar.com/dailystar/accent/5003.php>

  is recommended. It reviews several approaches to moving files from 
your old computer to the new computer you just received during the 
holidays. What is unusual about it and, I might say a surprise to me, 
he does a very fair review of the Mac approach vs all the difficulties 
encountered in the PC world. To summarize -  all 3 packages he tried 
had problems (he could only get one to work) and the FW approach to the 
Mac was almost too easy. It also really makes me appreciate what Carbon 
Copy Cloner does for us.

I recommend you pass this on to all your PC friends.
