Bummer that the sticker has been removed. The cards typically have the 102
part number sticker placed directly above the other sticker you listed. You
can enter the bios # (from Apple System Profiler) on that page I showed you,
but it will return that is a mac card because (obviously) the card is using
the mac bios whether it was flashed or not.

I can't think of any other way to tell the pc from the mac version. Your
observation that the ports are layed out differently might be the solution.

good luck,
Jason
