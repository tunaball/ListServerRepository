You can do better on the price, especially if you only need 2X. Check 
out dealmac for deals, or I like Meritline.com, they have a combo pack 
that you can try different brands all at once.  I have bought 3 
different brands at different times from there, and had no problems at 
all. The only problems I have had is some of the brands from office 
supply stores (hypermedia, maxell, some fuji). I have tried verbatim, 
and didn't have many problems, but I do remember not having 100% 
success either.
