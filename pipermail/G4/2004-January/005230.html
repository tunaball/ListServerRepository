Anne, I am sorry I didn't see the problem earlier; But here it is. OSX 
10.1 does not support Twain. Twain is the way scanners are recognized. 
OSX 10.2 supports Twain and most scanner companies did not get their 
Twain support ready until OSX 10.2.3 or later.

You might want to upgrade to OSX 10.3.2 at this point. You will find it 
much more responsive than 10.1 and besides getting scanner support you 
will get better printer support.
