Sandor,

The Mac OSX System Profiler cannot tell the difference between between
PC100/PC133 and reports everything as PC-100. Boot under OS9 and run the
'DIMM First Aid' and it will tell you what's really going on.  The
freeware,DIMM First Aid only runs under OS9, it was never updated to OSX and
I do not know of a better utility to check your memory.
