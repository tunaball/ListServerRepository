David I think Epson makes the best of these; HP is the most popular but 
not the best. The newest models use DuraBrite Inks, These inks are water 
resistant and have a very long self life. These inks will last a minimum 
of 25 years even on plain paper. This is very important for "important 
documents" and business documents, or anything needing to be mailed. 
Regular inkjet ink has no resistance to water.

Epson also makes the very best scanners. I work a lot with all kinds of 
scanners and Epson makes the best, by far. If you want to know why just 
email me roger at rogerdharris.com
