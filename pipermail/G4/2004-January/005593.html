Ah, the good ol' days.  Most of the time, I just hit backspace from the

> The jumping between mouse and keyboard is annoying and slows down my 
> email reading.

Ya.  It's cumbersome, which a Mac shouldn't be.  It's a Lemming POV to 
just "Suck it up."

I just got a Logitech Wireless Access Keyboard for $40 from Fry's (I 
know, I know).  Anyway, it has these 4 buttons on it: "New, Reply, 
Forward, Send."  In the Logitech Control Center, I have programmed 
these for the commands in Mail, so I have less jumping between keyboard 
and mouse than before.  Expensive solution that doesn't fix all the 
problems, but the moral of the story is that you should be able to use 
key commands for pretty much everything in Mail.  If you can do macros, 
or programming those all-too-useful F-Keys on your top row, I'm sure 
you can get back up to speed on Apple's program.  Good luck, and keep 
me informed if you have any breakthroughs.
