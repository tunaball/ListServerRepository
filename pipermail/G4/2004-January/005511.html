at www.xlr8yourmac.com , the CPU upgrade database has many results 
concerning the overall stability of sonnett upgrades-namely due to heat 
issues. i would think any fan could help, including a PCI fan if it is 
positioned in the best possible location. just be careful though, i 
know in the quicksilver machines it is easy to install a fan that is 
actually highly detrimental because it counteracts the airflow that the 
stock fan create.
