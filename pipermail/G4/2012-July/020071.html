> The Open Firmware resets have solved a multitude of strange  
> problems for me over the years. They can be in addition to  
> resetting the  PRAM.
>
> On startup, just after the chime, press and hold the following four  
> (4) keys:
>
> 1.  the command key (the Apple  key)
> 2.  the option key
> 3.  the letter "o" key and
> 4.  the letter "f" key.
>
> Hold them all down until a message appears saying something along  
> the lines of "release keys to continue".
>
> One at a time, type the following three commands, including the  
> dash, but not the quotes, and press the return key
>
> "reset-nvram"  return
> "set-defaults"  return
> "reset-all"	return
>
> The machine will flash the rom (rewrite it to original specs) and  
> reboot.  Check to see if you are still having problems.  Let us know.
>
