Scot,

Start up from the drive that you want to copy the eMail TO the open  
email.
Once you've done that "Open Mail"
You'll need to create the email account's just like those on your  
drive that has the eMail.

When you've done that and mail is open select from the drop-down menu  
"File" and select "Import Mailboxes..."

 From the menu that appears select the drive that you want to get the  
"mail" from.
