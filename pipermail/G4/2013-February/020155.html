sender, and are specifically expecting such an email,  NEVER CLICK ON  
A LINK IN AN EMAIL THAT CONTAINS ONLY A LINK.  It is almost  
invariably spam.

You can report spam to SPAMCOP.  You can google it.  They will parse  
the spam and report it to the proper authorities for you if you  
report it to them.
