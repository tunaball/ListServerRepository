>> Works fine here on my G4, 450 MP. Shutdown the machine. Let it sit for five
>>or
>> so minutes and restart. If that doesn't do it, reset the PRAM (restart,
>>holding
>> down the CMD+OPT+P+R keys until you get at least three bongs) and see if that

Not necessarily. If resetting the PRAM fixes the problem, then the battery's
probably OK. Failing battery usually is indicated by the date/time reverting to
sometime in the past, 1970s or earlier (depending on the machine, IIRC). See
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=58697> for details on replacing
the battery in most G4 desktops. For other machines, go to
<http://search.info.apple.com/> and search for the appropriate DIY battery
replacement; e.g., G5 battery.
