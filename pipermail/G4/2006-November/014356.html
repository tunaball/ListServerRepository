> I have a DP 1.25ghz  MDD and am having ethernet problems. The built  
> in ethernet port is basically not working and extremely slow. Under  
> System Preferences / Network / Network Status it shows the green  
> dot and states that its active, gives the IP address and connected  
> to the internet via built in ethernet. I am connected using DHCP.

>

Casey, it sounds to me like your modem or router needs to be reset.

Power it/them off for about 30 seconds, them power them back on - you  
may need to restart your computer as well.

Try this before spending bucks on PCI cards.  It's the cheapest fix  
you can do.
