> On my QS 800, I was having many problems with the system, so I
> reinstalled 10.4 with the  archive option, so my previous system is
> still in the application folder. I want to recover the bookmarks from
> Safari and the mailboxes from Mail.  Where would I go to retrieve them?

The Archive and Install should not have disturbed the Home folder, and 
all your bookmarks and mail should still be there and useable.  But to 
answer your question as to where things are:

In your Home folder, go to Library, open the Safari Folder, and there 
you will find your bookmarks.  For your mail, as long as you were 
running Mail in 10.4, and not 10.3 or earlier, you will find all your 
mail and its settings in the Mail folder, again in your Home folder, 
Library.
