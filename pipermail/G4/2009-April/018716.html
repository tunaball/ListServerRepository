It appears that the cause of this problem is the Airport module.
In fact, checking around the web this appears to be a very common
problem with many people experiencing the same kernel panic symptoms.
Sometimes replacing or even just reseating the Airport module is
effective in fixing it but sometimes the problem persists even after
replacement. In my case, if removing the Airport module seems to fix
the panics, I'll probably just leave it removed since I rarely do
anything with this system that requires networking. Also using an
external usb wifi card is an option.

Thanks to those who responded.
