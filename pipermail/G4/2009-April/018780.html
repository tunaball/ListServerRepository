First of all I would suggest that you take a look at your "System and  
Console" log files, there should be some information in one of those  
logs as to what/why you are having problems.

Note the system time when your problems occur and then correlate that  
time to the log files.
When you find something suspicious you can post the log files here and  
perhaps someone can analyze the reason.
