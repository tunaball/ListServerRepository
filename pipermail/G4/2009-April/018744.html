Craigslist (occasionally). Apple Store online (24/7). Various Mac  
vendors online (seek and ye shall find). eBay (Apple OEM as well as  
Chinese knockoffs at less than half Apple's price).

I bought a Chinese knockoff a few months ago and it works just fine in  
daily use. Make sure you get the correct wattage MagSafe for the  
MacBook Pro as there are several versions out there for different  
MacBooks/MacBook Pros.
