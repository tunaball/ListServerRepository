On Apr 23, 2009, at 10:28 AM, ronald basford wrote:

> Question - i have 512mb of ram - can i install a 1gb chip along with  
> the 512mb bringing the total ram to 1.5gb?
>

Yes, as long as the other RAM slot isn't dead (a lot of PowerBooks in  
that era had one dead slot). Before ordering the RAM, try the 512 in  
the other slot. If it works there too, order the new RAM.
