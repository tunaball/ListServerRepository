Running your serial, you have a Sawtooth unit made in Sacramento CA on 
the 12th week of March, 2000.

Stay away from KDS LCDs. They're junk.

Any LCD will work. Used ADC LCDs are fairly cheap, especially the 
15"ers. If you're looking to buy new, want widescreen, and can spend 
about $250, get an HP w2007 20". This is the monitor I bought when my 
KDS died. Dell LCDs are decently priced and are good monitors as well. 
Cheapo KDS and MAG LCDs last about a year, then their power supplies 
take a dump.
