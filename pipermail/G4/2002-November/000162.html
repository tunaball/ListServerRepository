> As long as it's mounted, they can have access to it (if you allow it).
>
> And in case you didn't know, your FTP server can be accessed with a 
> regular
> web browser by the people you give accounts to, via <ftp://your ip
> address>.
>
> You can also share your files via the world wide web if you put the 
> files
> in your Sites folder (you can't have any files named "index.html" in 
> the
> folder tho). How this differs from FTP is that *anyone* can have 
> access to
> the files, not just account holders. The URL for this would be 
> <www.your IP
> address/~your account name/>
