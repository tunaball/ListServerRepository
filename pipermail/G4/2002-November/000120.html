10.2.2 the built in server will do wonderful-its a decent unix server 
that can handle moderate traffic. os 9 is more difficult, do you want 
to buy software or do you want freeware? try looking at 
http://www.pure-mac.com/server.html and looking under the ftp servers. 
depending on your needs there are a few choices.
