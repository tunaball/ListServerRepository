Check the monitor; you didn't say if you took it to the store.  Did the store 
plug the keyboard and mouse into the same USB port you normally use (the 
temporaty relief suggests a possible USB problem)?  It's a very long shot, but 
power is another one of the things that may be different between your house and 
the store.  I've seen a printer not work when plugged into a perfectly fine 
outlet strip, but changing the outlet strip "fixed" it (everything else on the 
strip, computer, terminal, lamp, worked fine).  The point is, try changing 
everything including your socks.

And "It works fine here" seems to happen a lot at CompUSA, try somewhere else if 
all else fails.
