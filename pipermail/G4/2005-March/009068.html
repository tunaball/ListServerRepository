Current searches for closed auctions for "G4 933" on eBay yielded 
ending prices from $700 to $900. There are several current auctions 
that are showing bids in the $1200 range, although they have some 
extras, like two 80 GB drives, etc. You should be able to get a pretty 
good price for it on eBay. Limiting it to your local area will 
necessarily limit the price you can get, because the pool of people 
that want to buy it is much smaller.
