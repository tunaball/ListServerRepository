> I've just bought a new/used G4 733/9.2.2. But it inconsistently, 
> alternately
> freezes and misbehaves. I'm thinking extension conflict. And 
> ConflictCatcher
> doesn't exist anymore? Any ideas? A place to search?

Sure, ConflictCatcher exists. It's probably available on ebay and/or 
the Low End Mac SWAP list.
Without CC, just remove all non-Apple extensions and control panels 
from those respective folders--create folders in the system folder for 
"out of ext" and "out of cp" and move them there. Then restart.
OR simply get and install Panther--it's available very cheaply now with 
Tiger soon due out, and kiss extension conflicts goodbye for good. If 
you have OS 9 apps you must use, you can run them in Classic under OS 
X. HTH.
