Why not make it simple and do a "Find File" looking for "Norton" and/or 
"Symantec" and drag them to the trash. Just be careful not to delete 
the files you want to keep. If you do accidentally trash something you 
need, you can always re-install. Only problem with my suggestion is 
that Norton has many "invisible files". You'll have to select to look 
for invisible files when you do the "Find File".
Hope this helps.
