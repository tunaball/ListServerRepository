Hello,
I installed two OWC 1.2 Ghz upgrades in G4 400s, and I have been very 
happy with them. They installed without a problem, and I have had no 
problems with them. The customer service at OWC is second to none: I 
placed an order friday evening, and received it at noon on Monday.

The OWC upgrades include a small fan mounted to the heat sink for extra 
cooling. Don't the Sonnet upgrades use the existing heat sink without a 
fan? I like the extra cooling capacity of the fan.
