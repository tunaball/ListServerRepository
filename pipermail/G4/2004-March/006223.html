The Pioneer 107, along with the 106, 105 and 104 (also the A06, etc. 
variants) will work fine. If you don't want a DVD writer, there are 
many less expensive CD-RW and combo drives that will work. A great 
resource is the drive compatibility database at www.xlr8yourmac.com. 
You can look up your machine and get user reports on many drives and 
pick one that you can afford and find. The physical size of most every 
CD drive will be standard, as will the mounting hole locations.
