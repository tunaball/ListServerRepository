Melissa, I agree with Pastor Mac. In Eudora, pull down your "Special" 
menu and click on "Settings". Under the "Styled Text" setting check 
either "Send Plain Text Message Only" or "Ask each Time." With the 
latter, you will have to decide with each email. If you normally send 
lots of embedded pictures or use a fancy signature block, then use 
the latter setting and indicate "text only" for mail to the G4 
Maillist.
