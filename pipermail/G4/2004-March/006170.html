     In addition to dual CPU's and cooling stacks, you'd need a 
replacement fan assembly (the single processor 1.6's and original 
1.8's have only a single fan forward of the CPU; the duals have a 
double fan). I recall reading somewhere that going to dual CPU 
requires soldering/desoldering some jumpers on the motherboard, so 
any such future upgrade would undoubtedly void any remaining warranty 
(e.g. AppleCare). Can't swear it's so, though.
