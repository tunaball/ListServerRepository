> I spoke with Apple today, and they offer no quick answers to the failure

> the format in Eudora is too old, or that I have too much mail to import.

> imported about 700 of these; another box that should contain
> 500+ messages only imported 44)

If it's just a question of trying to import too much mail at once, you can
always move some of your mailboxes out of the Eudora folder where they're
stored, import *some* of your mail, and then move the imported boxes out and
the non-imported boxes back in and and import those. The folder in question
is ~/Documents/Eudora Folder/Mail Folder.   Unless you're on a really old
Eudora on OS9, and then that Eudora Folder will be in your System folder ->
Preferences.
