The warning is called a Kernel Panic and is usually indicative of a 
hardware problem. Have you added RAM recently? Bad RAM will frequently 
cause Kernel Panics. Have you added a USB hub? Some brands are more 
prone to this behavior than others. Try undoing anything recent that 
you have changed and see if it changes the behavior of the machine.
Good luck,
