> anyone know how to reset the pram on a flatscreen iMac besides the 
> keyboard
> method. Thought there was a way to do it via terminal.

Open Firmware variables can be manipulated from Terminal with nvram 
(man nvram for details). I don't know if you can use it to reset it to 
defaults, and I'm not sure it'd be wise to do so, if possible.

The proper way is cmd-opt-P-R at startup. Some suggest it's better to 
invoke Open Firmware upon startup (cmd-opt-O-F) and use the reset-nvram 
and reset-all commands. According to Apple Tech QA 1103, that is simply 
not necessary, since it's OF that does the resetting when it detects 
COPR.
