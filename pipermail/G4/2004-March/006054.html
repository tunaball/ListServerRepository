Kernel panic. For details, see Apple KB article ID 106227.

It usually (but not necessarily) indicates a hardware problem. If 
you're experiencing it only upon waking from sleep, it may be caused by 
a third-party device (USB hub, FireWire drive, PCI card, etc.) which 
doesn't support sleep correctly. Have you added any such peripherals 
lately?
