> If you leave an image fixed on a CRT screen long enough, you can burn 
> the phosphor and leave a permanent shadow.   Can something similar 
> occur with a LCD screen? There is no phosphor to burn, is there ??  A 
> related question is, do you need a screen saver for a LCD screen?

It sure can leave a ghost image. In fact it may do so a lot sooner. But 
the ghost image is not permanent: it also disappears after an equal 
amount of time it took to become burnt in. I have seen this kind of 
ghosting in as short a time as 15 minutes with Apple Cinema Displays...
