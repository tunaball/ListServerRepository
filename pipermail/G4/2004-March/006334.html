i am with netcom which was bought by mindspring which was bought by
earthlink.  during the mindspring days they allowed us to get additional
email addresses via their webmail system.  i got an address and NEVER used
it before earthlink took over.  still haven't.  got it to make sure someone
else didn't grab the user name, as it's a popular name.

within a few months i started to receive spam.  now i get about 10 a day.
thank goodness for earthlink's spamBlocker 'high' setting--it catches 100%,
as i have no addresses in my address book!

this email address could not have been harvested by spammers in the usual
fashion--from lists, postings, forwarded emails, people sending mail to me
and others not using BCC:, crawling the web, etc.  spammers simply took the
(popular) username, attached it to every ISP's domain name they could find,
and started sending bulk mail in the hopes that some of the spam would get
delivered.  which works for the spammers.  therefore, until i get an
anti-spam program, i won't be using that address.
