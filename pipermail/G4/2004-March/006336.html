I had a similar issue with my three LaCie HD enclosures (w/ Oxford 911
controllers) after I upgraded to 10.3.3.  Although my external LaCie DVD-RW
drive & Microtech memory card reader did still mount on the desktop.  Once I
downloaded and ran the new firmware from LaCie for the Oxford 911
controllers, the HD enclosures have worked perfectly ever since.  Can you
upgrade the firmware for your Firewire hardware?
Good luck,

> you can try booting in "open firmware"
> reboot, hold down the "CMD-OPT-O-F" keys. O is for the letter O.
> At the prompt type: reset-nvram
> when it completes type: reset-all
> the last command will reboot the system. This command line input has the
> same effect and does the same thing a cuda switch would.
