The off-line setting is irrelevant to POP accounts, which are 
(simplifying) always off-line (cf. 
<http://www.imap.org/papers/imap.vs.pop.brief.html>; see also your Help 
-- search for "POP mail"). I suspect you have POP.

pretty intuitive to me, but, de gustibus...) To actually see how 
they're being sent away, open the Activity Viewer.
