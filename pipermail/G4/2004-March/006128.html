Ole,

I do not have a Brother Laser printer, but  DO have a Brother MFC, that
started having the same issues after the OS 10.3.2 install. Would print if
only certain applications were open, if you used a classic application
nothing would print, would spool to the printer and then not print, etc. I
tried everything to correct it, with no success. My system is:

933 Digital Audio (upgrade)
1.2 Gb RAM
80 Gb HD & 40 Gb HD
OS 10.3.2

I finally just removed all of the old "drivers" and re-installed the
original OS 9.x kit that came with the printer and went to the Brother web
page for the OS 10.x  updates, and reinstalled the whole thing. Working fine
now.
