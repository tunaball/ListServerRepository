Hi Tony,

Fonts are in a suitcase in your System folderYou can drag the
incorrect/older fonts out of the 
suitcase and then let your installer complete their installation.  Old
fonts can be placed in another 
folder somewhere, should you ever need those versions.

Sounds like those fonts would only be used when QuickDraw GX is operating. 
If other apps also 
rely on Quickdraw you may have variant fonts being employed, which may make
no difference or a 
lot to the appearance of typography.

To reconcile conflicts you may need to have folders of the fonts in
question and swap them in and 
out of the Font suitcase when using different programs.

No idea what happens if the new fonts aren't installed.

But give it a try and let your installer complete its sequence.  Any issues
are likely easily reversible 
by uninstalling or searching for the new files and deleting them.

HTH,


Jon
