What software do you use most? Do you use Photoshop or Final Cut, which 
recognize two processors and can take advantage of them, or do you need 
speed for games or general applications. If the former, go for the 
dual, since those applications will make good use of the second 
processor. If not, stick with the single, since you wouldn't be getting 
much our of the second one.
