I would take the Combo version.  I was at 10.3.5 a couple weeks ago, so 
I just took both 10.3.6 and 7.  Some folks had trouble with 6, but 
here, everything is fine.

Then repair permissions again.  And run the update from the downloaded 
package.  Then I would expect the Software Update feature to work 
properly.
