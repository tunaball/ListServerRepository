I know a couple people who play NWN on Mac, and one of them said that when they updated to 10.3.7 they had to update their video card software and reinstall NWN. They aren't sure which of the two fixed the problem, because they did both at once.

I'd suggest you reinstall NWN and see if that fixes your problem.

Also, the NWN tech support boards are a good place to look. The couple of threads that I perused seemd to indicate that the first troubleshooting step is a reinstall of the game.
