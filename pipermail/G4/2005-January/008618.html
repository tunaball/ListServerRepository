> My problem is that the drive is a 200gb drive, but the all three OSes

>
> Neither Apple's support site nor Maxtor's support site were any help 
> in telling me what's wrong here. Is the IDE controller in my G4 *that

There's nothing wrong with your controller, it's just built to an older 
standard. You can get a new PCI IDE controller from most any Mac store. 
It'll just cost you 3-5X what the same card would cost for a PC.
