Safe Mode doesn't load third-party kernel extensions, so I would 
suggest moving any kernel extensions that you are suspicious of out of 
the folder [hard drive name]/System/Library/Extensions, reboot, and try 
running without them and seeing if it solves the problem. Then you can 
add them back one at a time until you find the one causing the problem. 
Of course you will be without whatever service the kernel extensions 
provide while you are doing this.
HTH,
