I had the same problem with my Quick Silver 733 mhz, OSX.3.2 (at that 
time, now 10.3.7). Installed a 250 gb drive and could only see 128gb.  
If I used it as a firewire drive, I could see the whole drive -- and it 
really wasn't that slow.  But I installed a SONNET ATA 133 controller 
card and now I can see all 250 gb. I also added a couple of more drives 
to the new controller. I believe that the new controller is slightly 
faster than the Apple controller..  I purchased my Sonnet cared on the 
net for $80.
