supplement my iBook that I currently use (actually, it is getting ready 
to go back to Apple under AppleCare for that infamous screen problem 
that has plagued the last of the G3 iBooks).

I love my 17" Apple Studio Display LCD on my G4 Tower at work, and was 
wondering if their are any DVI capable LCDs that y'all like that are in 
a similar price range or less than Apple's $699 (US$) price of their 
17" LCD? I'm also thinking of replacing a couple of our older B&W G3s
