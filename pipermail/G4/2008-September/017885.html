On my QuickSilver 733 MHz, I sometimes use the medium sized button  
beneath the normal off/on button to deal with a freeze.  The "Setting  
Up..." book says it is a reset button to be used "only when you cannot  
restart your computer with any of the recommended methods...."  The  
smallest button beneath that is the Programmer's button to be used  
ONLY for firmware updates, under specific instruction.
