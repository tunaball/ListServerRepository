Hello, Lars...
What have you done to restart the Quicksilver? You may have tried this, but
I would suggest unplugging your machine and letting it sit for at least 10
minutes. Be careful not to jostle the machine. Plug it back in and try the
normal start up by pushing in the start button. If that doesn't work, try
holding in the start button in for a full minute or until it starts. This
has worked for me in the past.
