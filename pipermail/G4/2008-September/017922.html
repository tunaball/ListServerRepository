Most of these older G4 Towers were only specced to handle 2 HD's.  
Hence the Power Supply has enough power connectors for the Zip and  
Optical and a main Hd and a slave.

Beyond adding video card or other card, you are going to drain out  
your Power Supply trying to add more internal devices.  You can use Y  
adapters but more power drain from your system the less the  
performance will be.

And no Power supply from a higher end model won't help. Quicksilvers  
and MDD PSU's are meant for their respective systems.

If you are really gung ho however, You can rig an ATX Power supply to  
run your G4 AGP model. There is a page called www.xlr8yourmac.com  
there is an older link for converting ATX PSU's to work on various  
Mac models. If you are comfortable with a Soldering Gun then go for it.

FWIW, if you want more HD space why not add externally? External HD's  
often have their own power and attach via FireWire.
