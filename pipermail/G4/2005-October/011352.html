Hi Wayne, thanks I had already done that and did not work.


As an aside, I had sent the same email to Epson and SURPRICE!!! They  
answered very fast.

The solution was to go into the "Library", delete ALL the Epson   
drivers from "Printers", then insert the DVD with 10.4 and  
reinstalling the Epson printers form the "Extras" folder. Then re- 
start and voila... it worked again.
