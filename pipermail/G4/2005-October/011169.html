I purchased two Acomdata drives about a month ago, and divided each into 3 
partitions, to back up 3 different Macs   One worked fine, but on the second I 
could not back up to the middle partition.

Acomdata tech support was helpful in trying, by phone, and then exchanged 
that drive for a new one, which I will be working with shortly.   No complaints 
about their service from me.

They did say that partitioning on a Mac could cause problems with their 
driver.   That seems questionable to me, and I will attempt to proceed with my 
partitioning plan, on their replacement drive.

Overall, I give them a good rating for my customer service experience.
