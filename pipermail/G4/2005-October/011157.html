I bought a G4/800 12" about 18 months ago and love it (Apple Refurb).  
This is NOT my primary computer and I would not use it as my primary  
due to the small screen size and my eyesight. :-) But for combination  
of size, weight, performance, and reliability I do recommend the  
iBook. If she intends to use it as her primary computer and wants to  
have more than one display OR use an external monitor with the laptop  
closed, then she needs a PowerBook.
