On Oct 20, 2005, at 5:43 PM, Eugene D. tunick, D.D.S. wrote:

> I just bought a G4 and am trying to transfer Email addresses from  
> Eudora on the PC to Mail on the Mac. Any ideas?

More information is needed. What have you tried to far?

Address Book can import vCard, text, and LDIF files. Eudora's manual  
says that its address book is saved in CSV (comma separated values)  
format, which is a text file. Open the Eudora address book on your  
PC, do a save as to your desktop, copy that file to your Mac, and  
import it into Address Book.


> Also, I'm trying to transfer names and addresses and phone numbers  
> from an odd database on the PC to Address Book on the Mac.
> Three hours of work with no joy yet.

Most databases can export their data to a tab or comma delimited text  
file. Have you tried that?
