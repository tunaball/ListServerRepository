> Q Could Norton's SystemWorks 3.0 and/or Norton's
> Internet Security 3.0 cause my copy of Safari 1.2
> (v125) not to upgrade?
>
> Because when I have tried to upgrade it in the past
> using OS X (10.3.9) alone with all of the fixes for

While Norton SystemWorks deservedly gets blamed for lots of problems  
(And most of us would recommend removing it.) I doubt that it's the  
source of your current problem.

Here's a couple of suggestions. First go to the Applications folder  
and do a get info on the Safari application and see what it's version  
is. That way you'll know if it was upgraded or not. Then try logging  
in as another user and see if the problem goes away. Send your  
results to the list.
