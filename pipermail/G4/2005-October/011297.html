This may or may not be helpful to you, but I got weary of jumping  
through Appletalk hoops a few years ago.  I had heard of IP printing  
and that the Appletalk card on the HPLJ4M could be replaced with a  
JetDirect card for IP printing. New, they were nearly 200 bucks, but  
I found several used ones on ebay and bought one, delivered, for  
under 40 dollars.
I have not missed Appletalk one bit.
