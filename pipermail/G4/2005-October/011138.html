On Oct 2, 2005, at 10:15 AM, shopdog wrote:

> an iPhoto album, the option to have it change the desktop every few  
> minutes is greyed out.  It is not greyed out if I choose a picture  
> folder on my hard drive, or if I choose an apple desktop pattern.
>
> Does anyone know why the option is greyed out?

I'm guessing this is a file permissions issue. Logged into the  
account you are as, at the moment, you may not have sufficient file  
privileges to do this. in Finder, right click the drive or folder  
that your pictures reside in and check under "Ownership and  
Permissions" to see if you have "Read and Write" privileges to this  
folder. If not, and logged in as an Administrator, you should be able  
to change this and try that setting again.
