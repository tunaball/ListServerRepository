Not quite the same but close.  I have a 1.25 dp mdd with 10.3.8  four 
HDs and 2 Optical drives and some time ago it shut down due to a 
momentary power failure.

When I tried to start back up I had the same symptoms of the light 
coming on as long as the button was pressed but nothing happening.

I thought the ps was shot but I knew power was getting to the light.  I 
opened up and removed all the drives and then removed the battery to 
check the voltage.  I couldn't see anything wrong so I put the battery 
back in and pressed the startup button and it tried to start up with no 
hd installed.  I quickly installed my boot drive and everything 
returned to normal.  I then installed my back up drive and left the two 
smaller drives out as I really didn't use them.  Everything has been 
fine now for a couple of months.  I haven't tried installing the extra 
2 hard drives so I can't be sure they were not pushing things toward 
overload.

In my case, I am thinking that somehow the pram got scrambled and 
removing the battery was the solution.  You apparently have more of a 
problem since a fuse was blown but maybe this will provide some clues.  
When closing the door you may be causing some problem with the start up 
circuitry so I would search in that area.
