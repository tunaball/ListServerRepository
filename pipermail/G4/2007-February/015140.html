Be VERY careful here.  Results have varied widely. Some swear by theirs.  I used a Mercury upgrade in my G4 Quicksilver, and it went bad on me, and it was replaced by OWC.  But, then a few years later the replacement started to go bad on me, too, and at that point, I just replaced the upgrade with the original CPU and sold the computer and replaced it with an Intel-based iMac.  I think that the culprit in my case was overheating.  Even though the upgrade daughter board had its own cooling fan.  Heat can be a real killer in the long run.
