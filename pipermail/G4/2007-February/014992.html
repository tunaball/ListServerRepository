Todd Naber wrote:
> Hello Fellow PowerMac G4 owners,
> I'm using a PowerMac G4 Digital Audio 533 and was wondering if anyone 
> knew if there ever was a add-on drive/card that could be inserted into 
> the front of my G4 (instead of the zip module) that allowed for media 
> cards? I remember something like this was available/rare, but can't seem 
> to find any info. Any help would be great,
> applguy

I saw pictures of a G4 with a media card reader as you describe in an 
eBay auction a few months ago, but I don't know the details.  I think it 
was a standard PC card reader, though, to fit a 5 1/4" drive bay.  You'd 
need one of the PC card reader with a cable that's routed out back 
through the PCI cover plates to the rear USB ports, though.  A lot of 
the internal PC card readers plug into pins on the motherboard, and I 
don't think you'd be able to use one of those.
