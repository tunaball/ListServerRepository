Gregory Cortelyou:
> I have an older G4/350/AGP that I really love and I want to put a 
> Pioneer 107 DVR in place of the original DVD rom drive.

This is possible. I have replaced a CD drive with a Pioneer 105 in my 
G4/733 and it works perfectly well.
But there are two things you should consider:

-- I'm not sure 10.3.4 has got support for the 107 yet. In that case 
you will have to install a patch in order to get the drive to work. 
Look at http://www.xlr8yourmac.com/ for mor info.

-- iDVD requires 733 MHz or more in order to work, according to the 
information on the iLife box.
