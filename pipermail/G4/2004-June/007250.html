On Friday, Jun 4, 2004, at 15:59 Canada/Eastern, Thomas Bulat wrote:

> Does anyone on this list know which internal Apple Modem I most likely
> have in my vintage 400 G3 Lombard Powerbook (built in 1999)? [...]

I don't know, but you should be able to find out by interrogating the 
modem with a terminal emulator (e.g., ZTerm). The AT I commands are 
what you need, AT I6 most likely. If you don't have a term emulator, 
follow the instructions in Apple KB article ID 75407.
