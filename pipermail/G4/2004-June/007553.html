Deling,

How old is the mouse?  It is probably or possibly breaking. I have gone 
through at least 3 MS intelli mice, they seem to need replacing every 
year or so. The buttons go or the optical sensor goes haywire (shooting 
the cursor to a random corner). The good news is that MS has good 
warrantee support and if you go to the MS support page web page they 
will likely send you a new mouse for Free. Sounds like a hardware 
problem with the mouse since the same problem happens on both machines 
you tested it on.
