This is not quite accurate. iTunes 2 (or was 3 the last one for Mac OS 9 -- 
I don't remember) can play MP3 files encoded (_not_ encrypted) in iTunes 4; 
however, only iTunes 4 or later can play files encoded in AAC (which is the 
same thing as M4A). Moreover, files downloaded from the iTunes Music Store 
are copyright-protected, and can only be played on authorized computers. See 
Apple KB articles ID 93071 and 150345.
