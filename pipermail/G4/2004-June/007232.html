ATAPI started as a specific formulation of ATA for EIDE CD-ROM and tape 
drives. ATAPI allowed CD_ROMs and Hard disks to coexist on the same 
bus.

Now, i have no clue what the difference is. as far as i know apple 
still uses atapi optical drives, and it seems that there are some (but 
few) atapi hard drives (the vast majority are ata or ultra ata) so 
perhaps apple supports atapi optical drives, but not atapi hard drives.
