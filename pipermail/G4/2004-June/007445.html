     WAIT! Don't reinstall anything until you go to a computer supply 
shop, purchase a can of compressed air, open the drive tray, and give 
the innards a few good blasts of compressed air. The symptoms you 
describe, especially on an older machine in which the drive is 
infrequently used, can be reproduced with a diode laser that has 
gotten some dust on it over the years. Even my not-quite-2-year-old 
MDD G4 was making funny noises and not reading some CD's until I 
applied this treatment the other night. Now the CD drive is silent 
and works every time.

     No guarantee that this will solve the problem, but it beats 
reinstalling the OS from scratch.
