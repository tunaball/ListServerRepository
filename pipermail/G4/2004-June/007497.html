I have a no-name (I think its was like Q-Val or something like that but 
the box itself has no info on it) USB 2.0 5 1/4" drive enclosure that 
will work with 3 1/2 and 5 1/4 drives.  I have a 4x DVD-ROM from a 
Sawtooth in it, and that is hooked up to a Yikes 400 running the 
current 10.3 through a CompUSA USB 2.0 PCI card.  It works, but has 
personality.  Specifically, if a disk is in the internal DVR 107 and I 
put a disk in the external drive, it won't mount.  However, mounting on 
the external first and then inserting the internal recognizes both 
disks.
