Your options are the following:

1) Use  a rendezvous capable printer.
2) Use Microsoft's free Remote Desktop Client for the Mac. This 
enables you to run your PC from within your Mac - i.e., it appears on 
your Mac's screen and  allows automatic printing to your Mac printers.
3) Another possibility is a PC product called PC Maclan. Apparantly, 
its wizard automatically sets up bi-directional  PC/Mac printing. I 
haven't tried this and am hoping someone out there has and will send 
us all his or her impressions.
