is the setup a dsl modem(router) to a hub with no router?

as the Asante FR 11004 or if you want the ability to to add wireless 
FR1104-g, 54mb(airport extreme).

If you have a Router HUB, then just follow the instructions that came 
with the router hub  to do the setup.
