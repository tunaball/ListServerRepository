> Just thought I'd drop a note to share my success with using the

>
> gettext 0.11.5
>

> identified by the frontend as a model 2000U, and by scanimage as a  
> 1220U.
>
> First time I've been able to do X-native scanning with this setup.  
> If you've got one of these collecting dust, or were using Classic  
> to do your scans, this is a workable alternative. FYI.
