I recently purchased a USB card from Comp USA for under $35 (U.S.) it  
has 5 external USB ports and 1 internal.
It works fine in my daughters AGP G4, has a CD with it to install  
needed drivers.   As far as video cards go I purchased an ATI Radeon  
9800 Pro special addition (256 megs of ram, works at 2X or 4X speed  
on G4 towers) for my 2001 Quicksilver, it is however pricey at $238  
(U.S.).  Other World Computing has several Video cards listed, you  
can also Google it by searching  for (AGP video cards Power Mac G4),  
EBay is another source for used video cards, if you want to save  
bucks, however you take a chance using EBay.  OWC has a nice parts  
finder on their site that lets you enter mac model and select what  
your interested in, Memory, drives, CPU's, Video cards ect.  it then  
gives you the parts that they have,  hope this helps a little.
