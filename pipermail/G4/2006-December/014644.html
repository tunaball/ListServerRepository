I'm sure that the auto launch programs that Richard is referring to is 
regarding the application to start-up when the user does the login 
process. It's located in the "System Preference" panel under Accounts. 
You need to select the tab called "Startup Items" after you have 
selected the tab you can delete all of the items in that file and 
nothing other than that required system inits will start-up at login.
