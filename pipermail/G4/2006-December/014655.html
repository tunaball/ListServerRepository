Hi Earle -
	I was pretty happy with the OS X VueScan drivers for my SCSI UMAX  
2400S on my Sawtooth G4/400 machine. I suspect that they make one for  
your scanner too.

Check them out at  http://www.hamrick.com/

I think they have a free trial.

OTOH. I suggest you consider put the cost of the drivers (not really  
very cheap) toward investing in a new USB scanner that would be  
compatible with OS X out of the box.  I did this recently and have  
been very pleased with the improved performance of my new scanner  
compared to the 4 yr old antique it replaces.
The software that came with the Epson scanner I got works a lot  
better and more intuitively than the Vuescan stuff I was using. And  
the higher resolution is a big plus. And I'm glad to be rid of the  
SCSI connections.

John
