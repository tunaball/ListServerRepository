I don't see anything here related to the launch of Camino. I do see  
this Clean My Mac helper app working, and Spoltight (running MDS  
process) crashing. So it appears a problem with indexing, not that I  
should think a system crash would result from that, but it does imply  
a problem

Have you started your Mac by pressing the Shift key after the initial  
chime and holding it until you see the spinning gears during your Mac  
startup? This launches OS X in safe mode bypassing the loading of  
external kernel extensions, prevents startup items from launching, and  
some other healthy disk checks perhaps not relevant to your problem.  
The link below describes safe mode

http://support.apple.com/kb/HT1564?viewlocale=en_US
