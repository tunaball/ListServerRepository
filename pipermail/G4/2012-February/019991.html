Amir,

Do you have another cable that connects between the 1st, the lower  
disk drive and the 133Mhz data buss.
If one one or more leads have gone bad you will not be able to load  
from your installed HD drives.
However the above doesn't explain why you can not boot from a DVD/CD  
because the DVD/CD drive uses the 66Mhz buss. Except that perhaps a  
couple of leads are shorted in the cable and is preventing any loading.
