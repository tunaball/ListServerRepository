Don't bother with an upgrade. A Mac Mini will be a bigger speed bump  
and less problematic then a processor upgrade. However if you really  
need speed the G5  is the way to go. My opinion is based on the down  
time and strange behavior my G4-400(APG) displayed when I upgraded it  
to a Sonnet 1.2 Ghz.

Of course the absolute cheapest way to get more speed is to max your  
RAM and run the G4 in OS 9.  OS X is a pig.
