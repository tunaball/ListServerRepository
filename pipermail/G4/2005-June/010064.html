On Jun 12, 2005, at 10:55 AM, Kevin Willis wrote:

> I was looking at a buying a G4/350 online, and I was wondering what  
> kind of CPU upgrades are available. Everymac.com lists the CPU as a  
> daughter card. Will that make the CPU from my Gigabit G4/400  
> incompatible?

In general CPUs are not backwards compatible.

http://www.macsales.com will give you a good idea of the upgrades  
that are available for that generation of machine. In particular be  
careful that the cost of the G4 + upgrades is not greater than a new  
Mini.
