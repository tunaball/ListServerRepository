There was maybe a dozen posts very recently about the pros of a SATA 
drive vs ATA.  Most folks spoke of a significant increase in

today -- Sonnet $64 from newegg.com.  I plan on making everything work 
this weekend.  Check the SATA posts during the past month for more 
info.
