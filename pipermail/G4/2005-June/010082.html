Mac G4/867 "QuickSilver" Specs...
The Apple Power Macintosh G4/867 QS, dubbed
"Quicksilver" for its revised case design, features an
867 MHz PowerPC 7450 (G4) processor with the AltiVec
"Velocity Engine" vector processing unit, 256k level 2
on-chip cache and 2 MB level 3 backside cache, 128 MB
of  - PC133 SDRAM,  - a 60.0 GB (7200 RPM) Ultra
ATA/66 hard drive, a CD-RW/DVD-R SuperDrive, an NVIDIA
GeForce2 MX (AGP 4X) video card with 32 MB of SDRAM,
and a digital audio sound system with a
highly-efficient Tripath "Class T" amplifier in a
translucent silver and white tower case with a fold
down door on the side that makes upgrading very easy.
This model ships with the Apple "Pro" keyboard and
optical mouse. Custom configurations are also
available.
