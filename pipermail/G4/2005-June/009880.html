Reality check. There ain't no such beast as Mac/Win "hybrid programs".

Some developers find it more convenient to distribute both Win and Mac 
versions of their software on the same CD (Type-on-Call comes in three 
flavours -- Win, Mac, Unix -- on the same CD). The CDs may be hybrid, 
but the software isn't. The decision to do so or not is a purely 
business decision.
