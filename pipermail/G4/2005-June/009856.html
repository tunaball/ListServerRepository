On Jun 1, 2005, at 8:08 AM, Alex wrote:

>
> On Jun 1, 2005, at 10:14, Crandon David wrote:
>
>> I have a DP500 Gigabit Ethernet G4.
>> I already have two hard drives attached to the same bus.
>> I'd like to add a 3rd, and from what I understand it would be 
>> connected to the same bus as the DVD-ROM drive.
>
> It is possible. But it's not a good idea to have a hard disk share the 
> channel with an ATAPI device. The ATAPI command structure is more 
> complex, the DVD-ROM drive is significantly slower, so, due to the 
> nature of the ATA bus, your new hard disk's performance would likely 
> be sub-optimal.
>
> The best solution is either to add a new ATA controller card, or to 
> use the new drive externally, in a FW enclosure.
>
>> do I have to do the master-slave thing (this computer does not 
>> support cable select)?
>
> Yes, of course. If you can't use either of the two optimal solutions, 
> and your new drive will share the channel with the ATAPI device, at 
> least make sure the latter is set to slave, and the hard disk to 
> master.
