Just bought the least expensive 256MB ($15) stick they had for my  
powerbook.

Shipping was free. (I opted for insurance for an additional $1.) I  
got email at each stage of the process. It showed up on time. It works.

http://www.dealram.com is a good place to check prices.

I have no loyalty when it comes to memory purchases. I usually buy  
the least expensive memory I can find locally, figuring that if it  
doesn't work I'll just return it.
