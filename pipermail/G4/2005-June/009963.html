> What and where is Patchburn?

This program generates and installs so called device profiles for
CD/DVD-burners that are available on Mac-OS X systems. This allows many,
otherwise unsupported burners to be used directly with Mac-OS X,  iTunes and
DiscBurner.

http://www.patchburn.de/
