I sorely lamented the "loss" of floppy drives, until I belatedly found 
out about Jump Drives!
Who _needs_ a floppy drive, when you can have a little 512 MB USB Jump 
Drive, that holds over 350 high-density floppy discs worth of data? For 
pennies...

Of course, if you have a few boxes of HD discs and no drive to use them 
in, I guess you DO have a problem...
In that case, you can usually buy an external HD Disc Drive and use it 
to your heart's content.
But, I'd bet you'll eventually convert to using those Jump Drives. 
They're quite cost (and space) effective!
