> Iomega provided special drivers for its zip drives. Your G4 was  
> probably delivered with OS 9 and The Iomega stuff should have been  
> included with the OS. Macintosh removable media have pretty much  
> always depended on software to perform an eject. If there is a disk  
> inside it should mount and show on the desktop or, if it is a blank  
> or some unrecognized disk it should be ejected by the system. You  
> might have a look inside the case to be sure it's plugged in.
>
> If the machine is running OS 10 because it was upgraded it's  
> possible that the software is missing. The Iomega web site is a  
> place to start.
>
> My G4 still has a Zip drive in it but it got disconnected a couple  
> of years ago to accommodate a third hard drive.
