Open your System Profile and click on "Applications and Extensions" to 
make sure that you have the TrendNet application or extension loaded. 
If you find either package, click on the "Application or Extension 
Name" and the second window will show where the package is located. 
Then you can delete the data.

Also do a find using the "Every Where" choice and then delete all 
occurrences of the TrendNet application.
