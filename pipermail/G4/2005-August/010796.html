Dan,
     Just to add another voice to this, i also have a
PowerMac MDD , the 1.25Ghz FW400 model, and it  
only came with a Combo Drive. I moved the combo-drive
to the lower bay and installed a Pioneer 109 ($46.00
at newegg) in the top bay. Set both drives to cable
select, and installed "patch burn" (no need for patch
burn if you are running tiger). You have to remove the
front bezal on the tray to get the door to open the
way it should. It all works great.

A great place to go for info on drives and details on
the install is http://www.xlr8yourmac.com/
Frank.
