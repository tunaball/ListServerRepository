Ron for what it is worth, i have the same setup (diff
drive mfg), but you should use CS on all the drives. I
have three HD in my G4 MDD 1.25 and they are all set
to CS and work fine.

I would say try tial and error with your drive, trying
every jumper setting that looks like it should be CS
until you find one that works.
