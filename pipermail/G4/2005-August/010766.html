I have used VolumnWorks (SubRosaSoft.com) to eliminate a partition on 
my MDD G4 (was four, reduced to three). It worked well; however, I 
did make a complete backup of all partitions before performing the 
operation (the program actually strongly suggests this as well). It 
did allow me to make the change without reformatting the hard drive, 
repartitioning, and then reinstalling. I had considered iPartition, 
but chose VolumnWorks, primarily because of good results with other 
SubRosaSoft products.
