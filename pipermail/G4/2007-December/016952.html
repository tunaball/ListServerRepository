On Dec 22, 2007, at 8:21 PM, Kathi Anderson wrote:

> Computers: 766 G4 Quicksilver, 1.25 G4 MDD, and a Dell PC.
> I know this is a Mac list, although I am pretty sure there are  
> plenty of dual users out there.
> My question is how can I get the URL from my Apple Laserwriter  
> 16/1600 so I can print from my Dell.


I have a similar setup, but now running OS X 10.5 (Leopard).  Click  
the "LaserWriter 16/600 PS" icon in the Dock, and click the blue  
"info" button, then click the "General" button.  You will see the  
printer URL below Driver Version.
