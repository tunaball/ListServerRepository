I,m having a similar problem, recently, with a DP450 gigabit ethernet machine. It shuts down for no apparent reason but it will not power up unless I let it set for an hour or so.  I'm thinking it may be heat related.
 
A couple of years ago I replaced the power supply because the machine would not power up, this was the advice of some members of this forum, but further investigation proved it to be the power button (switch) that was malfunctioning.  This was a whole lot cheaper and easier fix than the power supply.
