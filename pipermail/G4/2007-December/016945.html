Not to spam, but I wrote up my experience with upgrading a Gigabit
Ethernet DP500 G4 with a Sonnet Encore/ST 1.8ghz processor. You can use
the same processor on your 350, as long as it's got AGP graphics. If
not, there's always the Encore/ZIF for the PCI graphics ("Yikes")
models, which will bring your CPU speed up to 500mhz or 1ghz. 

You can read about installing the Encore/ST at
http://www.briandeuel.com.
