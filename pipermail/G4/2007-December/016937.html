Yes.  You need to find out for sure what kind of connection the display
requires.  If it's DVI, you either need a video card with a DVI port, or you
can get a VGA to DVI adapter.  The same applies for ADC: either use a video
card with an ADC port or you can get an adapter (the first place I'd look
for the adapter would be http://macsales.com/).
