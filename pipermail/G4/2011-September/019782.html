> I upgraded my G4 with a SATA card, SATA drives, and a SSD. It ran much faster but had to trial and error 3 different cards before it worked... took 2 months with back and forth shipping. And I think the extra heat took a toll on the power supply. Never ran quite right. Ended up getting a used G5 for $299, moved all the hardware across, and everything ran great. I think the Sonett card that's bootable works best in the G4, but for not much more money you can get a used G5, put the SSD in that and be better off. That's my experience.
