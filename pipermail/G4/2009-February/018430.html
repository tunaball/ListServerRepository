I recommend that you always keep some "Memorex AIR DUSTER" or some  
"CENTURY  Duster" air in a can and blow out the inside of your  
platform every 6 months. If you don't get it done every 6 months the  
do it every year.

Blow out the entire insides, the Power supply - from the front and  
back, the Mother Board, in other words hit it from every angle and  
then some.

I believe that is why my 10 year old G4 is still running on the  
original PS with four (4) internal drives and all expansion slots full.

If you need a new/reconditioned Mac G4 Power Supply go to Mac Pro  
OnLine at:
