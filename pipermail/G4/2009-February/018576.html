That's a unique spelling, Necole. Anyway, the steps to follow are  
basically to back up personal data (if needed), boot from either the  
OS X or OS 9 disc, run the right utility (Disk Utility in OS X or  
Drive Setup in OS 9) and make two partitions on the disk and install  
that OS onto one of them, and then boot the other install disc later  
and install onto the other partition.

This is assuming your G4 boots OS 9 natively. I believe that would be  
any of the graphite models.
