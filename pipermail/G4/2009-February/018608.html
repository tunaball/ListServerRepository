> All lower case typing with  no capitals, even to start a sentence, is 
> dangerous. How many times have you seen a reference to "mb" when the

> unit of temperature as in "The noise of this receiver is 25 K.". Proper

> if it means using the shift key once in a while.

> spelling errors that have been "corrected" by software. By all means use

> wrong letter.
