Well, my first thought is to split it into two files-- using ZipIt.  It 
will let you .zip a file into more than one segment.  You can then send 
both, and the recipient can rejoin them.

Another option, if you have .mac, is to set up the file for download 
from your webspace.

You can also put it in your public folder on your .mac webspace, and 
have the person get it from there.

For huge files, I find whalemail to be useful-- 
http://www.whalemail.com, although that costs money, so you really need 
to have a good reason.

I'm sure other people have good/better ideas.
