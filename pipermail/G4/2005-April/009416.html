I noticed some gray spots on my 15-inch Apple LDC display (in the menu bar
area) that would not come off with a damp cloth and cleaner.  I then
remembered a few years ago when I suspected a dead pixel and was advised to
massage the spot with my finger.  It worked then and worked again today!  I
massaged the spot for a few seconds and the blemish is history.  It is a
cheap fix that works sometime.  I got lucky today.
