I had a similar problem after installing a Matrox RTMac PCI card in my 
G4.

I did a search on the problem and found a suggestion that you may have 
to zap the PRAM.

You do this by starting up and immediately holding down the OPTION 
COMMAND P R keys until the start-up chimes are heard again.

I've done that and it seems to work, so it may solve your problem.

Regards,

Alastair.
