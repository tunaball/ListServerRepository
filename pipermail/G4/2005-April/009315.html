The last resort I use to get sound in is Sound In.  Hook up a mini jack 
to mini jack wire from your headphones jack on the stereo and plug it 
into your sound in on the mac.  If you do not have a sound in then you 
will need a mini plug to USB sound device such as Griffins imic.

Then you need a program to record the sound.  With the imic there is 
Final Vinyl (in OSX), there are others in OSX you will find on 
VersionTracker.com.  For OS9 Coaster is a fine choice.

This is your last resort as it is nice to get the data directly by 
copying instead of re-recording, but it is far better than not at all.
