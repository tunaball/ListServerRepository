Don't want to rain on a parade, but I had nothing but trouble with 
D-Link's G520 and I pulled it out as it not only didn't help things 
work right, but the wireless networking didn't work well at all (poor 
reception compared to my Sonnet or Airport based machines in the same 
location).

Suggestion - return the D-Link and splash out on the Sonnet Aria G card 
for $80 or so - try newegg.com or macmall.com they seemed to have 
decent prices.
