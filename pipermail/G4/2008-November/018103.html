You can easily replace your existing optical drive in the G4 with a  
nice new Pioneer SuperDrive for as little as $29.99 from OWC:
<http://eshop.macsales.com/item/Pioneer/DVR116DBK/>

For DVD burning under OS 10.3.x, you will need Roxio Toast, or NTI  
DragonBurn, or similar program.
