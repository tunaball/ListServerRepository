Unfortunately, the 'best' email program is the one you are used to!

I was a Eudora user for many years (since before v. 3.0) and was  
unhappy with their lack of updates -- the old-fashion clunky look and  
non-standard mailbox/address structure.

I tried several other mailers including Internet Explorer, Outlook  
Express, GMail, etc.  Finally I gave Apple's OSX Mail.app a tryout and  
decided to stick with it.  Once you get used to its handling of junk  
mail, it works fine.  It's not perfect, but it's OK.  It is very  
nicely integrated with the Address Book, which makes it easy to send  
emails to groups, etc.
