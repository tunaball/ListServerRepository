Hi Pat,

If the 733 is a Digital Audio model, it does have the 128GB hard
drive limit:

http://lowendmac.com/ppc/digital-audio-power-mac-g4.html

But if it is a Quicksilver, it may not have that limit:

http://lowendmac.com/ppc/quicksilver-power-mac-g4.html

For workarounds see the following:

http://lowendmac.com/macdan/05/1024.html

Personally I put a SATA drive and controller card in my G4 Sawtooth.
Large capacity, fast interface, I'm really happy with it.
