Since you say that only NEW photos are showing the color shift, it sounds to 
me that you, somehow, have activated the "Labels" feature for those items 
which are appearing off-colored. OS-X v10.3 brought back the "Labels" feature from 
the older OSes.

I know that if it were a monitor problem, the color shift would be appearing 
ALL OVER and not just on certain items. Your menu bar, desktop icons, 
everything, would be color shifted.

That said, I suggest doing a "Get Info" on the items that show the color 
shift and check their label status.
