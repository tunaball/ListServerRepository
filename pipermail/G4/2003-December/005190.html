I have a Macintosh G4 400 MHz - 128 Mb RAM - OS 10.3.2 - Safari - Mail 
- AppleWorks

scanner and Iomega 1 Gig Jaz drive (at the same time).
My 10 Gig hard drive has 4 particians with Panther & OS9.2.2 on their 
own particians.
