yes, CCC can do incremental backups quite easily, but not so robustly 
(yet). by simply setting the Preference to "synchronize source to 
target" you have an incremental backup-but there is no going back to 
previous versions of documents/files.

that being said, i have a CCC schedule set to backup/sync my boot drive 
to two separate firewire drives, they alternate every other day-so that 
gives me 2 previous days of file changes, but takes up 2x the size of 
my source drive.

i believe mike bombich is also working to make the incremental backups 
more robust, but give that it is donation-ware, im not expecting too 
much out of him.
