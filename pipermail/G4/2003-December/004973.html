The "Wind Tunnel" referred to is a particular model G4 that had (has) a 
terribly loud fan that runs at full speed almost all the time. It was so loud that 
Apple finally devised a fix-it that provided a replacement power supply/fan 
with different heat tolerance limits and a quieter fan for those owners who 
pitched a hitch at them.
