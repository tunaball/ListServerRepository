Greg, it is easy to put a gig of RAM in your Yikes; Panther will purrr 
on 512 mb and over, running the apps you speak of. It is TOO easy to add 
an internal drive on the Yikes; and much cheaper than an external HD. 
Get a 7200 rpm drive. A 40 to 80 gig is plenty for what you do, and when 
you get the G5 you will use the Yikes less. Keeping your cost down is 
good as you save for the G5.

As routers go; Cheap is great (10/100). Most routers work on Macs even 
when they don't say so. Unless you are putting your macs in different 
rooms go with ethernet and CAT5 wire.
