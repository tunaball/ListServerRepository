At 9:53 PM -0500 12/4/03, Jim Reamer wrote:
>Hey,
>    I have a G4 933 and an Epson C 80. I would like to be able to print
>webpages without the background color. Is there a simple way to
>accomplish this?

Finally, a question I can answer! :@)

Are you using Internet Explorer? If so, go File->Print Preview. At the
very bottom of the window, you will see some check boxes. Uncheck
"print background."
