i would try deleting anything related to internet explorer in 
YourDrive: Users: YourUserName: Library: Caches
and also YourDrive: Users: YourUserName: Library: Preferences

in mine, i have one folder called "MS Internet Cache" in the Caches 
folder, and "com.mircosoft.explorer.plist", and a folder "Explorer" in 
the Preferences folder.


i would delete them, and any other Internet Explorer files you see in 
those folders. restart the computer, and try again. one of those prefs 
or caches may simply be corrupt.

sandor
