Tough one. First go into system  prefs and tell it to do nothing when 
inserting a blank disk.

Then go into iTunes prefs and choose AIFF as your burning pref.
Change the speed setting because no pioneer we have will do 52X.. and 
it is set to maximum as default. turn it down to like 8x.
Make sure that you have not overloaded your playlist .
Then don't use memorex anymore.
