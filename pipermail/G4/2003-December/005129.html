If I remember OS9 well (and I don't) I believe for audio you need:

Audio CD Access
Apple CD/DVD Driver
Apple Audio Extension

As for DVD, the driver above should do the trick. If you're having 
trouble with Audio CD playback, try using Audion. http://www.panic.com/ 
It's shareware, limits you to 30 minutes per launch. I use it on both 
Mac OS X and Mac OS 9 so to me it was worth the 20$ shareware fee. I 
love it, it's a great app. Someday I plan on writing my own audio 
player for Mac, down with shareware! Long live open source!
