I have Panther on a B/W G3 500, which the same motherboard as your 
Yikes. Panther (10.3.2) works great on it. I do have 768 MB of RAM and 
a 32 MB PCI Radeon video card (Quartz Extreme-enabled hack), which 
helps perfomance. Still, I would think your machine would see some 
speed improvement from Panther; it certainly made the B/W snappier. It 
has been very stable for me. If you don't have SCSI or other stuff like 
that you should be fine.
