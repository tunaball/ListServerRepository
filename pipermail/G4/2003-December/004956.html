Mail will import your mail without problems - look in the menu for the word
"Import" - but you will have to manually recreate your mail account in Mail.

Sometimes Mail will choke on a mailbox. In that case you drag/drop the mail
box from Entourage (just the mailbox without the sub-directories!) to the
desktop and you either import them or you drop them on Mail and it will
import it automagically. I've done this a several times and Mail barked on
me only once. Didn't want to import a mailbox with approx. 6500 messages. I
think you shouldn't have any probleems.
