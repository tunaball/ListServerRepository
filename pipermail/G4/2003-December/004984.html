As a subscriber to the Entourage mailing list, I have come upon this 
source of Applescripts for doing the job:  Paul Berkowitz's shareware 
scripts "Export-Import Entourage X" from MacScripter.net at:  
<http://macscripter.net/scriptbuilders/>  That should make the job 
easier. Personally I use Apple's Mail as my main email app and MS 
Entourage as a back-up. If you are not in X, (still using an older 
version, say MS Office 2001 in System 9.x), then search the same site 
for another solution.

Another way is to use a Palm handheld device and sync to Entourage and 
then sync (with Apple's iSync) to Mail.
