Gregg,

I bought both my iBook and G4 from MacWarehouse (they've just been
bought by CDG, though) and for 10 years have been very satisfied. I
think their prices are not quite as low as some but customer service
is excellent. They will help you with anything you have bought and
don't have a strict policy about with what - they just try and help
with anything.

I've wondered about the RAM thing myself. Apple always says any
other RAM but theirs is not covered under warranty, and if I have
a problem suggest I take out all my third party RAM. Well, that
would be all the RAM in the machine. Not likely!

But maybe it would be safer to go with Apple RAM, if you can
afford it?
