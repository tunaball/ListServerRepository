> I'm presently running 10.4.6 on my G4 Dual MDD 867. I remember there  
> was some reason -- problems I read about -- for not upgrading to  
> 10.4.7.. What about 10.4.8? Does it introduce any problems?
>
> If it matters, I have a couple of ATA cards (133 and 66) and a USB  
> 2.0 card installed.

Very few people have problems with any Apple dot.dot release. (Of  
course those who do are often quite vocal about the fact.) My advise  
is to run disk check and fix permissions, back up, and then upgrade  
with the combo upgrader. If you run into a problem roll back using the  
backup. Your other choice is to wade through all of the comments at  
sites like macintouch or macfixit to see if anything scares you off.
