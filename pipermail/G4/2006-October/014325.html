Wayne,

I can't help you with Mail and .Mac but one thing that I would recommend is to back up your Mail database in another email application.  Mail databases are prone to getting corrupted IMHO and I do this.  What I generally do now is to export my messages as mbox text files and import them into Thunderbird.  I would recommend something similar for anyone that needs to protect their messages.  When I was using Claris Emailer I backed up to eMA but eMA doesn't handle my current application.  Taking the trouble seems to almost guarantee no corruption and vice versa.
