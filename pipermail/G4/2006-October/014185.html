If you just want more disk space, why not add a Sonnet
tempo serial ATA card? For $80 you get to use as big a
drive as you like and choose from modern SATA drives
at 150Mbps. I have one in a series 1 B&W G3 with a
Maxtor 150Gb drive. Boots os 9 and 10.
