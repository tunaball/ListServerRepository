On 2 Oct, 2006, at 18:05, SLarsonIH at aol.com wrote:

Can anyone tell me how to open up the CD/DVD module on my Wallstreet?

Look on the side panel of the CD/DVD module for a small hole near the  
tray open button. Insert a straightened out paper clip in hole and  
press gently. There should be a "click" sound and the tray should  
open for you to remove the CD.

You can do this with the computer turned off and avoid having the  
machine try to startup via the disc.
