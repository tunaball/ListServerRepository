Testing your PRAM battery is a no-brainer.

1) Turn off your computer

2) Unplug it from the wall and let it sit - from ten minutes to overnight.

3) Plug it back in, wait 2 minutes, and start it.

4) If your Mac has forgotten the date and time, the battery is no good.
