You can test the battery with a voltmeter.  It should be 3.6 volts, 
although they seem to work fine if they're a little bit lower.  If it's 
under 3.2 volts I'd replace it.  Also, if the reading seems okay at 
first, but then the volts start going down, I'd replace it.

I check the voltage with the battery installed in the motherboard, and 
the computer unplugged, but if you remove the battery to test it, that 
should be fine, too.
