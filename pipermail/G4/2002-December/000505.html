As someone else mentioned, you'll need an ATA controller card. 
Sonnet, SIIG, and Acard are two brands that I know of. I bought a 
SIIG to add a third drive to my G4 400 AGP & didn't need any 
additional cables or parts. Installation of card and drive was a very 
quick & easy process, and if I want to add a fourth drive I'll be 
able to drop it right in.
