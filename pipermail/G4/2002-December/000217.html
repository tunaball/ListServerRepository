If you are referring to the 867 Quicksilver Single processor, I own one and
I am very satisfied with it. I had a strange problem with the Superdrive
about 8 months after I bought it but I was able to convince Apple it was in
fact a Hardware failure and they replaced it. Other than that it sits in my
"computer room" and hums along in OS 10.2.2 without a glitch except for the
occassional self-induced ones. I feel it's a great machine and I sometimes
tend to neglect it because I use my Pismo laptop for most of my home
projects because I can sit in my easy chair watching TV. But for scanning
projects and DVD authoring it works well.
