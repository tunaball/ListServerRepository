Get a set of wires to plug into your stereo headphone jack on 1 end,
and plug into your audio input jacks on the other. Play Real Audio
into the headphones and back into the computer, capturing and
converting to MP3 with SoundJam or Itunes etc.

A friend showed me his set-up while he was running it. I have not
personally done this. Hope this helps.
