Greg,

Your explanation for the old drive 'cranking up' is spot on. Not to 
mention, you've failed to copy many invisible files over to the new 
drive. Boot from the old drive, and use Carbon Copy Cloner (available 
at http://www.bombich.com/ )to make a perfect copy onto the new 
drive. Then test the new one and wipe the old. Then pay the author 
what you deem the software's worth. This is an essential piece of 
software, IMHO. Hope this helps. . .

Rick
