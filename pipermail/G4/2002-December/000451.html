I have just started to experiment with the whole DVD, VCD thing myself.
Haven't gotten into the Divx thing yet. But from the few months I have been
looking around here's some sites that should give you some great information
and some cool tools to help you along. One of the biggest problems is
finding the hidden Mac stuff in some of these sites. Even though some of the
information is for PC's you still can get what they are talking about and
use the Mac software.

http://www.dawnload.net/video_software/dvd_rippers/mac_dvd_ripping_tutorial.
cfm (you should probably visit here first. When you download this not only
do you get instructions, but you also get most of the tools that all the
other sites talk about you needing)
http://www.vcdhelp.com/forum/index.php
http://homepage.mac.com/rnc/
http://www.vcdx.cjb.net/ (this one has been down for a few days, might just
be his ISP)
http://homepage.mac.com/tgpo/home.html

Head-up, just because some of these sites have DVD or something else in the
URL they have just about everything you need for other types of video,
enjoy. Wayne.
