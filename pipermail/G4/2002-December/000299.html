I'm networking my Quicksilver with my son's PC and also going wireless to my
laptop (Pismo). Your best bet, security wise is to get a router. That will
allow you to network the PC and QS with ethernet and keep everything secure
behind a hardware firewall. I'm currently using an Ansante 4 port
"FriendlyNet". The QS has Ethernet built in, the PC may need what is called
a Network Interface Card (NIC) setting up ethernet on the PC depends a lot
on what version of Windows you are using, but it's fairly straight forward.
Routers can cost between $59 and $159 and NIC cards are pretty cheap, under
$30.
