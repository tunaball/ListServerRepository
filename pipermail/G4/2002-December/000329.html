I had a mac and pc networked with a Linksys 4 port cable/DSL router for 
over a year, now using it to network two macs. Although they will tell 
you they don't provide support for using it as a networking device, 
rather than just a router, they acknowledge that it does have that 
capability. That means that in addition to hooking both computers to the 
internet via the cable modem, it allows them to communicate with each 
other for file sharing, etc. Added benefit is that router also acts as 
firewall device.
