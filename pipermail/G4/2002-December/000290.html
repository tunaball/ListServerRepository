What OS? Software Update on OS 9+ has been known to fail for numerous
reasons (search Apple's Knowledge Base or the Discussion Forum at
<http://www.info.apple.com/siteindex.taf> for details). You might have to
dump the Software Update prefs file and try again. The standalone updates
don't rely on the Software Update Engine, Scheduler, or prefs file;
therefore, they work.

FWIW, Software Update works flawlessly in OS X, at least it never failed
for me and I haven't seen any failures reported.

HTH
