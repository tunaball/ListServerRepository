	I recently (just a little over a week ago...) upgraded my 266MHz Beige G3 to a 500 MHz G4 - mine is a ZIF-socketed CPU, not sure what yours is...but mine is certainly *much* snappier in performance. The second site is about as cheap as any.  You also might try the list sponsors here...
