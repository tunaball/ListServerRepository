<<  but if I put the 800 in I get no chime and nothing on the screen.  >>

That condition indicates to me that the card wasn't seated fully in the slot. 
Check carefully that the processor card is FULLY seated before trying to boot.

If it still doesn't at least make some kind of noise at power up, you may 
have a dead card. Even if the processor chip had quit on you, the machine 
should have made some noise even if only the discordant glass 
crash/screeching brakes/raspberry/clink-clank-bong of a bad RAM check.
