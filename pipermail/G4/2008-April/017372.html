I'd go for 1x 1TB HD instead of 2x 500GB HDs because it'll draw less power
from your power supply and generate less heat inside the case.  Were you
planning on making a RAID array of the 2x 500GB drives or something?

> I've researched SATA HDs and PCI controller cards for 
> internal and external SATA conversion/use. Seagate and 
> Western Digital look good and Sonnet Tempo and SIIG 
> controller cards look good too (I also scanned what was 
> available at OWC).

I, and some friends of mine, have had worse luck with WD drives than with
Seagate drives, and Seagate offers a longer warranty, too.
