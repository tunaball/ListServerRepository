>
> Regarding the master/slave switch question, could you elaborate?  
> The reason I ask is that back in the distant past when I last did  
> this, it was necessary to have one device on an IDE channel as a  
> master and the other as a slave, unless the devices and IDE cable  
> allowed Cable Select.

This is correct. The potential for confusion comes from the fact that  
older 40 wire cables were not consistent in implementing Cable  
Select. 80 wire cables implement Cable Select by definition. All B&W  
and newer Macs use 80 wire cables. (I've never supported older Macs  
that use ATA drives so I don't know about them.)
