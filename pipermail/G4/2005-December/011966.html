Correct. The short answer is that the terms master and slave are  
misleading. Neither device on the cable is the master or slave, it's  
better to think of them as device 0 and 1, and slower or faster.  
Slower devices don't slow down faster devices, but since they take  
longer to transfer information they tie up the shared communications  
channel for longer periods of time. While the faster device is  
talking it runs at normal speed.
