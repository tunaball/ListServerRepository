This type of question gets asked frequently, but as far as I know  
Apple's CPUs are model/generation specific. Also note that early  
Sawtooth motherboards are not dual CPU compatible. There are  
utilities that will tell you wether or not yours is. See the  
macsales.com CPU upgrade section or Sonnet's web site.
