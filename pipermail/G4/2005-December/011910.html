On 13 December, 2005, at 3:45:03, Mark Swanson wrote:

> My TiBook is and has been working fine but my Quicksilver (two  
> drives) is giving me a problem.  .......my 200 gig  went schizo and  
> won't let me log in.
> .......The larger drive won't recognize my user password or my  
> administrator password
> .......Is there a work around besides reformatting and re-installing?

Try booting the QuickSilver to your OS-X.4 DVD and use the "Change  
Password" command to replace the old password with something else and  
then re-boot.

I was able to enter a customer's eMac and recover all the user  
identities and files by doing a password change from the OS install  
disk. After confirming that no files had been lost, we recreated the  
individual passwords back to the originals and all worked well after  
that.
