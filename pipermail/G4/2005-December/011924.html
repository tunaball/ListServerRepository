> I am driving an MDD 1GHz dualie with 1 GB RAM and an 80 and 120 GB  
> HDs running Tiger. I just bought my wife a 12" 1.33GHz iBook  
> running Tiger. Will the OS discs from my dualie work on the iBook

Apple doesn't ship machine specific releases. The version that ships  
with a particular machine will have the drivers needed for that  
machine and all other currently supported machines, so you should be  
able to use the most recent release you have on both machines.
