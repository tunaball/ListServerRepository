I had a problem importing my mail from Netscape 4.x on OS 9 into 
Netscape 7.1 on OS 10.3.  The address book imported, but not the e-mail. 
  I finally figured out where the mail in OS X Netscape 7.1 was by 
searching for inbox.  I also located the mail from from Netscape 4.x in 
the prefs folder of the OS 9 system.

I made myself a new POP account in Netscape 7.1 with a dummy input 
server such as dummy.mail.com.  Then I found the inbox for the dummy 
account.  I replaced this new empty inbox file with the one from the OS 
9 pref folder.  Much to my delight, I had access to all my old e-mail.

Presumably, I could then move the messages from the dummy to the real 
account used by OS X.


Try a few things. It just might work.
