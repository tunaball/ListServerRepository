I'm following this thread because i was once in the same boat trying to 
get an airport card for my G4 Sawtooth and having no luck then I found 
this page : http://home.earthlink.net/~metaphyzx/Wireless.htm
which list the cards pci pcmcia and usb adapters that are compatible 
with the mac and it also tells you what software drivers you would need.
I opted for the following PCI card in my G4

Vendor   Type      Product ID nterfaceChipset OSSupport Driver/s Used
Motorola 802.11b/g WPC1810G  PCI Broadcom 10.2-10.4 Airport 3.1 and up  
I paid 25.00 for it and that was with shipping.
It's reported in Apple System profiler as an Airport Extreme Card
