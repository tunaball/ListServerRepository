On Dec 28, 2005, at 12:32 PM, bigthor at webzone.net wrote:

> I'm purchasing a Sony VDR-VC20 DVDirect DVD Recorder. The manual  
> states that it connects to a Mac via a Hi-Speed USB (USB 2.0- 
> compliant) interface.
>
> My question is, my Mac has USB 1.1, right? Will the Sony VRDVC20  
> DVDirect DVD Recorder work with my Mac's USB 1.1, work slower, or  
> not work at all?

I doubt that USB 1.1 would be able to keep up when trying to burn a  
CD or DVD. Get a USB 2.0 card, they're cheap - $10-$50 depending on  
name brand, etc. Make sure it's Mac compatible and/or returnable,  
last thing I knew one common chipset was compatible and the other  
wasn't. Don't know if that has changed with Tiger or not.
