OOPS, what I meant to say was an ethernet hub, (if all of your current 
ports are in use) then connect the WIN XP PC to the Mac via ethernet 
and enable printer sharing. You'll find printer sharing in the systems 
preferences under "Print & Fax" and check mark the box that says "Share 
my printers with other computers"

This is what I am doing in my set-up, except that my other computers 
are first connected to a wireless router that has 4 ethernet wired 
ports. All of my computers along with my internet cable connection are 
connected together through the router (a Belkin) either wired or 
wireless, so that all have internet access. Never have a problem 
printing unless there are jobs in Que. and the one that I want to print 
I want in a hurry, I can cancel the other jobs and ask who ever is 
printing from the remote location to start their print job again.
