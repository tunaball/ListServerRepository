Did you go to the "System Preferences->Sharing" pref panel and enable 
the "Personal File Sharing"? Once you do that, go to the interface 
under the "Network" panel and enable Apple Talk on the appropriate 
interface (e.g., Airport or Ethernet or whatever).  You may also want 
to check to make sure your Zone settings are correct.

I am not sure how OSX works with OS9 print sharing - as my network is 
all OSX and print sharing works just fine.
