The USB port on the PowerBook may not have enough power to drive the USB
drive. I have that problem with a USB2 drive on my G4 PowerBooks. The Drive
came with a special USB cable that draws power from 2 USB ports. It works on
the TiBook but not on the AlBook because it cant attach it to two ports. The
instructions that came with the drive state some laptops don't have enough
power to drive the drive unless you use a powered USB hub or attach the
cable to two ports.
