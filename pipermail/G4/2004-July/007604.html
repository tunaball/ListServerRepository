> > So it works like this?

> > Cable Modem---->Netgear Router--->Airport Extreme
> > 
> > If so, does the Netgear router support port mapping?  Your Airport 
> > Extreme does.  What you need to do is map port 21 to the G4 IP address.
> > 
> > If you look in the Airport Setup Util:
> > 
> > 1) Highlight your base station name
> > 2) Click Configure button
> > 3) Click "Show All" on left nav
> > 4) Click "Port Mapping" on top nav
> > 
> > You will see a screen that says "public port	private ip addr
> private 
> > port"
> > 
> > Now, go to your netgear setup (web based or otherwise), find the 
> > similar screen.
> > 
> > Map Public port 21 to your 733's IP address.  If you have not changed 
> > the FTP port on your G4, then map to port 21.
> > 
> > If you expose your port to the outside world, I would be sure to have a 
> > secure FTP server and disable anonymous access or everyone with port 
> > scanner will be hitting your machine.
> >
