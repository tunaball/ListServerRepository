At 18:47 -0400 7/6/04, Jerry Bunn wrote:
>I'm not sure what you mean by "with the option to download and SAVE 
>the packages."  When I click "Check Now" it only shows software I 
>never downloaded in the first place and some I have chosen not to 
>download like "iPod update" because I don't have an iPod.
>How do I get the files I have downloaded on software update?

1. Launch Software Update
2. After it has determined what files you need, select one and click 
on the Update Menu
3. You have three options under the Update Menu: "Download Only", 
"Install", "Install and Keep Package"
3. The "Install" option is just the same as the "Install" button in 
the lower right of the Software Update window. The last option 
downloads, then installs the update and keeps the package on the hard 
drive. The first option just saves the package to your hard drive. 
The packages can be found in the Hard drive/Library/Packages folder.
