Hey, Lavode,

This is actually a good sign ; ). That is, you know that there is 
something in your build that is not quite right.

Before you try something simpler, as an alternative go to your Assets 
Window and click on the Log tab to see if there were any errors (in red 
I think) in the Build you did. It's a great way to see if any of your 
DVD elements is off in some way or if DVDSP is having trouble with it.

One more thought: how are you attempting to start your DVD when it is 
inserted? I think on my home machine it starts right up at the Home 
menu I've set, but I think in DVD Player, for instance, and maybe in 
lots of controllers for DVD machines, you have to press Play or Menu or 
something to get the first screen to appear. Are you not getting any 
screen/menu at all?
