I have installed some H-P's and found them to be OK, I have also 
installed a number of e series Lexmark printers and found them to 
excellent.

They are not carried in as many places and the cartridges are best 
bought on line.  Lexmark also has a series of extended life 
cartridges that work great and bring down the cost per page.  The 
initial price is higher than usual but worth it.  I have lexmark T520 
with duplex and have had no problems with it, outside of an 
occasional paper jam, for 3 years and had a lexmark before that for 
about 6 years.
