I recently purchased an Epson CX 6400.  It replaced my trusty C80 and 
Cannon scanner.  The new all-in-one works great!  Photos are clear, 
sharp and waterproof.  The scanner works well enough to copy 8 X 10 
photos with excellent results and the software that comes with the 
CX6400 adds lots of flexibility to the machine.  I have used Epson 
printers since my old Stylus Color II and have never been unhappy with 
the products.

Good luck with whatever you get.

Sig
