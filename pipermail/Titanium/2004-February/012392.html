I think it has something to do with the restrictions on advertising 
in the UK.  From the comments sent to the register, the majority 
think that the kids were somehow "harmed" by all the "negative" 
attention that they are getting.  They see this big bad company 
exploiting children and even touting their guilt in front of millions 
of people.  Do they not think that these kids were compensated for

that we'll all go out and buy tons of Pepsi and rot all our teeth

Maybe they are just pissed that they can't buy iTunes this way, but 
they can't buy them via the Pepsi promotion either, so what's the 
point?
