On Feb 12, 2004, at 9:46 PM, T Molnar wrote:

> my junk mail filter doesn't seem to be working at all.
> I put it on (training) and despite junk mail coming in it doesn't even 
> mark any of it as junk.
> none what so ever.

You should be manually marking items as junk if the filter doesn't 
catch it. That will help train the filter to the kind of junk mail you 
receive. It will start to mark them as junk after that and it will get 
better as you continue to mark and (possibly) unmark messages that it 
identified wrong.
