Have you tried running OmniDiskSweeper to see if your partition had any 
huge cache or log files? Photoshop, Word and the CUPS printing server, 
for example, can create enormous (invisible) temporary files.

In general, any partition should have at least 15-20% of free disk 
space to guard against suddenly expending cache, log or swap files, as 
well as to prevent excessive file fragmentation.
