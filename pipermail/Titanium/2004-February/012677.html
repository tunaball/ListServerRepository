> Hi TiList -
> Other than clock speed and harddrive size, is there any difference 
> between
> the 1.0ghz 17" Albook and the 1.33ghz 17" Albook?  I'm thinking of 
> buying
> the 1.0, but want to know if I'm missing out on some good L3 cache....?
>

The rev A 17" Powerbook has:

* 1 GHz G4 (older) processor
* 256 KB L2 cache
* 1 MB L3 cache
* the NVIDIA GeForce4 440 Go video card
* no USB 2.0

The rev B:

*1,33 GHz G4 (newer) processor
* 512 KB L2 cache
* no L3 cache
* the ATI Mobility Radeon 9600 video card
* USB 2.0

So with the rev B, you get a faster and more modern G4 processor, 
double the L2 cache (which apparently more than makes up for the 
absence of L3 cache) and a much better video card, as well as USB 2.0 
capability. Ciao,
