I haven't found any information on the internet about Canon's recent release
of printer drivers but I visit regularly just to check and found new drivers
posted today.

Good news for folks like me who have Duplex paper handling units installed
on BJ printers: they now work properly in Panther. Select long-side binding
and it now prints the front and back in the proper orientation.

The one I downloaded (version 2.3.2 for the i960) seems to speed things up
as well.
