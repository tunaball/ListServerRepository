What format is the digital out on the DAT.

I Have an M-audio Firewire 410, it has S/PDIF in and out on phono 
sockets and optical. It is a goor companion for powerbook as it is 
portable. It may be overkill for what you want to do, there may be a 
cheaper solution.

It shows up in the core audio (in X) so you would just select it as 
your input for your sound editing prog and record each one in. If its 
9 then you need an ASIO compliant recording app.

Hope this helps
