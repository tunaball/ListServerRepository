Try Peak LE - It's a 'lite' version which may have come with a CD burner
etc.

Open it up, click the record button, check the levels and the press the
record button.

Click stop, save the file, do some fade ins/outs, cut bits out, normalise it
and save ready for an audio CD for Toast etc.

Download a trial at:

http://www.bias-inc.com/downloads/freeTrials/
