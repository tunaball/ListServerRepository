Edirol UA1-D (www.edirol.com) offers SPDIF i/o on RCA Jacks for about 
80 buck US.  Then you should be able to record direct to your sound 
edit app, and then import to i-tunes.  It does not provide analog 
monitoring though.  You can use your ti's sound out for that.
