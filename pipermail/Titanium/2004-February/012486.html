> What are the best recommendations this list can provide regarding the

> Base Station before, but at a minimum of $200 US, it is a bit pricey... I'm
> looking for ease of setup, being able to shut out others in my apartment

I don't know if anyone else has given you advice, but I've used both the
Linksys wireless gateway/router/4-port hub (802.11b) and the D-Link wireless
gateway/router/4-port hub (802.11g).

Both do a pretty decent job, but D-Link mentions Macs in their
documentation, while Linksys does not.  In fact, I've heard that Linksys has
gotten downright hostile recently towards anything non-Windows.
