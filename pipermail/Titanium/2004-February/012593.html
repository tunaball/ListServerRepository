John,

I'm using a USB 2.0 card without any problems. You need at least 10.2.8 
to get the USB 2.0 speed. 10.3 works great. Never had any hiccups, even 
when I removed the card before turning it's power off. 10.2.7 gave me a 
lot of kernel related problems when I tried things like that.

The brand I'm using is dynalink, which I believe to be a Dutch brand. I 
can't imagine that they create the hardware themselves. One thing I 
like about it is that the USB-plug plugs straight into the card, no 
flimsy connectors attached.

Hope yours will work as fine as mine,
