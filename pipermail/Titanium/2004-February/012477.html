Hey Dave...

If you've got ports in the back covered by a flip-down door, then you do 
indeed have a Titanium PB, and you have a 1GB limit to RAM.  If your ports 
are on the sides of the laptop, then you have a new Aluminum PB, which can 
handle up to 2GB of RAM.
