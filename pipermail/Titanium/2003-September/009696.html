On Tuesday, September 2, 2003, at 08:08  AM, mac2 mail wrote:

> What professional features are you requesting to be added?

-- Options for attachments a la Outlook Express.

-- Template emails.

-- EASY access to enabling/disabling accounts (not a long trip through 
preferences)

-- A way to turn of display of .pdfs in the email body. (why do some 
display and others not when there are multiple .pdfs attached?)

-- Consistency in the way media attachments show up in the body of the 
email (why are there sometimes players and sometimes files --even when 
the same type/creator/etc. --even in the same email when viewed at 
different times?)

-- A simple, quick way to render embedded HTML in a particular message 
without a trip to the preferences to turn it on for one piece of mail.

-- Better handling of links in messages with odd HTML

-- a single command to strip off attachments and throw them away while 
keeping the email

-- an archival method that would do the above in one fell swoop for an 
entire mailbox.

-- TAB NAVAGATION FROM PANE TO PANE !!!

-- A way to move through a message list without changing the 
read/unread marker

-- A way to move through a message list that reverses the direction you 
go when you delete.

Is that a start??
