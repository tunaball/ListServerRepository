Under the 'blue' Apple menu, on the far left of the Menu. You will 
see, "About this Mac" etc...down three or four lines from the top: 
System preferences, Dock, Locations..

My Swedish is non-existent, but there are a couple of possibilities 
for "Location": platsen,  lokalisering, lage, sparande, or maybe 
forlaggning, anlaggande

If you find it, almost certainly on the Apple menu, you will see the 
default set to 'Automatic' (in Swedish), with a choice of Network 
Preferences..." also. select Network preferences, set up a new 
account, include only the modem, or cable, Airport, that you will use 
on the account, and name it. It will then also appear in "Locations".

You can also find it, at the top of the page in System preferences--> 
"Network". Select network and there is a drop-down 'button that says 
Location. it is right at the top of the network window. Select 
'New..." and there you are. So, two ways to find it. Hope that helps.
