Hi,

I used the Buffalo card for a while and it worked fine. The Belkin and 
SMC-cards also work. I did , however, get tired of having a protruding 
antenna so I switched back to the internal "b" airport one....
As a base station I use the Buffalo WBR-G54, and it works without a 
hitch. I extend its range to my studio, which is a separate building 
across the yard, with a bridged Buffalo WLA-G54. No problem there 
either.
