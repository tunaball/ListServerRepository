My daughter had a similar problem with her 155" TiPB. If I remember 
correctly, we unplugged the power supply, took out the battery. Waited 
30 secs to 1 minute. Pressed the reset button in the back of the 
computer (tiny round thing requires a ball point pen or paper clip). 
Held down that thing for 30 secs. Put everything back together. If that 
doesn't work, I'd have to agree that it's a problem with the power 
supply.
