On 9/11/03 5:42 PM, "Khyber Courchesne" <khyber at urbisnet.com> wrote:

> I just got a sony tape deck adpator at the apple store for my ipod, but the
> shielding is pretty poor - as the car speed goes up, so does the static.
> Anyone recommend a better unit than the sony?

I haven't been able to find any unit that is shielded better-- if your deck
supports a CD changer, you may be able to get an AUX in that goes through
that port.  The other alternative is an FM injector-- it's like an FM
transmitter, but it actually goes in the antenna line, so you get really
great sound quality.
