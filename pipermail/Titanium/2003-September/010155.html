Bigger "airport windows" and the fact that the screen itself acts as an
"airport window" will be a distinct improvement.

Also, moving the "airport window" away from a "2.4GHz sink" (ie. the LiIon
battery, which acts as a supressor of 2.4GHz RF) will have a *HUGE* affect
on the range.  That was something that the designers didn't know about until
they had already committed to a case design.
