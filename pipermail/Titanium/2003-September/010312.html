I hope this is not a sign of things to come.  I got my Al 15", and 
before I even turned it on, I noticed that it would not stay latched.

very reliably.  usually when I try to close it, it kind of half 
latches, and then pops open.  The hook looks like it is coming out of

but the hook does not want to fully engage.

have to send it back and are quoting 7 to 10 days.  Bummer.
