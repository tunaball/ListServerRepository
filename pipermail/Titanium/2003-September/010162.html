My 17" does no better than my former Ti-800 and Ti-500. However, all 3 
get (got) a solid 4 rings under 30-35', 3-4 rings at 30-40' and 2-3 
rings at 40-50' out on the deck at our house. Gray, snow or extreme 
Base Stations also were all the same.

Orientation of the BS (sides when looking at the Apple are best), a 
higher gain external antenna or a separate BS in the house used as a 
repeater or on a switch in the shop wired to the house seem to be the 
best choices for extending the signal in your case.
