If you shod have a DVD Drive, and with at least 512 of RAM, a 1GHz  
processor and enough free space on your HD, it should load OK. Was  
your system install DVD good? If you need a larger drive, it can be  
installed without too much fuss. On the Titanium all you have to do is  
remove the back case and the drive is right there. You need a torx 8  
screwdriver to remove the two screws on the side of the battery  
compartment and the drive comes out and a new on in. There are  
instructions on the internet if you need them.
