I was fretting about the possibility of M$ dropping Office for Mac, and 
how that might affect the Switch campaign. Then I remembered that, 
among people I know at least, PC users don't even know Office is 
available for Mac — even though it was on Mac first.

So the upside is that losing Office wouldn't affect most people's 
perception of the Mac. The downside is that Apple hasn't been able to 
get across that the Mac has Office in the first place.
