Intel to launch 3.2GHz P4 on Monday
By Tony Smith
Posted: 20/06/2003 at 13:14 GMT

Intel is expected to announce a 3.2GHz Pentium 4 on Monday at $637 a pop.

And it is believed that ATI will unveil its latest Pentium 4 chipsets

The new processor will support a 200MHz frontside bus quad-pumped for
