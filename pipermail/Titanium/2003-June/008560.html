You can do what I did:  become a registrar yourself, via OpenSRS
( http://resellers.tucows.com/opensrs/ ).  That way, you can buy,
transfer, or renew domain names for $10.  And you don't have to
worry about whether or not you can trust the registrar...you *are*
the registrar.
