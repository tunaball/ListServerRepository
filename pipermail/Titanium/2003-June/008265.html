On the TiBooks (at least the later ones) there are little plastic ports
about 1.5" long on either side of the wrist wrests. The antennae sit behind
these, as they are more RF "transparent" than the Titanium case. The one on
the left side is easily accessible behind an adhesive strip when the battery
is removed. If the antenna wire is not aligned perfectly behind the plastic
strip, reception could be diminished. Sometimes it can be re-aligned or
massaged back into place.
