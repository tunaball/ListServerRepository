I recently got a leather full-keyboard pad from Other World Computing 
as seen here:

http://eshop.macsales.com/Catalog_Item.cfm?ID=3814&Item=OWCLSPTIG4

I've had it nearly a week and have noticed a marked (no pun intended) 
improvement in keyboard oils on the screen -- i.e., none, now.  I used 
to have to heavily wipe down the keyboard and trackpad area before 
closing the lid, and even that still left some marks.  But after a good 
cleaning and using this, the screen is nice and clean and I just have 
to place it and close.  I recommend it.  This is on a 15" TiBook.
