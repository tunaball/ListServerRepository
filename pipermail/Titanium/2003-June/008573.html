 MS Intellimouse Explorer Optical Wireless. I adore mine, it is awesome. My wife has the MS 
Intellimouse Optical Wireless Blue and it is really good too, but my favorite is the Explorer.

I have used MS optical mice exclusively since they came out. I never had good luck with Logitech 
so I steer away from them (the driver for Logitec mice had a nasty tendency to overrun the 
keyboard buffer on Windows NT 4, which would lock up the machine until rebooted).
