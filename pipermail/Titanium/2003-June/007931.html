> how would i know if my ti book is in sleep mode. (will i see the small 
> light on after i
> close the lid?)
Yes, once you close the lid, you'll see the small light on the lid of 
the TiBook, opposite to its position below the screen. Just try and 
you'll see.

> and also while i let it in the sleep mode, do i need to unplug the 
> power?
You can leave it plugged.

> also i had read about some people burn their keyboard when they didn't 
> do it right.
I guess that if you *don't* close the lid and put the TiBook in the 
bag, the heat could damage something (though I think that the first 
think to be damaged would be the screen).

>  how would i know if i am doing it right?
 From the small light. If it is on the computer is sleeping.
