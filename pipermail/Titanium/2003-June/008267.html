I have had a number of different airport connection problems, but not 
with my Ti. Okay, range isn't great as other Macs I have but that's 
not the problem.

If the card works fine in another computer, but another airport card 
doesn't do any better, then the problem is internal.

My daughter's iBook worked great for the first month and then the 
range of no more than 10 feet started. Tried all kinds of things and 
then called Apple support. They had me try all kinds of things and 
then sent a box to have it shipped back for repair. There was a bad 
internal wire connection apparently. It has worked flawlessly for the 
last year.

Recently my son's iMac didn't connect, at any distance. The card 
worked fine in a laptop. I tried a lot of different things and was 
about to zap PRAM when I thought I would trash the preference file 
first. That did the trick.

With reception, abeit poor, I am thinking that the internal antenna 
wire is your problem. Not where it plugs into the card necessarily, 
but maybe where it connects to the motherboard. If in warranty call 
Apple Support, you might need to send it back.
