>hi. question about iskin

I originally intended to only put the iSkin on when closing my Big Al lid, to protect the screen from finger oils, which I had already seen signs of. But it looked too perfectly designed to prevent dust and liquids from getting into the keyboard, so I just wound up leaving it on the keys all the time, open or closed. It inhibits typing a bit, but I haven't reverted to inserting it and removing it all the time. Strangely, the finger oil marks, in the shape of the keyboard, aren't visible to me.

Although the iSkin is designed for the TiBook (and similar) keyboards, it fits OK on the Big Al.
