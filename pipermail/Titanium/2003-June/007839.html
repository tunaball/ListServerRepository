FWIW, I had the problem when I replaced the DVD drive with a combo drive 
in my TiPB 500,15 inch.  The mounting frame which holds the drive does 
not secure it very well on one side.  That allows the drive to slightly 
drop(when warm?) and then the CD hits the case when you eject it.  I 
added a stack of long narrow rubber pieces along the very outer edge of 
the drive to keep it in line with the frame.  (The rubber pieces are 
designed to tape on the bottom of something to keep it from 
sliding--Home Depot).  The bottom of the PB holds the drive inplace in 
the frame and the CD goes in and out just fine.
