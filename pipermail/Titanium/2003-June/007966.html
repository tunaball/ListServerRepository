You are right, I looked it up, and it seems that the stream will take 
approximately 12.5 GB / hour of recorded stream. Compared to EyeTV, 
which will store almost 20 hours using the same disk space. The quality 
difference is huge, and the Formac is very useful for converting analog 
video tapes to digital video, and has many other uses. You should 
decide wether you need a video converter with a tv tuner, or a TiVo 
like PVR. If you just want to watch TV programs live, then you will 
probably appreciate the superior quality of the Formac box.

If you decide to go with Formac, then you should be aware of this 
alternative software:

http://mitzpettel.com/software/vidi.html

It is amazing compared to the program that ships with the product. And 
free!
