The tech I spoke with at Griffin warned me that inserting the iTrip so that 
it's perpendicular to the iPod as you suggest is dangerous, because the 
iPod jack is quite fragile, particularly the outer ring. If you drop the 
iPod with the iTrip installed that way you risk destroying the jack which 
will be very expensive to repair. That has evidently already happened to 
several people.

According to the tech, Griffin added the dummy plug to fit into the 
FireWire port cavity for precisely that reason, to protect the jack on the 
iPod.
