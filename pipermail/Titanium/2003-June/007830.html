Maybe it bears repeating: keep a THIN credit card near your TiBook. 
When a CD won't eject, insert a corner of the card into the CD slot, at 
about the center, pointing up at about a 30-degree angle, while the 
drive is attempting the eject. The CD will slide out under the credit 
card.

This tip was given me over the phone by Apple help and it's never 
failed. I cut an old credit card down to size just for the purpose; 
it's replaced my old straightened-out paper clip.

Most of my eject problems seemed to be cured when I went to 10.2.6, but 
an extra-thick label, like the Memorex glossy labels, will still make 
problems. I'm told this is because of a deflected top plate, so watch 
that right hand-heel!
