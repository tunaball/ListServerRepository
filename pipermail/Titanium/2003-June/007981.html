We bought 4 x Canon Lide 30 and 1 x Canon Lide 50 (only sold in Europe so
far). We dumped more expensive HP and Epson scanners. Umax is junk. The
Canons work very well in OS9 (OK in X but must resort to some Classic
supporting apps such as OCR). I play in X.10.2.6 and work in 922. The
software package is a bargain for the price and your original CD allows 3rd
party software updates directly at the Canon support site. The slim/light
scanner even travels well with a Ti in a briefcase-- and, as has been
mentioned, the USB bus alone powers the scanner. Buttons power up on hot
plug in with about 10 seconds to attach/scan. Scans are even good enough for
Photoshop. Highly recommended!
