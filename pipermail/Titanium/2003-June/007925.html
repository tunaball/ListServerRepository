I have a really, really cheap FM thingy that is sold in Radio Shack. It
works most of the time. It has a manual tuner so you can usually find a
blank section on the dial. The ones that have two or three frequencies would
never work for me.

One thing to keep in mind is where your auto antenna is. It is fairly easy
for me because it is etched on the glass of one of the back/side windows
(Subaru Forester). If yours is drilled into a rear fender or the roof, I
would imagine it could be tricky for an FM sender.
