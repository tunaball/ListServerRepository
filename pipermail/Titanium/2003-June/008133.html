I've got it mostly working now. I completely took it apart, even the 
little white "clips" (there are 3).  Under the rightmost white clip, I 
noticed a lot of "gunk".  I removed it with a toothpick and afterward, 
the spacebar would stick down.  Turns out there was more gunk under the 
little metal piece the top  bar slides under.  Now it is a bit better, 
but not completely fixed. I think I will need a new keyboard.
