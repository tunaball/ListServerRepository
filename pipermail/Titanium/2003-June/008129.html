hi: i did a little looking around and found this thread at the apple 
support boards.  
http://discussions.info.apple.com/WebX?14@95.cuoTauMemki.8@.3bc114d8/3  
   i found that if i press firmly right below the left command key i can 
cause a freeze.   on the above referencedthread, the guy who posted 
said he solved this as follows:

"Bascially I found that a small orange ribbon cable that passes right 
under the area I mention above had become trapped under a section of 
the frame (there's a Torx screw that normally holds the frame down 
firmly in this area, this screw had gotten loose).  The trapped cable 
had become abraded and was starting to short whenever I pressed on that 
area, causing the hang."

  i'm not so keen on trying that as i don't even see an orange ribbon.   
another response to the thread said he had a new logic board installed.

has anyone else experienced this.  thanks, eric
