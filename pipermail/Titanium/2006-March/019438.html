Hi, Trevor,

AOL instant messaging (AIM), which iChat uses, transmits on TCP ports  
5190, or also 5191, 5192, 5193
MSN chat uses port 1863
Yahoo messenger uses port 5050

It appears that a firewall somewhere is blocking port 5190, but 1863  
and 5050 are allowed through. I'd bet if your Dell colleague used  
AIM, he'd fail also. Since your iChat/AIM works in Switzerland, it's  
not your PowerBook. Either the firewall administrator has a bias  
against AOL, or nobody has come along yet to ask for access to AIM;  
just MSN and Yahoo.

Or perhaps the firewall administrator intends to block all chat but  
doesn't realize that MSN and Yahoo operate on different ports. If you  
inquire about opening up AIM and he says no, you may want to neglect  
to mention about MSN and Yahoo already working.
