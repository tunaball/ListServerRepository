Make sure you're looking in the system /Library/Logs/ folder, not in  
your user library.  Alternatively, open System Profiler, click on  
Logs on the left side, and choose the panic.log file.  To see Logs in  
the contents pane, you'll have to have View->Full Profile set in the  
menu.

If you still can't find it, then apparently your computer hasn't  
logged any of the panics.
