One thing to watch out for with Panther and an original PowerBook G4 
400/500Mhz, is that if you use firewire drives, you may have problems 
with 10.3.2.

I (and a couple others I've talked too) have experienced jumpy mouse 
behavior with random clicks/drags when doing large firewire Finder 
copying.

10.3.1 does not have this problem so I've had to stick with it for now, 
since I normally have 3 external firewire drives mounted and do lots of 
video copying.  I've not heard of it affecting newer models, just the 
original 400/500Mhz.
