I used to sell TiBook Paint, and stopped due to match issues.  Here's the
email I send to people who inquire:

"Unfortunately, I've found that TiBooks vary somewhat in color from one
machine to the next, so I've stopped selling touchup paint (I don't want the
touch up job to be less than perfect).  However, in order to get a perfect
match for your machine, I recommend going to a local car paint shop, and
having them color match your tibook."
