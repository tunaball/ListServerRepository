I feel relieved this issue is known. I am also sad that I had to quote 
a discussion this old for a bug this serious - it's a critical bug on 
the operating system that leads to an unrecoverable denial of service, 
dating back to the previous point release!

> Several have reported thoose panics under such conditions and changing
> that habit to instead disconnect before sleep has solved the problem.

Oh yeah, but this is incredibly stupid and inconvenient. Picking up 
one's laptop and disconnecting a USB cable suddenly becomes a 
potentially unnerving behavioural action, forcing the user to keep 
going through a lot of open documents and save, etc...

I wonder when this bug would be fixed, and also whether we all have it 
on our systems by virtue of being Powerbook users.

As a side-note, I've noticed the incidence happen much less when the 
following 3rd party software were not installed or disabled -

USB Overdrive X
uControl
Logitech Control Centre

There is an obvious common theme here...
