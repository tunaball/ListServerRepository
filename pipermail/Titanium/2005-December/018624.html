One thing you need to be aware is Apple are very careful about which 
parts they cover under warranty and which part they don't. At the time I 
had my Ti 400, the plastic "frame" sandwiched between the Titanium parts 
was not covered, for example. So this is the first thing you should 
check. Was your Applecare a 1 year? if so you could always call up, and 
extend to the three year, and then get the lid looked it (if it is 
indeed covered).

If not, your best bet would be some local technicians who specialise in 
Apple products. The  Apple store  is probably a good starting point.
