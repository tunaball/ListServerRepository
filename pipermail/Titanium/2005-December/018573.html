On 5-Dec-05, at 9:34 AM, Mikael Byström wrote:

> Ardeshir Mehta said:
>
>> Though I had to save everything as an .rtf file - if I saved it in  
>> Word format my iMac would unexpectedly crash
>
> How can you do layout with rtf-format?

You do the layout just as you do when working with anything else in  
MS Word. But when it comes to *saving* the file, you click on "File",  
then choose "Save As...", and when the little window with the file  
name, etc. comes up, click on "Format:", and then choose "Rich Text  
Format (RTF)".
