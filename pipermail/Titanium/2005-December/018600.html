On Dec 8, 2005, at 9:10 PM, David DelMonte wrote:

> I have a 1Ghz AlPb. Is it possible to upgrade the processor? I did  
> this with a Powerlogix unit on my old G4 and it kept that machine  
> going for ages..

You can take it to 1.92 Ghz with a Daystar upgrade which uses a  
Freescale MPC7447A PowerPC core.  The MPC7447A runs cooler at 1,920  
Mhz (power dissipation - 21.0 watts) than your original MPC7447 at  
1,000 Mhz (power dissipation - 29.3 watts).
