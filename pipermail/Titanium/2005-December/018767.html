>  My wife and I are trying to make a decision about
>  moving from 10.3.9 to 10.4.  I've been off list for a
>  while so I don't know the verdict.  She says she read
>  something that indicated it's not a good upgrade.  Are
>  those of you who have upgraded quite happy?

I've done all the upgrades incrementally as they've come out, on my 
15 inch PowerBook 1.67MHz. I've never gone the route of the clean 
wipe because I didn't have time or enough free disk space. But I've 
never had any problems. I'm now using 10.4.3 with no problems. Just 
make sure you are backed up first, just in case.

After about a week of heavy, daily use, I usually reboot as things 
sometimes start slowing down, mostly because of decreasing hard drive 
space (less than a gig) with large cache files from many applications 
open (especially Safari). That immediately gets things back to speed 
(and about 3 or 4 gigs of free space). But that's not a 10.4 issue, 
I'm quite sure (it happened in 10.3, too). I know, I know, I should 
have more free space ... I'm working on it. :)
