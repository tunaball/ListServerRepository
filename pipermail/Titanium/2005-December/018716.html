Solaris 10 runs in VirtualPC6, but you'll have a hey of a time  
getting the X-server configured.  I'm running it in VPC6 with the  
Xorg server.

Solaris will NOT run in VirtualPC7.  The kernel doesn't recognize the  
VPC7 cpu, and it won't even boot.  Neither will BeOS, which also runs  
fine on VPC6.
