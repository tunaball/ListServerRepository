If you have one of the earlier models of G4 (say 400/500/550/667 Mhz), 
there is a small reset button on the back panel which can get "stuck" 
behind the case and jammed into the on position. If this happens the 
symptoms can be very similar to what you describe.

See the following URL:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=88095
