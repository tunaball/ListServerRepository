Notice the hole next to it--that's the security cable hole, not the 
headphone jack. If it was the headphone jack, we would be able to see 
two distinct shaded areas--a short length of grille slots and the PC 
card slot.

look like the keyboard goes to the edge. Enlarge the picture to twice

space between the keyboard and the edge. Also, look at the point 
where the power button should be, and you'll see a slight blemish.
