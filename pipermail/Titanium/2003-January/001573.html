This is really a Windows/wireless issue now and mostly off-topic for 
TiBooks. For configuration details, you would probably do better on a 
Windows wireless list.

However, to tinker on your own a little further, his configuration should 
be set up similarly as your TiBook, whether that be by setting DHCP or 
static addresses. Always turn off encryption and any MAC filtering on the 
ABS first, if you have that set, to take security out of the picture. They 
can be reenabled once you get the basic connection working.
