Launching Safari Help - helps.
Not being an ass with my suggestion, there are a few ways of doing it that's
all.. If you launch Help there's a section called "Working with Bookmarks",
choose "Organizing the Bookmarks Menu" and it's all there.

The pane on the left you see when you are viewing your Bookmarks is for
organization.. You have to physically move them over into the Bookmark Menu
(at the top of the Collections Pane).

Since you mentioned it - I also noticed that Safari lets you use CMD-1,
CMD-2... to launch shortcuts on the Menubar. Nice.
