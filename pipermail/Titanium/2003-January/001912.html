For those who haven't tried it - don't hesitate.

I completely understand the need for many to remain with Quirk.. I already
know that I will have some EXTREMELY challenging files to convert with ID.
And if you are service related, you have no options here.

But for comparison sake, the step from Quirk to ID is literally (to me
anyway), about 10x the experience of the step from OS9 to OSX. I do LOTS of
Illustrator work (too bad for Illustrator being such a sucker) and the
continuity between applications is almost flawless. In fact I could say it
is overwhelming.
