i think the subject line is pretty straight forward. I think the 17" & 
12" is a great idea. And to those whom are whining about 1GHz here's 
one funny, and memorable story I have.
	A customer comes up to me and says "I just bought my new PowerBook G4 
awhile ago and now Apple releases an improved PowerBook G4 Titanium 
today that is at least twice as fast as the one I bought and nearly 
half the price that I paid." (obviously there's a little bit of 
exaggeration on his part.)
	I replied,	"Well, I would ask where's your commitment? I suppose 
you're going to tell me that if you hadn't gotten married. You could 
now find a new wife who's TWICE as good-looking at HALF the age?"
(Now, I knew this customer and his wife for a long time so it was a 
tongue in cheek sort of joke. He smiled and laughed at the metaphor and 
to this day I don't think the topic has ever crept up again.)

Something to think about.
