After using Safari for a couple of days, I realized that I just can't 
give up tabbed browsing. Although Safari may load a few percent faster 
than Chimera, it doesn't matter if I have to wait for a page to load. 
With Chimera, a page is usually loading while I'm still reading the 
article (command click). Tabbed browsing is just to efficient to go 
back to non tabbed browsing.

As for the spiffy new 17" gigahertz models, they sure are nice, but I 
never find myself in a situation where I max out my 500 mHz processor 
and wish I had a 1 gig processor, I just go do something in a different 
app. The days of waiting for my computer to finish a task while I sit 
and do nothing are long gone. If I didn't have a Powerbook at all, I 
think I'd go for the one gig 15". I don't think I'd want to carry 
around anything that cost over $3200.
