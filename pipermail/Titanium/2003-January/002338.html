Glenn L. Austin wrote on 1/19/03 8:42 AM:

> You really can't directly compare the G4 and the Pentium.

But you can directly compare results. Say by comparing real tasks that you
do with both systems. And, it isn't hard to find that a 1 GHz G4 processor
in a Mac is substantially faster than a 1 GHz Pentium processor in a Windows
machine. However, it doesn't take long to find that a 3+ GHz Pentium
outperforms a dual 1.25 GHz Mac - all other things being fairly equal.

The place where Apples outshine Windows is in ease of use, dependability,
ability to easily play with lots of other peripherals, design, etc. We are
behind in speed - I hope that changes soon, but I don't expect it.
