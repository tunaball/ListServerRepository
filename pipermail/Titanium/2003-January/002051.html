As  mentioned, the cable modem remembers the MAC address (hardware 
address, not Macintosh address) of whatever it's connected to and 
doesn't let anything else connect to the Internet, so you'll need to 
unplug the cable modem for at least 20 seconds to reset it.  After the 
20 seconds, plug it back in, and it will detect whatever's plugged into 
it whether it be a basestation, wireless router, etc.
