Robert asks...

> If you carry a spare battery for your Ti and you know it won't be used
> is it better to leave it charged or without charged?

Why charged, of course!  It's not much use otherwise unless you like to 
carry dead weight with you :-)

Seriously, I carry a charged spare battery with me most of the time.  I 
swap batteries every 2 or 3 weeks and each one happily has 4 glowing 
dots of power when unused.  Over 8 months now and I've not noticed any 
degradation of useful life.
