> For Christmas I got my roommate a Belkin  USB WiFi reciever so he can
> share my DSL connection via Airport.  But I can't seem to open the ABS
> to configure it.  I get errors that say The AirPort Admin Utility was
> unable to read the configuration of the selected base station.  Any

I believe you mean that:

Your computer is a Ti with an Airport card in it.
You have an Apple Airport Base Station (ABS) connected to DSL.
Your roomate has a Belkin WiFi card in his computer.

If so, this has nothing to do with the Belkin card.  I believe I have seen
the "unable to read" problem if either the Airport Admin Utility in your
computer, or the software in the ABS are down-rev.  (Understand: These are
two different softwares.)  You can download the latest softwares from Apple
and fix them.
