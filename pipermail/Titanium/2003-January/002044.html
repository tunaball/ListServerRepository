>When hooked to a TV or external monitor to watch DVD's, I've 
>"fooled" the LCD into shutting off by closing the lid and putting 
>the machine to sleep, then unlatching the lid and raising it 
>slightly to wake it up. After the HD spins up, but before the LCD 
>comes on, shut the lid again and latch it.  Make sure you have a USB 
>mouse hooked up to click on "Play Movie" on the TV/monitor screen 
>and sit back and enjoy.  If I have both a keyboard and a USB mouse 
>hooked up, along with an external display, I can wake the machine up 
>without raising the lid at all and the LCD stays dark while I work 
>with the external display.
>
>This is with a G4/400, so your mileage may vary with other models.
