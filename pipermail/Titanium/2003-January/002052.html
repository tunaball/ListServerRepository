(As previously posted on this list) I have a 3Com wireless gateway in 
the offices of our firm, connected by ethernet cable to the DSL 
modem.  This gives all of our offices wireless access to the

have an airport card in my Ti (makes this an ON-list post!)
The guy sitting near to the modem/3Com wireless gateway, who has a PC 
laptop, could in principle connect his PC by ethernet in to the modem.
