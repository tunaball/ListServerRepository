Malcolm - my vote still remains with Eudora: for speed, simplicity, 
restorability, ease of use.

I clearly admit that the interface is very old-fashioned, and almost 
seems anachronistic amongst all my other X programs, but I still see 
no advantage for me in any other programs I have looked at, so far.
I try to maintain an open mind.

I have looked at or used - over the last 14 years with email - Profs, 
All-in-1 [DEC], HPDesk, ClarisMail, Entourage, Outlook, Mailsmith, 
Lotus Notes (by FAR the worst), Mail, Netscape......
but I have been solidly with Eudora for almost six years.

I suppose that I would like to move to Mail, if I had to say 
anything, but when I have tried the move a few times, I quickly went 
back to Eudora.  Maybe I should persist more.
