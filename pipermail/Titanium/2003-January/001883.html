There is an option in both Image Capture & iPhoto to delete photos 
after downloading them.  If that is enabled, all the photos would have 
been deleted on the card after being transferred to the Mac.  Since the 
card mounts as a removable disk on your Mac, you can easily copy the 
photos back to the card if needed.

I would suggest that he put the card back in the camera & confirm that 
it is still formatted to the camera & to also check to see if the 
images are there.  If they are, the problem is on his PC's end.
