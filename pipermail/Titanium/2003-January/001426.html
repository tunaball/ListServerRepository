It's made of pretty thick plastic. Feels very sturdy and solid.
You can move the stand around your desktop very easily. It's interesting how
it seems to 'slip' around.

Depending on how stiff your screen hinge is - I would recommend holding onto
the base when opening/closing the lcd. No other considerations that I can
think of. I think it is VERY hard to flip. The book, although advertised as
'light' still weighs 5.5 lbs.. More than enough to keep it all steady.

Drop into a store to check one out if you can.. I waffled on my decision for
so long, if only I had seen this in the first place.
