>Last I knew Sears sold Apple, has this changed?

Sears sold iMacs and iBooks I believe.  Circuit City and Best Buy 
used to sell Macs, but Apple sent secret shoppers into all their 
retail resellers and found that the sales reps in those stores 
actually spent considerable energy talking folks interested in Macs 
into buying Wintel.  Apple felt that it was better to remove the 
opportunity to negatively market their products and voided reseller 
agreements with those outlets.
