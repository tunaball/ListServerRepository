I agree. My reference to Microsoft threats was in the mid-90s (as 
Shawn pointed out, there was never an official announcement, but they 
did publicly state they were "considering" dropping Mac versions).

that considering they are also developing IE, which is a giveaway. In 
fact, pointing out the success of M$ MBU would be good evidence to 
put in front of companies like DeLorme, who think there isn't a 
business model in developing Mac software.
