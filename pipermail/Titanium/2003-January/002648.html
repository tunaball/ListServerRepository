Jim Soriano said:

"Somebody (Trevor?) was having problems with HP printer drivers in OS 
10.1.x and 10.2.x.  I just ran across this blurb from Mac911 on it 
being fixed.  I can also comment that I think it's fixed on my 
TiPBG4-800 running 10.2.3, as I've been printing up a storm the last 
week on my OfficeJet d145 with no apparent memory leaks or spinning HP 
Communicator tasks.

"So, the solution may indeed be to upgrade to 10.2.3 and HP's 4.6.3 
release."

I bought an HP DeskJet 350 CBi last June, and have had constant problems with its failure to communicate with my Ti 800 (running OSX 10.1.5), and was told by HP that their drivers for the portable printers are less stable than for their other printers.  However, they have a new patch -- "hp inkjet 2.0 patch" -- which I just installed earlier this week, and now it works perfectly.

Thus, they must be getting their act together with respect to OSX printer drivers and patches.
