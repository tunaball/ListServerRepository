<james at ameeti.net> wrote:

> Is there a way to close the lid of the Ti without the cpu going to sleep.
> I do not want to have to have the computer plugged into an external
> monitor.

I think there is a magnetic switch in the latch. If you close it far 
enough, it detect the presence of the lid and switch to sleep. The latch 
doesn't have to catch for it to sleep. It's built into the hardware so 
there is no software override, I believe. You can close it and put a rubber 
eraser or other spacer to prevent it closing all the way, but that's 
probably the best one can do.
