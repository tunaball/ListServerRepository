Am Dienstag, 07.01.03 um 12:23 Uhr schrieb David DelMonte:

> 9am PST = 12 noon here in Washington DC, which should mean 7pm CET. 
> There is a link to watch the broadcast of the keynote on Apple's main 
> webpage.

I doubt this:

	PST = UTC - 8
	CET = UTC + 1

Hence

	CET = PST + 8 + 1 = PST + 9

You can also try the time zone tab in System Preferences -> Date & 
Time. It shows right now 12:56 CET and if I put it to Los Angeles it 
shows 3:56 PST which is a difference of nine hours.
