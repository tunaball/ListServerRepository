Well, I took the plunge and updated to 10.4.1.  iScroll2 works great  
on my Rev. A Lunchtraydeluxe (17-inch PBG4).  For those of you  
interested in system compatibility, this is cut from the MacUpdate  
description:

> iScroll2 is a modified trackpad driver that adds two-finger  
> scrolling capabilities to supported pre-2005 PowerBooks and iBooks  
> on OS X 10.3. Supported models include aluminum PowerBooks  
> introduced from 2003 to 2004 as well as all G4 iBooks.

It's especially nice since I figured out how to two-finger scroll  
properly after reading the MacOSXHint about it.  I was so used to fn- 
trackpad with uControl that I was using both hands to do the  
scrolling with iScroll2, resulting in very rough and unpredictable  
scrolling behavior (holding finger down from left hand and sliding up/ 
down with finger from right hand).
