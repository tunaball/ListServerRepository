It can be done for the iBook, so I would say yes. The problem is these 
are horribly expensive, the labor plus materials to replace a 12" iBook 
LCD runs around $700 if the screen is new. There are usually a bunch 
available at eBay. As for doing it at home, it should be easier than 
with an iBook because of the way the casing is designed. You may want 
to google for it because there are pages explaining how to tear apart 
the laptop and how to replace most of its parts.
