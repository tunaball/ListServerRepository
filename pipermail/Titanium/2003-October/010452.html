On Thursday, October 2, 2003, at 09:04  AM, Ron Cooper wrote:

> What is the best way to come home from the road and back up changes in 
> 12" to the G5? I have downloaded Retrospect trial. It looks like I 
> need to use that to back up the 12" and then need an external HD to 
> back up the G5. Any recommendations? I remember someone in the group 
> mentioning "Disk Images" once upon a time?

I've had good luck with Carbon Copy Cloner backing up from my Ti to a 
firewire drive partition.  I use 'synchronize' which only moves over 
the the changes, so it generally runs pretty quickly, even though it's 
backing up about 30GB of data.  You'd just need a dedicated backup 
partition on the G5, that's all.
