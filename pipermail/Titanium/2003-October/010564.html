>  Apples AP Extreme...which I assume would go inside just like the 
> current AP card that  is in there now.
>
> For the G4 which will act as the software router, is it better also to 
> go with a 3rd party card or must I outfit that with AP Extreme since 
> it fits INSIDE the computer?


Airport extreme cards do not fit inside Titanium powerbooks. In order 
to get 802.11g service for your Ti, you have to get a 3rd party card. 
The airport extreme card is designed only to fit in late-model 
powerbooks, imacs and powermacs.
