> Hey gang
  I just got a new bag for my great Tibook.  It was from Target. One of 
my frustrations about bags and back packs is that they are not stiff 
enough to stand up on their own unless you pay over a 100 bucks yikes. 
So I was shopping at Target and see they had a sale on memory cards, So 
I go down the computer aisle and there is this weird looking laptop bag 
designed by Micheal Graves. It's thin  stiff and stands up by itself 
and has an inner sleeve for more protection. The sleeve is a little big 
for the tibook (might even fit 17 incher) but I have been carrying my 
laptop in it for a week and I really like it. It has a side pocket that 
holds my ipod, phone and few disks and a little bag on the inside next 
to the laptop sleeve that holds my cord and mouse.

I love it, I  cant remember if it was 29 or 39 bucks but still a good 
bargain. The best part is that the laptop slides in the side of the bag 
not the top and just works. Some of Micheal Graves designs are silly 
but I love my new bag. I'm not sure I would travel with it because I 
need a back pack for that but to and from work its great . Please go 
look and let me know off list what you think.
Thanks TmB
