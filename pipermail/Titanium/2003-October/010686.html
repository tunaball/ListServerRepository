Is it an outstanding deal? I don't know about outstanding, but in my
opinion, it's at least a pretty good deal.  Personally, I enjoy it - a lot.
I use the email service all the time, have web pages up online, and use the
idisk and isync features regularly. But one of the biggest selling points
for me (which I didn't expect at the time of signup) has turned out to be
the free software and other incentives that are offered to members on a
fairly regular basis. One of my favourites was the recent offering of Sticky
Brain, which I enjoy using a lot. Bottom line - If you take advantage of the
services and freebies, the $99 price tag is easily justified.
