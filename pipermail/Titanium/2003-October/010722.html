Haven't tried any SACD/CD hybrids as my current collection is SACD 
only.  (Oh, and the hi-res is not (24bit/96k) it's a 1 bit DSD 
process that's supposed to be much better than the old CD standard-or 
at least those who though that 'perfect CD sound' was an oxymoron.)
As for DVD-Audio (along with SACD only), the RIAA is so afraid of 
'perfect digital copies' they have not agreed on a digital format 
(output, like the currrent S/PDIF for CDs) for DVD-Audio or SACD 
(which as usual are incompatible formats) so there is no way your 
internal DVD-VIDEO(ROM) drive could play the disk.  You'll need a 
DVD-Audio-capable player (Denon makes the least expensive ones) to 
listen to the DVD-Audio track.
