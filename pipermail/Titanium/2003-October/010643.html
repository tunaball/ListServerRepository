Expose
Mail.app
Preview

Overall it is awesome, but these are the three features that will sell me into upgrading 
immediately. I got to play with the WDC beta and within an hour I was totally hooked on Expose. 
Mail.app is my client of choice and Preview is critical at my office because everything we do 
eventually becomes a PDF, so we are looking at PDFs constantly.

As for the new fax functionality, it is very nice that it is now available system-wide but it is not 
life or death for us.
