I have one of the early 400mhz Tibook and have similar problem 
occassionaly. But I only have a printer conected to the USB and am 
still trying to figure out why it happens when it happens. At the 
moment I'm leaning towards a teori that the Tibook is in a sleeping 
stage (but with lid open) when I disconnect the power and then the 
USB-cable which by the way doesn't go directly to the printer but to a 
hub which in turn is conected to the printer.

I feel that if I take the USB first and then the power the situation is 
OK, but doing it in the opposite order causes the Ti not to wake up 
normally but require a forced restart. Most of the time the screen is 
totally black like Dennis describes but sometimes the screen displays a 
kernel panic.
