This is one of the things I use QuicKeys for. I know there are less-
expensive freeware and shareware with some of the same features, but when
I first got my TiBook, it was irritatingly time-consuming to hunt around
for the many different pieces of utilities that would provide the
features I enjoyed on my pre-X Mac. Even the X version of QuicKeys
doesn't have all the features it once had, but I still prefer it over a
bunch of stuff by different folks I have to remember to upgrade.

BTW, don't forget that an extra dock (or QuicKeys toolbar) with
frequently used folders is helpful. I also have to have toolbars for OS X
games and OS 9 games.
