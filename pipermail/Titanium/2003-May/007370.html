International travel is why I stay with Earthlink. I've had almost no 
trouble connecting via local phone calls in Holland, England, France, 
Italy, and Spain, whether using my Mac or my Visor. There is a 
surcharge, but it's minor.

But it depends on where you're going. In Holland, for example, public 
libraries in even quite small towns have public web-connected 
computers, which will let you get webmail. That's even cheaper, and you 
don't have to haul your computer around.

I'm looking forward to the next trip, because now I have AirPort, and 
I'll be looking for hotspots.
