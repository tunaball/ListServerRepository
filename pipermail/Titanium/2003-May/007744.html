>On Thursday, May 29, 2003, at 07:23  AM, xx wrote:
>> Where do you save the settings to?
>
>You want to open (with TextEdit or BBEdit) the file:
>/Users/<yourname>/Library/Preferences/com.apple.bezelservice.plist
>Near the bottom you'll see:
>	<key>kHWMax</key>
>	<real>1</real>
>	<key>kHWMin</key>
>	<real>(a number less than 1)</real>
>
>set the last variable to 1 (as opposed to whatever number is there 
>now), save the file, and it will always go to max brightness.
>
