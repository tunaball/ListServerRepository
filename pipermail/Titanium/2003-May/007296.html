Hey Folks,

I'm using the Lunchtray in the dark next to my sleeping wife.  The 
backlighting on the keys is real nice, but I find that the screen is 
so bright I can use the keyboard without the backlighting.  Even with 
the screen on the lowest brightness setting.  Definitely a no-can-do 
with the old Ti/500.  For those wanting an airport comparison, I was 
never able to maintain a connection in the bedroom with the airport 
down the hall with my old Ti/500.  Would go back and forth between 
0-2 bars on airport, constantly dropping me.  Now I get a full 4 bars 
(sometimes drops to 3 - probably due to slight interference from the 
baby monitor) in the bedroom.

This is a truly amazing machine.  To satisfy the naysayers, I'll 
start referring to it as "Lunchtray Deluxe".
