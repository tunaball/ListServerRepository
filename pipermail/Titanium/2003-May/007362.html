 From what I have read not all dongles are the same in performance, with 
some offering better range of signal and also some not supporting the 
"proximity sensing" feature of BT.

I have an Innovision Multimedia "Wavelinker" dongle which i paid around 
GBP 50 for 6 months ago.

Works fine, no drivers required, good range and proximity sensing.

To be honest no BT dongle you buy should need any drivers if it is USB 
Mac OSX should support it out of the box. Mine came with a CD but 
that's for PC users.
