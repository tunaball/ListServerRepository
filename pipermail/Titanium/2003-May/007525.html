At 6:50 am -0500 22/5/03, Loren Schooley wrote:
>How would I connect a VGA Ti to a Cinema? I found a FemaleDVI==>MaleVGA on
>Dr.Bott, but I need the opposite, MaleDVI==>FemaleDVI. Is connecting a VGA
>Ti up to the CD even possible?

You need two adapters - the VGA-DVI adapter and the DVI-ADC adapter. I
think Dr Bott does both. Also check out the Apple store.

But depending upon the amount of video RAM your TiBook has, the very best
solution might be at http://www.vtbookdd.com/ -- although they keep moving
the launch date goalposts.
