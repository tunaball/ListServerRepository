Or do what I did; I copied all the files I thought I'd need from my old 
computer to my new one, and then once I was done, I ran Disk Copy to 
image the old hard drive, saving it on my new one.  This way I have 
access to ANY file from my previous computer, and there are no worries 
about me losing any data...  Unless of course, my hard drive goes 
south, which is why I'll be using my DVD burner fairly soon to do a 
complete backup of everything.  ;-)
