Yesterday I bought the Nokia 6310i, together with an Acer Bluetooth 
(USB) adaptor, for use with my Ti-500 running 10.2.6.

The Bluetooth adaptor is sold for the PC world, and comes with a CD 
of drivers to install and many instructions.  To be sure it would be 
OK, I put my Ti-500 on the sales counter, inserted the adaptor; the 
OS (10.2.6) detected the Bluetooth device and added it to my Network 
options; then detected the phone of a girl assistant in the shop....

On the way home, I "paired" the Ti with the Nokia.

It now appears in the Bluetooth Preferences like this:

	Device Name: 	Trevor's Nokia
	Device Address: 	00-60-57-3c-c7-2a
	Device Type: 	Phone
	Device Services: 	Fax, OBEX Object Push, Audio Gateway,
			COM 1, Voice Gateway, Dial-up networking

	Paired: 		Yes
	Favorite: 		Yes
	Connected:	No

I then had to be sure I had all the Network Preferences correct, but 
NOW I am connected via Bluetooth to my ISP as I send this note..... 
Great technology !

Trevor
