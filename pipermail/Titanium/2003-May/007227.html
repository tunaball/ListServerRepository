Depending on how old the old CD player is, it might not work at all 
with CD-Rs.  CD-R discs are not as reflective as commercially pressed 
CDs, and so the CD player has to expect a weaker signal when reading a 
CD-R.  Some of the oldest CD players consider this weak signal to be a 
sign of no CD, and hence they can't read it.
