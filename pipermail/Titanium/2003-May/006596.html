The SoundSticks come with a sub ...  The iSub was sold separately to be 
used with your speakers.  It is my understanding that the iSub could 
not be hooked up at the same time as the SoundSticks.

If you are asking how we like the way the soundsticks sound w/ the 
included sub... I have used them for over two years with a Cube, Ti500, 
Ti667, G4 867, and now Big Al ...  I have not found anything better for 
a Mac ... Although I am sure others will supply us with their opinions.
