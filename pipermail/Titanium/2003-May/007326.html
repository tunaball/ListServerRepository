Don't buy the I/O Gear adapter if you plan to use a mouse with your Ti at
the same time. The shape of the adapter takes up too much space and won't
let you plug in a second usb device. If you h
ave an external keyboard with usb ports or will just use the adapter with
the Ti "en route", then go for it.
