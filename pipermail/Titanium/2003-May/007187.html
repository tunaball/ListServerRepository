Personally, I'd expect iTunes to be using more, and other applications 
to be using less; but the total usage will still be at about 100%.  I'd 
expect iTunes at more like 70%.  It might be worth checking what your 
total CPU usage is when iTunes isn't running and nothing else is busy 
working; It should be under 10%, mine is usually under 3%.  The more 
doohickies and widgets and doodads you have installed, the higher this 
will go.  It will also go higher if your mail is checking or your 
computer is performing some other background task.
