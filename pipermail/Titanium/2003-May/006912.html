There is a little trick to improve (in many cases) wireless range. Do 
it at your risk although I did it myself a couple of times and all went 
very well (increased range). Now with the facts and trick explanation.

On the TiBook one can access the antenna location removing the battery. 
Get the TiBook upside down. Remove the battery. You will see a 
*sort-of* flat band (or tape) running along the case on the right side 
of the battery compartment. In most (if not all) cases where the range 
on a TiBook is poor this flat band is not properly flat and actually 
slightly detached from the case. The trick is to *massage* the band 
with your thumb against the case (from top to bottom flattening down 
the band against the Ti case). Do this a couple of time: almost 
guaranteed your range will increase.
