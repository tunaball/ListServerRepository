There are a number of tools in OSX that may help you troubleshoot. As 
I said, watching the Console app while you recreate the crash; also 
the app called Process Viewer will show you how much of the CPU and 
memory each process is taking up.  This may reveal something; that 
reminds me... how much free hard disk space do you have on your OS 
drive/partition. If you do not have enough free space, the virtual 
memory could cause problems.

Also, have you tried going back to your original settings after your 
hacks? It is *possible* that the hacks forced a replacement of some 
corrupt preference.
