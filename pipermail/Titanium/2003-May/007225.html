In addition you can try different brands of CD-R and CD-RW media.

Just as a note a general rule is the CD-RW media works better in DVD players
than CD-R, for people who burn VCD or play music CDs in the DVD player.

The older the CD player the more picky it may be.

Good news is that CD and DVD players in computers are usually a lot more
lenient.
