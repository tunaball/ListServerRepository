> By the way, why did you write 'enter' (between quotes). Does Apple name it
> differently?

I put the quotes around it just to signify that it stands on its own as an
individual key. Many people mentally mix 'enter' with 'return'. Not only is
there an 'enter' key on Ti's but the 'return' key to the right end of the
keyboard - shares responsibilities with 'enter'. On a 'normal' extended
keyboard the enter key sits alone with the numerical keypad and does not
always perform the same way. Depending on what program you are using, one
has to be careful about what key is being suggested. Not that anything is
going to break, but just another one of those confusions.

Sorry, I tend to abuse quotes.. But I often use single ( '  ' ) quotes for
highlighting and double ( "  " ) for quoting. I know some grammarian on the
list is about to smack me..  8-)
