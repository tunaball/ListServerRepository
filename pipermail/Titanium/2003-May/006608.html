Well I never thought I would say this but I have concluded that FAXstf 
works better than GlobalFax internal for the Ti, the iMac or the 
Wallstreet.

Last night I put my Ti to sleep seeing as all important stuff had stopped 
coming in.....and this morning it was awake and on the screen in the FAX 
inbox was a JUNK FAX!!! 

Not that I like getting junk faxes, but it was bound to happen.
Point is, the laptop ,which is temporarily taking in faxes for me, was 
able to wake up when a ring was detected. This I could not get to work 
with globalfax. 
So I guess I can say that FAXstf 6.0.6 for OS 9.1 works fairly flawlessly 
on my Ti.
