I've been having similar problems with a PC Card CompactFlash reader on 
my 500Mhz TiBook with 10.2.6.  When the PC Card is inserted, the mouse 
becomes a little sluggish and everything is locked up.  If I eject it 
forcibly I get a kernel panic.

But, if I shutdown the PowerBook, insert the PC Card then boot, it 
works fine and iPhoto launches and can import the pictures and 
everything.  It is only the "live insert" that fails.

It worked fine several releases ago, but I don't know for sure when it 
started misbehaving.  One time after the crash lots of applications 
were pretty severely messed up and I had random kernel panics on 
restart so I ended up having to reinstall the OS.
