I don't think so. More likely a case of the app expecting a certain 
file at a certain allocation block [or blocks] and finding something 
different. Usual culprit: Bad Directory [because the directory, when 
accurate will show the correct filename for each block]. DiskWarrior 
needed. Regularly. Too many apps write incorrectly, at times to 
'no-write parts of the directory, and also, defragmenting helps. you 
don't want the Directory scattered all over... factor in a bit of 
misinformation and you have slowdowns, and/or application 'hangs'.
