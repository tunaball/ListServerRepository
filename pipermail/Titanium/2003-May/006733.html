On Sunday, May 4, 2003, at 01:10  PM, Tim D. Middleton wrote:

> I am the proud new owner of my first PowerBook. I have noticed that my 
> 17" comes with a PCI Card slot. Since I have never owned a pro Apple 
> laptop this is all new to me. Can anyone help me out with what I can 
> use that slot for?

Various expansion devices. I have a Compact Flash adapter, for 
transferring digital photos without draining my camera's batteries. I 
also have a Sprint PCS Vision PC card for making internet connections 
away from WiFi, ethernet, or landline phone jack. Finally, I have a 
high end PC sound card (Echo Indigo).
