>These days, in an effort to be compatible with the PC crowd, Apple tends to label the option key 'alt', and also add the brick going down the escalator icon.

You may be happy to know that this key is predominantly labeled 'option' along with a smaller 'alt' on Big Al. No icon in site.

>The Apple key has always had the Apple and cloverleaf logos.

Only old timers that go back to the Apple II, or newcomers who weren't there when the Mac started call this the Apple key. To original Mac users, back in the earliest System days (can't remember where it actually started numbering cuz they skipped a number or two), the cloverleaf symbol has and always will be called the Command key.
