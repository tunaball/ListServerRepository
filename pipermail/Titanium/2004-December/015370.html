At 15:54 -0600 16/12/04, Jerry Buc wrote:
>Is there a device for a TiBook/400 which will allow cable TV viewing 
>on the Book?

Jerry - about 1 year ago, I bought the USB eyeTV from Elgato.com.  It 
is really great for my needs.

I use it with my Al-book.  I have the local cable (RF connection) and 
my satellite decoder (RCA connectors) connected permanently to the 
eyeTV.  One key-stroke enables you to switch between the two.  I also 
use eyeTV to convert video tapes to digital files, and for storing 
content from my JVC DV camcorder.  It is a great device.  You can 
also use it to record (like TiVo) programs at times (night-time for 
me) for later viewing.  It also integrates with program schedule 
spublished on the internet (at least in the USA< as I understand it).
There is editing software included that does a great job (eg tidying 
up the ends of recorded stuff) and also saving it in VCD, QT or DVD 
format.
