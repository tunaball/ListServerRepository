You can buy brand-spankin' new Ti hinge pairs for US$90 from eBay vendor 
ibiricu. All TiBooks use the same hinge parts.

They're not difficult to replace, the two main issues are opening the 
display without destroying it, and gluing in the new hinges. See my page 
for some (hopefully) helpful and relevant infos:
<http://mywebpages.comcast.net/macdan/tibook_display.html>

I still don't have every detail up, but it should at least give enough 
basic info. Frankly, if you can't figure out any missing details, you 
probably shouldn't do it yourself anyhow.

Oh BTW, eBay vendor bobsbid1 has a number of semi-tested 
complete-with-hinges displays for BIN US$149, sold as-is however. Garbled 
description, but probably 1152x res for pre-DVI 'Books though they should 
work in DVI 'Books too.
