This is in Knowledge Base article 60446.

One thing that is also very important is to clean the screen FREQENTLY even 
if the marks are not objectionable. As mentioned here recently, in addition 
to oils, your fingertips may contain traces of acids or other chemicals 
that will permanently etch and mar the surface if given time to work.

Close examination with a magnifier used to examine PC boards shows this in 
many places on my screen.

Don't forget to clean the keycaps too while you're at it.
