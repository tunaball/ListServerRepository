>I'm looking for a sub-$500 (but the cheaper the better) laser 
>printer for use at home with my tibook.  The smaller the 
>size/footprint, the better.  Does anyone have any suggestions?
>

There are plenty of LaserWriters on eBay at bargain prices. I picked 
up a LW 630 Pro (16 ppm, PostScript, Ethernet, parallel, SCSI, etc) 
with a low page count (20k or so) for a couple of hundred dollars. 
They're not small, but the quality is good, the reliability is good, 
and best of all the compatibility is excellent. They usually last for 
150k pages or more, so unless you are a very heavy user then they 
should last almost indefinitely.
