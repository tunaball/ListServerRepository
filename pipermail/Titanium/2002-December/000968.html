>Odd thing, after my battery reaches 100% full charge, it then appears  
>to seep juice (the value drops a few percentage points) but the plug  
>icon still indicates it's connected to the power supply.   I've never  
>had a battery drain while plugged in before - anyone else experience  
>this?

Steve,

I believe you are experiencing the normal behaviour of the charging circuitry that has been used on PowerBooks for a number of years now. For reference, you may want to refer to Apple's Knowledge Base Document 8344, "PowerBook G4: Battery Does Not Show Full Charge in Mac OS X":
>The batteries [snip] are designed to avoid short discharge/charge
>cycles in order to prolong the overall life of the battery. Because
>of this, [snip] you may notice that the reported charge stays between
>95 percent and 99 percent. When the battery level eventually drops
>below 95 percent, it will charge all the way to 100 percent.
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=88344>

The document refers to OS X, but the same behaviour should also be seen in earlier versions of the Mac OS.

Hope this helps,
 alan
