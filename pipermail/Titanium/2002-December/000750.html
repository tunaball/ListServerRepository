The ability to run DOS on a brand new wintel PC is one of the things 
that holds the platform back.  Every processor, motherboard, etc etc 
has to be able to pretend its running on a glorified traffic light 
controller (Intel 8086) with a max of 640k of RAM.  A previous poster 
was correct, Apple is still a "niche player" in some respects.  If they 
want to continue to evolve and improve they have to be able to cut the 
dead wight.  Don't get me wrong, i was really angered by the 
performance of OS X on my G3 AIO (one of the primary reasons i got my 
867 Ti).  Is it a perfect situation? no.  Will i accept Apple making 
some difficult decisions to continue to grow the best computing 
platform on the planet? you bet.
