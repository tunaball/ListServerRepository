I have had one of the original TiBook since they were first introduced. I
would say that the screen marks on the screen ARE due to finger grease
transferred from the keyboard. However, if left, the grease will etch the
soft glass used on the screen leaving permanent marks - in the worst case
pits on the screen surface. If you see marks, clean them off immediately -
but don't use solvents
