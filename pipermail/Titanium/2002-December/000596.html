You can probably make your own if you have a chunk of appropriate 
material lying around, but if it's worth $15 to you, OWC has a good 
product at Macsales.com.  Do a search for Laptop Screen Protector.

adhesive dots (from your local hardware store) on the two front 
corners of the lid.
