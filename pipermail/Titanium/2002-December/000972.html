This is by design.  Starting with I believe the 550/667 models (maybe 
one rev later; the 400/500 don't do this) the power management is 
altered somewhat to help lengthen the life span of the battery.  Your 
battery will drop to about 95% before it will begin charging again; 
this is to prevent extremely short battery charge cycles to gain one or 
two percent, which aren't good for the battery.
