I did use GSM with my T68i. I selected the Ericsson T39 14.4 modem script (OS X). This worked with Cingular (in the San Jose area), but not with ATT Wireless here.

The method that worked with Cingular was the same as for any modem connection. I had major problems using IR with my T68i. I think I got the same message that you report. But it worked with bluetooth.

ATT Wireless told me that they don't support GSM dialing, only GPRS. Too bad, since they have a plan for unlimited voice minutes. After buying that plan for unlimited connection minutes, I felt a bit 'tricked'.
