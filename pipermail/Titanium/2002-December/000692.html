I paid $0.50 for a sheet of 4 eyeglass silicon pads. They are flesh colored
and are supposed to be stuck on the nose pieces of a pair of glasses. I just
stuck the four on each corner of my powerbook. It works just fine and I have
no problems with marks on the screen. They are not high enough that they
interfere with the latch.

You can get them in almost all drugstores.
