Marcus,

I just plugged the ethernet cable from the cable modem into the 
ethernet port of the G4 Tower, and used the Airport Admin Utility in my 
TiBook (550) to set up the wireless connection.  Everything sort of 
configured itself.  The software assigned the proper numbers, and 
everything (HTML, POP, STMP, etc) came through with no problems at all. 
  Back when I had a Windows machine the cable company was very helpful 
in telling me all the DNS numbers and Subnet Filters to enter.  WIth OS 
X, it was automatic.

So, for want of better help, maybe a call to the cable provider would 
do it?
