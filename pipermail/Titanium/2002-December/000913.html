I have been using a Startac with Alltel as my cellular co. for six 
months and it works great (14,400 connection).

Check to make sure your data service is enacted by your cellular provider.

Purchased cable from The supply net http://www.thesupplynet.com/ 
Customer Service 1-800-826-0279 . Cost around $50.00 and comes with a 
cd to install modem script. Be sure they know it is mac as they sent 
me a pc script the first time.

Just be sure to:

1 program your cellphone and remote acess configurations with the dialup no.

2. first plug in the usb connection

3. select the correct modem

4. highlight "ignore dialtone"
