I'm having no luck getting my wife's G4Ti/400 to recognize a new airport

date and I followed the card install procedure with the care of a heart

"unexpected network error (-3278) while communicating with airport driver.

I also ran disk first aid and techtool with no luck.  I then decided to
check the card in my ibook.  Swapped cards and fired up the ibook...instant
access...the card is functional.

I've installed 3 cards in G4Ti's and ibooks before and never encountered
