Glenn - I believe that this issue has been addressed before on this 
list, and from memory I think that the answer is yes on both 
accounts.  yes, they fit, and yes they will provide a longer battery 
life than the original one in an older Ti.

Battery capacity:	Rev. A	50   mAh	(I have this)
		Rev B	55.3 mAh
		1 GHz	61    mAh

I get the impression that the battery on my "original" Ti-500 is 
getting to the end of its life (or it may be that OS X is more 
demanding, or a combinationm of both) and I am thinking about getting 
a new higher-capacity battery.  Maybe in the new year ......
