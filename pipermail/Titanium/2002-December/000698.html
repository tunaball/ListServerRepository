Personally I think leather looks terrible when it gets marked so I would
spend money on Madarina Duck briefcases which are made from a balistic nylon
and considerably more stylish. It's like those bags you see a Mont Blanc
which the platinum fittings but as soon as they get a nick they look
terrible. Same as Bally designs too.

If you want a snug fit you're probably looking for a neoprene but I doubt
you can find one with a color to match if you're looking for brown that is.

Personally grey and brown doesn't match IMHO.
