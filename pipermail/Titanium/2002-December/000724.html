> The keyboard of my Ti bulges up from about 3 to 9, then in a V-shape down to C
> to N. This makes the the central block of keys spongy as the key board below
> them flexes. Anyone else seen this?

My 500 MHz TiBook does the same thing. When I got it almost two years ago I
bent small bits of paperclip into rings and placed them on two of the
magnets under the keyboard. A strange fix, but it made it a bit tighter and
less spongy feeling. It's still annoying from time to time.
