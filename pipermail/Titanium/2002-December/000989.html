I'd agree that the ABS is over-priced - especially if you plan on using 
it simply as a WAP.  'Course, the fact that it has a built-in modem in 
it and can connect to your ISP for you has some value - none to me, but 
probably to some.

But I doubt it "lacks in performance" - especially with respect to the 
limited Airport range on the TiBooks.  The fault there lies with the 
TiBook, not the ABS.


I personally use the Linksys WAP11 and I believe it gives far more bang 
for the buck than the ABS - especially at current prices.
