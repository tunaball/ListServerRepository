First thing to do when one of these 'Books won't start is to disconnect 
the PRAM battery and then try to startup.

Failed or failing PRAM batteries give this exact symptom. I hate to say 
how many simlar era 'Books I've been able to acquire for a song and was 
able to revive just by unplugging the PRAM batt.

To disconnect the PRAM batt on your 'Book, you'll have to remove the 
bottom plate and then lift the optical drive up to gain access.
