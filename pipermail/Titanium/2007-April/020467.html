The one way is to have the 'book' closed/asleep, and then plug in the 
monitor, and a USB device (keyboard, mouse, etc.), and it will then 
default to the external monitor, then you can open the 'Book to let 
it cool.  I've got a switcher that I use with my Dual G4, and I use 
both screens.  But, you can experiment to see if you don't need it 
closed, or just asleep to trigger the 'external' monitor thing.
