Yes, I think you might be missing the little triangle to the right of  
_Save as *imagename.jpg*_ If you click on that triangle marked button  
you will get the dialog box so that you can choose which folder you  
want to save to.

When you done that once, I think you may even find the choosen folder  
in the Drop-down list under the _Where: (Desktop default)_. Recent  
folders appear at the bottom.

> *Also, when I save a picture this way, sometimes it saves to the  
> desktop as Spaceball.gif, and Preview opens it as a BLANK window;  
> why is this?
------------
That one I don't have a good answer to, but I guess it has something  
to do with that Gif images often have a transparent background  
"layer" so that the backgroung colour of the page should shine  
through and that you haven't really saved the image but rather this  
transparent "layer".

Please also note that images on the web aren't all free to download  
and reuse. You should ask the owner  of the website and in that case  
they will probably help you with all parts of the Gif image too. :-)
