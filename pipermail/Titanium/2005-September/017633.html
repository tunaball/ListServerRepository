Leif Hall asks:
> The left hinge on my powerbook is broken. I noticed a few weeks ago 
> that the hinge was becoming more and more difficult to open until one 
> day it just snapped. The display and computer still work fine, but I'm 
> just not comfortable using it as a laptop anymore. Does anyone have 
> any ideas on where I can get problem repaired for a reasonable price?
You can replace just the hinge, but it requires peeling apart the 
glued-together display assembly.

My webpage on the topic:
<http://mywebpages.comcast.net/macdan/tibook_display.html>

A vendor on ebay "ibiricu" has new hinges, or search eBay for used 
hinges. Note that hinges are either left or right, you cannot swap 
side-to-side. Figure $65 each new (IIRC $125 a pair), or ~$50 used, new 
is the better choice IMO.
