I have upgraded several Ti books in regards to hard drives,  combo and
Superdrives without any issues.  Got to:

http://www.pbparts.com

and look up your particular model and download the illustrated instructions.
If you are so inclined, and it is fairly easy actually, you should not have
any problems.

Recently I upgraded a friend's Ti 550 by replacing his hard drives and then
installing my original superdrive from my Ti 1 GHz to replace his combo
drive. Worked great.  I had upgraded my superdrive to the more recent 845
model with 8 times DVD burning and Dual Layer in my Ti 1 GHz.


Good luck.
