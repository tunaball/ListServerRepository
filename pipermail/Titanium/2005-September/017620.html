Two ways, I don't think has been mentioned yet (IIRC):

1) Start dragging whatever you want from Safari. Hit F11 for Exposé  
(or move to your hot-corner or whatever). Place the item on the  
desktop. hit F11 again and you are back while still having full  
access to all your applications. (or use F9 if you want all  
applications to chose from instead.)
2) in Tiger, right-click or click and wait (set the delay in the  
mouse settings in the System Preferences)- chose add to iPhoto  
Library. I have found this to be very effective, so I wanted to  
mention it.
