Whatever you decide to use, two things to remember are:

Don't spray directly onto screen and let it run into edges. Spray onto 
cloth then wipe. (DON'T SCRUB)

Use a product that is residue free or rinse very well. I've used window 
cleaner at times.

I think I'll take Lisbeth's advice next time & use plain old hot water.
