Here comes the third kind :-):

You only need fingers that can withstand a very hot damp lintfree  
cloth. Make the cloth hot in clean hot water and squeeze it so that  
no drops can run into the sides of the screen. Wipe and repeat as  
necessary. Finally wipe really dry with a dry lintfree cloth.

Works perfectly and costs absolutely nothing. :-)
