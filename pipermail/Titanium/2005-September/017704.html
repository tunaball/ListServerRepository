Another old wive's tale.  Microsoft did not "bail" Apple out of  
anything.  They signed an agreement to provide Office productivity  
software for the Macintosh platform in addition to purchasing $150  
million in stock, and Apple agreeing to make Microsoft Internet

dispute over Microsoft's alleged copyright infringement of Apple's  
operating system.

The agreement lasted for five years and expired in 2003.
