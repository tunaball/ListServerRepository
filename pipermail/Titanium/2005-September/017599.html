There is an audio in jack next to the modem on the 1Ghz Ti books.
In the earlier versions of the Ti, there were no audio in jacks at all.

But in later versions...I think with the 667 DVI and the 800 and then the
867 and 1 GHz Ti books, Apple added an audio-in port.

Look for it next to the modem port.
