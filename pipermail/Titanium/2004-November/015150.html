Yes, you can use a bluetooth headset, up to a point.  I use a Sony 
Ericsson HBH-35 headset with an AlBook.  As you probably know, it won't 
work with voice recognition (quality bandwidth limits, I think).  
However, you can use it for iChat, and presumably things like 
Vonage/Skype, and such.

You will find the sound quality to be adequate for telephony, but not 
much beyond that.  Using it to listen to music or watch a DVD is not a 
pleasant experience, in my opinion.
