Hello,

I am currently using the Edimax EW-7108PCg wireless card in my 1GHz  
TiBook and it works great. The only negatives are that it sticks about  
1" out of the PCMCIA slot and that it uses its own control software  
instead of airport. The range with 802.11g is excellent.
