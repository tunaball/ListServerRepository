I don't have an URL for this (and it may be a semi-internal Apple 
document, though it's not marked so; I have access to a lot of extra 
stuff since I work for an Apple Authorized Service Provider), part of 
the AppleCare Service News bulletin detailing this says:

"Current APP Customers
With the introduction of APP 3.0, all existing APP 2.0 customers will 
be eligible for the new features of APP 3.0 for the remainder of their 
three-year terms."

So yes, PowerBook owners get global coverage and coverage of a monitor 
(if purchased at the same time, though the monitor isn't covered 
globally), and desktop owners get onsite service.
