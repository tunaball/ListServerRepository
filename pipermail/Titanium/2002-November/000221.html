Well, the 800MHz Tibook I had, and the one my wife had, both made it 6
months without any bubbling or flaking.  Our new 1GHz TiBooks seem to 
be okay as well, but we've only had those for a week.

Neither of us purchased AppleCare for the 800MHz ones, but we both did
for the 1GHz models, mainly to take advantage of the "double your RAM
for $40" deal...the Apple Store was required to do the RAM installation,
and thus would have charged us $60 apiece for the install.  Since we
were both going to get AppleCare before the first year was out anyway,
we went ahead and bought it on the spot, as notebooks with AppleCare
have the $60 installation feee waived.  Now that they've upgraded AC to
provide "anywhere" coverage for notebooks, I'm quite happy with the
decision.  And though nobody I've spoken to knows whether the paint
has changed on the new 1GHz models, I've got my fingers crossed.
