Ti 500 purchased 8/01. Early Jan spent 10 days on the Orinoco and in 
Trinidad and Tobago; in June spent 3 weeks flying all over China 
including 5 days on the Yangtze and the Ti hasn't missed a beat-no 
paint bubbling no problems at all except missing "feet."  I pack it in 
a "bubble" mailer sleeve from Staples and put it in a back pack. It's 
"bumped" down the conveyor of many airport security sites.  I did buy 
the Applecare at a "sale" price from SmallDog.

I like it so much I gave it to my wife and I just got a Ti 1gig/1gig. 
This one is even better - it "feels" a little more "solid" and the 
"feet" seem to want to stay on. Fan is on more than the 500 
particularly when it is plugged in but is unobtrusive to me. Haven't 
burned anything so far so can't report on heat. Newer, better, smaller 
charger with replaceable "pluggers" (Apple calls them duckbills)  just 
like my iPod. In fact I'm putting the "international plug adapter" pack 
on my Christmas list; they're useable on both the iPod and Ti 1 gig 
chargers.

Wirelessly surfing side by side :-)
