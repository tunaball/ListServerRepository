Did you try what Dennis suggested? i.e.:

> You can check on your own if you can get to the IMAP port by  
> opening Terminal and making a telnet connection to the mail  
> server's IMAP port:
>
> telnet <mailserver.college.edu> 143

You should do that from a terminal session on your computer. This  
will at least tell you if an IMAP server is responding on port 143 on  
that machine.

If you get some kind of reply back  (it may look like gobbledigook to  
you but you should at least see "connected to server_name") then you  
know that it's not a firewall/network problem.
