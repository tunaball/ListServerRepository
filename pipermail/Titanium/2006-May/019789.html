Nobody asked Henry if the fan is working. If not and the Ti is hot  
you have problem Henry.

If you just using the Ti too heavy and has placed it too tight on a  
non absorbing surface, then remedy either or both. The later by  
simply place something under the back corners of the Ti. Just to make  
the air flow better under it. Swedish match boxes are about 1 cm high  
and they are perfect for it.

If the general temperature in the room also get very hot I  
occationally have to raise the front corners as well, but I have  
never had to pay for some extra "gispmos" to keep it from running on  
fan, (I hate the noice of the fan)
