> Port 25 is for SMTP. That's the  port used to send outgoing mail  
> from you to the server for relay to its destination. They need to  
> enable IMAP or POP or both. I believe it is on by default when

> default setting during the install. If you can't get them to turn  
> on IMAP or POP, which they should do since it has no security  
> issues any different than native MAPI used by Exchange, then you
