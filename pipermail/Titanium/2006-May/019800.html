I notice that the $93 RAM seems to be unbranded (or if it is branded,  
it's not mentioned in the spec) and comes with no warranty. The $109  
RAM is branded Samsung and comes with a 60 day warranty.

Personally I go for branded RAM every time, to ensure that the chips  
on the DIMM have gone through the more rigorous testing a Brand  
requires, which translates to higher stability and better performance.

Not everyone would agree, and this topic has come up many times on  
the Ti list,  but you have my stance on it at least.
