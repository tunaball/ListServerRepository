I didn't think Chris sounded riled up at all.  You want riled up? 
Talk to those developers that have hung in with Apple through some 
major changes, after it dawns on them they need to port to two 
platforms to 'reach' the Apple "3%".

I see people referring to the last transition, from 68k to PPC, as 
being 'smooth'. It was. It was a transition to a superior platform, 
as opposed to an anachronistic one. Legacy apps ran faster in 
'emulation' on the new chips, than they did on previous 
top-of-the-line 'native' architecture.

The other 'smooth' part of the transition? Apple's market share went 
from around 12% to 3% (at which -approximate- point MS released NT). 
Oh, we had 'em right where we wanted 'em all right!
