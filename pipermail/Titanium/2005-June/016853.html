Frank,
    I've used Lexar, Cruzer, and Belkin "Jump Drives" right out of the 
box-- never had to format anything-- to transfer files back & forth 
between the PC & Mac platforms.  The most important point for smooth 
transition of Word documents between platforms, from my experience, is 
to check the box when saving Word files on the Mac that appends the 
extension to the filename (i.e., notes.doc).  The Mac doesn't need them; 
the PC is seems to be much more finicky about having the suffix...
