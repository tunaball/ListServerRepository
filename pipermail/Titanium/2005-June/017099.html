For those of you who are curious:

TCP confirms that packets are received. UDP just keeps sending packets,
without ensuring that you got the previous packet OK. UDP *should* be
faster, since it doesn't have the overhead of ensuring delivery. However, if
you're not getting all the data packets, it's hardly useful, is it?
