Usually your volume is muted or turned right down, either of these will 
result in no chime.
As the chime is still a hardware diagnostic it can not be easily 
disabled.
The mute or low volume does not stop the chime it simply makes it 
inaudible
Resetting the p-ram [apple+option+P+R] will reset this, or simply turn 
up the volume once booted

Hope this helps
