On Jun 28, 2005, at 2:44 AM, Alex wrote:

> Hints and tips for DIY powerbook repair....
>
> 1 have a tray handy for holding the bits that are going to come off  
> or out
> 2 Make sure you have all the tools necessary, the service manual  
> will tell you what you need
> 3 use a spread out blob of blu tack to hold the screws so they stay  
> in order and don't roll about
> 4 it is advisable to get an earthing wristband just incase you  
> static shock the innards of your pb.
> 5 slow and careful is the way and never force anything
>
> passed that have fun and put everything back where you found it :-)
>
