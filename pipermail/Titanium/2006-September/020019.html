I also have the 160 Seagate drive and it works great!

Here's my FWIW.

If you REALLY care about speed rather than space then get the BIGGEST  
7200rpm drive you possibly can.  when I went from the 60Gig 4200 to  
the 60Gig 7200rpm drive my startup time and application launching was  
literally cut in half.  But I ended up needing space so badly I HAD  
to get the 160.

My two bits.
