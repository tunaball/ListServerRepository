Well, one thing that comes to mind is a fine auto polishing compound  
(polish, not wax). It would be slightly abrasive enough to wear down  
the top of the keys and remove the lettering. But you would also wear  
down the texture and render the keys feeling slippery. Just don't  
experiment with various solvents because you might use the wrong one  
and dissolve the plastic itself.
