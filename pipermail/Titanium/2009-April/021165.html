Yesterday I upped the RAM in my TiBook from 768MB to 1GB. I didn't think it 
would make too much of a difference, but after using it today it really has 
helped alot.

I am running Leopard on it, and it is so much more responsive.

So for anyone pondering whether it is worth it - I would definitely say go 
for it - as long as you can get them cheap enough.
