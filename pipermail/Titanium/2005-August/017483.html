The airport expres nor extreme have Firewire port.

If your drive has USB port, I would sugest to buy ASUS WL500G Deluxe  
(I has USB 2.0) and format the drive as FAT32 or EXT2 (linux  
filesystem, works with drivers under 10.3, 10.4 drivers are on the way)
