David Brostoff <listaddr at earthlink.net> writes:
> Could you direct me to where it says a Ti drive is user-replaceable?
> AppleCare has insisted to me that the drive is not user-replaceable,
> even though the manual has instructions for replacing it. (Just to
> make sure, I called twice to get a different opinion.)

Try selecting your model at the bottom of the page here.
http://www.apple.com/support/diy/faq/

It never explicitly says your warranty won't be voided, but it does  
say that the replacement part is covered by the warranty.  The bottom  
line is this: if you do it properly, your warranty is fine.  If you  
screw up while doing it, your warranty is void.  However, if a  
service provider screws up while doing it, your warranty is still  
valid, and they'll fix their mistake.
