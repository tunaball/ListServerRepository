Whereas previosly I was bemoaning the poor battery live on my 15.2" 
FW800, I noticed that after:

1.  turning BlueTooth off

2.  draining the battery completely and fully recharging it

3.  installing Battery Update 1.1,

my battery life has increased significantly.

I get the impression that it was the Battery Update that made the 
largest difference.

It now shows at least 2h on full charge.  Right now it is showing 2 h 
35 (full screen brighness).
The most it has shown is 3h.

So somewhere between 50% and 100% more than last week.		:-)

Not as good as the Ti-500 was, but now "acceptable" I think.
