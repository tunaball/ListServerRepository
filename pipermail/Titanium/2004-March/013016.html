I have a Moto v600 Bluetooth phone.

To get the images off of my phone, I do the following:

1. "pair" the phone (you do this once - it connects the phone to the 
computer).

2. Open Bluetooth File Exchange (Applications/Utilities)

3. Browse Device. Select the phone.

4. The images are in the phone's picture folder.

Hope this helps.
