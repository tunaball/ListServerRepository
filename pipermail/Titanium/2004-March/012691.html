> Does anybody know how two WIFI enabled laptops can transfer files?
> 
> Specifically do you have to similar ABS on the Tibook or can this be
> done
> in a peer to peer fashion b/w the two machines?
> 
> Just exactly how does one configure things up on the PC laptop (Windows
> XP) side?

Wireless is really easy.

Set up an adhoc network on one machine, and connect to it on the other.  You
then have a wireless network to transfer data from one machine to another.

However, if you have ethernet interfaces on both machines, that will be
faster, and Macs have "smart" ports that don't require a crossover cable to
connect another machine to a Mac.
