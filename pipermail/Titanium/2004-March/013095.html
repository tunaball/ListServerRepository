Go to Crucial.  www.crucial.com

Stay away from cheap places; they tend to sell no-name chips that are 
prone to fail.  The cheapest of all (usually coast to coast, aka 
18004MEMORY) is certainly the worst of all.

It's very easy to install; pop the keyboard off, remove the memory 
cover with a tiny phillips on the iBook (not necessary for the TiBook), 
insert the RAM at a 30 degree angle, press it down flat, close the 
computer again.
