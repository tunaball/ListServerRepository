I wrote "Toni" at Verizon to complain about their dropping Mac support, 
and within a few hours received a reply from John, Verizon Wireless 
Data Technical Support. He wrote, in part,

> Verizon Wireless has developed a strong identity, as a company, for 
> doing things "right" when we jump into new areas of the wireless 
> service business and at this point, our business strategy and
> financial teams say there is not a strong business case that would 
> lead us to dedicate the human and financial resources required to 
> support Mac.
>
> As a result, we are not currently in a place to offer assistance in 
> the event that connectivity or other technical issues arise for 
> Macintosh devices.

So there you have it. If and when I find a company that covers my area, 
which is a little remote, and that supports Macintosh, I'll drop 
Verizon quick -- letting them know why, of course.
