I took the bottom off and used several layers of small adhesive rubber 
strips(from Home Depot)
to hold the drive in place when the bottom was back on. I put them on 
the outside side edge  The drive is held in place by friction anyway. 
Recommend
  you not open the machine with the top closed.  It should be open and 
hanging down on the edge of a table.  Otherwise
  the screen may be damaged.

I have also found that if the screws holding the drive in the mounting 
frame are loose, it can cause
the drive to be out of place. The drive is in a mounting fram which has 
4 mounting points with rubber gromets
