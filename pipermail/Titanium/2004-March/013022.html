I just got one of the 15" 1.25Ghz refurbs from the Apple Store.

Display is perfect, no stuck pixels.

Even though everything I read said it would have 2 256Mb memory cards 
installed, it came with one 512Mb card and one empty slot.  Pleasant 
surprise.

It had 10.2.x installed and came with an install/restore DVD for 
Panther along with iLife '04.

Everything about it seems perfect as far as I can tell, but as my 
brother says, the fact that I broke down and bought one means that new 
models will be announced shortly ;-)
