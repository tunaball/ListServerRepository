I have been travelling with my laptop(s) for a long time now and have 
never had a real problem doing this. Just stay alert and make sure that 
no one gets in the scanner before you (this person can delay you while 
another on the other side grabs your laptop).

Having said that, the TSA people have been nothing but careful with my 
belongings (I travel with lots of gadgets and get checked all the 
time).

Don't worry about it too much and if you feel your machine might get 
scratched toss a piece of paper towel or that thin foam which your 
computer comes wrapped in on the bottom of the bin before putting the 
computer in it.
