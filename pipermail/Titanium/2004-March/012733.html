It's a Radeon Mobility 7500 with 32 MB of DDR SDRAM.  Supported 
resolutions are 640x480, 800x600, and 1024x768; all are using 
mirroring.  Refresh rates are probably more dependent on the monitor 
you are using than on the iBook itself.  The iBook doesn't support 
video spanning.  It doesn't support older Apple monitors (eg Multiple 
Scan 17 or 20, etc.).
That's about all there is in terms of specs.
