 From my experience (in UK) Apple customer service has been always  
great.
There is always to wait and you have almost no chances to talk with  
the same person, but they try to help.
The problem is on the infrastructure behind it: their technical  
service replaced the screen of my TiBook500 and, while at there, they  
decided to replace also the logic board (for no obvious reason) with  
one at 400MHz. Then, after 1 year, I sent my Albook (graciously  
provided by Apple to replace my TiBook500-400) for a stuck key on the  
keyboard and a failing hard drive, and they forgot to replace the HD.  
They replaced it the second time, but they forgot to connect to the  
rest of the machine. And when I wanted to send it the third time,  
there was an outage of shipping boxes (more than 2 week to wait).
In all these occasions Apple Customer Service tried to help and to  
solve the problems arisen from their technical workshops.
BTW after waiting some time for the shortage of boxes, the Apple  
Customer Service just sent me a new computer....
