I have used Carbon Copy Cloner and SuperDuper. I started out with CCC 
and currently use SuperDuper. I replaced my TiBook HD and restored 
with a bootable clone on an external HD created with SD. After doing 
a complete backup, you can do subsequent incrementals (update mode). 
You can restore a complete volume or just a file or folder.
http://www.shirt-pocket.com/SuperDuper/SuperDuperDescription.html
http://www.bombich.com/software/ccc.html
