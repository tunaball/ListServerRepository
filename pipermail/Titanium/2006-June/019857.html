I think that modell like my early one has a keyboard that can be  
lifted from the screen side. There is two small button (right of esc  
and between F8 and F9) which you pull towards you. You may have to  
first  use a small screwdriver on the num look indicator light  
(between F4 and F5) which holds the locking device for the keyboard.  
Turn the screw a full 360. Mine is never looked so if you have bought  
your Ti-book used it may not be locked from the beginning.

The reason for this description is that I think you simply have got  
some small dirt cought under the return key. So by loosing the  
keyboard enough to shake it sideways besides the Tibook you may get  
the stuff to simply fall out. Otherwise you will at least get a good  
chance to look at it sideways and see what causes the resistance.

Good luck. :-)
