Almost 3 months ago, my Address Book lost al of 
its function (see my original post below).
I had a number of helpful suggestions from the 
List, all of which I tried or implemented, all to 
no avail.

Yesterday, I reinstalled Mac OS X 10.3, over my 
existing installation (which was neatly put in a 
"Previous Suystems" folder).  I then ran the 
10.3.4 Combo update.

My Address Book is now working perfectly.

The reinstallation also fixed the problem with 
Virex 7.2, that it could not access the eUpdate 
(it always said "cannot connect to th4e 
Internet").  It now works perfectly.

Who knows where the problem was, but everything 
is perfectly restored, BlueTooth works through 
the Address Book, Virex keeeps checking for 
updates, ....

I just thought that the members might like to 
know that sometimes, strange problems can be 
fixed by this approach.

regards,  Trevor
