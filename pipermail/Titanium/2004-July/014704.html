The 80gig is not the 7200 rpm.  I put the 60Gig 7200rpm in my Ghz, 
Superdrive.  It made wuite a difference in launching applications, and 
startup.  Cut startup time in half.  The downside is the power 
consumption, it dropped my battery life a little, but it was WELL worth 
the money.  I also bought the IOgear External enclosure, and am using 
my old 60Gig as a backup drive.
