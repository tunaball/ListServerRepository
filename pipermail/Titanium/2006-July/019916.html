You should have a hardware test CD if you can localise your original  
CD:s

I keep repeating and noticing that it makes a difference on the old  
Ti.s to check that all the screws are well seated at the bottom _and_  
under the keyboard. Also that none of the small magnets under the  
keyboard has come loose and ended up in the wrong place.

Can you (still?) start it in T-mode? Have you checked that it isn't  
just your screen which is faulty? Do you have a working screen when  
you start from the Tiger install-DVD? Do you have an external screen  
you can try it with?
