I know I am rather late on the scene, but I have been wondering why  
people don’t try the software that Apple supplies to do backups. I am  
of course referring to Apple Disk Utility. Just use the “Restore”  
function to do full backups. I have gone this route a couple of times  
and it works like a charm. Not nearly as fast as SuperDuper, but it  
works fine.
