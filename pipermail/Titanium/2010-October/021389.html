I second the suggestion to unplug the PRAM battery when your 'Book 
appears to be stone-dead. I've revived _dozens_ of machines that folks 
either gave away or sold dirt cheap, using this simple step.

Unplugging on a DVI TiBook requires one to (IIRC) remove the bottom cover 
and remove optical drive. Easy to do, just be careful of tearing those 
flat cables.
