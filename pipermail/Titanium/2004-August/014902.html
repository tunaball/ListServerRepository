What has happened is that your mail list is being sorted by name (From) or
subject or some other criteria. What you need to do is hit the "Date
Received" bar to have it sorted by date/time. Then your latest messages
should be grouped either at the top or bottom of the list (depending on
whether you have it ascending or descending).
