Just as a side note, on many mailing lists, you can view the raw source 
of full headers of a message to the list and some headers will be 
included with list subscription info.  For example:

List-Unsubscribe: <mailto:Titanium-off at lists.themacintoshguy.com>
List-ID: <Titanium.lists.themacintoshguy.com>
List-Archive: 
<http://lists.themacintoshguy.com:80/Lists/Titanium/List.html>
