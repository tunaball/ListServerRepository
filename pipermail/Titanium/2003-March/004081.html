Personally I love it because of the amount of features crammed into its 
size.  And with the new Sony Ericsson Clicker program it's become (and 
has the potential to be) even more useful.  But, if you want polyphonic 
ringtones, this doesn't have it.  If you want a bigger, higher 
resolution color screen, look to one of the newer and more expensive 
Nokias.  If you've got big, beefy hands, you probably want to avoid it. 
  If your idea of a perfect cellphone is one that you can use in the 
middle of nowhere, you probably don't want it.  Definitely check the 
GSM reception in the areas that you frequent the most.  The most 
complaints I've read about the T68 series is the "poor" reception.  
I've circumvented that problem by living in an urban area with dense 
GSM coverage.  But really, I've made and received calls with only 1-2 
bars on the meter without a problem.
