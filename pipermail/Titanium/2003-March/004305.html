>Also, is there a book or site where I can begin to learn terminal 
>and its commands.

Learning the terminal as such is pretty trivial. What you want to 
learn is unix. In particular use of the C shell, "csh". Pick up any 
unix book, and you'll learn enough to get you going. There are no 
good books yet about the specific unix that apple uses (Darwin) and 
how it differs from other unices.

There is an x-unix mailing list specifically about unix as it 
pertains to Apple. (One of the participants is writing a book for 
people just like you.)
