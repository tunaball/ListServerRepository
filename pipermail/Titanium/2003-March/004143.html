I haven't followed the thread closely, but surely the is VNC for the 
Mac. There are both VNC clients and servers for Mac OS X:

<http://www.redstonesoftware.com/osxvnc/>
<http://netmath.math.uiuc.edu/vnc/>
<http://sourceforge.net/projects/cotvnc/>

'automatic' fashion, since at least the server needs to be accessible
