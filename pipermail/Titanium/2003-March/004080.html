I had no trouble registering although I took a single license.

I hadn't seen Romeeo despite frequent visits to Versiontracker.com. I  
imagine the two can happily exist on the TiBook, although only one can  
be selected at a time.

The downside to these apps as I see it, or perhaps it is a Bluetooth  
shortcoming, is the inability to have other Bluetooth functioning  
occurring simultaneously. I can't use my Address Book Bluetooth  
integration, nor my Bluetooth headset paired with the T68i while the  
Clicker is doing its thing. That would be OK with things like  
Powerpoint and Keynote, but a disadvantage if using iTunes in the  
background and wanting to make calls or send SMSs in the foreground.

I'm not too sure where the failure to share resides - the apps, Apple's  
Bluetooth implementation, the T68i, but it would be good to get this  
issue overcome.
