Unfortunately for you, the internal power connector is on the logic 
board in that revision.  If you have good soldering skills, you can try 
reconnecting the plug to the logic board, but you could easily fry 
something in that process.

I think starting with the DVI PowerBooks, Apple moved the power 
connector to a tiny little separate board which only costs about $5, 
similar to what the white iBooks have.
