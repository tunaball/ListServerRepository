Officially, almost nothing.  Hard drives and RAM, as somebody else 
mentioned.  Not logic boards, screens, or even the top case.  You could 
probably get away with the clutch covers though.  ;-)
You might be able to swap optical drives, though the cable placement 
and drive size can vary, and there might not be the proper mounting 
hardware.

However, if you really want to swap things, you can generally swap 
anything between the same revision; ie between the 400 and 500, or 
between the 550 and the first 667.
