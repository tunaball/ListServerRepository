I used to have a copy of Office that I "borrowed" from a colleague. 
When I got my own copy, it kept on using the old license. Here's how 
you nuke the old license:

[~] %% rm /Applications/Microsoft\ Office\ X/Office/OfficePID
[~] %% rm ~/Library/Preferences/Microsoft/Microsoft\ Office\ Settings\ \(10\)

I hope that's of help to you.
