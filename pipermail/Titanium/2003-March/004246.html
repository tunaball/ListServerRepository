RCA, but it actually has a 2.5 mm tip and sleeve mini-plug surrounded by
collar. I know that the plug has different LEDs to indicate charging and
normal power. I am also pretty sure that Apple has copyrighted/patented the
design so there will not be any third party fixit kits for sale.

If someone wants to be adventurous your might be able to jury rig one of
those Cell Phone head set plugs to work. It will void the warrantee though.
