I agree with Massimo. For a new battery (as I just got), let it run 
right out, then fully charge it.  This should ensure maximum life. 
Some say repeat the discharge completely every 3 months.  For me, 
that happens anyway (whilst travelling, I often use the Ti-book until 
the battery is empty) at least that often.

Normally, my battery is in all the time, and my Ti-book is plugged in 
whilst at work and whilst at home.  The charge is between 95 and 100% 
all the time, like that.

My battery lifetime (end of life was when the capacity fully charged 
was only 4 minutes instead of 4 hours) was almost exactly two years.
