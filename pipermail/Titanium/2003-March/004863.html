I put my 17 incher on a printer stand (the old kind that people used 
to have for continuous feed stationery printers) on the desk. This 
not only raises it up a few inches, bringing the screen to a more 
ergonomic height, but it allows me to put a USB keyboard partly 
underneath and I can also feed the various cables underneath the 
stand and out the back.
