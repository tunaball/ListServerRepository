It is one of those funny little gadgets that people either love or dismiss
as being silly! It is considered high in the "coolness" scale but has
limited usefulness. I love it in certain applications like those in the
iLife bundle. I also find it very handy in Entourage for scrolling, and the
clicker for Command/K. I use the 'hold down click' universally to send a
Command/H. Mainly I love it for its pulsing blue light (very calming) and
for an alternate way of using my right hand for certain tasks that can
prevent RSI - which could bother me if I only used my Kensington Turbo Mouse
Pro.

But if you need a jog wheel with bells and whistles for intensive media
tasks, the PowerMate doesn't really cut it. There are many more precision
products that can do the job.
