One other issue just discovered.....Address Book activates via  
Bluetooth before the T68i rings. This happens consistently such that  
Address Book says 2 rings and pops up well before I actually here the  
phone ring. For those who haven't tried it, the Address book pop up  
also lets you send the caller to the T68i's voice mail system (if  
activated) from the TiBook, should you not wish to answer the phone. I  
have yet to get the Bluetooth headset to work at the same time however,  
which would allow me to not need to have the phone near me. BTW, I am  
using the D-Link USB adaptor, which is probably the smallest one about  
currently.
