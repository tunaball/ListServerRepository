I believe there are two generations of screens, 400-667 MHz had 1152 pixels
wide, 800 MHz and above are higher resolution.

I believe there are two generations of keyboards.  Up to 550 MHz is first
generation.  667 MHz introduced a command key to the right of the spacebar.

Visit http://www.pbparts.com.  They explain a lot of this.
