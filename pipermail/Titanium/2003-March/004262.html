Thanks, Philip - I just watched it with a flashlight.  Now I understand how
it works.  It's magnetic.  You can also pull it out with a refrigerator
magnet.  Smart folks at Apple.  No wonder why we're willing to pay so much
for these machines.

To answer my question, though, I suppose it is possible that the hook in the
screen/lid is damaged but more likely that the latch in the bottom is
damaged.  The hook is obviously made of steel, and looks beefier than the
latch plate in the bottom, which looks like aluminum (or titanium?).
