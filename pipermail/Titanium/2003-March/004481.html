I have to agree here. When you have really profound problems it is best have
all of them.. But use then prudently. Also you should remember that you need
several clean runs to have any confidence that you have fixed all the
problems, as some problems can mask other problems. I I had to select just
one utility, it would be DiskWarrior.

HAVING said that, disk utilities should be purchased AFTER you have
developed a practical backup strategy.. Not as a substitute.
