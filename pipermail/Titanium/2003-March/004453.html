printer ready, plugged into USB port. Launched PrintCenter,
selected Add->Rendezvous printers. Found printer in few seconds. 
Printed my paper.

Total time to get my first page printed out after having the printer 
ready and plugged in: around 30 secs max.

Software to be installed? zero. I am impressed.
