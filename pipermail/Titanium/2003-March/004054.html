Hi!

I found out my app was being discussed here, so I figured I could do 
some "in-advance" support work. ;-)

Here's the deal:

eSellerate messed up with the activation SDK and serial number 
generator... To compensate for this, I have published a new version 
that skips the "activation" step.

So, if you have had trouble registering, please download the new 
version from clicker.salling.com (it should say "1.0.0 (48)" in the 
about tab).

Also, If you have difficulties opening the iTunes menu, please rename 
the "Show Track Info…" item to just "Show track info". Some phones have 
a problem with UTF-8.

I will release a more reliable version later this week. Unfortunately, 
my day job is keeping me extremely busy right now, as I need to leave 
for a business trip.

Cheers,
Jonas
