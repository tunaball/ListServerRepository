>Usually the "Name" you have to put in isn't your local 'Mac' user name, they want the name on your email account, as in 'whatever comes before the @" . In other words if you're trying to get your mail from the addy in your email post here, you'd put in flanders1, or perhaps flanders1 at charter.net.  THEN, your password associated with the email account will work fine.

This is all about smtp or pop server setting, email user name, and password. Your network is working fine.

The first thing I'd check is to see if you are set correctly for a POP or IMAP account. Then check your user name as mentioned above.
