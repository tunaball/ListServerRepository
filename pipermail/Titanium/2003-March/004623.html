I would think that iCal would follow whatever you have your 'System 
Preferences' at.  If it's set for 24 hr clock iCal would be 24 hour. 
Just set the Time tab in the 'International' panel of the Sys. Prefs. 
You do need to quit and restart iCal for it to switch (or that's what 
worked for me.)
