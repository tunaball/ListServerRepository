If you ever need to figure out how to type a special character, the Key 
Caps application is the way to go; it's in your Utilities folder.  Just 
open it up, choose the font you're using (though most are identical) 
and hold down modifier keys (generally just option and/or shift) and 
see what special characters show up on the displayed keyboard.
