DiskWarrior, all the way.  Might be worth waiting for the next version 
of it if you mainly use OS X, however.
Norton has a tendency to screw things up sometimes, and can cause more 
problems than it fixes.  TechTool is OK, but just really won't fix as 
many disk problems as DiskWarrior.  Admittedly, all three are good at 
certain things and not others, but I'd call DiskWarrior the best 
overall.  If you have a big budget, buy all three; I've had problems 
bad enough that only one of the three will even touch the drive, or 
other problems where two of the utilities find something wrong but 
can't fix it, while the third fixes it.
