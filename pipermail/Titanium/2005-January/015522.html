Rob,

I keep it spooled up in the plastic retaining "clip" that it came in.

When I need it I unplug one end of the F/Wire cable from he "clip" 
and plug it into the camera and plug the other end into the P/B, the 
rest of the cable remains coiled and secured by the inside of the 
"clip".

Then when I've finished, I unplug the F/Wire cable from the P/B and 
the camera and push both end back into either side of the "clip" and 
then toss the spooled cable back into my bag.

if that would make it clearer?
