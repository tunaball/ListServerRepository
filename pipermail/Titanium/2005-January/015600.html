Lisbeth, if you have access to another mac, restart yours in firewire 
mode (hold down T when booting). Then connect through firewire to the 
other mac. You should then be able to see your old mac - backup your 
files and reinstall the OS, or run diagnostics, or erase the disk and 
start again.

David
