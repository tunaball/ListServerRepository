I doubt it.  They are different sizes and, I'm sure, have many other
differences.

But you have a very valuable card there.  If you have an old Ti (bigger)
airport card you should sell it on eBay, because Apple does not make them
any more, apparently they break a lot, and there are many people who need
them for their Titanium powerbooks and other older Macs.

You should be able to buy two or three of the newer (smaller) Airport
Extreme cards with the money you get selling the (bigger) old one.
