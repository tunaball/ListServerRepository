Yeah, I see wasted money on computers every day.  Our state's (GA) lottery funds provide schools with a technology budget - the caveat is that the money can only be spent on hardware that costs more than $1000.  No peripherals, no support personnel salaries, only computers.  My department makes these huge purchases with Dell, just assuming that everyone will want a new computer.  The 30% of my department on macs refuse these machines, so the computers just sit around until another one breaks down and they replace it.  SO much wasted money. Elementary and High Schools get lots of computers - macs and pc's that they can't use because noone can support them - outside the big city the school system budgets can't afford tech support.  Just no common sense in all of this, especially since we are in a hiring and raise freeze in the state.  I'm sure there will be another pallet of Dell desktops with flat panel monitors arriving soon.  At least we won't be short of monitors and doors!
