I suggest that you try and do the squeeze again in an effort to really 
get that connection noice. No CD in the drive while you're doing this. 
Feed the CD slightly från the left as it seem it catches it easier when 
you do that. When you get it working, make certain that you don' t lift 
the Ti with one hand any more and specially not at the right hand side. 
It seems to be some twist causing the dislocation. (Similar to the 
problem on some computers loosing battery connection.)

Also in an effort to make your Ti-book a bit sturdier tighten up the 
screws on the bottom and the screws under the keyboard. They tend to 
get loose and cause the Ti-book to twist easier.
