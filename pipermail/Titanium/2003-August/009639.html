This is a subject close to my heart, Ron.

I would LOVE for Apple to put a decent, well shielded, hifi quality 
analogue audio stage in the new TiBooks. Although the new 17 inch 
tibooks seem to have marginally better sound its not at all what I am 
looking for (0 audible hiss).

In the notebook world good quality sound seems to be low on the list of 
priorities for a consumer. It's also tricky as everything is close 
together, so it can be hard to shield the analogue stage from the rest 
of the electronics. however manufacturers have made PCMCIA cards that 
do achieve this. My feeling is when the new 15 inch model comes out you 
will still be disappointed, but I'd love Apple to surprise us.

Maybe we should all petition Apple about it or something! I don't mind 
paying an extra $100 for decent audio.
