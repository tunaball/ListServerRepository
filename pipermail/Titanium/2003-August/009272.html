On Saturday, August 9, 2003, at 09:30  AM, bee4 wrote:

> This is not really a Ti question, but I am using a Ti. I have
> set up too many outgoing mail servers in my mail account.
> Let me explain, in mail open mail preferences then open a
> mail account by double clicking it or clicking accounts. You will
> then see outgoing mail server, if you choose option you can
> view SMTP server options. My problem is I'd like to delete about
> 4 of these outgoing servers, does anyone know how to do this?

Look in /Applications/AppleScript and double-click Script Menu.menu.  
That will put the AppleScript menu in your upper-right menubar with the 
clock, etc.  Find the "Manage SMTP Servers" script in the pull-down and 
run it, and you should be on your way!
