the 80s Apple is getting serious and positive press in IT 
publications that formerly ignored them almost completely. The XServe

that even as good as the XServe is folks are cautious.  If the server 
version of Panther is a full price upgrade as Jaguar was (that's 
$1,000 per seat!), it will kill the momentum and Apple Marketing will 
be scratching their head and wondering why such a great product can't 
penetrate its market.
