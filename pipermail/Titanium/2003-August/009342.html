At 12:53 -0400 8/12/03, Greg Markle wrote:
>Wouldn't a 12' wide screen at a 4:3 ratio yield 12' x 9' = 108 sq. ft.?

It is 12' DIAGONAL.  Since the diagonal (hypoteneuse) on a 
right-angled triangle of sides 4 and 3 is 5,
then the sides of a triangle with a hypoteneuse of 12 would be 9.6 
and 7.2, yielding a surface of just fractionally over 69 square feet, 
as Paul Russell correctly calculated...
