Ron,

I have an iSight on my 15" Ti Book and everything is fine EXCEPT:

Don't daisy chain it off another powered FireWire 
device. Always use it alone or via a powered 
FireWire hub.

I had an externally powered FireWire hard drive 
that I daisy chained the iSIght off. When the 
camera activated - iChat crashed and the drive 
gave me the "this device wasn't put away 
properly" warning.

Trying to plug the drive back in failed (in both 
OS X and OS 9) Disk Utility and Disk warrior 
failed too. I ended up spending A$800 to get the 
data recovered.

Since that little "learning experience" and by 
following the rules of solo or via a hub (I use a 
Belkin one) it works great.
