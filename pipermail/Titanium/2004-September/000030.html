The keyboard for the 667 DVI, 800, 867, and 1.0 GHz models are the 
same.  Part # is 922-5199.

The keyboard for the 550/667 gigabit ethernet models is different.  
Part # is 922-4622.

There's something damaged in your keyboard.  Each key has a little 
scissors assy under it, and it pushes down on a rubber protected switch 
when the key is pressed.  Something has happened so that switches are 
making contact that aren't supposed to be, probably from just pressing 
adjacent keys to the ones with bad switches.

The cost of a new board is only about $130.
