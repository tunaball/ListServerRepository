I agree that one big list might be too diverse, 
maybe just some tuning of the existing lists is 
needed.

Rename (or refocus) the PowerBook list to cover 
G3 and earlier PB (dare I say it but called Pre 
G4 PowerBooks).  And rename the Ti list to cover
