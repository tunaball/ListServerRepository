However, I have to tell you about something I really like about the  
BT implementation on a Powerbook.

I recently bought an FM module with BT capabilities to work with my  
Hearing Aids. This tiny unit (about the size and shape of an iPod  
Shuffle) is made for use with BT capable Cel phones, so I was  
sceptical about whether I could use it with the Powerbook. Guess  
what! The module paired with the Powerbook and was recognized as an  
audio headset with no problem at all and now I have the audio from  
the Powerbook feeding straight into my hearing aids. I can also use  
the module as a BT microphone for sound input. Cool or what?
