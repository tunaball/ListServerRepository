Just a word of caution there...


Once you launch the beta, and you try to sleep your Intel Mac - it  
may crash on wake-up. Mine does, and I've read others do as well. It  
may be related to the way it "virtualizes".

Like VirtualPC, it has extensions that you can activate to allow  
seamless transitions between XP and OS X (haven't loaded them myself  
yet)

Check the Parallels forum if you haven't already

There are video drivers that allow you to have different screen  
resolutions

It runs well on the mini, I can't say much for the MacBook.
