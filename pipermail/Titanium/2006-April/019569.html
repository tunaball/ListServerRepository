Quicktome pro will do it, but you have to pay. There are several  
lower cost shareware or freeware programs that will do similar on  
versiontracker. I use AudioCorder (shareware) because it has a lot of  
nice interface features and flexibility.
For freeware, there is Audio Recorder, and Audio In, neither of which  
I've used, but the function isn't very difficult to implement.

Audacity is also a freeware open source recorder and editor. It's  
more complicated to use because it has a lot of editing capability.

Do a search for "audio record" or other similar strings on  
versiontracker and you'll find a few.
