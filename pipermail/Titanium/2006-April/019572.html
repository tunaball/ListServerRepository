While the 1.0 Ghz can't be upgraded to a 7448 processor, it can be  
upgraded to a 7447A clocked at 1.92 Ghz.  We have a fellow at work  
that had his done.  It cost him $500, took 4 days to get it back, and  
it works beautiful.  Technically the 1.0 Ghz can be upgraded to a 2.0  
Ghz 7447A, but you have to replace the logic board with one out of a  
1.5, which is most times cost probative.

I'd call their tech support dept at 1-877-439-8646 to get the full  
scoop.

Oh, and your PowerBook must be a 15" too.  The 12" units can't be  
upgraded.
