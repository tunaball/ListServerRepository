Listers:

Just to add to the woes, the "SecUpd2005-004" has an issue that affects 
users with multiple accounts.  Any standard, or managed accounts will 
not be able to log in after this update is installed.  I don't how many 
of this list will be affected as for the most part, we are all single 
(Admin) users on our powerbooks.  I just thought that I would post it 
here as some of you (like me) may have kids with managed or standard 
accounts using your laptop, or a desktop system back at home.

Symptoms:  'Standard' or 'Managed' user tries to log in and never gets 
beyond the first screen with the horizontal spinning Barber Pole.

The simple fix, until the apple engineers release a patch, is to turn 
any 'Standard' or 'Managed' accounts into 'Admin' accounts in System 
Preferences/Accounts.

Hope this saves someone out there some agony.
