Hi Thom,

I had the same problem on my TiBook. You can use almost all cards in 
the $30 range offered on eBay. However, you must beware that none of 
these cards will provide support for bus-powered devices like the 
iSight or EyeTV since the CardBus does not provide these voltages. You 
will always have to use an external power supply for your external 
boxes.
