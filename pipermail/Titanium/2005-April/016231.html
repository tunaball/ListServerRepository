OS 9 is not part of Panther.  It comes as a Classic compatibility 
option that you can install from your restore DVD/CD that shipped with 
your computer.  The Classic OS 9 operating system that ships on the 
restore disc is not a standalone bootable operating system.  It's a 
crippled version of OS 9 designed to provide Classic support only.
