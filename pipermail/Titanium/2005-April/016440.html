Robin,

Do you mean its in the dock (the small strip of icons that usually 
runs across the bottom of the screen)?

If yes, then all you need to do is click and drag on the icon until 
the mouse pointer is away from the dock, then release the mouse.

The icon will disappear in a puff of smoke.

If you mean the area in the bottom left hand corner of a folder 
window the do the same except drag the mouse out of the folder window 
before releasing the button.
