Hi

I have used Retrospect for a good number of years and have been very happy
with it.

It does a scheduled incremental back up of both our home systems, one night
it goes to a 500Gb LaCie drive and the following night it goes to an AIT
35Gb LaCie tape drive.

I also use Retrospect to once a month to Duplicate my system disk to a spare
Firewire drive.

I also do total system backups to tape once a fortnight.

Maybe I am being paranoid BUT I have had disk failures and system failures
and I have never lost more than one days work so I think it is worth it.
