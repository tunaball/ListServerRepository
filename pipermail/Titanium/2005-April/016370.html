I believe the accepted wisdom [no quotes, just accumulated memory from 
reading many posts] is that if SMART does not say your HD is about to 
fail then your HD may or may not fail, but if SMART does say your HD is 
about to fail then it will. As the saying goes, run don't walk to get 
another HD and transfer all your data to it, but then your backup is 
current so that may not be relevant.
