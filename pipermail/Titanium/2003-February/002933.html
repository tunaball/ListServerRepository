I stayed at the Sheraton a year ago this month -- around 51st and 7th Ave.,
I think -- and they had high speed connections via ethernet that worked
fine; I had no problem connecting with my TiBook/400 (using OS 9 at the
time, but I don't believe OS X would be a problem). If I recall correctly
they added $10/day to the room charge for the connection. Not too bad
considering parking in the hotel garage was about $37/day <G>.
