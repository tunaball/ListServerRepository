Holly,

When you unplug your monitor from your Ti, the OS "drops" the connection
through the external output. When you plug your monitor back in hit the
"Detect Displays" Keystroke on your Ti - I believe it is Command - F2.
Should spring right back on.

Apologies if you already know this.. You say you can't get it to "Detect" I
don't know what that means exactly - I don't think the detection part is
ever automatic. My CRT Apple Studio Display hooks up perfectly, screws in
fine - but I don't need to do it for the display to work either.

Hope that helps
