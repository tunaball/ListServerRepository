MacSlash carries this report about a software app for using your Sony 
t68i as a remote, like the Keyspan units!

http://www.macslash.org/articles/03/02/24/156244.shtml


here is the paragraph of interest:

"Yesterday I noticed this piece of software on VersionTracker and 
couldn't resist downloading.  It's called Sony Ericsson Clicker.  It 
lets you remotely control iTunes, Powerpoint/Keynote presentations, DVD 
player, and AppleScripts, all wirelessly from your compatible Sony 
Ericsson bluetooth phone!  It even has a built-in proximity sensor that 
can tell iTunes to pause the current track when you leave the room!  
It's still beta but it worked flawlessly for me.  This is so geek-cool!"
