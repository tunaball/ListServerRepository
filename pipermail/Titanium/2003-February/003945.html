As a professor, I qualify for this program.  I have tried to take advantage 
of it.  I have had nothing but trouble.  My customer service experience can 
be best described as nightmarish.  Consequently, I would warn you to be 
weary of this whole affair.  To me, this is a concept that is great in 
theory but it sucks in practice.
