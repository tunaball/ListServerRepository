Wireless authentication is one of my areas.... the solution that we (the
University I work for) have come up with is a product by Vernier Networks.
(http://www.verniernetworks.com) It is a captive firewall that provides just
the sort of log-in interface that you are seeking.  ON the client PC it
requires nothing more then a web-browser (and I believe there is a way to
hack it to work with a telnet client).  A Complete system is in the $4,000
range with enterprise solutions being pricier.  It may be overkill depending
on your application.  Check out NoCatAuth (http://nocat.net/) for an
open-source alternative that accomplishes the same task, albeit with a bit
more wonking with the software.
