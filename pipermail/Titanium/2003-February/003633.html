I have been using OSX and Classic mode since 10.1 and have been able to 
use Quark 4 & 5 in Classic pretty well.
We print to a variety of different Postscript devices - Creo-Scitex, 
Laserwriter 630, and as long as I can find them in the chooser, I have 
no problems.
I must admit that I WAS a Quark devotee for many years, but am building 
all of my new documents using InDesign 2.0 in OSX. It does take an 
adjustment of about 2 days, but its well worth it. Later Quark!
