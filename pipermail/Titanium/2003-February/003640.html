On  2/21/03 12:32, Bill Reburn wrote:

> Some of the problems I had were;
> 
> - Screen redraw is annoying as heck. We pay sooo much money for our displays
> and work environment - to have a software glitch screw over your document
> display on a regular basis was too much to deal with. I had to click the
> Quark doc's window widget everytime I did something in another app so that
> it would finish the redraw.
> 
> - Screen artifacts.. Part of the redraw problem - move a document around and
> artifacts stick all over the place. Window widget again to clean up.
> 

XPress has a shortcut to redraw the screen: Command-Option-Period. Give it a
try if you're tired of clicking the window shade widget.

There's also an XTension called "Classic Draw XT" that claims to eliminate
these screen artifacts, but it didn't work in my machine. In fact, it
worked, but did nothing. But you can give it a try, too.
