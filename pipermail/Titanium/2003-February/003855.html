> In Unix or Linux, when I put a .forward file in a user's home
> directory, mail to that user is sent to the address mentioned in the
> .forward file. It does not seem to work in OS X. Anyone who tried
> this and succeeded? Eduard.

Not until you configure and activate the sendmail server. It is not 
"on" by default. See the /etc/hostconfig file, you will find:

MAILSERVER=-NO-

then sendmail must be part of the StartupItems, be properly configured 
(/etc/mail/sendmail.cf) then a .forward in your $HOME would be takne 
into account (something like 'sudo echo "user at newaddress.net" > 
~/.forward)

Hope this helps
