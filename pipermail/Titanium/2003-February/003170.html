On Monday, February 10, 2003, at 07:23  AM, Murray E. Milligan wrote:
> Is there a way I can auto delete my trash upon exiting my OS X mail 
> program?

Preferences:Accounts
Choose the account, hit Edit, go to the Special Mailboxes tab, and 
you'll have your options for deleting Trash.  The option you want is at 
the bottom of that list.
