After a long wait, I finally received my Dexia Rack yesterday.  This 
laptop stand, along with two others, was reviewed by Adam Angst a few 
issues ago in Tidbits.  It sounded like just the thing for my situation 
and, lo and behold, it is!  Very lightweight, the Rack has legs so that 
it can be used either on a desk/table, with an external keyboard and 
mouse, or (and this is the good part) it fits over your lap, its legs 
resting on a chair, sofa, bed, or the ground.  I have tried it with 
both my 12" PB and my TiBook, and it works a treat.  If, as I do, you 
do a lot of computing away from a desk, you might want to take a look 
at the Rack.

I have absolutely no connection with the company 
(<http://www.dexiadesign.com/>), and just wanted to pass on some news 
about an innovative and useful product.  Fair warning, however: this 
company is a small one and the product is currently available only from 
them directly or through university bookstores.  However, production is 
being ramped up and supplies should be more plentiful in March.
