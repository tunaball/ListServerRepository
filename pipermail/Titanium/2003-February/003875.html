According to Eduard Hoenkamp:

>I simply do not manage to set the permissions on files on (non-boot) 
>partitions under OSX 2.2.4

That's because, as it says right there in Disk Utility, Repair Disk 
Permissions only works on OSX boot disks, so, unless you have a 
version of OSX on the other partitions, the files on those partitions 
cannot have their permissions changed.
