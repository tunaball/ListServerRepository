>After a long wait, I finally received my Dexia Rack yesterday.  This 
>laptop stand, along with two others, was reviewed by Adam Angst a

>situation and, lo and behold, it is!  Very lightweight, the Rack has 
>legs so that it can be used either on a desk/table, with an external 
>keyboard and mouse, or (and this is the good part) it fits over your 
>lap, its legs resting on a chair, sofa, bed, or the ground.  I have 
>tried it with both my 12" PB and my TiBook, and it works a treat. 
>If, as I do, you do a lot of computing away from a desk, you might 
>want to take a look at the Rack.
