> Does anyone use LimeWire re: downloading music?  Once it's downloaded, 
> how do you transfer it to iTunes 3 using OSX (e.g., 10.1.5), so one 
> can listen to it on an iPod, for example?

You can drag the files to the iTunes icon on the dock or you can open 
iTunes and "Add To Library" (command O) under Edit menu will open a 
dialog box you use to navigate to your mp3 folder which is your 
"Limewire Shared" folder.  You can locate the "Limewire Shared" folder 
under Limewire's preferences.
