I have an Agfa e50 scanner and still use the Scanwise stuff. Just open the
Photoshop folder, then the Plug-ins folder. Then take the ScanWise plugin
(there is a Carbon version) and drop it into the "Import/Export" folder.

Then launch Photoshop and see if Scanwise is listed in the Import menu item
under File.
