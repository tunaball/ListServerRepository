Sure, open the Stationery, and select 'New", then click in the body 
[text] portion of the stationery template, and "Insert Graphic" [use 
a gif for best results]. Then, select the graphic, and 'center it'.
Save the result, and when you use it, it'll display a top-centered 
graphic. The gif will open on the recipient's computer and the 
size,[in terms of 'breadth'] of the graphic willbe determined by 
width of the user's window.
