Hi Steve et al :

Well I finally got it working -- I guess that OS X doesn't like the 
native Canon Elura2MC card formatting.  I reformatted the SanDisk MMC 
card in my W2K laptop, put it in my TiPBG4 using the Dazzle adapter, 
and it indeed mounted.  I then took out the card, put it in the camera 
and took a few snaps, put the card in the Dazzle adapter in my TiPBG4 
and indeed it worked as it should -- iPhoto launches (yes, as you said 
Steve) and the PC Card icon is on my other right (i.e. the left, as you 
also said Steve) and I import my photos successfully.

Bottom line -- when in doubt, format the card in the target Mac/PC 
instead of the camera.
