Just to mention that the iTunes Music folder (as defined by iTunes ) is
actually within your personal directory.

My partner and I share 10gig's of mp3's between our iPods. You can tell
iTunes (in the Advanced panel again) to make any folder anywhere on another
partition or HD the "iTunes Music folder".

When either of us downloads mp3's, we "Import" them through iTunes, not Drag
and Drop. This help iTunes if the mp3's are not up to par. Often an iPod
will not recognize mp3's that iTunes is fine with. All our mp3's are
organized in the same place for easy access by whomever is logged in.
