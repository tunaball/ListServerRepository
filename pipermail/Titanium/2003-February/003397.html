Just to inform the List that I installed this new update 
(MacOSXUpdate10.2.4.pkg) through Software Update, this morning, on my 
(RevA) Ti-500, without problem.

most of which was the final "optimizing system performance" activity.

Note that I did an incremental backup just before I ran this update.

So far, I have seen no specific differences.  Certainly nothing negative.
