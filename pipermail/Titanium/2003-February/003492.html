Derek - re 'cache' in mail.app -- deleting those pesky addresses.

Under Window, there's a choice ADDRESS HISTORY.

These are all or many of the addresses you have received email from 
(including spam too!)
and there is a button REMOVE FROM HISTORY.   I assume you can select 
them all (with
Command-A ?) and remove them all that way.

Good luck

David

PS  You can also pick and choose which ones you want to ADD to the 
address book from
the same ADDRESS HISTORY window
