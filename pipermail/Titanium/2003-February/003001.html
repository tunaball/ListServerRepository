Kevin - I have been using my Ti-500 for almost two years, for much 
more than 8 hours a day, almost every day.

My only modification is the Ti-Tote&Tilt handle, which (aside from 
making it safe and easy to move around, and providing cooling air 
space) slightly changes (improves) the screen height and position.

I LOVE the trackpad, and the keyboard.

I have never had any head, neck, arm or wrist ache.  To me, the Ti is 
very ergonomic.  HTH.
