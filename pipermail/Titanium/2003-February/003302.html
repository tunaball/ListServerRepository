On  Tue, 11 Feb 2003 11:52:25 -0800 Christoph Pistor <pistorc at sbcglobal.net>
wrote:

> Dear list,
>
> I would like to know whether or not it is now possible to connect a new
> flat panel LCD display to a generation one Ti-400 with any of the new
> adapters Apple came out with. If not is there any third party solution
> that is affordable?
>


The 1st generation Powerbooks have ATI Rage Mobility 128 (2xAGP) cards with
8 MB VRAM. They come with VGA ports (Max 24 bit 1280x960 non-mirror). So,
probably a 15" LCD monitor would be best. A 17" monitor would be wide
enough, but it would produce black bands top and bottom to fit the 960 dpi
maximum vertical resolution. Just make sure it is a VGA monitor, not a DVI
or ADC one.

The good news is that you can get 15" LCD monitors extremely cheaply. My
guess is that soon they will no longer be produced.
