David,

I would suggest an archive and re-install using the system DVD that  
came with your computer, then update in steps to your current system  
level using Software Update (or alternatively use the .dmg of the  
10.4.8 combo update that you hopefully saved for such an emergency  
following your last system update). If your firewire drives do not  
appear as expected after this step I would proceed to test all of  
your hardware using the latest version (4.04) of TechTool Pro. A  
failure of your firewire ports (this can happen) would lead me to  
purchase an inexpensive firewire PCI card as a work-around.  
Macsales.com has them.

Best wishes for your success,

J H H
