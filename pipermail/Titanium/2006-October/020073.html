On some TiBooks, the connection between the Motherboard and the CD  
drive comes loose. You can sometimes correct this (at least  
temporarily) by pushing up on the bottom plate of the unit about 6”  
to 8” back from the slot. I had a Powerbook that did the same as  
yours and this procedure worked fine.
