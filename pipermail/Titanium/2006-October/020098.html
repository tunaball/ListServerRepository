You could try resetting the PMU: http://docs.info.apple.com/ 
article.html?artnum=303319

Also, try a different Firewire cable, and a different Firewire  
device.  Be careful, because a bad Firewire cable (or device) can  
damage working devices.  It's a bit of a catch-22 sometimes; it's  
difficult to tell what's broken without plugging it in to something,  
but by plugging it in, you might break the other hardware.
