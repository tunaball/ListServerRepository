I made the switch from G4 Powerbook to G5 iMac in January. Now it may  
be the time to switch back.

I calculated the difference in cost between a 24” iMac with all the  
goodies (2.33 Ghz, Nvidia 7300 video, 500G drive, 2G RAM) and the  
bottom line really isn’t much different than the top 17” C2D MBP. Of  
course if I want a 22” or larger display, there goes another $1K or  
more. And one would have to factor in the bottleneck created by the  
smaller slower drive and the slightly slower ATI video card. But then  
there is the matter of the Express Card slot for expansion and a more  
flexible DVI External Video arrangement.

Is anyone thinking of grabbing one of these gems?
