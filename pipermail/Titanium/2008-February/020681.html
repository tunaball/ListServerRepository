I had noticed that Repair Permissions (whether I used Disk Utility or  
any other, 3rd party, software was always either very slow or seemed  
to have stalled.

I have found that using this command in Terminal is an effective way  
or repairing permissions, with a % progress report:

			sudo diskutil repairPermissions /

			(password required)

Just in case it is of help to anyone else with a laptop and Leopard.
