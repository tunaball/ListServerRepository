> I now need a replacement power supply, but I noticed that the power  
> supply on my Al1.67 doesn't have a compatible connector for my Ti.  
> Does this mean Apple have changed the power rating? Or just the  
> connector?
>

I use the same white Apple PowerBook AC adapter for both my Al 1.67  
and Ti 400 -- they're the same connector, and the electrical ratings  
are compatible. The only difference between the connectors for the  
newer white blocks and the older UFO adapters is that the newer one  
has a charging indicator LED inside. This LED functions correctly  
with the Ti 400 (orange for charging, green for charged).
