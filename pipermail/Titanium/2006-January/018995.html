Your anti-virus (VShield = Virus Shield) looks like it isn't  
compatible with 10.4.4.

Just uninstall it. Yes, you do open yourself up to the multitude of  
Microsoft Office viruses out there, but you could just not use Office  
(if that is an option), or turn off active scanning -- and just scan  
the documents before opening them for the first time.

Or uninstall Virus Shield and install ClamXav -- a free virus scanner  
(based on clamav) for Mac OS X.  <http://www.clamxav.com>
