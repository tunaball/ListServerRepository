Well, it does seem likely that something in the backup could be  
causing problems; obviously, make sure you are using the very latest  
version of your backup software.  Some people have also found that  
installing the Combo OS updaters over the newest version will  
sometimes resolve problems seen after installing the Delta  
installer.  You might try using a different drive.  Especially, if  
it's possible, something on a different interface.  Changing to  
Firewire 400 might be enough to stop it, or you could see if a USB  
2.0 drive has the same problems.  Or, just downgrade to 10.4.3.  ;-)   
Of course, you might want to run DiskWarrior on both drives (or at  
least Disk Utility), and repair permissions too.

You can find the 10.4.4 Combo installer here:
http://www.apple.com/support/downloads/macosxupdate1044combo.html

Personally, I haven't had any major problems; I synced my 2G iPod  
once with no problems, and I use a FireWire EyeTV every day...  But  
no backup software or external drives.  I did have an issue with  
SystemUIServer hanging about once a day; I deleted its preferences  
and rebooted and it hasn't had any problems since.  However, it could  
have also been caused by my iPod; some people have seen  
SystemUIServer hang with an iPod plugged in, but start working when  
the iPod is removed.  I didn't learn of that issue until after I took  
the iPod back to my office, and I haven't tested it since.
