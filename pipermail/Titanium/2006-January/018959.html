selling, and due to the expense of internal airport cards, I am providing a
PC Card Wireless adapter in each of the sales.  I have a stack of Lucent
Wavelan Bronze (BroadCom chipsets).  They work fine--at my home network.

couple local WiFi cafes, I get nothing.  No joy.  Not even an indication of
an available network.  The cafes are not encrypted--not protected.  Any
ideas as to the cause and effect of this dilemma?  Any ideas on how to
