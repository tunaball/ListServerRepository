Best suggestion for a spudger I've seen was a plastic guitar pick.

I make my own spudgers from some thin Lexan (polycarbonate) strips I have 
in my shop, I nip off a 4" chunk and sand the end into a slim wedge. When 
the end gets buggered up I just resharpen it.

Another good tool is a plastic putty knife, which usually cost all of 
$0.79 or so at the local hardware store. Sometimes they need reshaping, 
but that's what sandpaper's for.

Really any thin stiff wedge-shaped plastic thingy will do the job.
