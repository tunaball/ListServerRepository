Did you check the box that said something along the lines of "Load OS 
9 drivers" before the reformatting?  I've had the same problem - I 
wiped a Ti/500's HD and installed Panther - didn't check the OS 9

computer will still not boot into OS 9.

here: http://www.lafcpug.org/partitioning_osx.html
