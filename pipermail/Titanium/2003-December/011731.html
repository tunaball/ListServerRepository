In case you're worried about whether you're actually a Unix geek or 
not, there are a few ways to determine the severity of your 
affliction:

* You have Terminal in your OS X dock - one Unix geek point - curable

** You have Terminal set to launch instead of the Finder - two Unix 
geek points - serious but curable

*** You type >console in the username dialog so that you don't have 
to use a bothersome GUI - five geek points - probably incurable 
without extensive and lengthy therapy

**** You reformatted your hard disk and installed Linux - ten geek 
points - there is no known cure for this (it's "terminal" ;-))
