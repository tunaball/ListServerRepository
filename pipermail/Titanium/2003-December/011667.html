>> Back in OS X.2 there was a little program that increased the speed of Safari
>> for ADSL modems, by changing the size of the packets. I would love to have
>> this back now in OS X.3 but don't remember the name of the program. Can
>> anyone help please?
> 
> Add these lines in /etc/startup
> 
> sysctl -w net.inet.tcp.sendspace=65536 > /dev/null
> sysctl -w net.inet.tcp.recvspace=65536 > /dev/null
> sysctl -w net.inet.tcp.delayed_ack=0 > /dev/null
> sysctl -w net.inet.udp.recvspace=73728 > /dev/null
> 


Or go to Versiontracker for

Broadband Optimizer
