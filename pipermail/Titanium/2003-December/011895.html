I find that it is best to go the USB route. The Griffin (no relation) iMic
is a cheap, simple device that will accept both earphones out and mic in
using separate plugs. I use it with an Altec Lansing stereo earphone/mic
boom combo that works quite well (although the quality of the sound in the
earphones leaves a bit to be desired). The input plug has a switch that lets
you choose between high level (line) or low level (mic) setting so you can
record from other devices such as tape deck, turntable, audio mixer deck or
voice recorder as well as a low level mic.
