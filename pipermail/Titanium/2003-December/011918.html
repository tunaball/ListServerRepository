Works just as well with plain water.

It should however be on a lintfree cloth and very very hot (you should 
just about be in pain squezing out the excessive water). Have the cloth 
_only_ damp so that absolutly no water can get in around the edges of 
the screen. Just wipe ones, heat the cloth again and repeat until the 
screen is perfectly clean and finally wipe it with a lintfree dry 
cottoncloth.

The hotter you can keep the cloth the quicker the fat smear from the 
keyboard will go away. If you keep a cover on the keys you will need 
less work on the screen. When you finished the screen you can give the 
keyboard and handrest the same treatment. It will be so much nicer to 
work with afterwards. :-)
