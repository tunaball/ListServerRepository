> I'm looking for a suggestion of a good bluetooth keyboard & mouse that I
> can add to my Powerbook.

I have the Apple keyboard (2 of them) and they are very nice, though more 
pricey. They go for months on a set of batteries even without ever turning 
them off. Bluetooth mice, on the other hand, do not and may only last a 
couple of weeks. The MacAlley suggested pricing for the bundle looks pretty 
attractive. You might want to be patient since all other options will be a 
lot more expensive.

Operationally, they are really nice for those who move their Powerbooks 
around each day and like the full keyboard and mouse at their desks; less to 
plug in. However, they do take a few, or sometimes several, seconds to 
reconnect when the machine or the mouse sleeps or goes dormant, and that can 
be frustrating if you're impatient to get going right away.
