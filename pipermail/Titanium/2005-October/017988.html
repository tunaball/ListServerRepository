Yassou Chris. I live in Greece in the summer (Skopelos). I also have  
ISDN there. OTE (the telephone company) provided me with a Netcom  
modem. That's the equivalent to your Sagem.

I bought a Level1 router. This connects to my ISDN modem. I connect  
that to an airport for wireless connections. The reason I do that is  
that the Level 1 only supports low speed wireless (802.11b) whereas  
my airport supports higher speed.

The nice thing about the Level1 is that it also supports broadband  
over ADSL, so it may be what you're looking for,

Here is their website: http://www.level1.com/products.php
