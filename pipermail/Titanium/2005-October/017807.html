Well, you can try resetting Open Firmware, perhaps...
Reboot, hold down Command-Option-O-F immediately.  You'll be brought  
to a white screen with a command line.  Type "reset-nvram" and press  
return.  Type "reset-all" and press return.  The second command  
should reboot the computer.
HTH,
