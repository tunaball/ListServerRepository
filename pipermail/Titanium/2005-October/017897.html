I'm steering well clear of these iMacs. Two friends have the earlier 
revision G5 iMacs, and I can honestly say that they are the noisiest and 
ugliest (imo) Macs I have ever used. One of them has so much trouble 
dissipating heat (in London, not the warmest of climates) that it 
sometimes crashes while running overnight. If you live in a warmer 
climate, I'd check to see if Apple have done anything to improve the 
cooling performance on these models.

The first time i saw the case design, I was rather troubled that Apple 
was putting G5's in there after having used their original G5 Tower 
case. Personally I am saving up to buy a G5 tower, which does take up a 
lot of space, and is rather heavy, but runs without the excessive noise 
generated by the (smaller?) fans inside the G5 iMac.
