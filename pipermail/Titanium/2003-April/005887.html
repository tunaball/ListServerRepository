There are two 667 configurations, the TiBook 667 Gigabit Ethernet 
(first 667) and the TiBook 667 DVI (second 667). In addition to some 
system buss, cache and video memory differences, the DVI version lost 
the IR port found on the Gigabit Ethernet version. The DVI version also 
has a slightly higher resolution. There's a nice chart showing all the 
TiBooks on LowEndMac <http://www.lowendmac.com/pb2/g4-guide.html>.
