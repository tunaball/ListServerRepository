on 4/1/03 8:47 AM, Geno Endicott at geno at barbaloot.com wrote:

> What is the real difference between the 550/667 VGA or the 667 DVI other then
> video output.


The system bus is 133mhz, and a 1MB L3 cache was added. The graphics card
improved to 32MB, and screen resolution was significantly improved. Apple
also added an audio-in jack. The battery went from 50 Wh to 55 Wh.

The hard drives on the stock models were also larger.


Other than that, the powerbooks are pretty much the same. ;)
