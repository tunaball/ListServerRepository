For those having LaTeX (or TeXShop) installed there is also the 
command 'texexec' that does what was requested (combing jpg's) and 
much more. For PDF it is almost miraculous: selecting pages, 
reformatting, resizing, splicing and combining pages into one pdf 
file, etc. (see e.g. 
http://www.eskimo.com/~c/debian/tetex-manpages/texexec.1.html). With 
OS X's orientation towards PDF, this is a valuable tool. Eduard.
