I will depend on your network setup.  Shared Networking in VPC 6 uses 
NAT and DHCP for your guest operating system to get an IP address and 
get it routed to the outside world.  It appears to me that it only 
works with a specific private Class B netblock.  I am using the 
"virtual switch" since our entire network uses static ip addresses, and 
that was the easy one for me to set up.
