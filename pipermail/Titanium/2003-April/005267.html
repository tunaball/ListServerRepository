More than I realized... Anyway, regarding Kenwood TC, I can't bash them.  In
fact, they were helpful on my two service-related visits.  I took my ibook
and wife's Ti400 in for problems (broken latch on ibook and Ti failing to
recognize an airport card).  I was waiting at the door when they opened on a
Sunday and genius bar Tim spent well over 30 minutes diagnosing the problem.
I had already done fairly systematic troubleshooting of potential solutions
(all of which he verified).  The airport card problem was/is documented in
the apple tech database (where the next step is replacing the motherboard).
I got an applecare case number assigned, left the Ti, got it back the
following Wed.

I must agree that MicroCenter here is pretty good in terms of mac-related
hardware and if I need anything the same day that's where I go (although I
buy 90+% of my personal and corporate equipment from macmall and the like).
