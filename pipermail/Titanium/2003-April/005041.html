I just got one of these gizmos and downloaded the Mac software from Belkin.
I also downloaded a shareware product called "Gamepad Companion" which looks
pretty good. The software can be accessed in a panel in System Preferences
(rather than being the stand-alone application that Belkin supplies).

Just one thing that caught me out first time is that you have to make sure
you hit the start button after making your settings or nothing will happen.
You might have to make sure that all your settings will work in your browser
to access wwii properly.
