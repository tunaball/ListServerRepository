>I've been looking for a use for this PC Card slot for some time, but can't
>seem to find anything to put in it, I wondered, what does everybody else use
>it for. It feels like I'm wasting a tiny bit or PowerBook real estate!

I regularly use a Compact Flash/PC Card adapter to transfer images from my
digital camera to my TiBook.  This allows me to move the images without
using my camera, thus conserving the camera's battery.  Plus, it transfers
the images very quickly.  No software drivers are needed.  I just plug the
CF card into the adapter and then plug the adapter into TiBook.  It even
launches iPhoto automatically.

The CF/PC Card adapters can be purchased for as little as $10.
