According to Apple Knowledge Base article 58442 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=58442>, you have bad 
RAM or there is some problem reading the RAM (maybe off-spec speed?). 
However, the error beep/sleep light flashing is supposed to occur on 
startup, not on wakeup, so I don't know if this applies for your 
case. It's quite possible that something on the MLB is dying and is 
affect the RAM. I once had a blown external L2 cache make the RAM 
intermittently unreadable (or, could have been RAM going bad that 
destroyed the cache memory, because eventually, one stick of RAM 
completely died).
