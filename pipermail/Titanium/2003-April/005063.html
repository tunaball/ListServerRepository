Actually Microsoft's Bluetooth Mouse, in its current version, will not work
with anything but Win XP.  I actually bought one, and tried it.  I was able
to see it in the Bluetooth panel momentarily, but then it could not assign a
passkey for the Bluetooth to the mouse.  It is not built to do this, and
will not work.  Their mouse has no was to store the passkey, like a phone
does.  I tried several workarounds, even tried a call to MS tech support,
and they insisted that it would not work, but thought it was cool I tried.
They stated that is was only designed to work with Win XP.  So I returned
it.  They stated that soon there would be a mouse coming out that would be
compatible with other systems, including OSX but did not know a timetable.
There are other companies that are producing Bluetooth mice, etc...and some
maybe compatible with OSX upon their release.  Not sure on this...but I
would think that at some point in the near future, that there will be
several options available for OSX.   For the moment though, the MS mouse
will not work.
