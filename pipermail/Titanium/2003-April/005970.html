on 24/4/03 18:43,  coccolithophorid at earthlink.net wrote:

> Does anybody know how to take 10+ .jpg images and turn them into a
> multi page PDF document? I want to email a PDF file to someone with a
> PC and have them be able to open it using Acrobat and get the
> thumbnails and all pages.

I opened 6 .JPG images in Appleworks and pasted them as floating graphics on
to a blank 6 page Appleworks Wordprocessing document, then used Save as PDF
in the OSX Print Center to generate the .PDF file.

Acrobat reader gave thumbnails for each page.

Hope this helps.
