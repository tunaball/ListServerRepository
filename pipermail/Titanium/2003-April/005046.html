The one thing that still does not seem to have been addressed in any 
release up to 10.2.4 is the ability of the Finder to completely bog 
down the machine to the point where you have to do a hard reset.

Here's one for the masochists among us to try:

1) Mount an SMB share

2) Shut down or otherwise disable the file sharing on the remote machine

3) Double click on the (now unavailable) volume on your desktop

4) Tear out handfuls of your own hair in frustration

I've tried to educate myself not to get into this situation but it 
still happens occasionally, e.g. when I forget to dismount a share at 
work before putting the PowerBook to sleep and going home.
