Kevin - you did not say if you are running OS X or Jaguar.
PC card recognition on my Rev A Ti-500 was an issue for me, early in 
the life of OS X.
By 10.1.5 (if I remember correctly) the issues were being addressed.
I have never had any problem with it since Jaguar (10.2 ff).

When you say "cannot get my computer to recognize this or any other 
PC card" do you mean that it does not show up in the list, alongside 
modem, ethernet, etc, when you open Network Preferences?
Precise problem description would help in enabling others to provide 
advice on this kind of issue.

What I don't like in OS X is that PC cards do not show up on the 
desktop, as they do in OS9.
Somehow that is a reassuring visualisation that the card is recognised.
