Having installed 10.2.5 on my PB/800 I can now say a few things:

1. There have been significant changes to the Bluetooth implementation.  
Keyboard, mice, and "other" fields have been added, and I now get no  
dropouts on my T68i through Optus in Australia. Possibly in the  
meantime Optus improved its GPRS service, but this change occurred as  
soon as I installed the update. It did take a little fiddling to get it  
working but now I am very impressed with its stability - and speed.  
Approaching 56k dialup speed. Nat., Safari is used with inline images  
and pop ups set to off. Page loading is v quick. (Don't ask about cost  
as we pay by the k, not the minute.

2. My battery life has improved significantly after several recharge  
cycles. Didn't happen immediately, but over a couple of  
days....interesting.

3. No other conflicts or difficulties presented overtly.
