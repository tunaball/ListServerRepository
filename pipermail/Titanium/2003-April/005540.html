After 10.2.5 installation last week, I have run, Disk Utility, 
System Optimizer 4.0 and Cocktail.
I have restarted my Ti-500 several times, to see if I see any 
start-up time improvement with 10.2.5.
My fastest start-up time, post 10.2.5 installation, is 1 min 4 seconds.

This is not faster than before, but on the other hand, I have never 
measured a faster start-up.

I measure from when the Apple first appears until the first bounce of 
an application that starts at LogIn (that is - for me - Folding at 
Home 3.24).

My conclusion is that start-up  time is unchanged.
