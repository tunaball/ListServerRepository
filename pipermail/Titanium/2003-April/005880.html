>- Are any of you using more than 900 fonts? If so, what if anything

>- Can anyone recommend a good font repair/analysis tool for OSX?

I use more than that, but not at once, of course, I use Suitcase 10.2 
in OSX and OS 9. It started as a necessity for my work with Quark in 
OS 9. For normal numbers of fonts and typefaces I believe OSX has 
very good font management. I am happy with Suitcase, however I know 
some very serious design/layout people that believe Font Reserve to 
be superior to it, especially in OSX. I use it out of habit and like 
the way it works with either FontMenus [part of it], or Type Reunion 
[solo, with FontMenus disabled].
