I use a really simple, yet flexible program called Audio In.  It was 
the only free/cheap audio recording program that does play-through to 
the powerbook's speakers.  So I could record from a analog source (like 
a tape deck) and hear the track through the speakers or headphones.  
Has a couple of quirks, tho, so FWIW.
