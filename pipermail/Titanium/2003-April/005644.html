Wow.  Chalk one up for Shawn!  Good idea. And in reply to Trevor, MS Office on the Mac, from Office '97 and on, is absolutely compatible between Mac and PC.  I have used both Office '97 and Office X, and have never encountered a compatibility problem in either direction.  What your switcher-friend may have heard about was a minor issue with Appleworks, in which Appleworks would append the wrong file extension to a document saved as a Word file.  It was saving Word files with the Applework's ".cwk" extension rather than the Word ".doc" extension.  This is easy enough to fix by simply renaming the file.  But I only recall that happening with Appleworks...I have never seen any such thing when using Word on a Mac.

So, tell your "almost ready to be a switcher" to go ahead as there is compatibility.  I think someone is feeding him a line of the really runny stuff to keep him in the camp of the "dark side" (ooow, ominous).
