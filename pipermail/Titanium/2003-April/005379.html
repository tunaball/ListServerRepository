Using the new ABS as a print server is probably the most attractive new 
feature, other than price, to anyone with a small network and multiple 
users .  (Think here the family situation where there is one wired home 
computer running Jaguar with multiple accounts and various wireless 
laptops all all sharing a cable/DSL connection and printer).  
Otherwise, the machine that is directly connected to the printer must 
always be in a 'logged on' state to work as the print server.  This is 
also true if you use the computer to receive faxes.

To date, I know of no way to wirelessly connect to the ABS' modem and 
use it to fax either in or out.  I realize that faxing is really kind 
of dinosaur technology (I rarely use it myself anymore) when compared 
to email, but, I still have a few dinosaur clients who insist on faxes, 
so it would come in handy once in a while.

How about ABS as a fax server?  Apple are you listening?
