Not sure why there is so much misinformation about this. The rants may 
make one feel better, but obscure the facts.

Florida law (for better or worse) dictates that AppleCare is available in 
Florida only if you have a Federal Tax Identification Number.

have a problem in Florida, the computers are covered by AppleCare. That's 
contrary to reports I have read, but IS confirmed by Apple.
