Christopher,

> I've been looking for a use for this PC Card slot for some time, but can't
> seem to find anything to put in it, I wondered, what does everybody else use
> it for.

I use a PYRO 1394 card as a second FW bus on my 800MHz TiG4. I connect an
external FW HD to the built-in port & attach my DV cam to the PYRO. I capture
video directly from my cam to the HD this way. No dropped frames so far. ;-)

Good luck,
