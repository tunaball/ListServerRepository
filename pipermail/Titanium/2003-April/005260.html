You can buy an 802.11b PC Card for about $30.  It would sit in the PC Card
slot the same way the Ti Antenna does and would be easily removable to use
your card slot for other purposes.  The 802.11b PC Card's are available
with the same type of "stubby" antenna on the end as seen in the Ti
Antenna.  They provide enhanced range over the Airport card due to the
antenna hanging outside the case like that.

To top it off, you could then sell your Airport card or use it in another
computer.

I don't think there will be much of a market for these two antennas.  Are
you sure it's not a late April Fools joke?
