> read the older manuals it recommends to not run the computer with the 
> display shut in FireWire Target Disk mode, as it may overheat.  
> They've left this tidbit out of the newer manuals.  For whatever 
> reason, the cpu runs at 100% load in FireWire Target Disk Mode, and 
> the computer will get extremely hot after extended use as a Target 
> machine.  I believe, without checking, that they recommend having the 
> power brick plugged in in FireWire Target Disk Mode because of the 
> elevated power requirements.  Keep that in mind when using this 
> feature.
