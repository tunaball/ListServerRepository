Apple decided to do something really silly on this last release of 
Powerbooks. The "best" model (for my needs) is the one with the 128 Mb 
of VRAM (as opposed to 64) and this is only available in either the 17" 
(as standard) or in the 15" Superdrive as a "build to order" option.

For this reason the only way for me to get a 15" with 128 is to build 
to order. Due to the way they have laid out the product codes the 
Resellers seem to be in the same position. Smalldog, Micro Anvika, 
Computer Warehouse, jigsaw24 all have the 128 machine as a build to 
order option requiring Apple to build it (and hence slowing down the 
delivery).

Annoying but that's the way it is.

the US Apple Store and then it should ship by the time I arrive in the 
states :)
