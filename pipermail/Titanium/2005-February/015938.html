Firewire cables seem to die fairly often...
In fact, if your drive doesn't work, try another cable FIRST before you 
try another computer or cable. Broken FireWire cables can sometimes 
damage things. I heard of a guy who couldnt get a FW HDD to mount so he 
tried another computer, and another after that. He fried the firewire 
controllers on 3 computers.
