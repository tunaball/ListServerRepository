Hi Tim,

White iBooks all use the same adapter for vga, whereas the Powerbooks
changed when they went DVI (667 DVI models and above) - earlier model
Powerbooks had VGA out.  The later models will need a DVI to VGA adapter.
Also, the 12" Powerbooks use a Mini DVI to VGA adapter.  All the adapters
came with each machine, but obviously you can't guarantee people at the
conference will bring them :-)
