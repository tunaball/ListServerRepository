Latch is a $90 part. On eBay it goes for about $50. Essentially all you have
to do is remove the 8 or so screws on the bottom case and take the bottom
off. Remove the spring/retainer and pop the piece out. Pop the new one in
and replace the clip.

Small dog electronics had some used clips for $40-50 a while ago the may
have one to sell you. Either way their tech support is quite helpful, even
if you're not yet a customer. <www.smalldog.com>
