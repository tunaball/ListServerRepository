Apple will replace the unit if it's still under Applecare, which it probably 
isn't if it's a TiBook 550. They may replace it anyway, but are probably 
under no obligation to do so.
The power cord is a coaxial cable with a center conductor and a rather 
fragile shield mesh. It can easily get damaged from frequent twisting back 
and forth. There appears to be a failure of the center conductor insulation 
and a short occurred, probably with a frayed piece of the outer braid 
conductor.
