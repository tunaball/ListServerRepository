I like the 15" units. They have about the same features and performance and 
are more portable and certainly cheaper. If you're a big movie fan and are 
going to watch a lot of movies with it, or do some heavy graphics work, and 
you really don't need to carry it around much, the 17" screen is really 
great.

Alternatively, if you're looking for increased general screen real estate AND 
portability, a 15" unit with a second lower-cost 15" LCD display may be a 
good way to go for a similar price. I use a bit 19" LCD display for most of 
my work, with the 15" PowerBook screen off to the side to hold additional 
windows. But if I need to travel, the 15" is a nice lightweight relatively 
compact unit to grab and go.
