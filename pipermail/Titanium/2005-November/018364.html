There's plenty wrong with Word.  Most people are just used to it.  
WordPerfect is a much better and easier to use program.  Word tries too 
hard to be clever (i.e. If I make a list, Word decides to indent it.  Why?  
WP has had booklet printing for ages.  It's easy to left/center/right align 
text *on the same line* in WP, which can't be done in Word without making a 
table.  And I haven't even mentioned "reveal codes".

I'd encourage everyone to Google "AlmostPerfect" and read how WordPerfect 
was designed and built compared to Word.  It's a fascinating story about 
competing with Microsoft and how a few missteps can kill your app.
