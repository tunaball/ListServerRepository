I upgraded about 10 iBooks and only had one problem -- one had the  
Finder crash and restart continually upon login, continually taking  
and release window focus, making it difficult to do anything else.

Ended up booting in target mode, downloading combo update on host  
computer, and applying to target drive.  That fixed things.
