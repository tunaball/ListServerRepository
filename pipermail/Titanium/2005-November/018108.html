They should be in the Library/Widgets folder and not the User/Library/ 
Widgets folder. If you deleted them then that's probably why they  
appear in the disk utility window with special permissions needed. I  
haven't had them to show up yet.

Look for the .plist files for each deleted widget and remove the  
file. If they're still on your drive activate each once to reset the  
associated preference file and the special permissions parade should  
end.
