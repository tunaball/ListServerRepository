i had planned to keep my 2.5 year old 12" powerbook until the intel  
laptops would come out, but over the past months the screen size had  
become a problem as i was working more and more with cad and  
multimedia software (archicad & director). as we say in french " i  
cut the pear in two" and went for a second-hand 17" 1.5ghz powerbook  
(30% cheaper than a new one). i've been working on it for 4 weeks now  
and i can easily say that i could have gotten a new one. no regrets.

also this to say don't regret your purchase, you know what you  
bought. let the intel-transition go by (12-18 months?), let the  
software guys upgrade their products, and then buy a new laptop.  
unless i have huge amounts of cash coming in from my work, i see this  
as being in about 18 or 24 months from now.
