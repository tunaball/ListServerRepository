One of the burning points that I brought up in my earlier posts was
about the fact that there have been no g5 powerbooks.  This is
precisely what I don't understand about your argument, Chris.  You
seem to say that IBM has PowerPC processors that are faster, smaller,
cooler, than anything else - like some kind of wonder processors
straight from the divine, but no one has seen these used in practical
applications.  Also, as far as the points you've made, such as the
