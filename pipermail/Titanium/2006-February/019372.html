iTunes is on my Powerbook.

My library is on my Dell running Windows XP.

I mount my Dell's HD via Windows Sharing.

The benefit here is that if I do get some new music or a new video to  
add, I can always manually drag it to iTunes library and sync it to  
my iPod even when the Dell isn't on or connected.  Then later I move  
the new file to the Dell.


I found that I prefer using my iPod rather than iTunes anyway, and  
use the iPod dock's Line Out to connect to my office speakers when  
not at home.
