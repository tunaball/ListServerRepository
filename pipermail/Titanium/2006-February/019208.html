There are tools for localizing apps, one of which your developer and you
should be using:

1.  AppleGlot and ADViewer, available free from Apple.  Very cumbersome
compared to #3 and #4.  In my opinion, an Excel spreadsheet is better.  Do
not use.

2.  PowerGlot.  Heard it was expensive, never tried it, never heard anything
bad about it.

3.  iLocalize by Arizona Software, $39.

4.  Localization Suite by Blue Technologies, free/donations.

I've tried #3 and #4.  They both have their advantages and disadvantages.
