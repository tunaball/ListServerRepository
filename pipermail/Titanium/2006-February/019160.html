On Feb 3, 2006, at 12:28 PM, Tom Klinkowstein wrote:

> Anyone know what providers and wireless services / cards will work  
> with a G4 Powerbook and what modem scripts they require (and  
> whether those scripts are in OSX)?

I used to have a Verizon card for my PowerBook that worked well (with  
the drivers already in OS X).  It provided about double the speed of  
dialup connections, however the Verizon coverage didn't fit my travel  
patterns.

So I now have a USB link to my cell phone and have wireless internet  
service with Alltel.  The speed is a little better (usually around  
300 Kbps), and it also required no additional software.  Alltel's  
Axcess MobileLink Broadband service also has spotty coverage  
(depending on which network you're on and what their capabilities are  
when roaming), but seems to better fit my travel patterns.
