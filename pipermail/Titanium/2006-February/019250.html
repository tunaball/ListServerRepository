My daughter's Ti 800 running 10.2.8 developed a similar problem where

administrator privileges", or something like that, would appear each 
time. BIG problem for a college girl. I took the machine with me, tried 
all the known tricks, scanned (Virex) for viruses, used TechTool Pro...nada!
