I have successfully used a number a number of different PCMCIA cards  
that are Altheros based (rather than Broadcom like the Airport  
Extreme card). You need the right software to do this, however. The  
OrangeWare Wireless Driver software has just been upgraded to 3.2 and  
is available at:

<http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/22297>

I have been using it with a 3Com Card for awhile now and it works  
fine. The driver installs in a System Prefs pane and has many more  
settings and readouts than are available in Tiger for Airport.
