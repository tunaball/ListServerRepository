If cost is not an option, Timbuk2 messenger bag (built to order) with 
the padded sleeve insert (Timbuk2  has one padded insert for every 
laptop model that apple sells, the one I got for my Ti 867 fits 
perfectly). If on a budget, Timbuk2 also makes a hybrid bag actually 
designed for laptops, so no laptop sleeve, instead if has a pocket for 
the laptop. Note of warning: the hybrid bag (they call it the 
"commuter") is not water proof. A friend of mine had his manuals soaked 
last week under moderate rain. Mine has a second waterproof liner 
sewn-in at the factory and is absolutely water proof.

I used a back pack when I had an iBook but people at the DC Metro rail 
get pissy when you have back packs that stick out too much. The 
messenger backs are starting to become popular because they hang with a 
lower profile and you can always swing them to your front without 
taking it off your shoulder.
