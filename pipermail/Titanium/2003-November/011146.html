Hi Les

The 203.113.195.xxx is the IP address and the number that comes after 
the : is the port number that the application is attempting to 
communicate across. Somewhere you have a firewall that is blocking 
ports 33286 and 5060.

Have a read through the Apple knowledge base article [ 
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=93208 ] which gives you 
all the info on which ports to unblock to get iChatAV working. Remember 
than unblocking ports does make your Ti and or network less secure.
