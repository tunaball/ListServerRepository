On Shawn's recommendation, I bought one of these cloths and tried it on my
800 MHz powerbook.  Result: Yes, I was able to buff out all the marks from
the keyboard, but I was not able to buff out the trackpad outline.  The
trackpad outline is even visible when the screen is lit.

My conclusion is that they are indentations, but they can be buffed out as
long as they are not too deep and sharp.  The trackpad leaves deeper and
sharper indentations, since it is a much sharper edge than the key corners.

Anyhow, thanks Shawn I needed a new cover anyhow.
