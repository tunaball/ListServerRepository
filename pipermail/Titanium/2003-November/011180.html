Finally, i solved the problem: I had a second monitor attached to the TI.
Therefore,
the Graphic Card assigned to the TFT and the Monitor each 4 MB RAM (the
Graphics-
Adapter in the 500 MHz has only 8 MB Ram). Unplugging the Monitor, restart
the TI
and the DVD-Player works!
