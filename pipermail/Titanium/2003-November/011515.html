Hi all:

Sorry for the belated posting -- a few months ago I asked for and 
received advice from you all on HD upgrades for my TiPBG4-800 (it came 
with a IBM Travelstar 40GB that had crashed hard...)

I purchased a 60GB Toshiba MK6022GAX (5400RPM, 16MB cache) from a 
vendor on eBay and I'm very pleased with the results.  Although I could 
not run empirical back-to-back tests between the Toshiba and the 
Travelstar, the Toshiba seems to be as quiet.  When running OS 10.2.x, 
the Toshiba seemed to provide faster system performance.  It was a 
painless installation.

YMMV

Jim
