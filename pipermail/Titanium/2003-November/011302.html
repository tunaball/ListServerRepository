Just watch out; you might not need the power adapter, but rather the 
plug on the computer might be loose.  One way (but not definitive) to 
test, is to hold the plug in different directions; if it lights up when 
you hold it up or down or something, the connection might just be loose 
on the computer end.  Also, you should unplug the power adapter from 
the wall; the 65W adapters will shut off as a safety measure if the 
voltage drops too much, as it can if it's on the same circuit as a 
refrigerator or some other similar appliance.

If it is the plug, you'd have to send the computer in to Apple for 
service.
