You need either a new logic board or a new display.  Give Apple a call, 
and they'll have it fixed for you in no time.  Until then, you might be 
able to use the computer with an external display; however, if it's the 
logic board that is messed up, you'll see the same video artifacts on 
both displays.  From your description though, it sounds more likely 
that you just need the display; one of the wires in the video cable 
probably got pinched or something.
