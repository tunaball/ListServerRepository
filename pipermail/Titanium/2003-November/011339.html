As a point  of interest I discovered almost all of the internal screws 
loose on my 500mhz tibook after about 2.5 years of use, both under the 
keyboard and after removing the bottom.  I was trouble shooting an 
intermittent shutdown.  This PB had not been anywhere for 
servicing--other than me replacing the HD, DVD optical disk and 
Memory--none involving those loose screws.  I do travel a lot and the 
machine has gone between home and work every day.  (My trouble shooting 
did not work and applecare replaced the battery wiring harness.)

Jesse Proudman wrote:

> I solved my overheating issue.  It had nothing to do with overheating at 
> all.  Apparently, the last time I returned my laptop to apple for 
> service, they never tightened down all the screws under the keyboard all 
> the way.  What was happening was keys on the keyboard were then hitting 
> this screw and some how put the computer to sleep.  I tightened the 
> screw and the problem went away.
