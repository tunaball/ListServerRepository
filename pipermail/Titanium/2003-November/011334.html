I solved my overheating issue.  It had nothing to do with overheating 
at all.  Apparently, the last time I returned my laptop to apple for 
service, they never tightened down all the screws under the keyboard 
all the way.  What was happening was keys on the keyboard were then 
hitting this screw and some how put the computer to sleep.  I tightened 
the screw and the problem went away.
