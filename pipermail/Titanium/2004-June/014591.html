> I've tried to use the battery to let it down to 94% and then recharge

I believe (and I'm certain someone knows more about this than me) that 
the battery actually trickles down to the range of 95% or so even when 
plugged in and then automatically charges back up to 100%, showing that 
the battery is charged all the way.  It continually repeats this cycle, 
charging to 100%, trickling down to 95%, then charging back up again, 
as long as it is plugged in.

As to why this happens, I don't know.  But that behavior seems normal 
based on what I've heard and/or read.
