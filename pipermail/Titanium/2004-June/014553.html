back in the late 90s there hadn't been a virus for Mac for about 7 or 
8 years; most Mac users quit using and/or updating their virus 
software, so a relatively easy to contain virus became pervasive very 
quickly.  Based on discussions and contacts through out the Mac 
world, I would venture to guess that OSX is currently in a similar 
phase.  An OSX virus would probably get as much, if not more 
publicity than a Windows virus right now, and if malicious, could 
affect millions of people.
