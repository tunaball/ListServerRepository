Well do you need OS 9 boot for anything?  I use a couple of programs 
that aren't Classic friendly (and there is no OS X version of) so I 
have to boot back in to OS 9.  That is the only real reason to hold on 
to a Ti that  I can think of.  Unless you are like me and really, 
really, REALLY like your ports in the back, but that's another story.

Have you looked at this site http://www.pbfixit.com ?  They have the Ti 
display, casing and all for $800.00 and $600.00 (the cheaper one is 
"b-stock" and may have some keyboard marks on the screen and or minor 
scratches on the casing according the their site).  I ordered a 
WallStreet keyboard from them a few months ago.  It was used and looked 
it with the typical wear marks but it worked.

But at any rate look around to some other sites too and you might be 
able to find the screen + casing combo for cheaper than the arm and a 
leg that Apple wants to charge I'm sure. ;-)
