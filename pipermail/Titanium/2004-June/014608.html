>So my TiBook (1ghz, superdrive, 1gb RAM) isn't seeing PC Cards!  any

Hi Sam,

Check the System Profiler, at least to see if the Ti-Book 'thinks' it 
has a PCI circuit. It probably does, since your installed video card 
runs on PCI. (or maybe they don't on the 1 Gigs?

Anyway, just use Apple System Profiler (or 'About This Mac'' under 
Apple menu, click 'More Info').

When my Firewire port was fried (due to Apple's neglect to include a 
breaker on the firewire port previous to the 800mHz Ti-Books), the 
Firewire circuit no longer showed up in ASP. I see 'firewire' in ASP, 
now, because I have a firewire card installed in the PC slot.

How many cards did you try in the slot? If only one, was a device 
driver needed/installed? And, does the card icon show up in OS 9 
(assuming you can boot up in 9)?
