I've used the standard automotive DC to AC converter, which has a 
regular AC receptacle. I then plug my PB brick into it.

It's been OK, but I would have preferred a single powerbrick that 
connects to the cigarette lighter and then into my laptop. It would be 
neater, and I wouldn't have to share the single outlet with the DVD 
player on long trips : )

YMMV, but not much as it doesn't put a lot of strain on the alternator
