I forgot to mention: The PCI circuit is listed at the top of the 
"Devices and Volumes" tab in Apple System Profiler. (Apple menu/About 
This Mac/More Info.../Devices and Volumes).

With the card inserted correctly, and any needed drivers installed, a 
'box'  saying, 'PC Card' should appear connected under the PCI 
section of ASP.
