> - STM Loop: a bit too large?

I bought mine from RadTech:
http://www.radtech.us/Products/Backpacks.aspx
(They'll ship overseas it seems)
I was thinking that it was going to be quite large, but actually it 
doesn't seem so big on my back.  I believe the Brenthaven is longer, 
but the Large Loop might be a little wider.  The bag has LOTS of space. 
  Numerous compartments for different things.  The laptop section (with 
cool self-healing zipper) seems that it was not designed SPECIFICALLY 
for the 17" powerbook, but it fits nicely and is well padded.  Granted, 
I haven't used it extensively yet (the trip it was purchased for is in 
a few weeks-- http://www.2004wwuc.org/ if anyone's interested ;-) ), 
but just wearing it around a bit it was very comfortable on my 
shoulders and back.

Most importantly, the price was right.  While most of the backpacks are 
$150+ (USD), this one is priced under $100.
