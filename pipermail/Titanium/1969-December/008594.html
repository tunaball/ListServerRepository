I use the Logitech Dual Mouseman Optical.

This mouse fits in my hand well, glides well, and has noticeably 
improved tracking (in gaming situations) due to its superior dual 800 
dpi sensors.

It can feel a little heavy at first, so take the screws off the bottom, 
and remove the erroneous piece of steel they obviously put inside the 
body just to add weight.
