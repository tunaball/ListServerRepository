If it sounds like the disk is trying to eject. A lot of computers have  
the drive slot slightly bent down in the center Try inserting the tip  
of a small screw driver or other flat object into the slot opening and  
twisting to bend to slot back up. This has worked on many drives I get  
in the shop for a stuck disk.
