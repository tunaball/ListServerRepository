In the past it has been suggested that perhaps the mail is going to some address that is forwarded to your address. A friend could have set you up this way at the beginning. I can't say that this is your problem or a real problem but it seems possible.

My list email shows as being sent To

<"PowerBook G4 Titanium List" <Titanium at lists.themacintoshguy.com>

 When I expand the headers ("BLAH BLAH" button in Eudora) I see one header that gives a clue as to myself being the recipient:

Received: from mail.ninewire.com (208.135.98.4) by mail.woz.org with SMTP
 (Eudora Internet Mail Server 3.2.4) for <steve at woz.org>;

There might be a way for the list administrator to cause the true recipient to be shown in the To field or the Body of the email. Alternatively, the list admin could occasionally send an email explicitly saying who it is being sent to, as a check for recipients. But you might have to wait half a year if this was done.
