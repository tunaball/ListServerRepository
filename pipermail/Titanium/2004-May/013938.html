Well, of course it must reconnect after a reboot!  I believe your complaint
is that it does not *automatically* reconnect.

Look at a couple things.  First of all, System Preferences > Network > Show
> Network Port Configurations and make sure that Airport is checked and is
at the top of the list.  Then, back up to Show > Airport, and set By
default, join > Automatic.
