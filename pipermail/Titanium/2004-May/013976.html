What I find interesting in the Barefeats Web site is that when you click on
the link for information about the MK8026GAX it takes you to the Toshiba
site where you find that the only drive that Toshiba U.S.A. does NOT provide

MK8025GAS drive but not the corresponding GAX drive. To find out about this
drive you have to do a search and go to some European or Asian sites, and
even there I have yet to find an actual spec sheet that one can download.

If anyone can find a decent PDF specification sheet on the new drives, I
