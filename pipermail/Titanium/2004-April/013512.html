An additional tip for anyone doing this without this type of deck. I go from 
VCR to the Canopus ADVC-100 digital/analog converter then into the laptop 
and onto a mounted Lacie 200GBHD. It locks the audio to the video so you 
never have missteps like you'll find going other ways. It's a tiny $300 box 
but it works like a charm. Highly recommended.
