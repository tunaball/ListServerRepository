2004-04-09 kl. 18.27 skrev Da Pen:

> Can I just swap the extensions folder
> from the old one to the new one to make this work?
---------
Don't do that! Their should be very few if any extensions in your 9.1 
that the classic version of 9 would like in your new OS 10 version.

Move your documents over to the new PB. Reinstall only those 
applications that you can't do without and cant afford to update to OS 
10 just yet. If you have very special preferences for thoose you may 
want to try and swap the preference for just that program . But don't 
do that until you decided  you can't bear with the one the program 
recreates when installed.

If you have a printer, camera etc which had special extensions 
installed in OS 9, try them without doing anything special. If it 
doesn't work with what is supplied by the OS 10 go to the producers 
site and get new drivers.

Search versiontracker.com for updates of shareware and freeware you 
used in OS 9 and think you can't do without. If you can't find them, 
rather ask us what you're missing and we may be able to suggest a 
program in OS 10 which does similar actions.

Dont install anything old applicationwise if you don't have to. It only 
cause potential trouble. :-)
