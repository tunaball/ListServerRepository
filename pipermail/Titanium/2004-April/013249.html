Sambouka typed this message on 4/7/04 6:25 AM:

> I can't seem to shut my TiBook anymore (the hook is not holding).
> Hence, i cant put my mac to sleep when closing it.
> 
> Any tips on how i can fix it?

Check to see if your keyboard has come loose. Sometimes if it has not been
secured with all tabs in their slots and the locking tab in place, the
keyboard can lift out in a corner or edge and cause the top not to shut
properly.

Also make sure something gritty or sticky hasn't lodged itself into a
crevice somewhere that the surfaces should meet - that could be preventing
the book shutting all the way.
