>From MacMinute - http://www.macminute.com/

 Apple increases performance of PowerBooks
 April 19 - 08:38 EDT   Apple today announced a new line of PowerBook G4
notebooks with faster PowerPC G4 processors running at up to 1.5 GHz; faster
graphics performance; and a 4x SuperDrive (DVD-R/CD-RW) for burning
professional-quality DVDs. In addition, all new PowerBook G4s come standard
with AirPort Extreme 54 Mbps 802.11g pre-installed for wireless networking
right out of the box. The new 12- and 15-inch PowerBook G4 models are
available immediately, and the new 17-inch PowerBook G4 is expected to be
available in early May. Pricing starts at US$1,599.
