You'll likely need to get your file into an audio program and downmix 
to mono (granted thats' a pretty big work around.)  Or there is a 
program called Detour from Rogue Amoeba 
(http://www.rogueamoeba.com/detour/) that might do it--couldn't find 
anything on the website.  The only other thing I can think of is to 
find a mono mini-plug and then when putting a stereo headphone jack 
into it it would do a basic mono mix.  Or check out some DJ 
sites--people who what to hear a mix into one headphone.
