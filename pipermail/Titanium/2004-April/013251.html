>Hello, I'm the new on the list. As a new infected Titanium Powerbook 
>G4 (ex-win) user, I'm interested which method you use to set your TI 
>inactive? I'm work on it quite frequently during the day so I wonder 
>if it's OK to put TI in the sleep mode without shutting down?

I never shut down my PowerBook unless I will not use it for more than 
3 days.  I routinely get 2-3 weeks of uptime before security updates, 
etc require me to restart.  You'll lose approximately 1% battery 
charge for every hour your machine is sleeping (depending on the 
battery life).

Hope this helps,
