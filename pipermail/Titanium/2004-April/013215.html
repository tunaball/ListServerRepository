2004-04-02 kl. 17.14 skrev Murray E.Milligan:

> Added a friend's email address to my junk mail list. Is there a way I 
> can edit his email out of the junk mail list?
--------
Try this

Mail Preferences
Choose Junk Mail button (or Tab)
Click the button Advanced

You will likely find that the rules have a line regarding you friend 
and my removing it (press the Minus button) you should get him back in 
your normal mailing list.
