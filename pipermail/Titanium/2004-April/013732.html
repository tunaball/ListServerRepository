I used to brag to my friends about getting approximately 2 hours of power on
my powerbook. Heck, I used to remember the days of showing off my Lombard
and 2 batteries.

After using Panther, I'm down to 1.5 hours. I can't live without Panther now
but am disappointed by the life of the battery now. Can't finish a 2 hour
movie. Yep, the hack "Silent Night" doesn't work on Panther either.

Yes, I've reset the PRAM, conditioned the battery, set processor speed to
reduced, and dim the backlight to as low as tolerable, and quit unneccessary
applications but it would be nice to have a little longer battery time.

I've been looking at some of those external batteries that appear to fit
under your laptop. Has anyone tried any one of those and have any
recommendations?

I remember visiting a place that had a battery that can also be used to
connect multiple devices like an iPod, cellphone, PDA, or whatever device at
the same time. I can't remember the name of the company. Any leads?
