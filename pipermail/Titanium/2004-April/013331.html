IMNSHO, Steve Wozniak stands as a beacon in a sea of overblown egos in 
an industry that needed the ego of a Steve Jobs in promoting the 
concept. But Steve Wozniak is the true soul of the entire industry and 
his every action since those early days earns him the respect he so 
richly deserves. We don't have enough Steve Wozniaks and it is really 
gratifying to see him recognized. Too often the form takes precedence 
over the substance. Woz, you're an inspiration and a shining example of 
true greatness, and I feel truly honoured to be on a mailing list with 
you.

Murray, in Montreal
