&gt;After having an AlBook (15.2&amp;quot; 1.25Mhz) for a few weeks, there
&gt;are a couple of things that really bug me about it.
&gt;
&gt;1) Why is the power adapter and cord white?  It looks like I lost
&gt;mine and am borrowing my wife's iBook adapter.  They should at least
&gt;have the cord/connecter near the computer match the computer cased
&gt;and be silver.
&gt;
&gt;2) The power cord sticking out on the left is really annoying.  I
&gt;use my PowerBook on the right armrest of my home theater seating all
&gt;the time and the cord sticks out over my cup holder.  I guess I
&gt;could change seats and put it on the left, but it just doesn't
&gt;belong sticking out the side like that.
&gt;
&gt;3) The sleep light on the display latch is WAY too bright.  I could
&gt;use it as a lighthouse.  I have used it as a flashlight.  When I'm
&gt;watching a movie and close the lid on my PowerBook this bright white
&gt;light keeps shining in my eyes. The bright power cord charge status
&gt;light is similarly annoying, but I can easily rotate it out of
&gt;sight.  Anyone have a suggestion for a semi-permanent way to dim
&gt;these?  I was thinking of writing on them with a Sharpie or
&gt;something.
&gt;
&gt;Don't get me wrong, I love this machine, but those couple of little
&gt;things just seem un-Apple-like.
