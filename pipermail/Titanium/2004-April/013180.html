ChronoSynch really works well keeping two computers synched. It just takes some time to create actions you want. Be careful of whether you are doing a right to left / left to right or bi-directional synch. I recommend you do a trial before synching and examine exactly in advance what is going to happen until you are sure of how  the app works. It is pretty cool.
