Based on growing up with a father in the aluminum industry:

Actually, aluminum oxidizes very quickly when exposed to air.  Aluminum
oxide is harder than aluminum, so the fact that it oxidizes quickly is a
good thing.

I'm actually happy that Apple went with aluminum -- my TiBook felt fragile
in comparison to my AlBook.
