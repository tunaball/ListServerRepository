> Once you get it on DV Tape just use iMovie to import it into a 
> project, clean it up, and either:
>
> "share" as a DV quality, Quicktime movie.
>
> Once you have that file you can use a superdrive to put the file on a 
> DVD and play it on you Mac.
>
> OR
>
> You can export it to iDVD from iMovie to make a DVD that will play on 
> on your DVD player.
>
> So on the Mac side you need a SuperDrive, iMovie and iDVD (or a copy 
> of iLife '04).
