Few days ago, can't remember where, I read about mass laptop 
confiscation somewhere in America due the use of piracy software (?).  
Guys, have you ever so anti/piracy police acting? Here in Serbia, we 
made our first steps in world of regular legal software distribution so 
I’m interested in methods police use in detecting illigal programs in 
western countries. I’m afraid we should invest another sum from state 
budget for training computer-educated detectives. At the moment our 
caps can’t make a difference between OS X and Win operative systems  
Every Sunday between 08-11 in the morning, in the park near the Student 
Cultural Centre in Belgrade you can still freely purchase all possible 
Windows software (3 Euro per CD!). It’s a quite good deal if you're 
going to use such a software at the crappy Windows based China machine 
in quietness of your home but if you are among 4% of Serbian population 
that use Mac’s (mobile machines) you’ll be on the target constantly 
whenever you go with it.
