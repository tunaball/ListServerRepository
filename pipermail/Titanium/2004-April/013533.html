Malcom,

I upgraded my TiPB 500 from a 48Gb/5400RPM Travelstar to the 60/7200, and an
VERY happy with the results. This is a MUCH, MUCH quieter drive than the old
48, and data moves quite a bit faster. I do a lot of photoshop work as well
as large website management, and everything seems to be much quicker. What's
interesting is that the 48Gb was an upgrade from an older, smaller 7200RPM
drive, with a smaller buffer, and the speed really didn't decrease that
much. But going back up to 7200RPM with 8Mb buffer made quite a difference.

I'm quite happy that I did this (and now I have a 48Gb drive I can use CCC
to clone to for backups.... :)
