After spending about a day figuring out how to connect my LG VX4400 cell phone to the PowerBook 17" to use it as a modem, I thought I'd share the answers.

Connecting to the Verizon Quick 2 Net (formerly known as Mobile Office) network is cheaper (uses existing air time minutes) but is slower. Connecting to Express Network is faster but requires extra subscription cost.

---

Express Network: 144 kbs     USB cable or Sierra PCMCIA AirCard
Unlimited use = $79.99 @ mo
Account Name: myphonenumber at vzw3g.com
Password: vzw 
Telephone: #777
Modem Script: 1_VZW_Generic_1X_ON_230400

---

Quick 2 Net (or Mobile Office): 14.4 kbs
Account Name: qnc
Password: qnc
Telephone Number: #777
Modem Script: Verizon Quick 2 Net

---

Verizon Mobile Office Support 800-922-0204

USB Driver: ProlificUSBSerialOSX1.0.6.pkg
