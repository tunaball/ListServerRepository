My HD (same as yours) runs at ~49c most of the time. It's only if the 
CPU temp gets near 70c that the fans start to ramp up and they drop 
back right away with the temp.

activity since 10.3.8.

difference in CPU temp and fan activity.

I leave mine on 'High' because the fans are so unobtrusive.
