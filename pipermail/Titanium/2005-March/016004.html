With a buffalo card no, you can't. You have to pull the airport card.

But with a Linksys and some other wireless cards, you do not have to pull
the airport card to make the other card work.  Once the new PCMCIA wireless
card is recognized, I just turn off the airport card in the menu bar, and
then the linksys card I use works fine.  I use the Linksys card to extended
range when out and about or I have a bit weaker connection with the internal
airport card.
