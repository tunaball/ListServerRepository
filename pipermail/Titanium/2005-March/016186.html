Sorry to be coming in so late on this thread. I burn multiple copies 
all the time with Toast using just 1 drive (internal supper drive) and 
just inserting 1 copy disk. Maybe because I'm using version 6.0.9 of 
Toast. Any way I hit the copy button on Toast, then insert disk and the 
screen that comes up has the option to change from 1 copy to how many 
you wish. Then I just hit record and feed in a new disk when asked. 
Hope this helps.
