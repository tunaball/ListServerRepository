Ive burned 2 copies at the same time one with internal drive and one 
with external FW drive. IVe done this with both Toast 5 but not 6 yet. 
(there used to be directions on Roxios web site).

With Toast 5 duplicate the application toast so one is labeled toast 
copy in the same folder. Open them both choose the different burners - 
burn away - copy the disk or file to your HD first.

Toast 6 should work the same.  You may not be able to do anything else 
at the same time or you will get a failed burn - experiment and see. 
With a Dual processor G4 ive had better results burning two at the same 
time (compared to a single processor) while multi tasking but too much 
multitasking as disk access is what likley causes the failed burn, as 
the processors do not max out.
