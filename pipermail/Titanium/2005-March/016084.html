> In the old Ti-500 days, I have got several such stuck CDs out with the 
> aid of a business card, to help the CD eject.   Slide the card half 
> in, under the CD, try to eject, na dhte CD sides out over the card.


I used to have similar problems. There was a "CD" release catch towards 
the right hand side of the slot opening, a paper clip straightened and 
push in there would force the computer to eject.

Other options are starting up the computer with the mouse button held 
down.

If the problem is not the mechanism for ejecting, but that the CD is 
getting "stuck" in the actual physical slot as it slides out, Trevor's 
business card technique is best.
