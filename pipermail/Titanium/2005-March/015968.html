You can get an Airport (not Airport Extreme) card and it goes in an 
internal slot inside the case, not in the PCMCIA slot.  It's slower, 
but completely concealed in the case (and takes advantage of built-in 
antennae in the case).

As for an actual third-party PCMCIA card, you'll need external drivers. 
  I know they're out there, but I'm not familiar -- perhaps someone else 
can help you there.
