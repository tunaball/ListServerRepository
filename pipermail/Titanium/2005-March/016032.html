Linksys 802.11b WPC11 PCMCIA Card
ver.  1.0, 2.5 and 3.0 only
With Ioxperts Drivers

I have also tried and assortment of other cards from time to time, borrowed
from friends and colleagues.   I like being able to switch on the fly
between the PCMCIA card and the internal airport at will, whereas with a
Buffalo card you have to take the airport card out.
I find the range is better with the PCMCIA card.  I use it when I seem to be
on the edge of a network...need a better signal than the airport card can
provide inside the PB.
