First of all, I'd like to thank everyone who shared their Titanium
experiences. I would have responded sooner but I dumped a large mug of
coffee on my iMac's keyboard just after I sent the initial posting and had
to run to the Apple store for a new one. It's always a tough choice when the
two products are so amazingly great. I think that I'm just going to wait

Tibook. I'll be using the new Powerbook in a wireless classroom setting

Extreme combined with the better antenna placement on the aluminum models is
making me lean in their direction.
