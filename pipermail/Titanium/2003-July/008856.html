> Any specific Sprint cell phone recommendations?

I would recommend the Handspring Treo 300 (it's a Palm/phone combo).  I 
love it because you can access the net right from the phone too.  Plus, 
I HATE carrying two devices.  One beef... don't put it in a pocket with 
change or anything else, or get a carrying case.  Mine is all scratched 
up because I didn't do that.  It works just fine, it just looks like 
hell.

You will need a wireless modem driver.  I recommend the one at 
www.notifymail.com.  It is well worth the money.

If you travel a lot, I would by a USB cable that charges the phone as 
you use it, otherwise you'll have a dead battery quickly if you use the 
'net a lot. You can get those for about $10 on ebay.  I got mine from 
www.eforcity.com

> Do I need a special PCMIA card? If so what do I ask for at a Sprint 
> store or
> do I have to purchase that elsewhere?

The PCMCIA cards are way cool, but alas, they are PC only.

> Do I need to purchase a special Sprint plan to enable this?

You will need a plan with unlimited PCS Vision.  That enables this to 
happen and you don't get charged extra for using the internet.

Now, I have heard around the net that Sprint may be limiting access to 
the internet using the phone as a modem.  When I bought my phone, that 
was one of the selling points from the salesperson.  I would make sure 
and ask the question.
