>ronnie

Upgrading to 2.6 is form 2.1 is many bug fixes and performance 
improvements. Running software update or downloading the update from 
Apple's website will not require you to delete anything after the 
update is complete.

Prior to updating it is a good idea to boot from the OSX CD and when 
the intaller window is up, go to the menu bar and select Disk 
Utility. From that app run Disk First Aid. Then quit, which will 
reboot the computer from the hard disk.

After upgrading and restarting your computer, you may want to open 
the app called Disk Utility in your Applications -> Utilities folder 
and click on the button "Repair Permissions"
