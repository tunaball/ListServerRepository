I am using VPC6 on the new PB 17".  It seems to work a little better 
than the previous version running on OS 9.2 on my G4 Tower.
I find in general that VPC seems to be OK for simple applications. 
As soon as you get into complex systems, VPC cannot handle it.  Maybe 
on the new G5 there would be some hope.
Even on the PB, the systems it fails to work well for me are
1) Yahoo messenger trying to use the "Talk" feature.  None of the 
audio is clear although it is trying and some words do get thru.
2) We use a financial application called Banner which is designed to 
work only with Windows and only with IE.  I can get it to start, but 
after that it will not function at all.  VPC just cannot handle the 
amount of activity required over the network.
I am running OS 10.2.6  and have 1 GB of RAM.
