Pros:
Mac OS X -- A  user and System Administrator's dream with some great 
software available
Location Manager -- allows me to switch locations (between 8 that i use 
frequently) in a flash
Built in Airport -- no PCMCIA card hanging out the side
Screen -- PC users who see the screen 2 years on tell me it looks 
amazing. I tell them they should see the new Powerbooks.
Lifestyle -- my life and my Ti seem to be a perfect fit

Cons:
Weak case, the Ti still looks pretty good but there are some cracks 
that have appeared (not caused by any rough handling etc)
Battery life after 2 years is looking weak
I had to invest $$$ in a decent sleeve and case to ensure my Ti looks 
as good as it does
Sound/Audio is low quality -- microphone located badly.
