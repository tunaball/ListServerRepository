I bumped by 667 from 256 to 768 and noticed a marked improvement.  I 
typically have about 20 apps open at once, and with iTunes and iPhoto 
open for a while (especially iPhoto) they can get quite large.  It's 
tremendously faster to unhide them from RAM than from virtual memory.
