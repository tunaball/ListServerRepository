No. Both have six outwards tips, but while the inhex has strait line 
between the tips, the torx has tips going inwards as well. Looking like 
a perfect star shape. Use <http://www.google.com> and the image search 
and try _inhex_ and thereafter _torx_ and you will see some good 
illustrations to show the difference.
