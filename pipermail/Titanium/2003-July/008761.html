> In my non-scientific tests, using lame encoded mp3's versus AAC at an

> (.aiff) in around 10 test cases.
>

> with greater clarity and greater depth to the music.

compatibility with mp3 players, but I recently bought an iPod so I bit 
the bullet and am now encoding 192 bit AAC.
