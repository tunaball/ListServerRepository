I have just upgraded my Powerbook 667 to 768mb from
512mb. I cannot describe how easy this process was!
Just remove the keyboard, push the two side levers to
eject the memory module, slide in the new memory
module and *presto* you are done (less than 10
seconds)! The system detected the upgrade immediately
and i was on my way typing notes in class. I have yet
to notice any improvements but I am certain this will
be evident when i fire up high memory applications
(esp. Photoshop) or when running several demanding
applications at the same time (Words, Powerpoint,
iTunes, Camino besides numerous background
applications :P).
