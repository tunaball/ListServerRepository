I use VPC and connect to my clients through Cisco VPN ... (I have 
networking set to Virtual Switch)... One reason to use VPC is that I do 
not want all my internet traffic to go through their networks ...  I 
can use Safari, mail, etc without them able to monitor any of my 
internet traffic from the MAC while connected.  My larger clients even 
stop POP mail traffic, so I could not check e-mail while logged in with 
a PC or if I used VPN with just the MAC.

> FWIW, I use Cisco VPN Client for Mac OS X 4.0(rel) with Mac OS X 
> 10.2.6 directly, no VPC, and it works fine.
