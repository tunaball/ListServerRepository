Painting chipping off is indeed a problem but 'delicate' is a relative 
concept I guess.
My Ti 667 had a hard landing on the floor off the table when my 
daughter caught the charger cord while running with her stroller.
Not a 'regular' fall, rather the kind one would see in cartoon: 
*swoosh* away from under my typing fingers and triple bounce on the 
floor before coming to a rest.

The case is bent and broken and the screen does not overlap perfectly 
in closed position but I did not even had to send the PB out to repair. 
Still working flawlessly (and that was almost two years ago). Maybe a 
miracle but I would put all my savings any other brand would have 
exploded into little pieces at first impact.
