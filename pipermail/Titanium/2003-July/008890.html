Check Universal Access; make sure it's set to color and not to 
greyscale.  You might try toggling it too, just to be sure.  If that 
doesn't fix it, there are probably a few preference files you could try 
deleting; just toggle both display colors and universal access 
settings, then do a search for anything containing "plist" and modified 
today.  Pick the two or so that are named com.apple.something.plist, 
modified at the same time or shortly after you adjusted the 
preferences.  They'll either be in /Library/Preferences/ or 
~/Library/Preferences/.
