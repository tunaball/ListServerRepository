With respect to www.broadbandreports.com, yes that is probably the site I used in the past, which is no longer free ("We're sorry, this test is currently off-line" -- where have we heard that before?).

I tried http://www.bandwidthplace.com/speedtest , and was told that my speed is 1.2 megabits per second, which is labeled as "fantastic."

Then I tried http://webservices.cnet.com/Bandwidth , which says that my speed is 1006 kbps; whereas, a "faster provider" is SBC -- which is MY provider!

Perhaps Jason Tertadian said it best when wrote, "[t]he "best speedtest site" is any site that you don't have to route over other networks to reach," and he suggested that I contact the "friendly folks" at my ISP, which I have done in the past to check my speed.
