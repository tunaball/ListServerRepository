I was checking Versiontracker and noted my Linky router has received  
yet another firmware upgrade. reviews were mixed so I trotted off to  
Linksys' homepage to note the announcement of a new wifi media adaptor  
which may have utility for Powerbook owners.

The wifi unit sits on your hifi or TV, and presumably receives signals  
from your PB via Airport or ethernet hub then via wired connections to  
your music/TV system. Comes with a remote control too to see the  
proceedings on the TC monitor, inc jpgs and TIFFS

http://www.linksys.com/splash/wma11b/moreinfo.asp

Mind you, the site makes no mention of OS X/9 compatibility!
