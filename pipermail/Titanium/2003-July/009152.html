I have T-Mobile and I have thus far only used it as a regular modem, 
i.e. not GPRS.  It only connects at 9.6k, but it's nice for 
emergencies.  I understand that GPRS gives you approx. dialup speeds 
(56k).

If you have paired the phone in Bluetooth, you should see the device 
show up in your Network preferences like the onboard Ethernet or modem. 
  Then you just configure it as "Ericsson Infared" type and setup the 
PPP preference using a dialup account.
