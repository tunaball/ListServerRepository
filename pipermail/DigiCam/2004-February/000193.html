I've been using Epsons for years and have been very satisfied with 
print quality. There are a couple things to keep in mind though.

1. You have to use them, not let them sit around for weeks at a time 
with no printing. That's how they get clogged.

2. You have to take control of the print dialog and go through all of 
the settings. By default, they will use their own color system. You 
don't want that. Switch to Colorsync instead.
