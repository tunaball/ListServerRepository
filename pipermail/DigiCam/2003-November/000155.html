Steve,

The 8x10s will look just fine. We have a 3 MP Olympus C-3030 and 8x10s look
great. Actually, what originally sold me on digital cameras was seeing a 2
MP 8x10 which looked pretty good too. The 2 MP was a bit soft (i.e. not tack
sharp), but overall, I was very impressed. 4 MP is really not that different
from 5 MP if you compare linear resolution (i.e. how many pixels per side or
per inch). I'd be surprised if you noticed much difference between 4 and 5
MP on an 8x10, although I'm speaking from a theoretical standpoint as I've
never personally viewed either!
