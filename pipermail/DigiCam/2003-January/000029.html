Don't know about the S70, but with FD88 and F707, have the choice of 
copying the MS within the camera - should be in one of the menus.

Also, it may be as easy as rebuilding your desktop for recognition of 
the MS to come back. I would try that first.

We are using a card reader [ZIO] and I like that alot - our host, 
DrBott, also sells readers. There is a Firewire one also, but have 
not had success with it recognizing anything but sporadically.
