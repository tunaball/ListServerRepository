Just got myself a HBH-200 Bluetooth headset. My previous HBH-35 went 
kaput. I must say I'm very impressed. Sound is great, voice dialling 
works perfectly (even in the car with music on), which I never got 
working consistently on the 35, and the display showing who's calling 
(name+ number) makes a huge difference when the phone is "lost" 
somewhere... ;-) It also gives you access to the call list, letting you 
redial any number/ name on that list showing that list on the headset 
display. The cord leading from the display to the earpiece is a little 
short, which limits where you clip it, but otherwise I haven't found 
any other negatives.

This is truly a headset that allows you to keep the phone wherever you 
want and only use the headset.
