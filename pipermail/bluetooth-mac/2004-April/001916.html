Hello,


First of all you need a dedicated data number for your SIM card. If any data
call comes through your normal phone number, it is treated as a voice call
and so not transferred to your mac. Thus, no mac program can answer it.


For fax please tray Pagesender, this should work perfectly after a little
tweaking (see the help sides of the program).

The best way to receive faxes on a computer is to use an fax to e-mail
service like gmx.de or web.de.

Both will provide you with a fax number, any fax send to this number will be
scanned automatically (no one can see the content) and this picture is send
to you via e-mail. The same procedure as a fax program would do on your mac.
