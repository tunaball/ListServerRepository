>Forgive my ignorance Mark, but if I buy an unlocked Z600, will that 
>work with the sim card from my Treo? That's a T-Mobile phone.
>
>Also, I read that the current software with the Z-600 doesn't allow 
>use of T-zones. Can you comment on that.

I'm not Mark, but I'll respond since I'm using the Z600 with T-Mobile 
in the USA...

You can just pop the card out of your old phone and put it in the 
Z600. The guy at my local T-mobile store did that with mine while we 
were troubleshooting t-zones. My SIM worked flawlessly in his Siemens 
phone and his in mine.

As for t-zones not working with the Z600...I have found that every 
other WAP site I try to access works just fine. The t-zones home 
page, however, is not yet viewable on the Z600 for whatever reason. 
Rest assured you can still access the rest of the WAP web. This is a 
known problem that T-mobile is supposedly working on. I don't find it 
to be a really big deal, but some people might.
