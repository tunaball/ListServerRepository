I used to be a Nokia user... changed to Sony Ericsson and never looked 
back.

I've had a T39, T68i, P800 and am about to receive a Z600. The most 
compatible with my mac and BT is a tie between the T39 and T68 (that 
I've used). The T68 has a very slow interface that drove me nuts. I 
actually changed back to the T39 at a point... The T68 actually also 
gave me quite a few "keyboard" problems: a few of the keys stopped 
working. The T610 I've heard is even more compatible with everything 
than the T68. The Z600, I've read, is the same as the T610 but with 
better reception and a better screen so I'm looking forward to that. 
The P800 does a lot... too much actually, including filling a pocket 
and emptying a wallet. ;-) ...it's also not 100% compatible with my 
mac... damn symbian thingies...

Between the choices you present me, I'd go with a T610, but I'd look at 
the Z600... the price is VERY similar on this side of the Atlantic. On 
that side I think it's called a Z610 or something... it's a clamshell 
design with big screen and big keys.

Happy shopping!
