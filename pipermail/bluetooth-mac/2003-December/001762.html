If I had to guess I would say that the keyboard would probably work 
fine, but the mouse would be problematic. I tried it with a new style 
Belkin 100 meter bluetooth adapter and the keyboard worked great, but 
the mouse would not reconnect after the computer woke from sleep, at 
least not consistently. It was pretty frustrating and I definitely 
wouldn't recommend it. I can only speculate on its behavior with the 
older D-Link, but it's probably safe to say that Apple isn't just 
trying to trick you into buying a second adapter.
