First, there's no reason to wait for the T630 if you've gotta have 
it. It can be found at the usual places but it's _considerably_ more 
expensive than the Z600 right now. Last time I checked there was a 
difference of about US$175!

Compelling is a subjective term. I find a flip design more compelling 
than the traditional Sony Ericsson 'bar of soap' design. That's why I 
was so thrilled  when the Z600 came out. It's got everything that the 
best SE phones have and it's in a form factor that I consider to be 
the best for me.

And as for styling...the striped and patterned Style-Up covers are 
right out of the Dalmatian and Flower Power iMac era.
