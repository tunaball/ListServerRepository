I have been using a P800 since December 2002.  I just switched to the P900
this week.  

In Dec 2002, there was not isync.  It was very difficult, I had to use my PC
for any type of sync.  The Bluetooth functions always worked fine, sending
and receiving pictures.

Today with isync it is very easy.  The Bluetooth speeds are fine for normal
sending a receiving of items and for syncing.

All files I have on my phone, I can transfer and use on my mac.

When you download symbian programs .sis, you transfer them with the
Bluetooth file exchange.

Please let me know if you have any other questions.
