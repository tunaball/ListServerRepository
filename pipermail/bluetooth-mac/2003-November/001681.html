The problem that I have seen is that once you start sharing your 
internet connection with BT, you can't change the connection. Every 
time, before you change your internet connection, you have to run a 
Stop BT sharing applescript.

Rather than restarting, if you would have run the Stop BT-sharing 
script, you should have been able to switch connections.
