A caveat: Telephone reception on the T68i is horrible with T-Mobile in 
New York.  Menus are also slow.

I have used the T39m, T68i, R520 and the P800 (own all of them) and 
found the T68i to be the worst by a long margin.... although the voice 
dialing and Bluetooth work great on the T68i.

The T39m gets very good reception and can be found inexpensively on 
ebay (around $115 shipped).  GPRS works very well on the T39m. T9 lags 
a bit.

The Bluetooth works great but it doesn't have a color screen.  The R520 
is bigger than the T39m, but it has a slightly larger screen and 
speakerphone.  They can be found for about $80 shipped (brand new).  
Haven't tried GPRS on this phone.

Good luck.
