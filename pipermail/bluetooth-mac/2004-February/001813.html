If you're looking to stay in the Sony Ericsson line, you can get 
their new phones in the US now even though the mobile carriers don't 
have them yet.

The T630 is a big upgrade from the T610 that T-Mobile is selling now. 
It has a beautiful new display and comes in a shade of silver that 
matches the new Aluminum PowerBooks perfectly. Sony Ericsson has just 
released a really cool black version as well. They're a little pricey 
right now because the mobile providers don't carry them yet.

If you're looking for a flip design, the Z600 is off the chart. I 
bought one a few months ago from GSMphonesource.com and I couldn't be 
happier.
