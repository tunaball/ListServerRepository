I had a Belkin BT headset and it was OK. I now have a Plantronics 510S and
it is far superior to the Belkin. It has great noise cancellation and the
ability to pair to two devices. I've used it with my Treo 650, my PowerBook
and the base station for home phone use. It is very comfortably, great sound
and good battery life. I don't have any interest in the company, just a
satisfied user.

It comes in different packages:
510: headset by itself.
510S: Headset with the base station for a corded phone.
510SL: above plus the auto lifter.
510SLPlus: above plus chargers and carry cases.
