On Sunday, June 15, 2003, at 05:31 AM, Raymond P Reedy wrote:
> What are the advantages of storing contacts in SIM? They then reside 
> in two places.

I believe the reason for backing up the contents to the SIM card is so 
you can remove it and transfer it to a new phone. Then you can copy 
your contacts from the SIM to the new phone's memory. The SIM card also 
has the information for your plan and whatever info the service 
provider needs to identify your number, etc. Not really anything to do 
with syncing to the computer.
