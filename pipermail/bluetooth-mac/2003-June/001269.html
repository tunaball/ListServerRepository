vCal - if you want specifics, I reckon 1.0 but that's a fairly wild 
guess.

But Symbian's vCal format is slightly odd so it doesn't surprise me 
that iCal gets confused. In fact, the .vcs (vCal) files open fine in 
iCal if you rename them to .ics (iCal). Sigh.

Note there's tonnes of stuff mentioning calendar sync for P800/7650 
within 
/System/Library/SyncServices/SymbianConduit.bundle/Contents/Resources. 
That, combined with the fact that Calendar omission is only in the 
'last minute news' section of help, suggests it's nearly there.

There's also an mRouter daemon which can be used to get IP from your 
Mac to your phone, which enables lots of things... but that's another 
story...
