> There are address fields in PhoneAgent, but maybe it displays different
> fields depending on what's available on your phone - but then that
> should include address fields etc on yours, surely?
> 
> With my k700, in PhoneAgent you click on 'Phonebook' and click
> 'Download' to transfer the entire contact list from your phone.  When
> the list appears in PhoneAgent click on the entry you want to change,
> click the edit button - an 'Edit' window should appear with a full set
> of fields including address etc  - and just add the extra info you
> want.
> 
> When you've finished just zap the list back into your phone.  When
> you've edited any info PhoneAgent auto-detects the changes and should
> activate the 'Upload' button on the toolbar, click that and you'll send
> the full contact info back into your phone.  Note that you can only
> upload info with a registered version of PhoneAgent.
