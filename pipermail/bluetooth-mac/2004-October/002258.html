following in an email dated 10/19/04 2:48 AM:

> I am looking for a phone that allows me to sync the complete address

> contract and from what I have read on Apples iSync site the Nokia 6620
> is not yet supported. I would be grateful for any suggestions or
> experiences.
> I have the SE T616 which is ok but doesn't offer a full (sync-able)
> address book. I would love to have a clamshell phone but from what I
> found googling Motorola phones are supposed to have a crappy OS
> (especially the address book) whilst the SE Z600 doesn't offer a full

I have a Nokia 3650 (same as the 3620). It syncs completely with Address
Book. The only problem is custom fields. If you use the generic names home,
work, mobile, etc then it is fine.

One quirk, if you edit certain items for a contact on the Mac after you sync
the contact on the phone may lose special settings. Those being image,
one-touch, defaults, custom ring tone, etc.

The calendar syncing is much less reliable.
