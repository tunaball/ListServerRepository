I have a Siemens S56 that syncs the full Address Book and Calendar 
functions to my Mac via Bluetooth. Neither Cingular nor AT&T still sell

satisfied (bonus points for not having a camera, which my employer does 
not allow).
Moto phones do not sync via Bluetooth for the Mac. The SE T616 was my 
other option, but I passed on it because of the camera.
