> Mine is a DLink DBT-120 dongle coming from apple. I wasn't aware that

There are various revisions of the D-Link dongles - the first one Apple  
shipped (the one I have) is now unsupported and cannot be updated with  
Apple's firmware updater.  You must have revision B2 or later for this  
to work - I think the update adds support for BT 1.2 with some kind of  
auto-frequency-shifting which makes BT and Airport networks coexist  
better.

You can check if the D-Link dongle you have is update-able by running  
the updater (which also updates internal BT chips in Macs) :  
http://www.apple.com/downloads/macosx/apple/ 
bluetoothfirmwareupdater.html
