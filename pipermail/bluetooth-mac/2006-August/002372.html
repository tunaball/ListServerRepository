Word on the street has it that David Gluckman, from gluckman at umich.edu, sent
the following in an email dated 8/14/06 6:13 PM:

>  I'm hoping some of you can give me a recommendation for a
> relatively inexpensive, reliable, comfortable headset.
I've owned several BT headsets. From cheap to expensive and the best one so
far is the Plantronics 510. I got the "S" model because it comes with a base
station for the house phone so you can use it with both. It is one of the
few BT headsets that will pair with a second devices.

It is comfortable, has noise cancellation, good sound and the battery lasts
long enough. I've recommended it to a lot of people and at least a dozen
have gotten it and they all like it.

YMMV.
