I just bought a P800!

Seems like an amazing phone in the few hours I've been "playing" around 
with it! Unfortunately the software that comes with it is PC only and 
iSync, Addressbook and Clicker doesn't work with this phone (yet, I 
hope!). The reason I had to let the T68i go was because the keyboard 
was getting worse and worse. The "c" and "4" needed to be pressed 
almost with a hard object in order to work. This one at least doesn't 
have a keyboard! Let's hpe the touch screen is reliable.

The phone interface is a bit PCesque, but not extremely. The Jog Dial 
and pen input mixed makes navigating very easy.
