This isn't totally Mac-related, but I just wanted to report that I got the
Jabra FreeSpeak Bluetooth headset for my T68i (which I of course Sync with
Address Book via Bluetooth ;) ) a few weeks ago and it works great.

The only caveats are related to range; I've experienced a significant amount
of static in the following circumstances:

- Anything more than 10-15 feet, unimpeded.

- Having the phone in a bag with a lot of other stuff surrounding it.

- Environments with a lot of RF interference (office with too much tech
stuff, floor of Macworld Expo ;) ).


But within those limits it works quite well. I generally keep my phone
either in my pants or jacket pocket or in the outer pocket of my bag, and
the signal is quite strong. Combined with the T68i's excellent voice
dialing, I rarely have to actually pick up my phone.
