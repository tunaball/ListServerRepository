Just a question on your setup, is the Bluetooth USB adapter plugged 
directly into the Mac or is it through a hub? Sometimes that's a 
factor, I have mine on a powered hub. when my setup conks out I just 
unplug it and plug it back in, sorta like reminding the Mac that it's 
there.

You'll notice that if you don't have a Bluetooth adapter on your Mac, 
and initiate a HotSync from your Palm, you'll get the same error 
message.
