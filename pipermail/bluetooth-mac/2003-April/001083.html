Anyone have a Nokia 3650 and successfully paired AND USED a Bluetooth 
headset?  I've tried the Jabra FreeSpeak for Bluetooth Phones, 
Plantronics M1000 and Motorola Bluetooth Handset but other than once 
being able to record a VoiceTag on the Jabra, I cannot hear or speak 
using any of the headsets.  They all pair up in the Bluetooth control

currently available).

Bluetooth-cursed!
