It may be the APN - check the script at http://www.webweaving.org/t68/  
to
see how you do that (*line with 'internet' in it) and add that APN line
into your script. That may well help.

Depending on your country your APN with T-Mobile will be something like

	internet.t-d1.de

and your username/password td1/gprs. or alternatively set the APN in
register 1 and let that '1' at the end of the phone number you are using
below reference that.
