I basically do not have any problems to synchronise my Mac (Mac OS X 
10.3.2) with my P900.

To circumvent the firewall problem - iSync does not work with the Mac 
OS X firewall enabled - I opened  port 3004.

Occasionally iSync starts and then reports any kind of possible 
problems. One recovery that always works for me is

	switching off the P900
	switching on the P900
	starting Bluetooth on the P900
	reestablishing the pairing with the Mac
	starting iSync

No settings have to be changed.
