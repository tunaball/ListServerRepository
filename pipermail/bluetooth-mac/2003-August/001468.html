I'm pretty sure the problem is with Apple.  Epson has had a perfectly 
good solution with a Bluetooth receiver module that supports their 
Stylus Photo 1280 and others.  OSX sees the hardware but pairing is not 
possible due to (and I paraphrase Apple), 'Bluetooth printing is not 
enabled at this time.'  'This time' has been a long time now and I have 
been stuck with, mislead by Epson at the time, two printer setups that 
I only purchase based on Bluetooth capabilities.  Maybe Panther will 
hold a solution.  I wouldn't hold my breath.  There have been no rumors 
to that fact.
