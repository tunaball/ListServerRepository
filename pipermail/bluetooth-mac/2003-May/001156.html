A while back I mentioned to the list that Belkin was selling refurbished
100-meter Bluetooth adapters for $12 (normally $60, I think). They were
backordered, but Belkin let you place the order and said they would ship
when more were available.

I was one of the first orders in the queue, and I finally received mine
today. The cool thing is that it was actually brand new, not refurbished. So
if you ordered one during the sale, you may be pleasantly surprised when you
get your shipment :)
