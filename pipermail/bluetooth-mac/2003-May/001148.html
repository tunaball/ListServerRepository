On 2003-05-12 15:16, Dr. Julie Armstrong wrote:

> If I am Bluetooth enabled, exactly how do I send a message via
> SMS/address book?

Open Address Book. You should see a button with a Bluetooth logo in the top
left of the window. If the logo isn't blue, click the button.

When the logo is blue, click on a contact's (cell) phone number and select
SMS Message from the pop-up menu.

>From there, you'll know what to do.
