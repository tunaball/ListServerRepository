Think of the HBH-30 as being a little coy in this transaction.  It 
will happily agree to a pairing, providing the suggestion is made by 
the T68i.  So, first set the HBH-30 to accept (turn off for 30 
seconds, turn on and continue to hold the button until the lights 
begin blinking in an alternating pattern) then tell the phone to look 
for the headset.  After the T68i finds the headset the password MUST 
be 0000 as the headset has no provision to change the code.

Good luck!

Ben
