I recently bought the JABRA BT250 with intention of using it with my 
Nokia 6230. I tried it with iChat the other day and it worked great! 
Note that I paired it to my PowerBook G4 12" to do this, my G4 at work 
(using an original D-Link Bluetooth adaptor) didn't work as it does not 
use the 'headset' profile of the Bluetooth specification.
