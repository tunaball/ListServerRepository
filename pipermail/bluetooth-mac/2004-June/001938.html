my wife has an N 6230, I have an SE T610, which is quite similar to the 
Z600. Here´s´what I´ve found:

The N6230 accepts vcard, vcal info over bluetooth, thus you can 
transfer address and calendar data, but isync doesn´t work. Modem etc 
works fine, though. And despite the smaller display you actually get 
more lines of text using wap for example, as the UI isn´t  as bloated. 
The font is smaller, though. The camera of the 6230 is vastly superior, 
my T610´s is totally useless, and I´ve understood that the z600 camera 
is about the same.
