I've just been told that OS X has a controlled shut down built in. No 
additional software or drivers required.

If you get a UPS with USB, and connect it to your Mac, you will find 
some additional options in the 'battery' menu. When the UPS battery 
gets low, the Mac will do a controlled shut down and restart (if you 
wish) when things get back to normal.

This is second hand information, but when I get my new UPS, we'll find 
out for sure.

BTW, I have an old APC Back UPS Pro and when it died, it was trivially 
easy to replace the battery. Good as new.
