I'm using one at this very minute. It's a beauty. I love the screen. 
The speed bump isn't particularly noticeable to me for the things I do 
(mostly email) but I have noticed that applications open a whole lot 
faster. I have a gig of RAM. I do wish that I'd gotten the blue tooth 
keyboard and mouse, though. I don't like seeing the wires. I opted not 
to because I use a trackball and won't be using the Apple mouse anyway. 
So, that's what it's like so far. And, oh, it's very light weight. ;-D
