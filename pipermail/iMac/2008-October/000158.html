Greg,

You can change the colour of fonts and/or background according to what  
mailbox the mail is directed to. All you have to do is create new  
rules in your preferences. Open preferences, click on rules (far right  
tab), click on the (+) and a new rule will be created. You just select  
the conditions under which the rule is applied, then what will happen  
when the conditions are made (which category the incoming mail has,  
and what colour you wish the fonts and background to be). Then click  
the activate checkbox to activate the rule and there it is!
