> I am sure this not is new issue but I stumbled on it today and was

> I have purchased a sharewear program so when I tried  to use is on  
> another logon it prompted me for a serial number.
>
> This to me is a bit of a folly as I though sharewhere was licensed  
> to a cpu. While I cannot follow there legal rules I think common  
> sense is that if it prompts each user  for a serial number it seems

It depends on the license under which the shareware is distributed.  
Some vendors allow multiple users on one machine, others allow one  
user on multiple machines and many variations of this. It is entirely  
up to the vendor. I always look at the license or ask the vendor  
before purchasing.
