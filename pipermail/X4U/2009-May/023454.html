Manual Setup, Internet Tab, set Connection Sharing to "Distribute a  
range of IP Addresses", click DHCP, enter DHCP reservations. You'll  
need the MAC addresses for each computer.

To get the MAC, Sysrtem Preferences, Network, TCP/IP, Airport, Airport  
ID at the bottom.
