Mail.app is extensible, and there are lots of extensions to get it to do much of what previous Eudora users want.

For example, with regard to attachments, many Mail.app users love:

Attachment Tamer  ($15)
http://lokiware.info/Attachment-Tamer

Here are some lists of Mail.app extensions:

http://www.tikouka.net/mailapp/
http://www.macworld.com/product/collection/6866/mailaddons.html
http://web.archive.org/web/20130123031749/http://www.hawkwings.net/plugins.htm

Dead?
