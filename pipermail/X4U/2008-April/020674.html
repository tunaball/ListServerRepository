You can flash a PC's ATi 9800 Pro to a Mac firmware.

These cards are quite common - they're quite distinctive when you  
know what to look for, and you can easily get one on eBay for £30 (or  
probably less now). It's not too difficult to do and although it's an  
imperfect solution, well, compared with the cost of an official card,  
it was the only answer that seemed to make sense when my G5's  
graphics card died.
