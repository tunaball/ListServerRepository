You realize that using the Redirect makes the message appear as if  
it's coming from the original sender and not you? If John sends me an  
e-mail and I forward it to Bill, Bill sees a message from me (with  
quoted text). If I redirect the message instead of forward, Bill sees  
a message from John in its original format (not quoted). This also  
means that if Bill replies to the message it will go back to John and  
not me.
