Those are "Quote" lines to indicate that you are not the original  
poster. If you want to forward without those, just select all the body  
of the message, and paste into a new message. Another way is to select  
the body and choose Mail>Services>Mail>Send To .... A new mail will  
open with the contents and ready to address.
