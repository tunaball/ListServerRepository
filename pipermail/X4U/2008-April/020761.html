> Any idea what the message(s) are and if they are important and how to
> stop them? I have seen the messages several times in the log.

It's related to using RSS and or a .Mac account. See
 <http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=304340> for details.
Additionially, this search at Apple Discussions reveals many hits:
<http://discussions.apple.com/search.jspa?threadID=&q=BestCalendarDateFromString&objID=&dateRange=last90days&userID=&numResults=30&rankBy=9>.
To eliminate them, turn off RSS feeds.
