> My medical claim form that I send in with receipts for reimbusement  
> is a pdf file. I can use Preview or Adobe Reader to fill in the  
> fields and print the form, but I cannot save it. I don't know if  
> the pdf has been specially "reader enabled", but I do know that  
> they used "Acrobat PDF Maker 7.0 for Word" to create it.

Formulate will allow you to save the changes.  (Actually the changes  
will be saved outside of the original PDF, which will be untouched.   
But the info will still be saved and re-usable.)

FormulatePro (free)
http://code.google.com/p/formulatepro/
