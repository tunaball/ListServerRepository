Murray,

  I am far from an expert in this area but all I can do is to relate  
to you what I have seen in communicating with some windows users.   
The first is AOL types they have a bug in their email program in that  
if you attach more than one image  to an email it is mangled by their  
email program to look like either garbage (no pic) or it looks like a  
virus. I do NOT know if this has been fixed (not likely from what I  
have heard) or it may come across as two email attachments, one a  
small file which is the "icon" that macs put on each pic (another  
word is thumbnail instead of icon). The other possibility is that  
they have a brain dead email program that does not understand the MAC  
attachments. When you attach a file at the bottom of the pulldown  
menu it says use windows friendly attachments. Make sure that is  
checked off.
