I had a similar problem (the sync going out of the side bar). Googled and found a solution that worked. Plug in phone, turn wifi sync off. Sync and eject phone then disconnect cable. Quit iTunes. Reconnect cable and when the phone shows up go back and turn wifi sync back on. Pull plug and it stayed visible in the side bar and recommenced syncing over the wifi.
