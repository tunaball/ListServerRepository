Flash is asking to use more memory on the mac, I think it is for streaming it says 'local storage' (mine is set to 100kb).
If you go to a flash movie in your browser and hold the control key then click on the movie you should see a settings button, click and it will open a small window called 'Adobe Flash Player Settings' you want the one that looks like a folder in the middle, adjust accordingly.
