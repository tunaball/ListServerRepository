The rotating beachball is a "wait cursor."  It is not at all  
necessarily a sign that there is a problem.  It comes up whenever you  
have to wait a pre-determined amount of time before the requested  
function will be finished.  So, if you are performing several actions  
at the same time, and they will take some time to complete, it is  
completely normal for the rotating beachball to show up.
