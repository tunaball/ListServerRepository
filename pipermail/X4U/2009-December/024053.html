Hi John,

Download the app to iTunes and connect the iPod.  Click on the iPod  
name under Devices at the left.  In the middle of the screen under the  
big apple from iTunes, click Applications and a screen of the iPod  
will open, and you also will see the app.  Select it and also Sync  
Applications if they aren't.  The app icon will appear on the iPod.   
You can disconnect.
