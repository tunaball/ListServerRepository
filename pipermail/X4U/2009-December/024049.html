   How can I get an App from iTunes (in Applications) to our iPod Touch?   
   The icon is not showing on the iPod.

Attach you iPod, then select it on the left.

You should see a row of tabs, with Applications on or near the right.
Find your application and check the "include" box (or whatever it's
called).  Apply the changes.
