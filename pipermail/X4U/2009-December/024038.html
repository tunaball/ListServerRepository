In "theory" one of them, in the middle will suffice.  

Real world is very probably another matter.  The walls and their mode of construction, your refrigerators, wall units, bookcases, and all sorts of mysterious things including microwave ovens and wireless phones effect the spread of your WiFi signal.

In my house, made in 1840 with plaster walls and brick construction, I have five deployed.  My last house, somewhat larger, needed only three.  You will install the AE and you'll see where the dead spots are and how strong your signal is and you'll take it from there, but sight unseen, I'd be surprised if one does it, properly.

Just my two cents.
