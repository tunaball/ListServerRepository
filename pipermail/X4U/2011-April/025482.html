Was he phone ever registered with FInd my iPhone? that has to be done beforehand IIRC . . .Apple recently released a Find my iPhone app for the iPhone which goes through the registration process.

If it wasn't previously registered and has Find my iPhone checked in Settings -> Account -> Mobile Me -> Find my iPhone then you won't be able to locate it. Likewise . . .if the thief has wiped it or turned it off Find my iPhone won't work either.

iTunes -> Preferences -> Devices will tell you the serial number . . .but that's about it.
