Note that the NTP client won't always "jump set" to the correct time.
Instead, it sometimes speeds up or slows down the clock by inserting
multiple, small adjustments.  This helps regular daemon processes work
better.

So, the "after waiting a while, it seems better" is normal.

Check the NTP client documentation for information on how it decides
what to do.
