>How do I connect an older IDE/ATA hard drive to a G5??


Apple explicitly says that SATA to PATA adapters won't work:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=86416

Several folks have hypothesized that these adapters will work, and that 
it is just that for some reason Apple does not want to support them.
