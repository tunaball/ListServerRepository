This is probably FAQ #1 for OS X. Select a screenshot you've just taken.
Select Get Info from the File menu. In the Open With area, change the App
from Adobe Reader to Preview. Then click the Change All button. The next
time you take a screenshot, the resulting PDF should have a Preview icon.
