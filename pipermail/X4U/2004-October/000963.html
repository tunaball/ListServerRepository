On Oct 5, 2004, at 9:01 AM, Richard wrote:

> Where can you tweak the "User Agent" info for Safari?
> So, instead of a website saying hey your on a Mac,
> I want it to say, Hey your on a PC.
>
> TKS -
> Rick


You need to turn on safari's debug menu in order to do this.  Simply 
issue:

% defaults write com.apple.Safari IncludeDebugMenu 1

and close/restart safari if you have it open and you'll have a new
"Debug" menu available.
