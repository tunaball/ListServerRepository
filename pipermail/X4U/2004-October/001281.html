On 24 Oct 2004, at 03:40, Robert Nicholson wrote:

> When using airport how is it possible to switch users without losing 
> your wireless connection?
>
> At the moment whenever I switch the connection is disconnected and the 
> other user needs to reconnect.
>
> If that person has the network password in their keychain then they 
> should be able to maintain the connection and should they wish to 
> connect to another network they should be able to specify that in 
> their own network preferences.

Assuming Mac OS X 10.3.x, go into System Preferences -> Network. Select 
Airport. Enter in Network name and password. Works here.
