If you have a Google e-mail account, you can
e-mail 1Gb worth of files to yourself and leave
them there. Or, you can use something like Intranets
<http://intranets.com>, which is more expensive
than .mac but comes with some features that
.mac doesn't. That's what we use within our
firm to get everyone on the same page.

I think there are also a couple of Mac-friendly
Web sites designed specifically to serve for
offsite backup purposes.
