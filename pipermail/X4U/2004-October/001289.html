On Oct 25, 2004, at 9:13 AM, Mary C.Youra wrote:

> ...but it doesn't seem to retain the gridlines around the cells.

The gridlines you see when making the spreadsheet in Excel are simply 
for reference while making it.  They don't print, and I assume they 
don't show up when you save it as a web page.
To put gridlines in, select the cells you want to have gridlines around 
them.  In the format menu, select Format Cells.  There's a borders 
panel which lets you select which lines you want to print out.
