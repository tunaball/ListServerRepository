I don't know how to open several links at once in separate windows, but 
I do know how to open several links at once in separate tabs.

If you save links as bookmarks on the Bookmarks Bar, you can open all 
at once if you click "Open in Tabs", or you can assign a group to a 
small menu and open them all at once in tabs.
