Mark,

Please not that as far as I know, Final Vinyl is the only software that 
includes the RIAA pre-equalization needed for proper spectral balance 
when playing back LP's.

If you decide not to use Final Vinyl you will need to run the turntable 
through a conventional stereo receiver first. Also please not that many 
newer stereo receivers also do not have a phone preamp.

David Crandon
