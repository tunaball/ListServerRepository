To clear the list in Entourage 2004, open Preferences:Compose and 
choose clear list at bottom of the compose preference pane. For 
Mail.app, choose the menu item Window:Previous Recipients. Select the 
addresses you want to get rid of and press the "remove from list" 
button at the bottom of the window.
