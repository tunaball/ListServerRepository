I've heard of this and wondered myself, while considering an Airport 
Express for music streaming in my house, whether it would turn out to 
be a big problem.
But despite several occasions when I've been on the phone - a Doro 
2.4GHz cordless - I've had AirTunes going away in the background with 
no issues whatsoever.
Perhaps it is a problem in locations with higher user density, but I've 
never noticed any interference at all.
