Trust me, I've gotten bit more than once by what I'm connected to :^/ 
You don't even want to hear about the weekend I spent trying to 
troubleshoot a network problem with my PDP-11 :^(  I thought it was a

After what I discovered tonight with my settings I'd also recommend 
going to the control panel and checking your settings.  I think I see

that and added a 3rd server I didn't know about until I checked to 
see which address I had correct.  Hopefully that's the last of that 
problem.

Paul, I don't suppose that you have another system on the network

usually something else to try that I don't think I've seen mentioned.
