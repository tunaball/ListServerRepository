On 10/19/04 11:04 AM, Mark Des Cotes <mark at astroprinting.com> wrote:

> At home, I have an external LaCie FireWire CDR/RW drive hooked up to my
> slot loading iMac. I'm running OS 10.2.8. Is there any way of getting
> the blank CDs to appear on the desktop similar to what happens with my
> Superdrive equipped G4 at work?

In 10.3, there is a System Preferences pane called CDs & DVDs, where you can
tell the system what to do when a blank CD is inserted. The option you would
want is Open Finder. I forget if this preference pane is available in 10.2,
but it wouldn't hurt to check.
