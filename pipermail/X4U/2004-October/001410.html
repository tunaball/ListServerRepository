If you want to go for audiophile quality try the TCC TC-750 at Amazon. 
I bought one about a month ago and it's totally transparent - no hum, 
noise, etc. Its output is Line Out and should be able to be plugged 
into a computer Audio In. I have it tied to a mini stereo system that 
didn't have a Phono input.
