On 10/7/04 6:33 PM, John McClernan <mcclernan1 at comcast.net> wrote:

> ...and a related question, how do you get .wmv audio files into iTunes, in
> order to burn a CD? Any help greatly appreciated.

I believe Windows Media Audio files are .wma, not .wmv. The Windows version
of iTunes can convert unprotected .wma files in order to import them into
the Library. The Mac version, however, cannot. I don't know why, exactly,
but I'm guessing it's due to the lack of parity between the Mac and Windows
versions of Windows Media Player.
