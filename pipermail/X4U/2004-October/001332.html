One issue to be aware of is the maximum size of your email partition 
with your server. Often these are limited at 5 or 10 MB. Thus, 
depending on what mail your are currently sending and receiving, you 
might be limited to a 3-4 MB file. It also depends on whether it is a 
POP or IMAP account. IMAP accounts can have a lot of mail stored on the 
server, if configured that way, and this further limits the size of 
files that can be sent.
