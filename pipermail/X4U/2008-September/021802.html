You can quickly regain about 1GB of free space or more by getting rid  
of unneeded language resources with:

Monolingual (free)
http://monolingual.sourceforge.net/

I also recommend that you run:

Cache Out X  (Free)
http://www.trilateralsystems.com/CacheOutX/

MacJanitor (Free)
http://personalpages.tds.net/~brian_hill/macjanitor.html

You can find out what is filling up your hard drive with:

DiskInventory X (free)
http://www.derlien.com/

GrandPerspective (free)
http://grandperspectiv.sourceforge.net/
