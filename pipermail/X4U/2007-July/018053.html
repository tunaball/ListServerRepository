> I just bought a soundtrack album in the store. The files are in .m4p.
> I want the real thing .aiff so I can play them on my stereo system. I
> am trying to convert them but the error message keeps coming up:
> (-42024). Any idea? TIA

You cannot convert .m4p songs into any other format. They are protected by
digital rights management.

One way around this has been to burn an Audio CD containing those tracks and
then rip the audio CD into whatever format you want (AIFF, MP3, AAC, etc.)
