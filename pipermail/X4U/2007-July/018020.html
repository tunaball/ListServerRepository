I have a client who has five remote workers. These people work from  
their homes and currently access the central office via VPN. Works  
fine, albeit somewhat slow short of spending $400/month on bandwidth.  
Be that as it may, email, shared forms, etc. are taken care of.

What they seek is a solution for the phone. The owner would like to  
have a single number for client to use. Entering an extension will  
ring the appropriate person at their home, but the client should be  
unaware of this. Also, the workers need to be able to make outbound  
calls on the company's dime.

We have a few possible vendors for this type of service on our list.  
I would like to hear from anyone who has looked into and/or  
implemented this type of thing.
