that others will pass laws in favor of the corporations.

However:

(and this is a big however). If US corporations own and control the

which corporate america decides to allow thru as we will.

taken out (as in a nuclear war scenario) but if you want to surf Bob's

corporation who wants to squash Bob, well...you and he would be S.O.L.
