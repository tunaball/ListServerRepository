Once the scripts are installed, when you are in Mail, look at the  
SCRIPTS drop-down meun bar iten at the top right ... it looks like a  
scroll ... and you may have to turn it on using the AppleScript  
Utility: Check the Preferences "Show Script Menu in menu bar" and  
"Show Librbray scripts" ... and then go to "Mail Scripts" ... there  
you'll find the one you're looking for: "Remove Duplicates .."

Hope that helps,
