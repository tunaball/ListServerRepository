We have one .Mac account and four people publishing calendars to it. 
Each person is subscribed to the other three calendars. We can each 
view anybody's calendar but we can only make changes to our own.

When one person publishes theirs for the first time, iCal give you the 
link that subscribers need. I then go to the other three computers and 
subscribe to that link.
