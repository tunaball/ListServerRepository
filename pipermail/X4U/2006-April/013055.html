Something's hosed in your startup routine. According to the manpage for getty:
"The getty program is called by init(8) to open and initialize the tty line,
read a login name, and invoke login(1)." You have something set to launch the
Terminal during startup?

I'd create a new admin user account, log into it, reset SysPrefs/Accounts so
that there's no autologin,  restart, login to the new account, and see if the
problem persists. BTW, 10.4.6 is the latest.
