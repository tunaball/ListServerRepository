Another list reply:

"You'll be able to publish calendars, but I don't think your  
wife's .mac account has any web space so she won't be able to  
publish. She can subscribe to yours though. She would either have to  
have her own .mac account or you would have to have a family  
pack .mac account <http://www.mac.com/1/family_pack_upgrade.html>   
which includes web space for each family member."

I went with the Family upgrade, as I want both of us, and perhaps  
distant brother, to be able to use all the features in iCal sharing  
and iSync.
