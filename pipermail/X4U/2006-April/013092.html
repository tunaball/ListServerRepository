In case Stroller didn't pick up on it, OEM stands for ORIGINAL 
Equipment Manufacturer. The OEM disk is only licensed to OEMs. 
Certainly it may work today or tomorrow. Even a borrowed CD from the 
neighbor CD may work. But the point is that Microsoft has year after 
year gotten more diligent at enforcing their license agreements. So 
while it is illegal today, and may work tomorrow, I would imagine in 
the not too distant future, the money will have been ill spent.

If you want to cheat, just borrow your neighbors. If you want to 
honor someone's property rights, then buy the appropriate version. 
Just don't play games like thinking that buying a ribbon cable makes 
youan OEM and then pretend that you are doing the right thing. And 
then you can accept the name that others would see you as.
