Turns out the problem was in the a/c powerbrick adaper's male plug for the

power brick, booted up to show my problem, and there WAS no problem. Once I

genius was quite sure that was the problem. I returned with the "brick", he
verified the problem, and he provided me a replacement on the spot after
confirming my AppleCare. Quite impressive service, especially from one of
the new "mini" stores.
