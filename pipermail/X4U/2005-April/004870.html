On Tuesday, April 19, 2005, at 08:45AM, Ean Kingston <ean at hedron.org> wrote:

>On the topic of DVD region codes, does anyone know if iDVD puts a region
>code on a DVD when you burn one?
>
>I'm asking because I've got a couple of friends who would like a copy of
>my wedding video but they live in different regions and I can't find any
>mention of Region codes in iDVD. If iDVD takes the region code from the
>same place as the DVD player software then I'm going to have a problem.
>
>My prefered solution would be to produce DVDs that are playable in all
>regions.

iDVD does not put on region codes, nor copy protection. It does not have that capability. 

An issue you might have, is if you are creating a NTSC DVD and your friends might have PAL DVD players that have trouble with NTSC.
