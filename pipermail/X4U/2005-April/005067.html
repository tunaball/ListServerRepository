> how can I get mine to not use explorer and do this cool feature? I could
> not find any parameters that were relevant...


I don't know why this is left to IE to control but:

1. Open IE and get Preferences.
2. Select Protocol Helpers and change FTP to Safari. Save and Close IE.
3. Open safari and type FTP address into address field. Press Enter.

FTP window opens. It is a finder window.
