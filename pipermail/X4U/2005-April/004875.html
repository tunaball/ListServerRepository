You will need an AirPort Extreme card in your iBook to access your school's
wireless network and also any hotspots
http://www.apple.com/airportextreme/hotspots.html

If you want to access a wireless network at home, you'll need to create one
using either an AirPort Extreme base station or an AirPort Express.
http://www.apple.com/airportextreme/home.html
