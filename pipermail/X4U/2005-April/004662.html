On 14 Apr2005, at 10:00 AM, Jim Freeman wrote:

> How many times can you revolutionize the toothbrush or the razor?


	As many times as people find new uses for them.  How about a WiFi 
toothbrush with digital camera so that your dentist can check your 
teeth on-line.  Want to predict which OS will include that feature?   
Or maybe a razor with an RSS newsfeed so you can catch up on the 
headlines while shaving.  Can that be very far away?

	I have noticed that Apple's tactic is to look at what people are doing 
with computers and include those capabilities in the next release.  If 
they have to change the basic design of the computer to do it, they do 
so.  Each new version is as revolutionary the last version's users.   
The revolution never ends.
