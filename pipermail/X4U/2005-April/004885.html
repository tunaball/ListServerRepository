Find out first which card is supported by your iBook.

The Airport Extreme and plain vanilla Airport card are
not interchangeable. Your iBook can use either one
or the other.

Airport Extreme computers are listed here:
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=107440>

Airport computers are listed here:
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106777>
