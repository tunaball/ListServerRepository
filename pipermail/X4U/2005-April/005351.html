I'm not yet clear as to why my original .pdfs are not allowing me to 
update but I can thankfully say that the Take Control people are 
monitoring these forums and that they have personally contacted me 
and forwarded to me the Take Control books that I ordered. :-)  Nice 
to see that kind of support.

For anyone else that may see the same problem, do note that I did 
figure out a work around. I had happened to keep the original .zip 
files that I received when I purchased the books. By upzipping them 
again, new .pdf files were created and these new .pdf files 
automatically updated themselves to the 'Updates are ready for you' 
format. I clicked on the link and abra cadabra, I had my 4 new Take 
Control books. Now I only have to find the time to get more reading 
done.

Thanks to the Take Control guys and girls.
