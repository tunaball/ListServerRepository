I bought Readiris 9 and installed it on a G5. It works after a fashion, 
and is quite accurate in recognizing various kinds of text. However, I

did respond in a slow manner.

I use OmniPage Pro X instead, although its modest error rate requires

update to this program to make it a little more accurate and handier to 
use with my Epson 2450 scanner.
