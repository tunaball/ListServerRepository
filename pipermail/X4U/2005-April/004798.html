Programs that run Macros are great for when you know ahead of time 
what it is that you are going to be wanting to automate and when you 
are going to want to do something over and over. But what the user is 
asking for is the ability to easily access the menus via keyboard 
shortcuts. Imagine not having a mouse and being able to maneuver the 
menus. A user does not have to ever have used a menu item and yet it 
is still readily available only using your eyes and your fingers on 
the keyboard.
