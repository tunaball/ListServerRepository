Hi Art,

Just in case you didn't know, pressing the "Save as PDF..." button or 
using the "Save as file: PDF" option in the "Output Options" section of 
the Print window creates a very low-res PDF (nigh on a screen shot) that 
does not embed fonts. It's apple's token offering to people who don't have 
the full Acrobat product.

Choosing "Save as file: PostScript" is more like what Jan wanted but you 
need to ensure you have an appropriate PPD selected for what you're 
creating your PostScript file for.
