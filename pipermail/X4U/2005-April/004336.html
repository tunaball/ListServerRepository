Have you actually tried deleting them from the terminal?

Open terminal in Applications / Utilities & type `sudo rm -rf ` 
(without the quotes, but with the space) then drag the files onto the 
terminal window. The paths to the files will now appear after the `rm 
-rf ` you just typed; now click on the terminal window again & hit 
enter.

If the files are on the desktop then you'll need to click on the 
desktop before it'll update to show them removed.
