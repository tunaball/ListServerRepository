When NeXT as a dedicated hardware platform died, the customer base went
to the NEXTSTEP OS.  When the NEXTSTEP OS as a dedicated OS platform
died, the customer base then went to OPENSTEP for Solaris and WinNT.
When OPENSTEP died, the customer base then went to Mac OS X.  Evolution.
