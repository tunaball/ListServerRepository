> If so, what is the difference in features on the iPhone 3G 8GB and the
> iTouch 8 GB models?

Well, remove the phone, camera and GPS features, they are basically
identical... Both do email, web browsing... and all the iPod functions.
Though you can only use the Touch on a WiFi connection, since 3G is a
cellular service.  Plus, Touch users do not get the iPhone software updates
for free, they have to buy those from the iTunes Store.
