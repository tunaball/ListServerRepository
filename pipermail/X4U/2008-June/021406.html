Sounds like your new drive is showing signs of sticksion. This condition
exists when the HD head is laying on the platters and the surfaces are stuck
together. The most popular method of repair is to remove the drive and hit
it on the side (firmly) while its starting up in an effort to free this
sticksion issue. Sounds kind of brutal but it works most of the time.
