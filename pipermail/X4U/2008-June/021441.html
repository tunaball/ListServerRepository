need to be understood. I would suggest that if anyone is really

subject matter (I don't claim to be a super paging person just one

*NOT* a light subject matter the math alone is something that is  
reasonably well know in queuing theory its not about horse racing  
odds or poker. By its self its reasonably straight forward but there  
are a *LOT* of "depends" that go into a decision to page out and  
depending on what type of OS you are talking about a swap can be one  
of two types logical and physical. There are also variants of paging  
(and why it happens) it really comes down to the OS and how  
sophisticated it is.
