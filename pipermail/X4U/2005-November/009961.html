There are some discussions at http://www.macintouch.com/iphoto14.html  
about iPhoto's slowness and file bloat. Maybe there's an answer there  
for you.

By the way, I also have a dual 1.25 G4 (with 1G RAM, OS 10.4.3,  
iPhoto v 5.0.4) with over 3,700 pictures and iPhoto takes 15 seconds  
to open the first time and about 8 seconds to close. Subsequent  
openings take about 5 seconds. My pictures were all taken with a  
Nikon 5700 at high quality with a good number of them run through  
photoshop ending up at around 3MB each. My iPhoto Library folder is  
over 11G in size. The Library.iPhoto file within it is about 16MB.
