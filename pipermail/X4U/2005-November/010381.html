I downloaded an application called iPhoto Diet which offers to remove
redundant backups, unrotated originals, remove leftovers and so on. I've
used it a couple of times, it seemed to be fairly processor but it did get
rid of a lot of stuff. It's downloadable from here:
http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/16463
I would recommend as a matter of course that you have a full backup before
running, just in case. HTH.
