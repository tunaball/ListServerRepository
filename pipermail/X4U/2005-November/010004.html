On Nov 17, 2005, at 3:28 PM, Lee Licata wrote:

> I have a sound file (either mp3 or wav file) that I want to add to  
> the sounds my 10.3.9 powerbook can make.
>
> I tried to find where the "base" sounds (Sosumi and al..) are filed  
> and could not. I searched by name, and even searched for invisible  
> files.
>
> No luck
>

Try dumping your sound in ~/Library/Sounds
If you don't have a Sounds folder simply create one. I don't know if  
an mp3 or wav file would work but .aiff does.

Those "base" sounds reside in Macintosh HD/System/Library/Sounds by  
the way. Don't put your sound in here. Keep it in your users folder.
