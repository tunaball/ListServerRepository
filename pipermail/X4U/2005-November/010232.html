Really? The result should be exactly as mentioned before: 28.05 + 0.15 =
28.2, because that's what you entered using RPN: first number (28.05),
enter it, second number (15% = 0.15), operator (sum them). Nowhere in your
scheme did you tell the calculator to take 15% of the first number. If you
got an intermediate result of 4.2075, without any operation, then I'd say
your HP 12C is in error.
