Here is the connection chain, as I used it:

Printer's 8-pin serial port > LocalTalk box  > 4-pin LocalTalk cable  
 > LocalTalk box > Ethernet bride serial port > Ethernet cable from  
the bridge's ethernet port to ethernet port of the computer. If you  
are already using the ethernet port of the computer for some other  
purpose, you will need an ethernet hub or switch.

As I mentioned, I used the AsanteTalk bridge, here's 2 links to check  
out:

http://asante.com/products/productsLvl3/AsanteTalk.asp
http://asante.com/downloads/misc/ATALK_OSX_TD.pdf  (has a special  
note about the LW-NTR)
