Hi Allan,
I figured it out.    I was configuring the printer in the web admin  
area and I noticed a POP/SMTP area.  The printer's content said it  
could email me error reports, paper jams, etc...  I thought this  
would be handy since the printer is down the hall.  Instead the  
printer started printing out my mail. To make a long story short, I  
removed the settings and it works fine now.  Besides this little  
snafu, this was the easiest printer I've have ever setup.
