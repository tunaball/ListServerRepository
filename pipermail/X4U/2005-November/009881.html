Personally I don't recommend it!

Whenever my designers start having weird startup issues or some apps won't 
open (eg. Distiller) or things are going slow, I check their fonts list 
and they often have scrolling pages upon pages of active fonts. When I 
reduce them down to between 20 and 50 they seem to go OK again.

It is a shame it has to be this way (with such modern OS's) but hey, 
that's what we have to work with!
