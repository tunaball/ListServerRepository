GraphicConverter http://www.lemkesoft.de has an excellent built-in dup
finder.  It doesn't matter what size the duplicates are, or if they are
different sizes, or what their names are, it finds them and compares them
and lets you eliminate one or the other or neither.  It's also very fast.
