I also try to avoid the messages.

Holding the Option key when choosing sleep/restart/shut down stops the 
message. You'll see the little "..." go away when you hold the key down.

You could also press Control and Eject to invoke the 
shotdown/restart/sleep dialog then press the first letter of what you 
want, r-Restart / s-sleep / Enter-shut down.

Pressing Shift/Opt/Comm/Delete will empty the trash without a message.
