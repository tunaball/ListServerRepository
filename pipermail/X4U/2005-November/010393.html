Based on the reading I've done on the apple discussion forums, it  
looks like our issue was probably directly related to the size of the  
sent mail mbox... apparently, as the size of an mbox approaches 1GB  
in Panther, or 2GB in Tiger, the greater the likelihood of  
corruption. So, in order to avoid future problems, I guess I'll have  
to set up a protocol for message archiving.

In the meantime, it looks like her 800+MB of sent mail messages are  
gone.
