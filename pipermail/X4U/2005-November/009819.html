On 11/01/05, Teddi Pomaika'i Stransky <teddi at book-reader.net> wrote:
>
> Is there some way to TURN OFF the "are you sure you wanna" messages I
> get for just about everything I do in Tiger?
>
> YES, I want to download the file,

Safari->Prefs_General->uncheck the Open "safe" files after downloading. See
Finder->Help->MacHelp->"Mac OS X 10.4: Safari can help prevent unsafe
downloads"

> YES, I want to empty the trash,

Finder->Prefs->Advanced->uncheck Show warning before emptying the trash

> YES, I want to shut down the bloody annoying machine ...\

CNTL+OPT+CMD+Eject, see
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106567> for details.
Optionally, get something like FruitMenu which allows adding a Shutdown
command to the Apple menu that doesn't bring up any warning alerts.
