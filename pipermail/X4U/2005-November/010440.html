On 11/29/05 3:46 PM, Jim Dean wrote:

> Software installed recently was Stuffit 10, Go Live CS, Illustrator CS and
> Photoshop CS.

Some of those programs install fonts. Make sure that in doing so, you still
have a version of Helvetica and Helvetica Neue active, as well as Lucida
Grande (and Last Resort, etc., that are in  your main fonts folder). And,
make sure you don't have more than one version active at a time.

You'll have to look in Application Support > Adobe folder (there will
probably be a couple in various places) to find all the fonts that Adobe
installs with its apps.
