Although LaCie is an excellent choice usually, stay away from the
"lightscribe" capable drive.  My experience with the dual-layer drive was a
very bad one, and I ended up having to return 2 firewire versions of them
because of instability.  [You don't want to know how many expensive dual
layer coasters they made -- on 2 different G5's.]

Toast 7 has the ability to compress long movies or files into 4.7 gigs.  I
have not really noticed an appreciable difference between a compressed DVD
and a full-length 8.x gb DVD.

There was an initial severe incompatibility with Toast 7 and the dual layer
firewire.  It may have been worked out, but you should question LaCie
specifically if you decide to go with the dual layer.

My $.02.

Bob
