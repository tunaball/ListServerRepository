Hi Folks,
   I have not been following this thread, but I have had a lot of  
experience with hangs lately.  In my case, for some reason, airport  
is hanging my system.

If you can log into the account after a safe boot (boot with shift  
key depressed), log into the account, and add activity viewer to the  
login items, at the top of the list.  Then reboot and log in.  This  
may tell you something.  If this fails, perhaps logging in as another  
user, then running top outputting to a file, then doing a fast user  
switch to the affected user might tell you something.

-Drew
