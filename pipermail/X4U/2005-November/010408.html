First, thank you very, very much for starting a new thread that has  
nothing to do with mathematics!

Second, when was the last time you cleaned your CD drive? Your  
problem may be caused by a piece of lint, a dust ball or something  
similar that's blocking the laser. The best way to clean the laser is  
to use one of those disks with a brush on it. Failing that, a very  
short blast of compressed air aimed into the drive while the tray is  
out may do the trick. Or it may be that the drive cable isn't firmly  
seated.
