>that has a special adapter-cable. We 
>purchased a Mac Mini today to which I hope to use the hp 1120c with 
>maybe a usb cable. Will I be able to do this?

No, I'm afraid not.  The InfoWave product provided a serial connection 
(the PowerPrint parallel to serial adapter), not a USB connection.  You 
could purchase a parallel to USB adapter, and you might be able to find a 
driver that will work, but you will end up spending just about as much as 
you would for a brand-new, better, inkjet printer, and it might not work 
very well.
