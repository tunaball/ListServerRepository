On Mar 7, 2005, at 5:35 AM, Robert A. (Bob) Hall wrote:

> I have two questions.
> 1. Does anyone know of a OS X printer driver available for the HP 
> DeskWriter 520? HP says they will not be providing one.

Take a look at Gimp-Print for Mac OS X:
     http://gimp-print.sourceforge.net/MacOSX.php3

There's also:
     http://www.linuxprinting.org/macosx/


They don't mention DeskWriters at all, but it may be close enough to 
some they do support to work.

BTW, Gimp-Print is included in OS X 10.3.

> 2. How can I drive my HP DeskJet 930c from two computers, a G3 and a 
> G4, without having to leave one on to allow printer sharing. Is there 
> some form of arrangement using a USB hub to allow this.

You either need to find a print server that supports Macs - most don't, 
and those that do usually only support Postscript printers - or get 
Keyspan's USB server:

http://www.keyspan.com/products/usb/server/    (~$120)
