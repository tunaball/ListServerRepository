Valerie Maples said:

> I cannot find the memory anywhere; can anyone help me with

You can max out the RAM in the HL-1440 for only $9 by purchasing this 
32MB RAM SIMM:
http://www.18004memory.com/focus.asp?sku=505058
The added RAM is necessary to be able to print at the printer's maximum 
resolution of 1200 X 600.

It is very easy to install.  You remove two screws to take off a door on 
the back of the printer, which esposes the slot.  You angle in the SIMM 
and snap it in place, and replace the door.

>Also, do you have to order a toner or is it with the items?

The printer comes with both a toner cartridge and drum when new.  If you 
purchase it refurbished you will have to ask what it comes with from the 
dealer your are purchasing it from.
