Success!
i posted a while back that I wanted to know if I could send wireless 
video over an airport express. Well I used a Toshiba network camera 
IK-WB01A (which) as 802.11b support built in, but I wanted the "g" for 
more bandwidth. Anyway I was able to do this by resetting the range of 
IP addresses in the Airport Express to the same range as the Toshiba 
network cam. Thought this might interest a few of you. it works very 
well.
