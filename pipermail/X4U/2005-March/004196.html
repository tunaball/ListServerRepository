The Mac OS X Conversion Kit: 9 to 10 Side by Side by Scott Kelby (published
by Peachpit Press) seems like the book you're looking for. There is a Jaguar
edition and a Panther edition. Obviously I recommend the Jaguar edition for
your buddy's mother, since that's what she's using. I question why he
upgraded her to Jaguar instead of Panther, but maybe there's some pertinent
info I don't have (she uses a Mac that supports Jaguar but not Panther,
etc.)
