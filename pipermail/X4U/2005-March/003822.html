Richard:

Sorry, I'll have to amend my prior statement that "Laserwriters" are 
supported.  Turn out only SOME laserwriters are supported.

1) apparently only poscript Laserwriters ( ie, none of the Quicktime 
based Laserwriters)

2) oldest one that I recognize is the Laserwriter II.  No Laserwriter 
1 is listed.  That's not to say that it WON'T work, but there's no 
specific PPD file for it, so you can't be certain.

In X 10.3.x, go to 
System>Library>Printers>PPDs>Contents>Resources>en.lproj, that 
contains all the Laserwriter PPDs, and you can see if your printer is 
listed there.

You would need  either a ethernet-to-localtalk or USB-to-serial 
adapter to hook it up, as Randy mentioned.
