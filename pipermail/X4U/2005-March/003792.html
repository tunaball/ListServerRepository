The HL-2040 comes with 8MB of memory, which isn't upgradeable.  If you 
print complex PDF's, large graphics, etc., this printer may not have 
enough memory.

<http://www.brother-usa.com/printer/printer_detail_AREA=PRINTER_1&PRODUCTID

Actually you could argue that the HL-5140 is the 1440's replacement.  It 
can print at a higher resolution, it has a faster processor, it has a USB 
2.0 interface, and it can hold much more RAM.  But it sells for almost 
twice the price of the HL-1440.

<http://www.brother-usa.com/printer/printer_detail_AREA=PRINTER_1&PRODUCTID
=HL5140.aspx>

Here are the specs for the HL-1440 by comparison.  As you can see, it has 
excellent specs for the price.  At the price, I've been telling folks 
with offices that they should consider purchasing one for each employee 
instead of purchasing one expensive workgroup printer.

<http://www.brother-usa.com/printer/printer_detail.aspx?AREA=PRINTER_2&PROD
UCTID=HL1440>
