    If you have not experienced the true joy of Smart Playlists yet, you 
are missing a lot.  I have 40 Gig of Music on my powerbook, and a 10 
Gig iPod.  I sync several smart playlists that limit the number of 
songs on the playlist and constantly revolve the music on the ipod 
either by least recently, or least often played.
    And yes move all the music to one itunes folder that both of you can 
use.
