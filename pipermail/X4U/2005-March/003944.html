To be a little more clear with some analogies (for what it's worth)

A hub is like a party line - all the devices connected directly to it see
all the traffic on it. Imagine trying to communicate in a crowded
restaurant.

A switch is like having direct lines - devices only see the traffic that's
specifically directed to them.
