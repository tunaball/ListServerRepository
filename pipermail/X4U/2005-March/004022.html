I've ordered one (wireless) and one (USB) used it for (1) day and 
shipped them back.

Its control is terrible - it would constantly move the cursor location 
by itself.  In other words, if you need "real" control of your mouse, 
stick to some another vendor's USB mouse.  I do a ton of image 
manipulation, web design and video edits and I can tell you it was 
extremely difficult to perform simple pixel edits with either of the BT 
Mouse.

I'm using a (BestBuy) $19.00 Logitech USB Two button/scroll mouse and 
love its perfect use EVERYDAY!
Hope that helps!
