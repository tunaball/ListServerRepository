I had this problem once as well... check to see if you can copy and 
paste between apps and documents.  The problem I had was with the 
process that runs the clipboard.  I am trying to remember how I went 
about fixing it, but I think there was a post somewhere on an Apple 
Discussion Board.

Look at the logs in Concole to see if there are errors referencing 
"PasteboardUI server" or PBS... this is the actual process you will see 
in Activity monitor.

The only discussion I can find now gives this command to restart it...

nohup /System/Library/CoreServices/pbs &

I think I had to delete some prefs or something too.
