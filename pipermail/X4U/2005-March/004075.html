Davis Instruments has a product called WeatherLink that works with 
their weather systems. It is a software/hardware combination. They have 
a version that works with MacOS X and USB Macs.  I just ordered an 
updated weather system from them for our local radio station that I 
hope to install within the next couple of weeks.  The WeatherLink 
software lets one analyze the weather data on the Mac, plus it allows 
you to upload data to a website to create weather crawlers, etc.
