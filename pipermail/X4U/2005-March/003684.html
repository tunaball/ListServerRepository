Just a few questins:

What version of Photoshop / Mac OS / Acrobat are you using?

Where is the "Create PDF" function in Photoshop's Print Option? Are you 
talking about the "Save as PDF..." button in the main Print window?

I'd be creating a greyscale doco, adding a spot colour channel then doing 
Save As to a Photoshop PDF. That'll give you a 2 colour PDF. You'll have 
to activate Separations Preview in Acrobat to see both colours as they 
will print.

But my recommendation depends on what versions of what you're running.
