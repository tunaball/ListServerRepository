Paul

phones/faxes from the US(?) to Japan are in the following format:

intl dialno.+countrycode+city/areacode+number. 

long distance/out of local area numbers in japan all begin with a zero
"0". but in intl dialing it gets dropped, hence the parenthesis above.

Japan's base numbers are 8-digit, thou as you can see, not always
written to reflect that... an easy way to figure is to work back from
the end of the string as follows, so you get:

1 814 4989 7451 (or maybe 00 814 4989 7451 in your case?)

obviously, it makes no difference to the computer how it's grouped, but...

hope this helps.
