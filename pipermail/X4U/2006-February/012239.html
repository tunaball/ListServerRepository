1. Command-F
2. Click on a search criteria pop-up menu
3. Choose Other... and a sheet drops down
4. Select Filename from the list
5. Check the Add to Favorites box at the bottom of the sheet
6. Click OK
7. Filename should now appear in the criteria pop-up menu under the gray
Favorites category

See, it's as simple as 7 steps!
