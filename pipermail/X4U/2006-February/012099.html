> I have iphoto 2.0.1 on Panther and was wondering if, after putting the
> photos that show up in the main window in their own album on the left,
> I can delete them (but not trash them) and still have them. I find it
> takes forever for the pics to load up in the main window (the
> library?). I have a B&W upped to 800 mhz but it's still pretty slow on

No, you cannot remove photos from the main Library and still keep them. But
what you can do to speed things up is to choose View>Film Rolls and then
close the disclosure triangles of all the rolls.
