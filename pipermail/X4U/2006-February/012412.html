Have you installed any Mail add-ons? Check in:
  ~/Library/Mail/Bundles
/Library/Mail/Bundles

Also, you may want to see if you have any input managers that could  
be causing problems:
~/Library/InputManagers/
/Library/InputManagers/

Also, check under System Preferences/Universal Access/Keyboard and  
make sure sticky keys is turned off as this may explain this somewhat  
bizarre behavior.

One other option is to try a safe boot or other troubleshooting  
techniques listed at apple's website:

http://www.apple.com/support/mac101/help/

Give that a try. I've not had any similar problems with my 10.4.5  
update and have a number of Mail Bundles installed (GPGMail,  
MailTags, etc.)

Good luck,

Norm
