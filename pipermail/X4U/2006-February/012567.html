Alexandre really helped me out:

Per Apple Article ID 86671, it says:

The PowerBook G4 audio line in port takes signals from audio  
equipment, including line-level devices such as amplified or self- 
powered microphones, and is specifically designed for high-level  
audio signal such as the signal from a powered microphone. If you use  
a nonpowered microphone with the audio line in port, it will result  
in a very low volume signal, or possibly no signal at all. Third- 
party options, such as Griffin Technology's iMic, allow the use of  
many mic-level sound input devices with your computer.
Article ID: 86771
Date Created: June 10, 2004
Date Modified: June 15, 2004
