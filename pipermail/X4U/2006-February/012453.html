As merely a datapoint for others' consideration in case they  
experience the same thing:

I have a 15" 1.5Ghz Powerbook (the last model BEFORE Safe Sleep was  
introduced).

I had used the Safe Sleep hack to get Safe Sleep to work, and it  
worked flawlessly up until 10.4.5

Since 10.4.5 however, "SystemUIServer" has not worked properly after  
waking from Safe Sleep.  I have been forced to hard-power-off the  
machine on more than one occasion.

It may be mere coincidence, but I mention it as a factoid for others  
to consider.
