Hi,

I think copying ~/Library/Mail and the ~/Library/Preferences/ 
com.apple.mail.plist to the Quad prior to launching Mail should do  
the job.

If you have launched Mail already, make shure it is closed and  
replace ~/Library/Mail and ~/Library/Preferences/com.apple.mail.plist  
with the ones from your old Mac.
Especially make shure not to keep any folders or else from the first  
lauch of Mail under ~/Library/Mail, otherwise you will see merged  
information of the old and new configuration.

I use this to synk the mail between my Macs at work, at home and on  
the road. As long as the accounts don't change you have only to  
synchronize the ~/Library/Mail between different Macs.

HTH
