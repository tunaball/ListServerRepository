Chris:  Yes, we did create several "Untitled Folder"s.

But what makes the difference, as John Switlik pointed out, is what "View" you are in.

If in "Icon" or "List" view, the "Untitled Folder" will appear totally empty when opened.
If in "Column" view, the desktop contents will appear in the first column with the "Untitled Folder" highlited, which means that the second column (empty) is the contents of the "Untitled Folder."

The fourth option, "Cover Flow" behaves as "Icon" and "List View" – that is, totally empty folder.

Make sense?
