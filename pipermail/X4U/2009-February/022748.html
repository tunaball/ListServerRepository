I have a G4 Mac mini that was located at another office. It was  
running OS X 10.4.11. It was having trouble starting up (showing a  
folder/flashing question mark), any attempts to boot off another  
volume, DVD, Firewire, etc., failed. It is used primarily for  
Internet, Mail.app and Safari, plus a little bit in Pages and Numbers.  
I brought it back to my local office where I have my shop resources,  
and was finally able to get it to boot off a spare Leopard 10.5.3 DVD.

instructions for resetting the PMU on this model consistently fixes
