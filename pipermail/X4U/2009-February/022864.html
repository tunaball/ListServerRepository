MacBooks, or MacBook Pros?

MacBooks have *always* had a glossy screen, from their introduction in May,
2006.

MacBook Pros have had a choice of screen up until the most recent unibody
"redesign" announcement, 10/14/2008.

You can check specs of individual models at
<http://lowendmac.com/macbookpro/macbook-index.html>
