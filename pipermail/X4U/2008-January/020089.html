I click on USB under Hardware in System Profiler, and then click on each 
USB Bus, and I find two that are described as built-in. The details show 
me what hardware is connected and at what speeds they are running. My 
mouse is running at 1.5 Mb/s and my keyboard is running at 12 Mb/s. 
Certainly not USB 2.0 speeds.

To be more definitive, I suppose you could connect a known USB 2.0 
device to the built-in ports and check in System Profiler to see what 
speed is shown. (I take it you have already done that as you show a hub 
listed at 480 Mb/sec. If it were attached to a USB 1.0 port, it would 
not show a speed greater than 12 Mb/sec.)

Or you could check Mactracker (app) to see what your computer model is 
shown to have.
Looking at Mactracker, none of the G4s show as having USB 2.0, while all 
G5 do show as having USB 2.0.
