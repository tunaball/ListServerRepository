There is a lot of interest in the MacBook Air here, and this is a tiny  
island in the Indian Ocean.   Whilst it is out of the price range for  
many, they are impressed with look of the 'Air' and this has now got  
them looking at Apple products generally.    We do have an Apple  
reseller on the island.

The pilot group are largely Mac users with many switching over to  
Apple after they have been sitting on a flight deck for hours  
comparing each others laptops on not just looks, but reliability of  
the OS.     Many of them are now waiting for the 'Air' to arrive in  
the Apple stores in Europe and Australia so they can have a good look  
at it, see what it does and does not do and make a decision from there.

Two have preordered them.    We also have a queue of people wanting to  
buy Thomas's MacBook when he makes the change to the 'Air'.   I  
believe the 'Air' is a great addition to the Apple laptop range.
