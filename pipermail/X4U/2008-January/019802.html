I bought a MacBook a few months ago specifically for travel. Had the  
MacBook Air been available, I would have seriously considered it. We  
already have an iMac, so the MacBook sits next to it and shares a

traveling, so wouldn't miss that; for home use I wold just buy the  
optional drive. Now that I have the MacBook, which I like a lot, I  
obviously won't buy the new Air, but when I need a new laptop, I'll  
give it or its successor serious consideration.
