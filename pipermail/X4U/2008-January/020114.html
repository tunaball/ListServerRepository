I have used Verizon Wireless for several months now, and while
it's not as fast as my Sprint DSL here in SW Florida, it's adequate
for preparing and publishing newsletters, which is my business.
If the power goes out or there's a phone line problem, it's my
fallback. And when I'm traveling, it's worked so far everywhere I've
been.

Something to consider: Verizon offered me the choice of a card or
a USB modem. I chose the USB for a variety of reasons, but the
downside is that it puts a heavy draw on my MacBook Pro's battery.
On the other hand, if you can usually find an AC outlet, that's no
problem.
