Ah . . .could be the high voltage power supply then (although I  
haven't heard that there was a systematic issue with them on iMacs  
like the one the old Plus had years ago) . . . also could possibly be  
something up with the OS installation . . .although I would tend to  
doubt that unless it's not booting at all and just crashes with the  
horizontal lines.

If it's booting behind them OK and it's just display artifacts . . .  
then I would suspect HW issue of some kind.

I s'pose you already tried the Disk Utility, repair, etc? Again, not  
likely to do anything useful to help . . . but I always try them  
anyway if nothing else seems to work.

You might try resetting the Open Firmeware as well  . . . just in case  
something in the video setting section of that got corrupted. I can't  
remember the exact command after you apple-option-O-F boot to get to  
the OF prompt . . . but a quick google should turn it up.
