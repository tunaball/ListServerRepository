Speculating on what Apple was thinking . . . I am thinking they see a  
future where devices like hard drives, DVD Drives, Cameras all have  
wireless built in. Kind of makes some sense, if you have 3 computers  
in your home why not share some of the peripherals? I'd bet that most  
of the purchasers of the Air Books already own other Macs.

Thinking paradigm shift, software comes via the Internet, backups  
happen wirelessly, is the weight and expense of the DVD drive  
necessary? I'd say nope.

I clearly remember my Windows using friends (and many Mac users, too)  
thinking it was stupid to not include a floppy drive.

So we may have just witnessed a change in direction for Apple.
