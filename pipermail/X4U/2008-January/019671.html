At 9 30  -0800 1/8/08, Zane H. Healy wrote:
>How do you tell people how to access your .Mac public folder?

Mac users can just go to the "Go" menu in the Finder and choose 
iDisk/"Other User's Public Folder" and enter the member name.

PC users can download the iDisk Utility at 
<<http://www.mac.com/1/idiskutility_download.html>> and run it, and 
enter your member name. I do this all the time at work on my Windows 
XP machine.

Or anyone can reach it using a browser pointed at 
<http://idisk.mac.com/[membername]-Public>
