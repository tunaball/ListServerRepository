Hi, Paul...it just so happens that I stuck my little toe into the dark side and got Office 2011 for Mac just yesterday.

Here's what I did..I typed out some text, formatted it as Times, then highlighted it, control-clicked it, Selected "Font" and at the bottom of the resulting dialog is the word "Default…"
Click on that and you get a box saying "Do you want to make Times the default font?" or words to that effect...plus a warning that all new documents will respect this change based on the NORMAL template.


Pretty simple and lickety-split.

Happy formatting!
