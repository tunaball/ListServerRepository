Hi Mitchell,

As a first step, run Airport Admin Utility and select your Airport  
Express unit. Click the Configure button. Then click on the Music tab  
(far right on my version). Make sure that Airtunes is enabled. If it  
is, try rebooting the AE and see if that corrects the problem.
