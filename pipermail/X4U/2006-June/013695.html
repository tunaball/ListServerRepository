As the original question was to 'stop PDFs from opening'. I am going to address that.

The 'official' way is to open Adobe Reader preferences, and then click on the Internet section in the left column. You will then see at the top of the window a check box for 'Display PDF in browser using:"

If you uncheck that, PDF's - no matter how they are presented by the webserver - should download to your hard drive and not display in your browser.

Another way is to go to /Library/Internet Plugins/ and remove the 'AdobePDFViewer.plugin'
