On Feb 1, 2012, at 4:04 PM, Al Grappone wrote:

> I received email that has 6 photos that were scanned together on one page. Is there a way or an app that I can use to save each  as a separate photo?

You can save the photo to the desktop, open it in Preview (assuming you have it) and then cut, crop, re-save etc inside the app. Otherwise, any photo editing app - Photoshop, iPhoto, Graphic Converter, Pixelmator, et al - can do it too.

Hope that helps.
