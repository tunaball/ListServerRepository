>>Sounds like a font conflict.
>>Use Font Book (in your Applications folder) to both Validate Fonts 
>>(and repair them)
>>and to Resolve Duplicates.

>>http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?path=FontBook/2.2/en/5285.html
>>
>>It is also possible that you have conflicting preferences files left 
>>over from an older version of Word:
>>http://word.mvps.org/Mac/Word2008Issues.html#LaunchCrash

>>http://word.mvps.org/Mac/TroubleshootingIndex.html
>>

>>http://word.mvps.org/mac/Diagnose.html
