If you read reports on how to optimize your performance you'll find 
that you degrade it by going with 4 or 8 modules.  The Apple website 
shows the systems as shipping with either 3 or 6 1GB sticks.  They 
will do configs with 4 or 8 sticks if you want, but the standard is 3 
or 6.

There is no option to have 1 or 2 sticks.
