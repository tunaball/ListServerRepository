I thought that it was "desktop transporter" but when I found that  
product on VersionTracker.com, it wasn't want I was remembering.

second computer as a 2nd monitor to the first computer.  When you  
move the mouse pointer to the edge of your primary screen...it  
automagically appears on the other screen.  I believe copy/paste  
worked fine between the screens, but obviously the secondary screens  
CPU is doing it's own thing, so any programs running on the other  
computer are running on their own CPU.
