On Dec 4, 2006, at 5:56 PM, Simon Forster wrote:

> What's your backup strategy? Daily back-ups to tapes, one for each  
> day of the week. One weekly copy stored off site. Monthly archives?

For work files, I do multiple backups to external hard disks and to  
an iPod whose battery is otherwise dead. For other stuff, such as  
music, just to external hard disks. I've got lots of hard disks, and  
have never yet lost files because of these backups.

I also do occasional CD/DVD backups of work files, and back up some  
really important stuff to my iDisk.
