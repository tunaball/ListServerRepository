I installed Office 2004 on my MacBook, which already had Office X.

This was some time ago, and Office 2004 has been running very well,
faster in several key spreadsheets than X had been.

IIRC, initially on occasion the wrong Office version would open, and
also I had to take a look at the new preferences.

I would suggest you ensure that 2004 opens instead of X, and also
make sure the preferences are correct (that Office brought them over
correctly). In some apps, with some preference settings, the last used
directory is proposed for new opens and saves (instead of the default
location). This may be not what you expect, especially when you first  
work with
a document in Office 2004.

Overall, Office 2004 works very well on MacBook, with 10.4.8.
