Are you sure it's not _supposed_ to behave like this?

I sold a customer a Sweex KVM [1] this year - it is PS/2 but they  
also do a USB model [2] and I would imagine this might well use a  
standard chipset or otherwise be rebadged as yours.

On this KVM the manual says that double-clicking the CTRL key will  
operate "screen switching" mode, but on the underside of the switch  
box itself it says to use (I think) the PC's PrntScrn button. Once  
this mode is activated the display switches back & forth between the  
two displays, alternating every 5 seconds or so, until you press the  
escape key on the keyboard (to tell the switch "this is the screen I  
want to use now, thanks").

I know Macs don't have a PrntScrn button equivalent to that on PCs,  
but maybe it's possible you've accidentally triggered the KVM's  
screen-switching mode in some other way? Next time it misbehaves, try  
escaping first!
