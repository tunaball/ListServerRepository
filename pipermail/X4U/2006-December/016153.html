Warning! Alert! Caution!

FWIW, be very careful with reseting Safari. That action deletes the contents of
~/Library/Safari/, which includes your Bookmarks and History files, and may
also muck with your login.keychain (~/Library/Keychains/) and cookies
(~/Library/Cookies/) files. I never reset Safari without making copies of those
beforehand.
