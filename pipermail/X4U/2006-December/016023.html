Problem solved.
The above info was a clue, since my usual send/receive application, 
SeaMonkey, had not yet been turned on this morning! Aha!
I searched my Dock, and found Entourage was active.
I opened it, and found a message in the *outgoing* box, that I had put 
there a couple of months ago.
On opening that message, I saw a small line of text below the message 
window that said, "'Send & Receive All' in 5 minutes." or words to that 
effect...

I couldn't send that OLD test message, because I had never properly 
configured Entourage. So, it kept trying to send that test message every 
time it was activated, but all it could do was beep and tell me it was 
still trying to send it.

I deleted that test message and all's quiet now. ;-)
