<http://dealmac.com/Wondershare-PDF-Editor-Pro-for-Mac-or-PC-downloads-for-free/1020543.html>

Some hints and tricks for downloading and using Wondershare PDF Editor Pro:

They ask for your e-mail address to download and register your copy.  It's likely that they are going to spam that address.  So, instead of giving them your real e-mail address, create a free temporary e-mail address at which to receive your registration code using Mailinator:
http://www.mailinator.com 
Instructions:  Give them an e-mail that you make up in the @mailinator.com domain.  e.g.: download at mailinator.com
After you give them that e-mail address, and they say that they have sent you the registration code, go to the Maininator Web site, put your made up e-mail address into the field, hit Return, and pick up your e-mail from Wondershare.

There is a mini-tutorial for Wondershare here:
http://www.wondershare.net/mac-pdf-editor/guide.html

It's not entirely obvious how to delete text in a PDF in Wondershare.  Here's how you do it:
http://www.wondershare.net/mac-pdf-editor/guide.html#tab_02

Enjoy!
