---- Mark Des Cotes <lists at marksmandesign.ca> wrote: 
> I have to create a small animated movie loop of a local film  
> festival's logo. This logo will appear on the big screen at our local  
> cinema before each movie during the festival. They gave me a DVD  
> showing me the animation of their old logo. I have no problem  
> creating the animation but they said they wanted to keep the  
> soundtrack that plays in the background of their old animation.  
> What's the best way to extract audio from a DVD (AUDIO_TS and  
> VIDEO_TS folders)?

If the DVD is not encrypted, you can use MPEG StreamClip to rip directly from the DVD and export to Audio.

If the DVD is encrypted, you would need something like MacTheRipper to copy it to your hard drive first.
