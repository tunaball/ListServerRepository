Thanks to everyone who was scratching their head as I was.

Problem solved! o save everyone time, should they encounter a similar  
problem, here is the solution: visit your computer at night, or turn  
off the room lights.

I noticed that all of the lights on my external stack of drives (I  
have three) were not on. The iPod is daisy-chained off of the last  
drive in the daisy-chain. I turned it on, and Viola!, the drive lit  
up and iTunes popped to the front, showing the iPod. Synching took  
place immediately.

We had a power outage several weeks ago, brief, but enough to turn  
the computer off. The drive must have gone down at the same time, but  
since I only use it for backup, I didn't notice it.

Thanks for acting like a sounding board and letting me solve my own  
problem.
