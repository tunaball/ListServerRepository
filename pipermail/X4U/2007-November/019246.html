> ...if they're not searching my home library (under Leopard) :-( e.g. a
> search for 'com.apple.ActivityMonitor.plist' turns up NOTHING :-(

1) Go to Finder -> File -> Find...

2) In the 'Searching "This Mac"' window, select the "Kind" pull-down
menu, and choose "Other..."

3) In the "Select a search attribute" dialog sheet, find the attribute
"System files", and click on the "In Menu" checkbox.
