Neil,

I assume that you have probably solved this by now, but you made me 
very curious, and today I finally had a few minutes to finish 
figuring it out for myself.

I have a domain called "adajessup.com" and it has a few subdomains, 
including "daly.adajessup.com".
I followed the instructions at: 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?path=Dotmac/Member/en/gen367.html>

and here's the result:  <http://daly.adajessup.com>

The secret, after creating your CNAME entry at your domain host, is 
to put your site folders and index page in the /Web/Sites/ folder on 
your iDisk.  I think your error was putting "homepage.mac.com" in 
your CNAME entry.  The correct entry is "web.mac.com".

Cool! Thank you very much for pointing out this new feature of 
dotmac!  I'll play with it after work today.
