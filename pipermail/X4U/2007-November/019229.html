I had that problem, I solved it by removing the dust from a small opening in
the back of the iMac. It also collects under the screen in the numerous
openings in the bottom. I was amazed it was such a difference after I did
that.

I shut down the machine and unplugged it before I did the dust sucking with
a plastic covered paperclip.  I'm rather anal/obsessive about static
producing tasks in the dry climate I live in at 7,000 feet.

Hope that's it. karen
