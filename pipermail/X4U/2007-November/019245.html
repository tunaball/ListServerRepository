Maybe you missed it or it occurred on another list. CMD+F, click Kind, select
System Files, click don't include button, and select include. Put in
ActivityMonitor.plist and be surprised. Save the search and use it any time you
want to search everywhere. Alternatively, you could modify the
default_smart.plist buried in the Finder.app for all your searches.
