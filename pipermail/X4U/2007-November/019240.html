Then have him click on the iPod icon in the "devices" section on the  
iTunes sidebar.  Then click the "info" tab.  There is a Contacts  
section.  Make sure that "Sync Address Book contacts--->All Contacts"  
is checked, not just "Selected groups."  See if that works.
