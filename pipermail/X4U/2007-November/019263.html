> I have a friend who has seen the light. She has a Mac iBook and  
> wants to use her Dell A670 printer.
>
> I tried to navigate the Dell website and after 5 minutes I wanted  
> to commit harikari.
>
> Any suggestions how and where I can get the driver for a Dell A670  
> printer. I tried the Apple support page and didn't get much further.

Dell probably don't offer any Mac drivers for their printers, but you  
may have luck configuring it through CUPS <http://localhost:631/>.

As you will see from <http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi? 
make=Dell> some of Dell's printers are rebadged but otherwise  
identical to other manufacturers' models; I think some may now be  
uniquely Dell. You will also see from that page that some of Dell's  
printers are useless under anything but Windows, but you may be lucky  
- using CUPS myself I have found that some printers require little  
more than the right PPD file copied to the right place on your Mac  
before you're able to configure it correctly.
