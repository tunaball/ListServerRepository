Popping sounds after updating to Mac OS X 10.4.10
Issue or symptom

After updating to Mac OS X 10.4.10, a "popping" sound may be heard  
from external speakers connected to an Intel-based Mac. The sound may  
be similar to a sound heard when the computer goes to sleep or wakes  
from sleep.
Products affected

     * Mac OS X 10.4.10
     * Intel-based Macs

Solution

Download and install the Audio Update 2007-001
