The replacement battery arrived today, and prior to replacing it I  
thought I'd check the figures again in System Profiler.

Co-incidentally with the cycle count being incremented - for the  
first time in 5 days, or the first time in 5 months? - the charge  
capacity has dropped MASSIVELY:

Battery Information:

   Battery Installed:	Yes
   First low level warning:	No
   Full Charge Capacity (mAh):	2442
   Remaining Capacity (mAh):	2403
   Amperage (mA):	0
   Voltage (mV):	12501
   Cycle Count:	303

Compared with the charge capacity of c 5500mAh on my mother's  
identical laptop, this would seem to clearly confirm my observations  
of shortened (by an estimated 50% from new) battery life.

The icon of a grey-outlined battery next to the clock today shows  
inside it a black power-plug symbol and next to it the word  
"charged". It seems to me that this is the first time in a while that  
I've noticed this black power-plug symbol, but of course "to notice"  
is an inconclusive condition.
