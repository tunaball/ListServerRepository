On 2/2/08 12:21 PM, "Cindy MOORE" <cindypmoore at yahoo.com> wrote:

> When I shut down my G5, it stops and says "mirroragent canceld shutdown". Then
> I'm given a dialogue box asking if I want to "continue". By clicking this I'm
> able to shut down. My computer hasn't always done this. Any suggestions as to
> why and how to stop it? I'm running Tiger PS10.4.11.
> Thank you, Cindy Moore

Based on some quick googling, Mirror Agent is a component of .Mac. To keep
this from happening, you can disable iDisk or open the .Mac pane in System
Preferences and change the setting from "Automatically" to "Manually" for
the "Create a local copy of your iDisk."

It also sounds like this hasn't always solved the problem.
