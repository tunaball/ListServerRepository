No. Best solution IMO is to get another 200+ bare HD, one of these
externalcases <http://www.pcmicrostore.com/PartDetail.aspx?q=p:10500933> and
<http://www.amazon.com/Macally-Firewire-Storage-Enclosure-PHR-250CC/dp/B0000B3ALD>,

needs, putting a bootable backup/clone, using Carbon Copy Cloner, SuperDuper!,
Disk Utility, etc., onto it, ensuring that it can boot the machine, swap the
HDs, and subsequently erase and partition the oldHD residing in the new case.
