I recently did this and found a couple of apps that helped out.   
You've already ripped the disk, so now you have to rip out the  
audio.  I used bbDemux for this.  In my case it ripped out AC3 files  
from the VOB files created by MTR.  Then I ran the AC3 files through  
mAC3dec to encode them into mp3s which I imported into iTunes.

Both apps were pretty much just drag and drop simple.  bbDemux and  
mAC3dec can both be found on VersionTracker.
