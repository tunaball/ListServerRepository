On Feb 26, 2008, at 8:08 PM, Konrad Waibel wrote:

> As an adult sensitive to light, the cataract replacements seem to  
> have made me more so. The Mac Book Pro will work very nicely, and I  
> have the option of adding on a large display.

I've had the lens in my right eye replaced due to cataracts, and  
subsequent laser surgery to treat a secondary cataract. I also live in  
sunny Southern California and find the glossy screen choice a no  
brainer for me. The brightness and clarity is so much better. As  
mentioned in other posts, the difference is most noticeable when the  
matte and glossy screens are compared side by side. Even more so in  
direct sunlight as the matte screen was nearly unusable unless set to  
full brightness and the glossy screen looked brilliant at half that.  
My sensitivity to light has never been an issue with regard to the  
glossy screen nor has glare.
