Dave,

I believe you're seeing "ToolTips" with URLs on mouse hover events.  
The easiest way I know to disable this is to open a Terminal window  
and enter:
	defaults write com.apple.Safari WebKitShowsURLsInToolTips 0
To turn it back on change that 0 at the end to a 1.

Good luck,
