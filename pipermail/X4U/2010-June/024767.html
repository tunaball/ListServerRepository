EMC picked up Dantz's Retrospect and has brought it up to speed with 
version 8.2. for ~$95. I use Carbon Copy Cloner, 
shareware/donationware. It will do what you want for $10-15. I

With Time Machine or any good backup program, you can backup any part 
of any volume to any other disk volume.
