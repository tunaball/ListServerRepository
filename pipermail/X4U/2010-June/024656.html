If you have a high speed Internet connection, you could get a wireless  
router. I use Apple's Airport extreme. It was very simple to set up  
but you might want to get some help setting up your network.

Once you do this you will get the added bonus of being able to reach  
the Internet via your iPod Touch in a fairly large area. That depends  
on the actual topography of your home.
