On Apr 16, 2012, at 6:20 AM, Joe Sporleder wrote:

> what y'all would recommend as a good alternative camera for the Mac  
> minis, since they don't have a built in camera.

You can use any Webcam on the market if you pick up a third party  
driver:

Macam (free)
driver for USB webcams on Mac OS X. It allows hundreds of USB webcams  
to be used
http://webcam-osx.sourceforge.net/

IOXperts Webcam Driver for Mac OS X  ($20)
http://www.ioxperts.com/products/webcamx.html

CamCamX ($10)
http://b-l-a-c-k-o-p.com/CamCamX.html
makes non-Apple webcams iSight compatible
