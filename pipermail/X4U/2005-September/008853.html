On 9/21/05 6:30 PM, Michel Treisman wrote:

> If Option is held down, during Start or Restart, this allows the
> startup disk to be chosen at that time.
> 
> But not for me.  It has stopped working, I don't know why.
> 
> Where is the fault, and what can I do to put things right?

It only allows you to choose a startup disk if you have more than one
disk/partition with startup folders present. If you have, say, OS 9 and OS X
on the same partition, you won't have a choice.

If you *do* have two startup disks or two startup partitions present, and
cannot get the choice at startup, it means that one of the partitions/disks
is not seen as a valid startup volume. If one of the disks is external, make
sure your cables are not loose! If all of the disks/volumes are internal, it
might be time to break out disk troubleshooting software.
