> I am trying to install iPhoto 4 from the iLife 4 CD, and I get the

> "iLife cannot be installed on this computer.
> This installation cannot proceed because one of the following  
> applications is running:
> iTunes
> iPhoto
> iMovie

>
> None of the three applications are open at the time of

I believe that there is an iTunes helper that is activated at login.  
Go to System Preferences and in Accounts/Login Items delete the  
iTunes Helper from login items.

Logout, login, install.
