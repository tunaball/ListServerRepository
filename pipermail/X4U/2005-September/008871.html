I moved my iTunes directory to another volume once and it worked 
beautifully.  The way I did it was:

1. Made sure iTunes was not running
2. Copied the "~/Music/iTunes" to the new location
3. Opened iTunes and changed the iTunes Music folder location in the 
Advanced tab of the iTunes Preferences window.
4. Deleted the "~/Music/iTunes/iTunes Music" folder (the original 
folder containing the songs.)

It seems that iTunes keeps the music database files ("iTunes Library", 
"iTunes Music Library.xml", etc.) in its original folder, 
"~/Music/iTunes", and only lets you move the folder where the song 
files are located.  So if you moved those too, that could be the reason 
why it can't find the songs (?).
