Do either one of you have a routable (or public) IP address (i.e. not  
in the private address space as defined by RFC 1918)?  I know that in  
the past, when I would try iChatting from my home computer (with NAT  
on our home network) to my husband on a business trip (also on a  
network with NAT), we could not get sound or video to work; text and  
file sending worked fine.

When I would try the same test from work with a routable IP on my  
computer, the video, audio, and text all worked.  I'm sure there is  
some workaround for this, but I've not had enough time/interest to  
try to figure out where the issue lies and/or how to fix it.  These  
results were true when both of us were on Panther and are still true  
even now that I'm on Tiger (he's still on Panther).
