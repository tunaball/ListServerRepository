Don't know if this is relevant, but read this in iPhoto's help section.




Working with photos taken in RAW format

If your camera has a RAW setting, taking photos in RAW format lets  
you save every bit of photographic detail as part of the actual photo  
file. iPhoto 5 imports RAW-format photos just like any other photo,  
organizes them right next to the rest of your shots, and lets you  
edit them using advanced editing tools designed to give RAW  
photographers all the options they need to create the perfect image.

Note: Mac OS X version 10.3.6 or later is required for importing and  
editing photos in RAW format. To see a list of RAW-format cameras  
that are compatible with iPhoto 5, visit the iPhoto website.
