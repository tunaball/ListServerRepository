I don't like the flip phones. I had a Motorola and returned it. The volume
in the earpiece part of it was lousy. Even a full volume I could barely hear
anything. And the signal strength I got it was poor too. I got a Nokia
instead, not as good looking but a WAY better phone, excellent earpiece
volume and much better signal strength in all areas. I will never go to a
flip phone again.
