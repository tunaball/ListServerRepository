to be garbage.  (Sometimes CR's error was that they probably didn't test 
the products for as long as I had, other times it was because they were 
looking at the wrong things.)  Could I write to CR and relate my 
experience with those products and warn others not to purchase those 
products?  No.  Could anyone else write in to CR to warn me about other 
products that CR made a mistake in recommending? No.
