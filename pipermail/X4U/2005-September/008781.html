On Sep 17, 2005, at 1:33 PM, Vern Andrews wrote:

> I created a user account for my boys, and now when I boot my iBook,  
> it always boots into my kids account. How can I set my iBook to  
> startup in my account instead?

I don't think you want to start up in your account either. You want  
to keep the kids at bay. You can however, set up your machine so that  
it starts up to the login screen and from there you can log into any  
account you want.

System Preferences/Accounts/Login Options (you may have to click on  
the lock to unlock that function). In the Login Options pane deselect  
"Automatically log in as:"
