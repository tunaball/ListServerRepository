If you haven't used them for a while they become somehow deactivated.

Widgets belong to the Dock process. So if you have Widgets running and
switch to hierarchical view you can find them if you expand the triangle
next to the Dock process.

I have not used my Dock for a while, so there's not triangle next to it
although I have two widgets running. Then I open Dashboard, et voila, in the
background I can see a triangle added to the Dock in the Activity Monitor
and attached to it the Calendar and Calculator Widget using 20 and 18 MB
(what a joke! People would tear Microsoft into pieces for such kind of
bloat.).
