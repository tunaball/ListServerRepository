On Sep 15, 2005, at 2:00 AM, Oliver Marshall wrote:
> Is there such a thing as a backup package, ideally open source or  
> cheap, for Tiger ? Im after a backup package rather than a copying  
> package like Rsync or Psync; something that will do incremental,  
> differential, and compression, as well as scheduling and ideally  
> backup to ftp.
Retrospect, by Dantz (http://www.dantz.com/), is the best known  
solution.

Apple's Backup 3, part of their .Mac package, claims to do  
incremental backups.

For more start at Apple's home page, click on Made4Mac, and chose  
"Productivity & Utilities" from the Software list on the left side of  
the page. Then choose "Backup and Recovery" from the "Select a  
category" area.
