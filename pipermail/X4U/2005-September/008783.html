You may want to download this free utility to make clearing several 
caches quick and easy:

Yasu
http://www.jimmitchelldesigns.com/yasu.html

I know that there are several multi-function utilites that will do the 
same thing, but Yasu has the advantage of being free, and also that it 
doesn't have many features that can get you in serious trouble if you 
don't know what they are.
