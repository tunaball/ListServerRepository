On 9/17/05 7:57 AM, Brian Lalor <blalor at bravo5.org> wrote:

> Good morning, all.  I just bought a NEC 3540A DVD writer for a Power
> Mac G4 I've got at home.  Having just lost the 80G drive on my
> PowerBook, I decided I needed to start taking backups seriously...
> The drive supports the full array of DVD media (including, I just
> noticed, DVD+R DL).  According to the Apple knowledgebase, DVD-R is
> the only media supported by the SuperDrive.

Maybe the original SuperDrive only supports DVD-R, but now there are many
Mac models that support burning to DVD+R, DVD+RW, and DVD-RW.
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=300665

I seem to recall that one of the 10.3.x updates brought this support, so
make sure you're updated to 10.3.9 and you should be okay.
