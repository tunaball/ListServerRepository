I've been using Verizon's wireless broadband service with my PowerBook
15-inch, and it works fine. Was even able to use it while stuck on the
runway at Charlotte. Verizon's service is 1xRTT/EVDO. The card I'm using
with the service is a Sierra Wireless 5220. I know that they are also
shipping a 560 card with the service.

For me, the big upside was that I popped the card into the PCMCIA slot and
it immediately worked. I didn't have to install any additional drivers. I
haven't had that same experience using this card with a Dell laptop.

Cingular and Sprint also have broadband wireless services, but I haven't
tried using those.
