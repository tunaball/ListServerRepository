Hello:
	I fixed it, in case anybody is interested:  I performed a Google  
search and found the following article:

http://www.macosx.com/content/faq.php/q624/Problem-With-Finder-Help.html

taken from the following article:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=107013

	Basically, I just renamed the help cache directory (i.e. got it out of  
the way), and it was rebuilt and now Help helps me fine. :)
