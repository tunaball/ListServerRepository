If you hit the center button once while the track is playing, you can
scroll through the track using the wheel.  Of course you can't hear it
while it's scrolling so I don't know if it will help you in the end. 
Also, mine seems to have the behavior of progressively getting faster
as I hold the FF button down.

Actually, that just got me thinking ... so I did a little test.  It
seems that for a "normal" length song (5 min) it will progressively
seek faster and faster as I hold down the FF button, but for a 30 min
podcast it seeks at a consistent speed of about 20 sec.  I suspect
it's because the 5 min song fits entirely into cache while the 30 min
podcast does not.
