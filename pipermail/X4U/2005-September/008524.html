And of course, because they're human, they're agenda driven.   
Sometimes you can figure out their agenda, other times you can't.

Their car reviews work fairly well because they've invested a ton of  
money to collect the statistics.  Sometimes their car reviews get  
biased because the reviewers like yugo's and horses and think the  
combustion engine is the single largest threat to the earth.  Other  
reviewers are more even handed, you just have to pay attention.

Their computer reviews generally take many liberties mostly because  
of the astronomical number of permutations in software/hardware/ 
accessories.  It usually works ok for the casual user, but often  
there are much better options left unevaluated. The reviews tend to  
be pretty superficial for my tastes.
