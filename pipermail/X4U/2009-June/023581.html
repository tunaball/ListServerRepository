Find your station in iTunes, (under "Radio", select genre and home in, etc,
as you would) then drag the one you want into the column on the left
("Source Column"), but not into an existing playlist, so it's there by
itself. Then when you click (select) it in the left column (once) a playlist
will open in the main window area with _one_ item, the one you want.  Double
click that and you're playing.

I've been trying to find a way to save a playlist on your Desktop to
double-click which will launch iTunes and start playing the station, but
it's a little complicated to explain and I'm not sure what it does
precisely. An item in the Dock would be cool.

Hope this doesn't hurt your head.
