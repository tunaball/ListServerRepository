Yes, you can run Windows and Windows software on your PPC Mac.  You  
need an emulator called "Virtual PC" from Microsoft.  I used it before  
I switched to Intel Macs.  A little slow, but it works.  Pay attention  
to the OS limiations before purchasing, though.

http://www.apple.com/downloads/macosx/system_disk_utilities/virtualpc.html
