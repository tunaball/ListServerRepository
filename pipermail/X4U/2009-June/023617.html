The question wasn't about running Windows, but about running PPC  
programs.  You don't need Virtual PC.  Rosetta is built into the Intel  
version of OSX since the Intel Macs came out.

There might, however, be some program-specific issues that you can  
research on Google before you make the leap to Intel.

Unfortunately, without someone on this list who has 1) an Intel Mac  
and 2) a copy of the PPC version of FCP, there is no way to know for  
sure.
