I rely on SpamSieve to deal with spam, not rules. After training, it 
typically flags spam with an accuracy rate of 99.7% or better. However, 
there is another way to deal with spam if you have a MobileMe account. 
I use aliases when I have to enter an email address online. When an 
address begins to attract spam, I simply delete it and create another 
one. As a result, I now get fewer than one/day.
