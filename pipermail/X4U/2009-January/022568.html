It's solved.  He found a solution on the net.  I didn't one could find  
one.  Otherwise I wouldn't have asked for it.
In the SYS prefs in Universal Access select "Mouse" and on top one can  
see "Mouse Keys" On or Off.  His one was set to ON.
He told me he didn't do anything.  And I didn't know this was  
possible.  Now I know and it's solved.  Thanks.
