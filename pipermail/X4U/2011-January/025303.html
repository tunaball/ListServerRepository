The solution is to use a real text editor.  Text Edit is more of an  
ultra-lightweight word processor.

I use:

Tex-Edit Plus ($15)
http://www.tex-edit.com/
...because of it's ability to clean up text from the Internet with  
one keystroke.

But if you want something free, you might prefer:

TextWrangler (free)
http://www.barebones.com/products/textwrangler/
