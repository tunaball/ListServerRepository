>I just picked up a Nikon Coolscan V from eBay and downloaded 
>VueScan. What tips can you tell me for scanning slides and negatives 
>with this combination?

Which adapters do you have?  I have the adapter that lets me do 
strips of film, and the one for slides.  Slides are a piece of cake, 
I usually just do a basic scan using light cleaning and maybe light 
grain reduction.  Negatives can be a lot tricker.  If you're doing 
colour, you'll have ICE, but if you're shooting B&W you won't. 
Depending on what film you're shooting VueScan might have a profile 
for your film.

Colour negatives are where I've had to monkey around with the 
settings under the 'color' panel the most in order to get a good 
scan.  In the Filter Panel the "Restore colors" and "restore fading" 
can be surprisingly useful on occasion.

The main thing is to get in and play around, see what works best for you.
