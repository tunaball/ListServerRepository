Filemerge comes with Snow Leopard Preinstalled, and maybe Leopard as well. If you use Tiger you must install the Developer tools. Filemerge is included in developer tools in the folder:
/Developer/Applications (at least under Snow Leopard). 

It will at least show the differences of two folders gui style. Although the rest is up for you to do manually.
