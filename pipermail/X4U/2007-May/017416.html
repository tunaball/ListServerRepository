A new Mac, as part of its initial setup will bring you into the Migration Assistant, prompt you to hook up the other computer via firewire and give you import choices. If you are purchasing a used computer and have Tiger installed, you can explicitly launch /Applications/Utilities/Migration Assistant.app and get to the same place. It's a piece of cake.
