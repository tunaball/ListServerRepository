Hello list,

Yesterday I asked for help on setting up a Canon LBP 3360 printer on  
a network. I am pleased to say that the problem was solved and it is  
now running. We got it right by making all settings on the printer  
default and then re-opening one by one. What I found strange was that  
the manual and my Apple consultant said AppleTalk must be on at the  
printer side, but we turned it off. Reminds me a bit of the days  
under OS 9 when we turned all the goodies off and then on again one  
by one to see what was causing the problem.
