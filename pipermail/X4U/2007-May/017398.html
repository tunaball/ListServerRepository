I have on occasions seen a considerable speed improvement by running  
iDefrag. I have seen defragmentation utilities derided here as being  
unnecessary, but as I understand it OS X's on-the-fly defragmentaion  
only touches files of 20meg+ - I guess that on my machine there may  
have been much fragmentation on smaller files. Whatever, I can't  
argue with the results - it definitely made a difference here.
