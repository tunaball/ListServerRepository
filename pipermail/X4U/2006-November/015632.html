    Specifies volumes sizes.

     Syntax

   -v{Size}[b | k | m | g]


    {Size}[b | k | m | g]
            Specifies volumes size in Bytes, Kilobytes (1 Kilobyte =  
1024
            bytes), Megabytes (1 Megabyte = 1024 Kilobytes) or  
Gigabytes (1
            Gigabyte = 1024 Megabytes). if you specify only {Size}, 7-

            treat it as bytes.
