TextEdit does word count?  Where and how?

To answer your question, BBEdit Lite 6.1, if you can find it, will give you
the statistics you want.  One would think its bloatware successor,
TextWrangler, would also, but BBEdit's count agrees with Word and InDesign,
while TextWrangler is not even close.

In a typical word processor (for instance, MS Word) add the "number of
paragraphs" to the "number of words."
