In addition to my K&E, I also have a 10-inch Japanese Sun Hemmi Model  
153 Electrical Engineer's Slide Rule, made of bamboo, which I bought  
on the streets of Tokyo in 1950.  I paid about $3.00 for it.  It got  
me all the way through a BS in EE at Georgia Tech and an MS in EE at  
Stanford.

It has all the usual scales, plus a set of very accurate vector  
scales for engineering, for example:

   3 + j4 = 5 @ arccos (0.6)

It does complex algebra routinely.

BTW, I'm not completely a Luddite.  I also have an Intel iMac with  
1GB RAM, about 750 GB of HD, System X 10.4.8 and a good fast internet  
connection.
