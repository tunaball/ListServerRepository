On 11/4/06 4:30 PM, revDAVE wrote:

> Question: I'm confused - how could they both be the same resolution but also
> have a 2 in. difference in their size? Shouldn't the 22 in. have more wide
> pixels than the 20 in.?

Not necessarily. On an LCD monitor, pixels can be different sizes. My first
LCD monitor was a Viewsonic 15", 1024x768 pixels. Now you can find that
resolution on 12" monitors -- same NUMBER of pixels, but everything will
look smaller on the 12" -- but will be the same size *relative to* the rest
of the screen.
