Almost always an alignment problem of the drive and slot. If cooling  
doesn't get it out, try a little pressure on the case to flex down  
the corner where the drive is. I set the laptop in my lap and press  
front right and back left slightly.
Another try would be a thin slip of paper slid in over the disc to  
help guide it to the slot on eject.
Either way, you need a trip to warranty service to align the drive  
and slot.
