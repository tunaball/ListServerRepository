On 11/29/06 12:15 AM, Paul Moortgat wrote:

> I use a G5/2.5 and I just did a restart with the sound switched off.
> I also use a headset witch is also selected in the soundprefs.
> I didn't work.

Unplug the headset.

In Sound prefs:

Sound Effects tab: Show volume in menu bar (checked). Checked Mute button.

Output tab: Digital Out > Optical digital out port.

Input tab: Line in > Audio line-in port.

I just tested, and this works on my G5 Dual 2GHz. No startup chime.
