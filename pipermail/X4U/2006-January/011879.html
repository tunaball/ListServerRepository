I enjoyed your thoughtful post about why you prefer FireFox to 
Safari. On this one  point, though, I want to point out a feature you 
might have missed in Safari. If you have put folders in your Tab bar, 
then if you open the bookmarks page (with the "book"  icon at the 
left end of the Tab bar) then you can go to that tab's entry and on 
the right side, in the middle column labeled "Auto-Click" you can 
put a checkmark in any of those folder tabs and when you go back to a 
web page you will see that you can open all the tabs in that folder 
with a single click.

I use it all the time. This is a minor thing, compared to 
right-clicking a folder tab, but it IS a nice feature and I don't see 
that you can do it in FireFox.
