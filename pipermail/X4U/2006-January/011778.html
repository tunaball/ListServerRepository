Backstory before I get to the question (in case it's helpful):

My 15" Alum Powerbook running 10.4.4 froze up during the log-in 
process this a.m. for the first time.

I had been running it for a half-hour in Target (firewire) mode to 
update my main iMac after a trip out of town... just manually 
dragging and dropping certain folders (nothing

main account in the desktop mac. I've done this before and haven't

After I unmounted the Powerbook from my iMac and unhooked the

woo-hoo it loaded fine.

The only non-Apple thing I see in the menu bar is for Now Up to Date.
