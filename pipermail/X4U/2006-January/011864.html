SiteBar. Free, opensource, really universal, with plugins to better  
integrate it with Firefox, Explorer, Opera, etc. See my snapshot at  
<http://www.inf.unibz.it/~franconi/sitebar.png> (please also  
appreciate the Safari-looking theme of Firefox...).
It relies on you setting up a mysql database (possibly where you have  
your http server).
<http://sitebar.org/downloads.php>
