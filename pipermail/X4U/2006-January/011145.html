Yes I do have have files in folders though I have made a playlist as  
suggested in apple's help files and on this list but that did not work.

Another members on this list sent me the following:
>

>  try clicking the '+" sign at the bottom left side of the screen.   
> Name the album you want to burn.  Now go to your library and  
> isolate just the songs you want to burn.  Highlight them and drag  
> them to the new folder on the left of the screen.  Click the new  
> album collection (left side) and check it to see they are all there  
> are you wanted.  Now you should have a "Burn Disc" option on the  
> top left of the itunes page.

This worked

Thanks for all the suggestion to help with what ought to be simpler  
than winking.
