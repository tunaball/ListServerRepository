I'm not familiar enough with any of the Mac disk utilities/testers to  
recommend any of them, but if you can get access to a PC there is  
free disk diagnostics software at Hitachi's site. The best disk  
testing software on any platform is Steve Gibson's Spinrite ($90),  
but it is also PC only.

I'd have to agree with Stroller in that I would suspect the drive  
before I would the controller on the motherboard. Even though hard  
disk failures are so rare that most consumers will never see one, the  
physical failure of a hard disk's mechanics is far more likely than  
that of the electronics on your motherboard.
