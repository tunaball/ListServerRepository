I read a "best of" article in MacWorld and they recommended the 5th gen
version of the Speck Toughskin.  I'm pleased with it so far.

http://playlistmag.com/products/complete/96-detail.php

That has thick rubber that offers great protection from accidental drops.

install film and not have to open anything to access the controls.

http://playlistmag.com/products/complete/85-detail.php

I already got a scratch on the enter button in the short time I had the
iPod.  That's why I ordered the film.
