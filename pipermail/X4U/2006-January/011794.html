> How does one access the chooser to change the printers in os9 classic from
> panther? (assuming the computer is NOT dual bootable in 9)

Launch a Classic app, that'll display the Classic menu bar, click on Apple icon
in the upper-left hand corner, and, if you've put the Chooser into the /System
Folder/Apple Menu Items/, select Chooser.
