In anticipation of all the replies saying "It's perfectly stable for  
me" then all I can think is that you don't have several windows open,

days without a crash in Omniweb.... and then really unfortunate  
because I loose the clutter of windows that I've been meaning to  
refer back to since the day before yesterday.

The reason I'm addicted to Omniweb is the thumbnail view of webpages  
in the tabs. It's the killer feature that keeps me using this  
browser, even though it's such a colossal pain.
