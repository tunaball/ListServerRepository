On 01/05/06, George at Dorothy Hennings <hennings at verizon.net> wrote:
>
> How do I use CCC to copy an external disk drive, and then make a Disk 
> Image?

CCC->Prefs->select the Create a disk image on target option.

> How much smaller in GB or MB will the Disk Image be?

Probably larger, since I don't think it's compressed. For that, use Disk
Utility->Help->Creating a disk image.

> Can I put the Disk Image back on the same external drive and replace 
> the external drive contents at the same time? (Assuming size is much
> less).

Only if you manually erase the external HD before copying the image to the HD.
CCC won't do that.

I suggest that you check CCC's Help and the CCC forum stickies at
<http://forums.bombich.com/> for more details. If you have other questions,
post them there. BTW, why are you bothering with disk images?
