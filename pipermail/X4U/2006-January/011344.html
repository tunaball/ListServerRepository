Do you have iTunes 6.0.2? Have you tried Cmd-Shift-M? The keyboard  
shortcut opens or closes the MiniStore pane at the bottom of the  
songlist.
	When the MiniStore pane is open, every time you select different  
songs by different artists i.e. go from "If Not for You" by Bob Dylan  
(New Morning) to "If Not for You" by George Harrison (All Things Must  
Pass) the selections in the MiniStore change to [sorta] reflect the  
differences. Close the MiniStore pane and it stops recommending new  
music.
	It's just a new feature, nothing to get excited about.
