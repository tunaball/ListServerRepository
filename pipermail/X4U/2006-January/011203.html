> After some experimentation, I have found the following settings
> convenient/useful for (Apple) Mail:
>
> [Rotate Right] to [Scroll Down] when reading a message;
> [Rotate Left] to [Scroll Up] when reading a message;
> [Click] to [Send Key] -> [Delete] to delete a message:
> [Long Click] to [Send Key] -> [Down Arrow] to move to the next
> message (without deleting the current one);
> [Click & Rotate Right} to [Send Key] -> [Right Arrow] to expand a
> thread; and
> [Click & Rotate Left] to [Send Key] -> [Left Arrow] to collapse a
> thread.
>
> These allow me to go through a Mailbox quickly and with minimal
> keyboard/mouse usage.
>
> Can anyone suggest customized settings to program for other
> applications? Thanks!
