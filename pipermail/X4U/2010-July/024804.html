See:
http://support.apple.com/kb/TS1448?viewlocale=en_US

You can safely ignore "Warning: SUID file X has been modified and  
will not be repaired" messages.

You can also usually ignore any "ACL found but not expected..."  
messages.

These messages are accurate but are generally not a cause for concern.
