On Jun 28, 2010, at 9:33 PM, Jim Warthman wrote:

> My question is, what other 3rd party backup applications (e.g.  
> ChronSync, SuperDuper) support incremental backups, with the  
> ability to maintain multiple versions of files that have changed -  
> like Time Machine?

You are asking for "versioned backup" software.  Programs like  
SuperDuper create "clone" backups, not versioned backups.

for your needs:
