XP will check and repair that NTFS volume.

Inside of XP, get properties on the volume, and then click on the  
Tools tab or something like that. Inside that you should be able to  
check the disk for errors. Also, check the 2 check boxes in the lower  
part of the window. It will probably want to reboot. Let it.
