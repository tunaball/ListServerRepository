I don't know if it ever won awards, but I truly miss Adobe PageMill  
from OS9.  I always seemed the most intuitive, to me at least, for  
editing simple web pages full of thumbnail photos generated from  
graphics programs like GraphicConverter.  What I always liked is that  
it worked from a Site point of view, with each page shown graphically  
linked to other pages that contain weblinks to it.

Nothing easier than dragging-and-dropping one page's icon onto the  
highlighted text on another page, and having an automatic link  
established.

I've tried Freeway, et. al., but still haven't found anything as easy  
to use as PageMill.  (And that includes iWeb).
