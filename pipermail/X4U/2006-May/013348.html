Ok, here is what you do ....

1) Go to the Inbox and highlight an e-mail account
2) Hold down Control (not Option), and while holding down the Control  
key, click the mouse
3) Choose Get Info from the menu that pops up
4) Another menu will appear ... From the pull down menu, choose which  
e-mail account you want to view messages on the server
5) Highlight the messages that you want to delete from the server
