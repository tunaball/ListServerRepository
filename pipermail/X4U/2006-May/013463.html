On 05/10/06, Jesse Leo <jleo at projectsupport.com> wrote:
>
> I know it's kind of like finding a needle in a haystack, but a client here
> gets very frequent crashes in MS Office 2004. Seems to mostly happen when
> she highlights text to change font, or hits the drop-down within Entourage
> to change font. Most people are telling me there is a damaged font -- any
> ideas what I can start looking for and how to troubleshoot?

Assuming she's running Tiger (no computer/OS version mentioned), launch Font
Book, select All Fonts, click in the Font window, CMD+A, to select them all,
and then File->Validate Fonts-removing any damaged/corrupt ones. When that's
finished, then CMD+A in the Font window and Edit/Resolve Duplicates.
