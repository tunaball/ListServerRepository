Apparently, you need the ATI MAC ROM image flashed (from a MAC version of
the card) to the PC card. There have been reports of limited success with
various ATI video cards. Start with this article and follow links:

http://forums.xlr8yourmac.com/action.lasso?-database=faq.fp3&-layout=FaqList&-response=answer.faq.lasso&-recordID=33548&-search

Seems like a lot of trouble and could be a crap-shoot.
