On Apr 28, 2006, at 5:59 AM, DZ-Jay wrote:

> I'm giving it a shot, thanks!  Hopefully it'll fix a long-standing  
> grip I've had with form widgets in Firefox on MacOS: that the  
> <select> boxes do not get focus when I "tab" through the page.

This can be accomplished through a simple setting change in Firefox.  
I don't recall where I read this, but I nearly ditched Firefox in the  
beginning before I found this tweak was possible.

1. In Firefox, enter "about:config" in the address bar and press Enter.
2. Find the setting for "accessibility.tabfocus" in the list.
3. Enter a "7" for the value and press Enter.

You should now be able to tab between all page elements, including  
<select> lists.
