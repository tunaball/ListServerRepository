Michael Grant said:

>How the heck do I reset my modem (eMac built-in) without restarting the
>whole bloody computer?


You could send it a reset command in a terminal program like Zterm:

Zterm (free)
http://homepage.mac.com/dalverson/zterm/

Just type ATZ into Zterm and hit return.  That should do it.
