Sometimes jobs just get hungup, have noticed that with printing in 
classic. Also because it's a classic app you can get info on it and 
increase them memory on the app. Are you ip printing or appletalk? Also 
one important note, you might want to have your users check the page 
setup of whatever app they are printing from. More often than not, the 
default settings for the paper size is US Letter Small. Some printers 
don't know how to handle this paper size, make sure they are selecting 
the proper paper size in the page setup.
