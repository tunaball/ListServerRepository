Hi,

I made the same decision some time back but keep finding myself dragging 
PDF's to Acrobat so that I can edit or do other tricky things with them.

Anyway, to do it you select the PDF in the Finder and choose Get Info. 
Then turn down the "Open with" tab and choose Preview. Then hit the 
[Change all] button and you're set.

Also, in the earlier version of Acrobat (6.0) it took an inordinate amount 
of time to load. If you upgrade to 6.0.2 you'll find the loadup time is 
significantly faster. Not quite as fast as Preview still but the change 
may be significant enough to make you want to stay.
