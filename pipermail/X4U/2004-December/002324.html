just to add :

I call on a regular basis skype users (Girlfriend, family, friends) in 
Geneva (Switzerland) from the bay area. Until now, I found this is the 
best solution for voice conferencing with mixed clients (PC, Mac). The 
sound is clear, easy to understand even with people with a poor 
connection. It is also quite impressing when you actually do a 
conference with several persons at different locations.

Saves me a llllooooootttt of money.
