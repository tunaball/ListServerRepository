> Hold down the Fn key when you press the delete key...
>
> Combine with the Alt key to delete words (in most apps)
>
> Delete: backward delete char
> Fn-Delete: forward delete char
> Alt-Delete: backward delete word
> Fn-Alt Delete: forward delete word
