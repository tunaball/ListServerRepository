Abiword 2.2 was just released, and there is a native Mac OS X 
version.  http://www.abisource.com/download/  It's a 4MB download, 
and with just a basic look, I'm rather impressed.  It handles MS Word 
(opened a Word 2004 document with it), RTF, HTML, and a couple other 
formats.  It's not quite what I'm looking for, but it's very close. 
In fact, the next time I get ticked off with MS Word, I just might 
switch!

I'd seriously recommend anyone that doesn't own MS Word to download 
it and try it out!  After all, it's free, what do you have to loose 
:^)
