Erase the HD in Disk Utility.  Install OS 9 and offer the latest OS X 
system you legally purchased.  If you try to install Panther, it 
immediately goes into the computer "set up".  I have had very good luck 
selling Macs on EBAY.  Hopefully, you kept the boxes that the iMacs 
came in; that makes it so much easier to ship them.  There is no need 
to hand these computers down.  Older Macs easily sell.  I just sold a 6 
GB/333Mhx Clamshell iBook on EBAY and there were plenty of people 
bidding.  I was very surprised at the amount it sold for.
