As Marc points out, with a .Mac account your mail is always available 
though the mac.com website. It will also synchronise your Safari 
bookmarks & iCal appointments between machines (and your mobile phone), 
and gives you 250meg of file-space which can be mirrored on each 
associated Mac.  Beware it's slow & unreliable, but you can test it 
free for 90 days, so might be worth looking at.
