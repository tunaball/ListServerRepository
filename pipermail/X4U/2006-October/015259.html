Paul Moortgat said:

>A friend who has his 630 Apple printer is looking for a Printer  
>Utility for use in OS X.  Is there one?


The Apple LaserWriter 630 was a Postscript Level 2 printer with both an 
Ethernet and a LocalTalk connection.

There is a generic Postscript Level 2 driver in GimpPrint, included with 
OS X, and there are also specific drivers for the LW630 native for either 
PPC or Intel.

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=301175

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=301175#appledrivers

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=303057

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=303057#appledrivers
