I used a D-Link DP-311P to hook my LaserJet 4000 to my Airport network and
it worked flawlessly.  I had to abandon it when I upgraded to a Netgear
WGR614 and WPA because the D-Link only supported WEP encryption.

D-Link now has a Model DP-G301 which is 802.11b & g and supports WPA as well
as WEP.
