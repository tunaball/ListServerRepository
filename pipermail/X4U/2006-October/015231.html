That is arguable. However, consider the leader lines...

Page One:

	"Mac attacks rare but rising"

Click through to article and the headline changes...

	"Security analysts: Mac attacks rate but may rise"

Read the article and you find...

	"targeted iPods used with Microsoft Windows-based computers."

So, when you get to the real story, it turns out to be "Devices  
connected to Windows OS have a higher risk of being compromised" and/

Wow, now there is a couple of real mind benders. The lead headline is  
misleading, then the article title retreats to a more realistic but  
speculative position.

CNN is simply selling ads and smearing Mac in the process but I bet  
all the tech editors and writers own Apple stock.
