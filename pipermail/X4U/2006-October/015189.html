Setting a Mac to target disc mode and connecting it via Firewire does 
nothing more than allows the hard drive of that computer to mount as if it 
was an external hard drive.

As far as I remember, when it asks you to boot your old Mac in target disc 
mode, ignore these instructions and just connect the external drive. You 
can then choose the startup drive when asked by Migration Assistant.
