I had this problem for some time, tried all kinds of things, but  
eventually it stopped.  I think it is because I had Mail running on  
two computers and the signatures were off.  .Mac sync'd it over and  
over until I finally got it the same.  One other thing you could try  
is going to Preferences > Accounts > Accounts Information > Server  
Settings and change the server port to 587.  That worked for me.
