version of Debian Gnu Linux. I have never investigated what hardware  
(ie hard drive) it contains as it has worked faultlessly out of the  
box with both Linux and Mac clients. It comes with 2 USB ports and  
built in printer support. At one time I had them hooked up to an  
ethernet switch which was connected to an Air Port Express and that  
worked fine except for streaming video while other parts of the  
network were in use.
