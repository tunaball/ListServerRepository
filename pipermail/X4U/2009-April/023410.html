Having static IP addresses on the local network helps with connecting  
and sharing files on the local computers. If you are using Bonjour on  
the Macs then this is not a  big issue but if you have mixed  
computers, ie. Windows, Lenix and Macs then it is helpful. Also having  
a static IP can help you to connect from the outside world back to  
your computer on your local network.
