Alexandre,
After only 5 months of normal-type use, my Mini's logic board failed.  
Luckily, I had AppleCare. My local Apple store replaced the logic  
board, but in the process, scratched the heck out of the outside white  
top of the case, which was absolutely cherry. (The Mini had not been  
touched by anyone since I first installed it in my computer desk  
shelf.) I complained to the manager and Apple picked up the cost of a  
new case. Let's hear it for AppleCare!
