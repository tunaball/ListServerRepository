At 6:32 PM +0200, 4/16/09, alexandre wrote:

>i bought the entry-level mac mini (2009) a few 
>weeks ago and decided to upgrade the ram and the 
>hard drive myself. all went well.
>
>does this void the possibility of getting applecare at a later date?

Adding memory or replacing a hard drive does not 
void AppleCare. Breaking something while doing 
anything to a computer voids the warranty. If 
Apple can find that something you did led to or 
caused the breakage of items that would otherwise 
be covered by AppleCare, then AppleCare will be 
null and void. A significant dent on the exterior 
of the case can void internal repair coverage by 
AppleCare. If there is no physical damage, then 
AppleCare is going to cover your rear.
