On Apr 1, 2009, at 7:45 AM, James Hurley wrote:

> I will be getting the new Mac Mini. It sports an 800 speed firewire.
>
> My external HD is 400 speed. I can connect the two with a 6 pin to  
> 9 pin firewire cable.
>
> Of course I will lose the advantage of the 800 speed of the Mini.  
> Any advice on whether I should bite the bullet and cough up the  
> dough for an 800 firewire HD?

You probably won't even notice the difference.

However, if you want to take advantage of the FW800 interface on your  
mini, you don't need to purchase an all new external drive.  You can  
just take the mechanism out of your current external drive and put it  
in a new case that offers a FW800 connection.
