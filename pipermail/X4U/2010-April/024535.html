to enable app sync there's a warning saying apps will be replaced with

believe that message should go together with the first one.

before sync'ing. If you've purchased application A and go to its page
in the AppStore, even if there's a price there, clicking on the
download button will pop up a message saying that the download is
indeed free because you already purchased that (assuming you're logged
in with the user that bought it of course).

Only then I saw this was not necessary.
