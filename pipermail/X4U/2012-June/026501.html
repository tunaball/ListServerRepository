At that point the question becomes, which is cheaper, an iMac with 
32Gb of RAM, or a Mac Pro with 32Gb (or more) of RAM?  For those that 
need large memory configurations, I really recommend running the 
numbers on both the system you're thinking of, and on a Mac Pro. 
When I bought my latest system in late-2010, the difference was 
fairly significant.

I'm sitting at 24Gb, but I'm thinking about taking it up to 48Gb.  Of 
course I'm also a heavy user of Photoshop and other photography 
related software.
