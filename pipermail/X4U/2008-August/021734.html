> I would like to be able to get Apples MAIL.APP to retrieve email

Ed: yes it is possible. I receive and send google mail through Mail on  
my Macs and iPhone.

Go to the Gmail Help Centre and look for the article on "Enabling  
POP". The article tells you what to do on the Google side and on the  
Apple Mail application side.

Note that Google requires a non-standard port (995) to be set under  
the advanced tab in the preferences for the mail account for Google  
and uses password authentication. It also allows SSL encryption and  
this should be checked.

Here is a long link, in case you can't find the article:

http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?answer=13273&cbid=1bzp0ekwjznmj&src=cb&lev=answer

Good luck.
