Zane H. Healy said:

>One specific thing I'm interested in is how to calculate how much physical
>RAM my system really should have.


Check out this neat free (donation requested) utility:

Do I Need More Memory?
http://www.hillmanminx.net/dinmm/index.html

DINMM reports when pageouts are happening, and how often, and suggests to 
you whether you need more memory.  DINMM uses a clever way of estimating 
your total memory requirement ie. the total memory that would be required 
to avoid pageouts for the typical set of applications that you use.
