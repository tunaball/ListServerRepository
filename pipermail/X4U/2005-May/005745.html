On 20 Feb 2005 04:46:15 +0900, Kenneth Philbrick wrote:

 > Google does not provide Gyaz instructions for setting up a POP   account. I have been trying to use their Apple Mail instructions as a   guide but cannot seem to make it work.    Can you provide step by step directions that meet Google's needs?

REPLY: 

The important settings are as follows: - 

Receiving tab in the Accounts preferences

   Advanced: [/] Require secure connection (SSL): [ Alternate Port ]

 - Outgoing Mail Server dialog   Authentication: 

SMTP Authentication

   Advanced: [/] Require secure connection (SSL): [ STARTTLS ]        

     [/] Override default port: [ 587 ] 

Thanks again, Goichi Hirakawa
