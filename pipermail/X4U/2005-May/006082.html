>Depends on what spawned them. Sudo kill -9 is what I use, but that

Zombies never can be killed - they're already dead - that's why 
they're known as zombies.
A zombie process uses no time, but they may hold other resources. 
When a process dies it remains in the process table until their 
parent process checks on them. Normally this is so soon after they 
die that you never notice.  Zombies are still waiting for a check by 
their parent.
