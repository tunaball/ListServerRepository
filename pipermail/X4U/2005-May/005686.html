I have started ripping CD's with mp3 VBR with a minimum bit rate of  
192 Kbps.  For me, this difference between this and 128 mp3 is flat  
out amazing.  Use a visualizer like vTunes-Skyscraper and compare the  
same track ripped different ways - it's amazing to see and hear  
what's been thrown away in the lower bitrate-encoded tracks.  I  
bought a retrospective album by Swervedriver that I ripped this way -  
since I had many of the tracks already (ripped at 128 Kbps mp3) I  
thought they had remastered the audio!  So much richer, definitely  
worth the extra hard drive space.
