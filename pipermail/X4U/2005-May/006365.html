I agree that mindless "ichatting" isn't too productive. My clients 
use it for other reasons though.

Take just one as an example. Prior to iChat one client flew someone 
from Sydney Aust to San Diego CA every six weeks to maintain contact.

Now they have an G5 iMac in the San Diego office with an iSight and 
achieve the same thing without leaving Sydney. With 10.4.1 they can 
now have a 4 way video chat or up to 10 people on an audio conference 
call.

They have re-couped the cost of the iMac in less than a couple of 
months due to savings in airfares and hotel rooms.
