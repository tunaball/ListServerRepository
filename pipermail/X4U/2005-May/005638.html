On 5 May 2005, at 03:50 am, John Bryan wrote:

> Hey, has anyone figured out how to get the weather widget in  
> Dashboard to take non-US cities/countries?

1. Enter the city name

2. Hit the Return key (the widget brings up a 'validating' message)

3. Choose your required city from the list if the widget finds  
several possibilities

4. Click Done
