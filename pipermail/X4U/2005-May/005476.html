Was just poking around in my System Profiler, and my 1Ghz 17" is now  
reporting a DVD-RW compatible burner for the first time ever.  That's  
news.  Have to check it out.

<civitan at jeffporten.com>" from a dropmenu with the "c" key.

All sorts of gizmos that used to require third-party software is now  
built-in.  Dashboard replaced WeatherPop; uControl no longer  
necessary now that I can disable Caps Lock remotely.

My Internet connection seems to have sped up quite a bit; Safari and  
downloads are both humming along much more quickly.
