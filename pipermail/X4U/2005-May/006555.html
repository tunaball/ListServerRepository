Can't answer your question, since I don't use Stickies. However, I do
suggest that you join Apple's ADC at <http://connect.apple.com/MemberSite>
(it's free and also provides you an opportunity, if you wish to exercise
it, to download developmental software), then use the bug reporter at
<https://bugreport.apple.com/cgi-bin/WebObjects/RadarWeb> (which requires
an ADC membership) to submit bug reports or enhancement requests. It's the
fastest way to let Apple know something's not working the way you expect it
to or the way you want it to.
