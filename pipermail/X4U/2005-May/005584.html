Okay, because Michael Gmail demanded it, I hereby announce the  
existence of MessageLaser 0.1, which lets you zap your Mail.app  
messages with Finder Spotlight tags.

MessageLaser 0.1
May 4, 2005, requires Mac OS X 10.4 (Tiger)
© 2005 Jeff Porten

MessageLaser is an AppleScript that allows you to assign arbitrary  
keywords (also known as "tags") to messages in Mail.app.  These  
keywords are then immediately searchable with Spotlight indexing.
