Just as a point of interest, I visited the "zaptastic" Web site will 
booted into OS 8.6 (my b&w G3 can do that), using Mozilla.  I have OS X 
on a different drive than OS 8.6.  The zaptastic widget still 
auto-downloaded, and even though it isn't set as my default download 
disk, the widget was installed on the disk that had OS X on it!  Scary.

For folks looking for a quick, easy way to disable or uninstall widgets:

Widget Manager is a free Preference Pane for OSX 10.4 that allows you to 
inspect, remove, and disable Dashboard Widgets. To remove a widget 
without it, you would have to browse your Library/Widgets folder, delete 
the .wdgt file, and either log out or force-quit the Dock. 

http://www.downtownsoftwarehouse.com/WidgetManager/
