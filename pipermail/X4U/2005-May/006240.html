The transition from using a drawer for the mailbox list (a drawer that
can open from either side of the window) to a pane on the left side of
the window is a somewhat disconcerting change for you, and probably a
number of other users. However, it does more closely align the purpose
and presentation of the mailbox list with that of the analogous Source
list in iTunes and the Album list in iPhoto.

So far as the performance is concerned, I really can't comment on that
as I much prefer Mailsmith as my email client. Spotlight might start to
change that preference as it mitigates one of my reasons for preferring
Mailsmith, but until there is a way to completely turn off HTML support
until/unless I specifically ask for it and the Rules support becomes as
rich as Mailsmith's filtering support, I'll avoid Mail.
