There are not any drivers AKAIK to date to use an iSight on Windoze  
systems.
All your chat associate needs is a program that will allow them to  
chat, and you have to use the same.
Personally, I use iChat while my friend uses AIM/AOL chat. Once the  
other person has you on a list, or chooses to chat with you, the rest  
is handled by the systems themselves.
I have been chatting using it since iSight came out.
You can also use Yahoo (and there are other 3rd party software), but  
the video quality is not as good IMHO.
