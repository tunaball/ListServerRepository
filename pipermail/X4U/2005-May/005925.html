One day I sat down and compared different bit rates to the original  
CD, I took some works from my favourite black musician Erykah Badu.  
All of them sound pourish, shades are lost in different degrees, so  
you get to take a decission having a 20GB iPod. The chosen compromise  
was finally 224 AAC, and 320 AAC for a few exceptions that deserve  
it. I would say as well that if I had an iPod with 20e3 GB then I'd  
use some loseless codec.

I took into account some comparisons on the web as well, this one for  
instance was really helpful:

     http://members.chello.nl/~m.heijligers/ipod/Compression/ 
compression.html

I can't enjoy 128 AAC at all, something that makes me spend less  
money that I would in the iTMS.
