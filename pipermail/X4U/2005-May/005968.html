     I found it interesting that a good many of the widgets only work  
when one is on-line.  It seems that Apple (and probably the kid in  
Redmond) is moving toward assuming that everyone is on-line all the  
time.   How long before dial-up is a thing of the past?



     If this is truly the way things are going, it seems a sort of  
digression.  At first people used workstations that connected to  
giant computers.  Advent of the personal computer was considered a  
revolution.  Now it seems that personal computers are devolving into  
workstations for the internet.  Will it really come to that?  What  
are the ramifications?  More control over who does what with their  
computer?
