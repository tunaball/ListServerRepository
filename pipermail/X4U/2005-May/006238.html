Bring your Pismo up in target mode whine connected to the Bondi by  
firewire. (Target mode is achieved by holding "t" on boot up or using  
Startup Disk Preference.)  This should allow access to the PISMO and  
connected Drives as drives on the Bondi. (Note: Apple claims Tiger  
works on computers that have original firewire drives so the Bondi  
may not work.)
