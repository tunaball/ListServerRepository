Huh? Apple has learned that people muck with the System folder and 
then cry. For the very few that need to search it, the capability is 
there. Better to leave the dangerous tools out of the toolbox that 
every user gets by default.

Apple has also watched people turn off journaling when they shouldn't 
be. Way too many people do things just because they can without any 
real understanding of what the result will be. Apple has been very 
successful at creating a computer that 'just works'. They do this by 
making it a bit more difficult to break than the dark side computers. 
If you or I decide to search and destroy in our System folder, we are 
less likely to whine to the world about what we did when we do it. 
We'll quietly go get a Firewire drive, boot from it, and fix our boo 
boo. Kinda like last night when i removed the Roman.bundle keyboard 
layout. Way too many people would complain that it was named wrong 
and that it shouldn't be named using the 'Roman' term. They'd hate 
that their keyboard no longer worked and would cry to the world. I 
was smart enough to laugh at myself.
