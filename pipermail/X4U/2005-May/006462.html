Mark,

This:

System Prefereces--->International tab-->Times (customize.. button)

In the text field that shows the example of the time format, click  
and hold on the hour portion. It should show a drop-down menu that  
lets you select from "1-12", "01-12", "01-24" etc.  This will allow  
you to select to display in 24 hour time.
