A lapsus calami, surely. In most cases, download is faster than upload. 
In specific settings (e.g., SDSL) they can be equal. I don't know of 
any in which d/l is by default slower than u/l. In Roy's particular 
case, he uses ADSL -- in which d/l is always faster than u/l. That's 
why it's called asymmetric.
