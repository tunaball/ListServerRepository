On 10/22/09, Michel Treisman <michel.treisman at psy.ox.ac.uk> wrote:
> On 22 Oct, 2009, Roy Van der Westhuizen <roy at ion.co.za> wrote:
>>
>> Can someone tell me how to make bookmark folders alphabetic in Safari?
>
> First click on the open book symbol on the left hand of the toolbar
> (left hand end in my copy of Safari) to display a page of bookmark folders.
>
> If XXX is a bookmark folder drag XXX to the desktop. The original
> XXX folder will still be present in the display. Delete it [using the delete
>key]..
>
> Open Desktop/XXX/ and click on Name to order the files alphabetically.
>
> Drag Desktop/XXX back into the display of Safari bookmarks at the
> desired position.
>
> I feel I have probably got some terminology wrong. I hope this is
>understandable.

Totally understandable and works like a champ w/Leooard and Snow Leopard on my
G4 PPC and iMac ICBM (Intel-chip-based Mac). Do note that most people don't
want their bookmarks alphabetized, but rather want them in a heirarchial order
by usage or importance.
