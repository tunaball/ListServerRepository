Just to help ruin your day, I just thought about a few other things 
for you to keep in mind.  Though I'm not sure how easy it will be to 
research them.

1. How long does it take for the camera to go from turned off to 
ready to shoot.
2. How long between the time you push the button to take the picture 
and the picture actually being taken.
3. How long before you can take another picture.

I wish we had paid a bit more attention to items #2 and #3 when we 
purchased the Kodak.  Doesn't help that it was largely an impulse 
buy.  We missed a *LOT* of good pictures of our kids thanks #2.
