Safari, Firefox, or Camino.   I do use statically allocated DNS
servers (at work and at home).  There have been several
complaints in the Apple discussion forums regarding this
exact behavior but no one has nailed it down to a single
issue.

Have you tried logging in under the Guest  account in Leopard
to see if the behavior exists there?  Have you made any other
changes at the same time (upgrading your wireless network,
changing ISPs, etc.)?

Leopard because it is not an issue for everyone (I've checked
with other Leopard users in my organization as well).

Other suggestions:

- reset Safari (I don't see how this could help with other browsers)
