> My Quicksilver G4 won't boot up beyond the initial

Art: if holding the house button down while restarting doesn't eject  
the CD, try opening the CD door with your fingernail. With luck, you  
will be able to see the small hole where you insert a paper clip; or  
you could always pull the tray manually (gently).

Good luck.
