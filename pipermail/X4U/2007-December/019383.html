Dear David:

I also wanted a reasonable priced black and white laser printer for  
every day use.  I can highly recommend 2!  First is the Samsung 2010,  
it sells for just about $100.00 and is very dependable.  It connects  
with a simple USB connection.  Secondly, if you wish to be a bit more  
adventurous, there is the Brother 2070N.  It is a network printer and  
can be accessed my any Mac on a local network.  I got mine for about  
$100.00 also.  Output from both is excellent.  The Brother is very  
fast!  The Samsung is fast but not as fast as the Brother.

Tim
