Are you sold on the whole "pocket camera" bit?  Personally it sounds 
to me like the two of you need a Digital SLR, either a Nikon or 
Cannon.  I believe a couple pocket cameras do offer clip on Macro 
lenses, but very few do 10x Optical Zoom.  Only look at the Optical 
Zoom when looking at cameras, the digital zoom is nothing more than a 
marketing gimic.
