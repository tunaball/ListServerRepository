It appears that Apple has heard the complaints of those of use who prefer
the original usage of the % key.

Mac OS X 10.4.3 or later: Calculator may perform division when using %
button
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=303325>

"In Mac OS X 10.4.3 or later, Calculator's percentage button ("%") may
unexpectedly divide output instead of calculating percentage (for example,
it may divide by 100). Note: This button is only available in the scientific
calculator of the Calculator application."

Whether they change it back or not (I say provide a preference setting) is
still undecided:

"This document will be updated as more information becomes available."
