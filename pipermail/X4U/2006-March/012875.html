For those not following closely the http://winxponmac.com/ website  
has for some time allowed individuals to make a donation to sponsor  
the booting of Windows XP on Intel Macs. This reward is now nearly  
$13,000 and in the last few hours the first solution has been  
submitted for testing.

The photos previously posted by Narf were, like this video, very  
blurry and led to some speculation about their authenticity however  
Narf is known from his postings to OnMac's forums and is said to have  
some credibility. In order to meet the criteria for winning the pot  
the solution must provide for dual-booting between Windows XP & Mac  
OS X in a fairly traditional manner (in the same way that grub & lilo  
traditionally allow Windows XP, Linux, BSD &c to be dual-booted).
