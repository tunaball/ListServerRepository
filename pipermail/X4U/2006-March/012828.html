At 4:37 PM +0100 3/12/06, alexandre wrote:
>hi
>i re-installed my system after ma hard drive died last month. since 
>then, everything (desktop, iApps) uses "am" & "pm" instead of a 24 
>hour clock (as before). i've gone through all the system preferences 
>(time & date, international) but i can't get rid of them. am i 
>missing something?

Go back to System Preferences. Choose Date & Time. There, choose the 
"Clock" tab (to the right of "Time Zone" in the tab bar). In the 
Clock section, you can uncheck "Show AM/PM".
