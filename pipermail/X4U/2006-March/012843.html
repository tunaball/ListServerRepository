What I did recently to solve "missing help" was to go to
~/Library/Caches/ and delete com.apple.help.ui, and then go to
~/Library/Preferences/ and delete com.apple.help.plist and
com.apple.helpviewer.plist.

That fixed it for me. That was all I could remember of others' more
technical suggestions. But maybe this is enough to get you going
again?
