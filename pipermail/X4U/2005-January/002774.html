Having actually played with one at MWSF, I can verify that there is 
indeed a power brick on a cable long enough to hide on the floor 
beneath the Mac mini.  The pictures don't do it justice in terms of 
size.  It's MUCH smaller than you think.

I tried out a couple of apps (they had the new iLife '05 loaded on it) 
and they worked just fine.  Although it doesn't share exact specs, I'm 
sure, it's basically a G4 iBook in a box.  It will be fine for its 
intended purposes, I think.
