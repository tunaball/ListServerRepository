On Jan 16, 2005, at 10:44 AM, Stroller wrote:

>  I could just press the enter key, as this is highlighted in blue, but 
> I almost never want to shut-down the machine - does anyone know if 
> it's possible to change the default, so that I can put my Powerbook to 
> sleep just by pressing the power button and then "enter"?

Press control + eject.  Then press the "s" key for sleep, "c" or escape 
for cancel, "r" for restart, or enter or return for shut down.
