That's the problem with waiting for revised models. No matter how long 
you wait, they're will always be a better one a couple of months away. 
You have to weigh your situation. Do you really need a new computer? 
Does your current machine lack in any way that impedes your 
productivity and justifies purchasing a new machine? I drool every time 
I see a new G5, but my dual 1 GHz G4 is running like a charm (knock on 
wood) and I just can't justify, no matter how much I want it, the 
purchase of a G5. Although, I must admit, this new Mac Mini would look 
good at home in the place of my 400 MHz iMac.
