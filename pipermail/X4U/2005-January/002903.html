Don't know if this will help your problem. But after experiencing 
similar speed reduction in Safari and trying various fixes I finally 
fixed it by removing all the cookies. Under 
Safari>Preferences>Security>Show Cookies Click on Remove all. After 
doing this I found a great overall speed increase in Safari's page 
loading and it also solved a problem where some images didn't load on 
some pages.

Hope this helps.
