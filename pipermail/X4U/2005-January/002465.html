I figured if anyone would know it would be the people on this list.

I was watching TV at a friends house the other day and during a 
commercial (can't remember for what) I pointed out an imac in the 
background. Later that same evening while we were flipping channels I 
pointed out a PowerBook (I think is was on CSI). My friend (PC guy),

showroom.
