If you do have the 17" AlBook then I highly recommend you get a 65W 
adaptor. I have the 17" AlBook at home and the 15"AlBook at work. I can 
use the 15" adaptor (45W) to power and slowly charge the battery of the 
17" but if I try to run my external 7200RPM bus-powered Firewire drive 
while using the 45W adaptor the drive won't mount. It obviously needs the 
extra juice!
