On 10/12/05 12:32 PM, Paul Moortgat wrote:

> I've a Canon manual where I don't see the word "COPY" on screen, but
> printed the word is there.  I use Acrobat 7.
> How can I get rid of that word?

You probably can't. Canon put it there like a watermark, probably to alert
users that they are looking at a copy and not the original.

Does Canon charge for paper copies of their manual? If so, that could
explain why they felt the need to watermark the PDF -- to avoid people
printing out copies and underselling Canon on the price.

If you really need it gone, I would write to Canon and ask either A) for a
different PDF or B) for the directions to unlock their security features.

If you import the individual pages of the PDF into a layout application like
QuarkXPress, and print from there, does the watermark still show up?
