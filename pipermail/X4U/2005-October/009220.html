All you need to do is boot from the Tiger software. When it comes up  
and you finally have a menu bar at the top, just go up to the menu  
bar and find Disk Utility. It will bring it up. Highlight the first  
thing that comes up. It's usually in a different format like: 93.2 GB  
ST9100823A. Highlight that one and it will erase the hard drive and  
put the two partitions into one.
