If you want to use the OpenOffice.org distribution, you'll need to  
have X11 installed.

Instead, I'd suggest using the NeoOffice distribution -- it's based  
off of OpenOffice.org 1.1.4 and doesn't require the X11 windowing  
system.

Find out about it, and download it, from here:
http://www.neooffice.org/
