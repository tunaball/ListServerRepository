Next to the Sepia button? Really? On my iPhoto 5 installations (3 of
them), it is the leftmost button among the third group of editing tools,
Sepia being the rightmost of that group, separated by a dividing line
from the Adjust button. From left to right I see: Rotate (divider)
Constrain, Crop (divider), Enhance, Red-Eye, Retouch, B&W, Sepia
(divider) Adjust, Done, and a pair of navigational arrows.

Contrast and Brightnes, among others (like Saturation, Tint, and
Temperature), are found in the Adjust palette. Enhance is a one-button
"Quickie."
