I've no idea if it is transferrable if you sell the Powerbook.

Having said that, get it.  That 231 Pounds could save you from having an
expensive paperweight.  I sure wish I'd had it on a PowerBook 520c I bought
10 years ago, as soon as the 1 year was up the display went out :^(

For things such as an iPod Shuffle, it's a rip off as far as I can tell, but
for a computer, it's money well spent.  Think of it as piece of mind.  By
the time it runs out, if the system is still running, it's likely to last
for another year or so, and by then if it dies, you'll want a new system
anyway.

Having said that, my almost 2 year old G5 2x2 is the first system that I
purchased it on, and I have yet to use it.
