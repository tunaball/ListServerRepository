I think the message referred to the old Mac extended keyboard, which  
was the best at the time. Stroller is right about the USB and clear &

I also wanted a similar keyboard for my G5, and bought a Matias  
TactilePro, which came set up for the Mac, but with a setup also for  
Windows. This is the best keyboard I have ever used on Windows, Unix  
or Mac computers. I recommend it without reservation. The only  
objection that some may make to it is the healthy sound of the key  
strokes. I do not work in a super-quiet office so it does not bother  
me, and after getting used to it, I like the sound.
