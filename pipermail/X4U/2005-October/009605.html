: 
: Can someone explain if all or just some apps will take advantage of quad
: processors?

All OSX-native apps are multi-threaded and thus benefit from
any multi-proc system.  However, particular apps that do not
lend themselves well to threads won't see as much speed-ups
(e.g. emulators).  I want to say that Classic apps also suffer
from this very same problem, but I'm not sure.
