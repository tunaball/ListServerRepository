Current version is 3.0.3. Downloadable updater is available on the  
Alsoft site <http://www.alsoft.com/download.html>.
     You should take a moment to read the home page info concerning  
start up CDs and newer Macs. If your current version of the DW CD  
will serve as a boot volume on your mac, then the updater is all you  
need. If your Mac is newer than the version of DW that you bought,  
then you'll need to order a new CD--it's free for a shipping charge.  
If you have a second disk or partition with a current version of OS X  
on it, just put DW on it and reboot from that.
