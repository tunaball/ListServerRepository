>
> I have an IMAP connection to a server running Herald. Mail on the 
> server is listed in my inbox.  Recently my TiBook stopped seeing mail 
> on the server that was sent to me by anyone with the same second name, 
> Treisman, as me.  I.e, mail from my kids, or email I send myself to 
> test this  out. Any other mail shows up in my inbox, no problem.

Delete the mail account in preferences & add a new one with the same 
settings. All the mail is on the server, so you won't lose any. It's 
best to do this when you're connected to the university network (rather 
than at home) as syncing the messages will be quicker.
