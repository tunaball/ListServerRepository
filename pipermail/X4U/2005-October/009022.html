On 10/1/05 12:54 PM, Scott Phillips wrote:

> How can I set the default application back to MS Word? (G4 running
> 10.4.2)

Click to select a Word document; Get Info on the document. In the Get Info
window, look down at the Open With drop-down menu; it probably says AbiWord.
Click the drop-down and Choose Microsoft Word. Then, click Change All. It
might take some time, but it should do what you're looking to do.
