>I've had some strange behavior out of my PowerBook and wanted to see if 
>anyone has experienced this or can offer any advice.  It's only done this 
>twice and I can't recreate the problem, so the details may be a bit fuzzy.

         Here is something that seems to work, but this has no scientific 
basis that I can cite.  So it may be pure fiction, but it works for me.

         When shutting the lid on a powerbook make sure it is awake.  It 
seems that if it has already gone to sleep, shutting the lid confuses it 
when it tries to wake again.

         As to transporting a powerbook, it would seem from Apple 
information that transporting a powerbook while sleeping is okay.   The 
manual does say to wait until the harddrive stops before moving, but when 
sleeping, the harddrive should be stopped.
