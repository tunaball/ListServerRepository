Try clicking (and holding the mouse button down) on the iTunes icon in  
the dock. When the menu pops out there should be an "Open at Login"  
option. Yours probably has a check mark by it. Select it again to turn  
it off.

Seems to be a fairly common problem around here where people  
accidently turn this on.
