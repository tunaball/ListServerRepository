One of the real nasties is Boinc. Another is Timbuktu Pro. Put both of  
those on the sideline for a while and see if you clear up some of your  
operating problems.

Instead of using Timbuktu, try iChat to synchronize with your friend  
and let him control your machine remotely with it.

Then, start shutting down the "non-essentials" like BetterFinder  
Renamer, iOrganize, iVisit, Weather, and your program monitors. Get  
rid of the fluff and you have a smoother operating machine, especially  
if you only use the darn thing "once a day".

I am beginning to think that most of your problem comes under the  
title "Owner/Operator Error".
