On 2 Feb 2005, at 11:57 pm, Eddie Hargreaves wrote:

> Apple released iPhoto 5.0.1 via Software Update today. Well, at least 
> to some people. It hasn't shown up in mine yet.

Its not showing up in Software Update is due to the presence of some 
prior iPhoto packages in Library/Receipts. Temporarily moving out 
anything containing 'iPhoto' will cause SU to add iPhoto 5.0.1 to its 
list of available downloads. (I can't claim credit for this, since I 
was similarly affected on one of my machines and came across this 
solution over at Apple Discussions.) Be sure to replace the packages 
where they came from when you're done.
