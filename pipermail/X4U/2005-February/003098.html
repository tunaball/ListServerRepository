I have a contextual menu item called "LockPopCM" that does this. Highlight,
Right/Ctrl-Click and select unlock all.

http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/22348

freeware, some shareware:

TrashLockedFiles: <http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/18227>
