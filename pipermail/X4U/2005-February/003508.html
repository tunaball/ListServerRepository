I should have written back yesterday (I get digest) to say the problem had been solved.  I wrote Gyaz author and within minutes he responded with the following:

> The important settings are as follows:
> 
> - Receivig tab in the Accounts preferences
>   Advanced: [/] Require secure connection (SSL): [ Alternate Port ]
> 
> - Outgoing Mail Server dialog
>   Authentication: SMTP Authentication
>   Advanced: [/] Require secure connection (SSL): [ STARTTLS ]
>             [/] Override default port: [ 587 ]

His instant service was a surprise since it was in my mind that his native Japanese needed to pass through a translator - not so.

I had been trying to extrapolate Google's instructions for Apple Mail and could not get anything to work. The tips above May help Jim Freeman.
