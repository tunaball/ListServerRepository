I have not found any bugs yet. I am running 12 to 18 applications at 
once on a 1.8GHz G5, and have updated to 10.3.8 on 2 internal drive and 
on 3 FireWire drives. I downloaded and used the combo update. All boot 
up flawlessly and behave as expected. I thought that I had a big 
problem when I could not get the computer to wake up from sleep several 
weeks ago or start up after shutting down. The update makes no 
difference. It turned out that the G5 is a little bit defective because 
it does not work within the specified temperature range; all I had to 
do to get it to wake up or start up was to warm it to 16 or 18 degrees 
C. The room temperature this winter dropped to about 4 degrees C at 
times. I had not noticed the problem earlier because I usually have the 
G5 running 24/7.
