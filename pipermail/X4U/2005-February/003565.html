If you're wondering whether you can send sound wirelessly from the Netgear
to the AirPort Express and from there to your stereo equipment, that WON'T
work, because you're actually bridging two segments of a wireless network,
and both sections of the wireless network need to support it. Netgear
wireless routers don't do so.
