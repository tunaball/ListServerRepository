Agreed. Except that I'd like a better graphics card 'cause my 12" 
PowerBook spends great chunks of its life connected to a big, second 
monitor. In fact I think Apple got their market a bit wrong with the 
12" PowerBook. I bought it because I want a small, lightweight laptop 
as I lug it around with me everywhere. However, at home and at work, I 
plug into a range of peripherals - including large monitors. Given the 
option, I'd have spec'ed ny Mac to the fastest portable CPU and good / 
best graphics card. That way I'd still have my small laptop but better 
power when at my desk.

Just my ha'p'worth.
