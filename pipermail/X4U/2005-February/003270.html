Stan Gould said:

>I've heard that OnyX is a good utility to use, for general 
>maintenance. Anyone have experience with it? Any cautions?

It is free and it has a very nice interface.  Unfortunately the latest 
version has included a couple of new abilities that can be dangerous.  
For instance, I would never allow it to clear the virtual memory swap 
file.

In general, I would recommend that you not use any of OnyX's capabilities 
that you don't fully understand what they do and why you want to use them.

A better choice, if you aren't a power user, might be Yazu, because it is 
less likely to get you in trouble:

Yazu (free)
http://www.jimmitchelldesigns.com/software.html
