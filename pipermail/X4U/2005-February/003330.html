I had a few problems earlier, before the update, that didn't go away 
with it. One was in Mail where I lost the Spelling feature. Later 
noticed it also had disappeared in Pages and TextEdit. Apple said Erase 
the drive. I had a partition so I copied my entire Volume to the other 
volume and did the optional "Erase & Restore (?) choice after loading 
the original OS Disk. Worked perfectly. Did Software update. Worked 
perfectly. No changes to my work spaces, files, or settings that I can 
see except I now have Spelling back everywhere. Worked perfectly. You 
may have to do the same. Don't know for sure.
