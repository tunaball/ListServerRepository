I use an old H-P network laser printer with my G5 that has given very 
good service and no trouble over the years on several successive models 
of Macs. If I were to get a new printer, I would probably get a network 
Brother black and white laser printer and buy a separate, cheap color 
printer for occasional work. This would practically eliminate problems 
with the jets, and would be cheaper to operate.
