When you add a rule, you'll see a pane labeled "Perform the following 
actions" at the bottom of the dialog box. In addition to your current 
"Move Message" to mailbox X action, click the + button and select "Stop 
evaluating rules" from the popup. This will make it stop checking rules 
after it moves your message, so the Junk filter doesn't get a chance to 
misfile it.
