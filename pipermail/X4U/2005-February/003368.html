Hi Sam,

Apple's recommendation is to install OSX on your highest spec Mac and then 
you can image and restore that to your lower spec macs. This says to me 
that moving a G3 direct installation onto a G4 would not be wise as it 
will be missing things needed for the G4.

It may work fine for you but there could be thing missing.

Personally I'd install a new OSX onto the G4 and then transfer the G3's 
Home folder over to the G4 (must keep the same login names) to ease your 
setup process.
