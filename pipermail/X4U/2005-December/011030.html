One issue is that many powerbooks and iMacs have built-in
mics. To use Skype with these computers, one only needs an
ordinary headset (earphones only). I've used a Virgin Airlines
earmuff successsfully.

But other models, such as Power Macs and iBooks, don't
have the mic and require an external mic. For my G5, I bought
a standard microphone with a standard mini plug. Then I
bought a Griffin iMic. On my G5, I plug the mic into the iMic, the
iMic into a USB port, and my headset into the headphone jack
on the G5. I feel this is a better solution than the USB mic
because I can use any old mic, and because the iMic has come
in handy for other uses as well.
