>Delete both of those files.  In general, to fix problems with Apple Help
>Viewer, I recommend the following:
>
>1.  Quit Apple Help Viewer.
>
>2.  Open your home folder > Library > Preferences and delete these two
>files:
>
>~/Library/Preferences/com.apple.help
>~/Library/Preferences/com.apple.helpviewer
>
>3.  Open your home folder > Library > Caches and delete this folder:
>
>~/Library/Caches/com.apple.helpui
>
>4.  To improve the likelihood of this working, log out, and to improve it
>even more, restart your Macintosh.
