Hi, B;

While I can't answer the technical aspects of your question, I'd like  
to put in a recommendation for Reason. Reason is a synth/recording  
studio..a professional counterpart to Garage Band.

GB is excellent, but Reason offers far more piano types ("Refills" as  
they call them) and far more customizability, so she can make it  
sound like she's playing at a smokey club or at Lincoln Center.

I have a low end keystation which  does have velocity-sensitive keys,  
but the keys are not weighted. I use this setup mainly to create  
musical environments for filmwork I do.

hope this provides a helpful idea to you.
