Get a Mighty Mouse.  I love mine.  Even though I thought I was going  
to hate it at first.  When I switched to the Mac, way back when, I  
welcomed the single button mouse (it's a long boring story of why).   
But the mighty mouse gives you back all the functionality of the 2  
button mouse with a scroll wheel and still keeps the feel of the  
single button mouse.  Apple has truly done some excellent design in  
this piece of hardware.
