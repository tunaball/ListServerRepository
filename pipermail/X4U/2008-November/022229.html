> Spotless is a great way to force Spotlight to rebuild its index.  You  can

> 
> Open System Preferences (in the Apple menu) , choose the Spotlight  pane,
> click on the Privacy tab, and drag your main drive's icon into  the Privacy

> clicking on it once) and click on the "-" (that is, the minus button in the
> Privacy Window).
>

> rebuilt. (You won't be able to use Spotlight while its  database is being
> rebuilt.)
