> On Nov 7, 2008, at 8:35 AM, Paul Moortgat wrote:
>
>> I just upgraded Tiger to Leopard.  I can't get widgets.  I should  
>> press F12 to get them, but the only thing is that a CD is ejected  
>> if there should be one inside.
>> How do I get widgets on the G4?  I've still the old normal mouse.
>
> Look on your dock for the Widgets icon. It looks like an instrument  
> guage with red indicator hands on a black face and is labelled  
> "Dashboard" when your mouse touches it. Click on it once and your  
> widgets will appear on the desktop.
>
> To manage the widgets that are available to you, click on the plus  
> symbol in a circle that appears at the lower left corner of your  
> screen. From there, select or deselect the widgets that you want to  
> appear when you launch Dashboard.
