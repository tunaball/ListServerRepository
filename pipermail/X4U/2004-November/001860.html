A couple more tips for keyboard junkies, with Full Keyboard Access enabled,
in Cocoa applications:

1.  If you see a blue halo around a button, it may be pushed with the space
bar.

2.  If you see a blue halo around the tab names in a tabbed window, you can
change tabs using left and right arrows.
