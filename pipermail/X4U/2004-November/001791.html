If you know he's online but not showing as such then the chances are 
that he's "hidden" or "invisible" in some way. Most instant messaging 
services give the option to go online & yet not show as such - if your 
friend is using iChatAV he can go to Preferences > Privacy Options and 
use options like "allow people in my buddy list" or "allow specific 
people" to see his real availability.
