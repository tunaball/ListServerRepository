On Nov 27, 2004, at 9:37 PM, Jeff Winchester wrote:

> Am I missing something here? Is there a way to restore (un-minimize) 
> an app from the keyboard?

Control + F1 toggles full keyboard access.  Control + F2, activates 
focus on menus.  Tab or arrow key to the window menu, select unminimize 
the appropriate window from the keyboard.
