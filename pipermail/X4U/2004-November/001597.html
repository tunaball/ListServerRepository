Since it's for a website, I'm not sure what to say, but I try to 
limit my fonts to OpenType fonts which are intended to be the 
replacement for both Postscript and Truetype fonts, and the same font

Since you're working on a website, I believe you need to limit your 
choice of fonts to ones that will normally be installed on other 
computers.
