See the lengthy discussions at
<http://www.macfixit.com/article.php?story=20041101075016967>,<http://www.macfixit.com/article.php?story=20041102083044472>,
and<http://www.macfixit.com/article.php?story=20041104083036323>.

These two tricks seem to work for most:

1. Sys Prefs->Network->Show built-in ethernet->delete the IPv6 address (if
it's there).

2. Get SafariNoTimeout and set it at 600 (default is 300).

These have fixed it for me, so I didn't try the DNS relay trick.
