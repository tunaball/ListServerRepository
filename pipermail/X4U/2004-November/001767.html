> below is the message I was going to post. I was just about to hit the 
> send button when I realized that the load on the cpu had gone back to 
> normal.
> Any hints on what might have happened (and how Mail has self-healed)?

Mail.app can spike the CPU when running indexes.  Over time, these can 
become complex or corrupted and indexing will take an inordinately long 
time.

I just got Mail back to its normal spiffy self by reducing the size of 
some mailboxes (by breaking them up into smaller folders), deleting a 
lot of older mailboxes, and rebuilding the rest.
