I just did this. Here's how I did it.

I went into iTunes and changed the iTunes Music Folder location in the 
Advanced tab to my second hard drive. Then I went to iTune's Advanced 
menu and selected Consolidate Library. A warning came up that said 
"Consolidating your library will copy all of your music into the iTunes 
Music folder."

So I let iTunes do it -- and now I have all my music on my new drive.
