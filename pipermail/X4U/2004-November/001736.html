Try www.dealmac.com.  Deals are updated daily.

I have both a Samsung SATA and a Hitachi IDE, I've heard good things about those companies and personally have had no problems.  But these days, most hard drives are all pretty durable and some carry 3 year or more warranties.  I know that the Samsung does at least.

Check out www.newegg.com, I tend to buy most of my stuff because they have great prices, excellent shipping (saver shipping only takes 3 days from CA to the northeast), and a pretty good return policy.
