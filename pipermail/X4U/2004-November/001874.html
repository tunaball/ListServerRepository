Often the problem can be fixed simply by deleting the printer setup and 
reinstalling the drivers and re-doing the setup.

See this Apple tech article for how to troubleshoot printing problems:
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106714

You might want to try Print Center Repair:
http://www.fixamac.net/software/pcr/index.html
