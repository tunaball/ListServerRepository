On Sep 19, 2004, at 08:41, Paul Moortgat wrote:

> Ho have a VGA on a G5 I think I have to buy another video card which 
> has a VGA out.  Which is OK?
>

Your G5 has a DVI port, which you can use with a DVI-VGA adapter. There 
should be such an adapter in the box. If you're already using the DVI 
port, and need to use the ADC port with a VGA monitor, there are 
adapters available for 20-40 USD.
