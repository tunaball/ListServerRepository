Yes. You should have a DVI-->VGA adapter in the box with the G5. Plug 
it into the second DVI port, and plug the AVerkey into the adapter.

Just in case the question comes up:  You do not need both DVI ports to 
run a 30" monitor.

 From the http://apple.com/displays page:  with two connectors, or Dual 
Dual Link (DDL), you can drive two 30-inch displays .

My addition to that: You can also drive 1 30" display, and 1 other 
display.
