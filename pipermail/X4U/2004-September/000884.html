I also had the same experience about the change in iDisk' size. After 
checking the .Mac home page, I found the following message.

Product Updates and Upgrades:


250 MB of Storage for .Mac Mail and iDisk
Released September 29, 2004.
.Mac Mail and iDisk combined storage space for full membership has been 
increased to 250 MB. Default settings are 125 MB of mail storage and 
125 MB of iDisk space, and you can designate the storage to fit the way 
you use your .Mac account.

I guess the issue has to do with the increase in the size of iDisk's 
storage.
