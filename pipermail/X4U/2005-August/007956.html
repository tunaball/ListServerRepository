Up until recently, you could use a Smart playlist, with a "last  
played not in the last n days" clause to achieve what you want.

But, the latest round iPod firmware updates broke that functionality,  
so they won't update dynamically, only when you sync with iTunes again.

If you haven't updated to the most current firmware, that feature may  
still work so don't be in a hurry to update.
