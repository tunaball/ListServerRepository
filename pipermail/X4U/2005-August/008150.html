On Wednesday, August 17, 2005, at 11:40AM, revDAVE <coolcat at hostalive.com> wrote:

> When I click the burn button in iDVD 5.01 it automatically opens the DVD
>writer on my internal desktop G4.  This is fine ... However, how do I get it
>to burn a disk from my external DVD writer - pioneer 109 instead?
>
>-  I tried to insert a blank disk and close the door on the Pioneer ... But
>iDVD did not recognize it...

Instead of clicking the Burn button, use "Save as Disc Image..." from the File menu. After the disc image is created, you can then use other apps (Finder, DiskUtility, Toast, etc.) to burn the disc image using your 109.
