On Aug 19, 2005, at 9:32 AM, Simon Forster wrote:

> What's the easiest way to import audio tapes into iTunes? I've got  
> hundreds of tapes and I'm missing some of my old music. I'd like a  
> (relatively) hassle free way of getting them into iTunes.

I use Griffin iMic and Griffin Final Vinyl.

My setup: Cassette tape deck line output to the iMic. Run Final Vinyl  
which you can save its output to .aiff format. Import the .aiff file  
into iTunes.
