Coming late to the discussion...

On 2 Aug 2005, at 16:09, DZ-Jay wrote:
> 	Unfortunately, Mail does not have "outgoing" filters, it only filters 
> "incoming" mail.  The best way to do it that I have found, and what is 
> recommended in the Help file, is to set yourself as a BCC on all 
> outgoing messages, and filter them when they come back in.  A copy 
> will still remain in the Sent box, but your incoming copy will be sent 
> to the mailbox of your choice.

Another workaround is to use the applescripts provided in Mailscript 
(http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/16217)
There is one that allows you to filter at once the sent emails (Filter 
sent messages).
Of course you have to remember to do it every now and then....
