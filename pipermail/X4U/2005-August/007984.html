Alexandre,

I have this printer and I routinely scan my documents wirelessly from 
it to my powerbook G4 running 10.3.9. According to HP, it was not 
intended that the scanner should work, but it does, and, quite well!

Be cautioned: following the setup routine EXACTLY and it should work. 
Make sure your base computer is on and if it is a laptop, have it 
connected to its power source. Having your machine go to sleep thru the 
15+ minute setup will really screw it up!
