Does it crash when logged in as a different user? If so, the easiest fix may be to reinstall from scratch. 

If not, trash the Finder plist in your User Library Preferences folder and restart. Try running "Preferential Treatment" which looks for corrupt plists.
<http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/22790 >
You could abandon that user account--move your files to a new account.
