On 8/5/05 9:51 AM, Cindy Moore <bgcyclist at netscape.net> wrote:

> I'm not sure what I have done, but I get an annoying sound every time I hit a
> key stroke. How can I turn this off without turning off all the sound?

My first thought: go to System Preferences > Universal Access.

Click "Keyboard" and see if "Use click key sounds" is checked. If so,
uncheck it.
