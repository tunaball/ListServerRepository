Sherlock has been a real mess lately (10.4).  It changes languages,  
channels go blank, etc.

Deleting ~/Library/Caches/Sherlock, and any Sherlock files in  
Preferences seems to get it working for a little while, but  
everything goes crazy again eventually.

There are threads on the Apple Discussions forums about it and people  
who have reported it as a bug have had their bugs designated as  
"Duplicate", so it appears to be a known problem.  I wouldn't be  
surpised if Sherlock goes away given that most of its "channels" are  
being done as Dashboard Widgets.
