Not much info to go on, but I'll take a shot in the dark...  there is 
a possibility that you are experiencing a channel conflict... meaning 
that your neighbor's WAP and yours are both using the same frequency 
(channel) to broadcast on. This can easily happen if you both are 
using the default channel the WAPs came configured to use. Some 
client systems may be able to separate the two broadcasters, but some 
will not, hence your G4 and G5 systems connecting up fine, but the 
laptop cannot.

Bring up the administration tool for your WAP (usually a browser 
these days) and look for the channel ID setting (usually among the 
"wireless" settings). Choose some other channel (doesn't matter which 
you choose) and see if each of your Macs are able to connect.

Hope that solves the problem.
