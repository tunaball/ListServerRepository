OK. I can only think of one reason why the new folder
button won't work. The volume currently displayed in the
finder window is unwritable. It's an read-only optical disk,
or it's a locked drive, you don't have write permissions, or
it's full.

This confuses me. I don't understand why you started Terminal,
but once you were there, why didn't you check the permissions?
