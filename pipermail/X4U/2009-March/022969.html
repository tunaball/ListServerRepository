No, Ed, you have to believe: they didn't break into your PayPal account.
These phishers send the notices to everyone they can, whether they have a
PayPal account or not. My husband, who does NOT have a Paypal account,
receives them too! These phishers either A) buy a list of email addresses to
phish or B) simply decide on a domain -- earthlink.net, comcast.net,
charter.net, etc. -- and then they use software to start coming up with
every possible permutation of email addresses for that domain.
