On Mar 5, 2009, at 11:29 AM, Joe Sporleder wrote:

> If one has one of the new Mac minis, or even a Mac Pro and already  
> have a good monitor, and so not really interested in the 24" LED  
> Cinema, are there good alternatives that you'd recommend of a  
> camera with microphone that'd work with iChat AV?

An old stand-alone iSight would be ideal...if you could find one to  
purchase.

You can use any webcam with a USB connection, even those that say  
that they are for Windows-only, with:

Macam (free)
driver for USB webcams on Mac OS X. It allows hundreds of USB webcams  
to be used
http://webcam-osx.sourceforge.net/

IOXperts Webcam Driver for Mac OS X  ($20)
http://www.ioxperts.com/products/webcamx.html

CamCamX ($10)
http://b-l-a-c-k-o-p.com/CamCamX.html
makes non-Apple webcams iSight compatible
