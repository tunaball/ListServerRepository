Yes. It is annoying. But then I'm still of the mind that they should  
all be separate programs. Not only do I like the "separate programs"

potentially has the most bugs was tied to a bit of the OS (the Dock)

capabilities it allows (writing apps in Javascript and HTML for  
instance). The rest seems to old-school Desk Accessory to me.
