Gardner Byron said:

>Can somebody please tell me what I need to do to play avi files on QT  
>version 7?

See:
http://www.lordofthecows.com/avi_qt.php
which breaks down the situation nicely, and provides links to what you 
need.

You can play almost any AVI under OS X with:

VideoLan Client:
http://www.videolan.org/
http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-macosx.html

or

MPlayer
http://mplayerosx.sourceforge.net/
http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/mplayerosx/MplayerOSXB8r4.zip?download
http://easynews.dl.sourceforge.net/sourceforge/mplayerosx/MplayerOSXB8r4.zi
p

Or, with the proper codecs installed, QuickTime will play just about 
every type of AVI file.

An Apple KnowledgeBase article entitled "QuickTime : About Playing .AVI 
Files" can be found at 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=93199> .

The problem is that there are a number of different types of AVI files 
and you need to have the AVI codec installed that works with the AVI file 
that you want to play.
