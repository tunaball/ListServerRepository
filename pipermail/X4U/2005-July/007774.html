My brother's eMac also started having kernel panics and displaying  
other weird behavior (freezes, spinning beach balls, etc.) after  
upgrading to Tiger.

Extensive hardware tests showed no problems, but I eventually pulled  
a stick of third-party RAM, and the problems cleared immediately. The  
vendor replaced the RAM for free, and he's had no problems since.

RAM may not be your problem, but upgrading to Tiger apparently  
brought out a RAM problem on my brother's eMac.
