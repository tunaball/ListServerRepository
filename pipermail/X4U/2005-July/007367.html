Does it meet one of the "exempt" criteria? If you look in the Junk  
Mail panel of Preferences, the default is that it won't label  
messages as junk if the sender is in your address book, previous

Considering that much of the "junk" I get is from addresses within my  
own department (either spoofed or infected PC), that often defeats  
the filter with default settings.
