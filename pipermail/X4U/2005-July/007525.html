A little reading on Apple's knowledge based found the fix:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=42989

They now require you to use "homepage.mac.com/username" instead of  
"idisk.mac.com" because of webDav volumes.

You also need to place a period before the folder name.  So this now  
works:

http://homepage.mac.com/stevetalkowski/.Public/shot05.jpg
