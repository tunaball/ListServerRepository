> A friend recently bought a Sony Cybershot P200 digital camera. She

> wants to put them into iMovie. Unfortunately, the movies have no  
> sound when imported into iMovie. You cannot export it and have  
> audio, too. Does anyone have any idea why the audio is encrypted so  
> we cannot extract the audio along with the video? I have a clip  
> here and I've tried everything. She also has QuickTime Pro 7, so  
> that's not the issue. It's almost as if the mpg buries the audio  
> and nothing on the Mac side is able to extract it.

Use MPEG Stream Clip to convert and de-multiplex (demux) the MPEG to  
DV. You can then edit it in iMovie with sound intact.

<http://www.alfanet.it/squared5/>
