It is as yet undocumented by Apple, but you can indeed do Boolean 
searches with Spotlight.


(-...) = NOT
|      = OR
space  = AND

Groucho|Harpo Chico(-Zeppo)
gives you a search for Groucho OR Harpo, AND Chico, but excludes 
instances in which Zeppo appears.

Apple intends to make Boolean searching much easier in future versions of 
OS X, which is why they haven't even mentioned this capability yet.
