I completely agree: try to get your data out and only afterwards start 
playing with the HD.

I had a bad crash in my previous TiBook and I had to recover 70Mb of 
unbacked-up data.
I could connect to the TiBook in FW target mode, but I did not have 
accees to the folders.
Probably the directory was damaged and the only way I found to rescue 
them was DataRescueX:
http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/1133
It saved my 70Mb... though it did not work at recovering a 600Mb disk 
image file

After recovering the files I just reformatted the HD and everything 
went fine...
