To get a smaller menu font in iTunes.
Write in Terminal:
defaults write com.apple.iTunes AppleDis\playScaleFactor 0.92
A scale factor >1 will enlarge the font.  To make it normal make it 1  
instead of 0.92
One can also use iChat, iCal, Safari or Mail.  The symbol and menu  
lists also change.

I got this from MacUp in Germany.
