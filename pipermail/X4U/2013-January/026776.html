Having the same problem with a WD 3tb on a Mystic running Tiger, 
behind a Seritek 1S2 SATAI PCI card, which is likely the stumbling 
block on my system. WD claims it will work, Disk Utility sees the 
full 3 tb,  but no luck. You may want to try throttling down the 
drive speed with a jumper (check with Seagate) or partition the drive 
in GUID, which has boot issues. Neither option worked for me. I'm 
considering formatting with a newer machine which may not work due to 
speed issues with the newer SATA III standards.
