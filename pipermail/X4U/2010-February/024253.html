I have had a 4-bay Drobo unit just over a year and have had no problems.
Filled it with 2 * 1 Tb and 2 * 500 Gb drives and have had no problems
running continuosly attached to a MacPro on my desktop and using it
as a Time Machine backup volume with OS X 10.6.

Bought it from a company called Transparent Computers in the UK.
