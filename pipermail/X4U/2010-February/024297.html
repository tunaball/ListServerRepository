Paul: I'm not sue why the second internal drive isn't showing up as  
GUID partitioned in System Profiler. I just checked and mine all do,  
so it may be that the drive didn't get partitioned as GUID after all.

I use Disk Utility, select the drive to repartition, select Partition,  
change from current partition to 1 Partition, and then go to the  
Options... button
  and select GUID. and finally, Apply.
