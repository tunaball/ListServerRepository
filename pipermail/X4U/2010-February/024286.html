I've had great success with Acomdata products for my home systems and in my office. I'm currently using the ProDrive Dual model for my work laptop, which has USB2.0 and Firewire 400/800 interfaces with two 1TB drives in the enclosure. It's fast and reliable. The fan noise is a tad loud but it has a great price point at around $240 for 2TB.
