I'll second the suggestions for both of these brands. I have a Canon  
Pixima printer & a separate Canon scanner myself that I'm very  
pleased with, and when they released the Pixima range of MFCs I did  
spend a day or two lusting after them. Many of the European models of  
Pixima do direct-to-disc CD-printing, which is really nice; Canon  
disabled this feature on US models because it is patented by Epson  
over there, however I believe there are some grey-importers that  
offer them in the US.

For Christmas I bought my mother a Brother. I don't think that model  
does duplex, but photo-quality is fine and with built-in networking  
it was a bargain £75 or so. It was a breeze to set-up (umm... the  
first time, at least) and scanning works over the network as well as  
printing.
