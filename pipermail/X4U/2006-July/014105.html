Most blank mail messages are probes to determine whether an email
address is valid.  (Same goes for messages with a sentence or three of
random text.)

They're sent from zombies, so replying is pointless.  There's a
0.0...1% chance that adding the sending IP to your blacklist will
help: any actual spam to you will almost certainly be sent from a
different zombie.

Discard and move on with life.
