usbserial cable. The cable uses the prolific pl2303 driver and I have  
the latest version; it is even a universal binary. I think that at  
one point I had this working correctly but now something is wrong.  
After hooking up the braille display to the computer, I can get just  
one response from it. For instance:

however, after that first response, unless I either reboot or unplug  
the cable from the usb port and replug it, I get no further  
responses. If I do unplug and replug the usb connector, I then get

There is nothing defective in the serial cable or the usbserial

any suggestions as to how to try to solve this problem, I would be
