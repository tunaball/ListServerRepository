You also need to open port 5988 and some of the ports need to be  
opened for both TCP and UDP.

Here's a page describing what you need to do.

http://how2s.org/index.php/ 
Howto_get_Apple_Remote_Desktop_to_work_behind_a_router

Get ssh working first as a sanity check.

If this doesn't get you up and running then you need to describe your  
network. Are you using Internet Sharing on one of your Macs or a  
hardware router? By "Internet web" IP I assume you mean your public  
IP address, yes? Are you trying to connect to the public address from  
inside or outside of your network? (Some routers will not let you  
connect to your public address from inside your network. I found this  
out the hard way when I got a new router.)
