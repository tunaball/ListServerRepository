On Jul 10, 2006, at 4:56 PM, Neil wrote:

> I don't know how to use the Apple dictionary

Open the Applications folder in Finder. Double click on "Dictionary".

Click into the field at the upper right corner of the window. Begin  
typing. Matches appear as you type.

Double click on the match of your choice to see the definition.
