You don't say if you need a laser printer or a color inkjet.

Here is a list of Brother laser-based multi-functions that do duplexing:
<http://www.brother-usa.com/mfc/mfc_ataglance_psvar=ZADDFEATURE&psvalue=2&P

Brother is especially good at Macintosh-compatiblity with multi-function

for all features.

range:
<http://www.usa.canon.com/consumer/controller?act=ProductCatIndex1Act&fcate
goryid=105>
Not all Canon models are Macintosh compatible, but those that are are 
great values, good performers, and Canon has amazing service and support.
