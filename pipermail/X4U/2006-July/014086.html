On 7/11/06 2:00 PM, alexandre <mac.k at a2k.ch> wrote:

> how does one navigate through tabs in safari?

Command-Shift-] and Command-Shift-[
These shortcuts are listed in Safari's Window menu as Command-} and
Command-{ since Shift turns ] into }

You can also still use Safari's old keyboard shortcuts of
Command-Shift-right arrow and Command-Shift-left arrow

To see all of Safari's Keyboard shortcuts, go to this location:
file:///Applications/Safari.app/Contents/Resources/Shortcuts.html
