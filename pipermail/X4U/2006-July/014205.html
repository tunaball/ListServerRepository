This happened to me last year. In addition to the black box, my 
computer was speaking the text in the menu bars. It drove me 
absolutely batty. After two days I was just about to reinstall my 
complete system, when somehow I realized what it was. I don't 
remember how I found it, but this is what has happened:

One of the items in Universal Access has been turned on. You likely 
hit the shortcut key combination to enable this option.

Go to System Preferences/Universal Access/Seeing and turn VoiceOver 
off and turn Zoom off.
