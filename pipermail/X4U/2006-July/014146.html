visit macupdate.com and versiontracker.com and search by "gps."  
You'll find several 3rd party softwares. You'll probably need a USB  
(Mac) to Serial (Garmin GPS) cable, but some of the software sellers  
also have the cables.
You can also google gps mac or something similar and probably find  
other sources for sw and cables.
Quite often, the 3rd party stuff is miles ahead of the stop-gap stuff  
the hardware outfits cobble together.
