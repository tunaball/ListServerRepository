Here's a little tidbit of information that might come in handy with  
the dictionary...

If you are in TextEdit or Mail, you can highlight a word and control- 
click the word which brings up the Apple Dictionary with the correct  
spelling or alternate spellings.

I don't know if this works in any other Apple programs or not.

Y'all have fun...

>> I don't know how to use the Apple dictionary
>
> Open the Applications folder in Finder. Double click on "Dictionary".
>
> Click into the field at the upper right corner of the window. Begin  
> typing. Matches appear as you type.
>
> Double click on the match of your choice to see the definition.
