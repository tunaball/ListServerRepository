The Inspector is a version of the Get Info window that is more of an active
palette rather than another window. It will show you the information of
whatever you have currently selected in the Finder and it will change when
you select something different. It will also display the combined
information when multiple items are selected. When the Finder is not the
front-most application, the Inspector disappears.

I find it useful when I want to see some information on files in a folder,
like permissions or installed languages. I can just arrow through the list
and the contents of the Inspector will change.
