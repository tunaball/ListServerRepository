> I just ran into a situation where a Intel Mac user needed the  
> functionality of the old 'Key Caps' CP; any one know of a  
> replacement??

System Preferences>International>Input Menu>check "Keyboard Viewer"  
That will put a menu item of a flag (American, in my case) in the  
upper right of the menu bar with "Show Keyboard Viewer" as one of the  
dropdown selections.
