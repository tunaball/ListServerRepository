Ralink's drivers worked for a PCI bus card in a G4 desktop and a PC  
card in a G4 Powerbook. Both are Belkin cards. Seems I've read it  
depends on the chip set in the card. The Belkin cards are not very  
expensive and you get 802.11g speeds. The Ralink drivers worked for  
OS 10.2 through OS 10.4.7.
