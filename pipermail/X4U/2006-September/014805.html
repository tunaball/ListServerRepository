My Netgear WGT624 wireless router/firewall allows for blocking of sites 
or restricting of sites by time of day. You can also restrict use of the 
internet to a schedule. The blocking is done by IP address, so you could 
block you daughter, but not yourself or someone else.

Although the Netgear WGT624 is listed as a wireless router, it has four 
ethernet ports, allowing it to be used as a wired router as well (which 
is what I do, as I don't trust wireless).

Hope this helps.

Stan
