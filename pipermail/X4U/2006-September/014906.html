I ask this because recently I thought my G5 had stopped mounting  
external firewire hard-drives; I figured it was a software problem  
because my iSight worked fine. It was only after I tried connecting  
another device to the front firewire port that I realised that only  
this one was affected & that drives (and my iSight!) plugged into the  
back work fine.

I'm really hoping that in my case it's just a loose connector between  
the motherboard & the front-panel firewire port, but I'm kinda scared  
because of an incident which occurred around the time this failed. I  
had a firewire device sitting on the floor and connected using a long  
cable, turned my back and about two minutes later saw my recently  
adopted house-rabbit had just taken a chomp out of the cable,  
severing both the braided-metal shielding and more than one of the  
wires inside!! I hope this did not cause a short-circuit and hardware  
damage, but I haven't yet shut down the machine to check the cable  
for looseness.
