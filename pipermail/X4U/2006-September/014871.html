When my startup chime stopped (G4 - 400) one of the Apple 'geniuses'  
at the Apple Store (Palo Alto) told me to reset the non-volatile  
RAM.  Here's how:

Restart while holding down Command, Option, 'O' and 'F' keys.  This  
starts up in 'open firmware' mode.

Type:

    reset-nvram    CR  (= carriage return)
    reset-all          CR

You may have to do this more than once.

Good luck!
