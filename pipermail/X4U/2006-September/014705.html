It sounds like your G4 needs the older Airport card, and not Airport  
Extreme.

Strangely enough, the older card lists at $200 (if you can find one)  
while a new Airport Extreme Card is $49 from Apple.

Refurbished Airport cards can be found for about $100 (eBay).

If you have an Airport Extreme Base Station, it *will* be compatible  
with the older Airport card.
