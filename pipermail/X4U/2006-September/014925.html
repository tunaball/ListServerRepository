Note that not all Belkin cards use a Ralink chipset - only some of them.

I know that these ones <http://networkned.co.uk/Wireless.php> use the  
Ralink rt2500 chipset, but am not aware of any other Belkin cards  
other than these two models (and the USB equivalent) which do. I  
haven't checked in a while, mind, but don't just rush out and buy any  
Belkin thinking that it'll work.
