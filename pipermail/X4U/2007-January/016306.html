That reminds me! Thanks George! I use a program from computer 
maintenance that has proven itself invaluable. It's even Freeware! It's 
called Onyx and I believe the latest version is Onyx 1.7.7. It does 
everything from rebuilding permissions to optimizing your disc to 
clearing caches. I've used it for a couple years now. I also use 
DiskWarrior which I can't recommend enough either. TechTool Pro is 
another good one. But for the money, (or lack of), Onyx can't be beat!

• OnyX is a maintenance, optimization, and personalization utility for 
Mac OS X. It also makes it possible to configure certain hidden 
parameters of the Finder, Dock, and Safari, to remove a certain number
