My last two Macs were refurbished machines bought directly from  
Apple. Both were still current models at the time of purchase but I  
got them at close to a $800 off the regular price (plus free  
shipping). They had full warranties and I was able to purchase an  
Apple Care plan for both. They even qualified for two special  
promotions Apple was having. At the time they had a promotion going  
on that if you purchased any iMac they would throw in 256MB RAM for  
free. I asked about the refurbished Macs and they guy said the  
promotion was for "any iMac" so I got the free RAM upgrade. They were  
also having a promotion where you received a $159 rebate if you  
purchased a printer at the same time as a Mac. I picked a $179 Epson  
printer from their list which cost me $20.
