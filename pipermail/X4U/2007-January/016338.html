I'm 99.999% sure they're incompatible.

PCI had 3.3V and 5V
PCI-X can use most 3.3V or dual voltage PCI cards
PCI-Express is something new, different and incompatible

The typical PC motherboard has PCI-Express and PCI slots right now, just
like they used to have ISA and PCI.  I really think Apple dropped the ball
on their handling of slots the last few years, but it might have not been
worth the effort to handle it better.
