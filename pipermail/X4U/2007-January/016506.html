The "archive and install" option means that you do NOT have to reinstall
apps and re-enter serial numbers -- all of your information is archived and
available to the OS afterward. This is different from a "fresh install",
which does wipe your entire drive, requiring you to reinstall apps and
serial numbers, etc. Hope this helps in the future.
