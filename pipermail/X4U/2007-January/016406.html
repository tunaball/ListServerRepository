On 1/19/07 1:36 PM, Gerry Van Tol <vantol at mac.com> wrote:

> Does anybody know of a function in iTunes or a add-on program that
> will give me an indication per song for which play list it is
> selected? I have a lot of play lists and found that I select a lot of
> the same songs in various lists. Now it's a nigthmare to clean these

I don't totally understand your question, but I think this will help you:
Right-click on a song and choose "Show in Playlist" and it will tell you all
the playlists the song is currently in. You can then jump to the song in any
of those playlists and remove it if you want.
