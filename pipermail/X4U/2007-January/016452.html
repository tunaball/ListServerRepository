Create a playlist, give it an identifiable name, then drag the  
podcasts to the name of the playlist in the left column of the iTunes  
main window.
Make sure there is a check mark to the left of each podcast and they  
will play in succession. You can, of course, change the order by  
simply dragging the podcast name up or down.
Hope that helps.
