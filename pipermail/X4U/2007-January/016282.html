Shapeshifter comes with an Uninstaller -- if you didn't keep it when you
installed it, try downloading Shapeshifter again so that you can truly
uninstall it. 

This is from Unsanity's Shapeshifter FAQ:

> 7 - How can I uninstall ShapeShifter?
>  The best way to get rid of ShapeShifter is to use the built-in uninstaller.
> Double-click the "ShapeShifter Installer" icon and click on the "Uninstall"
> button (the installer will move all ShapeShifter files to the Trash - it will
> never actually delete any files on your system).
> 
> Finally, log out and then log back in to complete the process.
>   Did this help you resolve the problem? yes - no

You  might want to try this process, which may do a better job of
"uninstalling" than you can (given that there may be invisible files or
files named something that's not an obvious association...)
