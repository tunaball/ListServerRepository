BTW, I'm glad to hear I'm not the only one that still prefers PageMill.  In

solution and the likes of GoLive and Dreamweaver.  In other words a good
solution for the average person.  I also wonder if GoLive will be in CS3, or
if it will be Dreamweaver, it looks like CS2.4 premium actaully offers both.
