You can easily tell if your applications are PowerPC applications by  
highlighting their icons in the Finder, and choosing File —> Get Info.

In the resulting Get Info window, under General, if your application  
is a PowerPC application in will say so under Kind.

You can check several applications at the same time by holding down  
the shift key and clicking on all of the applications that you want  
to check to select all of them, and then choosing Get Info.
