> I would like to store a copy of my wifes MacBook HDD on my MDD computer.

> How do I copy her HDD to one of my storage drives?

Why? I suggest just getting a bootable, external HD (they're relatively
inexpensive), use Carbon Copy Cloner, SuperDuper!, etc. to put a bootable
backup/clone on to it, and bypass mucking with your machine and dealing with
the differences between an ICBM (Intel-chip-based Mac) and a PPC machine.
