Tray,

If you use Mail as your e-mail client then you might want to check  
the Mail Downloads folder in your library, Users/username/Library/ 
Mail Downloads. If you ever double click on an attachment in Mail to  
open it, a copy of the attachment is saved to this folder. If you  
haven't checked this folder it's possible you have lots of files  
there that are taking up space on your HD. The first time I checked  
it I had a couple of GB worth of files there. It's perfectly safe to  
delete these duplicate files as it doesn't affect the files attached  
to your e-mails. After discovering this I've taken to dragging  
attachments to the Desktop and opening them from there. Afterwards I  
can move the file where I want it or delete it without any worry.

Out of curiosity, if anyone checks thier Mail Downloads folder and  
deletes the files let us know how much space gets cleared up.
