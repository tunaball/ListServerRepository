I've solved my Lion problems. The problem was with eset cyber security. I 
hadn't seen the notice from Eset about uninstalling and reinstalling until 
after I'd installed Lion, and since I wasn't having the problems Eset 
listed, I didn't think I needed to reinstall it - bad choice!!! But I'm 
not going to attempt to reinstall Eset until I'm sure lion is working 
okay. When I uninstalled the Eset package, it apparently took my plist for 
login items at startup with it, but when I did the uninstall on the 
external drive I was ready for this and saved my 
Library/Preferences/Loginwindow.plist somewhere else and put it back after 
uninstalling Eset Cyber Security and restarting.
