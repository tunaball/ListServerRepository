>i've looked at some of the software available but it looks all a bit
>complicated. any suggestions for a hassle-free solution?

There are a couple of answers to that...

The official line is that you need an AirPrint-capable printer, 
which is still a small subset of Hewlett-Packard's inkjets and 
nothing from any other vendor.

However, there is a freeware (donationware) utility for your 
iMac called "AirPrint Activator 2.0" that will make any shared 
printers on your network available for AirPrint (this is the 
solution we use with our Canon and Epson printers). The one 
glitch we've found with this is if any of the Macs on your 
network are running the software firewall, the printouts don't 
make it through. Since our router provides the firewall, this 
isn't an issue for us and we keep it turned off on our Macs.
