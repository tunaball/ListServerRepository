My guess is that with that many disks, you're trapped in a huge Spotlight indexing. You can stop it in the System Prefs by going to Spotlight and then Privacy. Deselect the disks you don't need indexed--generally all the other boot drives, and you should see some relief. Also, if you reboot, hold down the Option key after the startup sound and you'll get a list of drives--both bootable and "Recovery" to choose from.
