	My iPod (not a nano) recharges while the laptop is sleeping.  I've  
got firewire instead of USB, but I wouldn't think that would make a  
difference.  (I've been wrong using similar logic before, however.)

	When it tries to synchronize, I just shut down iTunes and it stops  
synchronizing but continues to charge.  I also sometimes just eject  
the iPod but leave it plugged in; it stops synchronizing but  
continues to charge.  I seem to remember there are also some settings  
in iTunes preferences that one can set to keep it from synchronizing.
