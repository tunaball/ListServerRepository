If you want to convert the DVD to different formats (avi and mp4),  
Handbrake is excellent and free.
<http://Handbrake.m0k.org>

If you want to just rip the DVD to your hard drive so that you can  
duplicate it, use MacTheRipper, also free.
<http://www.macupdate.com/info.php/id/14414>
