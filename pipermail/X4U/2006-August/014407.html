Apple has a history of making choices for their users that tend  
towards simplicity over configurability. (Windows and Linux tend the  
other direction.) So it is with the paths searched by Spotlight. But  
you seem to be aware that there are ways to include /usr in your  
Spotlight index, right? I did it the manual way, and it works just  
fine. So when I search for "rsync", it will find /usr/bin/rsync. And  
when I search for "hosts" it will find /private/etc/hosts.
