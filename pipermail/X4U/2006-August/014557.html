Paul, tell us what model, G-3 or G-4 and Operating System you are  
using.  If you already have an airport card installed in your iBook,  
you should be able to connect.  Open Utilities and launch Airport  
Setup Assistant; it will find the service for you.  Be sure to check  
the "Show AirPort status in menu bar" under Internet Connect.  If you  
have all black wave bars, you are in!
