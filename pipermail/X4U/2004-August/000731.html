> I am able to boot into open firmware but have not yet figured out how

> boot from there to the CD-ROM. For some reason holding down 'C' doesn't
> seem to work for that. Any suggestions would be greatly appreciated.

Try restarting with command-option-shift-delete-4; this has worked for 
me when a C boot would not boot from the built-in DVD RAM drive. The 
"4" comes from the number shown for this drive in System Profiler on MY 
AGP Graphics G4. It's possible yours could be different.
