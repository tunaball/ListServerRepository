> I would like to update my Apple mail program to include my contacts in
> entourage. Apple mail seems to have limited import capabilities ... So what is
> the best way to do this?

Drag contacts from Entourage to the desktop to create .vcf files, then drag
these files into Address Book; they'll then be available for Mail.
