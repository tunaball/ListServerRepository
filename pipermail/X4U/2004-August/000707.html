I never got around to installing 10.3.4 so I did 10.3.5 combo tonight. Seems
to have gone fine. Can't see anything different yet. Good or bad. The
applications I care most about right now: Dreamweaver MX 2004, Fireworks MX
2004, Photoshop CS and InDesign CS all fired right up. Fonts from Font Agent
Pro auto-loaded. All is happy with the world.

I did it "safely" by first booting from my OS X install CD and I ran DFA. It
came out clear the first time through. Then I reboot and repaired
permissions. Then I installed 10.3.5. I also turned off my external drives
for the process since they have all my backups and if anything were to go
wrong, I didn't want to risk any of the data on it.
