On 8/12/04 8:39 PM, "revDAVE" <coolcat at hostalive.com> wrote:

> Is there any way to get Windows Media audio .wma or  Real audio .ra  files
> imported into iTunes?

You can import WMA files if you're running Windows. Just drag them into the
iTunes window, and select Advanced > Convert to [whatever conversion format
you have selected in the prefs].
