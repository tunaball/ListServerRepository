This is what I have done when this happened to me. Back up everything 
of importance to you, or clone it to a firewire drive and delete your 
login. Then recreate yourself. Make sure you delete all traces of 
yourself. That way you can use the same name and password. Sometimes 
the keychain just goes south, not sure why. It should only take you an 
hour to get everything back to normal. If someone else has a better 
idea do tell.
