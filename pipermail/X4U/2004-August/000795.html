On Aug 11, 2004, at 02:24, Kirk McElhearn wrote:

> If that doesn't work, try making an alias of the iTunes folder on the
> external drive and placing it in your Music folder.

And I didn't see if anyone had mentioned this, but how about deleting 
(or simply moving to the Desktop) the iTunes preference files in 
~/Library/Preference/, then re-setup iTunes pointing to the external 
hard drive?  You may have to "add" all of your music back into the 
Library file after this, but it should work.

Or, first, you may want take a look at your "iTunes Music Library.xml" 
file with Property List Editor or Text Edit.  You'll see the path to 
your Music folder listed.  Is it showing the correct path to the 
external drive?  If not, then you may want to do the preference 
cleansing.

Good luck!
