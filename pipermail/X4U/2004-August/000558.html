Hi all,

I just spoke with Macromedia Tech Support and they confirmed an issue we 
are having that I thought other FHMX users should know about - or probably 
already do.....

In FHMX 11.0.2 special characters are being substituted, I believe at 
print stage. Examples of substitutions are copyright symbols becoming 
apostrophes, bullets become the Yen symbol, quotes just disappear and em 
dashes do something that I forget right now...(and no doubt there are 
other characters that substitute also).

You can see this when you print to a printer or print to PostScript then 
Distil a PDF. I'm yet to test if you can avoid the substitution by 
creating an EPS file then distilling that.

The workaround our designers are using thus far is to convert all text to 
paths before printing or creating PDF's.

Does anyone else have this issue or any suggested fixes/workarounds?
