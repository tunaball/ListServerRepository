IIRC . . . I think you can assign a keyboard shortcut for anything  
that has a menu command . . . in Mail there is Save Attachments under  
the File menu.

The important thing to remember is that when you type in the menu  
selection you have to match case exactly . . .as well as enter the  
ellipsis character and not just 3 periods at the end.

The macosxhints.com article should still serve as a decent guide for  
setting it up; then the only hard part is remembering the shortcut:-)
