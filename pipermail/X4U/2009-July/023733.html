To make a screendump of a DVD movie (or anything) go to Terminal and  
type:

screencapture -ixP ~/Pictures/dvdshot.png

After pressing the Return key, press the spacebar and you'll get a  
camera icon.  Make sure the picture you'll take it from is the top  
window in front.
Then press the mouse and you'll get a window where one can select the  
format to save the picture in.  One can also choose a new name instead  
'dvdshot'.
