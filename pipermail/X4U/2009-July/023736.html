Ed,

I'm replying off list because for some reason my messages take a  
couple of weeks before they show up.

A smart folder is not like any of the other folders. You cannot drag  
messages into them, nor can you create Rules to manage them through  
Preferences. What you can do is double click on the smart folder and  
a list of it's own rules will appear. Whatever parameters you enter  
there will determine what appears in the smart folder. Keep in mind  
that the smart folder will display all messages that meat the  
criteria but will NOT move the messages. The original messages are  
still in their original folder.

Example, I use smart folders to display all messages from today, both  
sent and received. Another to display all messages from yesterday  
(the content of these two smart folders change each day) I also have  
a smart folder that displays all messages received that are not from  
one of the many e-mail lists I belong to. That one makes it easy for  
me to keep track of incoming messages without having to weed through  
hundreds of list messages.

Hope this helps. If you have any more questions just ask.
