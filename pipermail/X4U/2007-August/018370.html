Got my copy yesterday. Imovie has some nice features. One can make a  
movie for burning really quick. What I did like in previous versions  
is the sound control which has been taken away.  You used to be able  
to control the volume at certain parts in the click. In other words  
if you have added a song and it covers the sound of the movie you  
could go into the time line and adjust the volume for only that part  
of the click and not the whole song.(hope that this makes sense). The  
new version you can only do the whole song. Any way if any one has  
seen a way of doing this I would like to know. I like the new iphoto  
but it also has problems. Some pictures when you double click on them  
instead of opening the picture and displaying the picture I get a big  
exclamation mark. If I try and use the slide to adjust the picture  
smaller it goes right back to the small icon. This only happens with  
some pictures, not all. Bob
