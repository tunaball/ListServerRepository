I once put a second optical drive into a MacPro 
and found when I activated the Eject menu, it 
showed both optical drives and I could choose 
which one to open or close.

To activate the menu, go to Hard 
Drive/System/Library/CoreServices/Menu Extras/ 
and double click "Eject.menu". A new icon will 
appear on the right end of your menu bar.

If you have already done that, then John 
Baltutis's solution might work as well.
