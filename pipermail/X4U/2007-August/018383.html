Just in case you missed it, Apple has this offer:

"iMovie HD 6, previously distributed with iLife 06, is available for download
for those who have purchased and installed iLife 08. at:
<http://www.apple.com/support/downloads/imovieHD6.html>."

So, while iMovie 08 might have less capability than iMovie 06, both are
available for the price of iLife 08 and since iLife's less than $80 US, that
puts iMovie's cost at less than $15 (less than a movie w/popcorn. BTW, Apple's
take on the new iMovie is that it's designed to make simple movies and get more
normal users to bite. Including the offer for iMovie 06, lets power users do
both.
