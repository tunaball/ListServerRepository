Purchasing iLife 08, I realized a few things.  I LOVE the new iPhoto,  
could care less about iWeb, I use Dreamweaver CS3, I haven't opened  
iDVD but I guess it's better?  I actually sort of, kind of like the  
new iMovie but after trying it, I was finally convinced that it was  
time to purchase Final Cut Express 3.5.  I really like Final Cut  
Express...I would have liked to have purchased Final Cut but it's a  
matter $1,000.00 vs $300.00.  So my suggestion, save pennies and get  
Final Cut Express, you'll be very pleased.
Also, I purchased iWork 08 and I really like it.  Pages it vastly  
improved, KeyNote is better than ever and the new spreadsheet app is  
on par with Excel.
Oh, I forgot to mention GarageBand, I've never liked it, ever since  
it was introduced.  We used to use a program called Acid something or  
other but they've never released a Mac version.  We continue to look  
for something similar but still haven't found it.  We were actually  
hopeful regarding Garageband, but it is sadly lacking the features  
that Acid had.
