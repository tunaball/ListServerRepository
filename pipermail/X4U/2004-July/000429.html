: Shall I respect the burning speed?
: If my CD burner is capable of 24x, can I burn at that speed a CD-R 
: rated at 12x?

You may not want to do that.  The lasers tend to be more intense as the
spindle speed increases.  If you put in media that was designed for
lasers at a certain intensity, you could end up damaging your media or
any other hardware inside.
