There is another method for saving the so-called unsavable movies.

Using Firefox (and, my guess includes other Mozilla based browsers), 
load the page with the said unsavable movie.  Choose Tools>Page Info 
(⌘I).  Under the Media tab, choose the movie file, and click on Save 
As…

I prefer this method because it avoids re-downloading the movie and 
reference links.
