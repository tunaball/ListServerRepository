Joe, I sent a reply to the list but it never seemed to be posted.

Try turning off printer sharing on the printer that does not have the 
printer attached. You may need to quit the printer setup utility and 
restart it. I think your printer will show up then. That is the way it 
works on my systems. I am at 10.3.4 though.

HTH
