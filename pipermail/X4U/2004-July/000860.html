The only company that I know of that has made an effort to specifically 
be compatible with the Macintosh for this purpose is Olympus. 

MacSpeech even offers a refurbished version of the Olympus recorder:
<http://store.macspeech.com/detail.html?sku=iListen_NEW_DS2000_Transcriptio
n&cart=3297952848117353#refurbed>

You can also attach a microphone to an iPod to the same effect:

<http://store.apple.com/1-800-MY-APPLE/WebObjects/AppleStore?productLearnMo
re=T7419LL/A>

Of course, as any audiophile will tell you, there are always ways to get 
audio into your Mac, but I assume that you are looking to do so in the 
most seamless way possible.

feedback on this:
