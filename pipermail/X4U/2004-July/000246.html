Nobody has mentioned the, uh, "hang" or "freeze," when there is no 
beachball--just a still life of what previously was a dynamic process; 
and there is no recourse but to restart.
I call this a crash--or system crash--because the system is no longer 
responsive.
Granted, they do not occur at all often, but never? The only 
circumstances in which I can believe that may be the case is the near 
total avoidance of any and all 3rd party software--and then I remain 
still somewhat amazed at the idea of never experiencing such a loss of 
system responsiveness.
