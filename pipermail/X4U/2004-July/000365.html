On Mon, 12 Jul 2004 09:03:23 +0200, Kirk McElhearn wrote:
> The shell stores it in a history buffer, and, when you end your session,
> writes it to a history file. Do ls -a in your home folder and you'll see
> either .bash_history or .history (for tcsh).

if you're new to this, don't forget you can use the $history command which will display it for you. or, if you know you're after something specific (like say a long scp command) you can:

$ history | grep ssh

and you'll get all of ssh commands in your history.
