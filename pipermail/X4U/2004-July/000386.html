Sorry to reply to my own mail but I have solved my own problem.

I was applying for an account on    "www.spymac.net"
and was waiting for a reply from them with a link that I had to click 
on to activate my account.

In trying to fight Spam, I set up a rule about 2 years ago with the 
word "spy" in it. Any mail with the word
"spy" got filtered to "Deleted Items". So all those replies to me from 
"Spymac.net" got deleted automatically and
therefore I never could see them. I just heard them arrive.

So I deleted that "rule" and all is well !

I guess i will just have to convince the Spymac guys to change their 
name.  :-)
