dreaded "Intel Inside".  Are they trying to align themselves as an  
"UNIX based PC platform" such as those Intel/AMD workstations already

leaps nor bounds -

I've spent years trying to find a "Solid" platform and after trying a  
loaded Win XP Pro system using AMD chips, nothing beats the fully  
loaded G5.  I could never imagine trying to use Apple's Motion, DVD  
Studio, Macromedia Studio, importing Video, using numerous  
applications, X-Windows and other UNIX tools, etc without having to

Once the announcement is made, this will be the biggest "kick in  
teeth" I've personally ever had done to me.  I guess the G5 at that  
point can be placed in to corner with the other has-been systems -  
I'll set mine next to my C64 and Amiga3000.
