     I have a Miglia TV card that I use to record shows from  
Satellite.  The shows are recorded as a Quicktime movie in DV  
format.  Other compression options are available, but I was told DV  
was the best one to use for making DVD's.
     After recording, I launch iMovie HD and then drag the QT movie  
to the clips pane to import it.  After importing, I click on the iDVD  
button and add my chapter markers.  Then, I click on the "Create DVD  
Project" which launches iDVD.  Once iDVD launches, I can close and  
quit iMovie and work on the project in iDVD.  From there, it is just  
a matter of selecting your menu themes and burning.  Of course before  
it burns it is encoded in to DVD format.  That process takes about 5  
hours on my 1Ghz G4.
     I hope that helps.
