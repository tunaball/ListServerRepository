> Hi all,
>
> I like to use Preview to quickly view groups of photos. Sometimes  
> for myself, at other times it to show customers. I use it to narrow  
> down which ones I need to open in Photoshop. My question is, is  
> there a way, when you have multiple images open, (single window  
> with thumbnails appearing in the drawer)  to close just one image  
> without closing the entire window? I'd like to be able to close  
> certain photos as my customer picks and chooses which ones he wants  
> and doesn't want. Is this possible?
>


  Using Preview, If you open a number of images, they will show in  
the drawer on the side.

  You can then add images to the drawer by dragging them in.

  I have not been able to find an easy way to remove them from the  
drawer.

  However, if you Command(Apple Key)-Delete them the will be removed  
from the drawer BUT they will also be sent to the Trash from their  
location in the Finder as well!

  This may work for you , but you may have to work with a copy of the  
files if you want to preserve them.
