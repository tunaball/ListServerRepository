>  > My digital photographs were burnt on a CD-R on a PC. They are not
>  > showing up on my Mac. I have a PC laptop with an IEEE 1394 connector.
>  > Can I move the files unto my LaCie Firewire drive? and then on my Mac?
>  > TIA
>
>Yes
>
>1) Format the firewire drive on the PC/Mac as PC format (MS-DOS
>Filesystem in Apple Disk Utility) , then copy files over
>
>2) Get a utility on your PC that will read a Mac formatted disk, then
>copy files over.


You may also want to consider transferring the files by network.  Put 
both Mac and PC on a network, enable Windows Sharing under the 
Sharing preference on the Mac, and then copy the files to the Mac via 
file sharing.

If you are not familiar with file sharing between OSX and Windows, 
read about it here:

      http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=107083

Good luck.
