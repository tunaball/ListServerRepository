A while ago I asked the list about a good way to compose and send  
html email from within OSX. I thought I would report back on the  
success of the project:

I used Mailings (http://www.limit-point.com/Mailings/Mailings.html)  
to handle the groups and actual mailing of the html. I used  
Dreamweaver to compose the "web page" that would be emailed (any html  
app would probably work).

Mailings used Apple Mail and Address Book and Safari in the process.  
It ran flawlessly and I heartily recommend it as a solution for  
sending html email.
