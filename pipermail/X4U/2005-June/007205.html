on my hacked Cube, but a bit flakey under Tiger on the TiBook. You can find
it here

http://projects.tynsoe.org/en/geektool/

radar. You can put whole web pages, though I tend to just grab the radar
picture

http://image.weather.com/images/maps/current/curwx_600x405.jpg
