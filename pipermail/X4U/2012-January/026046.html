DO NOT purchase one of Brother's "pancake" laser printers.  (The  
really low profile ones.)  They are inexpensive to purchase, but they  
constantly need expensive consumables and they are not as robust.

If you are only looking for something that is cheap to purchase, and  
which has wireless, and you don't care about anything else, have a  
look at:
http://www.amazon.com/Samsung-Electronics-ML-1865W-Wireless- 
Monochrome/dp/B004P2SIV2
