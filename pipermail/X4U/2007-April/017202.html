Zane,

Try rebuilding your Mail database. You can do this from the command  
line with the following command:

/usr/bin/sqlite3 ~/Library/Mail/Envelope\ Index vacuum

Make sure Mail is not running when you do this. You should notice a  
difference -- it worked wonders on my machine with over 100,000  
messages and dozens of mailboxes and mailbox folders.

You can find more about why this works here:
<http://www.hawkwings.net/2007/03/03/scripts-to-automate-the-mailapp- 
envelope-speed-trick/>

RIck
