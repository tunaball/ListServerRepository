would see improvements with the move to Intel and thus to new  
chipsets. But still, USB 2.0 on OS X is slow. I have not yet seen an

somebody offered a hack by looking at Darwin and fixing the problem.  
Similar to what folks did when broadband access was slower in OS X  
than it had to be. But I am not aware of any explanation or fix to  
this phenomenon.
