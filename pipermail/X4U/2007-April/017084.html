On 3/17/07 11:04 AM, Allan Rube¹ <allan at nhbungalow.com> wrote:

> The only way I can open my empty dvd drive is to open Toast and then do the
> eject command. In the finder, F12 doesn't work nor does command-e.
> 
> Any suggestions would be appreciated.

Go to /System/Library/CoreServices/Menu Extras/ and double-click on
Eject.menu

This will add an Eject icon to your menubar that you can use to open or
close your drive.
