I'm currently running on 10.3.9, though I'm not sure how much of an 
effect that will have on this.  Just how capable of handling mail is 
Mail.app?  I use Eudora Pro for my day to day email.  However I have 
a couple of administrative accounts that I use Mail.app to keep an 
eye on.  With only 12,596 messages in the one box, and 2727 in the

some puny machine.
