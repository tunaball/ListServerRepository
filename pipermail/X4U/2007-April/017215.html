Three companies  that I have worked for used ;

Creative Manager Pro - Do not much about his software, just that we  
used it. Seemed to work, for the company. Do not remember a lot of  
interaction with it.


Clients and Profits - Scalable, has been around for quite a while.  
pretty robust, used at smaller design shop and a medium sized ad  
agency. Look in the back of most Design magazines and a few computer  
mags geared to creatives and you will see ads for these guys. They  
used to offer a demo (on disc or download).

I believe either of these does some if not all of what you are  
looking for. Clients and Profits did offer customization for one of  
our firms, they created specific modules to meet the requirements for  
one of our bigger clients. They were decent people to work with, this  
was back in 1996.

Hope that helps.
