You should have your friend move any Adobe preferences out of the main 
Library/Preferences folder. Move them to desktop and if the problem 
persists they can be returned.

I have also read an article some time back on how the "Legal" folder can 
cause quit issues... how I don't know and with CS or CS2 I can't remember. 
The directives were to just delete all the Legal folders from each of the 
CS app folders.

Has your friend checked their directory with something like Disk Warrior?

Have they looked up the Photoshop Crash Logs in the Console app?

Just a few things I'd do.
