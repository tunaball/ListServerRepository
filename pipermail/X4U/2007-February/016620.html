I've been very happy (as I've said before and it hasn't changed) with 
my TrayDock from WiebeTech. I can use both ATA and SATA drives in the 
same Dock (just have to buy different trays for different kinds of 
drive). When you buy the TrayDock it comes with one tray, and you can 
choose whether it is ATA or SATA. Later, if you want to have access 
to another kind of drive, you can buy a tray for that kind and put it 
in the same Dock.
