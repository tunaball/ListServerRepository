Here's a page at the Apple site about getting USB printers to work 
with Airport Base Stations. 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=107418>

I know that not all USB printers support printing from the base 
station. The other day I found a list of supported USB printers, but 
I can't find it today. I do notice that at the end of the referenced 
page, it says to contact your printer manufacturer to find out if 
your printer supports the Airport connection.  Also, note that for 
people trying this with multifunction printers, only the printing 
function can be used over the  Airport connection, not scanning or 
the rest.

Here is an unofficial list of printers that support the Airport Extreme:
<http://www.ifelix.co.uk/tech/1013.html>
There are some Brother printers on that list.

That page links to a page at the Brother site that offers a 
workaround if your printer seems not to work with the base station: 
<http://global.solutions.brother.com/Library/en_us/faq/faq000512_000/>
