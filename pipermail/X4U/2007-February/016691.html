Hard to say. Spinning HDs down and back up might have some effect on their
life-expectancy. FWIW, I've run my G4, 450 MP, for over six years, 24/7, except
when replacing HW or updating SW which necessitates a shutdown or restart, and
never sleep the computer or HDs, just the display. My mate, however, also never
sleeps the computer or HDs, but does shutdown each evening. Neither machine
suffers from "narcolepsy."
