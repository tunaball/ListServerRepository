Right. Whenever the Mail app can't connect to a mail server (the  
server didn't respond fast enough or any of a number of reasons) you  
get the password dialog. I don't know why that is the case, but  
there's not much I can do about it (I did file a bug report).

Instead of quitting Mail, I just cancel the dialog box, go to the  
menu Mailbox->Online Status... and tell it to take that mailbox back  
online. Unless there's still come kind of connection problem, that  
fixes it.
