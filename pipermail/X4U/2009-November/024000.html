Airport Express is a little more expensive then a Linksys, D-Link,  
etc... But it will allow you to connect the printer to it rather than  
a computer. Drawback is that will not provide you with any wired  
network ports, just up to 10 wireless ports. The alternative is to go  
with a cheaper 3rd party wireless router and a wireless print server  
to connect to your printer. Or spend the big bucks on the Airport  
Extreme Base station or Time capsule (Which might fulfill  your backup  
needs.
