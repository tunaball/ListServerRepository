Turns out the problem was with Stuffit Deluxe 11.02 and the  
contextual menus item. An updated contextual menu plugin is available

ftp\\:ftp.smithmicro.com/stuffit/stuffitcm.plugin.zip

if your stuffitcm.plugin is dated before Jan 5, 2007 and your version  
is 11.02 this may be a problem. I was unaware of it affecting my  
computer as I very seldom control click on icons on my desktop. :)
