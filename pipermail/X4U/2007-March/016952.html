On 03/02/07, Lee Licata <lalicata at alum.rpi.edu> wrote:
>
> Have a G4 PB, System 10.4.8, and all up to date.
>
> Is there a way to change where Spotlight searches. It now searches
> "computer" and I really want the default to be "home."

That's not Spotlight, but Finder's Find. Click on the Desktop, click on Home,
enter a search term, and, when it finishes,  click on the upper, left-hand
button. Click on the Desktop and CMD+F. That should pop up with Home selected.
Note this won't work with Others, only between Computer and Home. I don't have
any Servers, so I don't know it work with that option.
