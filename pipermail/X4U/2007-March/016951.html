For anyone who uses 'Spotlight', I would strongly recommend that you  
change to 'Notlight' (v. 1.4) which has a much better and more  
intuitive graphic front end.  (It uses the same Spotlight kernel and  
index.)

You can search a single file, a folder, a group of folders, a volume,  
or an entire disk or group of disks.

Go to:

    http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/29015

for more info and a free download.
