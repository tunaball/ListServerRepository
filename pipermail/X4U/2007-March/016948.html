> It's a yellow shield icon in the group of icons at the right end of

> install. If they want to watch the installation process right click  
> on the icon and choose the appropriate option (or maybe just double  
> click on it).
>
> Another option is to go to the Automatic Updates control panel and  
> choose to let it download and install updates automatically. Then  
> they will just get a notice that they need to reboot on occasion.
