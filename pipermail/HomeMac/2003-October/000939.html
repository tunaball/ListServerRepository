Regarding your question about having the date and time show together on the
menu bar, this is not possible with Apple's clock as Adam correctly pointed
out. However, it is possible with a small utility called "iClock." You
should be able to find it on www.versiontracker.com.


Good luck,
B

> To answer your first question, you cannot have the date appear right
> alongside with the time, but if you click on the time, a menu pops up
> with the date. As to your second question, you cannot boot from OS 9 or
> earlier on any newer Mac, including the 17" 1 GHz iMac. It comes with
> Classic emulation in OS X, which starts automatically when you launch
> any OS 9 app. Virtually ever OS 9 app will work fine in Classic, with
> only a few exceptions. If for some reason when you start Classic (from
> the Classic System Preferences pane), you should be able to reinstall
> Classic with the Software Restore discs that came with your iMac.
> 
> Hope this helps. :)
