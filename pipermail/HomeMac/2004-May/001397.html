Denise,

My wife and I have an Olympus D-390 that works great with iPhoto.  It 
dropped to $100 the last time I checked at Target.  Very nice starter 
digital camera.  I leave the macro (close-up) option enabled and 
everything else is automatic for 99% of friends and family pictures, 
unless the distance gets too far, then I switch back to regular.  Hope 
this helps.
