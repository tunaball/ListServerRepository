> Chris,
>
> Here's what cured the problem for me in system 9.x:
>
> Control Panels>Energy Saver
>
> Choose Sleep Setup Tab
>
> Click on Show Details in lower right corner
>
> Put system to sleep when it's inactive--move slider to Never
>
> Check box for Separate timing for display sleep--move slider to 30 
> min. (or
> whatever you prefer)
>
> Check box for Separate timing for hard disk sleep--move slider to 35 
> min.
> (or whatever you prefer)
>
> The computer still goes to sleep, as you have told it, but I NEVER 
> have a
> problem waking it.
>
