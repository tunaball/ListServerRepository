I just tried to do what you want to do and found that after you do the 
first formula you can select that cell plus the cells you want to 
calculate and the then select fill down from the calculate menu. In C1 
I entered =A1+B1. Then I selected cells C1 thru C10 and then Fill Down 
from the Calculate menu. The formula in C2 is A2+B2, in C3 it's A3+B3, 
etc.
