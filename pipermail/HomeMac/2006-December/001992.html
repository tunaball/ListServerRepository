Macheist's Killer Software Deal

by Ed at Smalldog.com

Macheist.com has declared this “The Week of the Independent Mac  
Developer,” and they’ve put together a collection of excellent  
software available for the Mac at a steeply discounted price of $49.

They’re offering Delicious Library, FotoMagico, Shapeshifter,  
DEVONthink Personal, Disco, RapidWeaver, iClip 4, a Pangea game, and,  
as a bonus, if enough people buy the bundle, Newsfire and Textmate as  
well.

These excellent software titles would cost $288.75 if purchased  
separately – that $49 price is incredible.

According to Macheist, “Perhaps best of all, each of the developers  
participating have agreed to donate 25% of each bundle sale to a  
charity of the customer’s choice. When the bundle sale is finished,  
MacHeist will be making donations to 8 participating charities in the  
Mac community’s name.”

There has been some grumbling that MacHeist is not good for  
developers. However, the developers have chosen to participate in  
this very limited offer. All of these titles are great; this is  
almost too good of an offer to refuse!
