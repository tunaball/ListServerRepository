iLife '04 comes with all of Apple's new computers, so you would have 
iMovie 4 and iPhoto 4 as well, but it does not come with Panther alone. 
If you buy Panther with an older computer that came with the original 
iLife, you will not get GarageBand or updated versions of any of the 
other applications.

Hope this clears this up once and for all. :)
