I've used The Chip Merchant in San Diego, CA for years and have 
always been very happy with them. Good pricing, excellent support 
(tell them what model you've got and they'll determine the right 
model of RAM), and no hassles.

One time I suspected a SIMM (or was it a DIMM?!) had gone bad after 
about 3 years. They said to send it to them and they'd check it; if 
it was bad they'd replace it at no cost. It was and they did within 
about 2 days!
