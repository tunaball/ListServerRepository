On Wednesday, May 28, 2003, at 02:15 Canada/Eastern, Duane Murphy wrote:

> If you want to do cross-platform discs, you should get a copy of Toast.
> Its reasonably priced and very powerful.

On the other hand, since you've been using a Zip disk anyway, you can 
use it to share your pictures with your kids (if their Wintel machines 
have Zip drives). Get a Zip formatted for PCs or take a blank Zip and 
format it for MS-DOS (Disk Utility -> Erase -> Volume Format: MS-DOS 
File System -> Erase). After formatting it will be mounted like any 
other disk, and you can copy the JPEGs to it. It will be readable on 
PCs -- though it may not be as elegant a solution as a hybrid CD burned 
with Toast.
