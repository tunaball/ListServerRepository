Jane,

Look in your old computer for a folder called "Identities" that would 
be located in one of two places. it will either be in the Outlook 
Express folder itself, or located in the "User Data Folder" that will 
be in your Documents folder. Then put the "Identities" folder in your 
new computer directly inside the Outlook Express folder that holds the 
actual Outlook Express application. Then see if it shows up when you 
are running classic in OSX. If it is properly in place you should have 
no problem.
