On 6/24/03, Jane Sprando <janesprando at msn.com> wrote:
>
> I have Os 9.1, 9.1.1 and 9.2 updates on zip disks. I would like to burn them
> on a CD.
>
> 1. Can I just drag the files to the CD? Or do I need to use Disk Copy?

Copy them to the CD as .img files. Then when you want to use one, pop in
the CD, copy the file to the HD, eject the CD, and double-click it. BTW,
use this routine to ensure that everything went correctly.

> 2. Can I put more than 1 update on the CD, if they will fit?

Any number.
