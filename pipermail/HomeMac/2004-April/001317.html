I was a long time user of Eudora. I quickly changed to Cyberdog when it
was available what a dream. When Cyberdog died, I went looking for a new
program. I am very happy with PowerMail <http://www.ctmdev.com/>. This
program is very much like Cyberdog and people also tell me much like
Claris eMailer which was another popular and easy to use Mail program.

My wife and even my children use PowerMail with out trouble. It's quite
easy simple and intuitive.

Give it a try.
