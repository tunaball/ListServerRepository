Duane,

That image was from Thunderbird, and it is not Thunderbird that has the 
problem, it is Mail.app. Since you are using a Mac mail reader, you may 
or may not notice it. When I used Mail.app, all links from Mac mail 
programs worked when received, but if I sent to a Windows or some other 
Mac programs such as Thunderbird they would not. When I switched to 
Thunderbird my link problem was eliminated for sending, but since 
Mail.app sends improper links, Thunderbird realizes they are wrong and 
they don't work, as they shouldn't when they are improper. My daughter, 
my friend, and I, all had or have the same problem with Mail.app.
