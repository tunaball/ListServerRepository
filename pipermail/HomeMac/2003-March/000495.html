clearly Eudora has been upgraded. This seems like a newer version of
Eudora that requires a newer version of CarbonLib. The version of
CarbonLib that Eudora needs cannot be run on OS 8.1.

1. Upgrade to a newer OS, probably 9 or X

Does Eudora have an automatic update setting? Do you have more than on
copy of Eudora on your machine?
