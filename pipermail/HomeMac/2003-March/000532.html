I'm with Duane, buy a larger HD than 20 gigs. I bought an 80 gig  
Western Digital drive this week for under $80. You will get more  
storage space, as well as speed because you won't have fragmentation. I  
would get a 7200 RPM drive instead of the 5400 RPM that came with your  
iMac. Western Digital has a Special Edition drive with an 8 meg cache,  
really is a quick drive. I highly recommend those, and I hated WD for  
some many years. 
 
Hope this helps a bit, 
groucho
