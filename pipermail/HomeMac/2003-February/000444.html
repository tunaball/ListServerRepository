I can only speak for using Eudora on a Mac....

When I prepare a new message, there are fields for To: , From (which 
automatically fills with my name/email address), Subject:, etc. 
There's also a field titled X-Attachments. I can drag icons of 
graphic file(s) (actually any file(s) into that area and they will 
become attachments.

I can also drag the icon of a graphic file into whatever spot I want 
within the body of the message. (I guess this is called inserting or 
embedding.) The graphic opens. Most (not all!) recipients upon 
opening the email message will see the graphic. I don't think AOL 
people can see it.
