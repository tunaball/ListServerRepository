I'm very satisfied with my Sony DSC-S75 digital camera.

(very handy) and it's battery life is outstanding: around 120 picts (1.2 MB
each), a quarter with flash, always being turned on and off during city
visits. My best friend has a Nikon 990 (great camera) and but has to
recharge 2 to 3 times more...
