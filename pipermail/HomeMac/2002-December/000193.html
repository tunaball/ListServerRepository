I've been using Eudora for Years!  Basically love it.

However, recently ran into a major snag. I tend to get a lot of 
email. I tend to leave messages (read or unread!) in my in box. It 
grows. And grows. And grows. Recently I went back to look at a 
message from several months ago. Found the message (by sender) in the 
"In" box listing. Opened the message, and it was a chopped off 
message that had nothing whatever to do with the desired message! 
This got happening more and more, even with really recent messages. I 
finally said the heck with it (actually, that's not what I said!) and 
completely trashed the ENTIRE In box and have started over with a new 
one.

Buried in the Eudora documentation (or was it their Tech Support 
knowledge base?) was a mention that the In Box and Out Box should not 
be allowed to become over 1 Meg.  This is basically impossible!

Oh, well. I still prefer it to others.
