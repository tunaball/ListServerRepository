I'd agree with your husband on that. It wouldn't hurt to take a 
printout of what your considering from MacMall to the local dealer and 
ask them what they can do. Don't forget to add in the price of shipping 
though.

For all of our major electronics/appliance purchases I've gone to the 
big chain stores to find what we wanted and wrote down model numbers 
and prices. Then I go to the locally owned/operated stores and show 
them what I've found and ask what they can do. I can't think of a 
single instance where they haven't met, or even beaten the "chain 
store" deals. Haven't tried that yet with internet deals, but that's 
next.
