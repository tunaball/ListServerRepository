This is also a repeat from an earlier message: one of the functions in 
Aladdin's 'Internet Cleanup' product is "SpyAlert."

<http://www.aladdinsys.com/mac/cleanup/index.html>

You mentioned having a cable Internet connection and worrying what 
dangers that might bring. I use a little Linksys EtherFast cable/DSL 
router which sits between my cable modem and my computers. This box 
does two things for me, it lets multiple computers (Macs in my case) 
share the cable modem connection, and it is a firewall to block any 
probing from unknown sources coming in from the internet.

Recently somewhere I read discouraging comments about Linksys's support 
for Mac users (although I haven't had any problems and haven't needed 
any support). The D-Link DI-604 which costs around $50 from places like 
Office Depot might be worth a look.
