On Monday, Feb 23, 2004, at 21:47 Canada/Eastern, George Booth wrote:

> I just ordered a Pioneer DVR-106 DVD-R/RW, DVD+r/+RW Writer DVR -106D 
> with Nero Burning Software and have 2 questions.

Nero is excellent -- but it's for Windows.

> I have a 400 MHz PPC G4 with 640 MB SDRAM and need to know if I need a 
> superdrive or anything else to make a movie [...]

To make a movie? You need money and talent, in that order (the latter 
being optional).

To write a movie to DVD? No, this drive should work fine with your 
machine, provided you have Jaguar or later. See 
<http://www.xlr8yourmac.com/> for details and instructions.

> [...] can I use it to attach to a TV to record movies?

If "it" is the drive, no, you can't attach it to the TV to record 
movies. You can only attach it to a computer. If "it" is the Mac, the 
answer is again no; you need a digitizer or converter (which can be a 
digital camera) to convert to DV the signal provided by the TV.
