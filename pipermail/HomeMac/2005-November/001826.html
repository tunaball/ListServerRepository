provider. Turns out that the highest speed my provider offers is 4Mbps/512k upstream, 
but in MY area (Lawrenceville, Georgia) the fastest is 3Mbps/256k upstream. I 
was then told a sweet lie about my area being able to have the 4Mbps access 
"in about 4-6 months." 

(THAT sounded alot like a lie I was told 10 years ago, when my cable TV 
provider in Houston told me that my area of town could only access 30 cable

years...)

Anyway, not uncoincidently(!), I then called my local DSL provider, and found 
out that my area of Lawrenceville has fiber optic lines, so if i elected to 
get DSL, I would not need a DSL modem. They could not answer how far I was from
