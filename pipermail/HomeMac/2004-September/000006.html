default). You are downloading these items by clicking on something in a
window. I would not expect that something get's downloaded without your
doing something (if this were Windows, I'd expect it :-).

You can check the Safari download window to see what files have been
downloaded. You can click to see where they are. I don't think it will
tell you where they came from however.
