Have you rebuilt the Entourage database yet?
Hold the Option Key when you launch Entourage. Do a simple rebuild of the
database when the dialog opens up. If it doesn't work you could try the
complex rebuild. The complex rebuild deletes more data from the database but
you will download all your messages from the server again.
