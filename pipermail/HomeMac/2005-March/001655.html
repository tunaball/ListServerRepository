Hi Jane,

All it means is that you can use these drives on USB, and Firewire 400, 
and 800.

Your iMac only has Firewire 400 so you will not get the increased speed 
of firewire 800,
and USB 1.1 not USB 2.
So if you only intend to use it on your Mac you do not need the extra 
that this offers a simple Firewire 400 drive will do, however if you 
want to use it on other computer that have USB 2 or Firewire 800 it may 
be worth the extra.
