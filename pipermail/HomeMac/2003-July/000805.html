In "Preferences" there was only one mention of "frames" and it was SHOW
FRAMES, which was checked.

So I couldn't understand why I couldn't open a web page with frames that I
wanted to see.

So today I thought...well, why not uncheck the box and see what happens?

Lo and behold, it worked...so I can now read web pages with frames!

I would have thought it would be the other way around--checking something
that mentioned FRAMES would activate it...but the world isn't always
logical.

As you can see below, I'm a writer, so I need my computer, even if I don't
understand how it works very well.

Thanks for hearing me out.
