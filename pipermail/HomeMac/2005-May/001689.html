Jane - it sounds like you're having difficulties adjusting to Macs after
using Windows previously. Nothing wrong with that - while much is the
same, there are a lot of little confusing differences for Mac newbies.

I'd recommend buying a copy of Robin Williams (no, not that one :-) )
'The Little Mac Book' or David Pogue's 'MacOS The Missing Manual'. Make
sure you purchase the copy for your version of MacOS. Much has changed
between MacOS 8 & 9 and MacOS X. There are also big differences between
different versions of OS X.
