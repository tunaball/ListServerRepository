Logitech has a free download of their Logitech Control Center for OS-X.  
Once installed, LCC is launched from its icon in the bottom "Other"  
section of the system preferences panel. Between that and the regular  
mouse system preferences control panel settings I have my Logitech  
mouse cursor moving as fast as I was used to when running under OS9.

<http://www.logitech.com/index.cfm?page=downloads/ 
software&CRID=320&contentid=6004&OSID=9&countryid=1&languageid=1>

 From the www page: "The Logitech Control Center (LCC) supports Mac OS X  
and allows you to program your keyboard and/or mouse buttons to perform  
different functions and to take full advantage of your Logitech  
keyboard, mouse or trackball."
