IMHO, what really matters is what you want the printer to do.

I print a lot of photos and would not consider using anything but my 
Epson printers for that.  I also have two HP Printers.  The 
Photosmart does a beautiful job on photos too but if I want to print 
text, it soaks the paper.  My Epson 1520 does both text and photos 
well.  My other HP is not even connected anymore.  The color, text or 
photo, was not the results I wanted.  I also have an Epson 2500 that 
I bought for my computers that use usb ports.  Mostly my HPs gather 
dust.  I have had my Epson 1520 for more than 5 years and not a 
problem.  I leave it on and it does it's cleaning routine at it's own 
will so it is ready to go when I want it. The Epson 2500 is a little 
more than 3 years old and it's cleaning process is much faster than 
the old 1520 so I do not leave that one on.

Just my experience with HPs and Epsons.
