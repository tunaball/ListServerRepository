I haven't used it to burn DVDs (except data DVDs), but I use it
regularly (i.e. daily) to burn VCDs and SVCDs.

Toast doesn't have built-in capabilities to author menus, etc. You need
to do that sort of thing with an authoring application. A really handy
"donationware" app for this purpose is "VCD Builder" by Johan Lindström
-- you can find it on Version Tracker. Assemble all your pieces and then
build a graphic "flow of control" in VCD Builder. It will create the
files you need, launch Toast Titanium with them already loaded in, just
waiting for you to insert a black CD and click Record.
