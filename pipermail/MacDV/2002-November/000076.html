Put the DVD in your drive. Open up Disk Copy Utility. Create New Image from
Device. Save it someplace convenient, like your desktop. Eject the DVD.
Then, still in Disk Copy utility, Burn Image. Find the image you just
created and select it. Put in a blank DVD and burn away.
