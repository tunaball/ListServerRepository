I have almost always used "shintaro" branded DVD-R discs in speeds from 
1x up to 8x. We have 3 Macs, G4, G5, an iBook and 3 stand alone DVD 
players and a Sony PlayStation.  I have only burnt a couple of 
coasters. But all successful burns have played equally well in all of 
the hardware we have. I have also sent my DVDs to relatives in US and 
UK and
they have had no problems with them.
"shintaro" is the cheapest I can find here in Melbourne, Australia. I 
buy them in spindles of 50 for less than a dollar each. I have never 
tried DVD+R.  I have found that our standalone players are picky about 
DVD-RW (re-writable) the Playstation in particular seems to have a real 
aversion to them.
