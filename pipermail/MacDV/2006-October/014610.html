I've just finished y first large project in FCE 3.5 (1.5 hour video)  
and iDVD 6 and also had some problem with pixelation in certain  
moving sections. The fix that seemed to work for me was having iDVD  
encoding to a relatively empty Hard Disk (avoiding chances of disk  
access interrupting the encoding process) and saving as an IMG file  
rather than burning directly to DVD. Then I burned the IMG file to HD  
later.

At least this seemed to work for me - of course it could have been  
something else completely.
