A media converter uses DV codec. DV uses 3.6mg/sec, which is about 13GB/hour

Since you are starting with pretty high quality, you could take the DV video
files and then compress to a different codec, like iPod video, H.264 which
will hold much much more. HandBrake is good for that process.
