You need to trick the Mac.  After the project has finished encoding and 
asks for a disk you put in a DVD-R disk then after it is verified eject 
it and replace it with a -RW disk.  There is no need to worry you have 
a fairly large amount of time during which to do the switch.  I used to 
do this regularly but with media cost well below $1 I no-longer  do it 
very often.  Less set-top players are compatible with -RW format than 
-R so if you are sending it as a preview for others to see you might 
get a call that they are unable to view your disk.
