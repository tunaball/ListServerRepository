I've been successfully burning DVDs in iDVD 3 and recently upgraded to 
iDVD4. When I went to burn my first DVD on 4, it got hung up on the 3rd 
stage "asset encoding". After a few hours, I force quit and and tried 
again. Same thing. I decided to start the project from scratch, 
starting with iMovie, then iDVD 4. Went to burn and yup, same thing. I 
finally let it go all night and the next morning there was a completed 
disk waiting for me.

I searched around on the web, and found that others are experiencing 
this problem as well. It appears to be due to the 2-pass VBR encoding 
process. Unfortunately, you don't have the option to encode at a 
constant bit rate with projects over 60 minutes. Luckily, I had made a 
backup image of my drive before installing iLife '04, and am 
considering going back to iDVD 3. Just thought I'd pass on the info for 
those of you considering the upgrade, but require a fast work flow. You 
might end up needing a dual processor G5 to use this $49 app!

http://macintouch.com/idvd4.html
