On Jan 6, 2004, at 6:43 AM, chrisandheather at shaw.ca wrote:

> Does anyone know if I can install a hard drive this size on my Blue 
> and White G3?

You can, but I don't know what the results would be. You may or may not 
be able to see 127 GB of drive. For details from Apple see 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=86178>

Your best bet, unless you want to install another controller card, is 
to put it in an external Firewire enclosure, which IIRC can even be 
cheaper than a new controller card.
