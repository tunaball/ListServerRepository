OTOH, it usually only costs ~$50 more to buy the drive in a FW 
enclosure to begin with. Personally, I've never found it cost effective 
to put a new drive into a FW enclosure myself. The price difference has 
rarely been more than $10 that I've seen and is typically only $5.

With respect to internal drives, OWC is selling the 120 GB IBM/Hitachi 
180GXP's for $99. I'd fill up internal bays with these (the FW version 
is currently $159).

Also, to use anything bigger than 128 GB you need an mirrored door G4 
or a different controller card. For details see 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=86178> .

Hope this helps, even though its a bit late.
