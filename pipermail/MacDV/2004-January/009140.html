A number of us use inexpensive Canon ZRnn models. I started with a ZR10 
and when it messed up a tape, I got a ZR40 which was around $400. 
That's a discontinued model I believe because others had been 
introduced. I didn't see any differences between the higher end models 
except for the size of the memory used to record still photos. I didn't 
need that capability at all, so I went with the lowest end I could 
find. It was an "open box" at Fry's, but it was in perfect condition. 
It looked like someone had just peeked inside and not even removed 
anything. After Christmas is a good time to look for these as people 
exchange gifts for different models.
