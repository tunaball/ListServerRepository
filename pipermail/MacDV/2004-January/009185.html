You can do this by conecting the two computers by firewire.  To this 
make sure the computer with the files you want to transfer is turned 
off (not just in sleep mode).  Connect the firewire ports then start 
the computer while holding down the letter T until a firewire symbol 
appears on the screen.  The other computer should now have the drive of 
the first computer mounted in the Finder.  You can copy files from and 
to it like any other external drive.
