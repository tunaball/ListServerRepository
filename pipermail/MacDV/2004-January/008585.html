At 5:00 PM -0500 1/5/04, KCIVORY at aol.com wrote:
>Can anyone tell me the steps involved in taking a track out of an iMovie and
>getting it to CD? I have done it once before and now cannot seem to remember
>the path of steps.
>I have made voice overs for public service announcements and need to
>individually place them on a CD for playback. TIA.
>
>Kelly Casey
>Experience Shows, Inc.

Launch iTunes. Open the project's reference movie
(it's found in the top-level folder). You may have
to drag the reference movie onto the iTunes icon.

Make a new play list. Add the track into the play list.

Select the play list.

Burn it.
