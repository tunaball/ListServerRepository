Eh? Your obtuse opinions aren't making sense to me. Apple's a monopoly 
because...why? Apple has not *forced* anyone to use their technologies, 
including Safari, Mail, etc. Microsoft *forced* PC makers to keep IE on 
machines and threatened to pull it licensing if they installed any other 
competing product (namely Netscape) alongside IE in the default 
installation for new machines. That's my understanding of the problem 
with Microsoft. It was leveraging its user base (97%) to force buyers 
(PC makers) to *exclude* competitors from 3rd party installations. Apple 
has done none of this.
