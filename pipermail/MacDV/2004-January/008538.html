No offense intended, but without the gimbal handle that's nothing but a 
tripod with it's legs cut off and weighted.  It's helpful, but doesn't 
do (at all) what an actual Glide-Cam does.  Without the gimbal your arm

GlideCam a GlideCam.  I've filmed stuff with a stunted tripod like that

saw when I used my new one...
