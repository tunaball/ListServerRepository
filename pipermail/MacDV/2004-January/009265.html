On Monday, January 19, 2004, at 03:05  PM, Daniel Beck wrote:

> What are the steps I need to take to copy one of my DVDs I made with 
> iDVD3 to another DVD using Toast 5?
>
> Daniel
>
>

Put in DVD.
  Press button marked "copy"
Direct it to where you want it to go.
In the end you should have a folder with Video and audio TS files 
inside.
Take out the DVD and put in a blank.
set it for DVD and drop the folder in the Toast window.
Burn.
