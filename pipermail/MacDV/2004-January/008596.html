On 1/5/04 at 5:00 PM, Kelly Casey <KCIVORY at aol.com> transmitted the
following electronic message:

>
>Can anyone tell me the steps involved in taking a track out of an
>iMovie and getting it to CD? I have done it once before and now cannot
>seem to remember the path of steps. I have made voice overs for public
>service announcements and need to individually place them on a CD for
>playback. TIA.
>

In the Timeline, turn off the tracks you don't want exported. Then
choose Export from the File menu, choose QuickTime in the popup, and
specify AIFF (or whatever audio format you're seeking) as the format in
the Save dialog.
