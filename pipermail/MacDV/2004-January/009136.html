DVD implies compression. It's compressed to whatever extent it needs to 
be to fit on the DVD. It's not in a form now that is directly 
importable to iMovie or Final Cut. The miniDV tape is what you want to 
use. Borrow, Rent, or consider buying an inexpensive DV camera if you 
want to make more movies in the future. You should be able to get 
something for around $400 new or maybe take a chance on something used 
from eBay.
