I would recommend an app called Still Life.  It's super easy to use, 
fast, and the movies it produces look wonderful!

As far as the problems you are having with the "zebra" patterns, it's 
probably because your images are actually too *high* in resolution, and 
any single-pixel details are going to flicker back and forth between 
the interlaced fields of video...  I don't really know of a quick and 
easy solution (that doesn't involve high-dollar broadcast graphics 
equipment anyway) -- anybody?
