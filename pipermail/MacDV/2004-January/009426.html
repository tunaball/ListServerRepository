Or even easier, using a crossover cable (special type of ethernet 
cable), which lets you just plug two computers together without a 
router.

And now, for the cool mac trick of the day: most modern macs include a 
auto-magic crossover ability in the ethernet card, which means you can 
just use an ordinary ethernet cable to plug them together and the 
computer will take care of the rest.
