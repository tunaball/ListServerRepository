Steve,
I don't know about you, but when I'm encoding a DVD, I don't do ANYTHING else with my computer, including checking for free space. Call me superstitious, but as soon as I click that "Burn" button, it's hands-off until the disc pops out, be that 15 minutes or six hours later.

I would suspect that if iDVD is using the boot drive for a scratch disc, it will delete the file when the job is done, unless the disc-burning process fails or is aborted. Only then might we find a tell-tale file... though it's probably invisible. :-)
