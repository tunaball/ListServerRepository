>I'd like a recommendation or two concerning importing video from S-vhs
>tape into a Mac G4 system. What is needed since the signal comes via
>composite video (or S cable) instead of Firewire?
>
>We want to edit footage using iMovie3 & then burn the final copy to disc
>using Toast 6 (or iDVD if that's better).
>
>Thanks ahead listers,
>
>Bruce

If you have access to a camcorder with playthrough (analog-to-digital 
conversion) capability, like my Canon ZR 40, for example, you can use 
that. Just connect the output of the VCR to the camcorder input (my 
ZR 40 has both composite and S-Video inputs), connect the FireWire 
cable from the camcorder to the Mac, configure the appropriate 
settings in the camcorder, and you're all set.
