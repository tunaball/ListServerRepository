Yes, as Coj writes, the camera must be in play mode, not camera 
mode. I have a much older Panny miniDV, so they may have changed 
things, but you should check the menus to make sure the AV 
connectors are set to be 'In' and  not 'Out'. Finally, to record 
to miniDV (as opposed to just passing the signal through) my 
Panny requires that you use the remote control's record button -- 
not the record button on the camera.
