By compressing the original Hi8 material into a DVD (MPEG2) you now can¹t
edit it.

You can start over again, with the original footage and convert via a
converter box to the DV format. Then you can import the footage into either
iMovie, FCP, or Premire.

Or, you can convert the MPEG2 video into DV footage via a program like MPEG
streamclip, and import this footage into your editing application of choice.
It won¹t be as sharp as if you take the original Hi8 and go to DV.
