Hi, I have a DVS2U.  the easiest way is to make a DV of your analog 
audio through the analog inputs and then firewire that into the 
computer.
  While you are down in the menus arranging to record analog to DV you 
could try setting the analog output to F-1. Then if it is going to come 
out the firewire it might.
