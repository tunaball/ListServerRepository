An updated version of a previous post of mine (for anyone with a 
photographic memory:-)

I've been using the LaCie 160 Gb Drive Firewire 400 drive (with a PB 
Titanium 667) for about 7 months - its very good and also now available 
in larger sizes all with Firewire 800/400 and USB 2. I use it for 
Audio/video files and as a boot drive when needed.  It's fast enough to 
let me import DV footage straight to the external drive with no 
problems (Sony TRV25e). Costs quite a bit less than £1 UK per gigabyte 
from my local Apple dealer or online purchase.
