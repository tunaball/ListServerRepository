I have a Sony Media converter. I wish Garageband could see it.
I have a mixer hooked up to it with a DBX comp/limiter inserted to the 
active channels.
I can put anything through here into FCP. Capture audio only/ stereo 
for convenience.
Big advice is to keep levels low. Average should be about -12  on the 
FCP meters.
If like me you have big VU meters to stare at then you want to see 
about a -3 peak
at most. (unless your limiter is sucking it up).
