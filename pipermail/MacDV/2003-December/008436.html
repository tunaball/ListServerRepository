Well If I am capturing a family film to my boot drive with iMovie and 
it runs into fragments of files laying around this hurts performance. 
It must.
Lets say you have a music folder you have been adding to for two years 
and you drop a song in it today. It does not land with the other files 
it just goes wherever.
Yes I could just drag the folder to another drive and delete and drag 
it back..
That defrags one folder.
Butt we arre adding and deleting things all the time. Putting them in 
there place and freeing up your free space so as to be contiguous 
improves the time it takes to find a file,
and keeps the free space clean.
