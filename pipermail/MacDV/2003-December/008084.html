On Dec 3, 2003, at 11:25 AM, Paul Williams wrote:

> The first of the two drives i was going to partition 3 ways: OS, apps, 
> docs.
> The second drive i'll probably partition 50/50 for video files and 
> scratch. What's a good size for a scratch disc? 40, 50 gig?

Repeat.  DO NOT separate your OS and apps -- keep those on one big 
happy partition.  It really is better to keep those together in OS X.  
If you must partition your main drive, just split it in two -- 80 GB 
for OS and apps TOGETHER, and 80 GB for media/scratch.

And keep the second drive as one big happy partition.  There really is 
no need to partition it if it's just going to hold your video and audio 
media.
