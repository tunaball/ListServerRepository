If you look inside the Toast application package, you'll find a
Photoshop document, Ocean.psd. That is what is used for the background
and menu layout. With a little care, you could replace the background
graphic with a different one and even rearrange/reshape the text boxes.
BE CAREFUL, though, as the layer structure must remain constant (Toast
assumes that all the layers are there in a specific order and with
specific content types/names).
