You can always digitize again from the DVD, but you will lose A LOT of 
quality since the DVD is highly compressed MPEG-2.

I'd say burn the edited version to DVD and keep the original tapes 
around if you ever need the footage again.

Or...  You could use Toast 7's new "spanning" feature and burn your 
iMovie and iDVD projects across multiple discs.  Toast 7 will break up 
large files/folders into chunks small enough to fit on a CD-R or DVD-R, 
and it puts a little restore app on each disc in the set for restoration 
of the whole archive or individual files or folders.  I haven't tried 
this first-hand yet, but hope to soon when my company gets around to 
getting the upgrade.  :-)
