hey jeremy,

you would get better results in stripping the video off the dvd by  
using 'mactheripper', 'handbrake', '0SEx 0.0101b' or similar  
products. they spit out the videofiles and those you can convert  
(with qt pro e.g.) into any other format.

of course snapz pro is a fantatsic app too, which is great for  
creating video tutorials and such.
