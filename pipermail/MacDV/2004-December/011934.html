Export your video as .dv .
You can do this through either way you mentioned.
.dv at full resolution.
Then just drag and drop it into the iDVD menu window, (not an import) 
but only where the buttons go, NOT where the preview moving picture 
goes (if one is in your chosen template).
If you need chapters do that in FCE. place the cursor at a chapter spot 
(do the first one at the very beginning) and hit "m" twice. Make it a 
chapter marker and give it a name.
