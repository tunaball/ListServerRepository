I'm using a M-Audio Mobile Pre USB with a Marshall Electronics MXL-V57M 
condenser Mic.  The Mobile Pre provides the 48V phantom power needed 
from the USB connection, and has two line-in jacks and two XLR 
connectors, a headphone jack and a jack for a stereo microphone.  
Probably more versatile than an iMic, but it may be overkill for your 
needs :-)

It sounds great to me, but I lack the studio monitors and the ears to 
hear any noise, and I haven't noted any latency problems.
