Mark,
I had a similar problem.   Here is what I did to solve the problem. 
I went to the Energy Saver and unclick "put the display to sleep" and 
unclick "Put the display to sleep when the computer is inactive"

This worked for me.  I have been burning DVD at least once a week 
since I learned this.  Hope it works for you
