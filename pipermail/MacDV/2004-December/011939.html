Do you mean Disk Warrior on CD (as opposed to hard drive)? If so, it's
possible that the CD itself is slightly scratched or dirty. Some CD/DVD-ROM
drives are more picky than others and may have problems reading the disk. If
this is the problem, try cleaning the disk and the CD drives in question.
