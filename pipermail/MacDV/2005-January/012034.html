I never had any success using  Toast for copying VIDEO_TS files. 
However, 'Popcorn', which is available from the Roxio site for $49.95 
(US), does a terrific job and makes it easy. It will also compress 
files from double sided DVDs to fit on regular 4.7 gig DVDs, and the 
results are extremely good.
