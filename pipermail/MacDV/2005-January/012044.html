Last time I saw that error in Toast, the DVD had not been ripped (copied) properly. It seems that toast 'looks' at the Video_TS folder and decides if all files are properly there before it burns. It could be that the DVD you are trying to copy is copy protected.

Here's what works most every time for me.

First go get MacTheRipper. <http://www.wormintheapple.gr/macdvd/mtr.html>

Then use MacTheRipper to rip (copy) the DVD to your hard drive.

Then use Toast's 'Make DVD from Video_TS folder' (or whatever it is- don't have Toast nearby) option.

Good luck,
