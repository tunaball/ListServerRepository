I wasn't complaining, just concerned. I too greatly appreciate this list 
and have used it to solve my video-related problems on several occasions.

Most of the time I'm a lurker - and in the process I've learned things 
about digital video that I didn't know or know I needed before I learned 
them.

I suppose a list like this might go a bit quiet as summer gets into full 
swing. I know I spend less time in front of a computer in the summer 
than I do in the winter - but I do live in Alaska where it's cold and 
dark in the winter. Right now we're approaching the longest day of the 
year, and, as such, it really doesn't get dark here. That means I'm 
outside as much as I can be.

I'm just glad to know that if I have a question about a video project,

figured out the answer and will gladly fire it my way.

This list is GREAT!
