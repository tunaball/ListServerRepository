This is a very common occurance when the audio was recorded at 12bit/32khz,
could be your problem.

You set the sample rate of the audio when you recorded it in the camera. Was
it 12 bit/32khz? Or, 16bit/48khz?

Check your camera, or if you have QT Pro, check the properties.

If you did not change your default audio in the camera, then it's most
likely recording at 12bit/32khz. Try converting to 16bit/48hz and then
reimporting the clip. QT Pro is very good, though a little slow, at
converting audio sample rates.
