Removing distortion is almost impossible.
You could try the audio filter eq and try to zero in on the offensive 
frequencies and cut them.
Then you would need to maybe use a compressor limiter to smooth it put 
and regain the gain lost to the eq, but all of that is liable to make 
it sound like crap as much as it is to fix it. Luckily FCP is non 
destructive.
