A lot of OS X Mail users may not be aware that they can make a rule to 
send all mail from a given list to its own folder, thus not clogging 
your regular inbox AT ALL. I divert mail from about 20 lists to 
individual list folders this way. That is how I am able to respond to 
new posts so quickly. I simply look at the total of unread mails next 
to the name of each list folder and when I see the number has gone up, 
I click on that list folder and read the new posts on that topic. Takes 
all the confusion out of lumping all list mail into the inbox.
