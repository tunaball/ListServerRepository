Do you have a FireWire Cable or an Ethernet Crossover Cable? With a 
FireWire Cable, you can simply make the PB HD an external HD to your 
desktop by holding down the T key upon boot sending your PB into FW 
Mode.

Search at Apple Support "FireWire Disk Mode" yields 77 articles.

<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=58583>

If you have a Ethernet Crossover cable, you can share the PB with the 
Tower or vice versa and similar result.

Search at Apple Support "Ethernet Sharing" yields 97 articles.

<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106658>

What OS are you using?

Are you familiar with the Apple.com Support Tab?
