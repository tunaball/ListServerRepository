Hi listas,

Many questions about to copy DVD I've seen.
Here the best solution. You can even copy DVD9 to DVD5 (DVD's bigger 
than 4.7 Gb) including DTS-, subtitles and menu's.

I assume the you people own the original DVD. Then you're allowed to 
make a copy.

Software you need:

DVDBackup 1.3 (to copy encrypted DVD's to your Harddisk)
                                       Doesn't this work then use OSeX

If size is bigger than 4.4 Gb then use DVD2OneX to shrink the DVD. There 
is allmost no loss of quality.
Excuse my bad english.

Now burn with Toast. Don't forget to include a AUDIO_TS folder. Some 
DVDplayers expect it to be there.
