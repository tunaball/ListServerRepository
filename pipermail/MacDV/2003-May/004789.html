Folks,

Virtix has released iBubble for iMovie.  iBubble is a package of 13 iMovie
titles that allow you to place iMovie titles where you want them, useful for
labeling people in a group picture, identifying details in your video,
making words come out of people¹s mouths with Speech Bubbles, or making
thoughts come out of a person¹s head with Thought Bubbles.  iBubble works
with iMovie 2 & 3 and OS 9 & X.

The press release with more details can be found at
http://www.virtix.com/PR/pressrelease030507.asp

The product page with sample images and purchase information can be found at
http://www.virtix.com/imovie/products/iBubbleTitles.asp
