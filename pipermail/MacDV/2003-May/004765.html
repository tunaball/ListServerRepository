>Anyone know a way to rotate video shot with the camera sideways? 
>Its ultimately for the web, so it doesn't matter that rotation would 
>goof up the screen size. Maybe something in QT Pro, or a QT filter?

QT PRo.

Open movie. Open movie properties (cmd-j)
Select Video Track from left pull-down menu.
Select Size from right. Click either rotate
button (the two on the bottom right rotate
CW and CCW respectively).

Save your movie.
