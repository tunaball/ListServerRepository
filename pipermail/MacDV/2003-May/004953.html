Perhaps the disk is fragmented such that the second time you
recorded it, it had to try to write it to the same spot on disk,
perhaps at the same time as complicated things were going on
in your movie that it was trying to display on the screen while
you recorded.

It might be best to use another application to record sound
clips off of cassette, and then import the sound clip from
disk into your movie.

Does anyone have a suggestion for a good, free (or cheap)
audio recording program for OS X?

There was "Amadeus" that sort of worked.  I have used
"Deck LE" under OS 9 (came with my USB audio device).
I haven't yet gotten a decent sound capture program
for OS X.
