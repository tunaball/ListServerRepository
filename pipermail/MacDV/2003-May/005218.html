This works allways:


Copy the DVD to your mac with DVDBackup 1.3 or OSeX if there's some newer protection.
Make it regio-free and disable all protections.\

Now write it back to a DVD-R with DVD2OneX. This program compies a DVD9 to a DVD5 if it's above 4.4 Gb.

If your DVD player can't play PAL (but I doubt it) you must translate the whole movie to NTSC
Erica Sadun wrote a lot about this stuff on this list and the iMovie-list.
