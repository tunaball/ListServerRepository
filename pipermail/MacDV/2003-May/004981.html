> You can buy the MPEG playback codec from apple.

Apple's $20 MPEG-2 playback component will allow MPEG-2 playback from Mac OS
9 or 0S X (but you'll have to pay separately for each OS).  If you're on OS
X, you can also try a free MPEG-2 player like VLC:

http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-macosx.html

VLC will allow simple playback, but none of the other cool things that
QuickTime allows, like converting to a different QuickTime format (codec).
