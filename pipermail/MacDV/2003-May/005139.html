> Norm, thanks for the recipe.  I assume that this same procedure works

> being turntable L/R outputs -> pre-amp -> amp -> iMic?  Any gotchas to

Actually you don't need the preamp in your equation. The company that 
makes the iMic (griffintechnology.com) has a free piece of software 
called "Final Vinyl" that allows iMic owner to plug the turntable 
outputs directly (well, though a RCA to stereo 1/8" adapter) to the 
iMic for capture. No pre-amp needed, works very well!
