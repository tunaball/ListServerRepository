One frequently used avi codec is Indeo Video 5.  There is a Mac 
plug-in for MacOS 9, but NOT for Mac OS X.  You might try booting 
into MacOS 9.  (There IS an Indeo Video 4 plug-in for MacOS X, but it 
won't decode Indeo Video 5.)  Here's the appropriate URL:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article2.html?artnum=60366
