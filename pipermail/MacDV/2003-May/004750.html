I wanted an easy way to burn multiple copies of a DVD video disk I made with
iDVD.

DVD Imager let's you create a burnable disk image from a VIDEO_TS folder on
your hard drive or from a DVD video disk you've created in iDVD or by other
means. You can then use Disk Copy to burn the image. It's a single function
GUI for mkisofs, which is included by permission from the author.
Applescript Studio Source is included.

http://lonestar.utsa.edu/llee/applescript/dvdimager.html
