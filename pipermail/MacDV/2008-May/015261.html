Actually the newest version of iPhoto lets you go past 99, I recently  
burned a 299 photo slide show on a DVD along with a second slide show  
that contained 103 photos.  I also added the full size files in the  
extras part of the DVD so they could print what ever photos they  
wanted to.  I realize that those are a lot of photos for one session  
but these were for a couple that was on the same trip to Europe as us  
and requested photos from me so I thought this was the easiest way to  
present them.
