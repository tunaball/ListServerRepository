Carl,

I have heard that for iMovie it likes firewire better than USB. May or 
may not be true but with my MacBook I just hook up the firewire camera 
to computer and then open iMovie. On the lower left of the viewing 
window is a button that you can click between a pair of scissors and a 
movie camera. Click the movie camera and your control buttons will be 
active. When you rewind and click the play button the movie should play 
in the viewing window with sound. If you want to capture as you view you 
click the import button.
