George,

In iPhoto after you select the photos and create the slideshow, select 
it by clicking in over in the albums pane on the left. When the slide 
show comes up there should be selections at the bottom for settings, 
adjust, and music. When you choose music you will be given the option of 
the standard iPhoto music or you can select your iTunes folder and go to 
you iTunes library to select your own music. I am using iPhoto 6 so I 
don't know if the physical makeup of the iTunes 7 or 8 is different but 
they should have the same ability.
