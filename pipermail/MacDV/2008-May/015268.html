Well. First, I'm assuming what you're doing is legal.

Get the QT file as you've already done. Then you'll need QT Pro.

Open the movie in QT Player, click Apple J (or on Windows, Ctl J).  
Then click on the audio portion, and then click extract. Save the  
resulting file.
