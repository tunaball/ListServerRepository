Hello,

I know you've asked about compressing but I thought you may be interested in 
what I've done.

What I've done is put the projects on video tape. The tapes are inexpensive, 
small, and hold an hour of video each. If a project is over an hour I break it 
down into hour (or fractions of) segments. Another system which is cheaper 
yet is to divide the project into 4ish gig segments and burn them to DVD. I 
don't burn them as a movie, I burn them as a file which unlike a video, is 
editable in iMovie.

Fellow G5er, Joe Scalise
