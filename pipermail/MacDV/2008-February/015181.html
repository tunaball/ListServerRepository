To help in locking the audio to the video, before exporting to tape,  
try extracting audio in iMovie, then exporting to tape.

I think what helps by extracting, the audio is "locked" to to speak,  
at the beginning of each clip in the movie and this helps maintain the  
sync with audio and video throughout the movie.
