> I want to know - does it convert to to a DV stream that iMovie will 
> *not*
> choke on because of length?

What happens is that as iMovie imports the data, it automatically 
splits the data stream into roughly 9 min 30 sec  (~1.9 GB) segments. 
You can then re-assemble the video in its entirety by dragging all the 
"clips" into the timeline in iMovie -no data is lost in segmenting the 
video.

As for getting the file you already have into iMovie, to the best of my 
knowledge you will have to split it into chunks. Quicktime Pro can do 
this, as I'm sure, can many other video editing applications. Someone 
else on the list can probably recommend other apps to split your movie 
into chunks.
