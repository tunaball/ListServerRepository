That bigscreen tv got an HDMI input?

If you have the earlier MBP with the built in DVI port, buy a DVI to  
HDMI cable.

Some new laptops require a dongle (miniDVI to DVI). If you have one of  
those, you'll need the dongle and the DVI-HDMI cable.

Works as a big 2nd monitor. You can choose extended desktop or mirror.

DVI-HDMI cables are easily available online. I have a 6' and 12'.  
Works great.
