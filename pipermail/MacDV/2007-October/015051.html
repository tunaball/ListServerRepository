Just keep in mind that the video recorded on a hard disk will not have as
high quality as miniDV tape, because of the higher level of compression.

> The formats/codecs in the hard drive cameras are supported in iMovie '08.
>
> MPEG StreamClip can convert most formats to use in iMovie 6. Copy and
> Convert (from a hard drive camera) may be faster than Capture (from a tape
> camera). But quality may be degraded.
