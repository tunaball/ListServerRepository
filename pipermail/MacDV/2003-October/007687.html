And here are the system requirements for Toast 6:

The system requirements are:

*	Power Macintosh G3 or later (G4 or higher recommended for faster 
video encoding)
*	Mac OS X v10.2 (Jaguar) or later (installing latest system updates is 
recommended)
*	200 MB of free hard disk space (more for video authoring, ~ 2GB per 
10 minutes of DV)
*	QuickTime 6 or later
*	CD or DVD recorder (optional, if using ToastAnywhere recorder sharing)
*	Recordable CDs or DVDs
