I've read on this list or the iMovie list that SVCD holds about 20 
minutes and VCD holds 1 hour. So I was immediately thinking about 3 CDs 
in place of one VHS tape. Depending on whether the original poster uses 
jewel cases, it seems like a bit of a wash on storage space. You might 
buy spindles of 100 CDs and put them back on the spindle to save room.

Also, you could be surprised one day when the tape is still good and 
the CD is not. I no longer trust media I burn myself, especially DVD. I 
had one go bad after about 2 weeks because it sat beside a window. I'm 
careful to keep my data CDs and DVDs in a file drawer now.
