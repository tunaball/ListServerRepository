On Wednesday, October 15, 2003, at 06:01  AM, Michael Winter wrote:

> <Regarding several posts about PowerBook drives not working in an 
> external enclosure off FireWire power.>
>
> I have to say the problem most likely isn't the enclosure. If the 
> drive takes more power than the FireWire bus can deliver, no enclosure 
> will work without an external power source. These enclosures are just 
> that, an enclosure with a couple plugs'n cables.
>
> To make it work, you really need to know how much power the drive 
> requires and compare that to how much power your computer's FW bus 
> supplies. Keep track of any other devices which may be drawing power 
> from the FW bus as well (i.e. an iPod). I really don't know for sure, 
> but my impression was that some portables didn't supply any power via 
> the FW bus and some supplied only a limited amount.
