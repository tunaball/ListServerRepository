This is a little less than timely, but I have found the Fry's generic 
firewire cases to work just fine. I bought a self powered one for the 
original 2 gig hd from my 233 wallstreet and it works just fine. I had 
my (expensive) maxtor 80 gig go bad and on a whim tried putting it in a 
full sized fw case from Fry's, and it also works just fine.

I too have had trouble in the past with OWC. Sometimes they are helpful 
others not.
