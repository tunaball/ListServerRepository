At 11:18 PM -0700 10/4/03, Carter Tomassi wrote:
>Let's say a friend would loan you his copy of DVD Pro to burn one 
>short DVD. Trouble is the animation is anamophic and needs to 
>auto-start without a menu. Now you know how to get the movie into 
>anamophic shape but will going iMovie to DVD Pro work? Or does FCP 
>have to be involved? Man, this is an expensive career choice!

You don't need FCP.

DVD Studio Pro will do the job, easy easy easy.

In the Outline tab, Select Track 1.
Open the Inspector. Set the track to 16:9.
Set the end-jump to Tracks and Stories > Track > Track 1
(This assumes you want the thing to loop. Otherwise
leave it at not set).
Drag your iMovie reference movie from the Finder onto
Track 1 in the Outline tab.
In the Outline tab, select Disc.
Set the First Play to Track 1.

Burn.
