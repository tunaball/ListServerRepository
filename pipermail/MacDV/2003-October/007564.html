In the most current version of imovie you can open your iTunes playlist 
and use any of them in iMovie.  I have used plenty of the songs from 
ITMS and they all work fine.  You can use iTunes to convert the files 
from ACC to MP3 but there is really no need as the ACC works fine and 
plays better at that bit rate.
