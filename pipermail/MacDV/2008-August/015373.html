Most older Sony Camcorders had the ability to act as a digital  
converter, it seems a lot of newer cameras have omitted that feature  
but you can get around it by recording analog input to tape.   
Apparently a lot of European cameras have the feature disabled because  
of a taxation  issue that would have a camera that can record from a  
analog input be taxed as a VCR with the money intended to go to the  
movie industry and help with piracy losses.
