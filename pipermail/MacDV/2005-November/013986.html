Tom,
Do you have any other video input application than iMovie?
If  you have FCP for instance, it will import from hardware that  
iMovie will not, using the "Non Controllable device" setting. I use  
this to import video from a DVD recorder.
There are quite a few applications available that are less stringent  
than either of these. Do a search on MacUdate or Version Tracker for  
a fire wire webcam capture application, this may work.

Other than this, if you have a video camera, you might try just  
taking a video of the screen output of the PSP.
