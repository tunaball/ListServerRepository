I agree, EXCEPT, I have a Canon ZR90 I got as a gift.
The two features I wish I could have told the gift giver about: an 
external mike jack, and a way to remove the mike to mount on an 
isolation mount.
Mine is locked to the body.

scenes, and do voice overs, but that is not always so easy, as some 
actors just cannot get the synching right, or do some sound editing in 
post production.
