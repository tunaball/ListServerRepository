Hi Brett,

For me, I would make all footage 4:3 outside of DVDSP as much as 
possible with FCX. Compose, edit and letterbox the footage in FCX, 
output, render in Compressor, drop into DVDSP and away you go. You 
won't even have to touch the track settings.

Some DVD players, if you use 16:9 and then ask DVDSP to LBX it for 
you, has a funny wavy light behind the footage, where the black bars 
are supposed to be and it's weird. That's what happened to me, and i 
had to figure out how to LBX all widescreen footage outside of DVDSP 
first.
