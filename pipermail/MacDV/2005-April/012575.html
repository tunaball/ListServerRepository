I think I found a weird workaround for the iMovie HD Titles bug where 
interlacing is distorted:

To see the bug launch iMovie HD and add a Title _before_ adding a 
Transition or an Effect: interlacing is distorted in the underlying 
video. After this also the Transitions and Effects distort 
interlacing until iMovie HD is re-launched.

Workaround: Launch iMovie HD and add a dummy Transition or Effect 
before adding a Title. You must do this every time after you launch 
iMovie HD if you want to add a Title.

<http://www.sjoki.uta.fi/~shmhav/iMovie_HD_bugs.html#titles_distort>

How's that for a workaround??!! Can you confirm this?
