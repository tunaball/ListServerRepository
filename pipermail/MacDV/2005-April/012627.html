Beware of some of those Brooklyn photo dealers. I received a follow up e-mail
after an order requesting me to call their 800 number to "expedite" my order.
I called and found out the only purpose of the call was to have a 
salesman pressure me to buy outrageously priced accessories for the 
camera that I had ordered. The camera was priced suspiciously low and 
was backordered for 8 weeks
which looks like a bait and switch scam. From now on I will deal with 
reputable dealers like B&H and others with good ratings.
