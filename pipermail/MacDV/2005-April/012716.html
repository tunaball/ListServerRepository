Hi Daniel,

Yeah, they work fine. I've got 40gb and 100gb 2.5 inch drives that I 
edit with (FCP 3 and Avid XpressPro) that are bus powered. You might get 
a few more hiccups than AC powered drives, but they're great for editing 
on the run. When we go out on multi-day locations, I often take a PB and 
little drive with me and get some editing done in the evenings. You can 
get them locally here, unless you really like OWC for some reason.

Tim Selander
