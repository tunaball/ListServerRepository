I'll pipe up.

Since I've been actively burning DVD's to archive a bunch of videos 
from the basement, I've used Fry's "GC" 8X brand DVD-R's as well as 
TDK's 8X's. They both work well. The TDK's were on sale at Target 
(still are) and CompUSA for about $9 per 20/25. The "GC"s even cheaper.

Can't comment on compatibility as I don't actually have a dedicated DVD 
player. I've no idea how fast they're burning and it remains to be seen 
how well they hold up.

If you haven't already, videohelp.com is a good site to check . . .
