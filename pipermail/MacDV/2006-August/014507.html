Neville,
You can export as a compressed Quicktime movie that can be uploaded  
and streamed from another server other than .Mac.  Go to Export under  
the file menu and then export it as a Quicktime.  You just need to  
have your web host capable of streaming (or linking to steaming)  
video.  My website (www.scubavisions.com) is hosted by Network  
Associates, but the video files are all on my .Mac account (go to now  
playing).  But I could be hosting them off Streamload or off my own  
server, if I wanted.
