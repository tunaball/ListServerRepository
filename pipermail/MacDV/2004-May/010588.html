>Hi,
>Just a quick one for you. If I burn a DVD in iDVD4 will it play on 
>my families DVD players in the USA, and also on players in the UK ?
>Thanks for your time.
>Neil

Assuming you're working with US DV (NTSC), it will play on
about 90% of recent US players and about 40% of recent
European players, many of which now support NTSC playback.

However, no, you're not going to produce a PAL disc, which
is the native format of the UK.
