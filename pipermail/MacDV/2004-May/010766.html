You need to convert them to .wmv files. I use Cleaner 6 and it works quite well.
Try to save the files as small as possible as they can get quite large. I've
also heard that .mpg is cross platform too but I'm not sure if it's mpg 2 or 3
or what. mp3s and .wav files are also cross platform. I've had no problems with
them on a PC or Mac. (I use osx)

If you don't have Cleaner I'm not sure what you're options are.  The PCs we use
only have QT 2 (!) on them if you can believe that so they won't play anything
that's not .wmv I'm assuming a properly configured PC with the latest version
of QT would be more compatible.

Hope this helps
