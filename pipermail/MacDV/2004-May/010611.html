> When I exported my 11 minute film to QT at highest settings the end

I certainly agree that QT is not a low quality format. The quality 
depends on the codec used, and the settings used. I'm not sure what you 
mean by highest settings. I found that iMovie's normal high quality 
settings were still awful by my standards. I've exported from iMovie to 
QT using the Sorensen codec, (Best, 29.97 fps, key frame 24, 500 
KBytes/sec) and gotten pretty good results. That's not to say they're as 
good as DIVX. Did a few experiments with what came along with iMovie, 
and stuck with that. Now I'll want to go look at DIVX :)

Good luck, and thanks for sharing your results...
