Nikon tells me it's their fault. They are not supporting any SCSI devices under
OSX. Perhaps there is some undocumented workaround but for now we'll have to
use OS9. For a big profitable company like Nikon I'm disappointed in their lack
of Mac support. How hard would it have been to write a proper SCSI driver
instead of making us either boot into OS9 or buy a Firewire scanner. When the
time comes to buy another scanner I'll be sure not to choose Nikon.
