If the turntable has a pre-amplifier or uses a crystal cartridge the 
quality should  be about the same. A magnetic cartridge has to have a 
pre-amplifier with a proper tone curve for the magnetic cartridge 
puts out almost no bass without the pre-amplifier. One of the other 
answers listed a URL which explains the need for a pre-amplifier.  Ed
