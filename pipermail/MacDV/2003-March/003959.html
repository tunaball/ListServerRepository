> Can someone suggest a good CD/DVD label making program for the Mac?

Define what you mean by "good." :)

Discus is okay, particularly for people switching from PC who like that 
"look and feel." It's actually a pretty good program but geez it's as 
un-Mac-like as OpenOffice.

A friend of mine likes Click-N-Design, a $20 commercial program, and 
from watching him work with it I'd have to say it gets my vote as the 
best "normal person" label maker out there.

Graphic types don't need a label program, they make the templates in 
Adobe Illustrator or Photoshop and work with it from there. Neato 
actually provides a set of these for several Mac programs (the two 
above plus Quark and Appleworks) free of charge on their website.
