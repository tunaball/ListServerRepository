>
> First export the iMovie with chapters to iDVD.
>
> Then open each of the other two iMovie projects and export them to 
> QuickTime files for iDVD (not directly to iDVD). Actually, I think you 
> can skip the export step and just use the .mov reference files that 
> iMovie 3 automatically creates in the movie project folders.
>
> Then open your iDVD project and drag those two files to the 
> appropriate menu screen.
>
> I recently created a similar DVD this way (one movie with chapters and 
> a second without on the same DVD). These are the steps I followed, 
> IIRC.
