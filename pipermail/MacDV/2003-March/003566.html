if you have to go about the "2 disc" routine, how about put the 1st half on one disc and the 2nd half on the other.  

That is what I have done with highschool football games using iDVD2.  And now with iDVD3, I can place chapters after each series of plays so the coaches can jump to any particular offense or defense series.

Since we usually have a 6 or 8 game season, it is not too much work to do after each game.

I've made this recommendation to the coaches of one of our local University Football Teams in Toronto.
