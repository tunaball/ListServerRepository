Thanks for everyone's attempt to help, but I ended up stumbling on a 
very simple way to open Premiere or Pictureview exported still files 
into photoshop without having to do File>Open>and manually click on 
each image while holding down shift.  It may also work for stills 
exported from Final Cut Pro.

What I did in OS 9.04 and Photoshop 6. and Premiere 5.1C, is to make a 
photoshop alias on the desktop.  Then open stills folder and select 
however many Premiere exported still images I want to open at once, 
drag the selected images over on top of the photoshop alias and they 
all open in photoshop.
