The primary visual effects house for both the original "Dune" 
miniseries and "Children of Dune" is Area 51.  They've done effects for 
Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Angel, the HBO miniseries "From the Earth to 
the Moon", The X-Files.  They use Lightwave, but I have not been able 
to discover on what platform.  Most shops use Lightwave on NT boxes 
because you can find very high-end OpenGL cards for these boxes.
As a side note, all of the footage for Children of Dune was shot on 
high-definition digital video.
