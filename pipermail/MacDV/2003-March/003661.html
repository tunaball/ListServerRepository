It might be some sort of conflict or interference from some other 
process. Have you tried logging in while holding the shift key down 
to keep most third-party login processes from loading? Do that and 
try the export to see if it makes a difference.

If you are running pre-OS X, disable any unnecessary startup items 
and extensions, and then restart.
