I have done similar things by hooking a DV camera to the DVD player and 
recording the selection.  I then imported it into the new project and 
worked with it from there.  The quality was good and it was an easy way 
of accomplishing this if not the ideal way to do it.  Working with the 
compressed video will force you to compromise some of the quality no 
matter what solution you choose and I chose this solution.
