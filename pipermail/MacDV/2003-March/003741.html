If you plan to get a digital camcorder, get one that has the 
"pass-through" function. This allows the camcorder itself to act as a 
digital bridge (analog-to-digital converter).

You would connect the output of your VHS deck to the camcorder's 
analog inputs, and use FireWire to connect the camcorder to your Mac. 
Then just play the VHS tape "through" the camcorder to capture it on 
your computer.

Most (but not all, IIRC) Sony Digital8 camcorders can do this. Many 
(most?) Canon digital camcorders (like my ZR-40 miniDV) can do it. My 
ZR-40 can also convert the other direction (digital-to-analog).
