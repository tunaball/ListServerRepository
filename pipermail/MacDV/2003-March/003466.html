I've had good luck with Extractor ( http://denisx.dyndns.org/extractor/ 
) to get the audio out.  You don't really have to do anything to set it 
up or use it, and its pretty fast. I have heard that it simply won't 
run on some systems though.

To get the video out, export from QT as motion JPEG A at 720x480 29.9 
fps, then drop the result on iMovie. You can just drop the MPEG 
directly on iMove, but the extra step seems to improve the quality a 
little bit.  To get the audio out, extract an MP2 stream from 
extractor, then convert it to MP3 with iTunes, and then import into 
iMovie. Viola - movie with sound.
