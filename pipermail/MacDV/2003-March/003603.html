> You should use iMovie's "Create iDVD Project" button ONLY when you 
> want to
> create a new iDVD project.
> To add movies to an existing iDVD project, use the movie browser in 
> iDVD --
> simply drag and drop the movies into your DVD.
> If you're storing your movies in a folder other than Movies, use iDVD's
> preferences command to tell the program where to look for your movies.
