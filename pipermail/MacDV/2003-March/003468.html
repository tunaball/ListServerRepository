After talking with Apple and reading some web sites, I found out that 
"option-clicking" (or "shift-clicking", or "command-clicking") can 
sporadically go away under OS X.  Rebooting fixes the problem.  I found 
that all the expected third party plugins, including Virtix Cinematic, 
worked after the reboot.

My ability to option-click went away went the moment I installed iMovie 
3.0.2.  This might be coincidence or not.  However, I would recommend 
rebooting after installing iMovie 3.0.2, just to be sure.
