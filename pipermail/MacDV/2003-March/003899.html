Further to Dwayne's query and Daniel's response :-

Another way to deal with this issue is to go to your "Home" window on 
your Hard Drive and open "Documents" and subsequently, "Movies" folders 
where your iDVD  and iMovie projects are respectively stored.   Just 
drag the projects to the Trash.
