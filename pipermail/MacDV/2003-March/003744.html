As I understand your question, I do this all the time. I gather 
together a number of music video MPEG-1 files I've downloaded off 
usenet, then I use ffmpeg X to convert them to VCD-compliant MPEG files 
(assuming they weren't already) and finally I use Toast Titanium to 
burn these files to VCD format. Seems to work fine.

If you are asking if you can put a few movies on, burn a disc, then add 
a few more movies and burn again, I think the answer is no.
