> Mac G4 with Superdrive

If you already have the Superdrive iMac, then all you need is a way to 
convert your (presumably analog/VHS) tapes into digital format.

There are several tools to help you do that, but all of them cost 
roughly $250-300 and up. The only way I know of to get around this is 
to find a digital video camera with a feature called "analog 
pass-through" and either buy it (for use in the future) or borrow it 
from someone.
