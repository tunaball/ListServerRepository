The player will play them sequentially. Assuming that you have Toast 5
Titanium and are using OS X, I would suggest that you give VCD Builder
(donationware by Johan Lindstrom) a try. It will let you build a menu
structure to your VCDs so that you can use the DVD player's remote
control to move from place to place on the disc.
