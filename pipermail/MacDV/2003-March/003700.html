After the action is created, including the open and save steps as part 
of the action, pull down the File menu and mouse over the Automate 
command.  Choose Batch from the Automate sub-menu.  In the dialog box 
that opens, click on the checkboxes that override the Open and Save 
steps of your action and choose the source and destination folders here. 
  The actions feature works very well using the Batch command ...better 
and more reliably than previous versions of PS.
