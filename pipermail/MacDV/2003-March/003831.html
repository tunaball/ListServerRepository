On Wednesday, March 26, 2003, at 07:08 PM, Aaron Kwinter wrote:

> I would like to scan some 8 x 10 photographs (both color and black and 
> white) so that I can put them into an imovie film that I am making. 
> Can someone recommend a high quality, reasonably priced scanner that 
> will enable me to do this. Thanks

Unless you want to do some serious zoom-ins on the photos, you don't 
need a high quality scanner because video isn't a high quality medium. 
You can get really nice results just shooting the photo with a digital 
camera under natural light. If you wanted to reproduce the photo in 
print with 300-600 dpi resolution, then you'd need a good scanner. The 
whole video image is only 720x480.
