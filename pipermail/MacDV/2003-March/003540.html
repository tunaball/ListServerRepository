Thanks for the input on using mpgtxwrap instead of BBDemux. For those
of you that have MPEG movies you want to bring into iMovie with audio
use mpgtxwrap not BBDemux. Both of the programs gave me video that I
could put into iMovie but only mpgtxwrap gave me an audio file that
iTunes could convert to MP3 and be used in iMovie. BBDemux's audio file
could be played in iTunes but not converted.
