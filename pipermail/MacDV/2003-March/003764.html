> photo?? Or, could someone tell me where in the manual to look for

Easy way:

  iMovie 3, using Ken Burns effect. Use QuickTime to import into FCP.

Alternate way:

  Still Life by Granted SW <http://www.grantedsw.com/>

Pro way:

  Adobe After Effects

This subject was covered in great detail recently on this list or one of the
other Mac DV lists. You might search the archives.
