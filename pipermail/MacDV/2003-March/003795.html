> Great DSR-30 DVCam deck

I've had a couple years experience with this deck, and be fore-warned, it is
not completely trouble-free when working with regular mini-DV tapes. I
actually spoke directly with one of the German designers of the deck from
Sony when troubleshooting a couple things, and he said that though it will
work with the mini-DV, it was developed specifically for the 'larger' DV-CAM
format. This unit is older technology (about 5 years).
