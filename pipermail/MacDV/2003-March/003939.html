I just got a new Ti book and it only boots into X.  I can't load the 
FCP3 upgrade because it wants to see the original 1.2.5 file, but I

suggestions?


Hi Danny,

I posted this earlier and have the following solution..

Install your FCP 1.2.5 on a mac with OS9 (I did it on my work)
Put the folder with FCP and the preferences-file with you (On a HD or CD or iPod)

Drop the folder with FCP in the OS9 applications  folder of your new mac
Drop the pref-file in the preferences of OS9 systemfolder

Upgrade

That's it
