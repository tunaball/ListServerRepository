I'd suggest that #2 and #4 will acheive the same quality and also the 
highest quality. I think #2 is the best choice overall as it gives you 
the ability to have a second chance at capture - if for some reason the 
capture fails, you have the tape to try again. With #4 if you miss it, 
it's gone.
