I think you mean pixels, and could you be a little more specific about 
what it is you want to do?

When people say "animation on a Mac," they are generally talking about 
either:

a. GIF animation of the sort you see in little icons, ad banners etc.
b. Flash animation of the sort you see in websites and "internet 
cartoons."
c. CAD or Bryce-based "flyby" landscape animations, etc.
d. Full-blown 3D animation based on wire-frame models, as seen in 
movies and games

Each of those areas has a completely different set of answers for you.
