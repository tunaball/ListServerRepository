Time lapse is built into high end Prosumer DV camcorders. You set the 
interval and the recording time and let 'er rip. No problem. I've been 
doing time lapse with Sony VX-1000 for years. It's also in the DVR-900 
and 950. Takes only a minutes to set up and go. Use it to shoot road 
trips. Have coast to coast time lapse recordings of the interstate 
highways.
