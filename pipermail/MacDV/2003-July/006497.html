On Tuesday, July 8, 2003, at 07:49 AM, Matthew Guemple wrote:

> Bitten in the back side by OS9!
> Well after arduously converting everything to AAC I get a new job 
> where everyone is still on OS9. Does anyone know a relatively simple 
> way to convert from AAC back to MP3?

Change your preferences in iTunes to Import with the MP3 encoder. 
Select the tunes you wish to convert, and select Convert to MP3 from 
the Advanced menu. There will be a quality drop, but not much, 
hopefully.
