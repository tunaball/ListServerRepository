On Thursday, July 10, 2003, at 04:53  AM, Neil Bennett wrote:

> As an example, when I was using iMovie if I had imported a 20 second 
> 'master' clip into the timeline, I could then paste additional clips 
> 'over the top' of this 'master' clip having the option of either using 
> the 'master clips' audio or the incoming clip audio.
>

If you want to use the master clip's audio, just "lock" the audio 
tracks by clicking on the padlock icons at the left end of the timeline 
before you start your video editing. Then you can "replace" part of the 
master clip's video with other video and you will only hear the master 
clip's audio.
