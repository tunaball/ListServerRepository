I'm actually kind of disappointed with Adobe's choice to discontinue 
Premiere for the Mac.  Maybe I'm just sentimental 'cause that's what I 
used to hone my editing chops -- or maybe it's because that leaves one 
less competitor for Apple to contend with to keep up the quality and 
feature set of Final Cut Pro (even though it's kind of in a different 
class than Premiere).

There's also the "mindshare" perception of those less-informed Windoze 
users who see a *MAJOR* player in the software industry dropping 
Premiere development for the Mac (even though it's an app that has not 
advanced much in the past few years -- but the Windoze folk don't know 
that)...

Just some thoughts.

- Mark
