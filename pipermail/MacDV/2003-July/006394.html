>'am wondering about these BNC outputs on the back of the Betamax player?

>Any ideas on the interface?

All you need is a BNC to RCA adapter which you
can get from any pro video supply store. You
should then be able to connect your Beta machine
to the analog in on your Canon via an RCA cable.
It's that simple.
