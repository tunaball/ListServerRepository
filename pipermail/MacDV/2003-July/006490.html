Note that iChat AV is only point to point. i.e., If more than 2 
locations are involved, iChat won't meet your needs.

You must have broadband for video.

The camera does not have to be a DV camera. Any firewire capable camera 
will do. Apple has the iSight (http://www.apple.com/isight/)

 From Apple's site:

Requirements for iChat AV Public Beta

For Audio:

Mac OS X v10.2.5 or later
Any G3 processor
iSight, or any microphone
56K modem connection


For Video:

Mac OS X v10.2.5 or later
G5, G4 or G3 procesor at 600MHz or faster
Video: iSight or any FireWire-based video camera*
Audio: iSight or any microphone (internal or external)
Cable, DSL, or or other broadband Internet connection

*DV camcorders require G5 or G4 processor.
