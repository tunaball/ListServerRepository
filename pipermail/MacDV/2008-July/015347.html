Found a new approach to MPEG Streamclip. I imported that full quality  
MP4 that I made with Handbrake and used MPEG Streamclip to convert it  
to NTSC DV. The quality looks identical to the MP4. And it still  
plays perfectly in Quicktime Player in a widescreen player.

But when I import the NTSC DV version into FCE, it does strange  
things in the Canvas. Screens that are all text are so big you don't  
see all the text. The parts with the widescreen video seem to be more  
widescreen than they really are. I tried changing the easy setup  
between basic and anamorphic, but that didn't seem to make any  
difference.

I guess the next step is to make a few test edits, then export from  
FCE and see how that turns out.

Steve R.
