> I assumed you've compared the properties of the problem sequence with one
> that works (right-click on the sequence in the browser and select Item

> the setup parameters, create a new sequence with that setup, and copy the
> tracks from the problem sequence into the new one. Then try exporting the
> new sequence.
>

> Apple Support. I've gotten a number of useful problem solutions there:
> Apple - Support - Discussions - Final Cut Express and Final Cut Express HD<http://discussions.apple.com/forum.jspa?forumID=936>
