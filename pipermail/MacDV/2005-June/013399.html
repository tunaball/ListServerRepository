On Monday, June 27, 2005, at 12:46PM, tamara buffalo <tmbuff at gmail.com> wrote:

>how can  I use that burnt disk to import it back to iDVD  or iMovie to 
>burn another copy or should I just use mac the ripper to copy on to Ext 
>HD and burn another disk  with popcorn?

You can use Disk Utility to make an image of the DVD and burn it from the Finder.

You can use MTR and copy it to the hard drive and use Toast or Popcorn.

>also how do I save a iMovie or iDvd project to ext HD. 

When creating a new iMovie, you are presented with a Save dialog box. Just save it to the location/HD you want.
