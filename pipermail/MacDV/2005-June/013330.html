I would still argue that MPEG Streamclip is free.  It simply relies on 
QuickTime for MPEG playback -- which *Apple* charges $20 for (not the 
developers of MPEG Streamclip).  If you want to convert MPEG-1 files, 
you can do so for no extra charge since QuickTime can play MPEG-1 files 
with no additional plug-ins.

Keep in mind that this thread was comparing two similar products, one of 
which is free and one of which costs money -- the free app being the 
better of the two, and BOTH of them rely on Apple's MPEG-2 playback 
component, which DOES cost money.

Just bite it and pay the $20 to Apple for REALLY GOOD MPEG-2 playback... 
  But don't buy DVDxDV.
