Look for an option in your mail client to "Display all headers" or  
perhaps to show "Long headers." That's where I got the links I posted  
previously, showing up as
	List-Unsubscribe:  
	<http://listserver.themacintoshguy.com/mailman/listinfo/macdv>,   
<mailto:macdv-request at listserver.themacintoshguy.com? 
subject=unsubscribe>

It's worth visiting the listinfo location - aside from unsubscribing  
you can also turn off mail delivery (useful for when you go on  
vacation, as long as you don't forget to turn it back on when you come  
back), change your address, and check the other lists to which you  
subscribe on the same server. Very neat.
