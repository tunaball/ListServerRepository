Strictly speaking, it can be done, but using the Canopus
isn't doing it. YOu'd have to remove the disk and grab the
files and convert them into usable MPEG. What you *are* doing

a low-end couple-of-years-out-of-date DVD recorder box and
go direct from TiVo/Replay/etc to DVD.
