When you buy the Full FinalCut Pro App in the box with it there should 
be 4 DVD's

Final Cut Pro Install
Soundtrack Loops (about 4Gb of sound loops)
LiveType Data 1
LiveType Data 2 (about 8 Gb of livetype data)

Without the last three DVD's you will have no additional files to use 
with the Livetype or soundtrack programs
