One strategy to try is to be sure that you don't have any unnecessary 
applications running during your export to tape. The best way to 
accomplish this is to log out and then log back in while holding down 
the SHIFT key (start holding down the key just before hitting Return 
after the password, and keep it down until your desktop has appeared 
and disk activity has stopped). This will keep most of your Startup 
Items and other unnecessary applications from starting when you log 
in.
