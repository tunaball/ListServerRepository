Likely, due to the way things are done on the Web,  the bitrate of  
the source video's audio is not 48KHz. DVDs require 48 KHz. You need  
to load the video into Quicktime, EXPORT the movie with SOUND  
SETTINGS indicating 48KHZ audio. That should do it.
