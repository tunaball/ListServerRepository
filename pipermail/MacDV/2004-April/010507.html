One workaround you could try so at least it would be easy to find the 
chapters when you re-import your movie(s) later would be to just add a 
second or two of black at the chapter break points.  Then if you needed 
to re-import it for tweaking, at least the chapter breaks will be easy 
to find.

If you just want to make more *identical* copies of your DVD, just make 
a disc image of the DVD and burn from that.
