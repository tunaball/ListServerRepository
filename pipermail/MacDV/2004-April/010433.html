Be sure you've updated to 4.0.1 -- it fixes quite a few problems relating to
encoding, burning, and multiplexing. Get it through Software Update or at
http://www.apple.com/support/downloads/idvdupdate.html

As for having to reencode everything, go to the Preferences dialog box and
make sure "Delete rendered files on closing a project" is *not* checked.

But note: After updating to 4.0.1, you'll want to delete those rendered
files. See this Apple doc:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=93735

After you do all this, check the Status pane, and don't click Burn until it
reports that all encoding is complete. (So much for multitasking...)

Use high-quality DVD-R blanks.

Don't use your Mac for anything else while the DVD is burning.

Brush your teeth and be kind to puppies.

Good luck!
