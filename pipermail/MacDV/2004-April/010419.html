This information might be useful to someone following on from the 
recent discussion about  LaCie 500GB disks:

I have for the first time  since buying the 500GB disk, tried to burn 
a CD on  a LaCie CD burner ( with Toast 5.2.1.)  I found that on 
plugging in the CD burner, the computer (G4 - OS9.2.2) would un-mount 
everything on the desktop and freeze, or, after starting-up with the 
CD burner plugged in, it would freeze immediately after desktop icons 
appeared for an instant and then disappeared.

I eventually solved the problem by switching off Silver Lining Pro 
Control Panel and the two LaCie extensions which were installed with 
the LaCie Big Disk.  I suspect it was the latter two which were the 
culprit.
Cheers all.
