It sounds like you are running iMovie version 4.x. It does have some 
audio issues when you try and edit the volume. I have noticed when you 
edit volume or "move" the audio track in any direction, the audio will 
play out of sync. What I do, is after I make an edit, I immediately 
save. Prior to saving, the audio is out of sync but after the save, it 
usually is OK. However, there have been times (on large projects) when 
not only do i have to save, but also quit iMovie and then reopen the 
project. Then everything is fine. Did you save your iMovie project 
after your edits BEFORE you created your iDVD? Now, about the photo 
montage. Did you put the photos into iMovie timeline or only in iDVD 
when you creating your motion menu? That could explain why when you 
select the Photo Montage menu button it just sits there. I apologize if 
above info is unclear or assuming too much.
