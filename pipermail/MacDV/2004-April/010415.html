my case. You can tell if the burn completed if your movie appears as 
a DVD icon on the desktop.  You run use the dvd player to check
if the DVD play.  I've always had to force-quit iDVD at this point.
On my iMac, the eject key on the keyboard refuses to work after
one of these hangs, but ejecting from finder does.
