Hi Chris

The power cord has a ring of light around it at the end that plugs into 
your iBook.  When it lights up amber the battery is charging when it 
changes to a green light the battery is fully charged. It should work 
with just a power adapter even if no battery is installed.   A battery 
on laptop should give you 3 years of reasonable service before it needs 
to be replaced.  The life of the battery depends on the number 
charge/discharge cycles, storage temperature and they just deteriorate 
over time.
