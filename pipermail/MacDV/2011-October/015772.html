> Go here for help on choosing bit rates:
> 
> http://www.adobe.com/devnet/flash/apps/flv_bitrate_calculator.html
> 
> In that page is an Adobe Air app that you can run from your desktop to

> 
> The direct link to the Air app on that page is here:
> 
> http://www.videoRx.com/apps/air/BitrateStarterRx.air
> 
> After downloading the .air file, double click and it will install.

> Another thing to keep in mind is your pixel size of the movie. You
> want both dimensions to be evenly divisible by 16 for best quality.
> Your size (1024x576) is, just wanted to mention it if you decide to go
> smaller.
> 
> Theres a chart for that too, here:
> 
> http://www.flashsupport.com/books/fvst/files/tools/video_sizes.html
