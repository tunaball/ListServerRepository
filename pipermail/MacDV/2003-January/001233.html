DVD Navigator 1.0

DVD Navigator allows the creation of chapter catalog files for DVDs 
viewed on the computer. These files can be saved and used to access 
particular parts of a DVD quickly. Chapter names can be entered. 
AppleScripts can be written from DVD Navigator to the DVD Player 
Scripts folder that will allow navigation to chapters straight from the 
DVD Player. Mac OS 10.2 required.

Download - 112 K sit at http://www.digitallyobsessed.com/software.php3
