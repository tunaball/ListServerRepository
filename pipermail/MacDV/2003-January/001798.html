http://www.vcdhelp.com/dvdplayers.php is a complete compatibility list 
web page. Watch the Sunday papers for the Best Buy or Fry's deal and 
then check the compatibility page to make sure it will play the 
different formats you want to play. I bought a Cyber Home CH-DVD 402 
for $39 after a $40 rebate at Best Buy and it works perfectly for Video 
CD as well as DVDs. The great thing about this page is that you can 
check off what you want the player to support, press search and get an 
instant reduction in the list of 2056 players to a handfull.
