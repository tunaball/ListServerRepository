Dear Craig,
What G3 do you have? Beige, Platinum, B&W or iMac?

I had a B&W G3. I spent $255 for a 550 PowerLogix G4 Zif upgrade that 
runs @ 600 MHz and @300 MHz 1MB L2 cache and $180 for a DVR-105 from 
OfficeMax that easily goes inside the B&W. That was a total of $435 for 
the hardware. Add OS X 10.2 Jaguar for about $100 these days and $49 
for iLife featuring iDVD 3 for a total of $584.

A little more than the $400 for the Formac. But a heck of a lot more 
processing power and up to date system and semi-professional multimedia 
production software.

However Craig, if you can get the Formac used for less than $400 from 
Ric, then maybe that's the way to go if the extra money is a problem. 
I'm worried that you won't be able to use any part of iLife without 
Jaguar. So you'll be falling farther behind for a few hundred 
difference.

Ric's offer is excellent for iMac owners who don't have the choice of 
getting a burner inside.
