Congratulations! Go get the Roxio Toast 5.2 updater to get your Toast 
where it should be. Consult your Toast Owner's manual about burning to 
DVDs. 1x DVD-R media is available for as little as $1 each mail order 
from various website resellers. Faster media costs a lot more. Toast 
has an MPEG-2 encoder but the interface is not the iDVD elegant. Next 
week we should see iDVD 3 that will be the one to get. It will be 
bundled with iMovie 3 and iPhoto 2 for $49.95.
