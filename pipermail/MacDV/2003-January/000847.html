The OEM that Apple uses is Pioneer. iDVD WILL recognize a self-installed,
internal Pioneer 103, 104, or 105. I have an A03 (103) in my 466 G4, and
have burned many successful DVDs. The iDVD "upgrade" can be ordered directly
from Apple for $19.95. It is a full install. I'm now running 2.1 with OSX
10.2.3.
