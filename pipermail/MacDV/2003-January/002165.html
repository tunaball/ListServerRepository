Did you check all of your jumpers?  The drive at the end of the chain 
should be set to "Master" and the one in the middle of the chain should 
be set to "slave".  Using "cable select" (usually the default) with 
drives from different manufacturers may cause problems? (although it 
shouldn't)  I've never used Western Digital specifically, but I've 
never had a problem with mixed manufacturer drives in the same 
computer, or even on the same IDE bus, and I always manually set the 
drives to "Master" or "Slave" mode, depending on where it's installed 
on the ATA/IDE chain.
