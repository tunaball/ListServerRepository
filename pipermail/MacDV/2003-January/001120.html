Under FILE use "Print to Video." You will get a dialog of settings. Set 
whatever you like (or nothing), click OK, and your screen will turn 
black. Shortly, a dialog will appear which tells you to prep the 
recorder, and, then, just go... This is an excellent feature for making 
loop tapes for trade shows, as you can repeat as many times as you 
like. You can easily make DV and VHS same time.
