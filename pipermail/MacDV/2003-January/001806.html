You'd need to use DVD Studio Pro ($$$) on the Mac to create
a short DVD image. Alternatively, if you have a PC, you
could use one of the many sub-$50 programs to author
your miniDVD (cDVD).

The main difference between a DVD and a miniDVD is
the media upon which it is burned.

As far as I know, iDVD will not burn to a CD blank.
