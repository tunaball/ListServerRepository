> If you are experience sluggish performance, you may find it helpful to 
> open up Terminal and run the Unix maintenance tasks.  It's KBase 
> article # 107388 but here is the summary below:
>
> 1. Open Terminal (/Applications/Utilities).
> 2. Type: sudo sh /etc/daily
> Note: Typing "daily" runs tasks normally scheduled for a daily 
> interval. Type "monthly" or "weekly" in place of "daily" to runs tasks 
> scheduled for those intervals.
> 3. Press Return.
> 4. Enter your Admin password when prompted, then press Return.
> 5. Quit Terminal when the task is complete. Monthly tasks usually 
> require a longer time to run than weekly or daily.
>
