>Does anyone have any experience doing this and can tell me how long it takes
>iMovie to render a 30 minute QT Movie, import it in to iDVD and then burn a

I haven't used iDVD on anything below 733MHz, but I've used DVD 
Studio Pro to do MPEG-2 encoding on a G4/450, and it takes about 3x 
the length of the QT DV file to encode. I find that pretty tolerable. 
I think iDVD would work fine with your system.

BTW, MPEG-1 took about twice as long as MPEG-2, since it is much 
higher compression.
