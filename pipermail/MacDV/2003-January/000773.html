> Thanks for the heads up - I just picked one of these up from the local 
> Office Max in Santa Cruz. A couple of questions, if I may:
>
> 1) Did you replace your existing CD/DVD drive in your G4 Mac with the 
> new drive ? If so, how did you get the old one out ? I have a DVD-RAM 
> drive in there at the moment and I just can't seem to work out how to 
> extract it. Do you need to take off the front bezel somehow ?

Yes you have to take off the front plastic panel.  There is a screw 
holding the drive from the front.  To remove the plastic bezel, it 
helps to remove the upper and lower handles.  Then the bezel will pop 
out (with a little effort).  Remove the screw and the drive should 
slide out.
>
> 2) How do you get iDVD ? Does Software Update autmatically detect that 
> you now have a SuperDrive installed and download it, or do you have to 
> buy it from Apple, or what ?

You have to purchase iDVD from the apple website.  It is not available 
as a download, the CD is $19.95, which they will ship.  Make sure you 
have you new drive installed and it is working before you install iDVD. 
  I installed iDVD first (O.K. I wasn't thinking.......)  and then the 
drive.   This really screwed everything up.  I wound up having to 
reinstall my system.
