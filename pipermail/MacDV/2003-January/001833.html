In reading about the new desktop G4's, I read Apple's warning about the need
to do a firmware update on the Superdrives. I researched the Pioneer web
site and saw the update notice for 103 and 104 drives. Just to be sure, I
emailed Pioneer re the 105 and their customer support confirmed no update
required.

"Thank you for contacting Pioneer Electronics, Inc.
Yes, the DVR-105 is up to date. It can be used with the high-speed media. "

Those with the 103/104 should download and install the update. No charge.
