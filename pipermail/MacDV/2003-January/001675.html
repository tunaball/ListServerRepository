>Hello:
>
>Can you copy a DVD made in DVDSP by using Toast 5.1.4?  I would 
>think you can just copy the data files as is from one burned disk to 
>the copy, but I wonder if anyone has experience with this?

Several ways. Here are two.

Easiest: Put in the disk. Choose "Copy" from Toast & go.

Redoable: Create a disk image of the appropriate size using
diskcopy, copy to that image and then burn with toast. This
lets you reburn without recreating the image.
