This is a bug in iMovie that is easily solved.  Double click on the 
audio track that is fading in/out and select both Fade In and Fade Out 
but slide them to zero on both and your audio will no longer fade.

This also happens occasionally  if you select a fade in or out on an 
audio track and then deselect it.
