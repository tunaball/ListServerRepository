proclaims it REQUIRES Jaguar, yet as we have all discovered truly does run
in OS9 just fine? A little "friendly persuasion" to finally buy one of the

Who asked Apple to sit on the fence? What does that have to do with the
legitimate complaint that the information released to the public is not 100%

OSX is the wave of the future. We'll all be there eventually. No one is
faulting Apple for being brilliant, creative, and always on the cutting
edge. However, if you're going to make a display that is OSX only, then make
it so. And if you're going to make one that runs on both systems don't lie
about it and lead people to believe they can't have one unless they run OSX!

Yes - that's a wee bit shady.
