Intentionally or not, OfficeMax is MISLABELING their Cendyne DVD-R 
drives.  I was in OfficeMax Friday, and noticed that their shelf 
label advertises the older DVR-104 model as a 4x drive!!

They have BOTH 104 and 105 drives on the shelf, but the $50 in-store 
discount ONLY applies to the 104.  My guess is that the above price 
will only apply to the 104. (adding Cendyne $50 rebate nets you to 
$179.99)

So don't bee fooled by their shelf labeling.  Before you buy, make 
sure of the actual model by looking at the box.  The specification on 
the back of the box clearly shows the model number at the bottom of 
the list:  DVR-104 or DVR-105.  The 104 box has blue graphics, the 
105 has gold.
