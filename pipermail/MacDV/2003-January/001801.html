Mini-DVD is simply burning a (small, 10-15 min) DVD disk image to a 
CD-R.  It will only work in a computer that has a DVD player.

If you want to do this, you'll need a DVD authoring program such as DVD 
studio pro.

For ease of use/convenience, iDVD with DVD-R is probably closer to what 
you want.
