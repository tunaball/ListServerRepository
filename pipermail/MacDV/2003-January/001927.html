Okay, here's a viewpoint from a "lower-end" Canon user. I got a ZR25 for
Christmas 2 years ago and have been pretty satisfied with it. For basic
"home movie" type shooting (i.e. holidays, kids' sports events, vacations,
special occasions, etc.) it's done a pretty good job. I've never had any
capture issues with it (500mhz iMac G3 DV SE & iMovie 2.0 --> 2.1.1). I love
the size of it. Recently spent 2 weeks in Orlando and often had it in one
hand, and my Olympus C720 still camera in the other... yes, a double-fisted
shooter!

The image stabilizer feature seems to work fairly well. At least I've had no
complaints yet.  For the price range, Canon's single-CCD camcorders seem
like a good value for many consumer-level shooters.

On the other hand, I have no cause to disparage Sony products. I've had a
Sony TV for a couple years now, and have been extremely pleased with it. I
would definitely consider Sony electronics when looking to purchase again.
