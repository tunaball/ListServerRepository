iDVD 3 in the iLife box is a full install. You can no longer order iDVD 
2.1. Those who did just before MacWorld Expo SF 2003 were converted to 
iLife orders for no extra charge. I know someone who had that 
experience.

Yes the Apple site, maybe the Apple stores, was the only place to buy 
iDVD 2.1. Now that iLife is shipping in a week, you have to pay the $49 
to get iDVD 3 with the rest of the suite. It comes on 2 DVDs. The iDVD

iPhoto 2, and iMovie 3, are a 250 MB install.
