I just was looking at the iLife sale page and it says that the minimum 
is G3 or G4 processor and 10.1.5. So I guess Apple is acknowledging 
that G3 is OK as long as you get a DVD-R drive inside one of the old 
towers. Here's the entire copy:

System Requirements

Macintosh computer with PowerPC G3 or G4 processor.
256MB of physical RAM.
Mac OS X v10.1.5 or later (Mac OS X v10.2.2 or later recommended).
2GB disk space required to install iTunes, iPhoto, iMovie, and iDVD 
(Apple SuperDrive required for iDVD) or 250MB disk space to install 
iTunes, iPhoto, and iMovie only.
