> For now, I'm very pro-miniDV for archival storage.

I've thought about this a hundred different ways, and there is no good 
way, but miniDV tape seems to be the best way. Right now, I'm saving 
most of my stuff as DVDs.  There are multiple copies floating around, 
so I figure when somebody notices deterioration, I'll dupe another

then we could burn the projects and restore/re-edit them later. That's 
my dream solution. To do that now, you'd have to shell out a thousand 
or two for an enterprise tape backup system.
