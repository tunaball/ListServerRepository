> Problems like this have been fixed in the past (for me anyway) by

> Toast Titanium 5.2
> iMovie 2.1.2
> QuickTime 6.1
> Mac OS X 10.2.3

> instead. See <http://www.roxio.com/en/interest/video/vidcapmac.jhtml>
