The new Panasonic AG-DVX100 24P DV Cinema Camera, the Sony PD-150, and 
the Canon XL1 series, which are "prosumer" cameras, all vastly 
outperform their consumer brethren.  All are 3 CCD, and the quality 
derived is far superior to any consumer single chip camera. I would not 
consider shooting a commercially oriented production without one of 
these cameras.
