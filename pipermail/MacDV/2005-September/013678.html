  Dennis,
Try extracting the whole sound track and exporting it to QuickTime.  
Then open the sound track in QuickTime and converting it to .aiff,  
16bit, 48khz.
Both iDVD and iMovie work best if all sound is in this format.
After you have done the conversion in QT then re-import it into iMovie.
