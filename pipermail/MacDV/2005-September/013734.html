way of knowing what the story behind the original post was.  He's now

trust the format).  How many commercial CD's have you ever had go bad 
that weren't attributable to someone's carelessness?  The humorous 
part of all of this is that copies of a commercial CD will normally 
go bad before the commercially pressed CD itself.

I won't even comment on the copy protection issue, other than to 
point out that I had said, that maybe this was a problem.
