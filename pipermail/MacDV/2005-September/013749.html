BTW, there's a way to get Snapz Pro to include the Audio. I dont have  
it in front of me now so I'll try to give you an idea of the move:

When it comes time save the screen capture of the video there will be  
options. In the Audio section, uncheck the field that asks if you  
want to include the audio track and then, check it again. This action  
should get your audio included on the capture.

Maybe Ambrosia have done this on purpose to keep anti-piracy people  
happy 'cause they will not let you know about this little work-around.
