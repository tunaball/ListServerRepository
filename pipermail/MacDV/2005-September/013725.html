I don't have a 110. But I recently installed a 109, and previously 
upgraded from a 103 to a 105, so I have done it twice.
  It is not that hard. Doing the screws in order is important. You have 
to be careful of that third HD. And you have to do firmware update.
  And for me,  the biggest warning is not to drop the whole deal on your 
motherboard.
I would go Pioneer unless you are looking at BluRay or some other 
future improvement.
