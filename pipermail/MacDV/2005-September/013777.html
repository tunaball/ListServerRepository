When you copy a DVD disc to your hard disk, you must follow the
instructions on this web page:
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=42724>

There are some special steps required (like selecting DVD/CD Master as
the image format) to produce a proper DVD image that will work
properly when you burn new disc from it.
