On 1/14/07 6:09 PM, "David Abramowitz" <david.abramowitz at verizon.net> wrote:

> Unless I'm missing something, the easiest thing to do is import the
> video into iMovie, make Chapter Markers for the chapters, then choose
> the Share option and pick iDVD as the target. This will open iDVD,
> which will automatically create chapters in the DVD based on the ones
> you made in iMovie. After that it's just a matter of selecting a
> theme in iDVD, and burning it. You'll either choose a theme which
> makes motion menus out of your chapters, or text menus. Having just
> completed converting our entire video library (old VHS tapes, 8mm
> tapes and digital tapes) into DVDs, I am very familiar with this
> process, and iMovie and iDVD make it pretty painless.
> 
> Hope that helps!
> Dave
