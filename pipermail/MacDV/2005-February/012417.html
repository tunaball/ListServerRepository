Here's something else you can try.

You mentioned that you've tried quitting other apps. But this may
leave background processes running.

Log out, and then log back in while holding down the SHIFT key (start
holding it down just before hitting the ENTER key at the end of the
password, and keep holding it until the desktop appears and disk
activity has stopped). This keeps unnecessary startup items form
launching, so your Mac will have just about the minimum amount of
other activity going on while you try to encode and burn your DVD.
