BTW Dorothy,

Do you have Photoshop? If you do there is a great feature under the 
File/Automate menus called "Web Photo Gallery". It helps you to easily 
build a scrollable photo gallery of any folder of images you throw at it. 
You can choose from a few different page layouts and what size your final 
images will be at.

Once the process has completed you'll have a folder containing all of the 
thumbnails, full size images (that you specified the size for) and the 
relevant html documents to make it all happen. Then just upload this to 
your website and load the main html page (I think it produces an 
index.html file) from your web directory.

I used it under PS7 to create a quick family album on the web and it 
worked a treat.
