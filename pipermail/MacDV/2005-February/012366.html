You would edit the video in iMovie. That's its primary purpose.

If you have a built-in DVD burner, iDVD (part of iLife) would be the
natural choice to set up and burn your DVD. iMovie can even start iDVD
for you and send it the edited video automatically.

If you are using an extenal DVD burner, the simplest way to burn your
DVD would be to use Toast. Toast doesn't have the flexibility of iDVD
for setting up menus and such for your DVD, but iDVD is not intended
to work with extenal DVD burners. There is a way to use iDVD from
iLife '05 to create an image of the DVD with the fancy menus, and then
actually do the burn with Toast, but that's a somewhat
more-complicated process.
