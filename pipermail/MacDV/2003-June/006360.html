> The pluses are numerous. The minuses are: only 3 PCI slots (one used)

There are FOUR PCI slots in the G5, with one already in use for the 
video card. That leaves three available slots.

As for the lack of internal drive room, keep in mind that at the MOMENT 
those mere "two slots" can hold a half-terabyte of drive space. Already 
announced are forthcoming serial-ATA drives with larger capacities.

And with FW 400 and 800 plentiful on the machine, external drives 
actually become more practical (if more expensive) than internal ones.

If that's you're biggest minus, I'd have to say Apple's really knocked 
out a home run on this one.
