There is another wrinkle in the academic purchase question.  Apple 
Academic has at least two prices lists, one for personal purchase, 
and one for institutional buying.  I am guessing that most people on 
this list are buying for themselves, so the pricing discussed above 
matches what I have seen.  For anyone buying for their school, FCP 4 
academic (but full version, not an upgrade) is $299, as of 6/18/03. 
This price has been stable for at least three weeks.
