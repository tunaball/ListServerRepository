I used to convert NTSC to PAL and vice versa when exchanging video 
with family overseas using my good ol' 8500. There is a shareware app 
called Matt's Hack TV 2 that allows you to input one and output the 
other in real time, using the built-in AV module of the 8500. It also 
worked with the Capsure PC card (input) and S-video port (output) of 
my PowerBook G3.

You can find 8500's for $50 or less on eBay, and Hack TV 2 is $10 
shareware. IIRC, it should also work with 8100s, Quadra AVs, or any 
other AV Mac. I never tried it with my ATi XClaim VR 128, but if it 
worked with the Capsure, I would guess it would work with other 3rd 
party cards. It doesn't need a lot of horsepower, since it just 
passes video through.

Of course, you also need a PAL deck. I had one at work, and also used 
my uncle's PAL 8mm camcorder when he was visiting us.
