For what it's worth, I've owned 3 Western Digital Firewire drives 
(pre-Oxford 911) that had their firewire bridge die shortly after the 
warranty ran out. The drives inside (5400 rpm IDEs) still work fine - 
one's currently in my machine internally and the others are in WiebeTech 
Desktop GB firewire/USB enclosures.

Because of these drives I don't recommend Western Digital.

just my .02...
