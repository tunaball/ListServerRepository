I don't recommend reencoding M2V files.

The key to longer video discs is a lower bitrate. I've been
playing with iDVD to see if I can trick it into doing a lower
rate, but it's not immediately obvious how to do so.

If you're going to use FFmpegX (or whatever), offer it the
original video files, not the stuff that iDVD produces.

And, why not just use a normal encoder like TMPGEnc on the PC
instead?
