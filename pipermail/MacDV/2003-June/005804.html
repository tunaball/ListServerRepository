At 12:23 PM -1000 6/8/03, Gary Fujihara wrote:
>Okay, pardon my ignorance...and thank you for the info.  Now, after I obtain
>a cable, and connect to my Mac, I assume that the FW device (camera/deck)
>will appear as a volume on the desktop?  How do I then initiate video
>transfer from device to Mac?  Is there some software I need?

Nope, it doesn't appear as a device.

Get a 4-6 FW cable (4 pin one end, 6 on the other). Connect 4
to your camera 6 to the computer.

Power on. Put in "play" mode (with a recorded DV tape in
there)

Launch iMovie. Use the control keys there to capture, whatever.

Sorry, rushed, -- Erica
