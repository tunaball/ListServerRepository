about the Sony consumer camcorders:

First, I see the TRV22 has a light rating of 5 lux, but the TRV33 (a

expensive camera have a poorer light rating?  (I realized the TRV33

performance, while the camcorder captures sharp and clear images." 
What the heck does that mean?  Is it progressive scan, or no?  It 
seems to be the system for all the Sony MiniDV camcorders, so I'm
