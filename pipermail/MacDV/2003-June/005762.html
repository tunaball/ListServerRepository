On Sun, 8 Jun 2003 11:10:33 +0100, Neil Bennett <ibenz at mac.com> wrote:
>I intend to use some music album artwork in conjunction with iTunes 4
>and iMovie 3, but I can't find the file where iTunes stores the artwork
>that you import and then appears in the bottom left hand corner of
>iTunes.

You can import artwork for a song in iTunes 4 by selecting the song, 
selecting File->Get Info (CMD-I), selecting the Artwork tab, and 
clicking the Add... button.

You can export artwork from iTunes 4 by selecting the song, selecting 
File->Get Info (CMD-I), selecting the Artwork tab, clicking on the 
image to select it, and then selecting Edit->Copy (CMD-C). You can 
then paste the image into any program that will accept it.
