On Fri, 27 Jun 2003 11:24:32 -0400, Matthew Guemple <mo.og at verizon.net> wrote:
>does anyone know where iDVD3 stores it's themes? I seem to be missing
>some, or they are not loading...

I found the themes inside the iDVD package:
/Applications/iDVD 3/iDVD.app/Contents/Resources/

You'll see the .theme files there.

It turns out that the .theme files are themselves packages. When you 
show their contents, open their Resources folders and you will find 
the music files, object graphics, etc.
