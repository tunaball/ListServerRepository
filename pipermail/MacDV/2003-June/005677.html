I had an earlier posting about DVDs that I ordered from shop4tech and 
was worried about the time it took to arrive.  They arrived today, they 
were ordered May 5th so one month to arrive, credit card was charged on 
the 6th the shipment date on the package May 14th even though the site 
claimed 24 hour availability.  The disks arrived in a well packaged box 
( 2 cake boxes of 50 disks) with sufficient bubble wrap to protect 
everything so I have no complaint there and the price was $0.89 so that 
is great.  I just finished burning a test DVD and that worked smoothly 
and it played fine on my set top player.  The disks are manufactured by 
Leda Multimedia which are disks I had previously used with no trouble 
so other than the slow shipping and fast charging to my credit card I 
am quite happy with them and look forward to burning the other 99 disks
