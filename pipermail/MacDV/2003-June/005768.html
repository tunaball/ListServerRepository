Besides Freeplaymusic.com  I found the following two sites give a balance
between "affordable" (a relative word!) prices without sacrificing quality.

Www.musicbakery.com  - all round good site  - price vrs. Quality.

Www.britishaudio.com - more affordable than others ONLY when they have a
sale that brings prices down to under $10 a selection  - Regularly
$18-30/selection ... A bit pricey for some people.  Fresh and different
styles that I couldn't find elsewhere.
