I don't know if you'd be interested in this, but I just watched a Dave 
Cross video in which he creates a sketch effect in Photoshop.

1 Basically you duplicate the base layer and desaturate it.

2 Duplicate that and invert it

3 You now have a color layer on the bottom, grayscale in the middle, 
and a grayscale inverse that looks like a negative on top.

4 Change the blending mode on the top layer to color dodge (the image 
will be almost entirely white)

5 With that inverted layer still selected, try a motion blur and set 
the amount for the degree of fillin you want on the sketch.

6 Then he moved a copy of the color layer to the top and set a low 
opacity to slightly color the sketch.
