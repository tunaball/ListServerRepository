> I made a DVD from an old VHS tape.  I later wanted to copy the contents to
> my HD for viewing w/o having to run the DVD player so I tried MacTheRipper
> and it gave the "this disc was made with deliberate bad sectors"

According to the documentation, sometimes the commercial DVD manufacturers 
put deliberate "bad" sectors to fool ripping software and I believe Mac the 
Ripper thinks it is identifying one. I've had this happen a couple of times 
and I think it was due to smudges or scratches. Try inspecting and maybe 
cleaning and try again.
