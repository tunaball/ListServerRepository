If you just use your REPLY function, the subject line should be the
same as the original, likely with a "Re:" in front of it (which
shouldn't affect the threading).

Note the address you are sending to when you reply. In some cases the
reply will pick up the address of the poster whose message you are
replying to, or it may use the address of the e-mail list (probably
what you want). Be prepared to change the address in the reply if you
need to.
