if you have attempting to burn a dvd from a stand alone dvd burner, you MUST 
finalize the disc before it will play on any player, including a computer with 
dvd playback.
most higher end powerbooks come with a built in dvd burner, or I'd recommend 
contacting powerbook1.com, or MCE, who have options to have a dvd burner 
installed. By far, that would be the EASIEST way to burn dvd's since what they 
install is native to os X and i movie, and any other apple software used for 
editing.
I have a 15 inch powerbook with dvd burner installed, having had it installed 
aftermarket by MCE, and I can't say enough GOOD about it!

good luck, and if you have any other questions, feel free to contact me.
