If it's a song you purchased through the iTunes Music Store, you'll need 
to burn it to a CD first, then import as an AIFF.  Make sure the AIFF 
file is the same format as your FCP sequence (16 bit, 48 kHz for DV 
usually).  If it's a non-protected file, just change your import options 
in iTunes to AIFF and convert it that way.
