In Belgium, the cable company wants to go digital next september.  To  
do so, one has to buy a 199 euro Digibox which will give you the  
digital channels and allows you to record the channel YOU'RE WATCHING  
on video.  No other channels!  I december one can buy a Digicorder  
for 299 euro which will make it possible to save 2 channels on disk  
while watching a third channel.  These 2 channels can't be recorded  
on video.  Only watch back.
If one doesn't want neither of these and want to keep the channels  
there are here today, on must buy a 50 euro box, otherwise on will  
loose 8 channels.
The digital age has moved in here.
