Hi Nick,
         I also have a Canon ZR70. I have had good
luck with all the DVD-R's I have used. I usually buy
the ones that are on sale. Verbatim, Fuji, Maxell,
even Comp USA brand. They all have worked just fine
with my SuperDrive. Just pay attention to speed of
disk you get. My SuperDrive burns at 4X speed. Get
disc that are 4X and you'll save time. I have used 1X
disc and they worked fine, just took a lot longer.

Good Luck,

Norm L.
