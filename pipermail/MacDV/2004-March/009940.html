The easiest way I've found to store DVD files for subsequent copies is 
to
create a parent folder for the TS folders, named with all caps and
underscores (like MY_DVD), then put the TS folders in there.  Then when 
I
want to burn it, I just drag the MY_DVD folder into Toast and burn it 
as a
UDF disc (deleting any .DS_Store files that may show up along the way).

Hope that helps.
