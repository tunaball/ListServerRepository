DeMuxing is splitting a qt video into audio and video tracks.  I 
learned about this when I tried repeatedly and unsuccessfully to import 
a short .mpg into an iMovie I was creating.  I learned that after 
demuxing I could import both and have the audio and video rejoined as 
separate (but equal?) tracks.  I use a program called bbDEMUX, which I 
found through a google search.  It was awhile ago, but I think my 
parameters were "mac demux"
