I have an issue with 10.3.3 and Final Cut: when exporting audio as AIFF 
for sweetening, it exports with the occasional glitch on FCP 
transitions, whether at dissolves or cuts (or ramps or whatever.) 
Sounds bad on our M&Ks, with too much work to fix to be practical. The 
workaround is to export as DV audio (which presupposes a project is 
using 48KHz) without picture, which exports the audio perfectly, but is 
still annoying.
