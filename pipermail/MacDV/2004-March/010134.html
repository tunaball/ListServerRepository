On an 800MHz G4 I find it takes 3t to encode and t/5 to burn to DVD.  
For example, one hour of video takes 3 hours to encode and 12 minutes 
to burn.  It surprised me too how long it takes.  Yesterday someone was 
telling me it's the audio encoding that takes the most time.  It's not 
just iDVD--Toast Titanium takes just as long.  I've decided encoding is 
best done overnight.
