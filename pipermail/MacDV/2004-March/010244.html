>  It would be good to be able to use the whole 2 hours on the DVD 
> media. I have 2 hours of video I need to put on one disc.

There really isn't "2 hours" on the media, there's a certain amount of 
space. That space might hold 1 hour, or maybe 4-5 hours, depending on 
the compression used to store the media on the disk. The flexibility to 
use the really high compression levels is only available in DVD studio 
pro, or by using a variety of shareware programs to create your own 
"homebrew" DVDs (which usually have very poor menu features).
