> First, iMovie 3 (and 4) don't make "DVD"s. They create QuickTime
> "reference" files that point to the DV Stream (not DVD) content in the
> clips. It appears, from your description, that you are leaving out the
> step that encodes the DV Stream into the MPEG-2 files a DVD authoring
> and burning program needs as input.
> 
> I would strongly suggest that the easiest solution for you, since you're
> using a G3 with external burner, would be to invest in a copy of Toast 6
> Titanium and use it. Toast includes an encoder that will do the job for
> you.
> 
> Note that encoding to MPEG-2 is not a fast operation, and it will be
> even slower on your G3 hardware. However, Toast will author and burn the
> DVD for you.
