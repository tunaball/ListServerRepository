Astarte is (was) a German company that produced a number of encoders and
decoders. Apple acquired most of their technology a few years ago. Their
product, M.Pack, is the item in which you're interested, but that hasn't
been commercially available since before the release of OS X :)

I don't know of anyone who is still using it. I know that my last copy
was on an old 8500 that I donated to a school when I got my B&W G3 (four
years ago).
