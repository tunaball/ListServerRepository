This can also happen if the video timecode abruptly ends during a 
capture now.    The computer continues to record audio which it 
attempts to sync with the truncated video tc.
Also you could be attempting to run 32 khz audio in a 48 khz 
environment.  There is a preference for 32khz audio.  good luck.
