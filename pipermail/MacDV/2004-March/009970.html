Not sure if you meant you couldn't find the drivers or that you  
couldn't download them once you found the page. I'll assume the first.  
I usually just type a company's name into Google, which is my home  
page.

You'll have to put the pieces of this URL into one long stream and make  
sure there are no extra characters between the pieces. Or go to HP's  
site, click the blue Drivers & Support link in the lower right, enter  
your camera model on the next window. There are two cameras; one has xl  
at the end of the name. I chose the plain one. On the next page there  
are drivers for Mac OS X and Mac OS 9.


http://h20015.www2.hp.com/hub_search/taskPageSelect.jhtml? 
lc=en&pagetype=software&prodId=hpphotosma75944&sw_lang=en&lang=en&cc=us
