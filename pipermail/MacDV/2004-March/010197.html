I would *NOT* recommend connecting your Mac to the PC via FireWire.  
Even if the hardware would allow a connection to be made, your PC 
wouldn't know what to do with a drive formatted in HFS+ (unless you had 
software installed on your PC that allows access to Mac disks).

I *WOULD* recommend getting an ethernet crossover cable (or two cables 
and an ethernet hub or switch) and just turning on Windows File Sharing 
on your Mac -- then just connect from your Windows computer to your 
Mac.  You could probably turn on File Sharing on your PC and connect 
via your Mac too.  That should be the easiest way to connect the two.
