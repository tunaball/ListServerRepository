>I believe your PC DVD-rom is at fault.  I have had similar 
>experiences with 3 or year old PC lap-tops they either don't play 
>them or play them but have poor performance (sound drop outs, jerky 
>video etc.).  Compaq Pllls with Toshiba optical drives have spotty 
>performance with -R media.  Probably the same performance issues 
>would be present on desk-tops that are one or two years older than 
>that.

Media is a factor but bitrate is also a factor.
If you want to play back on computers, make sure
you lower that bitrate a lot. iDVD uses 8 Mbps
for its 1-hour videos, a rate that exceeds the
playback capabilities of many computers.
