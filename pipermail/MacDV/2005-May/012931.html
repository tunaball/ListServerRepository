Several of us are having this problem. There are several threads in the 
www.apple.com "support" "discussions" area but no real solutions. If

it further than I have. Mine is a StorTec external bought from 
DealSonic. I've tried putting a Hitatchi 80gig, Maxtor 160, and WD 250

select, then master, then slave, then back to cable select.  Do you 
possibly have a "dead" firewire port? It's been mentioned on MacFixit

problem but other than that, no one has been able to help me. The 
enclosure could be bad but it was so cheap, it wouldn't have been smart 
to return it, since DealSonic charges re-stocking, etc.
