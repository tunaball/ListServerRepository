Been there, tried that... but it (not getting a fully "clean" record)
doesn't appear to correlate with head cleaning. I'm perfectly willing to
believe that my camera is running under spec. with respect to the energy

small sections of previously recorded ones (these are all Panasonic
DMV60EJ tapes).

Yep, a real pain in the you-know-what :-) Hence my request for good bulk
eraser. There seem to be some really expensive ones around for
professional studios that erase all manner of media but the availability
of smaller (less expensive) units seem to be thin to non-existant. Art
suggested a big-honkin' magnet but I think the tape also needs to be moved
from spool-to-spool while on the magnet so the field actually gets at all
of the tape surface. Just sitting a tightly wound tape on top of a magnet
just randomizes the edges of the tape.
