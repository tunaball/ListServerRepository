On Monday, May 09, 2005, at 02:53PM, <macminialacool at mac.com> wrote:

>I am using Final Cut Express 1.0.1 and am wondering how to improve 
>titles on a wedding vidoe I am working on that I shot with my Canon 
>miniDV ZR-30MC. The text usually is jagged and dithered. Anyway to make 
>it viewable/render in full quality?

Is it jaggy and dithered in FCE, iDVD, on the burned DVD or where?

In my experience, even though the titles look jaggy in iMovie, they go hi res in iDVD.

It FCE is anything like iMovie, you can also export to MJPEG-A codec and it will achieve full quality for you.
