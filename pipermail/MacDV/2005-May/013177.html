I use the MOTU MicroExpress USB MIDI Interface with my G4. They also 
make a smaller bus-powered one, called the FastLane, also USB. It's 
better not to use them with a USB hub, or at least to do a search to 
find the best hub to use if you must. The MicroExpress interface has a 
1/4" jack for Timecode input, if you need that, but the FastLane 
doesn't.
