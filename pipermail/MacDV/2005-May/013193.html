Hi, I've looked at a lot of great ideas here. This one is probably what 
you don't want to do, But I'll put it out there anyway.
Black and Code the tape with Final Cut Pro, Final Cut Express, [and I 
don't know if iMovie can do this.]
I used to use bulk erase magnetizers for audio tapes at work and they 
always left residual noise behind. Black and Coding removes all video 
and sound, and timecodes the tape so you never have to worry about 
blanks in your timecode. Somebody mentioned cleaning your heads. Good 
idea, too.
Frank Fitzpatrick
