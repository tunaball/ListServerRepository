>How exactly does one create a disk image.  Can you share the steps involved, please?

iDVD 5 has this functionality built-in.

iDVD 4 needs a couple files called Hurz/Pfurz in the root of your home folder. If those files are there, you can then control click on your burn icon and a window will come open where you can specify whether you want to burn directly to DVD or to image.

Here are some links explaining it in more detail:
