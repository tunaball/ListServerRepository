Hey Derek,

Not sure how the budget translates to Aussie Dollars (not just exchange 
rate but costs of equipment in the US compared to here) but the Panasonic 
GS400 (3CCD) is a very nice camera. It does very well under low light 
conditions compared with its competitors, especially the Sony cameras 
(with an inter-company purchase I could have saved over a thousand bucks 
buying Sony but I wasn't happy with low-light capturing). I was also 
pleased with the fact that it does true 16:9 - has the extra pixels on the 
CCD. It has a cinema mode but no doubt it is just stretching the 16:9 
mode.

Not too bad still shots too - if you're not too fussy. Eventually I'd like 
to get a "real" digital still camera though.

When I was assessing cameras for purchasing I found the comments at the 
bottom of some of the camcorder review sites extremely helpful as people 
were comparing real-world results from their purchases.

Anyway, best of luck!
