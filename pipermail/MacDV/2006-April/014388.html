Wow!  I just solved the problem.

I went to Sequence>Settings
Then I selected Load Sequence Preset.
I selected DV NTSC and all of the sudden, the timeline showed up on the 
camera
I ran "Print to Video" and it worked perfectly.  Looks great, too.

Thank  you for the fast response with some hints.

In the end, I think it was a combination of having to render a DV NTSC 
from the Motion JPEG A, and then resetting the sequence/project 
settings to the DV NTSC presets.

Thanks again!
Jill
