Sorry I don't have an answer but I do have an alternative method of getting 
the desired conclusion.

I do all my slide shows using imove HD and don't have a problem going to iDVD 
from there. Couple plus's when using iMove is you have more control over what 
pictures come up during certain words in a song (move pictures, shorten or 
lengthen time of a picture) and chapter markers are very handy on a DVD as well. 
One thing to note: When using iMovie it I think it must remain open until 
burning is over.

If you're unable to correct your issue you might want to try using iMovie 
rather than iPhoto.

Good luck.
