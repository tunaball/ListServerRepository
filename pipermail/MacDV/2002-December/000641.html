JC:

use Disk Burner (Apple's burning software that comes with CD-R 
equipped Macs)  to burn your picture file on a CD.  If you insert a 
blank cd your Mac should recognize it as such and ask you to name it. 
Then you just drag your picture files to the new CD icon on your 
desktop. When you go to eject that icon it will burn your CD.

Using Disk Copy is for copying CD's.  and you would not want to 
select NONE if this is what you are doing.
