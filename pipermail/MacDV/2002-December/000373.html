> I'm looking for some labeling software that will print decent CD

I am currently completing a rewrite of Label Printer Pro (6.1). It will 
be released in a few weeks. If you contact us we can provide an early 
release for your review.

Label Printer Pro currently prints several varieties of CD/DVD labels 
and their case inserts, plus a few varieties of VHS labels. In the next 
release I have just added miniDV labels and miniDV J cards (plus 
several other label types).

I do not have any VHS case inserts, however if you contact me and can 
provide EXACT specifications and/or samples and I will add them.

Although this program is a general purpose labeling package, being that 
I also am a videographer, my goal is to have all known video related 
labels in it by the middle of next year. The problem is only a few of 
the manufacturers have responded with specifications and samples for 
test.

If there is a label you want me to add as a template, have the 
manufacturer contact us or send us 6 to 12 pages of samples and any 
written specifications they might have for each label type to be added.
