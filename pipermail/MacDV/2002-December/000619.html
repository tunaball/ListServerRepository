Complete the first DVD. Using Disk Copy in OS X, make a disk image (Make 
it a CD/DVD master), saving it to your hard drive. Keep Disk Copy open. 
Choose FILE:New:Disk from Image. Choose the forementioned disk image. 
Insert blank DVD media. Wait a while. Play the copied DVD.
