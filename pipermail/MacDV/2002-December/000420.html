Most FW hard drives have two FW ports so they act as a hub so you can 
connect both the camera and hard drive at the same time. I also have a 
7200 rpm drive and cannot capture reliably directly to it but have never 
encountered a problem editing files on it or exporting them to iDVD.  As 
long as I take the extra step of capturing to the internal drive and 
then copying to the FW drive I encounter no problems.
