You need a SCART to (composite) video adapter (little block or cable).
SCART to S-video adapters also exist (only useful if your VCR does S-video
out).

SCART carries RGB, S-video, composite video, but not FireWire.

For SCART pin layout see here:
http://utopia.knoware.nl/users/eprebel/SoundAndVision/Engineering/index.htm
