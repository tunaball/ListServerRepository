I believe some DVD players actually support this "Photo Disc" format 
(JPEGs on a CD-R/DVD-R), though it may not be listed in any posted 
specs.  It may even work with a Mac/PC hybrid disc, as long as the disc 
is ISO-9660 compatible (Toast's default Mac/PC hybrid setting 
qualifies).  Now that I think about it, we have a JVC DVD player here 
at work that supports either "Photo" mode or "MP3" mode for CD-R discs 
-- it displays JPEG files on-screen from any supported disc (but cannot 
play MP3s and display JPEGs on the same disc without switching modes).

2 cents.

- Mark
