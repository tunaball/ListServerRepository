It would be good to have at least one partition on your system for 
just output of completed DV projects.  Easy to defragment (by erasing 
it).  Defragmenting (and on my system running Disk Warrior) is 
essential for some projects to output without dropped frames or 
glitches in the music).  Depending on the sizes of your future 
projects you may wish to set aside 15-20 GB for this (~ 1.5 hrs of 
video).  Any other partitioning depends upon your organization 
preferences.

the GOOD - partitions allow you to organize
the BAD  - you cannot consolidate the unused space on separate partitions

Good luck,
Mike (who put 6 partitions on his first 60 GB drive but only 3 on his 
last 200 GB drive)
