I also have been holding out and not paying for Quicktime Pro 6. Yes loading Quicktime 6 onto OSX will remove Pro 5, but you can have Quicktime 5 on OS9 and Quicktime 6 on OSX. Then launch the OS9 version of Quicktime, it will load in classic and you have access to Pro 5. In OSX you can use Quicktime 6 for buying music. I know some people would just say spend the money but I have yet to need the upgrades for Pro 6.

Dragging OSX iMovie to your upgraded Mac should not affect the OS9 version.
