>Besides price, what are the major differences in DVD Studio Pro 
>(version 2 Aug. 18 yeah!) and iDVD?

It's similar to the differences between iMovie and Final Cut Pro.
iDVD holds your hand and helps you build beautiful, simple DVDs.
DVD SP takes things to the next level. It lets you create
professionally authored DVDs with scripting, multiple angles,
languages, subtitle support and more. It offers web integration
(via DVD @ccess), etc. etc.
