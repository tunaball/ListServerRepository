I know BTV Pro has been mentioned for one or more things (like time 
lapse), but I don't recall anyone mentioning it for use with capturing 
Film. One of our user group members demoed his success capturing with a 
web cam and BTV Pro. There was no flicker because he set the fps to 
match the film. He didn't know if this would work with a DV camcorder. 
I wish I had a film projector to try re-capturing with BTV Pro instead 
of iMovie. Just thought I'd pass this along for anyone who wants to 
experiment.
