If the old render files have been replaced buy newer ones, you can probably
trash them.
You can open render files in QuickTime to see what is in them, or use
the Finder's Column view to view small previews of them.

Tom Wolsky's FCE4 book suggests that you move render files you suspect
are obsolete to another folder or the desktop, and then open the
project and see if FCE complains that they are missing. You can then
put back the ones that FCE needs.
