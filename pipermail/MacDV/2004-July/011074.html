Claire,

You want to use the iLink (=FireWire) cable to connect to your 
Powerbook.  That way, the signal stays digital all the way and there's 
no loss of quality.

If you use S video, the camera needs to convert the signal to an 
analogue format first with some loss of quality resulting.

Also, my PowerBook will not accept S video INPUT signals - it only has 
S video OUTPUT.  Yours may well be the same, so you may not be able to 
connect via S video without extra hardware.

You should also be able to connect the ProScan Hi-8 camcorder through 
the Sony miniDV camcorder to capture your old footage.

Hope this helps,

Colin McDonald
