As long as you get the aspect right, the actual size, resolution, or 
pixel count does not matter.
Most any size still is properly resized by either FCP or iMovie.
In any case iMovie native is 640x480 and FCP is 720 x480.
In Photoshop I think you have to increase the 480 to 500 or 520 to 
correct for square to
rectangular pixel conversion.
stills constructed in those sizes will be just right.
