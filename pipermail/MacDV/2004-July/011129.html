Karl: the Pioneer burner should work without any problems. The proof is 
in the pudding.

Try burning DVDs from the desktop (insert a blank DVD-R, give it a 
name, transfer lots of files to backup to, then click on the burn baby 
symbol next to the DVD in your sidepanel in the finder window). It 
should work.

A tougher test is to burn a DVD in iDVD.

I have earlier models of the Pioneer DVD in my G4s and they work fine. 
There's a hack available to boost the right speed for the DVD burner, 
but I haven't tried that. The lore is that the faster the burn, the 
lower the longevity of the resulting disk.
