>If it is a commercial DVD, it has encryption on it. MacTheRipper 
>will remove the encryption for you. DVD Backup should do it also, 
>but I like MacTheRipper better.
>
>Once you rip it to harddrive, test it with DVD Player. Open the 
>VIDEO_TS folder with DVD Player and see if it plays correctly (be 
>sure the original DVD is ejected - DVD Player can get confused).
>
>If the VIDEO_TS does play properly then burn it with Toast using the 
>'DVD-Video from VIDEO_TS folder' radio button in the Advanced tab of 
>the Video drawer.
