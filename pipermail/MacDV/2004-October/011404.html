That's an upgraded drive, and it's always a risk when you upgrade - you 
may find that the drive is not fully supported. While Mac OS 10.2.8 is 
supposed to support the 106D, it may not, depending on the path you 
took to reach that version of the operating system. In addition, a 
small number of Pioneer 106D's are not compatible with Macs because 
their firmware is very slightly different from that in other examples 
of the same model. In your case, though, the firmware looks ok (the 
product id says DVD-RW while in unsupported models the id says DVD RW, 
without the hyphen).

You might try patchburn 
<http://www.macwelt.de/magazin/0310/PatchBurn1.1en.sit> (at your own 
risk!) or check out the FAQ at
http://forums.xlr8yourmac.com/faq.lasso and read the DVD FAQs on the 
DVR-106D/DVR-A06
