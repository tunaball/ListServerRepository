Have you looked at Apple Article ID: 93804? Try temporarily removing 
the following two files from /Library/Quicktime/ to the desktop:

DesktopVideoOut.component
DVCPROHDVideoOutput.component

I had the same problem as you with iMovie 4, with FCP 4.5HD installed 
on my computer, and the above solved the problem of exporting from 
iMovie via File>Share to camcorder.
