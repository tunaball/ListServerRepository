I have two La Cie Porche and a Sarotech.
The Sarotech 80 gb. is over four years old and has never missed a  
beat, I like it because it has its power supply built into the  
enclosure, which helps keep down the cord clutter.
The two La Cies both have external supplies which adds to the  
clutter. One is 160 and the other 250gb, both are working perfectly  
for me.
However, have a look around some of the other discussion groups as I  
have read of some users having issues with La Cie drives.
