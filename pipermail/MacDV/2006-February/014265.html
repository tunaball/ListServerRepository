No way to avoid them, that I know of, but a way to modify them to  
something you like.  Just pick a theme and drag a new background  
image (could be a solid colored JPG) to cover whatever is there. If  
you are working in v6, use Transparent black template.  Not trite and  
simple.  Any audio you want to get rid of or replace go to the menu  
tab and drag the old audio out into a puff of smoke (so no audio) and  
drag in new audio (if you want menu audio).
