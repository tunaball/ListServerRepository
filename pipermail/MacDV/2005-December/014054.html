There are a number of programs that will allow you to record and save  
audio.  My hardware device is "PowerWave" by Griffith Technology.  
They have a cheaper and simpler interface called iMic. My software  
right now is Final Vinyl 1.1.2 but there is alos Audio Hijack Pro  
which I have successfully used and Audiocorder 4.5 which I have also  
used. Finay Vinyl is the simplest of the batch. I've been using it  
recently to transfer audiobook cassettes to CD's for my car. Never  
had a problem.
