On Feb 10, 2004, at 8:02 PM, James Asherman wrote:

> But they are not. Are they?

NO -- Audio CDs are their own thing -- it's a standard format, but 
neither WAV or AIFF.  WAV and AIFF are the formats used on the 
*COMPUTER* side of things -- either to burn to CD or rip from them.  
WAV and AIFF files are merely a means of transportation for the audio 
data in the computer realm -- they're all the same 1s and 0s, just in 
different "wrapper" formats.
