Just wanted to be sure that you know that, if you have or are 
planning to get a digital camcorder, you can use many DV camcorders 
as an analog-to-digital converter for importing old analog video. If 
the camcorder has "video pass-through" mode, you can plug the A/V 
cables from a VHS deck, for example, into the camcorder, connect the 
camcorder to your Mac via FireWire, and import the video into iMovie 
or other software.

I've done this with my Canon ZR 40, and it works fine.
