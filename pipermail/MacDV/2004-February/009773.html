And don't forget that static electricity is your enemy!!  A good wrist 
strap is worth the investment should you fail to touch ground before 
you get your fingers in the works :-)  If you don't have one, just make 
sure to touch the grounded chassis - but it must be plugged in before 
it is grounded!  The box surrounding the power supply is a pretty safe 
place to drop a static charge.  Spoken sadly from experience - at least 
it wasn't one of mine...
