Step 1:  Chill on the intoxication regarding the *idea* of free money.

"Emerging Artist" doesn't mean "emerging from not being an artist an having
done nothing to thinking about making a first work."  They mean, "emerging
into having a personal voice and into being recognized after toiling and
exploring art for quite a while in obscurity with an undeveloped voice."

Step 2:  Make art and be willing to dump your own money into it, because if
you're not, you don't care enough to emerge on any grant-maker's radar.  Do
this for at least...I don't know...two to five years.

Step 3:  Develop body of work that seems to follow a fruitful path of
exploration.

Step 4:  Apply for grants.
