Please join us for our next meeting of the BOSFCPUG, to be held this  
Tuesday, November 16th, from 6:30pm to 9:30pm, at the CENTER FOR  
DIGITAL IMAGING ARTS at Boston University in Waltham, MA.  Doors open  
at 6pm.

You can register to attend at:  
<http://seminars.apple.com/goToEvent.html?id=31322>.

We have three special guests joining us at our meeting:
- KEVIN "TELLY" MONAHAN, Graphic guru, "Motion Graphics and Effects in  
Final Cut Pro"
- WES PLATE, Automatic Duck Software
- STEVE BAYES, Director of Product Marketing for Media 100, Inc.

The BOSFCPUG is very happy to have Kevin, Wes and Steve as our featured  
invited guests. We'll focus on Motion Graphics and Effects in Final Cut  
Pro as well as the workflow of moving projects back and forth between  
Avid and Final Cut Pro HD, with a sneak preview of Steve Bayes' Boris  
Continuum DVD with Boris FX.
