I've had a good run with Site 5.  http://site5.com.

They have a range of plans to fit many needs. and include a lot of 
stuff to help make it easy to manage.

Last time I checked, they had prices in the $7 range.  I use 
godaddy,com to register new domains.  8.85 for .com's when most others 
are way more.

The founders are  a couple of web design guys who apparently couldn't 
find hosting solutions that suited what they wanted as web designers, 
so they created their own hosting company.  They're based in the SF Bay 
Area, and the servers are in New Jersey.  The data center they use just 
upgraded its lines to even bigger pipes.  Rare to have downtime, and 
they advise you well in advance.
