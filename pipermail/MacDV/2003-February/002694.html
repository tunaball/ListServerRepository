I've noticed a few people get upset about having trouble unsubscribing. 
After you've been on a list for awhile you tend to forget where you went 
to get started, so may I make this suggestion to smooth things out for 
everyone:

Put the unsubscribe instructions at the end of messages that appear. I 
think that without them it isn't so obvious how to do it. We seem to 
have plenty of people to keep the list going, so making it easier for 
those who don't want to continue shouldn't affect the quality of the list.

Also it wouldn't hurt to remind everyone every once in awhile to trim 
their quotations of previous messages (in a nice way of course) to keep 
things more manageable.

What would be great would be if the list software could recognize 
subjects that said "unsubscribe" or "unsubscribe me" and automatically 
take care of the chore and at the same time leaving that particular 
message off the list, but I don't know what is possible.

I've been really enjoying this list and have gotten a lot out of it. 
There are of course topics I'm not interested in, but I can skip those. 
I've found the Digest form the best way so far to look at the list 
because of the quantity of messages.

-Dave
