Will the TRV-900 do video pass through?  I think it depends on the 
TRV-900.  This discussion came around sometime back.  I checked the 
Sony web site at that time, and it clearly said that the TRV-900 
would do pass through.  And a list member reported that his would. 
However, the one that I have used does not.

My guess is that Sony added the pass through feature at some point, 
without changing the model number.  So older TRV-900 models can't do 
pass through, but those manufactured shortly before the TRV-950 came 
out can do pass through.  I haven't done it, but that's the info that 
I have found.
