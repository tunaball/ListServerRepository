I have no quarrel with folks listing Mac DV appropriate items to this list--in fact, I welcome them, but please observe some etiquette.

Put "FS: item name, etc." in the subject line. This will allow those not interested to skip your message. This also opens the channel for folks looking for items, such as "WTB:". If it is an eBay item, please use "FA:" as some people simply do not wish to undergo the eBay hassle, regardless of the item. In fact, you may want to post FS: here before listing on eBay.

Never post photos or long descriptions, this plugs up the bandwidth. Interested parties can always email directly for more information.

Thanks and good luck with your sales,
