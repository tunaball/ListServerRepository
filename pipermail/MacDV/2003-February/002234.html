Thank you Pam. I was wondering where they were too. I found the "Sound 
Effects" folder just where you told us to look. But I noticed that what 
was in it didn't all appear in the "iMovie Sound Effects" list. What 
wasn't showing up were all the ones not nested in a folder.

Turns out you have to have all of your sounds inside folders in that 
"Sound Effects" folder for them to show up. You can even create new 
folders for new effects categories. So that's the rule everyone. Follow 
Pam's lead to the "Sound Effects" folder, then put all you want in 
there but in folders — nothing loose. And both aiff and mp3 files work.
