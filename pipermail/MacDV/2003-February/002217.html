>From the Help text:

You can define up to 36 chapter markers in your video-editing software.
If a movie has more than 36 markers, it can't be imported.

If the menu (you create by dragging in a movie) has no buttons, iDVD
automatically creates two buttons on the menu: a button with the title
of the movie and a scene selection button that links to a scene submenu.

Each scene submenu has no more than six buttons. A submenu is created
for each six chapters in your movie.
