Well, if it's actually a "still frame", you can change the time in the
Clip Info dialog. However, if it is a rendered Ken Burnsian still, then
you need to adjust the length slider for that clip and have the Ken
Burns Effect rerendered (Apply button).

These topics are covered in the iLife Bible, but it won't be out until
at least late April (more likely May), due to production cycle times.
