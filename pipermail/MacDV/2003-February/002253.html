I encountered this while working with pre-release versions --  you've got
files from an older version of iMovie hanging around on your system
somewhere, and they're causing the problem.

You should do a Find for anything with "iMovie" in its name, and then delete
those items (after, of course, verifying that none of them are documents or
folders that *you've* created that have "iMovie" in their names).

Then reinstall iMovie 3.0.1.
