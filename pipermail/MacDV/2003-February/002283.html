Hi again Brenda,
You feed the DV camera your analog VHS signal and it passes on through 
to the computer digitally from the DV camera NO TAPE. When you are 
passing edited video back to the camera, the camera will output the 
analog signal which you connect to your VHS deck where you make a dub 
as you are making the DV tape too. If you have more than one VHS deck 
you can daisy chain them to all record the output at once.

lol. No the VHS VCRs can't plan DV tape. You want to research DV 
cameras use http://www.supervideo.com
