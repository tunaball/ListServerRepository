In iDVD, you bring in the movies just as you did before. Just drag them
into your theme and the buttons appear. They didn't take anything away,
just provided a shortcut from iMovie for those who want it. This feature
is primarily useful for those who want chaptered movies, although any
movie can take advantage of it.
