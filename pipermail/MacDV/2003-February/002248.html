The easiest way to transfer home movies to DVD without
editing involves the use of a standalone DVD recorder.
You can find some models of these for about $350-$400.
I personally use a Pioneer DMR-E20. Be aware that DVD-R
(and DVD+R) are not as compatible as normal DVD discs and
may not play back on all DVD players. Retain your old
videos for archival purposes and consider transferring
them to miniDV tapes for long-term storage. DVD-R's (and
DVD+Rs) are probably not a good long-term solution.
