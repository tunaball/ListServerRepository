You'll need to capture the movies and get them into the computer 
somehow if you want to use iDVD (are you?).  iMovie does this well, 
plus you get the added benefit of being able to edit things for free.  
What you need to figure out is if your Sony Camcorder supports 
pass-though video conversion (just play the movies into the computer) 
or whether you will have to record each movie to DV tape and then 
recapture it on the computer (time-consuming).
