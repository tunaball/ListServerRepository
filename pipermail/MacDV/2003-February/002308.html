Which Sony Digital 8 Camcorder?

If you have at least the model 140 and up you can connect an analog VCR 
or camera to the digital camera and record your analog tapes on to 
digital tapes.

I often wondered if you got a Hi-8 camcorder and connected it to the 
analog VCR if the resulting Hi-8 tape would be able to be digital-ize 
the video since the Hi-8 tape is readable in the Sony digital 8 cameras.

So perhaps that would work too if you don't have a model 140 or higher.
