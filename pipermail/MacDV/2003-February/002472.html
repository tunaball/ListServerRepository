I'm using a Dazzle Hollywood A/D converter with iMovie 3, and have had 
no problems at all, so far.  Of course, I am one of the few that do not 
appear to be experiencing the major speed problems (with the exception 
of the jerky scrub bar on playback) that others are experiencing.  So 
far, my experience with iMovie 3 has been quite nice.  I'm on a TiBook 
550 with 768MB RAM, running 10.2.3.  I'm still keeping iMovie 2 around, 
just in case, but so far, so good.

The only real complaint that I have so far is that the "Ken Burns 
Effect" doesn't have the option to ease in and out of the pans, like 
SlickMotion and Photo to Movie have.
