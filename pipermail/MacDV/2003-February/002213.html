Here is a good site if you are looking for various sound effects.  It 
is a search engine, that searches the internet for downloadable sounds.

Just type in a word or words to describe what you are looking for (ie 
"car crash", or "baby's cry"), and it will give you a list of  sounds 
that you can audition and download.  As with any search engine you may 
have to try several different key words before you find what you are 
looking for.

http://www.findsounds.com/
