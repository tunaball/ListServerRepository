MacDV at lists.themacintoshguy.com writes:
>We had our first class editing with iMovie 2.1.2, installed with 
>10.2.3, on flat screen 700 MHz iMacs, initializing the disk before 
>installing the OS.
>
>The students discovered that they couldn't add titles to the 
>timeline.  They could create and preview the titles, but when they 
>tried to drag them into the timeline or clip viewer, nothing 
>happened.  Clicking and dragging did nothing.  There was no ghost 
>image of the title following the mouse pointer's drag down to the 
>timeline.
>
>Has anyone seen this?  Any suggestions or work-arounds?  I got the 
>same results on my old G4 tower, and on an iMac upgraded to 10.2.4.

Try dragging the gray (highlighted) line that is the name of the title (ie
bounce to center) to the timeline.  You can't drag the thumbnail view as
in previous versions.
