Usually, its not a problem if they were synched before. If you're using 
iMovie, for example, you have a video track and audio track, and you 
just jam them both against the left stop, and there you are. If they 
weren't in sync, you just tweak the sound track back and forth until it 
looks right. Amazingly, you notice pretty immediately if the sound is 
as little as 3 frames off. With a little practice, you can get it 
exactly right fairly quickly.
