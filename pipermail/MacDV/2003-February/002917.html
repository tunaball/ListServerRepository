VCDs, in this context, refer to the actual discs. The i/o routines
QuickTime uses to read from a disk in OS X are pretty much restricted to
Mode 1 Form 1 disks and give errors when Mode 2 Form 2 block read
attempts are made.

Current versions of QuickTime will play VCD-compatible MPEG files just
fine -- they just won't play them from the actual CD media. The
shareware solution (MacVCD) allegedly works to play the discs (I haven't
tried it recently) as does the freeware solution (vlc).
