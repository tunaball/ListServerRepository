I used iMovie 3 on a 400 MHz PowerBook G3 (FireWire) this weekend. It 
worked with and outputted an old project just fine, but interface 
responsiveness was very, very slow and the application was a bit 
unstable. It crashed several times while rendering titles, and I had 
a couple other unexpected quits for unknown reasons. OTOH, the final 
quality of the rendered titles were superior to what iMovie 2 could 
do.

A huge pain is that it refuses to export to FireWire if the camcorder 
doesn't have a recordable tape in it. I wanted to make multiple 
copies of the movie. I normally use the camcorder as pass-through to 
the VCR, but not record to the camcorder (at least not after the 
first pass). This worked with iMovie 2, but I can't get it to do so 
in iM3.
