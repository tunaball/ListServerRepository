I wound up purchasing the Samsung/BeAll DVD-R media from Meritline.com

I got the 50-pack and I'm happy to say that they seem to be fully 
compatible with everything I've tried so far. They work great in the 
iMac, they work in the PlayStation2, and they work in my set top player 
(Panasonic RP-91)

So I would recommend these DVD-Rs for anyone looking for good 
compatibility.

They run around $1.65 or so each in the 50-pack.
