You can use MPEG Streamclip to rip (convert) the MPEG2 files to DV.  
The DV files can be imported and edited in iMovie HD.

The special paste feature you are referring to is called Paste over at  
Playhead, from the Advanced Menu. If you want it to Extract the Audio,  
make sure the box is checked in the iMovie Preferences.
