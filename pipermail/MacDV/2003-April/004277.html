I think the best way to go is to buy a used eMac, just as I did. That way you get a like new system that has already had the video
problem fixed. I paid $799 for mine (G4 700 version with Combo) and bought one of those $149 Cendyne 4x DVD-RW's .... I haven't had

goes.
