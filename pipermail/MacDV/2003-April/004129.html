>> although it shows up as a QT file. I think it was originally captured
>> via Premier... QT says it cannot play it because the compressor is

>
> This will happen if the capture system used a proprietary MJPEG card  
> like
> Media100, Targa2000 (old school?), etc.  If possible, you should have  
> the
> person who captured the video convert it into a non-proprietary CODEC  
> like
> Photo-JPEG (highest quality) or *Apple* MJPEGA or B.
>
> Otherwise, if you can just find out what system was used to capture the
> clip, you may be able to download a control panel or extension (for  
> Mac OS
> 9) that will allow you to work with the clip you already have.  That  
> used to
> be the case when I worked with Media100 and Targa2000 systems.
>
> Hope that helps.
