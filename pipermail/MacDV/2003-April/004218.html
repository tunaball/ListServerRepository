Yes, you have to use Disk Utility. It allows you to do some operations 
at the "device" level and others at the  "volume" or "partition" level. 
So you can reformat a single partition.

Erasing (formatting) a disk is one feature that was moved out of the 
Finder in OS X. You wouldn't believe how long it took me to figure it 
out the first time I wanted to reformat a zip disk in OS X.
