TK and everyone else,

It appears that buying an entire "album" from the Apple Music Store 
will be cheaper that buying each individual song.  For example, Kelly 
Clarkson's new album, Thankful, can be purchased for $9.99 for all 12 
songs.  Not that I would ever buy one of her album's (not my particular 
taste in music and this is the only album that I have been able to get 
the Apple Music Store to bring back so far; constant 504 error since it 
is so busy), but I just wanted to give an example of what I had found.  
I do not know if this would hold true for albums with less that 10 song 
or not, so don't beat me up too bad.

Dwayne
