If all you are doing with it is importing to iMovie from VHS or 
exporting from iMovie to VHS, you might consider getting a DV 
camcorder that has pass-through mode (my Canon ZR 40 is an example). 
It can be used for capturing video from VHS to the Mac, or exporting 
back to a VHS deck. Some of these are available for less than $600 
(my ZR 40 was about $500 a year ago).

This has the beneficial side-effect of providing you with an 
additional DV camcorder.  :-)
