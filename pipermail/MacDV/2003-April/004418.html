Just make sure you have some *FAST* drives (usually Ultra or Ultra Wide with
RAID-0), and you should do alright.  The old NuBus Targa2000 system I used
to use was *really* picky about drive speed -- would drop frames pretty
easily, but this was 7 years ago when drives weren't nearly as fast as they
are now.
