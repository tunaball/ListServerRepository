What part of the error message are you having a problem with? Whatever 
codec was used to compress the clip, you ain't got it. Ask the person 
who compressed the clip what he used, or have him try a different 
codec. QT Pro includes most of the common ones, but if he used 
something like DIVX then it may only play on PC systems. You might try 
downloading the OS X-only VLC Player and see if it will play in that. 
It's been a miracle worker for me.
