It is not difficult to change out a drive. Be aware of static 
electricity and ground yourself. Radio shack sells a wrist static strap 
cheap. Other than that it is pretty much unscrew, unplug then plug in 
the new drive case and button up. I have had good success with the 
inexpensive Ultra case I think I got from Tiger.com or something like 
that for only 39.99
