You want to OPEN the LaCie Drive case - unless you can return it for  
a refund -, remove the 3.5" HD from the inside, go to Comp-USA or  
Fry's or wherever you buy computer accessories in your area at low  
prices and buy a FW400 case for about $40-$50, put the drive in  
there, close it up, hook it to your eMac's spare FW port and fire it  
up. Then you can choose it as the scratch drive. That's your cheapest  
solution.

Oh and don't buy any more LaCie Drives. You can do it yourself for  
way less. I can make a 300 GB external FW400 drive for $99 all the time.
