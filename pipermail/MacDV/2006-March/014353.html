None of these programs care about 'resolution' at all. The very phrase '72
dpi' is meaningless. The *only* thing that matters is the pixel count
(width x height). Wipe the word 'resolution' right out of your brain and
you'll be far better off :-)

The NTSC image is 640 x 480 pixels and that doesn't change whether you are
on a 13" TV or a 40" TV or a 17" computer screen.
