>
>Without your camera connected, open Image Capture. A window should 
>open saying No Image Capture device connected.
>
>Select the Image Capture preferences under the Image Capture menu.
>
>Select  for When a Camera is connected, Image Capture. Or if you 
>prefer use the Other option for another application. Quit Image 
>Capture and connect your camera.
>
>If that doesn't work, disconnect the camera and try another 
>selection in the popup menu.
>
>When Image capture opens, it presents a window letting you select 
>where to down load the fotos, I usually download to an empty folder 
>named New Pictures and then drag that only to the iView Media Pro 
>alias.
>
>If this still doesn't work, open Image Capture before you connect 
>your camera and see what happens. It is possible your camera is just 
>incompatible but let's not give up yet.
>
>You can also check Apple support to see what they say about the problem.
