Whoa, I hope "every 10 shots" is an exaggeration, because though I don't own
that particular camera, that's pretty short battery life. And I agree that
rechargeables are the way to go, but there is one good way to extend your
battery life. If your camera allows for it, shut off the digital display in
the window on the back of the camera and line up your shots through the
eyepiece. That digital display uses a lot of energy. Since I started doing
that I can shoot pretty much all day without switching batteries.
