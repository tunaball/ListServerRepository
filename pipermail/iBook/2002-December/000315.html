> From: Joe <jabeleda at nbn.net>
>
> HI! Just installed Jaguar. I noticed when I take a screenshot
> (cmd-shit-4) the file turns out as a pdf. Is there a way to make the
> screenshot a TIFF file like 10.1?

Short answer: no.

Tinkertool used to adjust this preference for you, but Jag changed all 
that. Nowadays I take the screen shot then drag the file to a shareware 
app called Kunvert. It changes the PDF to whatever format I want. 
Useful for other types of PDF to, btw!

You can get Kunvert from any of the usual Mac download places.
