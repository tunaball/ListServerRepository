There is also ipodhacks.com, but the two you listed seem to update faster.
I got the case & car plug from xtrememac.com - good company, one of the
plastic things was missing and they sent me a new one no question.  The cord
coils which is great for storage and use.  Since my cargo van (aka dog
kennel on wheels) has only a plain radio I use an old fm transmitter to pump
the ipod sound through an unused station.  This one plays on any station,
not the limited 4 stations on the ones sold now.  It is sometimes hard to
get a clear station in the city and there is some engine feedback and a
whine when the ipod is charging - BUT - it is worth it not having to listen
to commercials and songs I don't like.  I plan to either get an fm modulator
or a new radio with miniplug input this spring.
