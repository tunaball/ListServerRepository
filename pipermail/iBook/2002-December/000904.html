If you're thinking of using an iBook without removing it from the case, 
watch out for overheating. There needs to be air circulation. I use a 
CoolPad almost all the time (I have the Podium model for my desk, and the 
smaller one for my lap and for travel). This not only provides additional 
air circulation for cooling, but offers a swivel that I find convenient.
