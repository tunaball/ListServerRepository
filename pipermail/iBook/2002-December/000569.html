For what its worth, I have 9.2.2 and 9.1 on my single partition icebook 
700 along with 10.2. No problems installing or using either. I find 9.1 
necessary for running DeLorme Street Atlas which never liked 9.2. There 
are other apps with the same problem. Apple recognized this and made 
both 9.1 and 9.2 classic-capable. I use a trim 9.1 for classic and keep 
my fat old 9.2.2 around in case I have to boot into it, which hasn't 
come up lately.
