Hi Jamie,

The preference file you'll want to delete is 
com.apple.windowserver.plist located in /Library/Preferences. After 
that, reboot, and your system should behave normally again. Some users 
also reported the need to zapp the PRAM (restart holding 
command+option+P+R for four chimes) although I didn't have to in my 
case.

 From what I gathered from other affected users, deleting the 
aforementioned file after using file sharing fixed the problem.

Jason
