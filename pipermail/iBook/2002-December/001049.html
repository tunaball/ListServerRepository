On 12/29/02 9:54 PM, "Adam Boettiger" <boettiger at pobox.com> wrote:

> Can anyone tell me if the airport card I am using in my G4 Pismo will fit in
> one of the newer iBooks, or must I buy a new card?

If it's an Apple Airport card, not a PC slot card, it will work in the
iBook. I'm not sure what PowerBook you have (Pismos only came with G3
processors, unless you have an upgrade card), but all of them since the
Pismo support Apple's internal Airport card. And all iBooks, clamshell or
iceBook, support it as well.
