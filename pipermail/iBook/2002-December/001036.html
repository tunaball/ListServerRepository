Although I am still using Entourage myself, I set up my mom's iMac for Mail
over Thanksgiving.  It was a little time consuming, but not too bad since
she did not have the thousands of saved items that I have (yes my rooms and
closets are just as cluttered).

Anyhow, simply go to the top menu Mailbox/New Mailbox to make a new folder.
Clicking on the Mailboxes icon in the Mail window menu bar will open the
mailbox organizing area (to the right or left depending on the windows
position).  You can then drag the new mailbox folder to a new hierarchy
position as you choose.
