I use SurfBest.net, $12.50/mo. unlimited dialups all over the US
which supplement my home broadband connection.

I briefly considered a pay-as-you-go ISP.  However, figuring 28
days away from home a year and 2 hours of connect time per day,
plus ATTBI outages, it was pretty close to the cutoff point for
a 4.5 cent/minute plan.
