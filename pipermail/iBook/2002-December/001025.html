Brian Pearce wrote:

> Explorer saves Bookmarks (or "Favorites") in the form of an HTML file; 
> look for a file called "Favorites.html" and try deleting it. (If you 
> haven't saved any bookmarks, you won't lose any by doing so.) This 
> should cause Explorer to regenerate the file, and hopefully, next time 
> it will work as it should.
> 
> By the way, if you have Bookmarks from Netscape you want to use in 
> Explorer, there are AppleScripts to automate the process of transferring 
> them from one program to the other. I don't know where they are just at 
> the moment, but they shouldn't be too hard to locate.
>
