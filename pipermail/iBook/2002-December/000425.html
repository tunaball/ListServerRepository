Hey Brian:

Easy. Open the iPhoto app. Under iPhoto on the menu bar, scroll down to and
open Preferences. In the box that opens up you'll see, a little more than
half way down, the following: "Double-clicking photos opens them in:" Click
the "other" option there and then click the Set button. That brings up a
window where you can navigate to whatever app you want to open to edit
photos (in your case, Photoshop Elements). Highlight the Photoshop Elements
app, click the open button, this sends you back to the iPhoto preference
pane where you now should see Photoshop Elements listed. Now, whenever you
double click on a photo in iPhoto, it should open up the image in Elements.

Hope that makes sense.

Ken Feder (new to the list)
