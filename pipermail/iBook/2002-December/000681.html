I can't be of much help with this, but generally speaking your ISP will 
be able to tell you what address to plug in to access their offerings 
of Usenet groups.

I might suggest to you that you'd be better off using a real newsreader 
program, such as MT-Newswatcher or Thoth or MacSoup or Hogwasher (and 
there are plenty of others as well). Entourage is a reasonably good 
email program, but a pretty bad newsreader.
