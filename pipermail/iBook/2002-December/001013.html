One of the most disappointing times in my life has been seeing my 
movies as blurred yucky things. "Can my camera really be that bad?"

Well, the answer is that Quicktime has several settings that default to 
the worst conditions imaginable so that the movie will run under all 
conditions.

I found HiQual, a free droppable from 
<http://www.synthetic-ap.com/products/hiq/index.html> which solves the 
problem and left me gasping in delight.

My videos in Quicktime that I had just made and though of as blurry, 
suddenly became sharp. The difference in the high quality setting and 
the lower quality setting is truly amazing. Save a video using the dvd 
option and then drag that onto the icon of HiQual. Option Drop toggles 
to the lower setting.
