1.33GHz).  I don't really sense any speed improvement.  I was a 
little concerned about putting a faster drive in due to heat.  I'd be 
more concerned with the 12" iBook.


I rarely boot my iBook, normally I put it to sleep.  And I leave most 
applications open so there is only a delay when an app has been 
mostly purged from real memory and it has to load form virtual.
