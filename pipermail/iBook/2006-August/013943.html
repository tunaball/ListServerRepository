On Aug 14, 2006, at 6:13 AM, G Henry Taylor wrote:

>> You can get instructions to take apart your clamshell at  
>> ifixit.com/Guide.

  There are other guides out there if you Google for Clamshell take  
apart or something similar, but the iFixit ones are very good. Read  
all the instructions a few times to thoroughly familiarize yourself   
with the procedure.

One good thing about the iFixit guides is the screw guide in the  
back. As you take apart the machine, tape each screw to the  
appropriate picture in the guide. Do this as you complete each step  
and before you move on to the next.
  It may seem like a lot of trouble as you go in, but it will be  
worth it as you put it back together again.
