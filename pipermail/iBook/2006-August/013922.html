At 10:46 AM -0700 8/12/06, Tom Meade wrote:

>Hi - It started with Safari, then Firefox, the Mail followed by other
>apps quitting soon after opening on my normally stable G4 iBook
>1GHz/768MB. So I ran DiskWarrior for a new directory, Disk Utility
>to Repair the disk and then Repaired Permissions. And it improved -
>Firefox ran but Safari still quit. Repeating the disk tests results produces
>"Needs Repair" all over again - same with permissions. Is this normal
>are is this a sign that the disk starting to fail?


What version of OS X?

Forget permissions until you have repaired your disk directory. 
(Which is what Disk Utility and DiskWarrior are for.) Run DiskWarrior 
and let it replace your directory.

Then, repair permissions, not from your OS install disc, but from 
Disk Utility in your Utilities folder.
