    Can't help you regarding the HD, but I would say upgrade the RAM. My
first Mac was a 300 MHz clamshell with a 6 gig HD and 64 meg RAM. I upgraded

it. (It will run Panther but the small HD is the problem there.)
    More RAM will also allow iTunes to run better.
