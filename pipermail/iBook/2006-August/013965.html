> I use  a G3 Clamshell iBook running OS 9.2.2 and a G4 iBook running  
> OS X 10.3.9.
> The G4 prints perfectly via my wireless network (2Wire 2700HG  
> Gateway router) using an Airport Extreme card but the G3, with its  
> old Airport card, does not.

Off the top of my head, I'd check to make sure whatever means you're  
using to send stuff to your printer will do Wireless "B" and "G"  
signals. (The old AirPort Card is "B," AirPort Extreme is "G".)
