Not nearly as often as I should! I have an AppleScript set up to make 
duplicates of important files on the iBook that change regularly (mail, 
Quicken files, Safai bookmarks), but that's just a backup copy on the

backed up every so often, but that's about it.

I'm about to buy a large Firewire drive specifically for the purpose of 
making regular back-ups.
