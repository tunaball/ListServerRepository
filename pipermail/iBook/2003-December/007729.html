I just received and installed a 512 Meg Ram chip from Crucial, after 
the good recs on these lists, in my new 17" PB.

This was the easiest to install Powerbook Ram I have ever installed... 
It just fit perfectly. The last three sims I bought from Ramseeker, ?, 
did not fit well and I had to force them. One I had to return and the 
replaced it. The half moon holes were miss-placed by 1/4 diameter or 
so.

So, for now, I also recommend Crucial.
