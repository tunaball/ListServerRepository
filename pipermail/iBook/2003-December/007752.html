> How do I turn off that VERY ANNOYING "feature" of autopreview 
> attachments in mail.app???

Under Mail's preferences (under "Mail" in the menu bar), in "Viewing," 
uncheck the box that "Display images and embedded objects in HTML 
messages". Easy as that.
