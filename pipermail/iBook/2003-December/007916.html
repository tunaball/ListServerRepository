Both Extensis and iView give free time limited demos -- I'd try them both --
right now I'm playing with iView on my iBook G4.  I like it so far, but the
$160 price is a little steep.  Compared to Extensis, iView very quickly
imported my iPhoto  library --- (about 1500) in less then 2min... Extensis
took about 15mins for the import...  I also like the image editing
capabilities of iView as well as the ability to build a cataloged CD-ROM of
images -- (not yet tested).
