You can always use the finder to organize your files.

I view them in iView Media Pro and select and then drag into a finder 
folder and then press the delete key to remove them from that new 
catalog.

My hierarchy may be something like

Florida:Broward:ARMLOX:
    alligator
    heron
    snake
    spider
    etc

The lower folders may be broken down by dates, etc.

Thus I can search for alligator and find all such files and see where 
they were taken. The finder does all of the work. And I love OS X's 
preview in column mode where I click on a file and it shows me the 
thumbnail.

I can also drag and drop a particular photo onto iPhoto or iView if I 
need a particular feature from them.
