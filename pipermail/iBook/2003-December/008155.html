I have seen a message at a forum, I think macnn, that shows how to change a
firmware setting to allow this. I strongly urge you to not use it. The iBook
is made to be used with the lid up. A significant amount of the cooling is
done through the top of the computer - ie around the keyboard - and keeping
the lid shut inhibits cooling. Heat is the enemy of electronics and using
this hack could seriously shorten the life of your computer.
