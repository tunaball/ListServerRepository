On Dec 11, 2003, at 9:17 AM, Erin Randel wrote:

> What have you used, what's worked, how should I pick?

Diskwarrior 3 is the best choice for rebuilding your directory.

You can restart holding down the command+s keys and (don't panic) when 
the black screen with white letters appears and the text stops look at 
the end for the instructions on how to run fsck and typo the info 
exactly as shown. You may need to do this 2 or 3 times. When done type 
exit and after your mac reboots, shutdown and reboot.

If that doesn't solve your problems, buy Diskwarrior and insert it in 
your cd drive, reboot and hold down the C key so as to start up from 
your CD...etc.
