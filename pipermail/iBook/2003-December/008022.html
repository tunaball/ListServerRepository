seen in public with one. I've had a Podium Pad for my desk and a Cool 
Pad for travel as far back as my 1400 PB. I use the Bra Pad 
(www.brapad.com), keeping my 'Book's appearance as youthful as 
possible. And it works on male and female iBooks - it keeps them firm, 
hard, and they won't sag or droop.

If the Bra Pad doesn't, there is a simple alternative: insist - firmly 
- that your iBook wear a paper bag when you appear with it in public.
