bought separately, you can connect them to your Powerbook and iBook via 
the Firewire port for electricity and the plug then goes into your 
headphone port. I am listening to them now, Willie Nelson!, and amazed 
at how good such a small speaker can sound...somewhat helped by the 
loss of hearing of the high freqs.
