The hard drive will be slower to access so the idea would be not to  
play the songs from the external hard drive while trying to access  
anything else.  There is a huge speed difference between 2.0 and 1.x  
USB:
   480 Mbits/s—USB 2.0
     12 Mbits/s—USB 1.1

12 mbits is still quick enough for mp3 play and some streaming video  
clips.

The other thing to watch out for will be if you get an external drive,  
it will need power.  Most of the bus powered drives work in 2.0 mode,  
some won't work well in 1.x.

There is one now that uses a 10/100 Ethernet connection in addition to  
USB.  This means with a home network hub (so all computers can share  
this device) and standard network cables (or just a cross over cable),  
you have a 160gb storage solution with a 10/100 interface.  While I
