> I have a little humidifier, made by Sunbeam, that holds a pint of
> water. I also have a thermometer that also shows the humidity level.
> I watch it and keep the humidity level at 50 per cent. No static and

If it's one of those ultrasonic ones, make sure that you
either use distilled water or don't have much mineral
content in your tap water.  We tried one once and it
deposited a fine layer of grit throughout the entire
house.  Not the best thing for moving parts, though
now that floppies are unheard of it's probably not
as bad for your computer as it was formerly.
