again. I'm in the process of setting up a new 15" AlBook, and soon will 
be in a position to sell both the USB drive and the clamshell 366 SE 
(no FireWire) iBook.

While I'll be willing to sell the drive to you (it cost me about $200 
new), I can't say that I recommend USB external drives. I think USB 1 
is fine for peripherals like a keyboard and mouse, but too slow and 
flaky for something like a hard drive.
