I set up my neighbors Linksys router for his Windows machine using my 
dual USB iBook, because he couldn't get it to work with his computer.

would work.
The hardest thing to do was update the software of  my neighbors 
Linksys (it wasn't a necessity but I thought I'd bring him up-to-date; 
it required downloading some shareware ( which I happily paid $12 for ) 
and then only took about a minute to do.
I am now running a Linksys router as a second node after my Airport on 
my wireless network.
