Hi Brett,

Just one more comment. I bought my iBook in July of 2001 as well. The 
battery was starting to lose its effectiveness before the end of the 
first year. In addition, the charger plug wasn't working properly and I 
had to jiggle it to get it to charge the battery. I sent my iBook in 
for repair. (And bought AppleCare just in case.) When I got the iBook 
back, the battery was in even worse shape and eventually it got around 
an hour or so. I talked to my local Apple dealer who said he'd look 
after it and he did. Today I picked up a new battery and gave him my 
old one. No cost.

It's nice to see a number greater than (1:00) at the top of the screen 
as I'm typing this and running on the battery -- (3:29) actually. I 
always run Airport on my iBook by the way.

Milton
