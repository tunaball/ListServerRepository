If you're already running 10.1.5 and finding it acceptable, then you're 
going to be absolutely delighted with Jaguar. It's considerably 
snappier/faster, boasts boatloads of new features and returns many of 
the best-loved "little touches" from OS 9 back into OS X -- and 
improves upon many of them. Classic will run better and be more 
compatible with hardware than ever before, more 
printers/scanners/cameras will work with it, and you have loads of new 
and improved apps to choose from.

Be sure to run DiskWarrior and PlusOptimizer on your drive prior to and 
after installing 10.2 for best results, and of course have a backup 
handy just in case.
