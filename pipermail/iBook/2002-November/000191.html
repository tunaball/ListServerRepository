>  My question is, what is an iceBook? Is this vernacular for a
> particular model series of iBooks?
>
Yes. The "iceBooks" are the current "style" of iBook ... small, 
all-white, square.

Prior to these, iBooks were large, clamshell-shaped things in various 
colours. If you've never seen one of those, here's a picture:

http://www.applelinks.com/news/gifs/indigotent.jpg

> One other not really  totally iBook question, what other applications 
> can I
> use to open gif files, besides QuickTime Player?

Your browser will display them, or Preview, or Appleworks. There's also 
the popular shareware GraphicConverter, pretty much any art program 
(like Photoshop) and dozens of others. I personally use a program 
called JView to quickly display JPEG and GIF files in Mac OS X. I 
assume that's what you are using.
