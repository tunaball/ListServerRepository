According to MacMinute...

"Apple expands iBook logic board repair program

June 21 - 12:04 EDT   Apple updated its iBook Logic Board Repair 
Extension Program to include iBook models manufactured between May 2001 
and October 2003. The program, which was launched in January, covers 
repair or replacement of the logic board in iBook models that are 
experiencing specific component failures. The program originally 
included iBooks manufactured between May 2002 and April 2003. In April, 
it was expanded to include iBooks made between May 2002 and October 
2003."
