Norm_M wrote:

> I have my main computer (iMac) and an iBook using airport connected to
> COMCAST. I use a router. COMCAST  Cable coming in goes to their modem,
> then to the router. I plug my imac into the router and my Airport base
> into the router.

Norm, I have a similar setup. However, since the Airport base station 
IS a router, couldn't you skip a step and get rid of your separate 
router, and plug your iMac directly into the base station? That's what 
I've done - one airport card in my iBook so I can go wireless, and my 
daughter's iBook (or any other computer) connected by an ethernet cable 
into the base station's LAN port.
