On 2004-06-14 19:44, Brian wrote:

> I have a hard drive that is dieing a slow painfull death.  I have
> backed up the important stuff.
> 
> The actual replacement is not an issue and I am set up to mount the new
> and old one at the same time.  But for the replacement I would like to
> just duplicate the current drive to the new one. Any suggestions on the
> best way to go about this.

- Connect both drives
- Start up from your Mac OS X 10.3 CD
- Quit the OS installer
- Run Disk Utility
- Select your existing drive
- Go to the Restore tab
- Drag your existing disk to the Source field
- Drag your new disk to the Destination field
- Click restore
