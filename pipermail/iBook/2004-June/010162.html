  Apple is not supported = Their training process does not include 
writing a script for their script readers (oops, highly trained tech 
staff) who answer the phones.  It also means that the setup disc they

Sams club has linksys wireless routers (b only) and a wireless card 
(laptop or desktop package) for $69.  You tell comcast you are using 1 
computer, get the router and plug in up to 4 computers into that... 
wait, need more?? plug in a hub into the router and it will support up 
to 250 computers.
