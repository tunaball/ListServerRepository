I replaced the CD-ROM drive in my iBook 500 with an old Lombard/Pismo 
DVD-ROM reader (SR-8174) I had laying about. Each different mechanism 
uses different faceplate mounting points, so of course the CD reader's 
face doesn't fit the 'new' DVD drive. I have a generic faceplate on it 
for now but it looks a tad errrrr . . . bogus. :-p

Is there any list/source/page/whatever that details the mechanisms used 
in the various iBooks? I'd find and order an appropriate Apple face to 
fit the mechanism I have if only I knew one was out there that would fit.

The CD-ROM I pulled is a Matsushita CR-177D, same basic jobbie as used in 
the Lombard's CD-ROM EBM (CR-176.)

If there isn't such a list already, I'd be glad to put one together if 
folks would take just a moment to open System Profiler, get their 
optical's model number and either post it here or send it to me at the 
address (macdan at comcast dot net) I use for this list. I expect to also 
put up other relevant details about the swap. Clamshell owner's info 
welcomed too, I have a Blueberry 400 (neé 300) with a Matshita UJDA-330 
CD-RW installed, which could also use a proper face.

Oh BTW, the iBook 500 w/DVD drive works great (as of course expected.) I 
also happen to have (again, just laying about :-) a combo DVD/CD-RW 
Matshita UJDA-730 with the same faceplate mounting points which is 
destined for our snow 700. I'm hoping to get a proper face for that one 
though _before_ I do the install.
