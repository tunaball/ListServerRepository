Hi Jim,

It is quite possible, but not certain that your KPs are caused by your airport/bluetooth card.
 It just happened to me and replacing the card corrected the problem. Here is the link to the iFixit instructions to replace the card.

http://www.ifixit.com/Guide/Repair/Installing-iBook-G4-12-Inch-1-33-GHz-AirPort-Bluetooth-Board/707/1

After you remove the existing card, slide your battery under the frame and push it in place to connect it and jump the tiny gold pins carefully on the logic board power socket just briefly to power it on. Notice if your KP issue persists. If it powers on normally, then replace the card. Make sure the new one is clicked firmly into place, pushing down on  the card directly over the connector. 

Good Luck!

Randy
