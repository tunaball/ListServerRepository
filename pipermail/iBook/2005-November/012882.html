Hi Tim

My husband's G3 600 ibook just had the exact same problem.  He thought it
was probably a loose or pinched wire, and disassembled it using the guide at
http://www.pbfixit.com/Guide/50.0.0.html

Nothing was obviously wrong with any of the wiring - he rerouted the wires
slightly differently through the hinge, and on reassembly, the display came
back.  The only other thing that he did was to unplug all of the connectors
from the screen to the logic board, one of which was the display connector.

Hope this is of some help!  It's certainly interesting to hear of someone
else having this problem.
