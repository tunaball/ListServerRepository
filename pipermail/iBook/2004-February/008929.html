If you want a nice system monitor have a look at iPulse, slick little 
apps that provides you with all info on what processes are running, 
memory, and general info. This is shareware and for a reasonable amount 
is well worth it. iPulse uses skins and there are lots out there that 
you can download or you can download icon factory and make your own.
