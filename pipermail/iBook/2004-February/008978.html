I've come late to this discussion so you may have mentioned this 
already but I wondered if you had considered FaxCenter which also came 
out as one of the early fax options for OSX?

http://www.macadvocacy.com/faxcenter.htm

I think Ben started it as a college project and it has been a work in 
progress with regular bug fixes and feature updates.  It is certainly 
better than Apple's poor excuse for a faxing functionality, costs $10 
and in the interests of maintaining low blood pressure I will not even 
start on Faxstf...

I used to use FaxExpress (in UK) but now I use FaxCenter (with Panther 
and Jaguar) and find it pretty good and getting better.
