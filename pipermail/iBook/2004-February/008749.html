Yeah, that would certainly have a futuristic look and feel to it, but 
two things come to mind about this: First, if you turn the lid so it 
faces the sun, then so are you--with the sun right in your face. 
Second, personally, I find it almost impossible to see the screen when 
out in direct sunlight. Maybe one of those dark clothes like old-time 
photographers used to use might help, but I think it would be mighty 
hot under there. If the solar panel were separate, then you could stay 
in the shade and the panel could bake in the sun.
