Beware:
I just had a long discussion with our apple care rep and she said that 
Apple is not considering the battery to be something that is covered 
under warranty.  (this is a new change in policy or procedure that was 
very poorly introduced)   If its bought as dead, they "should" replace 
it  but if you have had your laptop for a while (even just a few 
months), Apple will balk at replacing the battery under warranty since 
its something that will eventually die.

This is a HUGE issue with many schools as they have 100s of laptops and 
find bad or failing batteries more often.  The Apple care rep does not 
want to replace the battery because Apple will not reimburse the apple 
care rep for that.
