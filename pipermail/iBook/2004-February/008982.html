I've used eFax for years, first with their free, receive-only service, 
and more recently with the paid one, which includes a local area code 
and outgoing faxes from the computer. I'm fortunate to have it, too, 
since my fax line decided to get very weird and somehow (nobody has 
been able to figure out how) interferes with my office line. The paid 
eFax account is $12.95 a month, plus 10 cents an outgoing page. So, 
it's not exactly cheap, and will quickly outstrip the costs of a fax 
machine. If you need the ability to get and send faxes while on the 
move, though, it's indespensable.
