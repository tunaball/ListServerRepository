VST used to make an external battery charger for the clamshell. I have 
one I bought off their site as a refurb, it wasn't terribly expensive 
if I recall. The model # is SCHB if that helps searching for one. It is 
a cradle that will charge 2 batteries at once. ( It uses your power 
"brick" so that has to be good.) The power manager circuit in this is 
different from what is built into the laptop and will resurrect a 
marginal battery that won't charge in the machine.
I know swapping batteries on the clamshell is no fun but if you are not 
doing it constantly it might be worthwhile. Also it might bring your 
batteries back to life.
Just a thought.
