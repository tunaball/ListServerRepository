In regards to alternative wireless stations, I went with a D-Link 614+ 
station for our network. It was very inexpensive costing new from 
Office Depot $39 after sale price and rebates. It was painless to set 
up and only took minutes. My iBook immediately saw the station as soon 
as I switched on the Airport card. My wife uses a Dell with a D-Link 
card without many problems. One funny thing though, my Airport card 
never misses or loses the station while the Dell with the D-link card 
sometimes does. The Airport also seems faster even though the D-link 
card is the x2 type at 22 kbs while the Airport is the original 11 kbs. 
I assume the differences have to do with Win XP in her computer.

Hope this helps.
