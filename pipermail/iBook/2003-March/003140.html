Well, as it turns out, and the reason why it only started happening 
when I was on dialup, was that sendmail was working, except it was 
trying to send mail through the ethernet port, although I was dialed in 
via a modem.

After disabling the ethernet port, everything worked (I don't have a 
network at home anymore anyway).
