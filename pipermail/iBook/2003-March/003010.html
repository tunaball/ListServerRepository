> In the last few weeks I have heard plenty about the new iMovie and 
> iPhoto ..
> But NOTHING about the new iDVD ...

I've used it to redo my bio lab dvds.  The new themes are nice, simple 
and not overly distracting.  I used the one with the movie projector 
for class since it reminded me of the horrors of watching celluloid 
movies in grade school (and who of you were on the av squad?).  It is 
so easy to find your movies, photos and music with the integration with 
the other iApps.  Well worth the full $49 purchase price (course as an 
educator it was only $19.95 with keynote).

The only improvement would be to swap my 2x 04 dvd burner with the new 
4x 05.  Well, I could just get the drive and put it in my old 466 G4, 
along with a dual processor upgrade, of course!
