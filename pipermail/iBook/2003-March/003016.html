There is not, nor will there EVER be, a "trick" to booting OS 9 in a 
machine that can't. I mean, sure, you could redesign you own 
motherboard, located old ROM chips and add them to the mix, write your 
own BIOS drivers, move over all the other components and SOMEHOW make 
it work maybe ... but you'd be redefining "fool's errand" in the 
process.

It's a hardware thing. It ain't going to happen. PARTICULARLY when we 
move to 64-bit Real Soon Now.

Time to move on.
