>If you have the proper cable for your model of iBook (there are 
>slightly different cables for the "clamshell" and "icebook" models), 
>everything should work fine. What will happen is when you plug the 
>A/V cable into your iBook, the monitor signal will automatically be 
>sent out through that A/V port (and your screen should go blank, so 
>you'll want to be sure you're hooked up to a TV first), and you can 
>start up the DVD player from there. I used to do the same thing, 
>before I had a DVD player.
>
>(But note that as far as I know, this only works with OS 9.)
