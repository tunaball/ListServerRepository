The 12" ibook may not be as fast as the G4 powerbook but one has to ask 
the question: "Why do I have an portable computer?"

If the ibook is an additional, portable computer and one has an 
adequate desktop model, then the ibook is a very acceptable choice.

If one is only to own a single computer,, then the powerbook would be 
the way to go.
