Assuming you have all the proper settings in your Airport Base Station 
setup you still need to:

Create a location and select the airport option. Fill in all the info 
in the tabs. Check show the airport in the menu bar.
 From the Airport Menu:
Turn Airport On
Choose the base station you want to use, it must be check marked.
Choose Connect

Or choose Open Internet Connect and proceed.
