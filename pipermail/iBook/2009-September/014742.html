The DVI output from MacBook or any Apple laptop does not carry sound,   
DVI can input sound to your TV.

However it requires the proper conversion and I do not believe you can  
do it from most laptops.

I just plug in some external speakers and it sounds good. If you are  
using a multi media system you can plug the audio out into one of the  
inputs there are RCA to mini jack cables available.
