  Have you tried booting from the MacBook disc with the iBook in target disk mode? This may not work anyway, as the MacBook has an Intel version of OS X, and the iBook will need the PPC version of OS X.


  You may need to contact someone from the MUG with a PPC Mac and install it from there onto your iBook in target disk mode.
