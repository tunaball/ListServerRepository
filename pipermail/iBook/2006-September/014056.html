Hi Steve,

I had a Dual USB iBook (G3 600) when I bought my USB/Firewire external
hard drive.  In my experience, the iBook's USB ports were underpowered
for what was needed to boot up the drive.  I ended up having to buy a
powered USB hub in order to make it work.  I didn't mind, as the hub
was cheap and it's always handy to have a few more ports at one's
disposal.

I now have G4 Powerbook, and it still needs the external hub to power
the external HD.
