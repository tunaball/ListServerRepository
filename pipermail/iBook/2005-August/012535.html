Hi

Jumping on the bandwagon of iBook sleeves...

I've just bought a Crumpler sleeve for my iBook G4 and it's definitely not 
as nice as the Tucano one I had before. I just wanted a change even though I 
was happy with the Tucano ones to be honest. I'd emailed Tucano about how 
waterproof the sleeve was but hadn't received a reply. However the Crumpler 
one advertises itself as waterproof so I went ahead. It really doesn't seem 
to be that waterproof as the zip area seems not to close to tightly around 
the iBook and isn't waterproof - they mean splashproof.

I've just received an email from Tucano - "Our second skin cases are water 
resistant as long as they are not submerged completley under water as they 
may let some water in through the zipper."

So, if anyone's interested, get a Tucano one!

Matt
