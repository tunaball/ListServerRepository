How often do you run system utilities like Cocktail? The other thing  
that often causes this kind of problem os corrupt fonts. If you do  
not own something like Font Doctor you can download a demo (http:// 
www.fontdoctor.com/with_fl/index.html). The demo will not fix the  
fonts but it will show you if there are bad fonts. Also, You may  
first try trashing the iPhoto preferences. These preferences may have  
become corrupted.
