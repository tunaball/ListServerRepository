I'm not using it with my iBook, but I have a MicroSoft wireless  
optical mouse that works fine with my desktop Mac. It has two buttons  
and a scroll wheel, both of which work fine with all the apps running  
in Tiger. Right button works the same as "Control-click" and brings  
up contextual menus and the like. Since going to two-buttons, I now  
miss that capability when going to a one-button mouse (never thought  
I would say that). No drivers to install or anything. I just plugged  
in the receiver in a USB port, put batteries in the thing, and away  
it went.
