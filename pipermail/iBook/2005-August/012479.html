I have a Logitech Cordless Mouseman Optical.  It's a wireless two-button 
mouse with a scroll wheel, and it has a receiver that plugs into a 
computer's USB port.  I've never tried it with an iBook, but I've used 
it with my Power Mac G4 and it works great without any special drivers 
installed whatsoever.

So, to the original poster that was wondering about using the Logitech 
mouse with an iBook, I second the recommendation to simply give it a shot!
