hotel networks are funny.  Make sure it's not only configured to be  
automatic, but make sure that you have no DNS servers or anything in  
there.  Open up your webbrowser, and sometimes they will have some  
kind of authentication or something to register the MAC with the hotel.

Hotels are strange.
