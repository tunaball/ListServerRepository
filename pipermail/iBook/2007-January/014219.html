David Harris wrote:
> 
> My family bought me a GoGear 30 Gb Jukebox.  Anyone have any luck using 
> this with a Mac?

If it uses MTP/Plays for Sure, it may not work straightforwardly. You'll 
probably be okay if it uses standard USB mass storage (that is, it 
appears as a drive on the desktop).

The only MTP implementation I know of for OS X is XNJB, 
<http://www.wentnet.com/projects/xnjb/>.
