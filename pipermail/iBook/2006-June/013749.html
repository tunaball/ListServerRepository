I've recently has an odd experience with my iMac G5 where I was  
experiencing display failures, and Apple Hardware Test indicated an  
error with the Video RAM -- but then the problem disappeared on its'  
own, and a later test indicated no problems. Thanks to a screenshot  
of display corruption I had taken, though, it was eventually  
diagnosed as a probliem with the logic board, which is now being  
replaced.

I had no idea there were serious problems that could fly under the  
radar of Apple Hardware Test (but then, the iMac is the first Mac  
I've had *any* repair issues with).
