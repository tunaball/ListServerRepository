Most any external drive should work.  I would go ahead and get a 2  
layer DVD-RW drive, since they are getting less expensive all the time.

By the way, an internal may not be as expensive as you think.  Tech  
Restore has a pretty good overnight service.  I haven't had a failed  
hard drive myself, but I have heard good things about them.  Parts  
and service are a little over 200 bucks, which is about 75 dollars or  
so above and beyond buying an external drive or an internal drive and  
USB/firewire case.

http://www.techrestore.com/xcart/product.php? 
productid=16259&cat=290&kbid=1028&page=1
