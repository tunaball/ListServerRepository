I found the instructions on iFixit: http://www.ifixit.com/Guide/ 
49.4.0.html

Once you have the keyboard separated, you need to remove your Airport  
card (if installed), then loosen the screws on the RAM shield and  
remove it. After that, the keyboard connector is exposed and you can  
disconnect it.
