On 30 May 2006, at 22:46, António Matoso-Ferreira wrote:

> Thanks for your advise. I can see under "ATA" in  System Profiler I 
> see the optical driver.  I would like to ask you , what I shall do 
> after starting in safe mode?

In Safe Mode, put a CD in and see if it will be read. If it is, then it 
is a problem with the Epson software, as bluekatt said, and not the 
optical drive itself.

> After switch off the computer can I start again as usually?

If you just restart the computer with all the software loaded, and it 
is a problem with the software, the problem will return!
