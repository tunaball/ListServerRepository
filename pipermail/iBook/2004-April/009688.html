Try resetting the PRAM and PMU:
http://www.info.apple.com/kbnum/n2238 (Let it boot three times)
http://www.info.apple.com/kbnum/n14449

Also try resetting the NVRAM in Open Firmware:

http://www.info.apple.com/kbnum/n42642

    1.  Start up into Open Firmware by pressing and holding the 
Command-Option-O-F key combination during startup.
    2. At the Open Firmware prompt, type: reset-nvram
    3. Press Return.
    4. At the Open Firmware prompt, type: reset-all
    5. Press Return.

The reset-all command should restart your Mac. If so, you have 
successfully reset the Open Firmware settings.
