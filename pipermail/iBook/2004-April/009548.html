There's a very good program called VLC (video lan client), that is free 
(GPL to be exact ;-).

I think it does what you want, and on top of that it can play DVDs 
(ignoring region encoding) and can rip and encode them too, as well as 
being able to stream video across a network. It has plugins for QT, avi 
and divx, and I'm sure there are others too.

All in all, it's a great piece of software. Enjoy!

see www.videolan.org

Cheers,
-Angus
