Have you tried the Image Capture utility?  Go into your apps, there should be
one called Image Capture.  Plug in your camera, open the application, and it
should detect your camera.  Then click on "Download All" to download everything
from the camera to wherever you choose to download, or click on "Download Some"
to choose what you want to download.  You should be able to pull off the mpgs
this way off the camera.
