Thanks to everyone that responded.  Of course the solution was simply a 
matter of thinking in a logical fashion, and having someone else do 
that thinking for me ;) .  In case others have this problem and cannot 
find the solution here it is:

I went to system preferences on my iBook, and changed my IP manually to 
the same range as the AP, ie. 192.168.1.xxx (it had been using DHCP on 
a slightly different range, from my router).  I was immediately able to 
get to the admin page, and log in.

Thanks again, and thanks to Paul Kurtz especially for taking the time 
to give such a thorough breakdown of the problem.
