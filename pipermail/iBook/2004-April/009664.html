on 25/04/04 17:18, Paul Bernhardt at pbern10 at xmission.com wrote:

> I advise the following: If the iBook serial number is 'in the range'
> *don't* get the Apple Care. Apple is already committed to fixing all
> those machines (check to see how much longer the program runs, if there
> is a time limit).

They will fix them ONLY for the acknowledged video problem, and only for
three years from the date of purchase - same as Apple Care.

If you have another fault the RER won't cover it.

>If the serial number is not in the range, but is a G3
> Powerbook with a serial number before the range, or not long after it,
> do get the Apple Care.

I have seen video problems on earlier iceBooks, but only certain ones (think
it is 700Mhz onwards) are covered by the REP.
