Here's the conclusions of 45 minutes of conversation with Applecare 
staff.
A) yes, the actual Techtool 3 does give unwanted messages to iBook G4 
owners (as well as G5 owners I'm being told)
B) only the Deluxe version is tied to Applecare
C) iBook G4 owners can request Techtool 4 when the "Deluxe" version 
will be available
