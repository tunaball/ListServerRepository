Before you give up on the info on your HD, try firewire target
disk mode on the computer:  basically, power it off; connect
a firewire cable to a running Mac; power on while holding
down the "T" key.  See
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=58583
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=75414
