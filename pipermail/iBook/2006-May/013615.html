Paul,

The bong might be considered a good sign. My suggestion would be to
borrow an external hard drive (Firewire) that has a copy of OS-X
installed on it and see if it will boot up.

Another idea would be to connect the iBook to another Mac via a
Firewire cable. Using the "T" key. This could show if the hard drive
on the iBook is broken.

Possibly the drive is damaged.

I am writing to you off the iBook list. I see that you have a Quebec
email address.

an Apple Techie check it out. Call Microserv in Dorval, Quebec and ask
for Tony. He is the head of their service department. He is honest.

Microserv's telephone number is 514-636-5127

it for parts on eBay or you could pass it on to me. :-)
