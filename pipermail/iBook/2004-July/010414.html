> I picked up a VST battery charger in hopes of reviving a couple 
> batteries
> for an original (clamshell) iBook. It's giving me a series of red 
> flashing
> lights. One battery flashes 6 times, the other 8 times.

A red light (two or more quick flashes) indicates a battery error. (I 
think you're out of luck.)

Solid yellow light means a battery is charging; a flashing yellow light 
means that a battery is waiting for the other to be finished to charge. 
(The charger holds two batteries, but only charges one at a time.)

A quick flashing green light means the charging cycle is complete.
