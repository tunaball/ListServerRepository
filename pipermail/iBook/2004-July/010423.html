I'd like to suggest something - Cyberguys <http://cyberguys.com> sells 
a switch that allows the switching of a monitor between two computers 
for something like $20 - it's VGA and you provide your own cables (or 
get theirs).  It's not quite a KVM switch, as it only has the V part, 
but it's relatively cheap.

I've dealt with these guys a lot (I order all my ethernet cables, USB 
cables, and other goodies from them), and usually order over the phone 
(they have a catalog, as well).  If you want a real KVM switch, they 
have those, too.

We have that same setup to share a monitor between the Power Mac G4 and 
my iBook 600 when I want to mirror it.
