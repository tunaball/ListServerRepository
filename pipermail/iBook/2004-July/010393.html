My girlfriend just got a pair of Etymotics ER6 earplug type headphones, and she
likes them alot.  She got them for plane trips, and that many people like how
they seal out sound, but they also have a high fidelity sound.  However, I do
have to admit when I tried them, I was a little weirded out by how far they
have to go in to achieve full affect.  But I think with a little bit of time,
you'll grow used to it.  At 97 bucks, they aren't cheap, but they aren't as
expensive as the Bose either.
