> My question is, what options do I have?  Should I sell it for parts?
> Should I look for a replacement logicboard myself?  Where are good
> places to order replacement logic boards?

  suggestions
1. check with macresq.com about the repair cost. They have a 24 hour 
turnaround repair time for 'books. they could give you an estimate and 
a reccommendation

2. check with smalldog.com for repair vs sell for parts 
recommendationa. They also sell refurbs, maybe you could work out some 
kind of swap deal that would be cheaper than out right purchase if 
selling for parts is the only option

3. if your problem is the backlight in the display burned out, smalldog 
will sell the part for apx $20 and you can find links to the directions 
for replacing it in the ibook list archives.  Earlier this year in the 
Mar -April time frame I think it was a couple of people on this list 
ran into the backlight failure after the warranty had expired and they 
replaced the failed parts themselves.
