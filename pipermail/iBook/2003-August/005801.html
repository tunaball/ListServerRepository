I just answered my own question:  press the option (alt) and Apple 
keys, then click on the listing's icon (not text), drag it wherever. 
The answer was in iCab's preferences (General - check "sort lists 
with alt + command"). Guess my toe was over the foul line when I 
rolled the ball.
