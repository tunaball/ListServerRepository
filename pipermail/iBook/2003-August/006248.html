Don,

I can't think of any disruptions that should cause. They are just 
files. However, what I don't know is whether iphoto will pick up the 
new images into its library automatically just by placing the photo in 
the proper iphoto location. You may still have to "import" each photo 
to get it to appear in the library. Then again, maybe you weren't 
expecting it to work like this anyway. :-) Perhaps a more knowledgeable 
iphoto user can say for sure?

Once you're convinced all is correct on the imac (and you have a good 
backup!), then you  can safely delete the photos on the ibook.

Hope that helps,
Barry
