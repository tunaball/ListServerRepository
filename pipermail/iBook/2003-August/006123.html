On Sunday, August 24, 2003, at 10:34  AM, Chris Beaumont wrote:

> Does anybody know what might be causing a (suddenly) dead display on a 
> somewhat pristine 8 month old ibook?

Apple does and you should be on the phone to support discussing your 
problem. They will send you box to repair the pinched video cable (most 
likely the problem) or whatever other problem it might be. Since your 
book is 8 months old you should consider purchasing AppleCare if you 
intend to keep it for several years, or sell it now (after Apple fixes 
it) if you intend to upgrade.

Everyone who has a new book should simply call Apple when these 
problems arise. This saves your time and gets your book fixed faster. 
Asking what to do only extends the time until it is fixed.
