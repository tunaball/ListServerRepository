Netscape 7 (IIRC) has an importer that can take your mail and address 
book from older version of NS, Eudora, Outlook etc etc and convert then 
to NS7 format. Just start up NS7 and open up the mail component. Then 
click on Tools and choose Import...  You should be fine from there.

For bookmarks open up the bookmarks manager and choose Tools then 
Import. Select your old bookmarks.html file from NS4 and it should bring 
them all in.

Hope this helps,
