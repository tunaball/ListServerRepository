It's Y-ZERO-K. That's when all the record-keeping switched over from 
counting the years down in BC (A.K.A. "BCE") to counting up in AD 
(A.K.A. "CE"). It's a joke, since it wasn't until 700 years later that 
we started counting the years that way.
