I vote we start the new era the day the G5 hits the streets. although a 
good argument could be made to start the era the day the G5 was 
unveiled. For true historical accuracy, it might be best to use its 
conception date. But like with all conception dates, that might be 
subject to argument depending on how conception is defined.

In all reality, I think those of us who were here for the Dawn of the 
G5 will look back one day in the not too distant future and realize 
just how much our world changed with the G5. I think it will be very 
hard for anyone from here on to try and crack the Reality Distortion 
Field simply because those who would try can't even see it, let alone 
understand what they are seeing. Rather, they will continue to ride 
their horse and buggies while the rest of use use the new fangled 
highway and the new fangled transportation.

Even Wall Street is grudgingly beginning to get it.
