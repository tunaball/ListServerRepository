Interesting to read about all the trouble people have had syncing their 
palms to their macs. I guess I am one one the lucky ones then (I'm not 
complaining).  I used an old HP Jornada for years with my PC at work. 
When it finally died, I bought a Palm Tungsten T with Bluetooth. I use 
the cradle on my PC at work, and a Bluetooth adapter on my iBook at 
home and sync both computers to the Palm without trouble. And of 
course, the Bluetooth adapter uses a USB  port. The only complaints I 
have are that Bluetooth is pretty slow, and isync does some strange 
things, so I tend to avoid it when syncing to the Palm. It's OK for 
syncing bookmarks, address book to idisk, etc.

Just thought I would share my experience.

Barry
