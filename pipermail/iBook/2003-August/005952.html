I bought a 450 Mhz DP G4 machine around three years ago for $1200, and 
although I have gotten my use out of it, its now too slow to run most 
modern apps and OSX adequately..

YMMV, but I would keep in mind that you will probably end up having to 
get an upgrade card for it and figure that into the price when 
comparing it to a new machine..  Considering that the current owner 
probably paid over $2k for it, they rarely are willing to accept what 
the thing is really worth..IMO ~ $500, or maybe a bit more if it has a 
Super Drive..

Apple is still selling G4s - they cost around $1200.

But with the 64 bit G5 soon to be available for around $1700, all of 
the older family of hardware is suddenly worth significantly less. 
Unless you have some strong reason to run OS9 natively the new machines 
are a better bet..
