If your 14 is less than one year old, you still have full warranty 
coverage although whether a broken nib qualifies is unknown.

AppleCare is only needed for hardware problems for year 2 and year 3. 
During year 1 it only offers software support above the normal warranty.

So, if you are still under the 1 year warranty call Apple and see what 
they will do for you. And you can consider buying AppleCare if you are 
still under the 1 year warranty.
