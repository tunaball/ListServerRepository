Yeah. The mac version of windows media player only plays streams (and 
compatible with few versions of them at that) not local files/streams.

If you still want to play it one word - mplayer. 
(http://mplayerosx.sourceforge.net/). Virtually Guaranteed to play all 
.avi and .asf movie files in existence (thanks to open-source!). It 
definitely has better support then the M$ player, and don't even bother 
with quicktime for these files.

The skinny is - .avi and .asf are windows media formats, and Microsoft 
has pretty much no support for them on the mac.
