I was able to do this with Pacifist. 

Follow the instructions in this apple doc
(http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=42995) to get to the
particular package.

In my case, I was trying to downgrade from a preview version of iMovie
HD back to iMovie4 and the regular installer kept telling me that I
could not load iMovie4 because a newer version was already installed,
even though I had already deleted iMovie HD and the preferences.

Pacifist (look for it at versiontracker.com) was able to do the
install for me. It's a beautiful thing...
