I have been a happy owner of both iBooks and PowerBooks, but my current
machine is the latter.  To compare the 12" base models, for 50% or $500
more, the PB gets you:

+0.3 GHz processor
+256 MB RAM
+30 GB hard drive
+32 MB video memory
Bluetooth 2.0
DVI out with dual display capability and 20-23" Cinema Display compatibility
SuperDrive (DVD-R, +R, -RW, +RW) upgradeable
minus AppleWorks
shorter Airport range
hotter to the touch

and "objectively" :)
better keyboard
better fit and finish
