Hello Angus, I got my 1gb shuffle about two weeks ago
and I love it.  I had a 10gb iPod already but ive been
using my new shuffle alot more just because its so
light and small, its great for the gym or any time I
want to take some tunes with me when I go to the store
or whatever , I still grab my 10gb model when I want
to listen to my tunes in my truck. I have a stereo in
my truck that has a audio jack and I plug in my 10gb
and have my whole music library in the palm of my hand
, but when im on the go and away from me truck I reach
for my shuffle. I dont miss the screen at all , I like
to listen to it in shuffle mode , its like having a
radio station that plays all your favorite songs
without any commercials . And my little shuffle sounds
every bit as good and loud as my 10gb model.  In short
go get one you wont regret it.

Cheers, Rob
