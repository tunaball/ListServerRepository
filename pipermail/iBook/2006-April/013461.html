On Apr 9, 2006, at 6:57 PM, Larry Kollar wrote:

>
> Jim Manley wrote:
>
>> I want to teach my bird to talk so I want to make a recording on  
>> my Ibook to repeat over & over for an hour or 2 for him to listen  
>> to while I am out.
>>
>> How is the best way to do this on my Ibook?
>
> You can use Audacity (audacity.sf.net) to make the recording and  
> export it as MP3. Once you've done that, set up a playlist in  
> iTunes containing only the recording(s) you want to use to train  
> your bird, put it in loop mode, and hit Play.
>
> You'll also have to use the Energy Saver settings in System  
> Preferences to keep the iBook from sleeping.
>
> There are probably fancier ways to do this, but this one doesn't  
> cost you anything. ;-)
