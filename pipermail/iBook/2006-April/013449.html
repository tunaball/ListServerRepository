Ron Reames wrote:

> I have a G4 iBook, a little over a year old.
> The battery only charges now to 94%. Is this normal?

You might have to recalibrate the battery charger -- sometimes, it's  
as easy as letting the battery run down to where the iBook sleeps,  
then plugging in the power supply.

If you've already done that, try the calibration instructions from  
the archives:
http://listserver.themacintoshguy.com/pipermail/ibook/2004-January/ 
008216.html

Hope one of those help!
