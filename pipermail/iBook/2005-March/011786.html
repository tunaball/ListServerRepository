Hi Germaine

I've a G3 466 firewire clamshell which had OS9. It was quite slow so moved 
up to OSX 10.2 (Jaguar). It was quicker operating but really slow on 
startup. 10.3 (Panther) is a lot, lot faster on startup. It does have lot 
more than 196 ram though...

If you do move up to Jaguar or Panther don't forget to get rid of 
localisations that you don't need cos it saved just under 1GB. I used 
Macaroni.

Matt
