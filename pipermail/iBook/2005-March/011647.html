Got MacAlly's BTMicro wireless bluetooth optical mouse today and am very
pleased.  Smaller than Apple's, two buttons, scroll wheel, and very
responsive.  Best yet: it has it's own self-charging cradle.  The prospect
of frequent battery replacement, and even more so, the nagging "should I or
shouldn't I" doubt on replacing the batteries, has held me off from wireless
mice up to this point.  With this one, just plop it into the no-worry,
hassle-free cradle when not in use.  Only issue for me is it's rather loud
click.  Standard disclaimer of no connection to the company.
