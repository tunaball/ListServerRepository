Hi

I've TheMouseBT and I find that it sometimes wakes my iBook from sleep. It's 
a weird problem because it occurs about 10% of the time and doesn't happen 
every time in the same login.

I've tried to identify the culprit but all I've found to solve the problem 
is the remove my bluetooth dongle or logout and in again. It's especially 
disconcerting when sometimes I close the lid and the ibook keeps waking up 
and going to sleep again despite the lid being closed (it does seem to give 
up eventally and stay asleep!)

I certainly don't get the same problem with my bluetooth keyboard...

BTW - any views on BT mouse out there? I'm quite disappointed with the 
performance of my MacMice one. It's can be very erratic even on normal

mouse buttons that it shouldn't have and I end up with exposé jumping around 
the window in front of me!

Have you tested to see if the problem persists with a wired mouse? On my 600
