I have 2 Clamshell 300's with 160mb of ram and a 10 gig HD, and they 
run Jaguar great.  The only drawback to the system is only 800x600

bottom.  Other than that it works great.  I am running Panther on my 
333 Lombard, but it is noticeably slower than the clamshell with 
Jaguar.
