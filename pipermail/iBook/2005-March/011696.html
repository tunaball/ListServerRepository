PS. what I did was buy an external firewire HD and put all my music on
that, because I had already bought the iBook and upgrading was going
to be a huge hassle.  The tech guy at the Mac store suggested that a
fast HD in a firewire enclosure would actually be faster than an
upgraded  internal  HD in the iBook.  I still say you should do it now
while you've got the chance.
