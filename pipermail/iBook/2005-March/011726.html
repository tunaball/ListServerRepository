Hi

Two things:

1) if the "allow devices to wake from sleep" option is greyed out then your 
bluetooth software is not up to date. go to the apple website and download 
the bits at

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=120275
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=120276

or here if you're on jaguar still

http://www.apple.com/support/downloads/bluetoothupdaterformacosx1028.html

however, you can only update later versions of the Dlink BT dongle


2) i have the wake from sleep option active and it does work well sometimes. 
however, sometime it goes into this weird cycle of waking and then sleeping 
again, i assume as a result of erratic mouse behaviour. anyone else 
experience this?
