> Since yesterday, I am the proud owner of a ibook G3 900 14 inch. I was 
> wondering if it is at all possible to have the lid closed while an 
> external monitor is connected and a mouse and keyboard is connected to 
> the usb port. I have tried various sequences, but wasn't successful.

You can do this via a software hack, but the iBook is not really 
designed to be run in this way; it doesn't have adequate ventallation 
while closed, and the exposure to excess heat could cause component 
failures, or at least shorten the life of the components. (And, of 
course, using it would void your warranty, so if anything goes wrong, 
you could be stuck.)
