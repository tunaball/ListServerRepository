Zoom is nice because it can get you in on the action, however when it comes
to optical vs. digital, go for the optical (lens, usually
detachable/interchangeable doing the zooming) over digital (camera's chip
doing the zooming). Digital zoom can tend to fuzz out somewhat - which
granted, if you're doing family and fun stuff isn't really a problem but if
you're looking to do professional quality stuff, can make a difference.
Something else to consider - look for something at least 3.2 megapixels (I
think that #'s right, may want to double check me on that one!) in order to
take pics that can print at 8x10.
