> -rw------T   1 root  wheel  67108864  5 May 07:02 swapfile0
>
> what I don't know, is whether it's good or "bad" to have a 6 MG 
> swapfile like that?

If you just have one swap file, then you certainly can't delete it. 
Even if you have several, and some *might* be leftover, I'd say that 
you should regard these as untouchable unless you *really* know what 
you're doing. The operating system is your friend - don't dork with it, 
and it won't roll over and cough up stuff when you're counting on it to 
do work.
