I was not too clear on my first post. It does not have to be an  
attachment but could be a corrupt email itself.

Limiting the size of the message that is downloaded (Accounts/tools/ 
options)will sometimes stop the corrupt portion from crashing entourage.

If that works you download the remainder of each message individually  
to find the corrupt email.
