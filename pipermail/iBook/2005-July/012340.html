> I am thinking of purchasing a new 12" iBook for the family.  What  
> is the difference between the combo drive and the super drive?  The  
> apple store website does not explain.

The "Combo Drive" can read and write to CD-ROM and CD-RW discs, and  
read DVD disc formats -- the "Super Drive" can also burn certain  
types of DVD discs.
