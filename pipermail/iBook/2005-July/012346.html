On Jul 14, 2005, at 1:57 AM, David Harris wrote:

> I'm trying to install OS 10.4 onto my iBook 700 Mhz CD-ROM with my  
> external CD-RW/DVD-ROM drive but i cannot get the iBook to reboot  
> to continue the install.  How do I get the iBook to boot from the  
> external CD-RW/DVD-ROM drive?

David, I'm going to assume your external drive is USB based and if  
that's true, it cannot be used to boot the computer. You have two  
choices. First, contact Apple to exchange the Tiger DVD for CDs. The  
cost is about $10. Second, if you have access to a computer that has  
an internal DVD you can use FireWire target mode.
