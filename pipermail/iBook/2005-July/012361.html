I am using Virex 7.2.1 with Tiger with no problem. 7.2.1 is the older  
version and is primarily a manually operated antivirus app.   It has  
no background scans.   Version 7.5 has had a lot of problems with OS  
X, and Apple dropped Virex from its .Mac program. There is a brand  
new Virex out I think, but have no idea if it is truly Tiger compatible.
