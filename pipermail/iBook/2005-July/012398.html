Don't know if this is applicable, but a few months ago my 15" AlBook began
suddenly getting very hot. So hot that I had to turn it into a desktop
because there was no way I could work with it on my lap. Someone recommended
that I check Activity Monitor (I'm in Tiger), I opened it, found a mystery
application that was literally "burning up" a very big chunk of my CPU
usage, I force quit it, and I've never had the overheating problem again. I
don't remember what the app was, but it couldn't have been very important,
as everything worked much better without it.
