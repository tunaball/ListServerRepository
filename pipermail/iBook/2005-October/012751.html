I recently conducted a service seminar using powerpoint presentation  
from my 933 dual usb 14 ibook and a toshiba LCD projector with no  
problems.
The presentation contained text, PDF files from AutoCad 2002Lt (using  
virtual PC) and pictures imported through Iphoto.

I used the stock fade transitions from powerpoint, It was my first  
attempt with powerpoint and my ibook and was very impressed.

Hope this helps.
