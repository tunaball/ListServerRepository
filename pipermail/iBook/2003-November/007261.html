It has been just about a year since I moved from Entourage to Mail so 
my memory is a bit fuzzy -- heck it is fuzzy about what I had for

Anyhow, as I recall, I just started up in Mail and went to File>Import 
Mailboxes.  The top choice is Microsoft Entourage, it went smoothly 
from there.  The only "difficulty" is that all your old posts come 
through as unread.  Just highlight all of them and do Message>Mark As 
Read. As always, make a backup of your Entourage info before you start
