Similar story with my Dell Latitude CPi300 laptop. When I obtained 
it, it's screen only displayed a 400 by 600 pixel window; there were 
no other options in the display control panel. It took me four hours 
to locate a driver which worked to enlarge the window to its full 700 
by 1100 (about) screen size. Both Microsoft and Dell couldn't decide 
what the driver was to be called:  screen, display, video, colors, 
monitor, and other terms were all used interchangeably and 
confusingly. I finally took a shot in the dark, lucked out and 
downloaded a driver cryptically labelled "Neomagic Video Driver for 
NM2200 version A06" which enlarged the window to the full screen 
size. I have other horror stories about long efforts identifying and 
finding the correct drivers and other basic software for my various 
PCs in order to get them minimally functioning.
