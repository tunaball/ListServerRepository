There are a few possiblities for your error code:

1) Your drive is in need of repair. Try burning a CD using the Apple's 
built in Burn CD, under the file menu. Insert your cd, name it, drag 
files onto it, select File:Burn CD. If that doesn't work, call Apple 
Support for a probably repair.

2) If you can burn a CD using Burn CD, then your Toast software may 
need an update for 10.2.8, visit their website, or

3) Toast needs to be reinstalled or your preferences trashed.

4) You may have corrupt files you are trying to burn.

5) You need to reread the manual.

6) Something else...
