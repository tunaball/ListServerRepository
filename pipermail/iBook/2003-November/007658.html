	Congratulations! Although I went through my defense in summer of 1987, 
I still remember it all too vividly.  (Of course, I passed too. :) )

first-generation "portable" PCs. Two of them had to be allowed extra 
time to finish because the computers "ate" their files and corrupted 
their floppy disks.  I would imagine that today an iBook would prove a 
whole lot more trustworthy.
