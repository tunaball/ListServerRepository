I've looked at Firebird, but I'm sticking with Mozilla 1.5 until 
Firebird and Thunderbird mature a little; even so, I hope I might be of 
limited help.  I'm not sure if this is what you need or not, but the 
only Firebird directly I'm familiar with is found in:

/<your home directory>/Library/Phoenix/

(Phoenix was the previous name of Firebird.)  Be careful deleting that 
directory, though, because your bookmarks and other personal data are 
stored there.  If you delete it, you might want to back it up first.

The installation notes may also mean that you should delete the old 
Firebird application instead of overwriting it.  But that's just a guess 
on my part.

Hope this helps,
Mike W.
