I can attest, they use nothing but Macs. I had the privilege of working 
for Apple and was able to see beneath the surface. They are a Mac only 
shop. In some areas, they are networked for sharing, in some areas, the 
sharing is very limited. The entire Apple "System" is totally 
integrated and networked so they can access any and everybody at any 
time. Access to specific files is always on a need to use basis just 
like in any corporate structure. The other remarkable element is the 
respect each employee has for Apple and the Apple corporate community. 
They are a family and a community. Working for Apple was the most 
enriching and rewarding work experience I have ever had and I was very 
sad when I was forced by family circumstances to terminate that 
employment. I miss that nurturing environment very much. Apple is a 
great company to work for.
