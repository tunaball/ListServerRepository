I don't know of an actual keyboard combo for moving to the top of a Word
document, but on my PowerBook I can go to the View menu, then Toolbars, then
choose PowerBook. This gives me a little tear-off toolbar which has buttons
for moving to the top or bottom of the document without having to scroll.
Maybe Word on your iBook has View > Toolbars > iBook instead?
