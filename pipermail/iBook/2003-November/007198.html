My Powerbook feels a lot lighter now that I can use Mail.app's new 
threaded feature.

View:Organize By Thread puts all of the message to one thread inside a 
collapsible/expandable subject line. Use the right and left arrow keys 
to open or shut the thread. This really reduces the clutter on heavy 
volume lists and for those who don't like roaming threads, you avoid 
having to deal with them. Press right arrow and then down arrow to 
navigate the thread. Or select the subject and press delete.
