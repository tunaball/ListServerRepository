Robert,

I would doubt that Apple would remove support for the DV cam you are 
able to use now on Jaguar (despite it not being officially supported). 
My *guess* is that it would be the same for your other devices all 
well.

To be safe, when/if you upgrade to Panther, you might back up your 
current working OS with either Carbon Copy Cloner (latest version) or 
Panther's new Disk Utility which you can access from one of the drop 
down menus in the installer. If something doesn't work, you can then 
restore your old OS.
