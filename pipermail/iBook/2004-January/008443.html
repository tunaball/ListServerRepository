Firmware updates won't do it, the actual hardware is different.
11g requires much higher performance hardware than 11b, which means that a
radio that wasn't designed with 11g in mind probably won't work for 11g,
at least not very well. Since 11g didn't exist when the original airport
cards came to be, I doubt they would be compliant with the spec, unlike
the 11g cards that were designed to a draft version of the spec. The
differences between the draft versions and the final were minimal and any
issues were easier to correct in firmware.
