>> When using a notebook computer running from fixed line power over 
>> extended periods, it is advisable to remove the battery and store it 
>> in a cool place.
>
> Since I leave mine in most of the time, does this apply to me?  I 
> don't use the battery a lot...but some.

If you do remove the battery, your Powerbook then works like a desktop 
and a power failure will cause it to go poof and you will lose all 
unsaved data and suffer whatever consequences there may be. So, buy a 
UPS (universal power supply) to protect your Powerbook from this and 
also use the surge protection.
