Yep. Electric circuit boards don't like being squashed.

I picked up a Pismo with my right hand near the upper right hand corner 
and zzzzplllaftt it conked out. Fortunately, nothing was damaged and it 
rebooted fine. Tried it again, such a brave fool, and same thing.

So, I make it a habit when picking up the book, to cradle it in both 
hands evenly distributing the weight and trying to not create unneeded 
torque anywhere.
