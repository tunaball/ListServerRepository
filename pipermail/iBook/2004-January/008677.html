Matt,

I have a Graphite SE Clamshell 366mhz and I have a 512meg ram card in 
it for a total of 576. Currently I have Jaguar 10.2.8 running on it and 
find that it really is not too bad for speed. I have Panther coming and 
I hear that it runs faster than Jaguar so may be another thing to look 
at. Look on some of the iBook forums and they have speed tips that may 
help a bit more. The clamshells were only rated for 256 meg by Apple 
but they are known for being conservative. I have had the ram in for 
over 2 months without any problems.

Obviously the faster the machine the better but at the moment this is 
what I have and will use.....
