Hi:

When the player window comes up in Safari (you do use Safari, don't 
you?)  there is a little arrow on the right hand side of the player 
bar.  Click it, and a menu drops down and click on "Save as source".  
It will then prompt you where and will save it as a .m4a file which 
will then play in Quicktime.  You can then drag it to iTunes.
