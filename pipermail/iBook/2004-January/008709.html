Hi all,

I've had my little 12" G4 iBook for only a short time, and already it 
seems like home.  The only thing I miss from my Linux box is the big 
17" LCD.  Sometime you just want to read from one window and type in 
another, and it's difficult to do on a small iBook.

Well, thanks to a very bright Mac hacker, my MacOS X desktop now spans 
my 12" iBook and my 17" screen.  The iBook resolution is 1024 x 768, 
the 17" resolution is 1280 x 1024.  Room to stretch out and enjoy!

The hack is free and can be found here:
http://www.rutemoeller.com/mp/ibook/ibook_e.html

Have fun!
