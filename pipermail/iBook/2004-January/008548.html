I believe that the other replies might be missing the point...that your 
ibook IS on when you open it the first inch, but then it's off.

This is a classic symptom of a common problem on the G3 iBook Dual USB 
(Ice Books).  The video cable is being damaged by the hinge.  If this 
is the case in your computer, the computer is not actually off...just 
the screen goes blank.  You MIGHT be able to still see the screen 
contents with the right lighting, in that case. Another thing you could 
do would be to close the 'book again, and see if it goes back to sleep 
(pulsating light indicating sleep), or you could hit the power button, 
which would bring up the "really shut down...?" dialog, and then hit 
?enter or maybe it's the power button again, which would tell the 
machine to shutdown.

Or I could just be wrong :-)

If those happen, you can send it back to Apple for repair.
