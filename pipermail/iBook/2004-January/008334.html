I was considering FV awhile back but my search on the web for info 
found unsatisfied users, in it's inability to do a good job converting 
the RIAA signals from vinyl. I *think* there were bad reports at 
www.versiontracker.com (sorry - don't have time to check). I ended up 
buying a Phono Equalizer, and get beautiful results.

If you plan to go via an amp with a built in phono input, that will 
handle the RIAA signal, and the iMic will suffice as an input for your 
computer (as would their more expensive PowerWave).
