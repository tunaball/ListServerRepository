I am using a Netgear 802.11b router and have been very happy with it.  I
even bought one of their wireless ethernet bridges for my brother's old Macs
and it worked right out of the box.  I've noticed lately that the Netgear
802.11b routers have been selling for pretty cheap.  There are two
advantages with the Apple router.  The Apple router has a built in
usb/printer port and 56k modem.  If you want to go wireless and don't have
broadband, you'll have to get a router with built in modem.

-Guy
