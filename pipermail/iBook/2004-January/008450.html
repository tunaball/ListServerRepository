We've had earthlink high speed through our cable company for a year - 
two outages, both lasted all day - both in the last 2 weeks. Earthlink 
was very nice but said it was my cable company, but my cable company's 
phone system automatically switched my phone calls over to Earthlink. 
VERY frustrating. When I finally found a way around their phone 
switching, they wouldn't talk to me until I'd unhooked the cablemodem 
from my router and plugged a computer directly into the cablemodem.

Earthlink was right - it was the cable company's outage. I discovered 
that a carwash very near my house has free wireless access. They have 
little tables and a coffee bar. So that's where I'll go hang out next 
time I have a long outage, I guess. Maybe this'll motivate me to get my 
car washed once in a while <g>.
