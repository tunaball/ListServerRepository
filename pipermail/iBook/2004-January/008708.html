About Apple serial numbers: I guess most of you know this, but it's 
always nice to point out that the third place is the year, the fourth 
and fifth, the week. So a serial number that begins UV345 is November 
2-8, 2003. MenuCalendar is a nice little menu bar app that provides the 
week number information. See versiontracker.com for download.
