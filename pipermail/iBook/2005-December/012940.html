G'day,

Have you considered an external hard disk? It's a lot cheaper, performance is
better, and you won't have to modify your ibook.

I have this setup for recording on my 12" G4 800, and it works well. Get a
casing without a fan, and you should be fine. I built a 160GB external HD
(USB2/FW) for about AU$250 (~US$170), which is quite decent...

Caveat: if you use an external sound card, make sure the HD isn't on the same
bus - my soundcard is USB, so I use the HD on firewire.

HTH,
-Angus
