When I replaced the drive in my iBook I looked at
7200RPM drives and found that they would make the
laptop to hot. I ended up just getting a 5400RPM
instead. This is just my personal opinion, but unless
you absolutely need the speed increase i'd stay with
the 5400RPM drive.
