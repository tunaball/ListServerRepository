Diskwarrior will rebuild your directory and also optimize the drive. It 
is amazing how much this improves performance.

You can also run Repair Disk Permissions and Repair Disk in the Disk 
Utility. You'll have to boot off the CD or another drive to run Repair 
Disk.

Also fsck helps.

This Unix business installs and deletes so many files that the 
directory gets a bit buggy and the files scattered all over the place.
