I remember when I first started looking at the Internet, in 1989-90, and
purchased a book called Then Internet Starter Kit, by Adam Engst and another
book on Networking by Glen Fleishman. Well, you're in luck, because an
excellent recent release you should read is called The Wireless Networking
Starter Kit, by Glen Fleishman and Adam Engst!

They have a web site which will give you a head start:
www.wireless-starter-kit.com and you may find some reviews and helpful
advice at one of favorite review sites: www.mymac.com [disclaimer, I also
write for MyMac, but they are still a great resource anyway ;-) ]

Good luck!
