I have a key lime 366 iBook and recently bought a 800 mhz "icebook" 
w/combo drive...
Comparing the two, I would have to say I love the speed of the iceBook.
and I love the size of it. (actually, I wish Apple would make a 8 or 10 
inch iceBook)

What do I miss about the Key Lime? The looks, the sheer funkiness of it.
The glow in the dark (and light!) brightness of it.
But what I really miss about the clamshell design is the way the palm 
rests slope downward
towards the front.
The iceBook has the edge like most laptops and this edge presses into 
my palms.
Of course, I need to change my typing style. But to me, the clamshells 
were more comfortable to use.

If anyone is interested, I am thinking of selling the Key Lime to help 
pay for the iceBook.
