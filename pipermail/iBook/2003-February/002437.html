Just for interest, I ran the X11 graphics speed tests on my iBook dual 
USB 600 MHz.

I ran x11perf, which can be summarized by Xmark. I also ran glxgears. 
For comparison, I also ran the same test on an 800 MHz flatscreen iMac. 
The results are below.

My conclusion is that the iBook is not too shabby!

Test		Units			iBook	iMac
Xmark	( x SparcStation 1)	10.1639	16.4141
glxgears	(fps)				220		830
