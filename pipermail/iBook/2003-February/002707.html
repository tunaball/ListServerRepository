Basically, you should handle the signal as a phone-signal. You nedd a plug 
that convert from mini-jack to phono-male plugs to be placed in your AUX on 
the rear of an amplifier, to which you attach these nice biggies of high 
speakers you're talking about.

My iBook has such a connection to a Kenwood 5440 with JBL- studio monitors 
and everything from games, DCs, MP3s, MPEG4s – whatever audio sound like 
heaven.

I'm unsure about the american marked, but here in Denmark you pay close to 
nothing for the plug needed as weel as phono-cables in any radio/TV/audio 
store.
