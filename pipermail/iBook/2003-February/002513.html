Juno supports Mac OSX, but only in the Platinum version, which costs 
$9.95 per month.  There is no free Juno for Macs.

Also, live tech support is available, but it is NOT free.  It will 
either cost you $1.95 per minute, or a flat fee of $14.95, depending 
on where you are calling from.

I have no personal experience with Juno, so I can't comment on how 
good or bad they are.
