I bought mine on eBay as well. As I said before, I paid a lot more for the
color - $1100. But I was lucky because the woman I bought it from kept it
pristine. It came packed in the original Apple box, all manuals in the
cellophane wrapping, all cords with the original twistie ties! It had an
airport card pre-installed and the piece that was taken out was in the box.
The ram had been maxed out and she gave me a brand new Kensington bag with
the tags still on it! I swear it all came like it was factory packed.

The one thing that stinks about these clamshells is the flaw in the handles.
They all crack for no apparent reason. I have clear packing tape around mine
( you can't really see it) and it helps to strengthen it from cracking
further.
