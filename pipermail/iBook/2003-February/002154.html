> Is it possible to edit video that is in MPEG or Quicktime format? I 
> tried
> iMovie but unless I am doing something wrong that seems to only work 
> with
> video from a camera.

If you have Quicktime Pro, you can export those MPEG or QT files into 
"DV Stream" format. Once that's done (warning: enormous file size!), 
drop them into the "Media" folder of an existing iMovie project. Then 
open iMovie, and voila the clips will appear.
