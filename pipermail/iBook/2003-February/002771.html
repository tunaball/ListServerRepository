> Can someone explain how to partition an external hard drive? And then

> I use it?

A partition works just like another drive thus a partitioned drive will 
look, work and act like two drives.

Move all data on the drive to another drive since it will be erased.

Open Apple's Disk Utility.

Click on the icon of the drive you wish to partition.

Click on the Erase tab and choose Mac OS Extended, name the drive and 
click on Install Mac OS 9 Drivers if you intend to use OS 9. Probably a 
good idea to do this anyway. Click Erase.

Click on the Partition Tab and create your partitions. The steps seem 
obvious so I won't go through them. [Actually I don't have a drive 
connected so I can't see them.]
