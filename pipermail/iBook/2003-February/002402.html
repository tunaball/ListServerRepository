I have a 600 mhz iBook running 10.2.3 and suffered from the logout 
kernal panic. Throwing away the
.GlobalPreferences.plist file just onced solved the problem for me 
without having to lock a new version or reset things each time I 
logout/logon.
Sorry I cleaned out my logs recently - if it happens again I will save
