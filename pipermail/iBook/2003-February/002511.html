You need three things, the phone and account, a cable and a driver.

I used a Timeport with a Socket card via the PC Card slot on a Pismo (I 
have both for sale or trade if anyone is interested).

I am looking at the merlin c201 pc card now which using the 3G stuff 
and should hit 1.5 M some time soon, Right now it is maxed out at 140K 
or so, usually hitting 50K about the same as dialup. That service would 
be unlimited for $100 a month. Most of the 3G stuff (PC VISION) has a 
per megabyte charge that really can add up. Download one apple update 
while you are eating in a restaurant and you might owe Sprint 
$100000...  :)

There are wireless connectivity kits for a FEW of the newer phones and 
I will check them out again tomorrow when I go back for the C201.

I have a USB connection cable for my i300 but am working on getting it 
to act as a modem.
