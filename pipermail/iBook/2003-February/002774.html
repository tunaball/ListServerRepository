On Thursday, February 27, 2003, at 06:11 PM, Murray E. Milligan wrote:

> I'd like to back up the important files on my TiBook, running Jag 
> 10.2.4.
>
> My entire home directory I understand, but where do I go to locate the 
> favourites, e-mail address book and other files I should back up?

All that stuff is stored in the users/username/library/preferences 
directory. If you back up your entire home directory, you are backing 
up those preferences.

I use my .mac account and the Apple backup utility to back them up to 
my iDisk. Larger files I back up to CD-ROM/DVD-RAm.
