> You're going to need a second NIC (network interface
> card) for the PC. ...
> 
> ... Click on the Sharing tab and put a check
> in the box that reads "Enable Internet connection sharing for this
> connection." Now run a Cat-5 Ethernet cable from the second NIC to your Mac's
> Ethernet port. If your Mac has an auto-switching NIC you can use a standard
> cable, if not you'll need to get a cross-over cable (you should be able to
> find the cables you need where you got your NIC). Configure your Mac for DHCP
> and you should be in business.
> 
> There are other aspects of Win 2K's connection sharing feature you might want
> to acquaint yourself with, if so open Win 2K's help and do a search for
> "enable internet connection sharing."
> 
> Good luck...
>
