In my case, iSight cam caused CCC to fail to clone, it would freeze 
midway in cloning and actually freeze my whole computer, requiring a 
hard restart with the Power button. This happened repeatedly, until I 
disconnected the iSight, and then CCC worked normally.

Actually, I also found that the iSight interferes sometimes with other 
Firewire devices, such as firewire external hard drives. I no longer 
use the iSight. Others have had similar experiences with it.
