I have the same ibook as you, and when I went into an Apple dealer they 
suggested an external firewire case with as fast an HD as you can 
afford.  He said I could replace the internal HD, and go from 4200 (I 
think) rpm to 5400, but with an external firewire setup I could use the 
faster drives.

I haven't had the money to do it yet, but if you do go this way I'd 
like to hear how it turns out.
