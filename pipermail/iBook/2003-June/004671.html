> On Thursday, June 19, 2003, at 12:44 PM, Joe Jones wrote:

>> You can hook your iBook up to a display. The iBook has a proprietary 
>> VGA connector (the standard VGA connector is too big to mount on the 
>> I/O bezel) so you need an adaptor cable that comes with your iBook. 
>> If you've lost it you can buy a new one from Apple quite cheaply.
>>
>> The video card can only drive at two resolutions - 800x600 and 
>> 1024x78, and can only mirror the laptop's display (no monitor 
>> spanning without a hack).
>>
>> You can use any size display you want, just be aware that you'll be 
>> viewing it at 1024x768, so anything bigger than 17" would probably be 
>> overkill.
>>
>> Joe
