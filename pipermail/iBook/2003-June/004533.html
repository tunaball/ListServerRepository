On Thursday, June 12, 2003, at 03:59  PM, Mark Kippert wrote:

> Does anybody know of an application that will help me create a business
> plan? Either a stand alone app or a feature within an application? 
> Something
> that will walk me through a plan and prompt me for info.

It seems you are asking for two things:

1) Information that you need to create, run, administer a certain type 
of business, not stated.
2) Sofware that gives you the ability to structure these ideas, give 
them a time & date and checkbox and all of the other features available 
in a database.

Start with number two and buy filemaker and you can either create a 
structure to your liking or find one premade, maybe even free.

You can find all kinds of documents, text files and databases and 
applications, providing info on number 1. That data can then be 
integrated with your Filemaker databases.

Filemaker is easy enough to use that most people can be up and running 
in little time at all.
