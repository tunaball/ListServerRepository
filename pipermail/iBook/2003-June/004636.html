By "can't see it" do you mean that it just shows a blank page, or do 
you get a "not found" error?  I can't really speak for Camino, but I 
have seen problems in the past where a page shows up just fine in 
Netscape, but IE will display a syntax error for the page.  It seemed 
that Netscape was content to guess at what the page was supposed to 
show, but IE didn't want to guess, and let you know that the syntax 
wasn't correct.  (I always thought it ironic that IE was the one to 
complain that a page wasn't compliant with proper HTML syntax)

I would suggest trying your pages in several browsers, and see if any 
of them show any syntax issues.  You could also try sending your page 
through one of the free code validators that are out there.  
Unfortunately I can't recall the URL for any of them right now, but you 
should be able to find some with a Google search,
