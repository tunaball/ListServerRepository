There are probably a MILLION under-the-hood bugs fixed since v74, but I 
really can't tell much difference because Safari has been my default 
browser for three months already. Everywhere I go, it works fine. I 
still see the occasional rendering bug here and there (and there's 
still a way to report that to Apple), and the final does seem a bit 
faster to me, but apart from that -- it's the same great browser it's 
been since they added tabbed browsing (which I *literally* cannot live 
without).
