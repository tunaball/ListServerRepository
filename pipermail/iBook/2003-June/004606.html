> Is anyone using any sort of platform for their iBook? Which one and do 
> you like it enough that you'd buy it again?

Most of the time, I use the "platform" that God gave me -- my lap. :-) 
I often plop into a beanbag when reading email or hitting my daily 
websites during the evening, which supports my back & angles the 
iBook's keyboard perfectly.

When I'm doing something like typing all day, I snag a USB mouse from 
somewhere (G3 at home, G3 at work) & put the iBook on an old typing 
table we liberated from a closed business many years ago. The table is 
the right height for typing (hence its name) so I can work all day 
without my arms getting sore (bad positioning hurts me from above my 
wrist to my elbow for some reason).
