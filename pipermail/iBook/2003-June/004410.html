It's not enough to matter to me when I've got 40 gigs, luckily.  And back in the HFS days of OS 9, you'd actually save space when you had partitions.  There's a maximum sector (or some such) number, so a file that's 1k might take 153k (the smallest unit HFS would handle on a drive of arbitrarily large size) -- like they do on my old StarMax running 8.6.

I also like swapping out OSs, particularly Linux, which is yet another reason to have a partition laying around!
