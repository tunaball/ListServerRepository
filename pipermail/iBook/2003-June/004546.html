Why limit one's self to a pen drive when a CF+ card, smart media card 
or even the chewing gum memory stick can be used in so many devices 
that don't have USB ports. Add a usb reader for your macs and you have 
a multi platform device. Of course these devices are cute while limited 
to usb ports.

I've found plastic form fitted cases on a keychain for $10 that will 
hold four or more of the cards.

I use my 1 Gig microdrive (under $250) in my Macs with a PC Card 
adapter, in my Canon d10 and in my iPaq Pocket PC. Couldn't do that 
with a pen drive.
