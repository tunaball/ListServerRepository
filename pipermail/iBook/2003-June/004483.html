Word has a lot of options you can turn off that will lessen the drain 
on the battery. Word 4 was a winner. I could turn most such off and it 
would only go to the hard drive on very rare occassions. The object is 
to lessen the disk access so that the drive can go to sleep and not 
drain the battery. The two biggest drains are the screen brightness and 
the drive spinning/access.
