incompetence stories which runs the gamut from iMacs to memory, from

unveiling of the original iMac but while I was still in the support  
biz many of my clients chose to shop there - a time or two. The only  
store that can rival CompUSA in incompetence, lies, and questionable  
business practices is WorstBuy.
