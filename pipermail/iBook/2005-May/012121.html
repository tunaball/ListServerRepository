On Wednesday, May 25, 2005, at 03:19 PM, M.Milligan wrote:

> I have the newest 1.2GB 12" iBook. I've noticed the pie shaped wedge 
> symbol for the Airport status changes to grey sometimes. Only a reset 
> of my Airport seems to help.

Do you (or, if you live in a condo/town house/apartment, neighbors in 
adjacent units) have any 2.4GHz phones? They can interfere with the 
Airport signal, and the reset "fixing" the problem could possibly be 
coincidence. The Airport Admin Utility has a setting to work around 
interference, the trade-off being a somewhat reduced range.
