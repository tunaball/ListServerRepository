Hi; I was just clearing out some old e-mails and I came across this one; 
don't know how I missed it... has anyone responded to your inquiry?

Anyway, to your problem: What I would do first is to reset the Power Manager 
and see if that solves the problem, by sticking an elongated paperclip in the 
little hole above the Power botton. 

Also - even though the ibook does not have a pram battery - I would try the 
ol' Apple/Cmd-Option-P-R hand sign just for good measure.

Short of either of those tricks, perhaps the "charger board"  - the board w/ 
the battery contact pins - needs replacing. If charging was a problem also, 
and the reset/pram tricks did not work I would be sure that was the problem...

Lettuce know, and good luck,
