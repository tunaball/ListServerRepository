I replaced the optical drive on my iBook, and at the time I changed from CD
only to DVD/CD too. I didn't find it either complex nor time intensive: but
I am used to taking things apart then putting them back together again!

There are some great resources on the web which can help you work out what
to do. To decide which model of CD/DVD have a look at
http://mywebpages.comcast.net/macdan/opti_upgrades.html. For the steps to
take a machine apart, try http://uk.geocities.com/ibookupgrade/. 

The only other hint I'd give is to keep a note of every screw and where it
came from -- because they all look very similar but won't fit back in the
wrong holes!

I hope this helps.
