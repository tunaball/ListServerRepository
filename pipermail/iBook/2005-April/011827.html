In the last couple of months I've gone in twice, first to replace the DC 
power board and then the airport antenna lead (this one was harder).  I 
used a really nice guide on PBFixit.com that has a screw guide.  Each 
time I took some screws out, I taped it to the guide so that I wouldn't 
lose it and to know which one was which.  The second time I did it, a 
couple of tiny plastic parts fell out and I can't figure out where they 
go.  Everything seems fine without them though...said with my fingers 
are crossed.
