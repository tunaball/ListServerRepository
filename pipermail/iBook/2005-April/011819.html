I've been using an iBook G4/800/12" for about a year now. The drive can 
be noisy, depending on what you are doing and the disk. Burning is 
noisy, Cds with lots of printing/labels are a little noisy and it gets 
worse if the disk is printed labeled off-center or to one side. 
depending on what I do, I get 2 to 4 hours on my freshly charged 
battery after almost daily.
