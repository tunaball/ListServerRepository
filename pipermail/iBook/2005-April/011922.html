For a given size of HD the power usage (and therefore heat) doesn't 
increase with size.  It is typically higher with a higher performance 
drive but that is relative to it's contemporaries.  You simply can't 
keep increasing the power a drive draws, it will overheat regardless 
of case cooling.  If you are concerned about heat then avoid 7200 RPM 
drives would be my best recommendation.
