if the QT needs to buffer video frames in ram that could cause
the problem you describe.  512MB ram modules are pretty cheap
now--it's quite common to replace one of the stock 128MB's
with a 512 to reach the 640MB maximum, and then one hears
fewer stories of problems. :-)
