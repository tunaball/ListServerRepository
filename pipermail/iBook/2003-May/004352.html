Yes. I don't know why they stopped calling it "autopilot" in the 
current releases. You'll notice that there are two tabs there, "Tasks" 
and "Options."

What I meant was that you should checkmark each of the boxes presented 
in the "Tasks" tab, then go to the "Options" tab and check "restart 
automatically," then go back to the "Tasks" tab and press the "Run" 
button.

Cocktail comes with a terrific and clear "help" section that can be 
accessed in the "Help" menu. Check that for further information.
