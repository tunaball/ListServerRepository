The warning about not placing to much weight in what "gurus" at the 
Apple Store have to say, is to be taken seriously.

If you accept the configuration of the iBook HD, as delivered, 
usually without a partition for OS 9.2.2, you may not be able to boot 
into 9.2.2.

However, if you erase the HD and reinstall, checking the box for a 
9.2.2 installation before continuing, you will then be able to boot 
in either X or 9.22.

Len Ornstein
