Afaik, no fax device will work with a DSL modem. It needs a regular phone 
line. How about one of those inexpensive one-into-two phone line 
splitter? Put it after the filter and before the fax machine. Note, 
however, that if you're faxing out on your fax modem and the fax machine 
is turned on, it may want to play, too.
