wherever I go, I just let it sleep­­riding around in my briefcase, sitting
in the back seat of the car, going thru airport inspections and those x-ray
machines they have, stowing it under the airplane seat or overhead, etc. I'm
not sure your computer is any safer turned off than it is sleeping.
