I had actually been considering it.  I am was looking at the PocketPC 
products.  I currently have a Sony Clie S320 that is getting old and 
showing it.

I have used the PocketPC device I got for my wife and I like the way it 
handles external memory.  There are workarounds for palm external 
memory, but they are just that, workarounds.  The result is that I 
cannot keep the number of reference works I would like to keep with me 
(my manuals from work, that I use daily, are available in PDF).

One thing I am totally sold on with the palm is Palm Desktop, it is 
better than any other I have tried.  It is leagues ahead of the 
scattering of apple substitutes (address book, iCal) and  I am much 
more comfortable (probably from over 5 years of usage) with it than I 
am with the desktop that comes with officeX (also there still isn't a 
palm-entrague conduit so it just isn't worth the trouble).

I was really considering PocketMac, what was the trouble that you had 
with it? the biggest trouble I see is in installing software (that will 
always be a bit of a bear with windows based installers).
