On Thursday, May 15, 2003, at 04:36  AM, Per Brodersen wrote:

> I will sell my iBook shortly (in exchange for the 12''PB tomorrow!) 
> and was
> was wondering how to erase the drive *completely*.
> What software would you use and recommend?

Apple's Disk Utility will do this for you.

Insert your install CD and restart holding down the C key. Locate and 
open Disk Utility. Click on the Icon of your drive, the Erase tab and 
the option button. Check the box to zero the drive.

This will take a long time...
