Hi Everyone,

I'm fairly new to this list, have had my iBook since December.

I'd take some of the knowledge of the people at the Apple Store with a 
grain of salt. In the past 3 months, I've sent two people to the Apple 
Stores here in Southeastern Michigan, and both time they were given 
misinformation. Friend went to see about a 17" LCD monitor for her G4 
(with VGA/DVI connectors on the vid card) and was told she'd need a new 
computer to use Apple's LCD. A $99 adapter would have done the trick, 
and Apple sells them. <sigh> The second friend was looking for a new 
Mac, was really fed up with Dell, Gateway, etc. and wanted more info 
about switching. Sales person treated she and her husband like weirdos, 
weren't able to tell them why they should 'spend more' for a Mac. They 
ended up at CompUSA and bought another PC.

It has been stated in various articles headlined at MacSurfer.com, that 
the current, most recent iBooks are dual boot. You can check it out for 
yourself  <www.macsurfer.com>

Another friend who bought her iBook a few weeks after I bought mine, 
was told by SOS-Apple that her iBook wasn't designed to boot into OS 9. 
Sheesh...

Namaste,

Kathe
