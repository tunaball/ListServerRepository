I discovered by accident that in the Mail app in OSX, when you click on 
"Reply to Sender" without opening a message, it opens it up without 
loading the images. Thus, no connection is made back to the 
originator's server. When it opens, look it over to see if it is, 
indeed, spam, and then hit "delete" if it is.
