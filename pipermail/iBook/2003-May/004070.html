When my BellSouth DSL was installed, the DSL was connected to two of 
the four wires coming from the phone room. The other two wires were 
made available for a voice phone. This allowed me to have both dsl and 
voice at the same time.

If you remove the cover from your phone outlet you will see that there 
are four wires inside. Only two are being used if you have only the 
initial phone installation. The other two are available for what ever 
use you want.
