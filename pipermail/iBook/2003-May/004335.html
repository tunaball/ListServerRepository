Please allow me to make this exceptionally easy for you.

1. Go to versiontracker.com or macupdate.com and download a program 
called "Cocktail." It is free of charge.

2. Run the program, and put in your admin password when asked.

3. Proceed directly to "Auto Pilot." Check everything OK, then run it. 
Takes 5-20 minutes depending.

4. Repeat this procedure about once every month or so, or after any 
major system upgrades.

That's really all you need for "routine" maintenance, though I would 
also suggest investing in DiskWarrior3 when it's widely available.
