That's the very drive I self installed in my icebook 500. I installed an IBM
40 GB 5400 8MB in my wife's clamshell 300. No speed comparisons are possibel
given different configurations - buy will say that when the IBM access or
writes data that it is significantly quieter than my Toshiba.

Installation is sort of like massive surgery. Make sure you have a system to
keep track of the many different sizes of screws you'll take out. Actually
cracking (snapping off) the bottom case from the frame was way too exciting.

Enjoy the fun.
