> 
> http://www.googlegear.com/jsp/ProductDetail.jsp?ProductCode=101606

> It's 5400rpm, but I'm hoping the fluid will offset the extra heat.  It's

I have never tried any Toshiba drives, so I can't comment on that, but I
have a 5400rpm IBM Travelstar (40GNX) in my iBook, and I have had no trouble
whatsoever. I did notice that my left wrist rest gets a tad warmer (that's
warmer, not hotter) than it did with the stock (4200rpm Travelstar (30GN)).
Battery life hasn't, at least not significantly.
