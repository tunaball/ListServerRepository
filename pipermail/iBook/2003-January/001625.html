Well, as long as it's still OFF, the keyboard removed completely, (and 
with the battery out to make sure), you could get a can of compressed 
air, turn the iBook upside down over a white sheet spread on the floor, 
and blow the compressed air into the crevices.   Maybe it'll knock it 
loose.   As I visualize the interior of my iBook, I don't see how the 
screw could have gone far.   I mean, the iBooks are built like a brick! 
   Once you get the plastic covers off, sure, the screw could be 
anywhere...but if all you did was lift the keyboard then, no.

If you take the keyboard off completely to allow for vigorous shaking, 
then be careful not to tear the ribbon cable.
