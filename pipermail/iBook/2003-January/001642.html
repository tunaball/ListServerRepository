As well as my iBook, I own a tiny Fujitsu P1120.  It has a 8.9" screen, 40 gig hard drive, runs full windows XP with and 800mhz transmeta crusoe processor, and is a little bit bigger than a paperback novel.  I like it alot, though it doesnt have a cd drive, to keep the package small.  internal Wi-Fi, modem, and ethernet, are all there too.  For about $1000 too.  If you want something a little bigger, Fujitsu makes a range of laptops bigger than the P1120.  For example, the next step up is the 2120, which is a 10.4 inch screen, and a modular cd-rw/dvd drive, or so i hear. And its pretty well priced as well, I think.
