> Does anyone know of a product like GoBack from Roxio for the PC that allows
> you to literally "Go Back" after deleting a file, installing a buggy

Go Back is vital to Windows due to its fragile architecture - largely due to
its reliance on the Registry and DLLs. The Mac doesn¹t have that problem -
not with OS 9 and not with OS X. Unlike Windows, most Mac applications don¹t
install files that can affect the system as a whole. Yes, some OS 9
applications might install extensions which might cause a problem, but it is
easy to simply remove an extension from the extension folder and the problem
goes away.
