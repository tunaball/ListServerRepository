Here's the latest word on the upcoming OS X-native versions of Alsoft's
DiskWarrior and PlusOptimizer (from Alsoft customer service):

The new version of DiskWarrior (v. 3.0) will be available around
mid-February.  The new version of PlusOptimizer will not be ready for
another month or two after that (they weren't able to specify).  If your
computer ONLY has OS X on it, you won't be able to run the current versions
unless you installed OS 9 drivers when you installed OS X, so you'll have to
wait until both new versions are available (unless you want to reinitialize
your disk and install OS 9 drivers while reinstalling OS X).
