You could try appleisp, which I use for travelling. Their website is 
www.appleisp.net. They charge $14.95/month, so you could sign up for a 
month, then cancel when you didn't need it. I verified that they have a 
number in Long Beach. 

Their website gives instructions for setting up your OS and email client 
for their service. They also have good phone support. I'd probably use 
their system all the time except that my current ISP can give me a DSL 
line (it should be installed within a week or so), but appleisp cannot. 
They do have an option for a dial-up ISDN service.
