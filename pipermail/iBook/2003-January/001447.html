One option would be Key Design Center 3-d, which is a very out-of-date 
produced by Softkey, which I beleive is no longer published. You might 
be able to get an old copy somewhere.

It has a non-standard interface, but allows you to build up a structure 
and then look at it in 3-d, and walk through the model. It is aimed 
primarily at building/home design, and I have found it helpful for 
visualizing changes we may want to do in our home.

By the way, it has both 68K and PPC code, and runs great on a 600 MHz 
iBook, just to get a little back on topic!
