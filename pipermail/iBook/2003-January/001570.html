Have you tried calling Apple Europe directly to explain your situation?

just a matter of following the shipping instructions they give you over 
the phone, and waiting for them to do the rest and send it back to you?

authorized to replace themselves, such as keyboards:

http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=50031

replace your broken one.
