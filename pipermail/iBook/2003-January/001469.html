The first thing I'd do is to start it up from the Emergency Startup 
floppy that came with it. It may be called "Disk First Aid" or something 
like that. It needs OS 7.5.2 or later, according to "MacTracker," a neat 
little history application. Once you start it up from the floppy, you 
should be able to examine the hard drive and even run Disk First Aid to 
try to fix it.
