I held the new 12" AlBook in my hands the other day at the Pasadena 
store.
I didn't do a whole analysis. There were other people waiting to play 
with it too. I just remember thinking that it felt very
sturdy. The aluminum is really really nice. It just seems like it's not 
fragile.
I have an 700 iBook and the new Powerbook felt just as sturdy to me. I 
didn't notice any screen bowing like you said.
I didn't look for it or concentrate on that. But I did pick it up a few 
times and I would have noticed that.
One thing though, it did feel pretty hot on the underside and on the 
left hand side of the trackpad.
All in all, though, it was really beautiful. I loved the keyboard. And 
it looks and feels more portable and smaller than
the iBook. It's a knockout.
