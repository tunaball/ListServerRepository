Roberto,

Mac OS 9 will read the files from OS X without any modification. Mac OS 
9 will truncate the file names and if I remember correctly you will 
have to rename the files when you go back to Mac OS X. If your still 
intersted in modify your file names take a look at A Better Finder 
Renamer http://www.versiontracker.com/moreinfo.fcgi?id=11366&db=mac.

-Brad
