> 2. Has anyone had experience with the TransPod auto adapter?

I just bought one of these and am very happy with it. I have it mounted 
just above the stick in the center of my civic. The fm signal is better 
than the little irock transmitter that I bought at the apple store. I 
do not think you can use any sort of ipod cover with the transpod, as I 
don't have one and the fit is tight, but that is after attaching the 
included padded backing to tighten the fit. Overall I am very very 
happy with the transpod, it is extremely nice having the ipod up at arm 
level in the car and being able to switch between songs without having 
to look down at the passenger seat.
