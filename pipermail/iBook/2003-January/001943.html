I just signed up with syracuse.net. They have dial-up numbers all over 
the US and a few places in Europe and Japan. I've been with them less 
than a month, but so far I am very pleased with their service. I had to 
call technical support once when I set up my e-mail and they were 
patient and efficient.

After that rates depend on how long you sign up for.
