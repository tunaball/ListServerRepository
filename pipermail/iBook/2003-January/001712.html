Can't remember what the original question was, but I was thinking of 
the new 12" Powerbook before deciding on the ibook. If you look at the 
specs (I forget who ran the benchmarks) the 800 Mhz G3 was about neck 
and neck with the Powerbook, except for Altivec applications. Also, the 
new Powerbook doesn't have IR or a PC card...add to it the fact that it
