I asked a question a week or so ago about upping my clamshell (300mz) 
memory higher than the stated 256 sodimm in the top slot.  Seemed to 
draw allot of interest.  Lots of replies, with most feeling it would 
work with the latest firmware update.  That's all I needed to here.

I purchased a used 512mb sodimm off Ebay for about $80 and stuck it in. 
 (I already had the latest firmware.)  Poof, works fine.  Shows I have 
544mb of Ram.  OSX (which barely ran on this clamshell) purrs along just 
fine.  The only "real" test I can do to see if there truly is a speed 
increase is to time functions in Photoshop.  Saving, sizing, sharpening 
and all manipulations are noticeably faster now then when I had the 
256mb dimm in.

Anyhow, for what it's worth, adding the 512mb sodimm made a noticeable 
difference for me.

Thanks to all that replied.
