I almost never shut down or restart my iBook (600). I also carry it with 
me everywhere in a slip case inside my brief case. When it goes to sleep 
it obviously parks the heads, so moving it is fine. I even dropped it 
BIG TIME a few weeks back (I wrote about it on this list--stairs, 
concrete, tears--the works). It had been asleep and it woke right up 
fine and has been chugging along ever since. This baby is RUGGED. Don't 
be afraid of it.
