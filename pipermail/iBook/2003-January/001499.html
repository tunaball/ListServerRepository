Crystal,

In my experience, iTunes doesn't experience "slow down" with a large 
number of MP3s in contrast iPhoto's issues with several hundred photos. 
Although they use the same metaphors (ie. library, album), iTunes has 
an easier time since it really just plays MP3s, while iPhoto has to 
load graphics (thumb-nail size pictures) with live resizing, which is 
more "intensive" than what iTunes does.
