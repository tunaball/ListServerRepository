I had problems like that for quite a while. In my case I also had 
another computer on the network and they connected to each other fine. 
So I knew it wasn't the wireless card. When the internet connection 
would drop I would have to restart the router and the cable modem and 
then reset the DCHP. That always re-established the connection. 
Sometimes just reseting the DCHP would be enough. Comcast said the 
problem wasn't at their end but after buying a new router the problem 
persisted. I finally got stubborn with Comcast support and hung on 
until they moved me up the support chain a couple of levels. They did 
something at their end and the problem went away starting that night. 
It's been a year or more with no problems.
