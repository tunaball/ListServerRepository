Recently my Mother's iBook stopped charging it's battery.   So, I 
bought her a new aftermarket power supply.

I also installed Apple Battery Reset.

So far, no good.   Any power outage and ..." it goes out like a light".

But that's not why I think God Hates Me.

Today, I unplugged the AC cord from my 600 Mhz dual USB iBook just to 
untangle the cord.

And I got the "Running On Reserve Power" message !

I had taken this iBook on the road as recently as last month.   I was 
getting two hours plus on battery with the Airport running.

Now I get a 99% charge good for one freaking minute ?

So, I am pretty sure God Hates Me.

If anyone has an alternate suggestion, you can reach me at church, 
praying for forgiveness.   I just wish I knew what I did wrong.
