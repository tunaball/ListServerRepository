The following will give you more info on battery and its state......
Unplug the power supply and reboot, open terminal and type in
"ioreg -l|grep -i iobatteryinfo" (leave off the quotes) it will give you
stats on your battery condition. The main one is capacity. A new battery
should be 4000 or more. Mine for example says 2097 so I expect that in  
the
next year or so I will have to replace mine so I watch it.

A voltmeter should help to see if it really is charging but it could be  
that the battery is shot and accepting no charge at all.
