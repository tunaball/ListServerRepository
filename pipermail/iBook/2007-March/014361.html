Eric, your iBook may already be wireless capable with an Airport card. Check under the Apple pulldown menu "About this Mac", then "More info" (takes you to System Profiler), then under Network, "AirPort Card". It'll let you know if one is already installed.

If not, used AirPort cards for iBooks are available on eBay for about $60; new off eBay or from an online supplier, they're about $100 plus S&H. The antenna is built into the iBook's top IIRC. I wouldn't mess around with a dongle-type wireless system; too much to go wrong.
