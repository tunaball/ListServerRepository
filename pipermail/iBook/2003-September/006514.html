You might be facing a proprietary format issue here - if the camera is 
fomatting those small CDs in a non-redbook way (ie, requiring special 
reader software to access them) then you're pretty much out of luck.

If this is the case then you're unlikely to find a Mac version unless 
it's been released by Sony themselves.
