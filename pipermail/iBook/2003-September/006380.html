About a year ago there was some discussion of breakup of transitions in 
Keynote when displayed with a projector, using older model dual-USB 
iBooks. I had this problem on my 600MHz iBook, and although the 
transitions looked find on the iBook, there was severe break-up and 
"stuttering" when I projected the image.

I recently tried it again, and the problem has resolved completely! I 
am not sure why, but I suspect that it had to due with either the 
update of Keynote or the update of Quicktime, both of which are free.

For your information, I am running keynote version 1.1, and Quicktime 
version 6.3.

If you are having problems, you might want to try upgrading.

Good luck!
