I think I saw this on MacNTouch today 
<http://www.obdev.at/products/littlesnitch/index.html>. It intercepts 
attempts to send stuff out of your computer and lets you yeah or neah 
it. Might catch a trojan, jumk mailer or just keep you informed of all 
the applications now sending stuff back to home planet about you. 
Woops, why is the RIAA sending email from my computer...  :)

It's 20% of what I've been looking for, the other 80% would tell me 
about the data that is being sent.

Be careful if you use VPC and show it full screen rather than in a 
window. The popup dialog can jam things up since you can't get to it 
but don't panic, it times out so it will go away and hopefully 
everything will be ok.
