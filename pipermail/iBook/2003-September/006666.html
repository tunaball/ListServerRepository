Under Preferences->Composing->Addresses->Configure LDAP you must enter 
the name of the server, you must enter a string that defines the 
"search base", using a defined syntax. For our university server, the 
instructions are here:
http://www.washington.edu/computing/email/ldap.html
in case that would help you figure out how to do it.

Good luck!
