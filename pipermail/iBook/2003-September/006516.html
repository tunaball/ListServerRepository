> I have a CD500 that makes pictures and mpeg files and burns them to

Since you will only be able to get at the images via USB, try Image 
Capture. Open the appl and try various settings for downloads such as 
Image Capture and locate and select iMovie, as a try and even 
Quicktime...

Also, once you connect via usb, the camera should open as a hard drive 
and you can drag and drop the files on it. Look for the icon of your 
camera.
