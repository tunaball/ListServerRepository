You'll like the new iBook  a lot - the 600Mhz was the last model made 
before they vamped the graphics chip in the iBook range to allow them 
to use Quartz Extreme, although some late model 600's had 16Mb cards 
that supported QE, most only had 8Mb cards. The 700 and above have 32Mb 
Radeons.
