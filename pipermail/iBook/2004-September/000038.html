wireless" option on the new iMac (bluetooth keyboard and mouse, Airport

if we were to populate it with this configuration. Then it occurred to 
me: What would happen if you had a roomful of bluetooth keyboards and

for it, and it alone? Or, can one control one's neighbor's computer 
from your mouse and keyboard?
