Hi Ronda,

You have USB 1.1 so you will need to get a Firewire external unless  
you want to cook a turkey while files transfer. The Firewire is often  
$20 or $30 more but it is money well spent. Actually I didn't know if  
you needed a hard drive or a CD burner. The Burner is the biggest  
problem with USB 1.1; A hard drive would not be as bad but you are  
still better off with Firewire. Firewire will also allow for OS  
installs as a backup that is bootable.

LaCie is pricey; try OWC:
http://eshop.macsales.com/shop/firewire/

Their Neptune drives are pretty low cost: $106 for 80g Firewire w/cable
http://eshop.macsales.com/shop/firewire/owc-neptune-drives/

The Elite Drives are $130 for 80g
http://eshop.macsales.com/shop/firewire/fw400-USB2-combo-drives/

Roger
