Depends on the size of the drive. Not the capacity, the size. My 2 1/2" 
FW drive is powered via the FW connection from my Pismo or iBook. A 
friends 3 1/2" external FW Drive needs a power brick to work. Not sure 
this is universal, but I've yet to see an exception.
