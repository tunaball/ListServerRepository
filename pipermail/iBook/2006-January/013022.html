It all really depends on the drive and enclosure.  I bought a cheap  
enclosure with both USB and FW ports.  the drive only gets power when  
plugged into the USB so if I want to use firewire, I still have to  
plug in the usb power cable.  The USB power comes from a 2 headed usb  
cable so I have to have 2 ports to make it work... either 2 usb or 1  
usb and 1 firewire.
I also have the smartdisk that is powered off the firewire 400 or 800  
ports.  Works like a champ but no USB option.  Not all enclosure are  
the same so do your homework on the one you are looking at.

-- Scott
