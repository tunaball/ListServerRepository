I have 900 songs & I have looked all the inso up including album  
covers & lyrics. You can use Pearlyrics widget to automatically  
insert the lyrics but all else must be entered by hand. Selecting  
several at a time works on some fields but they must be the same for  
all songs. Most fields are different for each song.

I recommend the following:

http://www.songfacts.com/
http://www.musicoutfitter.com/
http://www.allmusic.com/
http://www.slothradio.com/covers/
http://www.google.com/imghp?hl=en&tab=wi&q=
http://lyrics.astraweb.com/
http://www.lyricsworld.com/
http://www.lyricsdepot.com/
http://www.stlyrics.com/
