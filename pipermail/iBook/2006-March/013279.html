Jim Manley wrote:

> The IRS has a PDF that you can type on & print it out.
>
> http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/f1040ez.pdf
>
> How does one make PDFs that have this option?

You need an appliation that supports the creation of PDF forms. As  
another lister pointed out, you can do this with Acrobat  
Professional. Some versions of Word (I think) support it as well, but  
I'm not sure how easy it is to use. You can also do it with (the  
abandoned, Classic-only) Adobe FrameMaker but it's not simple.
