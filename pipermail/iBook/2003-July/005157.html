>> way.  I need to move about 100 photos into an album and other than
>> doing it one by one, how can I move all of them in one operation?

In case you didn't get an answer to this yet, open iphoto and drag the 
folder over the blank window. Believe it or not, I once moved 1422 
images that were in a PC only and proprietary format that I couldn't 
read with anything else that I had into the iphoto window just because 
I was "doodling" while talking on the phone. It looked like I had 
choked my cpu because it just sat and spun. Preoccupied with the phone 
conversation, I ignored it for about 10 minutes and suddenly the screen 
popped up with ALL of those images viewable. I was thunderstruck with 
awe. I have since found a proper Mac reader for that particular format 
but I am still amazed at how well and how fast iphoto did the job.
