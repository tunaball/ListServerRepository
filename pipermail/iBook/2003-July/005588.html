I have not tried it as my iBook is not one of the ones it will work with but
I can read German and have the article (I subscribe to the mag).

The first things to check are if you have a Rage or Radeon graphic card?
(using system profiler)...As I understand it this will not work with Rage
cards (these only mirror the output)...(what I have)...

The second thing to do to ensure that this will work with your iBook is to
type the following in Terminal and see what output you get. You can copy and
paste everything inside of the single quotes if you wish to ensure there are
no mistakes. The command is basically a find and print whatever your graphic
card can provide for output from the hardware registry of the machine.

'ioreg -l | grep "display_" '

If you get exactly two lines of output and they list 'DualHead' then you can
use this script to expand the desktop...otherwise, isn't that 12" TFT great
:-)

Let us know what you get from these two steps and then we can proceed from
there if you wish.
