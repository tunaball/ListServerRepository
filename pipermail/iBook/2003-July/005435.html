It might be worthwhile to consider updating your CD/DVD drivers and 
perhaps trashing those preferences and other maintenance stuff if you 
haven't done this in a while. Also, take the CD to another mac known to 
work ok and try the CD there. You should be able to open the cd, see a 
list of files and locate the one you are interested in.

Oh, you might ask your friend if she tried opening the CD on her 
computer and double clicking on the file to open it. If she can't do 
this with the cd, its her problem not yours.
