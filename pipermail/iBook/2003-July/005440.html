Thank you for your help.  Yes, I tried embedding all fonts.  However, 
at work we took a field trip to a prominent printer in Michigan who 
prints the publications we produce.  I'd forgotten one of the people 
told us they deal exclusively with PDF's for their work, and require us 
to send them all art in this format.

I called and talked with one of the help desk people, and he told me 
that the reason Skia was  not embedding in my document was because it 
was a System Font, not a Printer Font.  He said they run into this 
problem occasionally.

When we took the project to my fiance's work and tried it on her flat 
panel iMac, it worked fine because she had the Skia font as a Printer 
Font.

For what it's worth, that's what I learned.
