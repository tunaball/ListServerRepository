Mac Fixit had this report:

Thursday, July 10 2003 @ 07:20 AM PDT
Troubleshooting Mail.app: Comcast.net conflict and solution 	

We previously noted some problems with receiving and sending messages in 
Mail.app when using Comcast.net, associated with the address switch-over 
from attbi.com.

If you are still having problems, try going to the to the Service Center 
section of the Comcast.net Web page, and in your main account, turn off 
the e-mail screener application.

This should allow messages to flow properly in and out of Mail.app once 
again.
