Sorry, I forgot to do that. For X, try Booklightening. you can get it 
from versiontracker. You download the demo and then send for a demo SN 
good for 24 uses.

For OS9, check out Clickbook at bluesquirrel.com  I used that for many 
years on OS 7-9. One of the great things about it is you can control 
the font sizes independent of the document font size.

  I would use it to print out booklet forms of the readme's and enlarge 
it enough I didn't have to strain to read it. Much easier to carry on 
the metro than a sheaf of papers even when they are stapled.
