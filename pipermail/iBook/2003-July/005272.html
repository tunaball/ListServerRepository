Here in Australia there have been several big cases on
the use of regional encoding on DVD's and games. LAst
year the ACCC (Australian consumer affairs basically

illegal with in australia. Even before this however
there was wide spread sales of region free players,
even big chains and dept stores such as Grace
Brotheres an Harvey norman had them on sale.

and use of mod chips in game consoles was legal in
australia. Pretty much because if you bought the

damn well please to it. Although it is still illegal
to copy for anything other than back up purposes.
