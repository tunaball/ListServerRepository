This contradicts the information about AppleCare on Apple's own web 
site; there's nothing I could find in the Terms and Conditions about 
equipment having to have been purchased at the same time as the 
computer, except for Apple Displays (one is covered if you buy it with

computer, or if they're included with the computer). AirPort Cards and 
AirPort Base stations are "included with the coverage of the computer 
with which they're used," according to the Terms and Conditions; 
there's no specific information about when they had to have been 
purchased. (Though AppleCare apparently isn't available to consumers in 
Florida.)
