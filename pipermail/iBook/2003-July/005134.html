I believe that the iBook dissipates heat via the keyboard, so it cannot 
do much of anything with the lid closed other than sleep.

Hopefully someone will correct me if I'm wrong, but I think that if you 
want the iBook to "wake" and do things automatically (like answer the 
phone for a fax call, for example), the lid needs to be open.
