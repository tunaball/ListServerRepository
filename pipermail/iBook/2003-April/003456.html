If you want to connect in an internet cafe w/ Airport..
Set network to Airport connect using DHCP..
When you install airport card, you can have a display across top menu (it
looks like a V)
drop down from there and choose the "base Station" available and you will
see a signal strength indicator on "V" and launch your browser..
If they have it configured correctly on their end you are on-line..
