This has happened to me often when I've been off on a trip, reconfigured
several times for different dialup, Ethernet and other wireless connections,
then got back to my office and couldn't connect using my regular setup (Snow
ABS and DSL router). The way I've solved the problem is to run the Airport
Admin utility and/or the the Airport Setup Assistant (both found in the
Applications: Utilities folder in OSX.) Of course if you're still in 9, this
may not work.
