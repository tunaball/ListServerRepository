You can't, at least not directly. You need a way to convert the S-Video 
to digital form. That means you need one of the following:

1. A device like the Canopus or the Dazzle Hollywood DV Bridge, OR
2. A digital camcorder that has what's referred to as "passthrough" 
(analog video ports for the in, a firewire port for the out).
