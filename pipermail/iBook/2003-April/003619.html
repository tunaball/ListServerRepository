My 12" iBook suffered the exact same problem. I couldn't stray more than 10
feet from the base station. It went back to Apple for repair.

The antenna is located in the lid and the cable passes through the hinge. I
don't really know what the exact problem was, but paperwork that came back
with it indicated the following parts that were replaced.

600-8601 PCBA REED SWITCH, R1 U220, P29
600-8599 PCBA ANTENNA COAXIAL CABLE U220

AppleCare took care of the problem. It shipped out on a Monday afternoon
(via the box Apple sent overnight) and came back to me on Wednesday morning
less than 48 hours after sending it.

Hope you purchased AppleCare. I bought it when I bought my iBook and I'm
glad I did.
