Further to everyone else's answers, I have a 12" 600Mhz iBook that 
plays DVDs beautifully.

Some notes to add though:

Some DVDs won't let you copy to the hard drive as an anti-piracy 
measure. You can probably get around this but I had a few problems with 
it on some disks (I was experimenting with region 1 discs in a region 2 
dvd drive, i know that VLC will play them but I was wondering if I 
could fool Apple's DVD player into playing the disk from the hard drive 
and ignoring the region code). My iBook's DVD drive has had a firmware 
update to remove the illegal region coding.

I find that I can get to just past the Mines of Moria on LotR: 
Fellowship of the Ring on a full charge with the screen brightness near 
full. That's about 2 hours in total. My battery is 18 months old now, 
so that's pretty good.
