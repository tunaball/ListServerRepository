The only substantial, documented problems with the earler version of 
10.2.8 affected one aspect of a specific model: the 10Base-T connection 
on the Power Macintosh G4 (Gigabit Ethernet) with dual-450MHz or 
dual-500MHz processors. There were scattered reports of other problems, 
but there are almost always scattered reports of other problems, and 
they're not necessarily due to a problem with the update.
