I was talking about my iPod. It's one of the first-gen 5gb ones and has 
a shiny acrylic top to it, which had got a bit scratched from car keys 
and the like while in my pocket. So I took to it with the Turtle Wax and 
after 10 mins of rubbing came out looking like new.

Encouraged by this I tried it on the lid of my iBook but got sore arms 
from all the rubbing before I achieved anything much. Then again, I'm 
not too worried the scratches since my iBook goes everywhere with me and 
does have quite a tough time, so it's only to be expected.
