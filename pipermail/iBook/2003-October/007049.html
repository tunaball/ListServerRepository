I think I have a solution/workaround. I have only tested this with a 
monitor, not with a projector.

If I go to System Preferences -> Displays and choose Millions for the 
colors instead of Thousands, the breakup goes away completely! It is 
counterintuitive that a higher bit depth would give less problems, but 
there it is!

DTL
