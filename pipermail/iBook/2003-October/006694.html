Well, I've seen this sort of thing.   On PowerMacs, you can get an odd 
flash when changing resolutions.   Or after a Force Restart.    I think 
I saw it once on my iBook.   Scared me but it went away.

I'm not an Apple Tech, (I wish!)  but I'd guess that it's a static 
burst of some kind.   You could look into getting an anti-static mat 
for your iBook.   I know I read about these someplace.   The plastic 
case isn't as well grounded at Titanium or Aluminum would be.

Put the iBook on the anti-static mat and ground the mat to a water 
pipe.   I had to do this with my PowerMac because my cousin has a 
freaky ability to crash computers by touching them...but just some 
times a month...
