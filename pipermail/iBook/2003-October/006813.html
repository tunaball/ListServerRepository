> Open CPU Monitor and select Display Expanded Window so you can see the

10.2.8 gave me a noticable reduction in idle cpu usage, meaning that 
while doing nothing, less of the cou is being used than before. When on 
battery this adds up and gives me at least an extra half hour over 
before. So 10.2.8 compares very nicely to earlier version of X.
