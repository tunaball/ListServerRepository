What worked for me was to remove the battery and power supply, let 
the machine sit for awhile (the bottom of the iBook was warm, don't 
know why but I think it's indicative of something out of whack, so I 
let it cool down), then start it up with the external power supply. 
Reinstall battery. Life goes on.
