Apple released 3 new iBook models, all G4 based.  The 12" iBook is 
now 800MHz, and the 14" comes in 933Mhz and 1GHz.  No SuperDrive, no 
Gigabit Ethernet.  AirPort Extreme, USB 2.0, and BlueTooth.  You can 
read the full rundown at <http://www.apple.com/ibook/>.
