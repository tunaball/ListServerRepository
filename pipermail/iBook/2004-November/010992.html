   Targus makes a small two-button with a nice scroll wheel. I picked it 
up at Best Buy for $80. It comes with a BT USB dongle (threw it on the 
PC desktop). It's model number MOBT90UO. If you have really big hands, 
I wouldn't suggest it. It pairs up without a hitch (every once in a 
while I have to "browse" the mouse in order to get the link to kick in) 
and gives you full functionality without adjusting any settings. It 
runs on 2 AAAs and has an on/off switch.

   I play Battlefield 1942 with it and the response seems to be really 
good.
