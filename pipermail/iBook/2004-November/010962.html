Have you tried turning the monitor on and then connecting to the ibook? Have 
you tried using the displays window in prefences to detect displays?

found no problems from my g4 ibook with several different monitors.

Just as an aside there is a great hack, Screen Spanning Doctor, at 
http://www.macupdate.com/info.php/id/15401 that allows the activation of 
screen spanning on ibooks (for some reason automatically turned off by 
Apple!) I'm not sure if it would be ok on your ibook but worth a look.
