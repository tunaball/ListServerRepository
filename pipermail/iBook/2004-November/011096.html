I wouldn't even bother. It is VERY difficult to get correct color from 
an LCD and as you have noticed, viewing angle and background lighting 
will change the color and contrast of your display. If you have to have 
color critical enough to require profiles and calibration, an iBook LCD 
ain't gonna work. Get a decent CRT monitor and calibrate that instead.
