On Oct 30, 2004, at 7:19 PM, CJ wrote:

> Hi, everyone
> My iBook's ac adapter tip snapped off (it was a yoyo).  I need to 
> replace it, but before I give $80 to Apple for a new one, I'd rather 
> buy a MacAlly PowerBook/iBook AC Adapter 
> (http://macally.com/spec/specialties/accessories/psac4.html) for ~$30 
> shipped.  Does anyone have any experience with this ac adapter?  Is it 
> better than the yoyo?  (Well, just about any ac adapter is better than 
> the yoyo, since this was my 3rd one).

I've been using the MacAlly adapter with my 700MHz white iBook for a 
few months. and it works just fine. The model number is AP5024 and is 
supposed to work with the iBook and PowerBooks. The only thing that it 
doesn't have is the ring around the plug that glows orange when 
charging and turns green when charged. The only way you tell what this 
one is doing is to check the menubar to see progress. Hope this helps.
