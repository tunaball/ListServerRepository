The PC2700 RAM does NOT work in the latest G4 iBooks. Found that out 
the hard way. Just get anything that MATCHES the specs. If it says 
PC2100 then that's what you should get.

Make sure the company has a lifetime exchange guarantee. It's a rather 
common thing to have RAM arrive DOA or go bad. You want someone who 
will stand behind it no matter what. Most RAM suppliers will do that. 
Just don't end up with one who won't.
