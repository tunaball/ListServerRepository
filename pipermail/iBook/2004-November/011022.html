If no one has told you, yet on the iBook, I have a 12 inch G3 and a 
14" G4. Because of the increased size of the G4's battery, I get more 
time with it than I do on the 12" G3. I get a little over 3 hours and 
a little over 5 hours respectively.

Of course it depends on what you are doing while running on battery, 
but just work, no tunes, no heavy computing, no airport, no need to 
write or burn cds, and in the shade so I can dim the screen, that is 
about what I get.
