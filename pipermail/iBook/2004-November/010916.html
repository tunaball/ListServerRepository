I've had the MacAlly adaptor for a long time and it has held up better 
than my Apple adaptor.  However, it doesn't have the ring that glows 
orange/green to show charging activity.  Also, the cord is shorter.

On the positive side, it is nice and small, lightweight, and better for 
portability when traveling light.
