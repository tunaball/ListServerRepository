Don't rule out buying ram locally. I went into my CompUSA, showed them the
competitive catalog prices and told them they had to match it. They did, but
the interesting part is, I put the chip in right there at the counter...and

brand, but in a different package (?), I put it in, and...perfect! Ram is
very iffy, and buying locally can save you a lot of wasted time.
