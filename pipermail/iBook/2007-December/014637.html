There are Dual Layer disks and there are Double Sided.  There are 
even Double Sided, Dual Layer for a total of 4 layers with 19 Gb of 
space.  I don't know of DVD-Rs like that but there could be.

Dual Layer disks can be burned without changing the disk.  The only 
double sided disks I've run into are video disks, often the opposite 
side has an alternate version of the movie, wide screen vs full 
screen.  These disks have to be flipped manually, at least in my 
player.
