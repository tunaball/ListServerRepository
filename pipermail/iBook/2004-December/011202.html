That's a sit-com situation if I ever heard one. Although I believe, 
those two AC adapters MAY be interchangeable. At least certainly, the 
one from the Al-Book will power the iBook 600 without any difficulties.
You can't tell from the AC cable or the AC direct plug (plugs the brick 
directly into your power socket) because Apple so brilliantly made the 
AC Adapter brick plug-swapable.

If I recall with my iBook (G3) 600 that its powerplug (that goes into 
the unit) was silvery and not iBook white.
Another guess would be an inspection of the bricks themselves -- the 
iBook's might look older. Maybe. (More scratches, dirt, scuffs).
Only last thing to do is to put on your reading glasses and inspect the 
power specs on the sides of the bricks -- the Al-Book's should have 
something bigger/later about it, whether it's power output or 
model/serial number or best yet, a later copyright year.
