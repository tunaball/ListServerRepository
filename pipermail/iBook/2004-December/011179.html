On 12/6/04 8:10 AM, "Russ Edwards" <russ.edwards at spymac.com> wrote:

> Thanks for all the help.  I found out two things doing my own research that
> people might want to know.
> 1.  You can burn as much as you want, according to the web site anyway.

Actually, you can make as many as you want of non-iTunes music playlists.
If there is even 1 purchased music from iTunes in a playlist, you can only
burn that playlist a certain amount of times.  I think it's 7 times, but I'm
not sure if that's changed here recently.  The number of times is listed in
the iTunes license agreement that you agree to.
