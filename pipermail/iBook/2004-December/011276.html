I have Sprint and I too use my cell phone for all my long distance calls.
But I also use their "Sprint 50 at home" (I think that's what it's called)
long distance service on my land line. This way, I get 50 free long distance
minutes at home, and you could use those for your faxing needs. I've never
yet gone over the 50 minutes, and my entire monthly long distance bill is
$3.53 (which is for taxes, etc.).
