I just resurrected a Minolta Dual for my brother - he inherited my 
souped up G4 when I got my 20" imac G5.
The G4 is a digital audio with a 1.3 owc processor and an adaptec scsi 
card and a gig of ram. The Dual is definitely scsi and runs easily on 
the adaptec pci card - with Panther no driver is needed for the adaptec 
card.  I bought VueScan for him and it is so easy to use and the 
results are great.  VueScan will also control his usb Epson Perfection 
1250 flat bed scanner.

Now as far as connecting scsi to usb or fw, I don't know.  I have tried 
my ancient 230 SyQuest via usb2scsi converter  - no dice, but my 
Castlewood Orb with the same converter works fine, even on my new G5.  
Anyone want a scsi SyQuest or two, and a dozens and a half 135 and 230 
cartridges?  Yes, I know I have a couple 256 usb2 flash drives too.
