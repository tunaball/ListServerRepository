> My daughter has a Classic Clamshell with a dead battery.  Where should 
> I look to find her one?

Other World Computing has new iBook batteries with additional capacity 
over and above the original Apple-supplied models; I'm using one of 
them now. Just be aware that they need to be carefully (and fully) 
charged and discharged over the first half-dozen cycles to make sure 
they reach full capacity. (There's information on the site about this.) 
And they're covered by (I think) a 1-Year warranty.

See <http://eshop.macsales.com/shop/powerbook/batteries/> (at the 
bottom right of the page).
