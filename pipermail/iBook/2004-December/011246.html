> This is on my iBook, running 10.2.8, in case that matters.

Sounds like the WindowServer process may have crashed and restarted. I 
think I've seen that happen once or twice.

No telling what caused the crash; I start seeing static zaps this time 
of year & that might have had something to do with it. If the system 
seems to be slow or acting flaky, running the stuff in Disk Utility 
(First Aid, Repair Permissions) might help. Sometimes, logging out & in 
is enough to clear up pokiness (I suspect Safari in 10.2.8 has a memory 
leak, but I just switched to Camino).

If everything continues to work properly, I wouldn't worry about it.
