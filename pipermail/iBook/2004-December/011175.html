This happens to me occasionally. It usually means I have to reset my DSL
modem. The method is: turn off the DSL modem, then unplug it, then pull the
power cord from the Airport Base Station and leave both off for two minutes.
Then simply repeat the process in reverse, and usually I'm connected again
and good to go. You don't say what your setup is, but when I get that
message, that's usually what works for me. Luckily it only happens once a
month, at most.
