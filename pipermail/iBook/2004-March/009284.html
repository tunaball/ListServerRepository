I use the the yoyo and the white adapter interchangeably between
a 2001 iceBook and a 2003 12" PB G4.  However, the 2001/yoyo combo
sometimes needs a bit of jiggling to get contact and start charging.

Personally, I prefer the white adapter for travelling since it
doesn't need the extra length of power cord to plug it in.  However,
one of the fold-up "wings" has a broken plastic pin, so it takes a
little bit of care to open and close it.
