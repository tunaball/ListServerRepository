Although there are many other email apps available, I find that Entourage is
well laid out and everything is easily found (especially compared to Mail
but this may be due to the fact that I have been using MS products for
several years now). If you are familiar with MS Office from work or previous
experiences then Entourage might be a good choice.

If localization is important, either Mail or Entourage will probably be made
available quicker than others.

On a more entertaining note, there are sound sets available for Entourage
that I have not seen for Mail and if you like to tweak the user interface to
a personal level this may be of interest?

<http://www.soundsetcentral.com>
