Brian--
What OS are you using?

I am not quite sure what you mean by airport favorites, but to make 
changes to your connection configurations in OS 10.3:
Open the Network Preferences, either by opening System 
Preferences>Internet and Network Preferences, or under the 
Apple>Location>Network Preferences. Use the Location pull-down menu; at 
its bottom is Edit Locations. Click on that, and a window will open 
where you can highlight the configuration and delete it.

OS 10.2 was similar, but the interface looked somewhat different.

If you're using OS 9, I *think* I remember that you can open the Remote 
Access control panel and delete a configuration from there. That might 
not be the best way to do it, though.

Hope this is what you are trying to do...
