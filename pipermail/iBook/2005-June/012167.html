Given that it is a G3 iBook, I'm assuming that it will only support  
the B speed.  You can often find a used originalAirport card for sale  
at the LEM swap list.  Otherwise, I would try eBay, or just a google  
search. You'll wind up spending abotu the same - $50, but you'll have  
guaranteed compatibility, won't have to deal with 3rd party patches,  
and it will fit nicely inside your iBook (the install is super easy),  
and you won't have to worry about having anything sticking out of the  
side of your iBook...where it could get lost or damaged.

Just my 2 cents...
