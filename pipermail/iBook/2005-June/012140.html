I did this with an orinoco card in my ibook G3 600 when you have the 
antenna connected it sits about 3 mils over the lip where the ram cover 
goes, so when the keyboard is seated on-top there is a very slight 
bulge in the center. If you did not cover the base of the keyboard with 
a piece of electrical insulating tape where the antenna touches and 
also the little shelf where it sit, after a period of time it kills the 
antenna and the card, My first card lasted just under 1 year.  but the 
cards are £16. so easy to replace if they die. i also have one in my G4 
sawtooth to get it in you need a hack saw and a steady hand, but the 
reception on these are alot better than with the airport cards.
