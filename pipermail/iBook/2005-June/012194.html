I've worked on laptops for many years now. Usually the symptoms described
are usually caused by either loose connections or the cable to the LCD or
the logic board. Other times this behavior is caused by frayed cables that
either have or are intermittently failing. If the problem is the former it
would require both the shell of the screen and the main body of the iBook to
be disassembled and have the connections reseated. If the latter the same
disassembly and replacement of the cable(s).
