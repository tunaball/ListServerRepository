It gets better. Hit the Radio "playlist," select a genre, and see how many outfits are streaming the kind of music you like. Even on a dialup, you can use the 24K streams if you don't have a really crappy connection (I did it quite often). If there's a website associated with the "station," they often have links to other formats and lower speeds if you need them. In other words, if there's not a 24K stream in the Radio list, one still may be available.

Two of my favorites are http://soma.fm and http://di.fm
