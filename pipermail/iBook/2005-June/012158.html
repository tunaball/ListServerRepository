>iBook to drive a not-yet-purchased Dell 2005FPW display at its 
>native resolution of 1680 x 1050.  Obviously I'll have to drive it

>Anybody on the list have experience with this or similar

I've used it, mostly at 1024x768 driving a NTSC display (TV).  I 
don't know if it will go as high as 1600 and I'm really doubtful it 
will do an odd size like 1680x1050.  I would check that out before 
you spring for the monitor.
