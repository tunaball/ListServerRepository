One thing to note about this is that the song you download from iTunes is in
AAC format with digital rights management (DRM). Apple allows you to burn a
CD backup (they recommend this in case of a failure with the HD etc...) and
that CD must get converted to normal music format (no doubt with DRM still)
as it does play in *normal* car CD players etc...

Officially there is a limit to the amount of CD copies you can make of the
iTunes item and also the number of computers that can listen to the music
purchased from iTunes at the same time.
