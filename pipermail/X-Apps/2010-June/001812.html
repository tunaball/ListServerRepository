At 09:56 -0700 6/22/10, Ed Graf wrote:
>I have Word 2008, rarely used.
>Any ideas on getting a .pdf to be readable in Word 2008?

Have you tried copy and paste from the PDF into a blank WORD window?

There are numerous reasons why it might not work:

.. The author may have protected the PDF with a password.

.. Your PDF reader won't allow copying of the text.

.. The PDF is really a cover sheet for a bit mapped image - You need optical character recognition for that.
