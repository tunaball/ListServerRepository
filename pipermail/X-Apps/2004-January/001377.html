> I have no problems with iSync. It works very well with HotSync Manager.

> 
> - sync the hand-held's memos with an iApp. Unfortunately Apple hasn't
> come up with iMemo (how difficult would *that* be?) So, if you go the

> have to either 1) keep Palm Desktop around so you can get to your notes

> 
>    http://mac-huwis.lut.ac.uk/~wis/programs/NoteTaker/NoteTaker.html
> 
> There is at least one other desktop application that syncs with memos.

>

> keeps automatic backup copies of the data in ~/Library/SyncService.
> Very handy.
