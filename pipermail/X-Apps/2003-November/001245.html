Yes, there is a way to make it stop. Which OS version are you using? In 
Panther if you go to the Mail Preferences>Accounts, under Account 
Information you will see 'Outgoing Mail Server', which contains a list 
of all present and past outgoing 'accounts'. Delete the .mac one.  In 
Jaguar you can do the same thing, it's just a bit more time consuming 
and I'd have to look up the process in my archives. It's not NEAR as 
intuitive/easy as in Panther
