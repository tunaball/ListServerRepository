Hi Jerry,

I've been using Mail with an IMAP account for some time now, and Junk 
is working fine. Have you turned it on in the Preferences menu? You may 
also have it in training mode, whereby it flags the message as Junk but 
leaves it in your in-box. You can disable that and enable the next 
option below it which instructs Mail to move Junk to the appropriate 
mailbox.
