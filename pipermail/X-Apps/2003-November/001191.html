I'm sure you've already thought about this, but it's probably a bad 
idea to update anything while you're in the middle of a project 
(unless, of course, it's a very long project and you have another 
computer you can run on).

At the very least, beg, borrow or buy a FireWire 400 (not 800) drive 
big enough to clone your whole hard drive contents to. Clone with 
Carbon Copy Cloner. Start up with that drive. Check everything out. 
Start up with your internal drive. Unmount and shut down the external.

Now do the Panther upgrade and test.
