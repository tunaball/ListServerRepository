I had the try-out version of PStill from their web-site and massive 
problems with printing fron Safari.

It took one e-mail telling them which URL does not print and I got the 
fix as a beta of the next version of PStill within two days.
In addition I searched for a possibility to compine distinct PDF's and 
to provide my (quite a lot) pdf's with page numbers, so I bought it 
then.

My suggestion: Install the try-out and if it doesn't work with Excel, 
write an e-mail to them telling your problem and provide them with a 
samle file which cannot be printed. They might program a fix and help 
you.
