On 10/25/03 4:55 PM, "Jim Soriano" <jsoriano at sumbeach.com> wrote:

> Looks like my installation of OS 10.3 on my TiPBG4-800 has stomped on
> at least part of Palm's HotSync software.  The Palm Desktop 4.0 app
> runs fine, but when I log into Panther I get the error message:
> 
> "The application 'Transport Monitor" could not be launched because of a
> shared library error: "8<Transport Monitor><Transport
> Monitor><HotSyncLib.PPC><>""
> 
> The error message also shows up if I try to do just about anything
> other than run Palm Desktop.  When I try to reinstall the package from
> the distribution I get a message saying "Sorry, the operation could not
> be completed due to a System error: (Access Denied)."  I do have
> administrator privileges.
> 
> Any ideas on how to fix this situation?

Go into your Accounts pref pane and first delete the transport and Palm
Desktop login items from the Startup Items tab. Then restart. Then try
reinstalling Palm Desktop. This worked for me. :-)
