You can use AOL as a dial-up ISP without using their
interface. Just open any browser after you're
connected and surf away. You could put a shortcut to
aol mail on the desktop, if you really want to use it.

forwarded to a web based email address. I'm not sure,
but I'd be surprised if AOL didn't offer some kind of
forwarding.
