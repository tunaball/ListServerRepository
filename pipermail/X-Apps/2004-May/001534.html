What I'd suggest then is to quit Mail, go into your Mail folder 
(/Users/<yourUsernameHere>/Library/Mail/) and in there will be a folder 
corresponding to each of your email accounts. Go into each of these and 
remove the Junk.mbox files. Relaunching Mail will create fresh Junk 
folders.
