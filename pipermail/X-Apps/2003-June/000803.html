They're at:
/Users/[your account name]/Library/Safari/Bookmarks.plist, from the
terminal, or simply in the the Safari folder inside the Library folder in a
Finder window.  You'll need a program like Safari Bookmark Exporter...

http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/19266

...to do much with them, since Apple has them in its own format.

Hope this helps,
