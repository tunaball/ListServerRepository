I have a client use VueScan, $40. You can even download it to see if 
it works, before buying it. Works great on just the setup you 
describe, SCSI adapter (Adaptec) plus UMAX Astra 1220S.

I did find I needed to set the Energy Saver settings so machine would 
not go to sleep. But that was not the fault of the scanner or the 
VueScan software; I believe it is between OS X and the SCSI adapter.

This is on a Blue & White G3, OS 10.2.3.

Still, if you have problems, consider that $100 might get you a brand 
new scanner, USB, that may just work with OS X.
