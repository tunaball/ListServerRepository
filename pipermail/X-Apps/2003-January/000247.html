Hello
There is another, faster way to do this

1. Pop a blank CD into your CDRW
2. A dialog window will appear asking you what you want to do with this 
blank CD ?
3. type in Title you want
4. Hit Open Finder
5. What appears to be an empty 660mb disk (image) will automatically  
appear on your desktop
6. Drag files you want to burn into this desktop icon
7.Drag image to the  Trash icon in the dock which automatically will 
turn into the Radiation Warning CD Burner Icon
8.Hit  dock icon and burning will commence
