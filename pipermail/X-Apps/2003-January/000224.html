The original poster mentioned having the files show up when he searched 
for ".". This leads me to believe that the mysterious missing files 
have names which begin with a period.

Such files would be hidden in OS X. The only workaround is to rename 
them, using something other than "." as the first character. Yes, there 
are other apps which will show them, and hacks to make OS X show hidden 
files, but these are being distributed to other folks, who should not 
be expected to jump through such hoops.
