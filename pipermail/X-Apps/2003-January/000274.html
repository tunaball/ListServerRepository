I had that problem on occasion as well and solved it only after some 
sleuthing.

I could force Mail to retrieve most times, but after I checked with t 
he ISP and made the adjustments, things worked better.

I also found that edits to existing settings didn't always "catch" and 
deleting the account and re-entering it fresh solved the problem.
