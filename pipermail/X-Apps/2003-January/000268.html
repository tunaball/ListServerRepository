> I use VueScan with an Microtek E6 (SCSI). It seems that I have to

>
> I have an G4 with OS X.2.3. and an Adaptec SCSI Card (a cheap one).

I have a UMAX scanner working with VueScan, and I use the following 
work-around:
Boot up with your scanner on.
Start up VueScan and verify that your scanner is showing up.
Hide the VueScan application.
Unplug the scanner.

When ready to scan, plug in the scanner and click on the VueScan dock 
item.

While not perfect, I can save the life of my scanner bulb, yet not need 
to reboot every time I need to scan.
