Another Former User of CopyPaste, I now use iSnip <http://isnip.net>.
a newcomer to the space, it has a user configurable clipboard history
defaulting to all clips, and now offers 'rules' which can move things
into various folders for saving as an option. you can edit any text clip.

its FREE.

its one remaining downside (upside?) is that its currently text only.
