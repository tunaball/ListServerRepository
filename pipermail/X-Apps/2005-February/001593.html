	I'm trying to find the Mac equivalent of a Widoze clipboard utility 
called Clipmate.

	It is simply a clipboard with a hundred-clip memory.  It is quite easy 
to use.  The Mac clipboard utilities I have found seem more complicated 
than they need to be.

	Clipmate remembers the last hundred clips and works with the standard 
clipboard commands.  When one copies something to the clipboard instead 
of erasing the last clip, it erases the one-hundredth-ago clip and 
keeps everything in between.  You never see it until you need to use 
one of those previous clips.  Then you open it and find the clip you 
need.  It also includes some other bells and whistles, but the 
important thing is that it's simple to use.

	The Mac clipboard utilities I have found want more attention from the 
user.  They want the user to manually stick a clip into a pigeonhole or 
possess other unnecessary complications.

	Someone please point me to a similar utility for Mac.
