If you belong to .Mac, you can download the free utility Mac Slides
Publisher. Select a group of JPEG photos in the Finder or iPhoto, and then
drag them onto the Mac Slides Publisher icon. Mac Slides Publisher will
automatically optimize them for size and then upload them to your iDisk.
