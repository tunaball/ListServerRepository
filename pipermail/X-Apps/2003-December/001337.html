Since I wrote that note, psync is available for Panther. Check the 
Bombich Software site.

CCC has a setting to delete files (and folders, presumably) not on the 
source disk. When I want a completely clean, new clone, I erase the 
target drive first.

You say you want to copy "3 specific (plus hidden files)" but a word 
got left out. If you want to clone only certain folders, you can do 
that in CCC, but only if the folders at the top level of the drive. If 
you want more control than that, you'll need a more complex 
application, such as TriBackup.
