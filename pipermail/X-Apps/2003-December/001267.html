You got close:
<your hd>/library/scripts/mail scripts/Manage SMTP Servers.scpt

This will let you delete inactive SMTP hosts.

Also be aware that some ISPs will not allow SMTP traffic other than 
their own servers... on a prodigy.net account, I had to setup my 
verizon.com smtp to send email when I was dialed into verizon.com 
sites....  some webmail services dont even do smtp anymore, you get 
your pop mail from pop.yourfancydomain.com and send smtp thru 
smtp.verizon.com.... how is that for confusing?
