Apple iPod discussion board asking apple to fully implement the new software

other features of the new iPods. I would suggest as many iPod owners as
possible join the petetion. Seems to me if it's technically possible it
should be a no-brainer for them, but like other Apple oversights it may take
a squeaky wheel to get their attention.
