Good suggestion. It's a very good resource that many people don't seem 
to know about. Just FYI, go to the Apple web site (www.apple.com) and 
click the "Support" tab. I did this for Glen's problem and typed "blue 
screen on startup" in the search field.

The first result under the Documents section is titled "Mac OS X: 
Troubleshooting a Startup Issue" and can be found at 
<http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=106464> . It covers the 
blue screen phenomenon, but since the instructions are fairly long, I'd 
suggest visiting the site and maybe printing them out to follow.

Hope that helps.
