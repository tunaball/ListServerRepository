I used to use the AW macros with a fairly elaborate spreadsheet that we 
use to keep track of medications. When I updated to OS X, I bought AW 6

pain because the SS requires that I jump around a lot and the macro 
feature was a natural for this.

the AW SS application and is not cheap. I do also use Quickeys for 
other things such as automatically checking mail and checking library 
book and video return dates.
