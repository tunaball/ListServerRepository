> I'm sure there'll be a lot of comment on the changes to the brushed 
> aluminum look of iTunes 4, with its flatter buttons. The one thing 
> that bothers me, though, is that text is not antialiased. It looks 
> pretty bad. Am I the only one seeing this?

Apparently I'm not. MacFixit has an item today about this and the 
solution:

"In the System Preferences under the General Preferences pane, there is 
an option for font smoothing.  Mine was set to turn off text smoothing 
for font sizes 9 and smaller.  I changed this to 8 or smaller, and then 
quit and re-launched iTunes, and viola, the font looked good again."
