The problem isn't finding a program, the problem is doing the basic 
research to figure out how to do voice recognition of this type. I've 
never used the dictation programs available, but what I read is that 
they are just about getting there when you can train them extensively 
on one voice. Dealing with interview tapes, especially on the phone or 
from an auditorium situation, is going to be a while. I have hope that 
digital recorders might make some difference, perhaps used with some 
noise-canceling technology like is built into certain earphone sets.

Meanwhile, a good medical transcriber is the way to go if there's room 
in the budget to pay one.
