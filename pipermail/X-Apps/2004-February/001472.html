The top of the heap for Mac CAD is Vectorworks 
<http://www.nemetschek.net/>
If by home design, you mean building a house from scratch, this is it. 
You feed in what type of foundation you want, sketch a floor plan, tell 
the program what spacing you want on the studs and what finish for the 
interior, and it builds you a 3d model with charts and graphs of how 
many bags of cement,  2x4's and sheets of drywall you will need! Then 
with another click it does the blueprints.

If you just want to play with interior design, I found this at MacUpdate
http://www.macupdate.com/info.php/id/13638
