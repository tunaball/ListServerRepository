At 9:35 PM on 3/26/03,  Geno Endicott wrote about "[X-Apps] start up items"

>  How do I add an application making in to a start up item in OS X?

Launch System Preferences. Click on the blue spot labeled Login 
Items. Click the Add button. Navigate to the app you want to launch 
at login and Choose it.  There you are!
