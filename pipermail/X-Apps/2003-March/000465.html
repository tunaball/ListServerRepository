I created several rules, two "Inappropriate Words" filters, and one for 
phrases. It catches most spam, and the rest is tagged by Apple's Junk 
filter.

The rules actually delete the messages, while I let the Junk filter 
just tag them.

I have issues, however, where messages are encoded such that the 
filters for words that I set up do not catch them! I am not too sure 
how to get around this. If you take a look at the raw source for such a 
message, you get a whole block of gibberish.
