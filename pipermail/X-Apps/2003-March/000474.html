FWIW, I paid for the upgrade to FAXstf X Pro today. All is as 
advertised, as far as I can tell. The installer seems to clean up after 
itself, there is only one application in your face, and that's a much 
nicer looking one. Viewing faxes is very nice. Resend and a few other 
nice touches are much easier.

One weird point: There's no word about how or where FAXstf stores fax 
files. None of the fax names are to be found on my hard drive, there's 
no folder with the "folder" names shown in the FAXstf application. 
Anyone know about this?
