Not possible on Mail.app AFAIK without script help.

Check out Eudora - Composed message is queued for delivery then you 
change queue to several delivery options including after specific date 
and time.

I switched to Mail.app from Eudora about a year ago mainly to see if 
Mail.app would handle my needs. It does.

There's a free version at 
http://www.eudora.com/products/eudora/download/
