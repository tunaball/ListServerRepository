The Apple doc I quoted gives low barrier to glass and high to 
bulletproof glass, fwiw.

Having fun here experimenting.

I got the signal to go from 53 to 65 by moving a metal object off the 
shelf where the base station sits.

Flesh and bones is one of the worst barriers -- my partner's standing 
between me and the base station was enough to send the signal into red 
territory (40).

And moving the laptop is enough to interrupt the signal, it takes a 
couple of seconds to recover signal strength. Perhaps, walking around 
with MacStumbler can give false negative results.
