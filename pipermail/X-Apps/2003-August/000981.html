On Saturday, August 23, 2003, at 09:01  PM, Thomas Karns wrote:

> On Saturday, August 23, 2003, at 05:46  PM, John McGilvary wrote:
>
>> The problem is that there are some accounts listed for my old ISP 
>> that it seems to default to.
>> Is there a way to remove the non existent accounts from the list of 
>> possibilities?
>
> Locate the file "Script Menu.menu" located in-> 
> /Applications/AppleScript  Double click the file.  This will place a 
> little script menu icon on the right side of the menu bar.  Click the 
> icon and navigate to--> Mail Scripts, Manage SMTP Servers and click on 
> it.  Select the servers you don't need and click OK to delete them.
>
> Tom K.
