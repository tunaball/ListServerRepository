There is a great app called "WhatSize".  I got it off of versiontracker 
a while ago.  The link in the app goes to 
http://www.id-design.com/WhatSize .

It goes through and gets the size of every folder in any mounted volume 
and you can erase folders from it as well...  worth a look for spring 
cleaning, plus for finding out who is storing large files on your 
server.  Also allows hidden folders to be seen.
