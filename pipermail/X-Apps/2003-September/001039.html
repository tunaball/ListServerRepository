Nither mgetty nor vgetty are ported to Mac (according  to freshmeat.net).

You'll need a new modem because the Apple Modem that comes in the newer Macs
(I think anything G3 or newer) aren't "voice capable" anyway.

Try CoMa X Voice/Pro 6.4.1 for Mac OS X <http://hartmann-b.de/index_en.html>

I've never used it but it claims to do what you want.  Their site is in
German and somewhat broken English - they do list the modems that will work
and USB <-> Serial adaptors that you will need; do read that part carefully
before you buy.


Good Luck,
