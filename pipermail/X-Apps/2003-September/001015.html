wrote:

> This reminded me of a tool that I've long thought would be an 
> excellent utility. It would make a snapshot of your Mac's hard drive 
> and save it as a text file.

There are quite a few tools able to do that. BBEdit Lite (free) can 
insert a folder listing into a text document; disk cataloguing 
utilities (such as Disk Tracker or CD Finder) were designed to do 
precisly that, and even to have a Finder-like interface; and, for the 
lean-and-mean philosophy, you can just use ls in Terminal and redirect 
its output to a text file.
