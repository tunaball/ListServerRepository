best bet is to stay with Oxford or Initio based devices

check sites such as http://fwdepot.com and others for a variety of 
enclosures

do you know what type of chip is on the board inside the ProMax 
enclosure?  Should be either an LSI or an Oxford 911 (or 900) chip

if its an Oxford chip you might want to try reflashing the firmware for 
the chip and see if that makes a difference.
