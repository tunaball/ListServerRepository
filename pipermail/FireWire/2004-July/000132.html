Do you have another Firewire case that you know is good... and a known 
good drive?

If so, you might try putting your unmountable drive in the known good 
case and see what happens.

If it mounts, you have some idea that the case is perhaps the problem 
center.

I've got two Western Digital drives that I had to buy new cases for 
after the Oxford 911 bridges (or something else) died.

Both seem to do fine, except that the 60GB seems to want to sleep when 
I don't want it to.  I'll eventually try the "swap the drives and 
cases" routine to see whether that makes a difference, but haven't had 
time.

If you have an ATA based Mac with room for a second drive, you might 
also try putting the drive in the Mac and see what happens.

If it mounts, you can off-load the data and then reformat, etc... put 
back in Firewire case and see what happens from there.

Hope these ideas get you some where.
