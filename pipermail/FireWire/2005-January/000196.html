I have attached a summary of the testing I did with the firewire/usb 
enclosure and SATA drive.
This is the results of several discussions yesterday on this list.
By the way these are all Maxtor drives.

>
> Basic problem
> The enclosure (Addonics Model AE5SACSUF) with the Addonics SATA to IDE 
> adapter works fine on USB2 but does not work properly on the Firewire 
> port.
>
> Results of various configurations.  Mac OS 10.3.7 (this is important) 
> G4 1GHZ machine
>
> Testing WITHOUT SATA to IDE adapter.  (tested 3 different sizes 80g, 
> 120G and 250G) Oxford 911 bridge chipset confirmed
>
> Version of Bridge	IDE Disk Size		USB Results		Firewire Results
> 3.5				80G				works			works
> 3.5				120G			works			works
> 3.5				250G			works			works
> 		
> 3.8				80G				works			works
> 3.8				120G			works			works
> 3.8				250G			works			works
>
> 4.0				80G				works			works
> 4.0				120G			works			works
> 4.0				250G			works			works
>
> Testing WITH IDE to SATA adapter.  (tested 200G drive SATA drive) 
> Oxford 911 bridge chipset confirmed
>
> Version of Bridge	IDE Disk Size		USB Results		Firewire Results
> 3.5				200G			works			fails
> 3.8				200G			works			fails
> 4.0				200G			works			fails
>
> Based on these tests, I would conclude that the adapter board from IDE 
> to SATA is failing.
> The drives all work fine under USB2.
> I have two of these little boards (both Addonics), they both fail.
>
> I wish I had a smaller SATA drive to test but I doubt any drive will 
> work with the SATA to IDE adapter board.
>
> Does anyone know of another adapter board that I can try?
>
