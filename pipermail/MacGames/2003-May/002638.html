well, if you manually delete something, of course it then goes into 
the trash where it stays until you manually empty it.  But if a file 
overwrites another file, or a save is saved on top of a previous 
save, then you're SOL.  Hosed.  Up the creek.

Hence my practise of making backups (in a separate location) of the save files.

So AS FAR AS I KNOW there's nothing you can do.
