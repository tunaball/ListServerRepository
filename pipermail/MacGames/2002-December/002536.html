I personally like Logitech's mice the best. Their optical two-button 
mice feels very nice and simple, and the tracking is really smooth 
(especially on their dual-optical mice). However, I guess the Logitech 
driver software isn't quite as powerful as the Kensington or Microsoft 
mice drivers, but it's good enough for most normal purposes.
