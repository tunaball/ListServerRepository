Your experience doesn't match mine. I found that on both my G4 notebook and
G5 dualie desktop that Tiger's Finder is faster and less likely to hang
(beachball of death) than Panther's. Tiger also boots faster than Panther
did.

However, lots of people initially reported similar stories to yours. People
who chose the upgrade option tended to have the most problems while those
who chose the archive and install tended to have the least number of
problems. There are also lots of third party updates/upgrades you'll need to
look into. You'll find a very informative discussion here:

<http://discussions.apple.com/thread.jspa?messageID=607363>
