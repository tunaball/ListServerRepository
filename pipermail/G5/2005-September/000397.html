I say yes. Why? Because if anything goes wrong you get on site  
repairs. The service person comes to your place and fixes it on the  
spot. I had to have a motherboard replaced on my dual 2 G5 last year.  
Without Applecare, I would have had to take it to a repair place and  
wait over a week for them to repair it. As it was, with Applecare, a  
service technician came to my office and replaced the motherboard the  
next day.
