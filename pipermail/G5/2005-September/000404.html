On thing that few people know, if you live in Florida, you can buy  
Apple Care, but it's no good.  Extended Warrantee's are no longer  
valid since some companies defaulted after Hurricane Andrew back in  
1992 and the Florida Legislature passed a law voiding these extended  
warrantees.  So, if you live in Florida, as I do, Apple Care is of no  
use whatsoever.
