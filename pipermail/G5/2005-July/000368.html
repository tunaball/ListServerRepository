Check your boot rom version in your system profile before you decide to  
do the update (in the "about this mac..." window).  Chances are good  
you have a newer rom version than that update.

When your machine is turned off, unplug the power cord for 20 seconds  
or so to reset the power management module.  I've done this in the past  
and it corrected similar symptoms.

If the problem persists- just reinstall 10.4.x, that may correct the  
problem as well.  Real hardware problems usually result in kernel  
panics, you seem to have a software glitch.
