You can use your old drive if you put it in an external enclosure.  
Typically these are firewire connected to the MacPro. Honestly, disk  
prices have dropped so dramatically that you would do better to  
purchase a SATA hard drive and install it in a spare bay in your  
computer. I have found (the hard way) that disk drive life is  
relatively short, so I plan on replacing my drives on a 4-5 year cycle  
maximum. It's better than losing your valuable photo collection.
