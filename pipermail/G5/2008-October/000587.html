Since it's happening to two different mice, I doubt it's a mouse  
issue.  I'm thinking, maybe the drivers.  On a long shot, shut the  
computer down, leave it off for about a minute, then restart.  If  
you're lucky, it's a driver issue and by doing this, it wipes  
everything from ram, whereas only rebooting, there is still a  
possibility that something is corrupt, a piece of it could still  
remain in ram.
