If you have external speakers connected no sound will go through your
internal (at least it doesn't on mine) speaker. My externals are also
connected to the analog mini-jack. You might want to check and make sure
they are connected properly. Once in a while our B&W G3 will loose startup
sound and the fix is resetting the pram.
