FM transmitters work well for some and not so well for others. It  
depends on your vehicle and where you drive. For me none worked well.  
Cassette adapters are okay but I wore three out - then wore out my  
car stereo. (I put about 36K a year on my car.) The best solution  
(and costliest) is buying a deck made for the iPod which plugs right  
into the socket at the bottom. Next to that, look at a deck that has  
an auxiliary input. That is the solution I settled on and I'm  
extremely happy.
