I've got a dual 2Ghz as well, I've had it for over 18 months now, and 
it replaced a G4/450 AGP.  Other than the fact that I suspect the 
bearings on the fan on the Video card (at least I think it has a fan 
on it) are going, I've been quite happy with the system.  I'm also 
sitting at 1.5GB, and I've replaced the 160GB drive with a 300GB 
drive and a 250GB internally.

My next upgrade will probably be 2GB more RAM, as at 1.5GB, the 
system is always starved for RAM, though part of that is how I use 
the system.  I'm expecting the system to speed up quite a bit with 
3.5GB RAM, as most of the slowness I see is related to swapping.
