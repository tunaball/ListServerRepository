Go to your local computer store and buy a tool to lift off the individual
keys.  Mine is a flat piece with 2 "U" shaped wires attached to the bottom.
You hook the wires around the key you want to remove and gently pull the key
up and off.  Clean the area under the key and replace it.

I have had the same problem with my bluetooth keyboard.
