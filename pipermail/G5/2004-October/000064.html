I have the Apple BT Mouse. It's been running for months on the first 
set of batteries. Response isn't as good as a wired mouse. Not having 
extra buttons or a scroll wheel is a pain, but I had to have Apple(!). 
The one thing I notice most is the lag that happens if you don't use it 
for a while (energy saving 'feature' no doubt). I wouldn't buy another 
one, but won't be selling it on either.
