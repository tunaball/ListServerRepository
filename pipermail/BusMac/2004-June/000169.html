We are a small environmental consulting company on the U.S. Territory 
of Guam (7 hours west of HI by plane).  We're an all Mac office with 
Airport Extreme and ethernet to tie in an HP Laser Printer and a 
Brother MFC all-in-on (which, by the way, works wonderful on our Mac 
network).  Still using my Cube alongside the brand new DP G5 on my desk 
because VPC doesn't run yet on G5s and I need it for QB Pro.  As soon 
as VPC can migrate over to the G5, I'm planning on venturing the Cube 
into OS X Server territory for a more central file management system 
....

Did not like the QB Mac latest version.  Two of my major gripes were 
that it doesn't do payroll, and you can not make

active projects list.  Not nice.
