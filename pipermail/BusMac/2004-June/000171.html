Hi Peter:

We have the 8820DN (D for double-sided and N for Network) 
<http://solutions.brother.com/mfc8420us/index.html>

It's setup with its own e-mail address (you can e-mail to the machine 
from anywhere in the world and it will print), scan to e-mail, and fax 
from any Mac on the network.

I remember that the initial setup was challenging until I figured out 
how to access the machine via web browser and modify settings that way. 
  They may have a newer model out already, but when it came to replacing 
our $7,000 Xerox copier this $900(?) machine has performed as well 
without all the paper jams..  (We need to take our 11x17s to the copy 
center, though ...)

Paul
