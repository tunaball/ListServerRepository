I'm a consultant who is working at a number of different projects. Because
the nature of my work changes from time to time, depending on the project, I
really like the idea that I have access to others who have done a wide
variety of things with their Macs.

I'm currently using a 1999 iMac, but after maxing out the Ram and installing
a new hard drive and OS X 10.3.4, it is plenty fast enough for all of the
work we do (there is no heavy-duty graphics processing). I use Safari,
Entourage, MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, FileMaker, and a bunch of utilities.
If not for the help of people from this list and others, I would probably
have had to buy a cheap PC.

I'm very pleased that I can continue to use this iMac, and although I don't
contribute often, I appreciate this list. I'm sure that the lack of
contributions is cyclical - after all, where else could we go for this type
of info?
