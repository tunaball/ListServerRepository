Opening a browser window to a mapped address in Mapquest is fairly 
trivial, that part I can do. The problem is that scraping the info off 
of free Mapquest pages is a dubious business practice at best, let 
alone unreliable... Mapquest business services are "starting at 
$4,400", although I'm waiting for some actual pricing.

I had hoped there was something else people were using. I'm very 
surprised at the lack of mapping software available Mac-side and that 
no one has stepped in.
