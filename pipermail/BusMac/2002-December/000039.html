Geoff Wallace said:

> On reboot OS 9 locks up with
>extension conflicts etc, sometimes will restart with extensions off,
>sometimes even has errors with extensions off.


What makes you think that you are experiencing "extension conflicts" if 
the machine still has problems even with the extensions turned off?

Sounds to me as if you need to update the hard disk drivers using Drive 
Setup.

If that doesn't work, I would suspect a hardware problem, such as a bad 
third-party PCI card, out of spec third-party RAM, or a USB peripheral 
problem.

I also would take a hammer to the CD-ROM disk for any "old versions" of 
Norton that you have.  Running outdated copies of Norton Utilities on a 
new computer is just asking for trouble.
