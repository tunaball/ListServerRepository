We are Realtors, Sugar Land, Texas, and we use Now Contact and Now Up to
Date. Great software, easy to use and very flexible. We decided NOT to buy
Real Estate software for several reasons (having our operational modes
dictated by some propeller heads version of marketing and/or transaction
management) and opted for an excellent, multi-user PIM and calendaring
system.
