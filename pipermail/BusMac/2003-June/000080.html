Your 8500 
http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=112360
has a DB-15 video connector which can be used with just about any VGA 
monitor through the use of an inexpensive Mac to VGA adapter.   Here is 
one for only $10:

HW1030AD	Mac to VGA Monitor Adapter, With Switches	$9.99
http://www.pidrus.com/misc.html
picture
http://www.pidrus.com/images_prod/hw1030ad.jpg

Your Mac came with 2MB of video RAM.  If you want 24-bit color at 
1024X768, you can add another 2MB of VRAM to achieve this, otherwise you 
will have 16-bit color.
