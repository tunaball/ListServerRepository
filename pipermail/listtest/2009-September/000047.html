I don't think there's many people here, but I'm curious about thoughts  
about new lists.

Some of my ideas are:

MacTouch - A place to discuss tablet computing on the Mac including  
the rumored tablet Mac.
iPhone - A place to discuss the iPhone, iPod touch, etc.
iPhoneGames - A place to discuss gaming on the iPhone, iPod touch, etc.
iPhoneApps- A place to discuss 3rd party aplications on the iPhone,  
iPod touch.
MacTV - A place to discuss Apple TV and Apple products in the context  
of home television


I also am planning on repurposing some of the existing lists to revive  
them a little.

Digicam - A place to discuss digital cameras, digital photo software,  
and their use for Macintosh Users. (removing the references to video  
and adding the idea of Mac software)
iTunes -  A forum to discuss the issues that relate to using, and  
troubleshooting Apple's iTunes and MP3 on the Apple devices in  
general. Including discussion of iPods and the iPod portion of iPhone  
and touch.
MacGPS - To include discussion of GPS on iPhone.

Thoughts?
