>chips, power supply switches, and stuff like that. Fortunately I'll 
>have help with the driver software on peecees and Macs but I'll have

For hardware and device questions, as well as Widnows driver 
questions, the USB Implimenters Forum <http://developers.usb.org> has 
a developer message board.

For Mac driver questions, Apple has a USB developer mailing list. You 
can find that and lots of other Apple developer lists at 
<http://lists.apple.com>.

This is a list for user level questions, not developers.
