Cube 450 standard hardware, OS10.2.3, 15" LCD
Numbers are inclusive, in watts

Power Supply ONLY:            5
Off; power supply idling: 11-12
Bootup:                                  42-65
Disk R/W                                 69-73
Disk spinning:
            LCD bright:                 52-55
            LCD dim:                     37-41
            LCD off:                      32-37
Disk idle:
LCD bright:                            55-60

Sleep:                                     10

These numbers are really phenomenal, a tribute to Apple's engineering. At 10
watts during sleep there is no reason to shutoff and restart even if idle
for days.
