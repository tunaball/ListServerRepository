The card that'll just drop in and work is the Apple OEM version of the 
Geforce 3. It was sold as an option on the G4 towers. They occasionally 
come up for sale at OWC or Macs4All for around $250. Be careful with 
the GF3 cards on eBay, you need the Apple OEM version if you want to 
use the bracket in the cube to mount it.

good luck.

-Hal
