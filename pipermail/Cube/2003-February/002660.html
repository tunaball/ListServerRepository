This is my report on 10.2.4 and the coma problem.

Prior to my 800mhz upgrade I had no cube sleep or coma problems. I used OS X
up to 10.2.2

After I upgraded to the 800Mhz board I immediately experienced the
coma(lights and fan start up but no wake at all).
With 10.2.3 and even yesterday with 10.2.4 same deal - coma.


                                 10.2.2             10.2.3           10.2.4
 w. 450Mhz              OK                   ?                    ?
 w. 800 Mhz            Coma             Coma            No Coma *


*Out of desperation (and jealousy!), I did a total reformat and partition of
my Seagate 80mb drive and started from scratch.
To my surprise - I now have no coma problems (OS 10.2.4).
I am completely without explanation as to why?
