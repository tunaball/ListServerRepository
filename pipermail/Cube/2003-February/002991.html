Gene. I was able to get a Radeon 7500 for $150 by buying a Radeon 9000, 
and trading it to a Radeon 7500 owner at Apples online discussion 
forums. The Radeon 7500 is most common in dual 800 towers. It was in 
the january 2002 quicksilvers.

If you don't know anyone personally, Mac forums are a good place to 
look, and most Radeon 7500 users are more than happy to swap for a more 
powerful card that has ADC on board.

Try to avoid paying an inflated price (anything over $150) just because 
you need it for a Cube...some people know we can only use a few cards 
to upgrade, and they take advantage of that fact.
