Unfortunately, iSync doesn't sync mail you store on your Mac. That was 
the first feedback I gave Apple. It'd be a great feature to have.

What you can do, is copy over the mailbox file, which is stored in 
/user/yourname/library/mail/mailboxes

You'll find files in there for each of your stored mailboxes. Once it's 
copied over, on the new mac you can import it. Just use the import 
mailbox command under the file menu.

Hope that helps.
