Just ran into this one recently myself - two machines - one with "On My 
Mac" and the other without - everything otherwise configured 
identically.

Just choose New Mailbox from the Mailbox menu (or ctrl-click a mailbox 
to get at it) and you'll see that there is always an On My Mac location 
available in the Location menu in the window that pops up.
