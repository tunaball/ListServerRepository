So it seems that with a 20in LCD and a PL 1GHz my
consumption may be as high as 73+20+10 = 103W.

73 highest W measured in this discussion thread
20 additional wattage to drive 20in LCD rather than
15in
10 for CPU consumption, 1Ghz MPC7455 Max = 22W, 500MHz
MPC7410 = 11.9W. Not sure about L3 cache power
consuption.


As mentioned, the cube specs rate its maximum
continuous power at 205W.  I should have some headroom
as long as I dont overload my USB and Firewire ports.
