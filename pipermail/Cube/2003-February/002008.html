Keep in mind that measurements from one Cube to another are not that 
meaningful. I had a 450mHz Cube that registered temps (stock) in the 
range of 32-44. When I got my 500mHz model (also stock) it registered 
tepms in the 16-24 range, but the case didn't feel any warmer to the 
the touch.

It is useful to track the temperature in a particular Cube as you make 
modes (fan, hard drive and video card) as it gives you something to 
measure against.

just my $0.02.
-Hal
