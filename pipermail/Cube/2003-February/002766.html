> But one step is missing. I don't understand how to boot my PC into DOS. Would
> you please explain how to do that part? I am running Windows 98 SE.

1. Take an empty floppy, insert it into your PC's floppy drive
2. Open a command shell, and type: "format a:/s" and press ENTER
3. Wait for the computer to finish formatting the floppy.
4. Restart. Do not remove the floppy from the drive; the computer should now
boot into DOS from the floppy.
