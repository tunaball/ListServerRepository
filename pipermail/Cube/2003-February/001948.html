> Chris,
> 
> Welcome to the party. Most of this information is available on the FAQ
> on either cubeowner.com or cube-zone.com.
> 
> The Cube takes standard PC100 ram, available at most online merchants.
> PC133 will also work, and is usually around the same price. You won't
> see any improvement in using the faster ram, as the bus speed on the
> Cube is set at 100mHz. Your Cube has 3 ram slots, each can hold 512MB,
> so the max is 1.5GB.
> 
> As far as hard drives go, you'll get recommendations for lots of
> drives. Seagate Barracuda drives (IV and V, I think),  and some others
> have fluid filled bearings, and are almost silent. I have a Western
> Digital Caviar with an 8MB cache that I really like. It's almost silent
> and really fast. You can add up to a 120GB drive. The drive controller
> in he Cub won't see space on a single drive beyond 137GB, so there's no
> point in getting a drive that's larger.
> 
> Hope that helped.
> 
> cheers,
> -Hal
