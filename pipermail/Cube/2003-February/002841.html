On torsdag, feb 20, 2003, at 11:46 Europe/Oslo, Simon Meyer wrote:

> I booted my cube this morning to discover everything displayed in 
> negative?
> It boots up with normal coloring, then goes to negative when I reach 
> the desktop. The monitors panel reports colors as being set to 256.
> Even when I change it back to millions there is no change. Rebooting 
> makes it default back to 256.
> Is there a preference I can delete to alleviate the problem or should 
> I be looking somewhere else?

Look in "Universal Access" in " System Preferences", sounds like you 
have activated "White on Black". There is a key-combination that will 
do this in the finder (Control-Option-Command-8) - chances are you have 
hit this combination without knowing...
Just hit the same combination (Control-Option-Command-8) - and 
everything is back to normal (hopefully!).
