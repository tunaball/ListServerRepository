Your backlight lamp has gone bad. Actually, there should be two of 
them, and it is unlikely that both of them went bad, it is probably the 
inverter that provides the power for them. The replacement lamps are in 
$100 range, and you can get them from www.jkllamps.com, i think 
Digi-Key is their distributor. But first open up that display to see if 
they get any power. If not, try to fix that. As I said, I doubt it is 
the lamps, ules there is only one inside.
