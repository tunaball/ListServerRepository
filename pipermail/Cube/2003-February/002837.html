You need to start here: http://www.linuxprinting.org/maxosx/. You have  
too choices: gimp-print and Samsungs GDI driver. I'd start with  
Samsung's driver as it explicitly supports your printer. If that  
doesn't work give gimp-print a try.

NOTE: It is very important that your printer is hooked up and turned on  
during the install.

Also the gimp-print folks are looking for feedback from users of  
parallel to USB convertors. See  
https://sourceforge.net/tracker/ 
?func=detail&atid=351537&aid=644565&group_id=1537 for the feedback they  
have so far.
