Well the iDVD 3 patch has taken care of running iDVD 3 without a 
SuperDrive. I've burned a Video CD but I don't have any DRD-R/RW media 
yet to burn DVDs yet. My point is there is no barrier between anyone 
and a DVD burner or Combo drive "inside" their Cube. 12 Screws and the 
aforementioned cables. That's it. End of story.

FYI: I found a Samsung 16x DVD read 48x24x48x CD-RW Combo drive for $70 
after $30 rebate in another CenDyne Box at Fry's last week that the 
Cube and Toast recognizes as well. When I want to rip MP3's or burn 
CD-Rs FAST, I just swap the drives out in 15 seconds and I'm up and 
running with that superfast CD=RW that can also read DVDs real fast. 
$240 for both drives.
