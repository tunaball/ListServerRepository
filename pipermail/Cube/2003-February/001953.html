On Saturday, February 1, 2003, at 05:59 PM, Kenneth Strain wrote:

> I have a 120 Gb Western Digital that I want to put in my Cube.  Do I 
> need to
> install a fan?

Most people will tell you that you don't need a fan and indeed the 
drive is running fine for them. However I had a different experience. I 
upgraded my stock hard drive with a 80GB SE Western Digital and it was 
running fine for a week and then it started doing a lot of calibrating 
and eventually some of my data got corrupted. When I took it out it was 
really warm so I decided it might not be a bad idea to put a fan in 
after all. Since I put the fan in (months ago) it is running 
flawlessly. Just wanted to share my experience in case you run into the 
similar problem.
