If you had a 7500 you would know it because it installs with either a 
radical mounting fence modification (because the video ports are 
further apart) or without the fence (which lets more air in to keep it 
cooler). The 7500 is also TwinView (extended desktop) which neither of 
the others are. System Profiler reports the 7500 as "Card Type 
ATY,DDParent, Card Name ATY,BlueStoneParent, Card Model ATY,RV200" etc. 
32 MB.
