Actually, Apple listed the only displays where this was supported:

  - Apple Cinema Display (ADC)
  - Apple Studio Display 17 (ADC)
  - Apple Studio Display LCD (ADC)

("For this reason, you should only connect the speakers to one of the 
USB ports on the Power Mac G4 Cube computer, or monitor listed above. 
You should not connect them to the USB ports of any Apple keyboard, 
other Apple computer, or third-party USB device.")

My bet would be that they didn't exactly forget about it, they simply 
decided that they weren't going to engineer the extra power into new 
products to support an end-of-lifed product.
