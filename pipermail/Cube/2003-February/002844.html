How do you have things set in the "CDs & DVDs" preference pane?
This sounds like what might happen if you have "When you insert a music
CD" set to "ignore".

You may need to trash the plist files.
I think they are ~/Library/Preferences/com.apple.digihub.plist and
~/Library/Preferences/com.apple.systempreferences.plist (where '~' is your
home directory)
