Received my IDE extension cables today from Cables & Connectors. Took 
another few minutes to heat a knife, slice the unnotched side of one of 
the male connectors to access and easily break pin #20 off with a pair 
of needle nose pliers. Then simply connected the two 6" extensions 
together, pluged the modified male connector into the Cube's female end 
and the unmodified female into the DVR-105.

Booted up and the Apple System Profiler says:

"CD-RW/DVD-R
Disc Burning Fully Supported.
Product Identification DVD-RW DVR-105
Vendor Identification PIONEER".

Eject key/F12 works perfectly to both open and close the drawer. I'm a 
happy camper. This mod is way easier than the tutorial would have you 
believe thanks to RichC's pioneering spirit
