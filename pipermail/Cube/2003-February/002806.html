Just to point out to everyone running Mail.app (and I think Entourage 
does this as well).  By selecting the portion of the original message 
before hitting reply Mail will trim your response to contain only the 
selected text.  I don't remember if it's the default setting, but it's 
controllable under the Composing frame of the Preferences

Quicker and easier than cleaning stuff up after the fact by a huge 
margin.
