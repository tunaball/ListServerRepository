You can try to hunt down a gamma correction program that would allow 
you to brighten the output of your display. You can't get too 
aggressive or you will loose your contrast but at least for OS 9 there 
were several Gamma control panels floating around out there. As far as 
OS X is concerned I have no idea as I haven't seen anything along those 
lines. You might be able to crank up the brightness via an adjustment 
screw inside the monitor's case but unless you know how to discharge a 
CRT I would leave it to an Apple service provider. There is the 
potential for some serious voltage to be hanging around inside that CRT 
that could send you across the room!
