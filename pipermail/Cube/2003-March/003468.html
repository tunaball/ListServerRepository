Just for interest you need not buy an entire FW HD enclosure.

Have a look at the DriveDock by http://www.wiebetech.com/ . Not necessarily
the cheaper doo-dah but a useful tool for teching with. Mounted on a clear
perspex plate it would look quite good by a Cube, although the blue alloy
isn't by cup of Earl Grey. Supports drives with capacities to 250GB (ATA-6).

Fwdepot also do some natty FW bridges that you can use to convert an old
external SCSI HD from 65 bucks.

As an aside, I know that we are all great fans of the super silent Barracuda
IVs, but that humming vibration Cube I had problems with was caused by a IBM
Deskstar. I have two GXP 60 Deskstar, a 40 and a 60, bought a year or so ago

boy, are they whiney and cause vibrations. I noticed this even in a tower.

little bleeder for that hint ]  but it made no different.
