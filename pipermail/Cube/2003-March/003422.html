to view your log files, which i normally would use terminal for, 
there's a gui log viewer util for os x.  it's called log master.  
search for it on version tracker.

this /may/ let you view your /var/log/system.log file - it will let you 
view it all you like - the /may/ part is that the system.log file may 
contain nothing of value in helping you to solve your problem.
