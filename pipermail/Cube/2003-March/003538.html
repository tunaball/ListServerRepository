You will get lots of recommendations on this. For my cents, I'd 
recommend the 80 GB Seagate Barracuda IV. It's cheap, fast, extremely 
quiet and I've used it for months without problem in a Cube with a hot 
GeForce3 video card and an 800 MHz then a 1.2 GHz G4 CPU upgrade from 
PowerLogix.

You can get much larger ones for much more money but unless you do 
video or images professionally or have a huge MP3 collection, 80 GB 
should prove to be more than enough. I barely use over 30 per cent of 
mine.

I got another to serve as a backup drive for my PowerBook recently for 
only $98 from newegg.com thanks to a note from a list member, Jesse.
