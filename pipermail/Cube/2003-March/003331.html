I've opened my keyboard, cleaned it, reassembled it, and now replaced it.

It's a very, very hard job with many small screws (~31), tiny rubber
cushions from under the keys, and LEDs (capslock, numlock, etc).

Unless you are really, really set on doing this -I wouldn't recommend it.
