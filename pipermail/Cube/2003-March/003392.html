Well, I think that this is part of your problem. The Radeon without the 
fan is not designed for use in the Cube. They get too hot, and with 
very little airflow in that space, there is no place for the heat to 
go. That alone can cause your Cube to overheat.

My guess is that your Radeon is overheating, causing other problems as 
well. Probably the combination of the additional heat from the PL 
upgrade and the already-hot Radeon pushed your Cube over the edge. Have 
you tried replacing the Radeon with the original Rage128 card to see if 
it is stable?

Just my $0.02.
