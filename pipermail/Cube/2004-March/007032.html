Just wanted to let you know that PowerMax has Cube systems for sale, 
and I just had a great buying experience with them... I decided, after 
my iBook ate it's OS for the second time and wouldn't boot [Had to 
erase the HD and reinstall all software both times ;^/], that I needed 
some backup for travel... So, I bought a G4 400 TiBook from PowerMax, 
and it arrived two days ago... PowerMax was great to deal with, and 
kept me informed vis-a-vis order status and shipping every step of the 
way [Thanks, Gary!]... The PowerBook arrived in a fabulously protective 
shipping container exactly when they said it would... I was concerned 
about buying a "used" computer - especially such an old version of the 
TiBook - but it looks like it's hardly been used at ALL!... Their 
prices are a bit higher than some other places I've seen on comparable 
items, but they have an unambiguous return policy and a nice 
warranty... As I said: Customer service rocks!...

I've put the PB through my own personal set of hard-use tests, and 
installed my entire software set on it... It's fantastic!...

If you want a used Cube, I would recommend highly that you check out 
PowerMax...
