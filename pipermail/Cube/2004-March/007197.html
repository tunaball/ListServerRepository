> The range values are actually meaningless because the sensor tolerance
> is very high, meaning that the values you get often do not match the
> real temperature at all. This is useful as a relative measure, so it
> makes sense to look at the temperature reading without GF3, and then do
> the same with GF3 card. That way you will see the relative change in
> temperature between two setups.
