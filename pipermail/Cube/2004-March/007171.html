start typing names and email addresses :)  just kidding.

open yer address book, select the entries that you would like to send 
your email to.  now, this is the really cool part.  once they are 
selected, go to the file menu for the address book application, and 
click New Group from Selection.  Now name the group.  it will be on the 
left-most pane of the address book.  Now click Send Updates from the 
file menu.  the group should appear in the top of the dialog box.  
check it.  enter a subject, then enter your new contact information.  
click send.  move on. :)
