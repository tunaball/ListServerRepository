Thanks, I'll check out the CD.

In the meantime, I have been experimenting with ejecting the 
"unrecognized" CD.

Restart, holding down the mouse, didn't work.

Then I played with Open Firmware:

This worked:

Restart, and AS SOON AS THE SCREEN BLANKS press CMD-OPT-O-F and hold.   
  The CD ejected.  Release the keys, and a normal restart ensues.

(Following the Apple Open Firmware instruction and pressing the 
CMD-OPT-O-F keys immediately after the start-up Boing as recommended, 
did not eject the CD - presumably the CD Eject sequence is engaged 
before the USB start-up.)
