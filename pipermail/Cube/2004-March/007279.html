with the AirPort signal.

9.1 to 10.3.2) and in my investigations I have found that some people 
were experiencing interference from cordless handsets.

The iMac is allways at four bars.

another poster when/if the problem occurs again. Luckily it has been 
good for weeks now so I'm crossing my fingers and pretending it never
