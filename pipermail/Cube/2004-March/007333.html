Another data point on this issue:

I've been using Macaroni on three computers for a couple of years now 
with no problems -- with Safari or anything else.  Certainly no Kernel 
Panics -- except of course a spate of them on my Cube, which after much 
gnashing of teeth, turned out to be the result of a RAM chip gone bad.

As far as I understand, it just runs the maintenance scripts that the 
OS would normally run in the middle of the night (but when many people 
have their computers turned off).

Of course, YMMV.........
