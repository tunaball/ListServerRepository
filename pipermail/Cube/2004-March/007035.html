I will refer to the computer you are connecting to as the "server" and 
the one you are connecting from as the "client".

On your "client" computer, go to the Finder and click on "Go" in the 
menubar, and select "Connect to server". If you click "Browse" you 
should be able to see all computers that have sharing active on your 
LAN. You can log into the "server" from there and get at the 
filesystem.

Hope that helps.
