I have a stock Cube in terms of the Video Card.  I've upgraded the RAM 
to 1.5 GIG, and the HD to a 60 GIG.  I'm running Panther on it.  It 
does very, very well (450 G4 CPU).  The only time I notice a problem 
with the Video is if I attempt a newer game that demands a lot of the 
video card.

If you are not into games etc, go for it.  For the most part you won't 
really see any difference.  Having said that, I've a ATI Radion 32 MB 
Video Card on it's way (eBay).  This card will add the extra 16 MEG of 
VRam & invoke the Quartz Extreme.  I'll let you know what happens.
