Well you will never have a memory alert, because it will create the 
virtual memory swap files until it chokes. I have a Lombard with 384 
MB, and yes, everything works just fine, until you realize that 1.5 GB 
on your had drive are occupied by virtual memory swap files, and that 
place is never freed unless you restart. Having a 8GB system partition 
doesn't help.
