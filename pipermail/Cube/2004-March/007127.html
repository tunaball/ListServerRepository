Gladys,

Well, you could boot the Cube in target disk mode, then boot the Pismo 
from the Cube's hard drive.  Install OSXvnc on the Cube's hard drive, 
and set it to auto-start on bootup.  Reboot the Cube and you should be 
able to connect to it with a VNC client; I recommend Chicken of the 
VNC.

Resources:
	http://sourceforge.net/projects/osxvnc/
	http://sourceforge.net/projects/cotvnc/
Binaries for both are available at the sites listed.
