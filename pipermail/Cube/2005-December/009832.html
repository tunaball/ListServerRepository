Geordie, 
 
I suppose you're referring to the oroginal HK Soundsticks (USB based).
If you lose sound again, try both: 
1. Changing the USB port(s) on your Cube or Apple LCD Display 
2. Open Audio MIDI Setup located in Applications>Utilities and change
the Default Output and System Output sources on the fly. See if sound
comes back. 
 
Yeah I know, Cubes are a little bit flaky concerning audio. 
 
Good luck,
