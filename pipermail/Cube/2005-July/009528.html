My office uses a Netgear wireless router and it is compatible with  
the Apple airport card in my Pismo.

I have used an Orinoco card and a Belkin card in my Wallstreet  
laptops, but I do not recommend trying to use a non-apple airport  
card in the Pismo if you can get hold of an original Apple AirPort  
802.11b card. They are available for around $70.00US through eBay if  
you have the patience to use the auction process.
