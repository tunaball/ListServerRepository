On Tuesday, July 8, 2003, at 11:57  AM, Richard Clark wrote:
>> Is there a Jag startup CD?

The Jaguar CD 1 is a startup disc. Your HD should be set to Master 
while the CD/DVD is Slave. How to set it to master is clearly printed 
on the top of the HD. You should have no problem installing Jaguar on 
the new drive from this CD. I really don't understand why you are 
having problems. I'm on my third HD inside my Cube and have never had a 
problem migrating one to another. Don't forget to apply the 10.2.6 
COMBO updater to your new installation of Jaguar right away among many 
other updates like QT 6.3 etc.
