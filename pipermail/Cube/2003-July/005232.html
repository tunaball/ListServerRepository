I have problems with mine and those disks that have really ornate 
labels. Seems the pads on the sides of the opening won't slide the disk 
out well if it is too thick. Also older disks seem to have problems. I 
think they may not be as true as the newer ones. Those I have trouble 
with poke a little bit of the edge of the CD out then get sucked back 
in. I have a pair of large plastic tweezers that work great in removing 
them.

If it is just spinning and not even making an effort to eject, could be 
the drive is going bad.
