Sometimes the disk just fouls on the lip of the opening.  Try to eject using 
the restart-while-holding-down-the-mouse trick, and then use a fingernail to 
help guide the stuck CD as it tries to eject until you can grip it with your 
fingers and pull it the rest of the way out.
