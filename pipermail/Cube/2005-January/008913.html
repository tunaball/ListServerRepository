I think the question of automatic synch-ing deleting your iPod tunes can be 
sorted in iTunes. When you connect your iPod, the iPod icon appears in the 
bottom right of the iTunes window, alongside the equalizer and visualizer 
icons. click on it and you get a window where you can un-tick the automatic 
update box. Then it doesn't do it automatically.

Well it worked for me...
