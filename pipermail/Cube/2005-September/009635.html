It's a standardized form factor across all IDE drives since the  
mid-90s (maybe even earlier).  The boxes are all the same exterior  
dimensions and the screw mount positions are identical as well.  I've  
pulled out drives from Cubes and put them into enclosures and pulled  
out drives from PCs and put them in Cubes. If the OWC enclosure is  
for a 3.5" drive, it'll work...
