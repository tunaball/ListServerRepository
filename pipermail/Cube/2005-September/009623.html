It is....umm... a DSL "thingie" :)))))
It's quite simple actually, modem comes from "MODulator DEModulator".  
Modems were used to modulate the digital signal into analog one to be  
transferred over the traditional analog phone lines, and to  
demodulate the incoming signal. Since DSL line is a digital one, no  
modulation or demodulation is taking place. However, since the device  
appears to perform the similar function as the traditional modem did,  
people still tend to call it a "modem".
