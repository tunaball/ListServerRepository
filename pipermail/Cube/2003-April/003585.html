Possibly.

What you need to check is if the G4 350 tower has PCI video or AGP. If it 
has PCI video (what we called a Yikes G4) then you cannot put your cube CPU 
in it.

If the video is AGP (A Sawtooth G4) then yes, you can put you cube CPU in 
it.
