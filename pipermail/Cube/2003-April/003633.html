Hi Hal!

I just bought the new HP PSC 1210 all-in-one and am very pleased with 
it. Software installation worked without a problem and sharing to other 
Macs in the home network went just as smooth. TWAIN drivers get 
installed, so Photoshop and Word can capture without problem, but the 
native Image Capture application did not find the scanner - I can live 
with that. Print quality is totally acceptable. The physical footprint 
is very small, so you can hide it in a corner (it will never match the 
stunning looks of the Cube). Purchase price was ridiculously low, and I 
am well aware that the ink cartridges and print heads will cost me an 
arm and a leg - but for home use, printing 2 or 5 pages per week, it is 
absolutely OK and if my print volume picks up, I've got my eye on the 
Minolta magicolor 2350...

Cheers,
Christian
