It doesn't read like your HD is about to become toast but you should 
have a good backup anyway.

I recommend an 80 GB Seagate Barracuda 4 (about $90+) in an OWC Elite 
Pro FireWire housing (about $100+). Install Mac OS to make it bootable 
and do backups to it frequently. I use the inexpensive and easy to use 
FWB Backup ToolKit.

If your internal HD ever goes out, you can switch the external HD in 
with no problem. It's considered a user doable operation and Apple even 
has a QT video on it. Look for the link on www.cubeowner.com.
