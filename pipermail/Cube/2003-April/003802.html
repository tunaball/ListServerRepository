I'm where you are. I've spent days trying to figure this one out, many  
people with this problem have found it's localized to USB hubs/devices.  
I've still got panics with only my mouse and keyboard and haven't  
gotten around to trying 10.2.5 with even those removed. If you look at  
/Library/logs/panic.log you should find the panic.log as listed in this  
path. If you look at it you'll probably see something about iousb etc.  
which makes me think that there is indeed something about USB screwed  
up in 10.2.5 because all my stuff worked fine up thru' 10.2.4 and  
started panicing witht the developer seed of 10.2.5. Search the Apple  
Knowledgebase for panic report and you'll find several docs about this.
