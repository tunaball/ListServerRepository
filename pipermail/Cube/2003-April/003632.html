Hal,

My wife and I have been happily using the HP OfficeJet d145 for about four
months now. I got the JetDirect box that plugs in to the d145's USB port and
stuck it on our home network. After installing the HP software for this line on
all systems, my Cube, TiBook/500, wife's generic PC and IBM ThinkPad all print
perfectly to the d145.

I haven't tried scanning with it yet, but we've made photocopies and
sent/received faxes manually. I'm very pleased with the performance so far.

The d145 is an inkjet, but it's fairly quick. I wouldn't want to print out a
100+ page document on it, but for a single to low 20-page docs, it works great.
It's a color printer, and does a servicable job of prints up to 8x10 (provided
your photo's resolution is high enough that it doesn't pixelate). The quality
isn't as good as HP's other inkjets that are tooled for printing photos, but
it's good enough for most stuff.
