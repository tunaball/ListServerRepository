For most of us in the real world, we are not looking for the ultimate 
hi-fi experience. You wouldn't start off with a cube/usb/mini-jack set

quality at an affordable price with aesthetics that won't take over 
your living space. The soundsticks offer just that. If your set up does 
what you want it to, then that's fine, but don't try and kid us that it 
is any way the last word because if you know as much as you claim to 
know, then you know it isn't.
