Brian,

Welcome to the cube clan :)

CPU upgrades are available from...

PowerLogix < http://www.powerlogix.com/ >
Sonnet < http://www.sonnettech.com/ >
GigaDesigns < http://www.gigadesigns.com/ >

All available upgrades should have a printed/movie manual, so you shouldn't have any installation doubts/problems.
< http://cubeowner.com/kbase/index.php > is the best way to start reading all about PowerMac G4 Cube upgrades.

As for upgrading the HD, there are also a lot of options for 7200rpm 3,5" HDDs. Mine's an 80Gb Seagate Barracuda IV and I just can't hear it spinning... honestly, it is quiet, fast, and temperatures have not increased since. I use 'ThermoInDock' app to monitorize it < http://www.versiontracker.com/dyn/moreinfo/macosx/9999 > and the highest pitch I've seen was 40°c.

Good luck,
