Another mouse alternative is the one I use, a Wacom Intuos2 graphics
tablet. The mouse is two button with a clickable scroll wheel and the
mouse itself is wireless. When I over do with the mouse and start to get
uncomfortable I switch to the pen for a day or two and the problems go
away. I find the pen takes a few minutes to get really used to, 5-10
minutes each time, but then it's quite comfortable to use.
