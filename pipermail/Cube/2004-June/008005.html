I have a Logitech MX700, Logitech Cordless Mouseman Optical and a USB 
Kensington Optical Elite.  All work just fine.  The only mouse that I 
have had issues with is the MS Bluetooth mouse  as MS does not provide 
suitable drivers.  It's basic functions were fine but it could not be 
configured (and neither could the keyboard).

BTW Michael - A wireless mouse feels totally different from a wired 
mouse.  As silly as it sounds, all wired mice have a 'drag' feeling 
when moving them around.  My opinion only of course.  YMMV.
