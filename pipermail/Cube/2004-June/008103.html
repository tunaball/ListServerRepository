Hello cubists,

This is not a success story, but rather a sad testify about my PL 1,35GHz upgrade adventure.

First things first... I've tried everything suggested by experimented mac users/friends/technicians and PowerLogix...

I've backed up my entire internal HD drive first, followed the manuals and VideoCD provided by PowerLogix, pressed CUDA button once, started up the Mac and both Mac OS 9 (firmware patch and system folder mods needed) and Mac OS X worked fine. Testing the hardware I faced the terrible issue that would lead me to corrupt my internal Seagate 80GB HD, my external FW 160GB HD and my internal iBook 20GB HD. YES, the FireWire was the culprit of all this ONLY when in Mac OS X. YES, all of this worked flawlessly when in Mac OS 9. YES, I've tested it with several independant FW disks and cables.

1. Had to run DiskWarrior on my iBook and my external 160GB HD in order to repair my data.
2. Had to format/repartition/reinstall Mac OS 9 and X in my internal 80 GB.
3. Fresh(es) OSs now. Still no difference. FW copy crashing in Mac OS X Finder, Spinning beach ball of death, etc...
4. Removed my internal Seagate 80GB and swapped it for a Western Digital 120GB. Reistalled. No go. FW not working.
5. Downclocked Seagate 80GB to ATA66. Reinstalled. No way Jose.
6. Remove firmware patches. Put back original CPU (stock 450MHz). No FireWire issues when in Mac OS 9 or X. Cube works like a genuine swiss quartz swatch now.
7. I'm done with upgrades for now... time to relax!

Cheers to all,
LM
