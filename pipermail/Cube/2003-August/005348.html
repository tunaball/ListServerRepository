I probably would have gotten the G3/900 rather than the G4/500 had it 
been available when I bought my upgrade. One thing to remember is the 
fact that the G3 only has a 512k cache, rather than the 1MB on the 
G3/500 stock or the G4/500. I'm sure that running at full processor 
speed help a lot, but more cache is better for some apps. VPC, in 
particular, runs better on CPUs with more cache.

Either way, IMHO, Powerlogix Pismo upgrades rock.

good luck!
