> Just HOW hard is it to upgrade my Cube from a 450 MHz to, say, an 
> 800mhz,
> with Sonnet's tools and supplies?
>

I recently upgraded with Sonnet's 800Mhz and the Cube Kit. The 
instructions and illustrations are very straightforward, and the Cube 
Kit includes the Torx drivers - they are adequate for the job, but if 
you have your own with conventional handles they will be easier to use. 
A large flat table, preferably without a carpet then you can do it 
without a wrist strap, the phone switched off, the children in bed and 
it is very straightforward -about an hour and half for the timid not 
having previously opened a Cube (me) and less than an hour the second 
time, or if cavalier!  I had previously upgraded processors on PCI 
boards, but nothing more.
