I recently purchased a second Cube (250$ canadian; I bragged about it 
in a previous e-mail).
Since I was not currently in need of a second Cube, I lent it to my 
sister.

My sister returned the Cube to me yesterday, and the case was full of 
scratches. I don't want to get
into the details right now, however, I don't have to tell you how I 
felt.

I decided to use some car turtle wax and see if I could buff out the 
scratches.  Sure enough it worked!!
The wax will not remove deep scratched, however, shallow scratches can 
be removed.  The turtle
wax does not damage the case in the least bit, in fact, it embellishes 
it greatly.  Be warned that you
have to put some work into it.  Simply apply turtle wax to a dry cloth 
and start polishing.  It takes time
and energy; the longer you work at it, the nicer the results.

Hope that others can benefit from this trick.

Phil
