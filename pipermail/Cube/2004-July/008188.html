> Reset the firmware this morning and the cube began cycling off/on this 
> afternoon. I also have ran onyX on the machine. John

Hello John - this is actually a well-documented problem, and there are 
more causes for it, but the most common one is the poser switch gasket. 
It is the ring-shaped thing that you can see around the top power 
switch when you pull out your core. There is a simple fix that solves 
this problem for many people - take a piece of paper and put it over 
the power switch (in a way that it ends up sandwiched between the power 
switch and the shell. The permanent solution is to get a new gasket.
This is described in detail in FAQ section of cubeowner.com I believe.
