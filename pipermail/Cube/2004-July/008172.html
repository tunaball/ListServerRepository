I've used both the Seagate (7200.7, I think Barracuda V) and WD SE (8MB 
cache). I prefer the Seagate for it's quiet operation. I don't notice 
much of a heat difference between the 2, and certainly neither one is 
hot enough to cause a problem in my Cube. I have a base fan installed 
and a 1.2gHz CPU.

Hope that helps.
