On 2005.10.07, at 06:37, Andrew in Ann Arbor wrote:

> Hello all
> A musician friend has a bunch of MP3s on his web site.
> If I had an FTP client I could download whole directories at once  
> instead of saving each song one at a time.
> Any recommendations for an FTP client for OS 10.3.9?

Well Finder would do! In Finder hit Command+K, enter the ftp site in  
following format
ftp://ftp.ftpsite.com
...and connect. The site will appear in the left sidebar. Select what  
you want and drag it where you want it.
I'm usually using Transmit though.
