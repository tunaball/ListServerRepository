On 4 Oct, 2005, at 23:51, Marty Levenson wrote:

I have a 450 with 1 Gb RAM and a 80 Gig Seagate Barracuda HD. Are there 
any issues with installing Tiger? I am currently running 10.2.8

You will need to have a DVD drive to install from the Tiger 
installation DVD. If you have a FireWire box with a DVD drive in it, 
you could use it as the startup drive and do the installation.

If not, you might be willing to spend the extra $29 or so to exchange 
your DVD for a set of CD installation discs from Apple and do it that 
way.
