Hi.

Depends, are you selling it as a collectable?...or as a used system?  I've 
seen cubes sold as collectables go for more then $400 on ebay.  By 
collectable I mean, unused/slightly used, in a sealed/unsealed original box, 
with all doc's, orginal keyboard, mouse...etc.  Don't ask me why someone 
would buy a $2000 system and never use it just to sell it at a loss a couple 
of years later (needless to say they are very rare :) ).  If you are 
intending to sell it as a used system you'll need to be more specific...how 
much ram does it have, what processor/speed...etc.
