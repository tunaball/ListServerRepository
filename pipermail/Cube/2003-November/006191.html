following:

> Does Panther's Fast User Switching 3D Rotating Cube feature works for
> everyone on this list? Just curious, because it certainly does not work
> on my G4 450 cube (with the stock ATI Rage 128 Pro 16MB VRAM on an AGP
> 2x bus) and a 17' LCD display.
> 
> (This a stock 450 cube with zero modifications except for a 1.25 GB
> memory upgrade.)


The Rage 128 Pro does not fully support Quartz Extreme, which is required
for the rotating cube effect. You need a 32MB graphics card or better to
support that effect. The fast switching feature itself still works, it just
won't be animated.
