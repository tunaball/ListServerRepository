I've just found this utility to Monitor the Temperature of your Mac: 
http://www.bresink.com/osx/TemperatureMonitor.html

It's pretty complete, and the author has a good explanation on his site 
of what you can expect from your system in terms of temperature 
measurements: in short, for the Cube, only if you have an original Cube 
with a PowerPC 7400 processor (some Cubes have a 7410) will you be able 
to read the temperature. And this reading is far from accurate :-/

The cheap indoor/outdoor digital thermometer, with the probe on top of 
the cube vent is a better solution!
