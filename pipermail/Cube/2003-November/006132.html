> I am thinking about buying a 17 inch or 19 inch LCD monitor. I  have a
> small desk and need to save space. Also, my vision requires at least 17
> inches.  My present  monitor was purchased with my Cube.
> I have two major considerations: price (of course) and reliability.
> I would appreciate suggestions. A refurbished monitor might be an
> option. I want to know about brands which work well for others. I can
> not afford $700 for the Apple monitor.

Bear in mind that if you go with a non-Apple LCD you'll have to spend about
$100 for an ADC-DVI adaptor (or use the VGA port, which kind of negates the
reason for having an LCD). Is a CRT not an option? You could get a very nice
19" for less than $700.
