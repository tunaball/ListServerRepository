Hi there to all,

Long time reader, first time poster :)

I think I might help You with this one... Please download the latest version of the CarbonLib extension for Mac OS 9 (v 1.6) from < http://www.apple.com/swupdates/ > or open your Software Update control panel and choose to do it online.

Restart zapping P-RAM (option+cmd+P+R) and let it chime 2 times. Now press cmd+option keyboards 'til the 'rebuild the desktop' dialog box shows up. I think this might solve.

BTW, do you also have the latest version of OE (v 5.0.6)?

Good luck,
