Apple G4 Cube is Nerve Center of Spamhaus: The Spamhaus.org is the anti-spam
organization headquartered on a houseboat on the Thames River in London,
according to this International Herald Tribune article. The G4 Cube controls
servers on five continents. It contains a database of 200 of the heaviest
spammers and the IP addresses of some 8,000 computers that the spammers use
to spam people. Spamhaus makes this database available to ISPs so they can
filter spam out of the e-mail boxes of their 160 million clients.
