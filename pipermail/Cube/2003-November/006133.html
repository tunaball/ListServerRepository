The ADC to DVI connector cable is only 39$. It's the DVI to ADC Display 
Adapter that is 99$! This display adapter is needed to connect an Apple 
LCD display on a PC (or on a PowerBook, or as a second monitor on a 
PowerMac).

To use a non-Apple LCD monitor, you only need the 39$ ADC to DVI cable.
