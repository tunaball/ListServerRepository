Not all PowerPC processors can report their temperature. I think it's 
the reason why Thermoindock will not launch. (the original processor in 
the Cube is a PPC7400 or PPC7410, the Gigadesign card have a PPC7455A)

You can try MenuMeters (great utility!): 
http://www.ragingmenace.com/software/menumeters/index.html
It can put a processor load graph in the menu bar, that has a menu 
displaying various information about the processor and the system. On 
my original 500 MHz Cube it displays the processor temperature.

have reported? Or can you put your Cube in deep sleep and wake it up 
without any problem?
