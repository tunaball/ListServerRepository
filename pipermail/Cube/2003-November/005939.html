I did the upgrade install vs the archive and install. This kept all my 
settings etc.  So far it works great.

For those of you nearly ready to either upgrade to Jaguar or to Panther 
from any version of OSX, run DiskUtilities/First Aid before you do the 
installation.  Fix your drive BEFORE upgrading. Not doing this can lead 
to some of the problems I've been reading about . . .
