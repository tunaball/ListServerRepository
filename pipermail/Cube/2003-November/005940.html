This is from the Mc OS X for Users list:

> I read on MacFixit that the installer failed to move some Palm files
> from the old system to the new. I believe only those who did the
> archive & install method were affected; those upgrading over Jaguar
> system would not have had a problem.
> 
> Last night I got HotSync and iSync to work again by
> 
> 1) moving "Palm Conduit Library.cfm" and "HotSync Libraries" from the
> old system folder to the new. They belong in the folder
> [hard disk]/Library/CFMSupport/Hotsync Libraries/
> 
> 2) reinstalling iSync Conduit 1.1 which is available from
> http://www.apple.com/isync
> 
> This looks like Apple's snafu and not Palm's. The good news is all my
> iSync preferences were preserved and none of my contacts or calendars
> were duplicated when I did the first sync under Panther.
