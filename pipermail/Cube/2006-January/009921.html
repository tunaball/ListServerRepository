On Jan 25, 2006, at 15:16, Gary Lauterback wrote:

> Hello Cube Owners ...
>
> My Cube's Core will not separate from it's housing.
> I've upgraded the RAM and video card. It also has an AirPort Card  
> installed.
>
> Any suggestions as to how I can get it to separate?
>
> Thanks for you time and suggestions.

Usual cause is the airport cover coming loose and preventing the core  
to come out. If it comes out for a few centimeters and then stops it  
is probably it. You can cure it by slipping something thin enough to  
fit between the core and the shell where the airport card is and the  
pull it out.
