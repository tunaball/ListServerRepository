> If you have a second Mac you could put it into Firewire Target Disk 
> Mode and boot the Cube off of the other systems CD drive. I would not 
> try booting off the other system hard drive until you know the 
> hardware is working. Otherwise you might end up trashing the other 
> disk and not have any running systems.
>
> I picked up a cheap 5.25" Firewire/USB enclosure a while back and have 
> used it at various times to read hard drives from failed systems and 
> with an installed CD drive left over after an upgrade to install 
> systems on original iMacs whose drive trays were used as cup holders 
> once too often. A spare external enclosure is one of those things I 
> think everyone should have available for emergencies.
>
