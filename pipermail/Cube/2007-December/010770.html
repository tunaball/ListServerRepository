 From what I've heard from other people, Leopard is usually equal or  
snappier than Tiger on the same hardware. Keep in mind that the  
feeling does not come form the raw speed of the operations - the new  
finder in multithreaded and more responsive, quick view speeds most of  
your tasks, the network (samba) stuff is significiantly improved. I  
hesitated until recently as well, but after doing it I would recommend  
the update.

I have it running on 1.5 MHz Powerbook and stock Cube.
