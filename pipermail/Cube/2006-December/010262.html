The Apple dual 500 needs to be modded to fit by moving a coil on the  
daughtercard, and also two spacers to make contact between the cpu  
die and the cube heatsink. The sonnet dual 500 just needs the spacers  
no coil moving needed. you can find all kinds of info and pictures on  
http://www.cubeowner.com and there was a writeup on accelerate your  
mac <http://www.xlr8yourmac.com/systems/cube/dual_g4_cube/ 
sonnet_duet_cube/dual_g4_cube_Sonnet.html>
