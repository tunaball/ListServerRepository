I use 2 different BT headsets with my Powerbook (built-in) and my Cubes
(D-Link adapters). Works fine with Skype, iChat, etc..

You'll need to make sure you have the latest bluetooth software (1.5 or
later) as well as the latest bluetooth firmware update (I believe 1.0.2 or
later) in order to have support for headsets.
