Ken,

Pressing alt key when booting will take effect only if you've got both systems installed in different partitions.
If you're not able to boot from a bootable CD (OS 9 or X), I'd try zapping PRAM: hold down simoultaneously command+alt+P+R when booting and let your Mac chime twice. You may then depress all keys and see if it boots 10.3.8.

How will you be able to determine if there's a system 9 properly installed in your Cube?

Open System Preferences and choose 'Startup Disk' pane. See if it finds a classic system 9 in your computer.

Hope this was helpful.
