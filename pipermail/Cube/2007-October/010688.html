Riba, you have at least two other means. I used a small external  
FireWire drive and Carbon Copy Cloner--installed Leopard on a small  
partition on the drive and booted my Cube from it and used CCC to  
clone Leopard to the Cube. The second is Gary's method of taking out  
your Cube's drive and installing it in another 867MHz or faster Mac,  
installing Leopard on it and putting it back in the Cube.
