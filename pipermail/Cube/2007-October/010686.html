Riba, is your Cube hard disk drive set to Master with the jumper?  If  
not, it won't function in Target Disk Mode.  This has been some  
concern to those who have installed optical drive upgrades to their  
Cubes and had to set the HDD as a slave with the jumper in order for  
the DVD/CD burner to work.

Might be worth checking.      D-B
