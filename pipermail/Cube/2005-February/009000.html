Ton,

I had a similar problem with a Cube yesterday.  The Cube is always lit 
up from the StudioDisplay button.  When I removed the Studio Display I 
could not get the Cube to start, unless I removed the core.  The fault 
was that there is a black washer about 25mm dia combined with a white 
disc, and this combined object had become misplaced (I suspect the 
previous owner!)

I replaced it -  quite complicated, I had to take the Cube casing apart 
- now it works fine - no cost at all!

I cannot be sure that this is your problem, but it sounds like a 
possibility.

Best wishes,

David.
