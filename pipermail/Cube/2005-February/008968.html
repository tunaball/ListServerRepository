On Feb 2, 2005, at 11:00 AM, Marc A. Le Pape wrote:


> 2- Regarding the alleged 128GB hard drive capacity limit  on the Cube 
> is it a confirmed fact? If it is,  is there any work around?

This is a known limitation of the cube's IDE controller. There are two 
work arounds:

1) Use external Firewire or USB.

2) Intech Software's SpeedTools ATA Hi-Cap Support drive - if you can 
live with it's limitations:
      http://www.speedtools.com/ATA6.shtml

See http://www.cubeowner.com/kbase_2/index.php?page=index_v2&id=133&c=5 
for details.
