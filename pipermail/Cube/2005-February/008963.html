Bruce: It could somehow be OSX 'Security Updates' related. I remember someone at this list couldn't install a MacOS 9.2.2 system due to not having the 10.3 up to date. As soon as this person updated the Panther system (including two security updates that were missing) he was then able to install it properly.

Concerning the way one has OSX and OS9 living together... I've had this setup right after swaping the original 20Gb for the bigger one (80Gb) and IMHO, having both systems in different partitions is better. Why? simply because you can press 'alt' when booting/rebooting and choose from which directory/partition you want your Mac to boot from.

Just my 2 cents,
