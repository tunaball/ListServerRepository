Quoth Bruce Carr (brucarr at rogers.com) at 07/02/2005 10:38:

> Does anyone happen to know of the little round plexi-glass cube USB speaker
> system works well with the mini? I tried them plugged into my G3 powerbook
> and it was all distorted - pretty useless.

That's what should happen. The Cube speakers draw more power than an
ordinary USB port can supply. Some people have been able to run them without
problems on a standard USB port, but you run the risk of shorting out your
USB port, or worse.
