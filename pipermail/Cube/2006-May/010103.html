>  I have a Cd stuck in the drive-it will not eject, no metter what I

Do you hear it buzzing, but the CD stays in? It is a common problem,  
after a while the rollers that push the CD out start slipping.  
Sticking a business card (or airplane ticket, those work  
beautifully :)) and wiggling it while the drive is trying to eject  
usually helps push the CD enough for you to grab it and pull it out.  
Recently I got fed up with such worakrounds and bought an external  
firewire box for dual layer DVD drive to be used for backups and as a  
replacement for built in drive.
