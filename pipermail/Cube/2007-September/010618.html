> Just replaced by old HD with a new one and stupidly left a CD in  
> the CD-DVD
> ROM drive, so now I can't install OS X on the new HD.

Just restart your Mac and hold the mouse button down during the  
startup procedure. This will cause any removable media to be ejected  
without having to use any command line text in terminal mode.

Also, there should be a small hole on the front of your disk reader  
that you can insert a straightened paper clip into and gently press  
the disk release lever to eject the disk.

Either of these methods are usually successful.
