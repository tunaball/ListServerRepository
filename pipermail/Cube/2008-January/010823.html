Hi Dan,

The NVIDEA GeForce 2 MX 32MB is a straight replacement for the Radion  
card and needs no mods apart from swapping over the bracket from the  
old card. In a hot climate, you may need a fan or a better heat  
exchanger but I've had the card for years without problems, although I  
did already have a fan fitted in the base bracket for CPU cooling but  
this does not give much extra flow over the video card.
