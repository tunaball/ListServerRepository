It is InTech's ATA Extended Capacity Support Driver for MacOS X, from  
the same folks that gave us SpeedTools. You can purchase separately  
from the Utilities Suite, I used with no trouble on a B&W and a  
Sawtooth, have not tried it in my Cube. OWC sells it a bit cheaper  
than InTech's site but you might check out their site and download  
their SpeedTools ATA Hi-Cap Support Driver Read Me file.
