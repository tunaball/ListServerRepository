What OS are you using?

I had an AirPort card go bad on me once, and there was no fixing it.   
It was just dead, but the Cube didn't even see it.  It was like there  
was no AirPort card installed at all.  Does the Cube see your AirPort  
card?  If so, check to make sure the antenna is connected and remove/ 
reseat the card.

What kind of Base Station?  Sometimes - about once or twice a year -  
my old Snow BS would go on the blink, and I had to reset it (Soft  
reset usually, but once or twice it needed a hard reset).  Even my  
Extreme BS has pooped out a couple of times, but using the AirPort  
Utility fixes it.  There's a special utility for OS X for older  
Graphite and Snow BS models, btw: The Extreme version doesn't work  
with them, so I have both.
