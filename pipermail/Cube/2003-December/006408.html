You are maxing out your VRM/DC/DC Power supply. Try  taking out the 
radeon 9000 pro card and putting back the rage 128 and you'll probably 
get your dvd drive back.  This is probably the easier way to test out 
this theory of mine.  When you do take it out, remember to push the 
reset button on the motherboard (I forgot the name) Oh, and unplug all 
firewire and usb perphs such as your usb speakers.
