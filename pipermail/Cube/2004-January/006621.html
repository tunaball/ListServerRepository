Hi Marty,

I have a Cube with your specs as well.

You do not have to update to 10.2.8.

Do this:

Insert Panther Disc 1  . . .
Double Click the installer . . .
Key in your Password . . .
Computer reboots into Panther's Disc . . .

Note: Mouse up to the Blue Apple ...
Click - hold - mouse down to Disk Utilities . . . Release . . .

When the Utility Program opens, click over to First Aid  . . .
Select your Hard Drive . . .
Click on the Repair Button!

When complete, exit.
Now you are back to the installation screen ... proceed.

These steps worked for me . . . I has a smooth transition from Jaguar 
to Panther . .
