Yes, I saw them; but didn't get a chance to listen to them.
The rubber ear buds come in different sizes (I believe 3) and the 
rubber is REAL soft; like almost not noticeable.  Apple rep.  said 
something like same style of compounds as used in contact lenses. 
The case is rather bulky (if you were to carry them around in it); it 
holds the ear buds, 2 sets of different size ear pieces, and the cord 
wraps around outside.

one nice advantage of the different size ear pieces that I see is 
possibility of more than one person using the ear buds with different 
ear pieces. or possibility of trading unused ear pieces for a second 
set of your size
