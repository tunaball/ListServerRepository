Don't plug them! Really, I mean it.

The power amplifier of the Cube USB speakers (that little black box) 
requires more power than available on a standard USB port. The Cube USB 
ports (and the ports on the Apple Displays) are designed to provide 
enough power for the speakers, but not the Ti USB ports.

What might happen if you plug them: weak sound, for sure; blowing your 
Ti USB port, maybe...

Take care!
François.
