A normal USB port supplies 500mA, so when you're plugging your USB 
amp in such a "normal" port the Cube speakers (or some driver?) 
throttle the volume level (and thus power consumption). The speakers 
are just half as loud, but they work.

On a Cube USB port they switch into specs-ignoring-hi-power mode and 
suck more than 800mA. I know for sure, I measured it ;-))

Oh, and everyone with a 7457 upgrade, please put your Cube to sleep 
as often as possible, let it sleep as long as possible etc., since a 
faster, but comatose Cube would be... a bummer ;-) I always put it to 
sleep when I go to bed (this is my work machine) and thus a flaky 
sleep mode would be really bad for me.

I actually still wait for the dual board with the on-board VRM, this 
looks like a *really* fine piece of engineering ;-)
