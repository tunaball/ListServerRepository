You might try booting in 'Verbose Mode' .
You can start one time by holding 'command-V' during boot.
Once you get the text screen, you can let go.
Or you can enter 'sudo nvram boot-args=-v' in a terminal window
and you machine will boot in verbose mode every time.

I like to watch the messages, gives me early warning about any OS
or hardware problems.
