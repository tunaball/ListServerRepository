I use a pair of thin tweezers.   As soon as the drive tries to eject (you can 
tell by the noise) reach in and grasp it with the tweezers.   I've got some 
with plastic tips, for removing electrical parts.   They might not damage the 
CD/DVD, but it's the only way I've found to get some of them out.   If you try 
to grab the disc before it tries to eject, the spinning disc will rub on the 
tweezers and that will probably ruin part of the tracks.
