On Apr 8, 2006, at 3:23 AM, Peter Lang wrote:

> Do you know it's possible to change from airport card b to g  
> (extreme) for cube without great effort?

Airport and Airport Extreme cards are not interchangeable. They have  
physically different interfaces. If you want 802.11g you'll have to  
go with USB. While an 802.11g will saturate a cube's USB 1.1 port you  
should still do better than 802.11b which usually works at 3-5 Mbs in  
real use.
