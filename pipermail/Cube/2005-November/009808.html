I probably am not the first to suggest this, but there is a manual CD  
eject lever located near the left edge of the CD slot as you face the  
front of the cube. I have several times liberated jammed CDs using a  
straightened paper clip and pressing downwards. I'm sure you can find  
more info about how to do this if you search the 'net.
