With a pair of tin snips one can modify the bracket, clipping off the  
end of it (the turn in a typical PCI/ISA/AGP card bracket.  Takes a  
second, and fits if you feel the need.  Operating without a bracket  
works fine, but leaves somewhat unstable the DVI/VGA/ADC and S-video  
jacks, depending on type of card you acquire,   No problem if you  
aren't the type who goes about tugging cables for fun.
