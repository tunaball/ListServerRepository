Unlike the GF2MX, I believe that only the Apple OEM model of GF3 will 
fit in a Cube but I haven't tried others. Apple OEM GF3's are offered 
now and then on eBay but usually go for $200+. OWC has gotten a bunch 
infrequently.

I also agree with Riba that the GF3 is really only for serious gamers 
at higher resolutions, i.e. above 1024x768. An 800 MHz PowerLogix CPU 
upgrade is a real help as well. IMO, if you play at lower resolutions, 
the GF2MX is just as good, especially with the CPU upgrade.
