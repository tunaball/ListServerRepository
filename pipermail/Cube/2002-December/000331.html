Sorry, that can only be done when you reformat. Back it up, reformat
("erase"), check the box that says "install OS 9 drivers", then restore your
backup. CarbonCopyCloner is by far the easiest and quickest (and free)
method of cloning your current OS X setup for backup and restore.
