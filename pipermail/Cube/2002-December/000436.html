I've never had it happen with a Mac, but we had a whole series of cheap 
ATX supplies the went bad.  Sleeve bearing fans would seize then 
everytrhing would overheat.  When the supply failed, these particular 
supplies put 55 volt unregulated chopped DC on the +-12v, 3.3 and 5v 
lines at the same time.  It blew chips off the motherboards, drive 
controller boards, lots of magic smoke set free.  I'll never buy an ATX 
supply from Thailand labeled "Green Tech".
