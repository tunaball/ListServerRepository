Actually, all you really _need_ to listen to music anywhere is the 
wireless card in whatever machine you want to control iTunes with and 
the Airport Express near the stereo you want to pipe the music to.
That said, an iBook without wifi is rather like a Sundae without 
chocolate - you're missing the best part of a laptop!

You'll want the older Airport card for the Cube (not Airport Extreme). 
OWC had a batch on discount a few months ago for $60 or so each. No idea 
if they still have them. They're pretty hard to find these days.

Unfortunately, my personal experience with the Cube and Airport Express 
hasn't been that good. I've got two Airport Expresses at opposite ends 
of the house and the Cube almost in the middle. Even though the Cube 
reports full signal strength, I still get audio dropouts on both units. 
They're much worse in the living room next to the big stereo & TV setup 
(lots of electronics) as opposed to the bedroom, which only has a small 
stereo. In all other aspects, the Airport Express rocks. Setup was a 
breeze and all of my wifi devices work well moving between the two units.
