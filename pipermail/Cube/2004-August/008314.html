Re-start holding down the cmd & S keys. At the prompt, type fsck -y(f, 
if journalling), ENTER
Repeat 'til if says OK, then type reboot, hit enter.

If still not OK, do the OPEN FIRMWARE & PRAM resets - see the Apple 
site for details...
