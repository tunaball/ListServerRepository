A couple of simple things to try:

1) The AC Power Supply housing is full of holes.
Get a can of air or use a compressor to blow out the device.
Dust can be murder on electronics.
2)  Be sure your connections are solid. (You've probably confirmed this already ...)
3)  If nothing works, either it's your AC PS, or your MLB ...

Let us know okay?
