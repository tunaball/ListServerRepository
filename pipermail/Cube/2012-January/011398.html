I am guessing you have checked or replaced the battery on the motherboard, as well as holding down the red cuda switch while it was unplugged. Or you have tried the monitor on another computer to confirm it is still working....  tried a new/ used working video card. Replaced the VRM board or at least had it checked for its working state. Booted up holding the space bar only till it completely boots up. Reset the PRAM, reset the firmware....  Tried new memory sticks.
If you have completely done all that and it still doesn't work, I would look for a working used plain Jane unit for around $150 and go that route.....  almost sounds like the motherboard has a point where it can not handle the voltage anymore or heating up at that point and it automatically shuts down.... due to wear or repetitive booting.
