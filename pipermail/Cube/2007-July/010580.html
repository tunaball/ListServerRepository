Yes, it's the CRT type. This was the middle one in the range of  
displays that were part of the Cube options originally; the ones on  
either side size-wise being LCD's. If anyone can find an engineer's  
handbook, that would be the most useful. BTW, it's the shell casing  
surrounding the electronics and tube thats dusty, and the  
electronics, and all very visible. I'll be using an 'E.cloth' to  
clean most of it - no chemicals - and a camera brush-puffer and tiny  
plastic vac cleaner for the rest.
