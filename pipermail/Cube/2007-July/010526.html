> I suspect that the cooling fan on your GF3 card gave up, and the

It could also be the monitor itself is failing. Try the cube with a  
different monitor to duplicate the effect. If duplicated, it is the  
card. If not duplicated, it's the monitor.

As to the issue of the black screen, you are experiencing kernel  
panics at startup. You probably have a corrupted installation of the  
system on your cube. Do you really mean "OS 10.4.1", or do you mean  
"OS 10.4.10"?

If your monitor problem gets resolved, try doing a new install of the  
system using Archive and Install but do not transfer your user  
settings so you can avoid any conflict or corruption in the  
preferences, extensions, etc.
