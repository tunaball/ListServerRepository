Op 11-okt-04 om 15:24 heeft Jim Sofa het volgende geschreven:

> I'm interested in purchasing an external DVD burner
> for use on my Cube.

I would buy one of the latest 8 X Dual burners (Pioneer, LG, Nec) at a 
PC shop and buy an external housing (firewire, USB?) put them together 
myself.

Will save you an incredible amount as compared with "ready to run" 
units. All you need is a screwdriver and some even don't need that 
because they simply clock.
