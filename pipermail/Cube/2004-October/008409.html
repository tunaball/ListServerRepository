Check dealmac.com daily for the best deals on just about everything 
mac.  A case with a Pioneer DVR-108 is about the latest and greatest, 
as it will burn DV-R and DV+R as well as double layer DV+R media.  I 
got one and put it in a case that had been holding an earlier Pioneer 
(DVR-105, I think).  Works just fine.

OH, AND BY ALL MEANS, GET ONE WITH A FIREWIRE INTERFACE.  Especially 
with a Cube where you only have USB 1.1 which is slow and not all 
that reliable for large transfers, at least in my experience.

--Steve
