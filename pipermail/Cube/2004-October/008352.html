Melanie,

You can use any ATA-100 hard disk available. They are perfectly compatible with your Cube's ATA-66.
Mine is a 80Gb Seagate Barracuda IV, and my Cube is dead silent and faster than with the original 20Gb Maxtor.
Yes, you can still use Mac OS 9.x (and get ready for Mac OS X ;)

For a step-by-step help, nothing better than visiting the FAQ section of < http://www.cubeowner.com/ >

Good luck,
