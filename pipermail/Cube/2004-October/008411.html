I highly recommend LaCie's DVD±RW Dual Layer packages:

http://www.lacie.com/products/product.htm?id=10114

DVD Write, Rewrite, and read speeds as follows:

DVD-RW:     16x 4x 12x
DVD+RW:    16x 4x 12x
DVD+R DL:  4x

Mine has been flawless. It's always powered on, but silent (no fans) 
and only makes any noise when a disc is inserted, but even then, I 
think it's quieter than the Cubes internal DVD drive.
