A few months ago my beloved Cube expired due to problems with the logic 
board and the bto GeForce 2 MX graphics card.  It was in the last month 
of three year Applecare cover, and after two and a half months of phone 
calls and correspondence, I eventually received from Apple a 
replacement machine in the form of a new USB2 eMac, as Apple couldn't 
source a replacement graphics card.  Nice and fast, but noisy, and I 
still miss my Cube.  Many thanks to everyone on this board for the 
advice and tips that have been invaluable over the last few years.

Yesterday I bought from a friend a G4 AGP 500 mhz tower, thinking 
naively that it would have an ADC connector which I could hook up to my 
15" flat screen (Apple only had the Cube back, not all the 
peripherals), but the Rage 128 Pro card fitted only has VGA/DVI output. 
  Do the 128 Pro, GeForce and Radeon cards fitted originally to Cubes 
fit a G4 tower, and, if so, do any Cube listers in the UK have one 
available they might like to sell me?  Please contact me off list at 
g.burnett at macunlimited.net.  Many thanks.

George B
