I have had periodic problems with Safari. Usually having it stop 
loading web pages mid-stream but also crashing constantly. Over at the 
Apple support discussions there were a few ideas to help solve these 
(which didn't always solve my problems.) One is to change the fonts 
that Safari uses. Deleting preferences and emptying caches also is 
recommended. Someone else said he had luck deleting the bookmarks file 
(but it is a huge pain to reimport them.) I've had luck running fsck 
and a utility like Cocktail or Onyx. But I also keep Camino around to 
use as a backup.
