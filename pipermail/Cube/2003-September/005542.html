I think Tom Bender, Tex-Edit's author, would appreciate everyone knowing that
Tex-Edit is not free; rather, it is shareware, and is the best $15 I have spent
yet on software. Easily the most AppleScriptable text editor out there,
including SimpleText or TextEdit, there are myriad scripts available at The
Official AppleScripts for Tex-Edit Archives, run by Doug Adams:

<http://www.malcolmadams.com/te/>

A lot of these scripts help automate the HTML process, and the entire site is a
boon to Tex-Edit users.
