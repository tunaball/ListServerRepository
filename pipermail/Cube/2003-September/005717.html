A point to note:  The commands to create these links is the
reverse of what you see when you ls(list) the links.

ie

ln -s private/etc etc

ls -l etc
lrwxrwxr-t  1 root  admin  11 Sep 25 20:27 etc -> private/etc

The first one says "link private/etc to etc"
The second one says "etc is linked to private/etc"

Get it?
