On 2003-09-17 12:32, Tom Hostler wrote:

> Remember too that you loose the 2 USB ports you have on a standard Apple
> keyboard, and if you don't have built in Bluetooth and have to add a USB
> dongle, then you loose 3 USB ports as one is used for the dongle to
> communicate with the keyboard & mouse.

Fun with numbers!

Lessee, "traditional" keyboard and mouse setup:

-1 port for the keyboard.
+2 ports on the keyboard.
-1 port for the mouse.

Result: No ports gained or lost.

Bluetooth keyboard and mouse setup:

-1 port for the dongle (if required).

Result: 1 port lost on older Macs, No ports lost or gained on Macs
w/Bluetooth.

Not exactly a loss of 3 ports, though.
