I am running the GF3 in my cube now but used the Rage Pro 128 up until 
a month ago. Not a huge difference.  The Rage 128 is a great card as it 
has worked with everything I tried and it's what - 3 or 4 years old.  
Ram makes the biggest difference short of a faster processor. Get the 
1.5 gig of Ram first and then whatever you want after.

The GF3 is noisy but I do not find it a bother.  I think a modified fan 
config can elliminate half the noise but that will happen another time.
