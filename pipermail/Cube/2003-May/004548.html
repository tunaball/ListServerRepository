Randy,

Are you sure that the display does not turn off?  There are both Screen 
Effects and Energy Saver options for blanking the display.  Do you have 
the display option enabled in Energy Saver?  Mine seems to turn off 
completely - or at least it seems to turn off the backlight, which is 
what's important to me.

You can also sleep the HD.  I figure a fanless Cube with HD & display 
sleeping is about as minimal as you can get with a non-sleeping 
computer.
