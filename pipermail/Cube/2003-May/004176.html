Hi Francine!  I'm trying to upgrade my Cube's HD also.  I found the
following information for my hard drive on my Apple System Profiler, under
the Bus/Hard Drive section of the "Devices and Volumes" tab:
* ATA Device Type: ATA
* Device Serial: N30K5S8C
* Device Revision: BAC51JJ0
* Device Model: Maxtor 92049U3

I have a 450Mz Cube with a 20GB Drive, and I'm running it on OS Jaguar
10.2.5.   If you have that version of OS X your System Profiler should also
provide you with the correct information for your HD.  Good luck!
