A short note to say that I just put a WD SE 250 GB drive in an old ADS 
FireWire case and the finder indicates that it can see all of it. 
System Profiler says it's only 128 GB big. But finder and get info both 
say it the full 233 GB (after formatting). Thought some of you might 
want to know that your old ADS cases may be fine for bigger than 120 GB 
drives.
