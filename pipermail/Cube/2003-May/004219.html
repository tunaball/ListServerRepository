Processor upgrade, Maximum RAM and 120 GB HD are the three most 
important to me. Keep in mind that the 120 GB 8MB buffer Western 
Digital Special Edition HDs are now only $80 at Circuit City. Last week 
they were $70 at Office Max. So that's not much out of pocket to 
maximize your internal HD. A single 1.2 GHz G4 upgrade from Sonnet is 
$578 at OWC so that's the most expensive thing. And RAM is fairly 
inexpensive. Shouldn't cost more than $69-$79 right now for each 512MB 
stick. So the RAM and the HD are very inexpensive compared to the 
processor upgrade.
