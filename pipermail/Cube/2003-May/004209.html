I wonder if you noticed the small clip on the "under" side of the 
original Maxtor HD. It was screwed to the side of the HD and fit into a 
notch inside the Cube -- effectively stabilizing the HD and eliminating 
any vibrational noise.

When you removed the original HD, you may have overlooked this clip. 
And if you did, this would explain the noise associated with the 
replacement HDs.
