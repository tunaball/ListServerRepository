be used for another use in the future.


>D-Link DWL-810+ is a wireless bridge that you can use to bridge your 
>LAN to your Tivo location.  It has an RJ-45 Ethernet jack to connect 
>to a device with Ethernet support in it.  As I am not a Tivo user, I 
>don't know if all Tivo's have Ethernet in them.  But, I have used the 
>D-Link wireless bridge for other tasks, and it works well.  It also 
>supports 128-bit and 64-bit encryption.  I got mine at the local 
>MicroCenter for US$79.95 and an additional $20 mail-in rebate.
