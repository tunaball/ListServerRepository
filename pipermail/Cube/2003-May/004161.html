Just to see if I could, mind you, I ripped a vob off a DVD movie
I just purchased (The Rocketeer) and copied it to my 20Gig iPod.

Next I hooked up the iPod to my 700Mhz iBook.  Then I mounted the iPod
via AFP (Go->Connect to Server) to my Cube.

Next I fired up DVDplayer on the Cube and clicked on 'File' and selected
the iPod/Rocketeer/VIDEO_TS folder and clicked the Play button.

The movie started, but there were a few too many skips/pauses.
So I hooked up my iBook to my LAN via my 10/100 switch instead
of Airport.

Cool.  I'm now watching the movie *Full Screen* with no problems.
Streaming it from my iPod connected to another Mac across the room!

I just get a kick out of this kind of stuff.
