Shannon emailed me the details, which I posted earlier today:

The Cube Saver cost of $119 includes the following:

* Overnight shipment of a custom, Cube sized box to the end user.
* Return Overnight shipping of the end users Cube to our Repair Center
* Replacement of the Power Gasket
* Installation of a DC to DC converter board brace. (ed. note: do not
confuse the brace with a replacement board. the brace is sometimes used to
keep a loose dc/dc board in place, so that you don't have to re-seat it
yourself when it starts misbehaving)
* Return overnight shipping back to the end user.

All of this is done within 24 hours.

The 3-way overnight shipping alone costs 3 x $28 = $84 (ed. note: leaving
less than $40 for parts and labor, which is on par with what any other AASP
should charge). The repair takes apx 30 minutes to complete by a trained
technician.
