On 10/20/03 1:22 PM, Lacorona at aol.com added a note to the human symphony,
when this was said:

> Is there like a relay deck for the cube? A deck is a device that has all the
> firewire/usb/phone cord outlets and etc that you can PLUG INTO THE CUBE and
> this deck goes on your desk at hand, and then you plug in all your wires
> (firewire/usb phone cord outlet, etc) right there at hand instead of having to
> tear 
> apart the desk and tip the cube upside down to get the connections
> disconnected 
> or connected???

Those would be called hubs. You can get a USB hub, a firewire hub (or a
combo hub that includes both), and if you need the modem port handy as well,
just get a phone line extension cable (male on one end to plug into the cube
and female port on other end)
