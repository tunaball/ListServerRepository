Yeah, it should work fine. Try it first to make sure everything works 
ok...but for the most part, I don't see any problems. I used to carry 
an external drive around with me everywhere so any mac I sat in front 
of was always my system :)

If you hold the OPTION key at startup, it will display all the bootable 
drives as buttons. Click the one you want to start from, and your 
golden. As long as the drive has power (or in some cases...is powered 
by the firewire cable), you can then choose it as the startup disk, and 
when you start the system up it should look at the external disk to 
boot.

Hope this works for you!
