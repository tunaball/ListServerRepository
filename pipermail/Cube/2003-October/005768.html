Yes, and this is normal. Most DVD discs are 'dual-layered', so that 
they contain twice the data (have you ever asked youself why the DVD-R 
media holds less data than the read only? :) During the duration of the 
movie, the laser reader needs to switch to a 'second layer' to continue 
reading DVD data, and at that point you have this small hiccup. This 
happens also with conventional DVD players.
