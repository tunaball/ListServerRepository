I've had one for the last 4 or 5 months (practically since they first
came out) and I'm quite happy with it.  It's a bit slow burning DVDs,
but it's alot faster than what I had before (nothing).

It's a bit tricky getting media into it, though.  You have to pretend
like it's a coin slot in a vending machine and give it a bit of a
shove to get it down to the takeup motor.  Not tough once you get 
the hang of it.
