One very good place (U.S.A.) is newegg.com.

There are step-by-step instructions at the Apple web site.  It is 
easy to install, but I recommend purchasing a long-shaft Torx wrench 
set.  I think you need a #8 and a #10 (it has been so long since I 
installed any drives in my Cube that I forget now--maybe a #8 is all 
you need).  Home Depot sells a nice set for about US$20.
