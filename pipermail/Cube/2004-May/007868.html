 > what are the possible solutions?  and what are the pros and cons of 
each?

I think that people would do well to look for solutions from the Unix 
world as well as from the Mac world.  As long as you're not afraid of 
the terminal and reading a bit of documentation before starting, there 
are some really powerful solutions out there.  People have been doing 
this stuff on Unix machines for decades.

I think you should investigate "rsync," which is ideal for 
synchronizing machines on the same network.  You can use ssh for 
transport if security is an issue.  There's even a Mac OS X / HFS+ 
adapted version, at http://www.macosxlabs.org/RsyncX/RsyncX.html

Here's an article on getting started:

	http://www.bombich.com/mactips/rsync.html

Hope this helps,
