Honestly, I ahven't used any of the tools you have mentioned, and I'd 
go as far as to say that you don't need any of these, at least not for 
the 'regualr' maintenance. For disasters I've always relied on 
DiskWarrior and Data Rescue X, but luckily I haven't had the need to 
use them on OS X - yet.
Everything for regular maintenance is built right into OS. You might 
want to use Macaroni as someone suggested or MacJanitor, utilities that 
ease the use of built-in system maintenance tasks.
