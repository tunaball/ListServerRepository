Get some of that information off your boot HD.  Your G4 runs best with 
20% or more of the boot HD empty.  You say you have 1.4 gig empty and 
once you start your 3 programs you don't have anything left - what did 
you expect - a miracle.  Give the processor some space to work with - 
remember, that 50 meg graphic needs to be tempararily remembered when 
you are using it (even if it's just where is the swap disk somewhere 
else) and so do all the system overheads that you cannot assign swap 
space, and so on, and so on, ...

I am now going to suggest that you do a house cleaning and dump those 3 
year old emails and all the crap you don't need.  But maybe you did 
this already, so move the 10 - 15 gig of info off your boot drive and 
put it on an external (assuming the orig 20 gig HD) or get a bigger 
internal HD.  Clear at least 10% of your boot drive or more, and plan 
to never fill it again.
