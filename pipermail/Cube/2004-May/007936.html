Mine has only been installed for about a week now.....

Speed, it boots up in 20sec, always wakes up from sleep, and has never
locked up at all. I went out and got Halo and it have been playing with high
graphics and still not issues, heat is also lower then stock, due to the
fan, it really keeps things cool.

Noise is still nill, I can hear the new 7200rpm drive, but never the fan
that is constant on.

You do have to install the firmware updates first, the cube will not work
with out them. 

I have no OS 9 experience, that has been LONG gone from my Mac's.

While it is expensive, it is still cheaper then a new Mac, and I must say it
is faster then the new 20in iMac 1.25 we have at work.

My cube....
1.35 PL
7200RPM WD 80gig with8mb cashe
1.5gig ram
G-force 2 (stock card)
