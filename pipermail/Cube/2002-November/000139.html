You can get a quiet Panaflo fan (~$20) and install it at the same time 
as the drive if you are worried. See CubeOwner.com for the fan's model 
number and how to hook up power to it. But, you shouldn't need it with 
just the drive, especially if you don't do lots of disk-intensive work 
on your Cube.
