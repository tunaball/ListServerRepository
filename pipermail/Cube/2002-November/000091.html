Oh, and one more thing:

Don't compare temperatures between Cubes. The CPU heat probe is 
totally inaccurate. It doesn't mean shit if your Cube is xx degrees 
hotter or cooler than another Cube. I had 3 Cubes (kept one) and all 
of them had *totally* different temp values.

Just use it to compare temps of the *same* Cube. Like when you add a 
new gfx card or if you worked on the CPU and removed the heatsink 
etc. Any changes here are interesting, like if your CPU runs 10 
degrees hotter than before, then it could be that you made something 
wrong when you reassembled your heatsink etc.
