I found my Cube 500 with it's original Maxtor 60 GB 7200 to be MUCH
hotter than the normal 450's with 5400 rpm drives. It would heat up the
CDRW drive to the point that CD's seemed dangerously hot. I thought
it was the CPU difference at first but didn't see how, especially with 
my
500 using the newer, cooler G4 7410 - and then I noticed where the HDD
was installed right behind the CD.

I've since replaced it with a Maxtor 120 GB 7200...it's so much quieter
than the old drive that I was quite astonished.  I can't really tell if 
it's
cooler or not, though, because I have a makeshift external fan below
my cube.
