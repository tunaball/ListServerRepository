I haven't done this yet but will in the future.

1. You can get a RS dual reading thermometer and drop probes at the 
intake and the exhaust and get the temp and the heat gain, which gives 
you even a better idea.

2. Use a Dallas Semi Conductor  Thermochron iButton to build a 1-Wire 
network to mon. temp.
