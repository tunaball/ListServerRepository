On 22 Jun, 2005, at 7:38, tobias wrote:

I have some stuff on external scsi Jaz that I’d like to load into my 
Cube so I can burn the data.

  I do have a Microtech SCSI-USB adapter, and when I connect the 
(terminator switch to “on”) Jaz to the Cube (either a powered hub or 
directly to one of the two usb ports), Apple system profiler sees the 
connection, but that’s it. No drive shows up, neither under X nor when 
restarting under 9.2

  Must I buy a firewire connection, or am I doing something wrong?

It is possible that you do not have the proper software installed in 
the Cube to recognize the Jaz drive. Go to the Iomega web site and 
download the latest version of their software. Install the software and 
restart the Cube with a cartridge in the Jaz drive. This may be the 
answer to your problem.
