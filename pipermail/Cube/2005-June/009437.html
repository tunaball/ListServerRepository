Couple of things:  Where did my Internet Explorer Go?!  Just kidding.  It remains in Apps, but it was removed from my Dock.

1]  No "W" page icons on .doc files and they default to open up with TextEdit.  Weird.

2]  When I take a snapshot of the screen, the PDF does not take up position in the first open space on the Desktop, it lands on another file or folder.  Frustrating.
