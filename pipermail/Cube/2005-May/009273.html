Helen:

There is a list that covers speech recognition software and the 
developers are there.  It is a very quiet list with many people that 
know almost everything there is about the software.

Also be aware that ViaVoice is no longer being updated and probably 
ceased to function beyond 10.2.x.  IBM has no plans to update it on the 
Mac platform ever and have sold off the rights to sell it to another 
company.  ViaVoice is dead in the water, unless something changes.

Here is the address for the lists

Scroll down to Voice Recognition list.
