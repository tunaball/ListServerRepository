My folks, who live in tornado alley, have had to replace every electronic
device in the house due to line surges during thunderstorms. Dad finally
listened to the wisdom of surge suppressors. You can buy suppressors with
guarantees for replacing whatever is damaged. A little more expensive, but
it might cover what the home owner's won't.
