Assuming that you are popping your email, set the PC client to _NOT_ 
remove mail from server. Set Mail.app the same. Then you should have two 
complete inboxes. You'll have to tell one of the clients to remove 
downloaded mail from your server, from time to time, or you'll go over 
quota.

With Mail.app, this is chosen via Preferences/Advanced. Your PC client has 
similar choices.

To the original poster: if, in fact, your two Mac's are networked, you can 
mount the drive which contains the mailboxes, and import directly from 
there. Saves a tiny step.
