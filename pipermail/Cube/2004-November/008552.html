Assuming you are using Apple Mail, copy the mailboxes from computer A to 
computer B. Open Mail on computer B, open File/Import Mailboxes, select 
Mail for Mac OS X, and follow the arrows.

Once that's done, you can open the imported mailboxes, and drag and drop 
into your old mailboxes, to the extent wanted.

For other clients, there is probably a similar route. Take a gander at 
http://www.emailman.com .
