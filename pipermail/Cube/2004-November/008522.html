>  Question to those of you, who have experience with the Sonnet 1.2 
> GHz upgrade:
>  Did you have problems with this upgrade or can you recommend it?

I installed this in my wife's previously-500 mHz Cube with 1G RAM. 
Took about an hour but wasn't hard. Turned the Cube on and everything 
just worked - no oddities or problems then or any time since. We 
already had OS X 10.2.x running before the upgrade. We never put the 
Cube to sleep so no info on that.

Benchmarks, actual performance and subjective impressions all point 
to the expected speed increase.

Hope this helps.
