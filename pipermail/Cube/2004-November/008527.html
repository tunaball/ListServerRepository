I purchased a stock 400 MHz "mint condition" Cube off eBay last 
summer and upgraded it with many goodies... the first being the 
Sonnet 1.2 GHz card which I chose after careful evaluation of company 
rep, warranty etc. In addition, I installed a different video card so 
I could run a couple of LCD monitors (20" and 15") and a larger 
faster 120 GB Seagate drive and more RAM. It has run perfectly from 
first upgraded boot up. I don't miss not running a noisy G5. Silence, 
or the next best thing, is golden. And the form factor still leaves 
me in silent (pun intended) wonder.
