Do you have the original software? If so try Disk First Aid. It should 
say on the Original Disk what key to hold when starting up. Then see if 
that will fix the problem. Another thing to try: take the battery out 
of the Cube and leave it out for a while then put it back in. Reboot. 
I'm not real knowledgeable but it won't hurt to try these.Some have 
worked for me.
Have you tryed zapping the P ram? Hold down Option, Control, P&R when 
you startup and let it ping three times.See if that fixes it. I hope 
you can get it going. Melvetta
