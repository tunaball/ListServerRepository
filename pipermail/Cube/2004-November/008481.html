I was just trying out a language application.  I looked for the 
uninstall info.  Here's what it said:

How to Uninstall the Program


Windows

Option 1 - Click the "Uninstall" button in the installer screen.

Option 2 - Click on "Start" in the Windows task bar, usually located 
in the lower left corner of the screen. Select "Programs." Select 
"The Rosetta Stone." Click on "Uninstall the Rosetta Stone."

Option 3 - Click on "Start" in the Windows task bar, usually located 
in the lower left corner of the screen. Select "Settings." Click on 
"Control Panel." Double click on "Add/Remove Programs." Select "The 
Rosetta Stone" and click "Remove."


Mac

  Simply drag the Rosetta Stone folder into the trash.
