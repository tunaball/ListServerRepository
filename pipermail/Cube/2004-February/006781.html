after doing a xbench test myself and owing "only" a nvidia geforce 2mx

then again, I do have a speedier cpu (1,2 GHz).

the results are:
	Quartz Graphics Test	124.88	
		Line	99.25	2.53 Klines/sec [50% alpha]

card.
