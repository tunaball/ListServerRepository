I recommend the PowerLogix 1.2Gig upgrade.  I've been very happy with
mine.  There's also a 1.4x available, but the price difference may not
be worth it to you (if I had it to do over I'd go for the 1.4x, but at
this point it's not worth it to me either).

As for the video card, you want to get one with at least 32MB so it can
handle Quartz Extreme.  The ATI Rage128 that comes stock in the Cube is
only a 16MB card.  Not good enough.  The ATI Radeon or the Nvidia
GeForce2 MX are two reasonable choices.  Both have 32MB and fit the
Cube without any modifications.  I've tried both and elected to go
with the Radeon because it beat out the GeForce2 in the Xbench tests,
but not by much.

I've got a couple spare GF2-MXs, so I might be willing to part with
one if you can't find any on Ebay.
