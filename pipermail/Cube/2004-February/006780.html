Hi Listers,

First of all, I have to thank Boris, Erik, Laurie, Joe, Sean, Gary, P. Mitchell, 'Dreeser' and 'Riba' for supporting me on this.

This was my procedure:

> backed up my home user data,
> booted using Mac OS X (aka Panther) install CD and did the 'reset password'
> booted iBook from (internal) 10.2.3 and created another user/short name (my sister) giving her 'Administrator priviledges'
> logged out my accout/logged in sister's account
> deleted my home user's account
> Opened 'Disk Utility' and repaired disk permissions
> restarted iBook
> sister's got an iBook for free and she's happy now!
