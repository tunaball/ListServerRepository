I'd suggest that anybody who wants to read it this way, install INN and 
mail2news.  Subscribed a custom address to the list and gateway it in.

I've been doing it for almost 15 years for most lists (only a couple of 
years for the Cube list).  I don't have any intention of supporting it 
for the whole community - I don't have the time or energy to do so - 
but it's really not that difficult to do yourself as long as you're the 
only one using it.  And you'll learn a lot about mail2news, news2mail, 
and INN in the process.

We're running *Unix*.

[To expand: the mailing list will only accept mail from subscribed 
address, so a gateway for more than one address isn't generally useful. 
  What a PITA.]
