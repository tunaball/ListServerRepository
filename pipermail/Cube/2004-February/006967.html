In the software update (after it is launched and found all your 
suggested updates) look in the menu at the top of your screen.  Look 
under UPDATE and MAKE INACTIVE.  This will make your choosen updates 
from the list inactive and you will not see them again unless you go to 
SHOW INACTIVE.  This lets you peruse your previous choices if you 
suddenly get an ipod or Airport or whatever.
