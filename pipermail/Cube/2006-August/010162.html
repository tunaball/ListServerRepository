Sorry, no.  Without doing fancy HD partitioning, the maximum size  
hard disk that the system firmware in a Cube recognizes is 120gb.   
The good news is that they are very cheap.  No problem hooking up an  
EXTERNAL bigger hard drive (Firewire or USB) to a Cube (except you  
can't boot to an external USB drive on a cube, nor can you boot to an  
external Firewire drive greater than 120gb I don't believe...)

Good luck!
