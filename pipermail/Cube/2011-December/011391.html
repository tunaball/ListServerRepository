If it still doesn't work after a pram reset, you can try resetting the nvram through open firmware.

Startup the cube and hold:

Command+option+o+f

Hold that until you get a white screen with some black text

Type:

reset-nvram
(press enter)
Then:
reset-all
(press enter and the cube will restart)

Hope this helps.
