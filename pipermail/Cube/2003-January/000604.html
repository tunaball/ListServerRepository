On Sun, 5 Jan 2003 17:10:30 -0500, Richard Rodman wrote:
> The color classic (is it a color classic II?) is a hot seller on 
> ebay.  I think they are rare and wanted by collectors

They are all part of a craze (started in Japan a few years ago) to 
create "Power Color Classics". Post people modify them to 640x480 and 
replace the original motherboard to an 040 processor. Some people 
install G3 and even G4 boards in them to run at 300+Mhz, dual video 
displays, internal CDs, etc. One person, as mentioned on the original 
post, put a G4 into one. Some wild paint jobs as well. The bug caught 
me for a while too. I saw Color Classics going for $80-$100 on ebay 
last year.
