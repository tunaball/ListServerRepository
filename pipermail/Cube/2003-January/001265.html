Forget about all your obsolete CD-ROMs. Get all your stuff from the Web.

Your QuickTime 4 is way obsolete. So is your 9.0.4 System. Run your 
System Update Control Panel repeatedly until you get to at least 9.1 
and maybe higher. You have to run it repeatedly because some updates 
won't even show up until you have run the previous set. So keep running 
it until the dialog box says there are no more updates available for 
your Mac.

QuickTime 6.1 is a FREE download from the Apple site or maybe from your 
system updater control panel.

Here's the URL where the web based installer download is:

http://www.apple.com/quicktime/download/
