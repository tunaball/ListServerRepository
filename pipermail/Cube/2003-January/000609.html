A week ago I received a "Lombard" Powerbook 300 MHz G3 on work. I 
loaded it with OS X.2.3 and it runs better than I expected. I've got 
384 MB of RAM, and when I have Mail, Chimera, ICQ, one java App and a 
couple of small utils loaded I still have about 40 MB of unused RAM. 
I'd say go for it - the performance will be acceptable for most home 
work, just load it with plenty of RAM.
