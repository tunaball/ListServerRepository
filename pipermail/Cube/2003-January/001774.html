Upgrading a CUBE or buying an old cube VS a new tower?

Buy  the new tower!

We love our CUBES yes we do- And we'll do almost anything to keep them 
up to date.
But at some point we will all have to give them up maybe for the 4ghz 
processor and the 800 Firewire
and faster buses and faster RAM and the Super Drives and everything 
else Apple will improve on in the coming years.

Buy the Tower and make the coming years easier on yourself to make 
those upgrades due to come.
