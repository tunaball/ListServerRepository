Following the advice of this List, I purchased a GeForce2 video card for 
my 450MHz Cube  but the results have been a disappointment.

Installing the card required swapping out the "fence" from the stock ATY 
128 Pro video card that came with the Cube. That was OK. But performance 
with the GeForce2 (under OS X 10.2.3) was not noticeably different (!). 
What did I forget? My GeForce2 was from eBay, and did not include any 
software. Should there have been a driver on CD? For OS X?

But the real problem came later in the day, when I attempted to play a 
DVD. Turns out the GeForce2 is not compatible with the DVD Player from 
Apple.

I quickly re-installed the original video card, and then DVD Player 
worked as usual: just fine.

Has anyone else experienced this (apparent) incompatability between the 
GeForce2 video card and DVD Player?
