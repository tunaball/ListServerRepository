Well, that might be the drive it came with but that's not the drive
it 'came with', if you know what I mean.  :-)  Originally the G4 Cube
shipped with a DVD-ROM drive. In Feb of 2001 Apple began shipping
newer models with CD-RW drives.

A combo drive (one that can read DVD's and burn CD-RW's) was
never a BTO option from Apple. If your Cube has an internal Combo
drive it is a 3rd party mod.  :-)
