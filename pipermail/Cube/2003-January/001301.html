My understanding of the prices are that the dual 800 is $800, the dual 
1 GHz is $1,200 and the dual 1.2 GHz is $1400. If you are planning on 
using multiprocessor aware applications like the iLife suite, for 
example, most of the time these will all be good choices. If not, a 
single processor upgrade would be abetter choice.

The big advantage over buying a PowerMac with these processor sets is 
the silence of the Cube will be mostly maintained due to the inclusion 
of and need for only one very quiet small 8 CM fan.
