It's true that it's quite disturbing to speak while you drive. But I found
it even more disturbing to hear your phone ring, and not be able to answer
it.
In this Bluetooth-way (or any other hands-free way) you'll be able to answer
the phone to tell the person in the other end of the line that you'll call
back later. (That is, if you can resist talking in the situation........)
Before I got this system which uses my cars stereo, I had a quite
rudimentary hands-free system, with a very little, very lousy speaker right
next to my ear. Now with the sound directed through the stereo I to my big
surprise discovered that using the phone while driving doesn't take away the
concentration from driving nearly as much as before.
