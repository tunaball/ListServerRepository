Cut-n-paste from MacMinute.com:

PowerLogix today announced several new additions to the company's PowerForce
line of CPU upgrades, including a dual 1.2 GHz model.


  a.. PowerForce Dual G4 1.2 GHz - for PowerMac G4s from the AGP to
QuickSilver 2002 models, featuring 2Mb of L3 cache per processor.

  b.. PowerForce G4 Series 233 single processor - available in speeds of
800MHz, 1 GHz, and 1.2 GHz, with 2Mb of L3 cache. Compatible with all
PowerMac G4s from the AGP to QuickSilver 2002 including the Cube; supporting
both 100 and 133MHz bus speeds.

  c.. PowerMac G3 800 ZIF - for the PowerMac G3 Beige, Blue and White, and
original PCI PowerMac G4. It features an IBM 750FX running at 800MHz with
512K of cache running at 800MHz.

  d.. Blue Chip G3 800 Pismo - for the PowerBook G3 Pismo, can be adjusted
instantly 'on the fly' to a lower clock speed in order to extend battery
life. PowerLogix includes software for this purpose. The Blue Chip G3 800
also features the IBM 750FX, with 512K of L2 cache at 800MHz.

  e.. PowerForce G3 800 - for PCI PowerMacs, featuring the 800MHz IBM 750FX.

PowerLogix said that the 1.2GHz models will ship in 1-2 weeks, while the
PowerForce G3 800 ZIF, Blue Chip G3 800 Pismo, and PowerForce G3 800 will
ship in 2-3 weeks. Pricing has not yet been announced.
