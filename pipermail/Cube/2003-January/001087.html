Err I'm a cube owner - I also have a DP 867 MDD, A G3 powerbook, a  
wintel PC and a cobalt Qube. Don't give a crap about the noise the DP  
makes - it's a workhorse, a helluva fast machine and actually ain't

Frankly the dual-CPU upgrades for the cube don't tempt me in the least

the location where they're being used. It's good to have options.

My point is simply this: post this stuff for yourself by all means, but  
kindly do not include us all in your hopeless generalisations - I for  
one don't give a stuff about MDD fan noise....
