I had rather frequent kernel panics while listening to iTunes and surfing the 
web. I also have an iSub with stock speakers. Curiously enough the panics have 
not been there from the begining. My conclusion is that the USB bus was the 
culprit, since the audio is going through USB and also my ISDN adapter is on 
the USB bus. I just forgot about music when connected to the net and that was 
fine. But ever since I installed the 10.2.3 update the kernel panics have 
dissappeared! Now everything just works. Don't know if it helps, just wanted to 
share my experience. In any case, 90% of kernel panics are related to RAM 
and/or USB
peripherals.
