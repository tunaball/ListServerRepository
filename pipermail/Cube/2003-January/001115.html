Greetings Cube-Listers:

I believe I read a posting in regards to if Apple is providing 
AppleCare services for the Cube.  Well, YES they are, as today they 
called to remind me that my original warranty is expiring.  I confessed 
that I had added a cooling fan in the bottom, and an unsupported Sonnet 
Dual 500 processor card.  They said they didn't care and would warranty 
all the Apple supplied parts for three additional years.  They even 
covered my "clear case" 17" Studio Display that was purchased shortly 
after my Cube.  I guess they need us, or our dollars.

Regards, Jim Dawson.
