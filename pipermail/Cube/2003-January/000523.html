1.
The cube should drive the DVI monitor fine with the adapter. There is no
conversion going on, just re-routing of the pins and digital vid info.

2.
The GF2MX card works in a cube without a fan, but requires a much bigger
heatsink than it probably has now to be truly safe. Find a picture of the
GF2MX that shipped in some cube configurations, and you'll see what I mean.

This topic has been discussed quite frequently on this list, so someone
should be able to provide a link to all necessary info and pics.
