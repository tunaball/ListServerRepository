There is only one cube, why have more lists?  It would seem to me 
inefficient.
I guess if you ask this in reaction to the OT/ moderator/ my little 
brain hurts from sorting through so many emails- stuff that has been 
going around lately, then I suggest this:

using the headers at the top of the columns in Mail, click them to sort 
the mail by different criteria
afterwhich you can shift-select groups of mail all at once and 
eliminate them and be reasonably confident you are not deleting 
anything you want to read

here are some suggested uses:

from-  good for eliminating all mail on any topic from someone you 
don't agree with
subject- read one from the thread, then pass judgment on all the others

all the off topic stuff keeps this place interesting, keeps people 
posting, and ultimately therefore useful.  But you need not thank us.
