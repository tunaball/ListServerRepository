Reviewer's Name: William Rabe billrabe at mac.com

   Drive Type: DVD-R DVD Recorder

Reader Comments: Love your site! I have been an owner of a G4 500 Mhz 
cube since they were released and have longingly looked at the various 
other G4 models being updated with the DVR-104 DVD-R superdrive on your 
site and iDVD working with the upgrades.

I bought a firewire DVD-R drive from OWC and removed the DVR-104 from 
the housing, removed the Cube outer case and after removing the stock 
CD/DVD combo drive from the cube, snaked the power and ribbon cable out 
of the cube and attached it to the DVD-R drive and it was recognized 
and works very well even though the cube looks like hell with the case 
off. iDVD works like a dream and I have burned 2 DVDs so far.

It is quite easy to do and the other drive can be replaced and the case 
put back on when I am done burning the DVD. Also, the external case 
with the DVD-R drive worked well before I took the drive out but iDVD 
didn't recognize it . This obviously will void any warranties!
