Here's the copy:

First Reader Report on iLife/iDVD3 Install w/Retail Superdrive/no iDVD2 
(Rumors Put to Rest) - Here's the first reader report on iLife/iDVD3 
installs on a system  that has a retail Pioneer DVR-105 (aka the latest 
"superdrive" retail model) and no previous iDVD install:

" Mike:
  Picked up iLife last night for my Dual G4 450 - recently updated with 
a Pioneer DVR-105 drive. I had no previous version of iDVD on the unit.

The install was flawless until the required Restart at the end of the 
install, and then the system crashed. I restarted, and the system took 
an extra 2 minutes to come back up, but the software works great!
  Thanks for the greatest Mac site!
  John
  San Diego "

So this should put the rumors of not working with other than OEM 
superdrives, etc. to rest.
