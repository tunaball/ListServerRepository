Apologies for not replying sooner - i was on vacation.
My gigadesigns G-celerator cube upgrade is now working ok.  I have it 
set to 1GHz as I don't have a fan.  I have the earlier version that did 
not require a fan.

The support team suggested that I change the voltage settings on the 
giga board.  I increased the voltage, and had intermittent success.  
Increased it again and everything seems to work ok 95% if the time.  I 
occasionally have a coma from which the cube will not wake - but only 
occasionally.

I'm not sure what the consequences of increasing the voltage are - r 
what effect it has on the system, and the recent instructions posted on 
Giga web site say don't do this as it will damage the cube.

My voltage is set slightly higher than that specified for a cube 
without fan on the 1 page install guide.

As yo0u are in contact with Giga, I'm sure they will be able to help.  
I can report a happy ending to my story.
