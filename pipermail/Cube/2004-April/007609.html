Bob,

1. Assuming you have a dvd/cdrw PM G4 Cube, and your cds are writing/reading OK in there, I'd suggest you to test your data cds in an external cd drive attached to your iBook. This could determine if you're dealing with a 'faulty' cdr drive.

2. Assuming you have a dvd/cd drive PM G4 Cube, I presume you're writing your cds using an external cdr/cdrw drive, right? So, have you tried to attach this drive to your iBook and see if they mount?

3. Before trying anything else... have you tried zapping PRAM and fixing permissions on your iBook?

Hope this helps,
