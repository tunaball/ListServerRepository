eMac for HS graduation last year. I'm very impressed with the new

Airport Extreme and Bluetooth options. Plus more free software.

optical in a stock Cube) is $799 -- which is *less* than your upgrade 
totals. As Michael mentions, if you sell the Cube, your bottom line 
is even better.
