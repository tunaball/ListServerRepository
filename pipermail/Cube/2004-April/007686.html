The Wiebetech Desktop GB enclosure arrived yesterday from Small Dog 
(best price).
As mentioned in an earlier post, I wanted to use the original cube HD 
for occasional
backup & storage after installing a new WD "caviar" in the cube (much 
quieter now!).

Overall, quality is great. The silkscreened logo (only one logo appears 
on the case) was a bit hit and miss.
Included in the box was a silver plastic stand closely matching the 
satin aluminum case, power cord,
firewire & usb cables, utilities CD & illustrated instructions.
The case will accommodate 3.5 & 2.5" drives. Screws are provided and 
slots are plentiful in the aluminum tray.
Best of all, with the exception of the seeking of the hard drive there 
is no added noise (no fan).    Rod

Link for wiebetech: www.wiebetech.com
