some of the 15"AL refurb pricing and thinking of making the switch.

My Cube is: 

Sonnet 1.2 upgrade with fan
WD 120GB 8MB 
1.5 GB Ram 
Radeon 7500 32MB twin-display card

All original boxes/packaging
