> But why have so many others had great success with them?  Is it really

> cards would handle international voltage needs as computers already > do.

It is not the issue of 'internatinal voltages' because inside the Cube 
all the voltages are the same everywhere in the world. Upgrade card 
handles it fine, but it also draws much more current from the DC board 
pushing it to the limits. At this point your DC board might be able to 
hold it, or it may blow up. Bar in mind that DC boards have not been 
designed to handle the load of faster processors.
Pretty similar like putting a 2MX with a small heatsink inside and 
hoping it will not blow up. Most of the time it won't, but it easily 
might.
