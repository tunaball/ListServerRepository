The ADC display draws it's power straight through the power brick
on the floor.  It does not go through the DC/DC module.

I've got a 22" ADC display along with a 7200rpm 80Gig disk, 1.25Gigs of
ram, and the Powerlogic 1.2Ghz CPU.  Also upgraded from the ATI 128 Rage
to the ATI Radeon (with it's own fan).  No heat problems, no sleep 
problems.
