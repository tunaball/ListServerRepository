You can buy the Panaflo fan as cube install kit (from either PL or Sonnet )
for $15-22.00.

The fan that was supplied with my Sonnet 1.2 is the Panaflo "L".  It is very
silent.  It's power cord plugs into the logic board directly; there was an
un-used connector that apparently was exactly for that purpose. (Hence the
fan mount that is built-in to the Cube frame.)

The 1.2GHZ Sonnet is down to $475 now - last week I paid $577! (OWC was kind
enough to give me a store credit...)
