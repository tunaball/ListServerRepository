> My Sony Spressa CD-R drive has just failed (tray motor/ejector), and I 
> need to buy a new mechanism.
>
> Because the FireWire case & power supply are still good, I want to buy 
> just the mechanism, and install it in the Sony enclosure.
>
> Does anyone have any recommendations for a CD/CD-R/CD-RW mechanism?

I'd recommend a Teac. They have never failed on me and are supported by 
iTunes/Burner.

> Or, how about a DVD/DVD-R and (why not?) DVD-RW?

Personally I decided against it. DVD recorders are slower than their 
CD-R counterparts at recording CD's. Since I record much more CD's and 
I rarely have a need to record a DVD, the choice was clear.
