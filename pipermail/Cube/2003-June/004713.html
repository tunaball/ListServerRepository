The bus on the Cube is ATA/66 and therefore will not allow you to see 
above 128MB NET. So anything larger than the 120 GB (net 112-115 GB) 
will not be a practical alternative. I have successfully initialized a 
200 GB drive in a MDD and then put it in an Oxford 911 ADS case and 
seen it all. I wonder if I could get the same result if I put it inside 
my Cube? Not likely. But maybe I'll test that and get back to you later.
