and add the other machine to your Cube. You'll regret selling it. I

find the last new one a few weeks after they stopped making them. One 
day I managed to talk myself out of it because I wanted portability. A

but I love this machine so much it'll never go away. Come hell or lack 
of service parts, she'll be serving...
