John Allan all Cubist,

I just got the FW Depot Concorde cases in and they look good.  I'll 
report back to how they work when I can.  Be careful with the HS-R case 
from Miglia.  That drive only allows for running Raid 1 which means you 
can only run it in mirrored mode (you don't get combined drive size, 
just duplicate size).  It does not allow you to stripe two drives ( 
Raid 0 ) which is what I wanted to do (and I think most other folks).  
I've researched the options so much that my brains are pouring out of 
my head.  The price of the Concorde and the fact that I had purchased 
from FW Depot before made the decision easy for me.  I'll report back 
soon.

Fielder
