I solved this problem by reinstalling the Palm Desktop. There is an 
easier fix. You need to reinstall the conduits for address book and 
datebook. When you installed the iSync Palm piece, those 2 conduits got 
moved. They should be in a folder on you drive in /library/application 
support/palm hotsync/disabled conduits

I think you just need to move them back to the /conduits folder and 
restart. then go into conduit manager and re-enable them.

Hope that helps.

-Hal
