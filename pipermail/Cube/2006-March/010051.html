Installed the Sonnet 1.2GHz a couple of years 
ago. Love it. At the time of purchase there was a 
lot of negative discussions around PowerLogic and 
Giga so decided to go with a slighter slower and 
cooler 1.2GHz CPU from Sonnet. Liked the

their tech or warranty support.

RAM etc. The upgraded Cube "TesserAct" has been a 
joy! Only machine I can't part with. Thought I 
would sell it after recently buying an iMac G5 
2.1GHz PPC model.
Couldn't sell it. Tough to part with art...
