I can recommend the Dell 2709 Display.
That's a TFT (of course:-) with 27-inch diagonal, but a 24" resolution of
1920 x 1200 pixel. A very positive side effect: the single pixel is 
larger, better to regard when your eyes (like mine) become elder.

I bought it here in germany for just 630 Euro with a 5 year Dell warranty.
That's an excellent display with many options (2x DVI - you can 
switch via button between the connected computers!, HDMI, VGA, and 
different others incl. a multiformat-Cardreader and USB-hub, of 
course Audio also). By the time: VESA is possible.

If there's interest, you could have a look onto online-reviews.
I bought mine after reading that review, unfortunately only available 
in german:
http://www.prad.de/new/monitore/test/2008/test-dell-2709w.html

It is my second of this size from Dell. The model before was made 
with better materials (brushed aluminium casing, really(!) excellent 
stand, but 250 Euro more expensive)
The actual has a cheaper exterior but a better TFT.

A good choice. And I'm not working for Dell, I'm just user like you all.
